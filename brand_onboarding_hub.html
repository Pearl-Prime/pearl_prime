V3.2 Marketing Systems

The seven V3.2 marketing systems govern how each brand communicates, engages, and retains its audience. Click any card to expand details. These systems are organized from measurement (EOIS, Contrast) through engagement (Audiobook, Binge Loop) to distribution (Social Wiring, Chaos Slot, Transformation Promise).

EOIS
+

3-Dimension Scoring System

  • Minimum composite score: 10/15
  • Each brand has defined EOIS ceiling
  • Ensures emotional resonance and market positioning
  • Part of core V3.2 brand validation
Controlled Contrast
+

4 Tension Levels

  • Level 1: Minimal tension
  • Level 2: Moderate tension
  • Level 3: High tension
  • Level 4: Maximum tension
  • Each brand has defined permission levels
Audiobook Dominance
+

Listening Moments & Voice Strategy

  • Defined listening moments for each brand
  • Emotional tone voice assignments
  • Somatic markers for listener engagement
  • 5-platform copy optimization
  • Cross-platform voice consistency
Binge Loop
+

Engagement & Series Pull

  • Unresolved hooks per episode
  • Identity arc development across series
  • Series-level pull and momentum
  • Listener retention mechanics
Social Wiring
+

Multi-Platform Content Strategy

  • 5 YouTube hooks per book
  • 10 Pinterest pins per book
  • 3 viral content moments per book
  • 5 quote cards per book
  • Coordinated cross-platform messaging
Chaos Slot
+

Controlled Unpredictability

  • 5-10% unpredictability allocation
  • Type 1: Format surprises
  • Type 2: Guest appearance variance
  • Type 3: Content timing shifts
  • Type 4: Narrative pivots
Transformation Promise
+

"10 Minutes After Pressing Play..."

  • Core transformation statement for each brand
  • Listener outcome clarity
  • Quality score minimum: 3/5
  • Measurable, specific results
  • Brand differentiation anchor
