The Platform

What Is Pearl Prime?

Pearl Prime is a publishing platform that produces emotionally meaningful, high-quality self-help books at scale — drawing from the real teachings and wisdom of attendees of the United Spiritual Leaders Forum.

Pearl Prime is powered by copyrighted AI intellectual property called Ei — Enlightened Intelligence. Created from the combined knowledge base of spiritual insights shared at the United Spiritual Leaders Forum, Ei is a unique blend of psychological and somatic heuristics, feeding genetic algorithms and machine learning. Ei is AI for helping people overcome personal hardships and achieve their dreams.

How It Works

Ei Creates the Content

Books, audiobooks, workbooks — produced weekly. Every sentence optimized for narration pacing and emotional impact.

You Upload

5 minutes per week. Download your packet, bulk-upload to Google Play, activate auto-narration, upload to Findaway.

Global Distribution

Google Play + Findaway Voices = 40+ platforms, 75+ countries. Audible, Spotify, Apple Books, libraries, and more.

You Get Paid

90.6% of all revenue, deposited directly to your bank account on the 15th of every month.
