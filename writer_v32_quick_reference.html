Social Wiring

Platform distribution assets — 4 fields for YouTube, Pinterest, viral phrases, and quote cards

youtube_hooks
list
Strings. Exactly 5 short-form audio clip titles.
Example
["3 breaths that actually work", "Why your nervous system is stuck"]
pinterest_titles
list
Strings. Exactly 10 SEO-optimized titles.
Example
["Nervous System Reset for Burnout", "Why You Can't Sleep..."]
viral_phrases
list
Strings. Exactly 3 tweetable phrases.
Example
["You're not broken, you're dysregulated"]
quote_cards
list
Strings. Exactly 5 Instagram/Pinterest visual text snippets.
Example
["The body keeps the answer in the nervous system"]
