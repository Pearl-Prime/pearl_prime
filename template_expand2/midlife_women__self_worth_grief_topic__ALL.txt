## BATCH: self_worth / shame (20 atoms)

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v01 — NOT ENOUGH ANYMORE — FACE HEAT
---
Karen sits in the meeting with colleagues twenty years younger. They reference things she doesn't know. Her face heats before she can ask. She's not enough for this room anymore. The heat is for being someone who used to understand everything.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v02 — DON'T DESERVE THE TITLE — CHEST DROP
---
Someone introduces Margaret as "senior leader." Her chest drops at the words. She got here by accumulating years, not accomplishments. The title feels borrowed. The drop is for claiming a status she hasn't earned.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v03 — SHOULDN'T WANT MORE — SHOULDERS CURLING
---
Susan wants more from her life. At 53. Her shoulders curl at the wanting. She should be grateful for what she has. Content with what she's built. The wanting feels greedy. Immature.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v04 — WHO AM I TO STILL BE TRYING — STOMACH SINKING
---
Linda is still trying to build something. A new project. A new direction. Her stomach sinks at the effort. Who is she to still be trying at this age? The trying feels embarrassing.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v05 — IMPOSTER IN HER OWN LIFE — THROAT TIGHT
---
Patricia looks at the life she built—family, career, home. Her throat tightens. She doesn't feel like the person who built this. She's imposter in her own accomplishments.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v01-05
---
v01: Karen's face heats at every knowledge gap. This reference: heat. That technology: heat. Each thing she doesn't know heats her.
v02: Every title feels borrowed. Senior: drop. Leader: drop. Expert: drop. Margaret shrinks from each one.
v03: Susan's wanting always curls her. Want more connection: curl. Want more meaning: curl. Each want feels wrong.
v04: Every effort sinks Linda. New project: sink. New skill: sink. Trying at 52: sink.
v05: Every accomplishment tightens Patricia. The career: tight. The family: tight. She doesn't feel like she earned any of it.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v01-05
---
v01: Karen asks about the reference. The younger colleague explains without judgment. "I didn't know that either until last month." The gap wasn't obvious to others.
v02: Margaret's colleague says, "You've earned every bit of that title." The years were accomplishments.
v03: Susan reads about midlife desire for growth. Normal. Healthy. The wanting isn't greedy. It's human.
v04: Linda meets someone who started a new career at 60. Thriving. The trying isn't embarrassing. It's alive.
v05: Patricia's daughter says, "Mom, look at everything you built." The building was real. The accomplishment was hers.

================================================================================
## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v01-05
---
v01: Face heating. Karen asks anyway. "Can you explain that?" The question exists. The heat continues.
v02: Chest dropping. Margaret accepts the introduction. "Thank you for having me." The title is used.
v03: Shoulders curling. Susan admits what she wants. "I want more from this chapter." The words are out.
v04: Stomach sinking. Linda starts the project. The trying happens. The sink continues.
v05: Throat tight. Patricia says, "I built this." Out loud. The claim is made.
