# NYC Executive Template Library

**Persona:** Senior Executive (VP+ level, 15+ years experience)
**Complete Set:** 4 mechanisms × 4 roles = 16 templates

---

## Persona Context

NYC executives operate in a distinct high-performance context:

- **Identity fusion:** Self-worth deeply tied to title, compensation, outcomes
- **Always-on culture:** Email at 11 PM, Slack on weekends, no real off switch
- **Visibility pressure:** Every decision scrutinized by board, investors, media
- **Comparison engine:** Peers who got the promotion, the deal, the exit
- **Control orientation:** Success came from controlling outcomes; anxiety comes when you can't
- **Specific triggers:** Board meetings, quarterly reviews, deals falling through, reorgs
- **Isolation at top:** Fewer peers to confide in, performance must look effortless
- **Physical manifestations:** Insomnia, chest tightness, the 4 AM mind race

### Template

```
{{character.name}} was in {{location.detail}},
heading home after a twelve-hour day,

Shoulders up.
Mind cycling through tomorrow's calls.

The day was over.
Nothing left to do.
And still, her system was on.

Where the person who anticipated the problem

perfectly.

Not hers.
```
