## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v01 — MOTOR AUTOPILOT
---

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v02 — NOTHING LANDS
---

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v03 — GOING THROUGH MOTIONS
---
Victoria runs the team meeting. Same agenda. Same check-ins. The routine happens through her, not from her. Her body knows where to sit, how to facilitate. She watches herself lead. Technically leading.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v05 — SENSATION DELAY
---
The partner shakes Sarah's hand—promotion official. The pressure of the grip reaches her three seconds later, muted and far away. "Congratulations." The word lands eventually. She was just promoted. She's receiving it on a five-second delay.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — EXECUTIVE MONTAGE
---
Rachel sees herself in fragments. Hand signing the document. Face in the earnings call. Body in the client dinner. The in-between is gone. She's a montage of professional gestures.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — GOOD AND BAD SAME DISTANCE

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — ROUTINE INTACT

Victoria's routine is perfect. 6am car. 7am desk. Meetings. Calls. Evening events. The structure holds. The person inside it is missing.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — TIME UNMARKED
---
Weeks end. Daniel knows this because the markets close. Months end. He knows this because statements arrive. The time passes. Nothing marks it from inside.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — BODY FUNCTIONS

Sarah trades because the markets open. Attends because the calendar notifies. Speaks because the meeting requires. The body maintains itself on schedule.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01 — COLD BREAKS THROUGH

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v01 — ENERGY THAT'S GONE — THROAT TIGHT
---
Rachel finds an email from three years ago. She's animated in the writing. Exclamation points. Ideas flowing. Her throat tightens reading. That energy is gone somewhere. The executive in the email built things with enthusiasm. The one reading can barely generate interest.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v02 — ALIVENESS MISSING — HOLLOWNESS
---
James used to feel the wins. Really feel them. Now the deal closes and there's hollowness where triumph should be. The aliveness that used to show up for success is missing. He can remember having it.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v03 — DRIVE ABSENT — HEAVINESS
---
Victoria built this career from nothing. Eighty-hour weeks felt like purpose. The drive that made those weeks possible is absent now. The heaviness fills her chest where ambition used to live.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v04 — FUTURE THAT'S FLAT — CHEST ACHE
---
The partnership track used to excite Daniel. The title, the equity, the recognition. Now he looks at it and feels nothing. His chest aches for the version of himself who could feel ambition.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v05 — CARING THAT LEFT — EYES STINGING
---
Sarah used to care about the markets. Really care. The movements, the patterns, the wins. The caring left at some point. Her eyes sting looking at old trading notes where she was obviously engaged.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — WAVE PATTERN
---
The waves come when Rachel expects them and when she doesn't. Old enthusiastic email: expected wave. Random Tuesday hearing herself described as "so driven": unexpected.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — ORDINARY TRIGGERS
---
James finds the aliveness in ordinary places. The conference room where he used to celebrate. The client dinner where he felt on top of the world. Triggers everywhere for a feeling that's gone.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — SAME LOSS DIFFERENT DOORS
---
Victoria finds the missing drive through different doors. Monday: old performance review where she talked about goals. Tuesday: colleague asking "remember when you were obsessed with making MD?"

================================================================================
## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — FORGETTING THEN REMEMBERING
---
Daniel forgets, then remembers. He starts to feel something about the partnership possibility, then remembers he doesn't feel that anymore.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — WAVES DON'T ASK
---
Sarah is fine in the morning meeting. Fine at the desk. Then she sees an old P&L where she'd written "YES!" in the margin, and the wave arrives.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v01 — WAVE DOESN'T DESTROY
---
Rachel reads the old email. Braces for collapse. The wave comes. But she doesn't drown. She can hold the grief and close the laptop. The loss is huge. She's still functioning.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v02 — MEMORY HAS WARMTH
---
James braces for the celebration memory. The wave comes—but there's warmth. He really felt those wins. The aliveness was real.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v03 — IT EXISTED
---
Victoria looks at the old performance review. She wanted that. Built from that. The drive existed.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v04 — FLICKER OF INTEREST
---
In one meeting, something flickers. Daniel feels something about a deal—brief, thin, but something.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v05 — MOMENT OF CARING
---
A trade goes well. For a moment, Sarah cares about the outcome. The caring is small but real.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v01 — ACKNOWLEDGES OUT LOUD
---
Rachel tells her spouse. "I don't feel like I used to about work. I don't know where the energy went." The loss is named.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v02 — KEEPS THE EVIDENCE
---
James doesn't delete the old celebration emails. They're proof. That version existed.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v03 — READS ON PURPOSE
---
Victoria reads the old reviews sometimes. Not to feel bad. To remember. She was that person.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v04 — MAKES SPACE
---
Daniel clears his weekend. Space for something to maybe return.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v05 — FOLLOWS THE CARING
---
The trade sparked something. Sarah follows it. Analyzes the pattern. Stays with the interest while it's there.
