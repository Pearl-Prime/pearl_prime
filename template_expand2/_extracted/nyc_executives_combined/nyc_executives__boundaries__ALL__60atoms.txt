## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — EVERY ABSORPTION SINKS

## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — EVERY ANSWER TIGHTENS

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v02 — EXPERTISE IS HIS

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v04 — SCOPE HAS COST

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v05 — AVAILABILITY ISN'T REQUIRED
---
Sarah looks at her phone. 11pm call. Nothing was urgent. The answering wasn't dedication. It was training people that she has no limits.

## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v04 — NAMES THE SCOPE
