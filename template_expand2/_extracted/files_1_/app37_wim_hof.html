Three rounds of the Wim Hof Method. Each round: 30 deep breaths to saturate your blood with oxygen, then a breath hold that trains your body's CO₂ tolerance and triggers adrenaline release. This is the same protocol that allows practitioners to withstand extreme cold and consciously influence their immune response.
