## FALSE_ALARM / RECOGNITION v01 — BODY PANICS AT NO — MID — HEART RACING
---
Linda's heart races before she says it. Her daughter asked for the car. Linda needs to say no. Two letters. Her heart is acting like the word will end the relationship. She hasn't said anything yet. The no is just forming. Her body is treating it like a detonation. She says yes instead. Heart slows. The danger was saying no. She avoided it.

## FALSE_ALARM / RECOGNITION v02 — STOMACH DROPS AT LIMIT — LATE — STOMACH DROPPING
---
The curfew conversation is coming. David knows his son will push for midnight. He needs to hold at eleven. His stomach drops before the conversation starts. Holding a limit feels like starting a war. The conversation hasn't happened. The stomach is already in battle mode. He extends curfew to eleven-thirty just to avoid the drop deepening.
