## SHAME / RECOGNITION v01 — WANTING TO SET LIMITS — FACE HEAT
---
Linda wants to be stricter about screen time. Really enforce the rules. Her face heats at the desire. Who is she to control another person's life? The wanting feels authoritarian. She relaxes the rule instead. The heat was for wanting to parent firmly.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v02 — WANTING TO KNOW — CHEST DROP
---
Marcus wants to know what's going on with his daughter. Really know. Ask the real questions. His chest drops at the impulse. Who is he to pry? The wanting feels invasive. He asks about homework instead. Surface questions only.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v03 — WANTING TO SAY NO — SHOULDERS CURLING
---
Karen wants to say no to the party. Just no, no explanation. Her shoulders curl at the authority. Who is she to deny him experiences? The wanting feels controlling. She says yes with conditions. The conditions will erode.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v04 — WANTING TO PUSH — STOMACH SINKING
---

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v05 — WANTING TO BE CLOSE — THROAT TIGHT
---
