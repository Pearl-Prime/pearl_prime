#### Variant 1

**Extended Exhale Breathing**

When anxiety gets loud, your breath gets short and fast.

The fastest way to signal to your nervous system *"We're okay"* is through your **exhale**.

When you exhale longer than you inhale, you're activating your parasympathetic nervous system.

That's the part that says: *"Okay. We can downshift."*

---

**Inhale for 4 counts.**

**Exhale for 6 counts.**

---

**Inhale through your nose... 2, 3, 4.**

**Exhale through your mouth... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6.**

**Again.**

**Inhale... 2, 3, 4.**

**Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6.**

**One more time.**

**Inhale... 2, 3, 4.**

**Exhale... 2, 3, 4, 5, 6.**

---

Did the anxiety disappear?

Probably not.

But did the volume shift? Even a little?

**You just breathed — while the anxiety was loud.**
