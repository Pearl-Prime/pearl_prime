## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v01 — CLIENT DELAY — 4 STEPS
---
The client hasn't replied in two days. Maya's chain starts immediately. If they're not replying, they're unhappy. If they're unhappy, they'll kill the project. Project killed means no portfolio piece. No portfolio piece means harder to get the next client. She's built unemployment from an email delay. The client might just be busy.

Fewer clients means income dries up. Income dries up means back to the day job. He's built career death from a Tuesday's likes.
