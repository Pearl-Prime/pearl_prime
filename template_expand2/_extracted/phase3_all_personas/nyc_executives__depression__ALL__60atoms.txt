## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v01 — MOTOR AUTOPILOT
---
Rachel is in the board meeting before she registers arriving. Her mouth is presenting. The slides advance. Her hands gesture at data points. She's somewhere behind her eyes, watching an executive perform. The board nods. The presentation is happening. She's watching from far away.

## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v02 — NOTHING LANDS
---
James closes the biggest deal of his career. Handshakes. Champagne. His face does celebration. The triumph lands three hours later, muted and far away. The deal closed. He's receiving the news of his own success on permanent delay.

## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v03 — GOING THROUGH MOTIONS
---
Victoria runs the strategy meeting. Same room. Same team. Same process. The motions happen through her, not from her. Her body knows where to stand, how to speak. She watches herself lead. Technically leading.

## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v04 — DAYS BLUR
---
Daniel can't distinguish Monday from Friday. Both had deals. Both had meetings. Both had the performance of being present. The days don't separate. They're one long gray stretch with market close as the only marker.

## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v05 — SENSATION DELAY
---
The client shakes Sarah's hand after the successful pitch. The pressure of the handshake arrives three seconds later, muted. "Congratulations." The word reaches her eventually. She won. She's receiving victory on delay.

## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — EXECUTIVE MONTAGE
---
Rachel sees herself in fragments. Presentation to board. Call with client. Review with team. The in-between is gone. She's a montage of executive gestures. Not a continuous person working.

## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — GOOD AND BAD SAME DISTANCE
---
Deal closes: muted. Deal falls through: muted. Both arrive at the same frequency. James's face does the right things. The emotions are performed.

## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — ROUTINE INTACT
---
Victoria's routine is perfect. 6am: car service. 7am: desk. 6pm: dinner meeting. 10pm: home. The structure holds. The person inside it is missing.

## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — TIME UNMARKED
---
Quarters end. Daniel knows this because earnings are due. Years end. He knows because bonuses arrive. The time passes. Nothing marks it from inside.

## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — BODY FUNCTIONS
---
Sarah makes the calls because morning arrives. Attends the meetings because they're scheduled. Closes the deals because that's the job. The body maintains itself on calendar.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01 — COLD AIR BREAKS THROUGH
---
Rachel steps outside for the car. The winter air hits her face. The sensation arrives immediately—not delayed. She's in her body. On the sidewalk. Cold. Here. The glass cracked.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v02 — LAUGH EMERGES
---
Something the partner says. James laughs before he can observe it. The sound comes from his chest, bypasses the glass. For a moment, the executive is real.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v03 — TOUCH REGISTERS
---
Her assistant hands Victoria the coffee. The warmth arrives immediately. Not muted. Present. She's in the room for three seconds.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v04 — NAME LANDS
---
"Daniel." The client says it twice. Then something catches. That's his name. He's in this negotiation, not watching it. For five seconds, inside.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v05 — NUMBERS BECOME REAL
---
The P&L shows the result. Sarah looks and for a moment, feels it. Not as information—as result. Her work made this. The number breaks through.

## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v01 — STAYS PRESENT
---
Rachel is presenting. She notices the drift starting. Doesn't follow. Keeps her attention on the board member's question. Specific words. Stays in the answering.

## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v02 — FEELS THE CLOSE
---
Deal signs. James notices the watcher pulling back. Doesn't let it. Stays in the handshake. Whatever this feeling is. He's feeling it.

## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v03 — LEADS FROM HERE
---
Victoria is running the meeting. Catches the drift. Comes back. Eyes on the team. The slide is Q3 strategy. Presents from inside her body.

## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v04 — HAND ON DESK
---
Daniel puts his hand on the mahogany. The wood is cool. He's about to drift. Doesn't. The desk is real. The title is real.

## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v05 — TRADES FROM HERE
---
Sarah catches the automatic mode. Stops. Looks at the position. Makes the decision from here. The trade is more considered.

# BATCH 21: nyc_executives / depression / grief
################################################################################

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v01 — DRIVE THAT'S GONE — THROAT TIGHT
---
Rachel finds an old email she sent at midnight, full of ideas and energy. Her throat tightens reading it. That drive is gone somewhere. She doesn't know when it left. The executive in the email built something. The one reading it can barely get through the day.

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v02 — AMBITION MISSING — HOLLOWNESS
---
James used to want things. Really want them. The next deal, the bigger title, the recognition. Now the hollowness opens where the ambition used to be. He can remember having hunger. The space where it was is just space.

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v03 — FIRE ABSENT — HEAVINESS
---
The opportunity arrives. Partnership track opening. Victoria should be thrilled. The heaviness fills her chest instead. She has what she wanted. She's not sure she has the energy to pursue it.

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v04 — VISION FLAT — CHEST ACHE
---
The strategy used to excite Daniel. The five-year plan, the growth targets, the possibilities. Now he looks at the deck and feels nothing. His chest aches for the version of himself who could feel possibility.

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v05 — CARING THAT LEFT — EYES STINGING
---
Sarah used to care about the results. Really care. The wins, the numbers, the progress. The caring left at some point. Her eyes sting looking at old performance reviews where she was obviously invested.

## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — WAVE PATTERN
---
The waves come when Rachel expects them and when she doesn't. Finding energetic old email: expected wave. Random Tuesday being described as "driven": unexpected. The former self surfaces in evidence. Then gone.

## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — ORDINARY TRIGGERS
---
James finds the ambition in ordinary places. The deal room where he used to pace with excitement. The pitch he used to rehearse with energy. Triggers everywhere for a feeling that's gone.

## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — SAME LOSS DIFFERENT DOORS
---
Victoria finds the missing fire through different doors. Monday: old career plan. Tuesday: colleague asking "remember when you were hungry for this?" Same loss. Different reminders.

## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — FORGETTING THEN REMEMBERING
---
Daniel forgets, then remembers. He starts to feel something about the strategy, then remembers he doesn't feel that anymore. The forgetting is a gift. The remembering is fresh.

## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — WAVES DON'T ASK
---
Sarah is fine in the earnings call. Fine at the desk. Then she finds an old celebration email and the wave arrives. For the executive who felt that.

## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v01 — WAVE DOESN'T DESTROY
---
Rachel reads the midnight email. Braces for collapse. The wave comes—she sees her former drive. But she doesn't drown. The loss is huge. She's still functioning.

## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v02 — MEMORY HAS WARMTH
---
James braces for the deal room memory. The wave comes—but there's warmth. He was really hungry then. Really alive in the work. The ambition was real.

## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v03 — IT EXISTED
---
Victoria looks at the old career plan. She wanted that. Built from that. The fire existed. Just because it's gone doesn't mean it wasn't real.

## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v04 — FLICKER OF INTEREST
---
In one meeting, something flickers. Daniel feels something about the strategy—brief, thin, but something. The vision isn't fully gone.

## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v05 — MOMENT OF CARING
---
The results come in. For a moment, Sarah cares. The caring is small but real. The former self flickered through.

## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v01 — ACKNOWLEDGES OUT LOUD
---
Rachel tells her husband. "I don't feel driven like I used to. I don't know where it went." The loss is named.

## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v02 — KEEPS THE EVIDENCE
---
James doesn't delete the old deal celebration photos. They're proof. That version existed. The hunger was real.

## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v03 — LOOKS ON PURPOSE
---
Victoria reads the old career plan sometimes. Not to feel bad. To remember. She was that executive. The fire existed.

## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v04 — MAKES SPACE
---
Daniel clears his weekend. Space for something to maybe return. The flicker needs room.

## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v05 — FOLLOWS THE CARING
---
The results sparked something. Sarah follows it. Looks at the details. Stays with the caring while it's there.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v01 — SMALL THINGS IMPOSSIBLE — HEAVINESS
---
Rachel needs to send one email. Routine follow-up. Her hands hover over the keyboard. The heaviness makes the email feel like a manifesto. The inbox has forty more waiting. The one email is impossible. Everything else is behind it.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v02 — NORMAL LOAD CRUSHING — FOG
---
James's calendar looks normal. Three meetings. Two calls. Things he did last month easily. A fog settles looking at the day. The normal is too much. The things he used to do without thinking require everything now.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v03 — BASIC TASKS STACK — PRESSURE
---
Review the memo. Return the call. Approve the expense. Victoria lists the basics. The pressure builds behind her eyes. Three things. She did fifteen things yesterday. Today three feels like drowning.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v04 — DECISIONS FREEZE — SLOWED MOVEMENT
---
Which meeting to prioritize? What to say on the call? Whether to approve the hire? Daniel's movements slow with each small decision. None are hard. All are impossible.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v05 — GETTING STARTED BLOCKED — WEIGHT
---
Sarah knows what to do. Open the spreadsheet. Start the analysis. The list is clear. Her body won't start. The weight of beginning is too much.

## OVERWHELM / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — EVERYTHING TOO MUCH
---
Monday: one email too much. Tuesday: one call too much. Wednesday: one meeting too much. Rachel's threshold keeps dropping. Tasks that were nothing last month are everything now.

## OVERWHELM / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — NORMAL BECOMES IMPOSSIBLE
---
James tracks what he can't do. Morning routine: too much. Basic calls: too much. Simple decisions: too much. The normals are all impossible now.

## OVERWHELM / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — PILE GROWS WHILE FROZEN
---
Victoria can't start. While she can't start, things arrive. Emails. Calls. Requests. The pile grows while she's frozen in front of it.

## OVERWHELM / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — DECISIONS COMPOUND
---
Daniel doesn't decide. The undecided things need deciding. More things arrive that depend on the undecided. The decision pile grows.

## OVERWHELM / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — STARTING STAYS BLOCKED
---
Sarah tries different entry points. Start with spreadsheet: blocked. Start with the call: blocked. Start with email: blocked. Every start hits the same wall.

## OVERWHELM / TURNING_POINT v01 — ONE THING MOVES
---
Rachel sends the email. One email. Takes forty-five minutes. But it's sent. One thing moved.

## OVERWHELM / TURNING_POINT v02 — MEETING ENDS
---
James survives the meeting. Doesn't remember most of it. But it's over. The fog lifts slightly. One thing happened.

## OVERWHELM / TURNING_POINT v03 — SOMEONE TAKES ONE
---
Her assistant says: "I'll handle the expense approvals." One thing leaves Victoria's list. The pile shrinks by one.

## OVERWHELM / TURNING_POINT v04 — MAKES ONE CHOICE
---
Daniel picks arbitrarily. The meeting will be the first one. Just picks it. The choosing happened.

## OVERWHELM / TURNING_POINT v05 — BODY STARTS
---
Sarah's hands start moving before her mind agrees. The spreadsheet opens. The starting happened. She doesn't know how.

## OVERWHELM / EMBODIMENT v01 — SMALLEST POSSIBLE
---
Not the whole email. Just the subject line. Rachel types four words. That's it. Tomorrow maybe the body.

## OVERWHELM / EMBODIMENT v02 — LETS MEETING HAPPEN
---
James doesn't prepare extensively. Just shows up. Lets it happen to him. The meeting occurs. He was there.

## OVERWHELM / EMBODIMENT v03 — ASKS FOR HELP
---
"I can't manage this today." Victoria says it to her assistant. Someone else takes the thing.

## OVERWHELM / EMBODIMENT v04 — REMOVES ONE
---
Daniel cancels one meeting. Doesn't reschedule. Just removes it. The day has less in it.

## OVERWHELM / EMBODIMENT v05 — MOVES ANYTHING
---
Sarah moves one paper from one stack to another. Not useful. But movement. Her hands did something.
