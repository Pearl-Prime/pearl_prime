## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v01 — THE CARING THAT'S GONE — THROAT TIGHT
---
Maya remembers how she used to feel supporting other creatives. Energized. Connected. Actually curious about their work. Now she's performing encouragement. Her throat tightens looking at old messages where she was obviously invested. That mentor isn't in this conversation anymore.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v02 — GENEROSITY THAT LEFT — HOLLOWNESS
---
Jake used to share feedback freely. Hours of it. Genuinely excited to help peers grow. A hollowness opens where the generosity used to be. He can see where it was. The space is still there. What filled it isn't.

## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v03 — ENTHUSIASM GONE — HEAVINESS

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v04 — PRESENCE MISSING — CHEST ACHE
---
Marcus watches himself on the support call. Physically present. The actual being-in-the-conversation that used to be there is missing. His chest aches for the version of himself who could really show up for someone.

================================================================================
## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v05 — CURIOSITY ABSENT — EYES STINGING
