---

---

---

## SHAME / RECOGNITION v05 — DIDN'T SAY NO — THROAT TIGHT
---

## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — EVERY LOAN WITHOUT TERMS

## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — EVERY ABSORBED MOOD
---

## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — EVERY AUTOMATIC COMMITMENT
---

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v01 — DECADES OF YESES
---

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v02 — SISTER DOESN'T KNOW
---

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v03 — TERMS ARE KINDNESS
---

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v04 — HIS MOOD IS HIS
---
Karen realizes: Michael's stress is his to manage. Absorbing it doesn't help him. It just depletes her.

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v05 — THREE YEARS IS ENOUGH
---
Patricia calculates: three years. She gave three years. That's service. The no wouldn't be abandonment.

## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v01 — SAYS NOT THIS YEAR
---
Face heating. Diane says: "I'd love to see everyone but I can't host this year." The heat continues. The no is out.

## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v02 — SAYS SHE NEEDS TIME

## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v03 — SETS THE TERM
---

## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v04 — KEEPS HER EVENING
---

## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v05 — STEPS BACK
---
