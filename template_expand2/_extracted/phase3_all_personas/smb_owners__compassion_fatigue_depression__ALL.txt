---

================================================================================

================================================================================

v05: Denise says yes to one ask—three more arrive. The community remembers who gives.

v02: One staff member cheers another up. Tony didn't need to be the only support.
v03: The sad regular talks to another customer. Jennifer isn't the only listener.
v04: Marcus says, "I hear you. Let me think about it." One need paused.
v05: Denise says no to one ask. "Not this year." The community survives.

================================================================================
## OVERWHELM / EMBODIMENT v01-05

v02: Staff needs support. Tony supports one person. Then steps away.
