## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v01 — SYMPTOM CHAIN — 4 STEPS
---

## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v02 — DAUGHTER DISTANCE — 5 STEPS
---
Emma canceled the weekend visit. Linda's chain: if she's canceling, she doesn't want to come. Doesn't want to means she's pulling away. Pulling away means the relationship is changing. Changing means she's losing her daughter. She's built abandonment from a scheduling conflict.

## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v03 — PARENT DECLINE — 4 STEPS
---
Mother forgot Susan's birthday. Chain: if she forgot the birthday, the decline is accelerating. Accelerating means nursing home soon. Nursing home means guilt and cost and loss. She's built institutional care from one forgotten date.

## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v04 — BODY SPIRAL — 5 STEPS
---

## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v05 — MARRIAGE CHAIN — 4 STEPS
---
Michael didn't want to go to the event. Patricia's chain: if he doesn't want to go, he's withdrawing. Withdrawing means unhappy. Unhappy means they'll become those couples who just coexist. She's built lonely marriage from one declined invitation.

## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — EVERY SYMPTOM CHAINS TO INCAPACITY
---

## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — EVERY CANCEL CHAINS TO LOSS

## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — EVERY ACHE CHAINS TO DECLINE

Karen builds decline from every discomfort. Knee: health crisis. Back: health crisis. Shoulder: health crisis. The body part changes. The chain doesn't.

## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — EVERY NO CHAINS TO EMPTY MARRIAGE
---

## SPIRAL / TURNING_POINT v04 — KNEE IS JUST KNEE

## SPIRAL / TURNING_POINT v05 — HE SAID YES TO SOMETHING ELSE
