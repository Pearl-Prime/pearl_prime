================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v01 — NOT ENOUGH FOR THIS — FACE HEAT
---
Jake sits in the partner meeting. These investors have backed unicorns. His company has twelve customers. His face heats before he starts talking. He's not enough to be in this room. The pitch deck is ready. He's not ready. He's sitting where real founders sit, pretending.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v02 — DON'T DESERVE THE TITLE — CHEST DROP
---
Someone calls Priya "CEO" in the introduction. Her chest drops at the word. She runs eight people in a co-working space. CEO is for real companies. Real leaders. Her chest drops for claiming a title she hasn't earned. The business card says CEO. She doesn't believe it.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v03 — SHOULDN'T WANT THIS — SHOULDERS CURLING
---
Marcus wants the company to succeed. To be big. To matter. His shoulders curl at the ambition. Who is he to want that? The wanting feels too large for who he is. He comes from nothing. He's building toward something. The building feels presumptuous.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v04 — WHO AM I TO LEAD — STOMACH SINKING
---
Twenty-three people work for Nadia now. Twenty-three people trusting her direction. Her stomach sinks at the responsibility she asked for. She hired them. Promised them something. Who is she to promise? The sinking is for having the audacity to lead.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v05 — IMPOSTER IN THE ROOM — THROAT TIGHT
---
The conference panel. Three successful founders and Alex. He's on stage because someone made a mistake. His throat tightens when they introduce him alongside the others. He doesn't belong in this lineup. The microphone is in his hand. He's pretending to be one of them.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — EVERY ROOM WRONG
---
Jake's inadequacy follows him everywhere. Investor meeting: not enough. Board room: not enough. Team standup: not enough. Customer call: not enough. The rooms change. The not-enough is constant.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — EVERY TITLE UNEARNED
---
Priya flinches at every label. CEO: unearned. Founder: unearned. Leader: unearned. The titles arrive. She shrinks from each one. Same shrinking, different word.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — EVERY AMBITION PRESUMPTUOUS
---
Marcus's wanting always curls his shoulders. Want growth: curl. Want recognition: curl. Want success: curl. The wanting is constant. The shame about wanting is constant too.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — EVERY HIRE RAISES STAKES
---
Each new person Nadia hires sinks her stomach. Person twelve: sink. Person eighteen: sink. Person twenty-three: same sink. More people trusting someone who doesn't trust herself.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — EVERY STAGE WRONG
---
Alex doesn't belong on any stage. Podcast: imposter. Conference: imposter. Demo Day: imposter. The platform changes. The not-belonging is constant.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v01 — THEY PICKED HIM
---
The investors picked Jake. Chose his company. They've seen hundreds of founders. They picked him. The not-enough is his feeling. Their choice is their choice. Both exist. The choice happened.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v02 — SHE BUILT THIS
---
Priya looks at the company. Eight people. Real revenue. Customers. She built this. Not inherited, not given. Built. The CEO title describes what she does. The doing is evidence.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v03 — WANTING IS ALLOWED
---
Marcus talks to a therapist. "The ambition—is it wrong to want?" The therapist: "Wanting is how things get built." The wanting isn't presumptuous. It's fuel. The curl doesn't have to be the response.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v04 — THEY CHOSE TO STAY
---
Twenty-three people could leave. Nadia doesn't chain them there. They stay. Choose to. Every day. The trust she doubts is a trust they give. Voluntarily. The stomach sinks for trust that's actively given.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v05 — THEY INVITED HIM
---
Alex didn't sneak onto the panel. They asked him. Sought him out. Thought he belonged. His feeling says imposter. Their invitation says colleague. Different data.

================================================================================
## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v01 — STAYS IN THE ROOM
---
Face heating. Jake doesn't leave. Stays in the partner meeting. Pitches anyway. The heat is there. He's still talking. Both things happening.

================================================================================
## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v02 — USES THE TITLE
---
"I'm Priya, CEO." The chest drops as she says it. She says it anyway. The title describes the job. She does the job. The drop doesn't disqualify the description.

================================================================================
## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v03 — WANTS OPENLY
---
Marcus tells the board: "I want this to be big." Shoulders curl. He says it anyway. The wanting is out loud now. The curl doesn't unsay it.

================================================================================
## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v04 — LEADS ANYWAY
---
Stomach sinking. Nadia leads the all-hands anyway. Makes the decisions. Gives the direction. The sinking happens during the leading. She leads through it.

================================================================================
## SHAME / EMBODIMENT v05 — SPEAKS ON PANEL
---
Throat tight. Alex answers the question. The words come out adequate. Not brilliant, not disastrous. He spoke. On the panel. With the others. The speaking happened.
