# ENGINE: Wanting to act boldly → shame about the wanting → shrinks back

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v01 — WANTING TO PUSH BACK — FACE HEAT
---
Jake wants to disagree with the board. The strategy is wrong. He has the conviction. His face heats at the thought of speaking up. Who is he to challenge them? They've built companies. He's just... him. The wanting feels too big for who he is. He stays quiet. The heat is for having an opinion he doesn't deserve.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v02 — WANTING TO LEAD DIFFERENTLY — CHEST DROP
---
Priya wants to run the company differently than the playbook. Less aggressive. More sustainable. Her chest drops at the ambition. Who is she to reinvent how startups work? The wanting feels arrogant. She follows the playbook instead. The drop was for having ideas above her station.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v03 — WANTING TO BE SEEN — SHOULDERS CURLING
---
Marcus wants to raise his profile. Speak at conferences. Share his thinking. His shoulders curl at the desire. Who would want to hear from him? The wanting to be visible feels embarrassing. He declines the speaking invite. The curl was for wanting attention.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v04 — WANTING TO ASK BIG — STOMACH SINKING
---
Nadia wants to ask for the intro to the Tier 1 VC. The company is ready. Her stomach sinks at the ask. Who is she to want that? The ambition feels too large. She asks for a smaller intro instead. The sinking was for wanting too much.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v05 — WANTING TO CHANGE EVERYTHING — THROAT TIGHT
---
Alex wants to pivot completely. New market. New product. New everything. His throat tightens. Who is he to decide the whole company changes direction? The wanting feels megalomaniacal. He proposes a small test instead. The tightness was for having a big idea.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — EVERY DESIRE TO ACT
---
Jake's face heats before every bold move. Disagree with investor: heat. Challenge the board: heat. Propose new direction: heat. Each desire triggers the same "who am I" response. The content varies. The shame is constant.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — EVERY AMBITION SHRINKS
---
Priya's chest drops before every reach. Want to lead differently: drop. Want to build something new: drop. Want to matter: drop. The wanting is always followed by the shrinking. The pattern runs regardless of what she wants.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — EVERY VISIBILITY CURLS
---
Marcus's shoulders curl at every exposure opportunity. Conference: curl. Podcast: curl. Article: curl. Each chance to be seen triggers the same "who would care" response. The curl is automatic.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — EVERY BIG ASK SINKS
---
Nadia's stomach sinks before every ambitious request. Big VC: sink. Important intro: sink. Major partnership: sink. The ambition always triggers the "too much" feeling. The sinking scales with the ask.

================================================================================
## SHAME / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — EVERY BIG IDEA TIGHTENS
---
Alex's throat tightens before every large proposal. Full pivot: tighten. Major strategy change: tighten. Complete reimagining: tighten. The bigger the idea, the more the shame. "Who am I" scales with scope.

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v01 — SOMEONE ELSE WANTED TOO
---
The successful founder at dinner shares: "I almost didn't disagree with my board. Felt like who was I to push back. Glad I did." Jake sits with this. They felt it too. The "who am I" isn't unique. It's not proof of illegitimacy. It's just a feeling successful people also have.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v02 — THE AMBITION IS INFORMATION
---
Priya notices the pattern. Every time she wants something big, shame arrives. But the wanting keeps coming. Maybe the wanting is signal—something true about what she should pursue. The shame is reaction. The wanting is direction.

================================================================================
## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v03 — THEY ASKED HIM
---

Nadia looks at the metrics. The company actually is ready for Tier 1 VCs. The ask isn't too big—it's appropriately sized. The shame said "too much." The data says "right size." They're different signals.

## SHAME / TURNING_POINT v05 — BIG IDEAS ARE THE JOB

Alex realizes: his job is to have big ideas about direction. That's literally what founders do. The throat tightens at big ideas. But big ideas are the job description. The shame and the role are in conflict.
