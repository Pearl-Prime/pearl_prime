## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v01 — INVESTOR DELAY — 4 STEPS
---
The partner hasn't responded in three days. Jake's chain starts immediately. If they haven't responded, they're passing. If they're passing, diligence found something. Something found means other investors will find it too. No investors means six months of runway burning to zero. He's built bankruptcy from an email delay. The partner might just be on vacation.

## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — SAME TOWER DIFFERENT FOUNDATION
---
The chain runs identically regardless of trigger. Monday: slow user growth → product-market fit is wrong → pivot needed → runway burns during pivot → death. Wednesday: engineer asked for time off → he's burning out → he'll leave → product stalls → death. Different starting points. Same endpoint. Jake's brain builds the same tower every time.
