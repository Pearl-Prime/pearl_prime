## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v01 — BILLS STACKING — HEAVINESS
---
Jake opens the accounting dashboard. Payroll Friday. AWS bill due. Legal invoice outstanding. The office lease renewal. Health insurance coming up. The heaviness arrives before he can process any single number. The runway shows eight months if everything stays flat. Nothing stays flat. He stares at the screen, not sure which line to look at first.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v02 — FUNDRAISING COMPLEXITY — BUZZING
---
Priya's head buzzes through the term sheet review. Valuation math. Liquidation preferences. Pro-rata rights. Anti-dilution clauses. The lawyer's questions. The investor's asks. Each term connects to other terms. She can't hold all the numbers in her head at once. The buzzing is the math she can't quite complete.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v03 — RUNWAY PRESSURE — FOG
---
Six months of runway. Marcus needs to cut costs or raise money or find revenue. Each path has twelve sub-decisions. Cut costs: who, how much, when. Raise money: what terms, which investors, what dilution. Find revenue: which customers, what pricing, what timeline. A fog settles. He opens a spreadsheet. Stares at it. Can't start a formula.

## OVERWHELM / RECOGNITION v04 — PAYROLL WEIGHT — SLOWED MOVEMENT
---
Twenty-three people depend on Nadia making payroll. The number is in her head while she checks the bank account. While she reviews the AR. While she calculates what's coming in. Her body moves slower the closer it gets to Friday. Each email takes longer. The weight of twenty-three paychecks is on her keyboard.
