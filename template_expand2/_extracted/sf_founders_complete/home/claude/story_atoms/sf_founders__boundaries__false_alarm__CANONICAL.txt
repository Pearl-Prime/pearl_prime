## FALSE_ALARM / RECOGNITION v01 — BODY PANICS AT SAYING NO — HEART RACING
---
Jake's heart races before he opens his mouth. The investor wants a board seat. He needs to say no—it's too much control. Two letters. His heart is treating the word like a deal-killer. He hasn't said anything yet. His body is already running "they'll walk away." He says yes instead. Heart slows. The danger was saying no.
