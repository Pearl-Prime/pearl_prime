## The Three States of Your Nervous System

To understand the war, you need to understand your nervous system's three states:

**1. VENTRAL VAGAL: Safe & Social (PEACE)**

This is where you want to be.

What it feels like:
- Calm, open, connected
- Can breathe deeply
- Can be present
- Can love
- Curious, not defensive
- Relaxed, not vigilant

Where you feel it:
- Soft face
- Relaxed shoulders  
- Deep belly breaths
- Warm chest
- Grounded feet

When you're here:
- You can think clearly
- You can communicate well
- You can connect authentically
- You can see love
- You can feel safe

This is peace mode.

**2. SYMPATHETIC: Fight or Flight (WAR)**

This is where you are after betrayal.

What it feels like:
- Anxious, vigilant, reactive
- Can't breathe deeply
- Heart racing
- Can't relax
- Angry or terrified
- Constantly scanning for threats

Where you feel it:
- Tight jaw
- Tense shoulders
- Shallow chest breathing
- Clenched fists
- Restless legs

When you're here:
- You check his phone compulsively
- You interrogate him
- You can't sleep
- You can't trust
- You see threats everywhere
- You prepare for battle

This is combat mode.

**3. DORSAL VAGAL: Shutdown (POST-COMBAT COLLAPSE)**

This is where you go when combat mode is too much.

What it feels like:
- Numb, disconnected, frozen
- Can't feel anything
- Heavy, like moving through mud
- Exhausted but can't sleep
- Hopeless
- "I don't care anymore"

Where you feel it:
- Can't feel your body
- Everything foggy
- Disconnected from yourself
- Empty inside
- Dead feeling

When you're here:
- Can't get out of bed
- Can't cry (too numb)
- Stare at walls
- Dissociate
- Give up

This is post-combat collapse.

## The Betrayal Trauma Cycle

After he cheats, you cycle between States 2 and 3.

Never reaching State 1 (peace).

The cycle looks like this:

**MORNING:** Wake up in collapse (dorsal) - Can't get out of bed, everything feels hopeless

**AFTERNOON:** Spike into combat (sympathetic) - Remember the betrayal, anxiety floods, check his phone

**EVENING:** Try to function (forced ventral) - Cook dinner, pretend everything's fine, fake it

**NIGHT:** Back to combat (sympathetic) - Can't sleep, mind racing, watching him sleep, rage building

**3AM:** Collapse again (dorsal) - Exhaustion hits, shut down, numbness

**This is the cycle.**

**This is war.**

And you're exhausted from it.
