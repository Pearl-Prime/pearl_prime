There's a teaching about human nature:

**"Give a dog a good name, and he'll live up to it."**

**Translation:**

When you call someone by their highest potential:
- They rise to meet it
- They become it
- They transform

**When you call someone by their worst qualities:**
- They sink to meet it
- They become it
- They stay stuck

**Your words and expectations literally shape reality.**

**WHEN YOU CALL HIM "CHEATER":**

What you're saying: "You are someone who betrays"

What he becomes: Someone who betrays

**WHEN YOU CALL HIM "MY LOVE":**

What you're saying: "You are someone who loves me"

What he hears: "I am loving"

What he becomes: Someone who loves

**This isn't denial.**

**This is creation.**

You're not pretending he didn't cheat.

**You're choosing which version of him to call forth.**
