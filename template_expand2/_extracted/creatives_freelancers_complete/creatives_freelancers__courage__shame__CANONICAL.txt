# ENGINE: Wanting to act boldly → shame about the wanting → shrinks back

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v01 — WANTING TO PITCH BIG — FACE HEAT
---
Maya wants to pitch the big agency. The one everyone knows. Her face heats at the ambition. Who is she to reach that high? The wanting feels too large for who she is. She closes the contact page. Looks for smaller targets. The heat was for having an aspiration she doesn't deserve.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v02 — WANTING RECOGNITION — CHEST DROP
---
Jake wants his work to be seen. Really seen. Awards, features, the recognition. His chest drops at the desire. Who is he to want that? The wanting feels arrogant. He tells people he doesn't care about awards. His chest dropped for caring about something he shouldn't admit.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v03 — WANTING TO CHARGE MORE — SHOULDERS CURLING
---
Chloe wants to raise her rates. Significantly. She's good enough. Her shoulders curl at the ambition. Who is she to ask for more? The wanting feels greedy. She keeps the rate flat. The curl was for wanting what she's worth.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v04 — WANTING TO BE KNOWN — STOMACH SINKING
---
Marcus wants to build a following. Be known for something specific. His stomach sinks at the desire. Who is he to want visibility? The wanting feels like ego. He stays small on purpose. The sinking was for wanting to matter.

================================================================================
## SHAME / RECOGNITION v05 — WANTING TO CREATE FREELY — THROAT TIGHT
---
Sasha wants to make the work she actually wants to make. Not client work. Personal work. Her throat tightens at the wanting. Who is she to put her vision first? The wanting feels indulgent. She takes another client project. The tightness was for prioritizing herself.
