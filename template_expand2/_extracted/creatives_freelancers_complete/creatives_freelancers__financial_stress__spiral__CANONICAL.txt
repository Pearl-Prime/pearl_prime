# ENGINE: One financial fact → chain builds catastrophe

================================================================================
## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v01 — INVOICE LATE — 4 STEPS
---
The client hasn't paid. Ten days overdue. Maya's chain starts immediately. If they're not paying, they're unhappy. Unhappy means they won't refer her. No referrals means pipeline dries up. Pipeline dries up means back to job applications. She's built employment desperation from an accounting delay.

================================================================================
## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v02 — SLOW MONTH — 5 STEPS
---
March bookings are light. Jake's chain: slow month means clients aren't interested. Not interested means word got out he's slipping. Slipping means reputation damage. Reputation damage means no one hires him. He's built career death from seasonal fluctuation.

================================================================================
## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v03 — EXPENSE SPIKE — 4 STEPS
---
Software renewed at a higher price. Unexpected. Chloe's chain builds: higher expenses means tighter margins. Tighter margins means can't save. Can't save means no buffer. No buffer means one bad month destroys her. She's built financial ruin from a price increase.

================================================================================
## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v04 — BIG CLIENT QUIET — 5 STEPS
---
The steady client hasn't sent new work in two weeks. Marcus's chain: if they're quiet, they're moving on. Moving on means his biggest revenue stream ends. Stream ends means income drops 40%. Income drops means can't pay rent. He's built homelessness from a client being busy.

================================================================================
## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v05 — TAX BILL HIGHER — 4 STEPS
---
Quarterly estimate came back bigger than expected. Sasha's chain starts: higher taxes means less take-home. Less take-home means can't afford to create. Can't afford it means has to take bad clients. Bad clients means worse work. She's built creative death from owing more taxes.

================================================================================
## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — SAME TOWER EVERY TIME
---
The chain runs identically regardless of trigger. Monday: late payment → unhappy client → no referrals → death. Wednesday: slow week → word got out → reputation damage → death. Different starting points. Same endpoint. Maya's brain builds the same ruin from any input.

**Surface swaps:** Missed deadline → late invoice. Budget issue → expense spike. Job loss → client loss.
**Engine intact:** Thought chain, catastrophe from neutral, chain contradicted by data.
**Resisted:** Making freelance chains "rational" because income is variable. Engine is the building pattern.
