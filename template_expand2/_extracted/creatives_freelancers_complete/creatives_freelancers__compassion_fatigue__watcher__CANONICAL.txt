# ENGINE: Performing care from distance → watching self go through motions

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v01 — MOTOR AUTOPILOT
---
Maya is in the mentoring call before she registers it starting. Her mouth is making supportive sounds. "That sounds hard." "You're doing great." Her face is nodding. She's somewhere behind her eyes, watching a supportive creative perform mentorship. The mentee seems to feel heard. Maya is watching from far away.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v02 — DELAYED CARING
---
Jake hears the peer's news. Their project got rejected. He says appropriate things. The caring arrives three seconds later, muted. By then he's already moved to advice mode. The emotion and the response are out of sync.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v03 — SUPPORT MONTAGE
---
Chloe sees herself in fragments. Hand typing encouraging response. Face concerned on video call. Voice warm in the voice memo. The in-between is gone. She's a montage of supportive gestures, not a continuous person caring.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v04 — GOING THROUGH MOTIONS
---
Marcus asks the right questions. "How are you really doing?" "What would help?" His voice knows the script. He watches himself perform a caring friend. The person seems supported. Marcus is present in body only.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v05 — SENSORY MUTING
---
The collaborator is upset. Crying on the call. Sasha can see the tears—theoretically. Her hand reaches toward the screen in an empathetic gesture. The touch isn't possible. The empathy isn't either. She's watching herself perform concern.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — CARE ON AUTOPILOT
---
The pattern runs all week. Monday mentoring: autopilot. Tuesday support: autopilot. Wednesday encouragement: autopilot. Maya is technically present for all of it. The caring is performed correctly. She's not inside any of it.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — NOTHING LANDS
---
Good news: peer got the opportunity. Bad news: another friend's project failed. Both arrive at the same muted frequency. Jake's face does the right things. Celebration, then concern. The emotions are performed.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — SUPPORT SCRIPT
---
Chloe has the conversations. "I believe in you." "That's so unfair." "You've got this." The words come from somewhere trained. Her community feels supported. Chloe watches herself be supportive.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v04 — DAYS OF DISTANCE
---
Marcus's care has been distant for weeks. Same conversations. Same supportive words. The connection that used to happen doesn't. His people get a performance.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — TEARS DON'T LAND
---
Sasha has sat with three crying friends this month. Each time, the right responses emerged. Each time, the tears landed far away. The sadness reaches her eventually, late, after the call ends.

## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01 — CRACK IN GLASS
---
Maya is in the mentoring call when something shifts. The mentee says something about their anxiety—the same anxiety Maya had at that stage. For three seconds, she's here. In this call. Understanding from inside. Then it closes. But it opened.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v02 — FRUSTRATION LANDS
---
Jake is listening to the peer's story when something breaks through. Frustration—actual frustration—at the system that rejected their work. The feeling is his, not performed. The glass cracked.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v03 — BODY RECLAIMS
---
Chloe tears up. On the call. Unexpectedly. The tears aren't observed—they're felt. She's in her body, crying with her friend. Then the distance returns. But the tears were hers.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v04 — NAME LANDS
---
"Marcus." The friend says it twice. Something catches. He's Marcus. That's him. He's in this conversation, not watching it. For five seconds, he's inside the supporting.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v05 — LAUGH HAPPENS
---
Sasha laughs before she can stop it. Something the collaborator said—actually funny despite the hard conversation. The laugh comes from her chest. She felt it happen.

## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v01 — STAYS PRESENT
---
Maya notices herself starting to drift in the mentoring call. She doesn't follow. Keeps her attention on the mentee's face. Their specific words. Stays in the listening for this one sentence.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v02 — FEELS THE FRUSTRATION
---
Jake lets the frustration stay. Doesn't observe it from distance. Actually feels mad about the unfair rejection. The feeling is his. He can just have it.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v03 — STAYS WITH THE TEARS
---
Chloe cries and stays in the crying. Doesn't pull back to observe. Her friend needs her present. She's present through the tears.

================================================================================
## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v04 — ASKS FROM HERE
---
Marcus catches himself going automatic with the questions. Stops. Takes a breath. Asks something from here, not from script. "What's really happening for you?"

================================================================================
## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v05 — LAUGHS FROM HERE
---
