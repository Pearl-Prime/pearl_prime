Catalogued five distinct variation styles for slot writing
Catalogued five distinct variation styles for slot writing
The 5 variation styles I've been using:

1. DIRECT

Straightforward, clear, no-frills
Says exactly what it means
Minimal metaphor
"Here's what's happening. Here's why. Here's what to do."
2. PEER

Casual, conversational
Like a friend who's been through it talking to you
Contractions, informal phrasing
"Look, here's the thing..." / "Real talk..."
3. POETIC

Lyrical, metaphorical, literary
Uses imagery and rhythm
More spacious, contemplative
"Watch what practice does... repetition has its own gravity."
4. COACH

Instructional, framework-oriented
Clinical but warm
Uses terms like "protocol," "assessment," "mechanism"
"Status check: ... Key change: ... Operational pattern: ..."
5. SOMATIC

Body-focused, sensation-based
Everything translated to felt experience
Addresses "the body" as the listener
"Feel what the body can do now..." / "Notice where you feel this..."
Same meaning, different entry point.

Each style reaches a different listener — or the same listener in a different mood. The assembler picks one per section, StyleTracker ensures variety so you don't get five poetic sections in a row.
