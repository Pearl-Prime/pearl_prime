EOIS: Emotional Outcome Intensity Score

Measure the emotional intensity of your book across three dimensions. Minimum composite score: 10/15.

As a brand admin, you'll use EOIS scores to verify emotional depth meets your brand's ceiling before publication.

Controlled Contrast

Four levels of emotional and narrative contrast. L4 (maximum) is reserved for critical moments.

Your brand has a defined contrast permission level — check submissions against this before approval.

L4: Maximum
Extreme reversal, peak revelation, emotional breaking point
L3: Sharp
Clear emotional shift, significant narrative turn
L2: Clear
Noticeable contrast, supports narrative flow
L1: Warm
Subtle variation, maintains baseline emotional state

Audiobook Dominance Layer

Optimize every book for the listening experience. Voice, pacing, and emotional tone are engineered for audio consumption.

Every book ships with audio-first optimization — your role is to confirm the listening moment matches your brand's audience.

Listener Moment
Voice Tone
Somatic Markers
Platform Copy

Key Components

  • listening_moment: Time of day and context (morning commute, evening wind-down, workout)
  • voice_emotional_tone: Speaker intent and resonance
  • somatic_pacing: Breath, cadence, emotional rhythm
  • platform_descriptions: Google Play, Amazon KDP, Apple Books, Audible, Spotify

Binge Loop Engineering

Create unstoppable momentum. Unresolved hooks, identity arcs, and binge pull copy keep listeners engaged.

Series momentum directly impacts reorder rates — verify hooks and arcs connect across your brand's catalog.

Hook
Arc
Pull

The Three Stages

  • Unresolved Hooks: Questions that demand answers. Curiosity gaps that pull the listener forward.
  • Identity Arcs: The listener sees themselves transformed. Small wins build momentum.
  • Binge Pull Copy: "What happens next?" Copy that appears in social, ads, and episode descriptions.

Chaos Slot

Controlled unpredictability. 5–10% of content embraces surprise, breaking patterns without breaking trust.

The chaos allocation is automatic — your job is to ensure it stays within the 5–10% band for your brand.

Transformation Promise

Every book delivers one clear promise. What does the listener feel 10 minutes after pressing play?

This is the single most important quality check — if the promise isn't clear and specific, the book doesn't ship.

10 minutes after pressing play, the listener will feel [specific emotional state or realization].

Example Promises

  • • Less alone in their struggle
  • • Clear about their next step
  • • Excited about a new possibility
  • • Validated in their experience
  • • Energized to take action
