How It Works

The Contrast Engine maps emotional intensity per chapter using a 1-10 scale. It enforces a rule: no two consecutive high-intensity chapters without a recovery chapter between them. This is invisible to the reader but measurable in completion rates. For Emotional Healing content, the engine is even more conservative — maximum intensity of 7 in early chapters, escalating only after trust is established through at least 3 completed sessions.

⚡ What This Activates For Your Brand
System Effect

Your books become "finishable" by design. The pacing engine prevents the most common cause of abandoned self-help books: the intensity wall at chapter 4-5.

Revenue Impact

Finished books generate reviews. Reviews drive discovery. A 31% completion increase compounds into a 2.1x review rate, which feeds EOIS and Binge Loop.
