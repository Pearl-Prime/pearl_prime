## §2. The 1-year arc skeleton

Every Plan (A / B / C / D in §3-§6) runs on this skeleton:

| Element | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Duration** | 1 year = 4 quarters |
| **Quarterly cadence** | 1 master teaching + 1 Pearl empowerment + 1 attainment-level conferral per quarter |
| **Per-quarter shape** | **Penance** (90 days of impeccable karma yoga in chosen pillar) → **Empowerment** (the world master gives the level's teaching) → **Alignment** (the volunteer becomes a vessel for one specific world-domain) → **Level conferral** (the new attainment-state name is spoken upon the volunteer) |
| **4 attainment levels** | Each plan defines its own names; all 4 culminate at a final attainment state in Q4 |
| **Pearl News feedback** | Per-level world-good-news domain; monthly Volunteer Digest reports the world's gifts in that domain + identity-formation tagline |
| **Annual closing** | At the USLF retreat following completion, sustained Karma Yogis are recognized in lineage record |

**Operator-locked constraints (V1 + this spec):**
- 4 levels in 1 year. Not 3, not 5. (See Q-OP-LADDER-LENGTH-01 for proposed 5-level variant.)
- Quarterly empowerment is a *special* Sunday — distinct from the weekly Sunday coordination meeting.
- Penance is NOT austerity-as-suffering — it is **impeccable karma yoga in your pillar** (= the V1 spec's definition of "active" = 4+ Sundays attended + ≥ 1 shipped deliverable).
- World Masters teach from World Master level — already-realized beings working for the world. Karma Yogis reach **Vairocana World Bodhisattva** by Q4 — *not* World Master class; an elevated attainment in its own class, named with appropriate humility (operator framing: *"What do we call it? World Dharma Bodhisattva."*).

## §8. Pearl News feedback-loop integration spec

Each plan in §3-§6 names a per-level Pearl News content domain. The domain is a **filter** applied to the existing Pearl News article corpus — selecting which articles a level-N Karma Yogi sees in their monthly Volunteer Digest. The domain mapping is centralized in a new config file:

```
pearl_news/config/level_domain_filters.yaml  (NEW)
```

Schema (proposed):

```yaml
schema_version: "1.0"
plan_id: plan_a   # plan_a | plan_b | plan_c | plan_d
levels:
  level_1:
    domain_name: "Personal redemption + cleared karma"
    pearl_news_tags: [redemption, fresh_start, reconciliation, ancestral_healing]
    minimum_article_quality: 0.75   # Pearl News EI v2 quality threshold
```

## §9. Quarterly empowerment ceremony shape

The quarterly empowerment is a **special Sunday** — distinct from the weekly Sunday coordination meeting (which continues at its own cadence). Per Q-OP-CEREMONY-LEN-01 default = 90-120 minutes total.

### §9.1 Setting

Zoom — same platform as the weekly Sunday. Active Karma Yogis attend; absent volunteers receive recording (Q-OP-CEREMONY-RECORDING-01).

### §9.2 Pre-ceremony (10 min)

Silence held collectively. A senior Dharma Steward names the alliance present in spirit. The lineage of the quarter's transmitting master is acknowledged.

### §9.3 Teaching (30-45 min)

The world master gives the level's teaching in their own voice, in their own tradition's frame. Translation as needed (Pearl_Localization). Volunteers receive without interrupting.

### §9.4 Empowerment / Alignment moment (15-20 min)

This is the ritual heart. Operator's framing (working language): *"The teacher is aligning the vessels of the world dharma."*

The master speaks the volunteer cohort into the level. The Karma Yogi receives — not as student receiving instruction, but as vessel being aligned.

In Plan A Q4 specifically, this is **Ahjan's Pearl Transmission** — the operator's own transmission work, named in his voice (`old_chat_specs/USLF_3_LA.txt:177`).

### §9.5 Naming (10 min)

The new attainment-level name is conferred. Each Karma Yogi receives the title for their 90-day period. Names are spoken aloud — the act of naming is part of the alignment.

### §9.6 Closing dedication (5-10 min)

Merit dedication. Sealing. The closing is brief and reverent — the moment is allowed to settle without commentary.
