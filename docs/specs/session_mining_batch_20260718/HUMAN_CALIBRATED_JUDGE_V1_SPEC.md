## Current Relevance

Human-calibrated review remains valuable, but only as advisory calibration and
triage. It must not become a hard ship gate, and it must not claim bestseller or
public readiness without operator-read approval.

## Reconcile, Do Not Rebuild

The judge should consume current acceptance layers instead of inventing a new
definition of quality:

- Pearl Prime narrative form SSOT
- Ch1/Mara same-person story progression and concrete-cost standard
- bestseller writing overlay
- reader-layer integration spec
- deep five-layer exercise spec
- atom deepening and repair artifacts
- evidence/source truth repair artifacts
- deterministic social/image/video gates where relevant to derived marketing
  assets

## Refreshed Rating Schema

A rating corpus should score samples on:

- story event, stake, turn, cost, residue, and same-person continuity;
- reader-state entry/exit and whether the section changes the reader;
- atom depth beyond bullet points;
- exercise/tool correctness and actual aha/integration;
- source, quote, composite, and external-story truth bridging;
- prose naturalness, register, and non-template feel;
- production-readiness boundaries for book, social, image, and video use.

Each score must retain sample ID, source artifact, rater, date, and whether the
rating is operator, editor, model, or synthetic.

## Guardrails

- Model agreement is evidence, not authority.
- A high automated score cannot override failed source truth, visual license,
  operator-read, or production-release gates.
- Start with small calibration sets and publish only agreement reports, not
  automatic release decisions.

## Acceptance Labels

- `STRUCTURAL_SPEC_PASS`: rating schema, sample manifest, and agreement report
  are produced with no public release side effects.
- `OPERATOR_READ_PASS`: operator validates the scoring rubric and at least one
  calibration slice.
- `PRODUCTION_PUBLIC_RELEASE_AUTHORIZED`: not granted by the judge.
