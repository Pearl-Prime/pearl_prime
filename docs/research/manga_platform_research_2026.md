### Market Entry Requirements

- **WFOE cannot self-publish** — must use MCN/distribution agent (20-30% commission for pure distribution, 40-60% full service)
- **Publication Operating License** (网络出版服务许可证) required — foreign entities excluded from holding this license
- **Tax:** 10% withholding (reducible to 5-7% via treaty) + 6% VAT
- **Minor Protection Law (2024):** Therapeutic/healing content encouraged, but NO supernatural, violence, romance for under-14
- **Ximalaya Kids:** Subscription-only, 60/40 platform-favored split, 3-7 day review for kids content
- **Kuaikan:** No public API, web portal only

### Market Size

China digital comics market approximately **$6.8B in 2025**, growing **15%+ YoY**.

### Content Gap

Therapeutic/wellness manga for youth is explicitly encouraged by the **National Action Plan for Mental Health of Children (2025-2030)** but virtually no foreign content exists in this space. This is a clear first-mover opportunity.

## Summary of Key Findings

1. **Blue ocean confirmed across all regions.** Therapeutic/wellness manga does not exist as a commercial category on any major platform.
2. **China is the highest-revenue opportunity** but requires MCN agent and regulatory compliance (Minor Protection Law alignment is a natural fit for therapeutic content).
3. **Korea/Naver Webtoon is the fastest path to global reach** via Canvas free-to-publish + 156M global MAU.
4. **US/Amazon Kindle is the easiest entry** with highest per-unit margins (35-70% royalty).
5. **Brazil/LatAm is high-growth** but low-ARPU; best suited for brand building and freemium.
6. **France leads Europe** with 55% webtoon growth; Izneo is a natural partner for European digital comics.
7. **Piracy is a systemic risk** (25%+ of webtoon traffic) -- DRM and watermarking are essential.
