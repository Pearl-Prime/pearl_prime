# Craft Bible — Psychological Horror

**Phase 2X.3 backfill (M3 Wave 1).** Pattern follows existing bibles in this directory.
**Genre slug:** `psychological_horror` · **Demos:** seinen, josei · **en_US product shape:** cult/OGN and webtoon horror; wellness embeds as *the thing that will not stay silent*, not as self-help copy.

## Reader promise

Something is wrong in the ordinary room. The horror is not a jump-scare catalog — it is the body's truth refusing to be ignored. The reader should feel the dread *before* they can name it.

## Proven comps (study, don't copy)

- *Uzumaki* — obsession as environment
- *The Summer Hikaru Died* — grief wearing a familiar face
- *Something Is Killing the Children* (Western) — cost of seeing
- Wellness embed: anxiety as an entity only the lead perceives; somatic trauma as a house that remembers

## Three-act skeleton (ep_001)

1. **Wound / refusal** — Protagonist flees or rationalizes the signal (a sound, a cold, a wrong reflection). Fleeing *feeds* it.
2. **Turn** — A survivor-vessel (never the brand teacher by name) models *meeting* the dread: one breath toward it, not away. Doctrine: the only way out is through.
3. **Renewal** — The horror is not defeated; it is *met*. Grip loosens one notch. End on the ordinary room, slightly changed.

## Panel craft

- Negative space and wrong-quiet over gore.
- Recurring visual through-line (a light, a stain, a sound-effect register).
- Heartbeat SFX at whisper register only — never BANG-register for therapeutic brands.
- One panel where the environment answers before the face does.

## Teacher-mode vessel (from `manga_mode_vessels.yaml`)

`the survivor who turns toward it` — wound: flee and feed it; turn: meet it; renewal: met, it loses grip. **Never name the teacher.**

## Anti-patterns

- Horror as punishment for "not doing self-care"
- Explicit therapy jargon in captions
- Defeating the monster = curing the topic
- Teacher named in-story
