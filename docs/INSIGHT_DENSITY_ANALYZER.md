# Insight Density Analyzer

**Prevents generic prose.** Simple and powerful. Lightweight, human-usable.

---

## Definition: Insight Density

**# of meaningful reframes**  
divided by  
**# of pages** (or per 1,000 words)

---

## What Counts as Insight

- A belief reversal  
- A counterintuitive idea  
- A named psychological pattern  
- A mental model  
- A self-permission shift  

---

## What Does NOT Count

- Encouragement  
- Affirmation  
- Explanation  
- Emotional description  

---

## Target

**At least 1 real reframe every 800–1,200 words.**

If a 5,000-word chapter has zero reframes: it may be warm, but not transformative.
