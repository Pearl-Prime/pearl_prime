# Proof-Loop email sequence (conversion-optimized)

**Purpose:** Exercise → Second exercise → Story → Book (→ More books). Two micro-reliefs before offer. Canonical source for funnel app templates.

**Placeholders:** `{{exercise_name}}`, `{{tool_link}}`, `{{second_exercise_name}}`, `{{second_tool_link}}` (append `?utm_content=email2_practice`), `{{story_body}}` or story block, `{{book_title}}`, `{{book_link}}`, `{{more_books}}` (list), `{{first_name}}`.

**Timing:** E1 immediate, E2 +24h, E3 +48h or +72h, E4 +48h after E3, E5 +168h. Minimum 48h gap between story and book.

---

## Email 1 — Immediate

**Subject:** Your {{exercise_name}} tool is here, {{first_name}}

**Body:**

{{first_name}},

You asked for it. Here it is.

**{{exercise_name}}** — one practice, no sign-up walls.

[Open the tool →]({{tool_link}})

Use it when you need it. Or use it now, before you need it — that's when it sticks.

— Phoenix Protocol

---

## Email 2 — +24h (second practice)

**Subject:** One more reset — {{second_exercise_name}}, {{first_name}}

**Body:**

{{first_name}},

Today: a second practice. Different from the first. Same idea — your nervous system, a few minutes, real shift.

**{{second_exercise_name}}** — do it once. Notice what changes.

[Do {{second_exercise_name}} →]({{second_tool_link}})

No philosophy. Just the link. Tomorrow I'll send you a short story about someone who used both.

— Phoenix Protocol
