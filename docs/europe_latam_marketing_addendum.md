### Per-Locale Summaries

| Locale | Market Size | Opportunity | Key Insight |
|--------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **es-US** | 27% of global Spanish audiobook market; ~600 new titles/yr | 7/10 | Fastest-growing Spanish-language segment. Treatment gap (21.4% prevalence vs 14.6% treated). Latin American accents preferred over Castilian. |
| **es-ES** | 57% of global Spanish-language audiobook share; Audible ~80% | 8/10 | +175% mental health sick leave post-pandemic. EUR 39M govt mental health plan de-stigmatizes help-seeking. +47% self-help genre growth. |
| **fr-FR** | USD 330M (2025), 23.7% CAGR to 2035 | 8/10 | Cultural shift: 64% of 18-35 bought self-help in 2024. Reject "self-help" label; use *developpement personnel*. Quality over quantity -- publishers cutting releases 20%. |
| **de-DE** | Largest in Europe; USD 705M by 2030, 25.1% CAGR | 9/10 | 44% employee burnout; sick days from mental health +54% over 10 years. EUR 10B+ annual economic cost. Prefer professorial, evidence-based tone. |
| **hu-HU** | Very small (USD 3.18M ebooks); declining book market | 5/10 (long-term) | Highest personal stigma in study group. No national anti-stigma program. Only 12% of EU audiobook releases in regional languages. Underserved but high unmet need. |
| **pt-BR** | +64% subscription growth (2024); +21.6% digital content | 9/10 | World's highest anxiety prevalence (9.3% / ~18.6M people). Spiritual/devotional self-help outperforms secular. Manga/webtoon GTM viable (40%+ CAGR). |

### Cultural Framing Requirements

| Locale | Do | Do Not | Key Terms |
|--------|-----|--------|-----------|
| **es-US** | Warm, relational, family-centered; honor *familismo*; use Latin American accents; embed *dichos* (oral folklore) | Clinical jargon; English-first; therapy-speak | *Sanacion*, *bienestar emocional*, *confianza*, *conciencia* |
| **es-ES** | Professional yet warm; evidence-based; acknowledge systemic workplace pressures; psychologist-backed | American motivational tone | *Autoayuda*, *desarrollo personal*, *conciliacion*, *bienestar* |
| **fr-FR** | Philosophical, intellectual, evidence-based; psychiatrist-author model (Christophe Andre) | Say "self-help" in English; prescriptive American tone | *Developpement personnel*, *bien-etre*, *epanouissement* |
| **de-DE** | Evidence-based, professorial, structured; Prof. Dr. credentials matter; scientific authority | Casual/motivational American tone | *Ratgeber*, *Personlichkeitsentwicklung*, *Achtsamkeit*, *Sachbuch* |
| **hu-HU** | Gentle, non-judgmental, normalizing; actively counter stigma; frame as self-knowledge not "fixing" illness | Pathologizing language; assume mental health literacy | *Onismeret*, *lelki egeszseg*, *szemelyes fejlodes* |
| **pt-BR** | Warm, accessible, hopeful; blend science with spiritual resonance; Augusto Cury model | Purely secular framing (spiritual/devotional outperforms) | *Autoajuda*, *autoconhecimento*, *saude mental*, *inteligencia emocional* |
