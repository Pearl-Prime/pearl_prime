# en_US 4-Arc / 48-Episode Structural Plans — Index

**Status: PLANNING DELIVERABLE, not authored episodes.** These are structural
outlines (per-brand arc loglines, one paragraph per arc/block) for the full
48-episode shape of every en_US brand in
`docs/agent_prompt_packs/20260723_manga_48ep_3catalog_series/ASSIGNMENT_MATRIX.tsv`,
produced following the operator's voice/quality read-approval of the ja_JP
pilot cell (`focus_sprint_workplace__aizawa_yuu__ja_JP__adhd_focus__nine_sounds_one_ball`,
PR #196). No chapter scripts, arc storyboards, or CI-gated content were
produced — only a structural planning layer, to be authored into full
episodes in a later wave.

`acceptance_layer: authored_candidate` intent throughout — no claimed cure,
no brand teacher named in-panel, self-help topics stay subtext delivered
through genre mechanics per the master dispatch's doctrine
(`00_MASTER_DISPATCH_PROMPT.md`).

**Genre-shape note:** `iyashikei` brands use a **4-block seasonal/atmospheric**
framing (12 episodes = ~3 seasonal cycles per block) rather than a
stakes-escalation arc framing, per the genre's craft bible and the master
dispatch's explicit instruction not to force false stakes onto seasonal/
diaristic lanes. All other genres use true escalating 4-arc structures
(obsession seed→spiral→exposure→reckoning; recruit→losing streak→
breakthrough→championship; inciting fight→training/rival→tournament→
reckoning; proximity→rupture→repair→commitment ladder; system optimization→
body/identity cost→resistance→re-humanization).
