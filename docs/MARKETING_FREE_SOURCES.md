# Free marketing sources (audiobook and industry)

**Purpose:** Quick reference for free audiobook/marketing data used in research and catalog planning. Full list and config: [RESEARCH_FEED_SOURCES.md](RESEARCH_FEED_SOURCES.md#marketing-and-audiobook-free).

| Source | What it provides |
|--------|------------------|
| **APA (audiopub.org)** | Research FAQ, 5-year industry data, annual survey (e.g. $2.22B 2024, 13% growth, 51% US adults listened) |
| **Publishers Weekly** | Audiobook/industry articles, Spotify year-end data, genre growth |
| **Spotify** | Year-end listening data, blog posts |

**Refresh:** Quarterly for APA; weekly or manual for PW/Spotify. Fetch or copy-paste into local Qwen3 prompts (see [continue_gen_research3.md](continue_gen_research3.md)); no Gemini.

**Config:** [config/research/marketing_feed_sources.yaml](../config/research/marketing_feed_sources.yaml).
