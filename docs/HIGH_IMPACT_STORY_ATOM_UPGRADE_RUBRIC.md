# High-Impact Story Atom Upgrade Rubric

**Highest ROI.** The system scales STORY atoms. If STORY atoms are weak, the entire catalog is elegantly mediocre. This rubric upgrades story atoms **before scale**.

Lightweight, human-usable, directly tied to emotional impact. Not governance or telemetry.

---

## Story Atom Must Contain

### 1. Specificity Trigger

Concrete detail:

- A place  
- A physical action  
- A sensory cue  
- A real sentence someone said  

**Bad:** *She felt overwhelmed at work.*  

**Better:** *She sat in her car outside the hospital and couldn't turn the ignition.*

---

### 2. Cost Moment

What did it cost them?

- Sleep?  
- A relationship?  
- Identity?  
- Self-respect?  

Without cost → no tension.

---

### 3. Internal Conflict

Two forces:

- “I should…”  
- “But I can’t…”  

Without conflict → flat.

---

### 4. Insight Pivot

A moment where thinking changes.

**Not:** *She realized she needed boundaries.*  

**Instead:** *“If I collapse, no one benefits.”* — That’s a pivot.

---

### 5. Residual Emotional Echo

After reading the story:

- Does it linger?  
- Would someone underline a line?  

If not → upgrade required.

---

## Pass / Fail Rule

**If a STORY atom lacks 2 of the 5 → rewrite before scaling.**

This alone improves book impact more than any planner optimization.

---

## Recommended Sequence

1. Compile 10 books.  
2. Pick 1 that feels flat.  
3. Run this rubric on its 5 weakest stories.  
4. Upgrade them.  
5. Recompile.  
6. Compare emotional difference.

If the difference is noticeable → scale the rubric across the catalog.
