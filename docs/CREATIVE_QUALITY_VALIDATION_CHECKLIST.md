# Creative Quality Validation Checklist

**Use after compiling each book.**  
This is qualitative, not structural. No CI, no JSON schema, no solver.

---

## A. Arc Strength (The Spine)

- Does the emotional arc feel believable?
- Is there a real turning point?
- Does tension escalate or just oscillate?
- Does the book *move* from something → to something?

**Red flag:** If you can summarize the arc in one flat sentence, it's weak.

---

## B. Story Quality (Impact, Not Just Presence)

For each STORY:

- Is there specificity? (real details, not abstractions)
- Is there vulnerability or real cost?
- Is there a moment of insight?
- Would someone highlight at least one line?

**Red flag:** If all stories feel like "case studies," not lived moments.

---

## C. Exercise Power

- Does the exercise produce a felt shift?
- Is it somatic or just cognitive?
- Does it link to the story before it?
- Would someone actually try it?

**Red flag:** If exercises feel like filler between chapters.

---

## D. Voice Texture

- Does it sound like a human?
- Are sentence lengths varied?
- Is there rhythm?
- Are there memorable phrases?

**Red flag:** If it reads like compliance prose.

---

## E. Ending Quality

- Does it feel complete?
- Is there a forward motion?
- Does it create identity shift?
- Does it avoid cliché uplift?

**Red flag:** If it ends in generic affirmation.
