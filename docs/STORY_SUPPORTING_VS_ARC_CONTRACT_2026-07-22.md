# Supporting STORY vs Protagonist Arc Contract

**Status:** Canonical amendment to `docs/STORY_TYPES_AND_STRUCTURES.md`.  
**Evidence:** July 22 audit found corporate-manager books could pass prose gates with unbound `research_fit` and no character through-line.

## Mandatory arc path

Every sellable `persona×topic×engine` cell must resolve its protagonist spine from:

`story_atoms/{persona}/anchored/{topic}/{engine}/`

Arc beats §§2/5/9 belong to that same-person spine. Missing bank or unbound research fit must be reported honestly; generic STORY may not be promoted into the spine.

## Supporting STORY taxonomy

Regular `atoms/.../STORY` content must carry one supporting role:

- `peer_mirror`
- `cautionary_other`
- `consequence_vignette`
- `atmospheric`
- `composite_case`
- `witness`

These roles deepen, contrast, or pace the arc. They cannot become a second lead, receive a competing book-length transformation, or occupy arc-owned beats.

`composite_case` is permitted only as a bounded supporting case with honest composite framing under `STORY_TYPES_AND_STRUCTURES.md`; it is never the protagonist substitute.

## Migration

The current mass of `STORY_TYPE: character_study` is an audit queue, not the target taxonomy. Retype only after reading each atom: preserve legitimate bounded character studies, reclassify rival-protagonist drift, and never perform a blind mechanical rename. Author missing arc banks separately.

The future strict production gate must distinguish `arc_missing`, `arc_unbound`, and `supporting_story_present`; supporting presence never clears either arc failure.
