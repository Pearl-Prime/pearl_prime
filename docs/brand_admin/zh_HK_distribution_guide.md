## 2. Regulatory Environment

Hong Kong maintains a relatively open digital publishing environment:

- **No pre-publication censorship** for ebooks or audiobooks.
- **Obscene Articles Tribunal (淫褻物品審裁處):** Content may be referred for classification, but wellness/self-help content is not affected.
- **Copyright:** Protected under the Copyright Ordinance (Cap. 528). Registration not mandatory.
- **Tax:** Profits tax at 8.25% (first HKD 2M) / 16.5% (thereafter) for local entities. No withholding tax on royalties paid to non-residents unless Hong Kong-sourced.
- **Language:** Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) is standard. Hong Kong Cantonese colloquialisms are acceptable in marketing copy but formal written Chinese preferred for body text.

### Narration Notes

- **Cantonese (廣東話) vs. Mandarin (普通話):** Hong Kong audiences strongly prefer Cantonese narration for casual/accessible content. Mandarin is acceptable for formal/academic content.
- For maximum reach, consider producing **dual narration** (Cantonese for HK, Mandarin for cross-market with TW/SG).
- Budget: HKD 1,200–2,500 per finished hour for Cantonese voice talent.

## 6. Submission Process Summary

### Ebook Launch Checklist

1. Prepare EPUB 3.0 files with Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) typography — same files can serve both zh_TW and zh_HK with minor localization.
2. Upload to Google Play Books (primary) and Apple Books (secondary) — same-day to 72-hour processing.
3. Register on Kobo Writing Life for HK storefront.
4. Contact HKPL Supplier Relations for library digital lending agreement — allow 4–6 weeks.
5. Set pricing in HKD (recommended range: HKD 58–98 for standard titles).

### Audiobook Launch Checklist

1. Commission Cantonese narration — source via Hong Kong voice talent agencies.
2. Submit via Findaway Voices for Apple Books Audio + Spotify distribution.
3. Contact Ximalaya HK for platform-specific deal terms.

### Manga Launch Checklist

1. Publish on Naver Webtoon (shared zh-Hant Canvas with TW).
2. Submit to Apple Books Comics HK and ComiXology for additional reach.
