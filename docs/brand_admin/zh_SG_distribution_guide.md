## 2. Market Context

Singapore is **English-dominant** but zh_SG brands specifically target **Chinese-speaking Singaporeans** (~74% of the population is ethnic Chinese, ~35% speak Mandarin as a home language). This creates a unique dual-channel opportunity:

- **Chinese-language platforms:** Serve the Mandarin-reading audience directly.
- **English-language platforms:** zh_SG content can also be distributed on global English platforms (Amazon KDP, Apple Books) with the Singapore storefront, reaching bilingual readers.
- **Simplified Chinese** is the standard written form in Singapore (not Traditional).

### Regulatory Environment

- **No content censorship** for digital books, audiobooks, or comics. The Infocomm Media Development Authority (IMDA) regulates broadcast media but ebooks/audiobooks are not pre-screened.
- **Consumer Protection:** Covered under the Consumer Protection (Fair Trading) Act. Standard terms of service.
- **Tax:** Corporate tax 17%. No withholding tax on royalties paid to non-residents (unless sourced in Singapore). GST at 9% on digital services sold to Singapore consumers (platform-side obligation).
- **Copyright:** Protected under the Copyright Act 2021. Registration not required.

## 6. Dual-Channel Strategy

The unique advantage of zh_SG brands is access to **both English and Chinese distribution channels**:

### Channel Matrix

| Content Type | Chinese Channel | English Channel | Recommendation |
|-------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| Ebook | Amazon KDP SG (Chinese), Google Play SG | Amazon KDP SG (English), Apple Books SG | Publish in both languages. Chinese version targets Mandarin-dominant readers; English version captures bilingual audience. |
| Audiobook | Spotify SG (Mandarin), Apple Audio SG | Audible SG (English), Spotify SG (English) | Mandarin narration for zh_SG brands. Cross-promote en_US audiobook for bilingual listeners. |
| Manga | Webtoon SG (Chinese text) | Webtoon SG (English text) | Publish Chinese version on Webtoon Canvas. English adaptation can reuse artwork with text swap. |

### Cross-Promotion Opportunities

- Singapore readers on Amazon.sg can discover both en_US and zh_SG versions via "also available in" links.
- Social media marketing in Singapore should target both English (Instagram, TikTok) and Chinese (Xiaohongshu, WeChat SG groups) channels.
- NLB (National Library Board) digital lending partnerships can feature both language versions.

## 7. Submission Process Summary

### Ebook Launch Checklist

1. Use existing global accounts (KDP, Apple Books, Google Play, Kobo) — enable Singapore territory.
2. Upload Simplified Chinese EPUB files with SGD pricing.
3. Set pricing in SGD (recommended range: SGD 9.90–19.90 for standard titles).
4. Enable "also available in" linking between en_US and zh_SG versions on KDP.

### Audiobook Launch Checklist

1. Commission Mandarin narration — source from Singapore or Malaysian voice talent.
2. Submit via Findaway Voices for Spotify SG + Apple Books Audio SG.
3. Submit via ACX for Audible SG distribution.

### Manga Launch Checklist

1. Publish Chinese-language version on Webtoon Canvas.
2. Submit to ComiXology and Apple Books Comics with Singapore territory.
3. Consider English-text adaptation for broader Singapore audience.
