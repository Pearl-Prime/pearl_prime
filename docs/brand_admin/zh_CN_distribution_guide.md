## 2. CRITICAL: Market Entry Requirements

**Foreign publishers cannot self-publish in mainland China.** All digital content distribution must go through a licensed MCN (Multi-Channel Network) agent or domestic distribution partner.

### Why MCN Agents Are Required

1. **Publishing license (出版许可证):** Only entities with a Chinese publishing license can upload content to domestic platforms. Foreign entities cannot hold this license.
2. **ICP filing (ICP备案):** Platforms require ICP-registered accounts. Foreign entities cannot obtain ICP registration without a domestic entity.
3. **Content review (内容审核):** All platforms conduct content review. An MCN agent manages the review process and handles government inquiries.
4. **Payment rails:** Chinese platforms pay to domestic bank accounts. MCN agents collect and remit internationally.

### MCN Agent Commission Ranges

| Service Level | Commission | What's Included |
|--------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Basic distribution | 20–30% | Upload, metadata, payment collection, annual reporting |
| Standard (recommended) | 30–40% | Above + content review liaison, marketing placement, platform relationship management |
| Full service | 40–60% | Above + content adaptation, promotional campaigns, social media management, KOL outreach |

**Recommendation:** Start with Standard tier. Budget 35% commission as baseline.

## 3. Tax Structure

| Tax | Rate | Notes |
|-----|------|-------|
| **Withholding tax (预扣税)** | 10% on gross royalties | Applies to all royalty payments to non-Chinese entities. Reduced to 5–7% under some tax treaties (check with your jurisdiction). |
| **VAT (增值税)** | 6% on digital content services | Charged by MCN agent on their service fee. Passed through to publisher. |
| **Effective take-home** | ~55–65% of gross revenue | After MCN commission (35%) + withholding (10%) + VAT on commission (6% of 35%). Exact amount depends on treaty rates and commission tier. |

## 4. Minor Protection Law Compliance

**The PRC Minor Protection Law (未成年人保护法)** imposes specific requirements on content accessible to minors (under 18):

### Compliance Checklist

- [ ] **Age-gate:** Content must support platform age-gating mechanisms. Confirm with MCN agent that age ratings are correctly applied.
- [ ] **Content restrictions for minors:** No content that "induces minors to engage in behavior harmful to their health" — broadly interpreted. Mental health content must not normalize self-harm or provide instruction.
- [ ] **Screen time features:** Platforms must implement anti-addiction features. This is platform-side, not publisher-side, but be aware.
- [ ] **Real-name verification:** Platforms require real-name registration for minors. Content marketed to youth (gen_z_grounding_cn) will have a verified-minor audience segment.
- [ ] **Parental consent mechanisms:** Some platforms require parental consent for minor purchases. Factor into conversion rate projections.
- [ ] **Data privacy for minors:** Content collection (surveys, worksheets) must comply with the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) for minors.

### Brand-Specific Risk Assessment

| Brand | Minor Audience Risk | Action Required |
|-------|-------------------|-----------------|
| gen_z_grounding_cn | **High** — directly targets youth | Full Minor Protection Law review. Engage legal counsel before launch. |
| panic_first_aid_cn | Medium — anxiety content may trigger review | Ensure content does not provide medical advice. Add disclaimers. |
| grief_companion_cn | Medium — bereavement content for all ages | Standard content review sufficient. |
| sleep_repair_cn | Low | Standard content review sufficient. |
| stabilizer_cn | Low | Standard content review sufficient. |
| inner_security_cn | Low–Medium | Attachment content should avoid clinical terminology. |

Obtaining a Chinese ISBN requires a licensed Chinese publisher. Options:
1. **MCN agent arranges ISBN** through their publishing partner (add RMB 5,000–15,000 per title).
2. **Self-managed ISBN** through a cooperating Chinese publisher (relationship required).
3. **ISBN-free platforms** — Douban Reading accepts digital-first content without ISBN.
