## 2. Regulatory Environment

Taiwan has **no content censorship regime** equivalent to mainland China's publishing approval system. Key regulatory notes:

- **No ISBN requirement for ebooks** — platforms accept self-published content directly.
- **No government pre-approval** for digital content publication.
- **Copyright protection** under Taiwan's Copyright Act (著作權法) — registration recommended but not mandatory.
- **Tax:** Withholding tax on royalties to foreign entities is 20% (reducible via tax treaties). Local entities pay standard income tax.
- **Rating systems:** Some platforms apply voluntary age ratings. Mental health content is not restricted.

## 6. Submission Process Summary

### Ebook Launch Checklist

1. Prepare EPUB 3.0 files with Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) typography.
2. Register on Readmoo publisher portal — allow 2 weeks for approval.
3. Submit to Books.com.tw via publisher services email — allow 3–4 weeks.
4. Upload to Kobo Writing Life, Apple Books, Google Play — same-day to 72-hour processing.
5. Set pricing in TWD (recommended range: TWD 220–350 for standard titles).

### Audiobook Launch Checklist

1. Commission Taiwan Mandarin narration — budget TWD 8,000–15,000 per finished hour.
2. Submit via Findaway Voices for Apple Books Audio + Spotify distribution.
3. Contact Ximalaya TW directly for platform-specific deal terms.

### Manga Launch Checklist

1. Adapt content to vertical scroll format with Traditional Chinese text.
2. Publish on LINE Webtoon TW Canvas as primary channel.
3. Pitch to Comico and Toomics for curated placement.
