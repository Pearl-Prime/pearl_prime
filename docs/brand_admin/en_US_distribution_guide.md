| **inner_security** | Medium | Attachment repair themes work well in relationship-focused manga. |

## 7. Next Steps for Brand Admins

1. **Verify accounts:** Ensure your team has active publisher accounts on KDP, Apple Books for Authors, and Google Play Partner Center by April 15, 2026.
2. **Prepare ebook files:** All 24 brands should have EPUB 3.0 master files ready. Run through KDP Previewer and Apple Books Asset Guide validation.
3. **Audiobook production:** Identify narrator candidates for your brand's voice. Submit audition requests via ACX by May 1, 2026.
4. **Manga pilot brands:** gen_z_grounding, identity_rebuild, emotional_resilience, grief_companion brand admins — coordinate with Pearl_Creative on vertical scroll artwork specs by April 30, 2026.
5. **Pricing strategy:** Align with Pearl_Marketing on per-platform pricing tiers. KDP $2.99–$9.99 range for 70% royalty. Apple Books and Kobo should match.
6. **Aggregator setup:** One team member per brand should register on Draft2Digital and Authors Republic to handle wide distribution.
7. **Monthly revenue review:** Schedule first revenue review for August 2026 (covering first full quarter of sales).
