ALARM & FEAR SYSTEMS FAMILY
Template Writer Spec v2.0
(Slot-Driven | Observer-Author | Gen Z | System-Compatible)

0️⃣ PURPOSE OF THIS FAMILY
This family addresses prediction-driven fear loops.
All topics must map to:
Threat Detection → Future Prediction → Nervous System Arousal → Avoidance → Reinforcement
Examples:
Social anxiety
Performance anxiety
Financial anxiety
Fear of flying
Health anxiety
Relationship anxiety
Fear of rejection
If the topic does not revolve around anticipatory threat prediction, it does not belong in this family.

1️⃣ ARCHITECTURE PRINCIPLE
The writer does NOT write:
SCENE content
STORY content
EXERCISE content
Those are injected by the system.
The writer writes:
Chapter arc
Power points
Insight sections
Reflection sections
Transitions
Integration closes
Slot intent instructions
The writer builds the intellectual and emotional spine.
The system injects the experiential units.

2️⃣ AUTHOR VOICE MODEL
Identity
The author is:
A pattern-noticer
A reflective observer
A synthesizer of other people’s experiences
Not the hero
Not a therapist
Not a fixer
Stories are about others. Insight is in the author’s voice.

Tone
Allowed:
“I started noticing something.”
“This pattern shows up often.”
“What’s interesting is…”
Not allowed:
“You must.”
“You should.”
Cure language
Motivational preaching
Clinical lecturing tone
Authority comes from clarity, not intensity.

3️⃣ BOOK ARC (12 Chapters — Fixed)
(Identical to v1.0; unchanged for structural continuity.)
PHASE I — HARDSHIP PHASE II — UNDERSTANDING PHASE III — EXPERIMENTATION PHASE IV — EXPANSION
Each chapter contains a single Power Point.
The writer must state the Power Point in one sentence before drafting the chapter.

4️⃣ CHAPTER STRUCTURE (Slot-Driven Template)

SCENE_SLOT
STORY_SLOT
EXERCISE_SLOT

5️⃣ SLOT INTENT CONTRACT (Writer Responsibility)
For each slot, the writer must specify intent.
Example:
SCENE_SLOT_A — Intent
Purpose: Demonstrate initial trigger activation tied to chapter power point. Must include: Concrete threat object + physiological marker.
STORY_SLOT_A — Intent
Purpose: Show prediction exaggeration reinforcing avoidance pattern.
EXERCISE_SLOT_B — Intent
Purpose: Behavioral experiment testing the chapter thesis.
The writer does not write the content. The writer defines what it must prove.

6️⃣ POWER POINT ALIGNMENT RULE
All writer-written sections must:
Reinforce the chapter thesis
Interpret the injected story correctly
Clarify mechanism
Advance behavioral understanding
If a slot injection contradicts the thesis, the writer must adjust the INSIGHT, not the slot content.
The spine controls interpretation.

7️⃣ SCENE INJECTION RULES (System Side — Writer Awareness)
Scenes will:
Contain a concrete trigger
Include ≥1 physiological marker
Avoid interpretation
Remain open-loop
Writer must:
Bridge from SCENE into REFLECTION cleanly
Interpret without over-explaining

8️⃣ STORY INJECTION RULES (System Side — Writer Awareness)
Stories will:
Contain trigger
Show prediction
Show avoidance
Show a behavioral shift
Resolve the narrative beat
Writer must:
Extract pattern
Tie to chapter thesis
Avoid exaggerating transformation

9️⃣ EXERCISE INJECTION RULES (System Side — Writer Awareness)
Each chapter must include:
One regulation-based exercise
One behavioral/prediction experiment
Writer must:
Introduce exercise naturally
Debrief exercise in REFLECTION or INSIGHT
Avoid motivational hype
Exercises are functional, not inspirational.
