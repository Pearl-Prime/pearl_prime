### The Genre Shell Revenue Gap — Why This Changes Everything

**CRITICAL FINDING (updated 2026-04-23):** Explicit wellness content underperforms genre-embedded content by 10–23x. The same psychological depth delivers radically different unit volume depending on whether it travels inside a genre shell.

| Title | What it actually is | Genre shell | Copies |
|---|---|---|---|
| Oyasumi Punpun | Depression, trauma memoir | **None — raw psychological** | 3M |
| Mob Psycho 100 | Anxiety, identity, worthiness | Psychic action | 2.8M |
| Vinland Saga | Forgiveness, purpose, pacifism | Viking violence | 7M |
| Frieren | Grief, meaning of life, presence | Fantasy adventure | 30–32M |
| Tokyo Ghoul | Identity fragmentation, marginalization | Urban horror | 47M |
| Spy x Family | Found family, attachment, childhood trauma | Spy action comedy | 41M |
| Berserk | Trauma, grief, perseverance | Dark fantasy | 70M |
| Evangelion | Depression, identity collapse, Jungian shadow | Mecha | $16B franchise |

**Implication:** Punpun with no genre shell = 3M. Berserk with dark fantasy shell = 70M. **The genre shell is the revenue multiplier, not the content quality.** An iyashikei-only catalog captures the 3M ceiling. A genre-diversified catalog reaches the 30–70M range.

**Catalog correction:** The initial JP series plan (sections 4–5) was drafted as explicit healing/therapy manga only. This was wrong. Sections 4–5 are now updated to reflect the correct genre mix per `docs/GENRE_PORTFOLIO_PLAN.md`.

### Topic Mix: Asian Market Fit is Primary

Our 12 topic domains map **directly** to Japanese genre manga — both iyashikei and mainstream genre fiction:
- Anxiety → iyashikei *and* psychological horror / thriller (Mob Psycho model)
- Burnout → workplace manga *and* isekai recovery (protagonist quits the system entirely)
- Grief / meaning → fantasy adventure (Frieren model — grief carried inside genre)
- Trauma / somatic → dark fantasy (Berserk model — body memory as world-building)
- Identity / fragmentation → urban horror (Tokyo Ghoul model)
- Found family / attachment → action comedy (Spy x Family model)
- Social anxiety → supernatural mystery (protagonist reads social cues via supernatural ability)

US illustrated self-help is **text-first** (Brené Brown, James Clear) or **Western cartoon** (Gemma Correll). The US manga self-help niche exists but is thin (No Starch Manga Guides, Wiley Manga for Success, *I'm Enough*).

## 7. TW Series Direction — `bright_presence_tw_seinen`

**Art style:** Hybrid manga + atmospheric European-influenced rendering  
**Format:** Manga page layout OR vertical scroll for LINE Comics TW  
**Color:** Muted literary palette — not pure manga B&W, not full Korean color  
**Platform:** LINE Comics TW, standalone tankobon  

Taiwanese manhua direction: slice-of-life + local cultural narrative. Topics: daily life anxiety, workplace social stress, family dynamics. **Not** explicit CBT framing — that reads as too clinical for TW market. The *Funeral Director*, *Day Off*, *Son of Formosa* comp titles set the tone: emotional, atmospheric, grounded in local experience.

Series concepts for `bright_presence_tw_seinen` (4 niche-tier series):
1. **每天回家的路** (*The Walk Home Every Day*) — Anthology of Taipei workers on their evening commute. No conflict. Observation + quiet internal life.  
2. **便利商店的朋友** (*Friends at the Convenience Store*) — Late-night convenience store as social hub. Rotating cast of lonely people finding brief connection.  
3. **週五加班室** (*Friday Overtime Office*) — Quiet social anxiety in open-plan office. 4-koma hybrid.  
4. **台北的靜** (*Stillness in Taipei*) — Urban iyashikei. Quiet corners of a loud city.

## 8. CN Series Direction — `qi_foundation_cultivation` / general CN brands

**Format:** Vertical-scroll tiáomàn (条漫) ONLY — manga page layout underperforms on Kuaikan/Bilibili  
**Color:** Soft pastels, full color mandatory for platform  
**Regulatory note:** Avoid explicit "negative emotion" framing in series descriptions for CN market (CCP content guidelines). Frame as "healing" and "growth" not "therapy" or "depression."  
**Topic fit:** Romance-wrapped healing (CN dominant format) > pure self-help framing  

Wellness themes in CN work best embedded in:
- Slice-of-life romance with healing subplot
- "Coming-of-age in the city" narrative (大城市打拼)
- Career growth + self-discovery (职场治愈系)

## 9. US Direction Decision Required

Two mutually exclusive paths — **do not mix**:

| Path | Art Style | Channel | Price | Target Reader |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Manga digest** | B&W iyashikei minimalism | Manga aisle (Barnes & Noble, Amazon manga section) | $10–16 | Existing manga readers who want healing content |
| **Western cartoon** | Gemma Correll doodle style | Mainstream self-help shelf | $18–28 | Self-help readers who don't read manga |

**Recommendation:** Start with manga digest (Path A) — it uses existing assets and pipeline. Attempt western cartoon (Path B) for `stillness_press` once Path A has proof-of-concept sales data.
