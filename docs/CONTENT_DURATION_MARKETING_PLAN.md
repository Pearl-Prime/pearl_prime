## S1 Executive Summary

Duration is the most under-optimized variable in content marketing. Most publishers pick a length based on genre convention or gut feel, then wonder why completion rates are low. This plan makes duration a first-class strategic decision grounded in three converging data sets:

1. **Platform algorithm science.** YouTube rewards completion rate over raw watch time [D:31]. TikTok videos under 30s drive 280% higher completion [D:33]. Webtoon's comedy tier requires only 12 panels [D:24]. Every platform has a sweet spot -- miss it and the algorithm buries you.
2. **Therapeutic dose alignment.** A 5-minute breathing exercise produces measurable HRV changes [D:16]. A 10-minute meditation reduces irritability by 27% [D:18]. A 60-second micro-mindfulness intervention, repeated 4x daily, reduces cortisol by 14% [D:42]. When content duration matches therapeutic minimum effective dose, users feel the effect, come back, and tell others.
3. **Generational attention windows.** Gen Z reads 9-19 minutes/day [D:12]. Gen X+ reads 32-46 minutes/day [D:12]. A 7-hour audiobook takes Gen Z three weeks to finish at their pace. A 3-hour audiobook takes one week. Shorter completion = more reviews = higher catalog velocity.

**The competitive advantage:** No competitor in the wellness content space systematically optimizes duration across format, platform, persona, and therapeutic modality simultaneously. This plan does.

## S2 Duration Strategy by Format

### Audiobooks

| Dimension | Short-Form (Gen Z/Alpha) | Standard (Millennials/Parents) | Extended (Gen X/Managers) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Duration | 1.5-3 hours | 3-5 hours | 5-7 hours |
| Chapters | 8-12 x 10-15 min | 12-18 x 15-20 min | 18-24 x 15-20 min |
| Platform sweet spot | Spotify (15hr/mo limit = 5 books) | Audible (credit model favors value) | Audible, Ximalaya |
| Therapeutic dose | 1 chapter = 1 guided breathing + 1 reframe | 1 chapter = full visualization session | 1 chapter = deep narrative therapy |
| Completion rate (est.) | 70-80% (1 week) | 50-65% (2 weeks) | 40-55% (3 weeks) |
| Competitive gap | No native-voice Gen Z self-help under 3hr [G:S3.7] | Crowded but fragmented [G:S3.1] | Saturated at 5-7hr; 3hr gap open |

**Platform-specific notes:**
- **Audible:** Self-help averages 5-7hr [D:3]; curates "under 3hr" collections [D:9] -- our short-form catalog gets featured placement
- **Spotify:** 15hr/month included [D:4]; at 3hr/book users finish 5 books/month vs 2 at 7hr
- **Ximalaya:** 268M MAU, 125 min/day usage [D:13]; episode format (10-30 min) outperforms monolithic audiobooks
- **Millie (Korea):** Summary audiobooks at 30 min dominate [D:15] -- produce 30-min companion summaries for all titles

### Ebooks

| Tier | Pages | Words | Price Point | Royalty | Best Persona |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Short read | 60-100 | 15K-30K | $2.99-$4.99 | 70% KDP | Gen Z, Gen Alpha |
| Standard | 150-230 | 50K-75K | $4.99-$9.99 | 70% KDP | All personas |

**Key data:** Average daily reading is 15-16 min [D:12]; mobile sessions are 3-5 min [D:21]. Design chapters at 5-8 pages (one mobile session). A 100-page short read finishes in 5-7 days. A 230-page book takes 2-3 weeks. [D:12][D:21]

### Podcasts

|---|---|---|---|

**Therapeutic alignment:** 10 min daily beats 70 min weekly for sustained benefit [D:18][D:38]. Prioritize daily micro-dose format.

### The Therapy Manga Market Gap

**Clinical evidence:** University of Tokyo RCT (PLOS One) proved manga-format CBT delivery produces statistically significant improvement in depression scores (BDI-II, p<0.05) at 6-month follow-up [M:1.4]. This is peer-reviewed proof that illustrated therapeutic content works.

**Market gap:** No dedicated "therapy manga" brand exists on any major webtoon platform. Webtoon has no "self-help" or "wellness" genre category [M:3.3]. Content appears under Slice of Life or Drama tags. First mover owns the category.

**Production plan by tier:**

| Tier | Series/Year | Episodes/Series | Total Panels/Year | Cost Model |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Micro (5 panels, 3x/week) | 4 series | 156 eps each | 3,120 panels | Lowest cost; social discovery |
| Short (15 panels, weekly) | 6 series | 52 eps each | 4,680 panels | Education + platform building |
| Standard (50 panels, weekly) | 3 series | 52 eps each | 7,800 panels | Full serial; Originals path |
| Volume (200 pages, quarterly) | 4 volumes/year | 1 each | 3,200 pages | Premium product; print + digital |

## S5 Platform Algorithm Optimization

| Platform | Algorithm Priority | Duration Sweet Spot | Duration Pitfall | Source |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **YouTube Long** | Viewer satisfaction + session depth | 10-20 min (self-help) | >25 min drops below 40% retention [V:S2] | [D:31] |
| **YouTube Shorts** | Completion rate | 15-30s (80%+ retention) | >45s retention drops sharply | [D:32] |
| **TikTok** | Completion rate (#1 signal) | 60-90s (43% more reach than shorter) | <15s lacks substance; >3 min low completion | [D:33] |
| **Instagram Reels** | Saves > Likes (2025-2026 algo) | 15-30s (40-60% retention) | >60s reduced reach; wellness = lowest engagement sector | [D:34] |
| **Bilibili** | Quality + engagement | 7-15 min (educational) | Short-form underperforms; not a Shorts platform | [D:35] |
| **Douyin** | Purchase intent + usefulness | 1-3 min (tutorials) | Repurposed TikTok content fails; must be Douyin-native | [D:36] |
| **Webtoon** | Episode completion + return visits | 40-60 panels (4-6 min read) | <20 panels feels thin; >80 panels risks abandonment | [D:24] |
| **Kakao/Naver** | Weekly return rate | 40-60 panels | Chapters under 40 panels underperform | [D:25] |
| **Spotify (audio)** | Completion signals | 20-30 min episodes | Audiobooks >7hr may not complete within monthly allotment | [D:4] |
| **Ximalaya** | Episode completion + daily return | 10-30 min episodes | Monolithic audiobooks underperform episodic format | [D:13] |

### Key Algorithm Hacks

1. **YouTube 8-min threshold.** All Therapeutic Mid content targets 8-12 min minimum to unlock mid-roll ads. Our ITE pacing (6 cycles/min breathing entrainment) naturally drives high completion [V:S6].
2. **TikTok 60s Creator Rewards.** Videos >60s earn $0.40-1.00/1K views vs $0.03-0.08 for shorts [V:S2]. Target 60-90s for TikTok-first content.
3. **Webtoon comedy exception.** Comedy/Slice-of-Life requires only 12 panels for contests [D:24]. Our micro-tier wellness strips qualify here -- lowest production cost, largest platform audience.
4. **Bilibili danmaku density.** Longer videos accumulate more danmaku comments, which signal engagement to the algorithm. 10-15 min videos with discussion-provoking content outperform shorter clips [D:35].

## S8 Revenue Impact Model

### How Optimized Durations Drive Revenue

| Mechanism | Duration Lever | Revenue Impact | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Higher completion rates** | Match format to attention window | Algorithm boost = 2-5x organic reach | YouTube: 100% completion on 8-min > 40% on 25-min [V:S2]; TikTok: >50% completion = significantly more distribution [D:33] |
| **Therapeutic effectiveness** | Match to clinical minimum dose | Better reviews, word-of-mouth, retention | 5-min breathing = HRV change [D:16]; 10-min meditation = 27% irritability reduction [D:18] |
| **Production efficiency** | Shorter content = more titles/month | Catalog depth = more platform shelf space | $0.42/short vs $4.05/long [V:S8]; 10x more shorts per dollar |
| **Platform monetization thresholds** | YouTube 8+ min = mid-roll | $5-12 RPM vs $0.05-0.15 RPM (Shorts) | [D:31][D:32] |
| **Audiobook completion = review velocity** | 3hr book completes in 1 week vs 3 weeks for 7hr | More completions = more reviews = higher ranking | At 41 min/day avg listening [D:6], 3hr = 4.4 days; 7hr = 10.2 days |
| **Attention residue afterglow** | Design content to end just before peak effect fades | Users associate the lasting calm with the brand | 15-23 min afterglow post-content [D:43] |

## S9 90-Day Duration Optimization Rollout

| Week | Action | Deliverable |
