## REFLECTION v01
---
The mechanism of depression that arrives in professional contexts is different from the textbook version because it does not look like depression. It looks like competence. You are getting things done. You are showing up. You are producing. But inside, the texture has changed. The thing that used to feel engaging now feels flat. The accomplishment that used to matter now feels hollow.

This is the mechanism I want to name: the flatness that coexists with functionality. The system that keeps you performing while removing the sensation that the performance means anything. You are depressed and effective. These are not supposed to be compatible. But they are. And the compatibility is what makes it invisible.

Because if you were failing, it would be clear. But you are not. You are fine. And the fineness masks the flatness underneath.

## REFLECTION v02
---
I have watched the way that the Sunday dread operates as a mechanism. It is not the dread of going to work. It is the dread of another week of the same flatness. Another week of showing up and doing the thing and feeling nothing about it. Another week of the absence of sensation that should be there.

The mechanism is that the dread itself is sometimes the only feeling that is strong enough to break through the flatness. So you become attached to the dread because it is proof that you can still feel something. This is the mechanism: the perverse relationship where the worst feeling is better than no feeling at all.

So the Sunday evening becomes the evidence that you are still alive.

## REFLECTION v03

The mechanism of scrolling as a form of rest is the mechanism that depression uses to protect itself. You are exhausted. You need to rest. But rest feels impossible because rest would require facing the flatness directly. So you scroll. You consume. You let the feed tell you what to think about.

The mechanism is that scrolling is not rest. It is avoidance of rest. But it feels like rest. It is accessible. It is easy. It is better than sitting with the flatness. So the mechanism embeds itself. Scrolling becomes your form of recovery. And recovery never actually happens.

So you remain exhausted. But at least you are not sitting with the exhaustion.

When everything is very much not fine.
