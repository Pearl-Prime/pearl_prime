# Gen Z Self-Help Master Script
## File 3: Decompression - Release What's Not Yours

### You're Carrying Too Much
You're worried about climate change, student debt, political chaos, family drama, your 47 group chats, the algorithm, whether you're doing enough activism, if you're productive enough, plus actual work/school. 

No wonder you're exhausted.

### The Backpack Visualization
Imagine your stress as a backpack. You've been handed:
- Student debt (not your fault - system failure)
- Housing crisis (not your fault - policy failure)
- Climate anxiety (you didn't cause this - corporate failure)
- Family expectations (their hopes, not your responsibility)
- Social media comparison (algorithm manipulation, not reality)

**The Script:**
"I acknowledge these burdens exist. I'm not ignoring them. AND I'm setting down what I can't personally solve today so I can focus on what I actually can influence."

### The 3 Circles of Control
Draw three circles:
1. **Control** - Your thoughts, actions, boundaries
2. **Influence** - Your local community, friend groups, spending choices
3. **Concern** - Global issues, other people's opinions, the past

You get to care about circle 3. But you can't live there.

### Practical Decompression Tools

**Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)**
- Breathe in: 4 counts
- Hold: 4 counts  
- Breathe out: 4 counts
- Hold: 4 counts
- Repeat 4 times

**The Brain Dump**
Every night before bed:
- Open notes app
- Write every worry/task/thought for 5 minutes
- Don't organize, just dump
- Close the app
- Your brain can rest now

**The Permission Slip**
Write yourself permission:
"I have permission to not save the world today."
"I have permission to rest without earning it."
"I have permission to care less about things that don't align with my values."

### The Boundary Scripts

**For family:**
"I love you AND I need to protect my mental health. I'm going to [limit/change/pause] [behavior] for now."

**For friends:**
"I can't take that on right now, but I care about you."

**For yourself:**
"Not everything requires my opinion or emotional energy."

### When Decompression Isn't Working
If you've tried these tools and still feel overwhelmed:
- Trauma might be stored in your body (try somatic therapy, EMDR)
- Your nervous system might be dysregulated (look into polyvagal exercises)
- You might need medication (no shame in this - seriously)
