# Gen Z Self-Help Master Script
## File 4: Action - Small Moves That Compound

### Why Massive Action Plans Fail
You've seen the Instagram gurus: "Wake up at 5am! Cold showers! Workout! Journal! Meditate! Side hustle!"

That's not self-improvement. That's burnout speedrun.

### The Micro-Action Philosophy
**One choice, repeated, compounds into a different life.**

Not 10 choices. ONE.

### The 2-Minute Rule
If it takes less than 2 minutes and moves you toward your goal, do it now:
- Drink water
- Text that friend back
- Apply to one job
- Delete one toxic follow
- Do 10 pushups
- Write one sentence

### The Habit Stack
Instead of building a new routine from scratch, attach tiny habits to things you already do:

**Already do:** Wake up
**Stack:** Drink water (bottle by bed)

**Already do:** Scroll Instagram
**Stack:** First, write one thing you're grateful for

**Already do:** Brush teeth at night
**Stack:** Lay out tomorrow's clothes

### Action Scripts for Common Blocks

**"I'm too tired"**
"I don't need peak energy. I just need to start. 2 minutes only."

**"I don't have time"**
"I don't have an hour. But I have 5 minutes. That's enough for now."

**"I'll do it when I feel motivated"**
"Motivation follows action, not the other way around. I'm doing it anyway."

**"What if I fail?"**
"Failure means I tried. Not trying guarantees nothing changes."

### The Weekly Reset Ritual
Sunday evening (30 minutes):
1. Brain dump everything on your mind
2. Pick ONE priority for the week
3. Schedule 3 specific times you'll work on it
4. Delete what doesn't serve that priority
5. Tell one person your plan (accountability)

### Momentum Tracking
Don't track outcomes (lost 10 lbs, made $X).
Track actions (worked out 3x, sent 5 applications).

You control actions. Outcomes are probability.

### When Action Feels Impossible
If you genuinely can't take action:
- You might be in survival mode (address basic needs first)
- You might be experiencing executive dysfunction (ADHD?)
- You might need rest, not more productivity

**The Script:**
"Sometimes the most productive thing I can do is rest so I have capacity tomorrow."

### The Anti-Hustle Reminder
You don't need to monetize your hobbies.
You don't need to be productive every moment.
Rest is not laziness.
You are not your output.

But you DO get to build a life you actually want to live.
Small actions. Repeated. Compounding.
