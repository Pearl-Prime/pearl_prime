# V4.6 — Binge Optimization Layer

System-level upgrade: **compulsion engineering**. Ensures full books do not close with resolution; they end with forward momentum into the next book or session.

---

## Purpose

Without this layer, books can end as self-contained units. Listeners stop. With it, every full book ends with a **Forward Momentum Trigger (FMT)** so that:

- The next book (or next session) has a natural hook.
- Identity and mechanism stay open; resolution is not promised.
- "What happens next?" is structural, not accidental.

---

## Forward Momentum Trigger (FMT)

Every **full book** (formats that are not reset/capsule — i.e. standard_book and longer, and short/micro books with narrative arc) must end with **one of**:

| Type | Description | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Unresolved adjacent mechanism** | A related mechanism or pattern is named but not explored; next book can open on it. | "The alarm fires and you survive it. But there is something else. The thing at 2AM when the alarm is not firing and you replay the moment anyway. That is a different room." |
| **Identity tension** | Who the listener is in relation to the pattern is left open. | "You act anyway. What you call that — courage, exhaustion, or something else — is not yet decided." |
| **Social cliff edge** | A social consequence or relationship state is visible but not resolved. | "She is in the circle. Heart loud. Hands cold. What she says next will land or it will not. Here." |
| **Curiosity hook** | A question or possibility is stated (not asked with "?") so the next book can answer or deepen it. | "You want to know why a four-second moment ruins an entire day. You want to know why you cannot move on. Here is the answer, and it has nothing to do with willpower." (Next book opens on that answer.) |

---

## Application

- **Applies to:** All formats with narrative arc (deep_book, extended_book, standard_book, short_book, micro_book). Not required for capsule_5, capsule_10, reset_2, reset_90s (somatic-only).
- **Where:** Final integration (carry line or adjacent sentence). The FMT is the **last beat** before silence — the line the listener carries, and the hook the next book can pick up.
- **Constraint:** FMT must not promise resolution. It opens; it does not close.

---

## Example (Loop → Next Book)

**Loop (book A) ends with:**
"You act anyway."

**Next book (book B) begins with:**
"But what happens when you do — when you speak, when you show up, when the room goes quiet and everyone is waiting — that is the next room."

The first book lands on action. The second opens on consequence. Forward momentum is preserved; binge is possible.

---

## Enforcement

- **Plan compiler / assembly:** Final integration slot for applicable formats must satisfy one FMT type (unresolved mechanism, identity tension, social cliff, curiosity hook).
- **CI:** Flag books whose final integration is purely summative or resolving. FMT presence is a release gate for full-book formats.

---

## Relation to V4.5

- V4.5 Writer Spec §8 (Integrations): carry line, reframe, no resolution.
- V4.5 Production Readiness: deterministic assembly, emotional QA, drift protection.
- V4.6 adds: **every full book ends with a Forward Momentum Trigger** so the catalog supports continuation and binge listening without requiring repair or ad-hoc hooks.
