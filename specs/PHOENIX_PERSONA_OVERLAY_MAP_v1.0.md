Persona Overlay Map
Demographic Personas  →  APAC Archetypes  →  Motivation Types


Architecture Decision
Production system: 10 Demographic Personas (unified_personas.md) — drives content atoms, voice specs, directory structure.
Marketing overlay A: 10 APAC Psychographic Archetypes — drives title language, hashtags, and platform discovery strategy for zh-TW, zh-HK, zh-SG, zh-CN.
Marketing overlay B: 10 Motivation-Based Personas — drives subtitle framing, reader psychology, and US/global marketing copy.

The overlays do not change how atoms are written or filed. They change how books are packaged, titled, and marketed.

How to Use This Document
This map has three uses:
	•	Writers: understand who you're really writing for — what they search for, what language they use, what motivates them to buy.
	•	Marketing: map each demographic persona to the right APAC search terms and motivation-based subtitle framing.
	•	Catalog planning: identify topic gaps — topics that the APAC or motivation overlay surfaces that the Phoenix topic registry doesn't currently cover.

Gap Summary
Three topics surfaced in APAC/Motivation research that have no current Phoenix equivalent:
	•	容貌焦慮 / Appearance & Identity — major zh-CN topic (Aesthetic Rebel persona). Xiaohongshu #脱美役 has enormous reach. Candidate for APAC-specific topic slot.
	•	原生家庭 as standalone topic — currently bundled under grief + boundaries in Phoenix. APAC research + Motivation research both surface it independently. Consider splitting out for zh-CN catalog.
	•	孤獨儀式 / Loneliness & Ritual (一人食) — the Ritual Loner's primary search topic in zh-CN. Not the same as social_anxiety. Different mechanism: not fear of people, but meaning-making in chosen solitude.

Note: these gaps are APAC catalog additions only. The Phoenix English system does not need these topics unless the US market data supports them.
