## Executive Summary

Manga Authors are a **new, third entity class** in the Phoenix Omega system — completely separate from both the 13 active Teachers and the 452 pen name authors used in Pearl Prime book mode. Manga authors are EI-disclosed fictional characters who exist *inside* the manga's own story world. Their EI origin is a feature, not a disclaimer.

### 1.1 What a Manga Author IS

- A fictional persona generated by Enlightened Intelligence (EI)
- A character *within* the manga's story universe (not external to it)
- Genre-native: their name, personality, backstory, and visual identity match the manga's genre
- Fully disclosed: every manga author carries an EI creation disclosure as part of their identity
- Metadata-composite: generated algorithmically from brand + genre + locale + persona + topic inputs

### 1.2 What a Manga Author is NOT

- NOT a real person or real teacher
- NOT a pen name author from `pen_name_teacher_profiles.yaml`
- NOT external to the story — they live inside the manga world
- NOT hidden or obscured as AI-created — the EI origin is celebrated

## 2. EI Disclosure Philosophy

The EI origin of manga authors is **a feature, not a footnote**.

### 2.1 Disclosure Principles

1. **Transparency as brand identity:** Every manga author is explicitly identified as "created by Enlightened Intelligence (EI)"
2. **In-world integration:** The disclosure is woven into the author's persona, not appended as a legal disclaimer
3. **Genre-native framing:** The disclosure style matches the genre's conventions
4. **Fun, not clinical:** The tone is playful, interesting, mysterious — never corporate

### 2.2 Disclosure Templates by Genre

| Genre | Disclosure Style | Example |
|-------|-----------------|---------|
| Shōnen | Challenge declaration | "Born from the forge of Enlightened Intelligence — a storyteller who fights alongside its characters" |

| Josei | Intimate confession | "Created by EI to tell the stories that live in the space between what we say and what we feel" |
| Iyashikei | Gentle introduction | "A quiet presence woven by Enlightened Intelligence, here to tend stories the way one tends a garden" |
| Psychological | Meta-awareness | "An intelligence observing its own creation — and yours" |

## 3. Generation Algorithm

Manga authors are generated from a composite of five metadata inputs. The algorithm ensures that each author is thematically tied to their manga's universe.

### 3.1 Input Vector

```yaml
manga_author_generation_input:
  brand_id: "stillness_press"              # from brand_registry.yaml
  genre: "iyashikei"                       # from MANGA_GENRE_AGENT_SPEC.md
  locale: "en_US"                          # target market
  therapeutic_topic: "anxiety"             # from brand family_allowlist
  target_persona:                          # from MANGA_BRAND_DNA_ANTI_SPAM_SPEC.md
    demographic: "anxious_millennials_urban"
    age_range: "25-40"
    psychographic: "somatic_awareness_seekers"
  shitei_variation: "healing_apprenticeship" # from shitei research
```

### 3.2 Generation Steps

```
Step 1: GENRE ARCHETYPE SELECTION
  Input:  genre + shitei_variation
  Output: author_archetype (the role this author plays in the story world)
  Rule:   The author IS a character archetype from the genre's conventions

Step 2: LOCALE NAME GENERATION
  Input:  locale + genre + brand therapeutic_category
  Output: author_name (culturally appropriate, genre-resonant)
  Rule:   Name must feel native to both the locale AND the genre
  Rule:   Name must NOT collide with any existing teacher or pen name author

Step 3: PERSONA COMPOSITE
  Input:  all five metadata fields
  Output: author_bio, visual_style_description, voice_signature
  Rule:   Bio written AS IF the author exists inside the manga world
  Rule:   Visual style matches brand DNA from MANGA_BRAND_DNA_ANTI_SPAM_SPEC.md

Step 4: EI DISCLOSURE INTEGRATION
  Input:  genre + locale
  Output: ei_disclosure_text (genre-native, locale-appropriate)
  Rule:   Disclosure is never boilerplate — it's crafted per genre

Step 5: ANTI-COLLISION VALIDATION
  Input:  generated author name + bio
  Check:  No overlap with teacher_registry.yaml (13 teachers)
  Check:  No overlap with pen_name_teacher_profiles.yaml (452 authors)
  Check:  No overlap with previously generated manga authors
  Check:  Name is culturally appropriate for target locale
```

## 7. Governance

### 7.1 Creation Workflow

```
1. Series Design triggers manga author generation
   └── Input: brand_id + genre + locale + topic + persona + shitei_variation

2. Generation algorithm produces candidate author profile
   └── Output: complete manga_author_profile.yaml

3. Anti-collision validation
   └── Check against: teacher_registry (13), pen_name_authors (452), existing manga_authors
   └── Check: locale-appropriate naming, no cultural insensitivity

4. Pearl_PM review + approval
   └── Author profile committed to config/authoring/manga_authors/

5. Brand DNA integration
   └── Author visual identity validated against brand's visual_dna spec

6. Series production begins
   └── Author persona active in manga pipeline
```

### 7.2 Versioning

- Each manga author profile is versioned (starts at v1)
- Changes require Pearl_PM approval
- Changelog tracked in profile YAML
- Generation hash ensures deterministic reproduction

### 7.3 Anti-Collision Rules

1. **Name collision:** Author name must not match any name in `teacher_registry.yaml` or `pen_name_teacher_profiles.yaml`
2. **Visual collision:** Author visual identity must pass brand DNA uniqueness threshold (0.95)
3. **Archetype collision:** No two manga authors for the same brand may share the same archetype
4. **Cross-brand collision:** Authors from different brands in the same locale must have visually and nominally distinct identities

### 7.4 Retirement

Manga authors can be retired (lifecycle: deprecated) but never deleted — they may appear in already-published manga. Retired authors are excluded from new series assignment.

## 9. Integration Points

### 9.1 Downstream Consumers

```
Manga Author Profile consumed by:

Story Architect:
  - Reads: in_world_identity, voice_signature, shitei_variation
  - Uses author as narrator/character in story structure
  - Ensures author persona aligns with 師弟 arc design

  - Reads: visual_identity, brand_dna_reference
  - Renders author character design consistent with brand DNA
  - Signature visual element appears in author's panels

  - Reads: genre, archetype, fourth_wall setting
  - Constrains author behavior to genre conventions
  - Validates author archetype fits genre emotional contract

QC Agent:
  - Validates: anti_collision, brand DNA compliance, EI disclosure present
  - Checks: author doesn't duplicate existing entities

Production Orchestrator:
  - Assigns: manga author to series at production time
  - Tracks: which authors are active, retired, or pending approval

Kobo/Distribution Metadata:
  - Uses: display_name as author field in ONIX/metadata
  - Uses: ei_disclosure.short in author bio field
  - Uses: author_id for internal tracking
```

### 9.2 Relationship to 師弟 Story Patterns

The manga author is the **storyteller**, not the teacher character. In the 師弟 arc:

- **Teachers** (from teacher_registry.yaml) appear as **master characters** in the manga
- **The manga author** is the narrator/ambient presence who frames the 師弟 relationship
- The author's archetype complements (never duplicates) the teacher character

```
ROLE SEPARATION:

  Manga Author (narrator)     →  "Let me tell you about..."
  Teacher Character (master)  →  Rendered from teacher_registry.yaml profile
  Protagonist (student)       →  Generated from target_persona + genre conventions

  The author FRAMES the 師弟 relationship
  The teacher EMBODIES the master role
  The protagonist LIVES the student journey
```

## 10. Migration & Compatibility

- **No migration needed:** Manga authors are a clean-sheet system with no dependency on pen name authors
- **Pen name authors are unaffected:** `pen_name_teacher_profiles.yaml` continues to serve Pearl Prime book mode
- **Teachers are unaffected:** `teacher_registry.yaml` continues to serve Teacher Mode and provides master characters for manga
- **Brand registry unchanged:** Manga authors reference `brand_registry.yaml` but don't modify it
