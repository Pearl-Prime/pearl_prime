## Root causes

1. **Topic duplication (INV-1, INV-4)**: `run_article_pipeline.py` ingests all feed items and picks topics by classification. No per-run uniqueness guarantee. `run_daily_news_cycle.py` passes `--limit 1` but doesn't constrain topic.

2. **Teacher not in content (INV-5)**: `resolve_teacher()` picks one teacher and stores in `_teacher_resolved`, but no step injects the teacher name into the HTML content as a byline or attribution block.

3. **No layout/sidebar (INV-5, INV-6)**: Never implemented as Python-generated HTML. Article content is raw prose without structural HTML wrapper blocks.

4. **teacher_id not top-level (audit clarity)**: Stored as `teacher_used.teacher_id` in output JSON. Makes automated auditing harder.
