## What Is Music Mode?

Pearl Prime — Enlightened Intelligence — is an AI-native publishing system. Music mode is a first-class brand archetype inside it. It does three things at once.

**It creates books rooted in your sound.** Two book variants exist. The `with-lyrics` variant interleaves lyrical voice — yours, in your register, with your signature devices — at chapter opens, closes, and one mid-chapter beat. The `no-lyrics` variant uses reflection essays, journal entries, and meditations on your music at the same positions. Either way the book is a book, not a song. One AI companion song is packaged with each release for the brand-admin bundle.

**It reaches Gen Z and Gen Alpha readers across 12 markets** — US, Japan, Taiwan, China, and growing. The books land on KDP, Audible, Apple Books, and Google Play in your reader's language. Companion songs and audio lead magnets land on Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube Music, Bandcamp, SoundCloud, Tidal, and Amazon Music through the separate freebie-and-funnel program.

**It works deterministically, not by hallucination.** Your survey answers — your themes, your healing intent, your touchstones, your voice register, your signature devices — feed an atom-bank pipeline. Pearl_Editor and Pearl_Writer author atoms keyed to six new slot pools that frame every chapter. Your taste becomes permanent system memory. Every book that ships under your brand is recognizably yours without ever putting words in your mouth.

## Your Voice, Authored as Atoms

For each musician archetype, Pearl Prime builds a `musician_bank` — your themes, your voice register, your healing intent, your signature devices, your touchstone tracks. From that bank, books are written.

Six slot pools frame every chapter. Three drive the `with-lyrics` variant:

- **LYRIC_OPENING** — your lyrical voice at the top of each chapter
- **LYRIC_CLOSING** — your lyrical voice at the bottom
- **LYRIC_BESTSELLER_BEAT** — one mid-chapter beat carrying your lyric

Three drive the `no-lyrics` variant:

- **MUSIC_REFLECTION_OPENING** — a reader-facing reflection on the music
- **MUSIC_REFLECTION_CLOSING** — a closing reflection
- **MUSIC_REFLECTION_BESTSELLER_BEAT** — a mid-chapter reflective beat

Plus two top-level second-person framings — `INTRO_2P_TEMPLATE` and `CONCLUSION_2P_TEMPLATE` — that open and close each book in your voice addressing the reader directly.

The process runs in four steps. **First, the onboarding survey:** 8 sections covering identity, themes, voice-craft, touchstone tracks, healing intent, lyric preferences, reflection preferences, and consent. **Second, atom authoring:** Pearl_Editor and Pearl_Writer translate your survey into atoms keyed to the six slot pools — every chapter draws from your atom bank. **Third, book assembly:** atoms mix into chapters by deterministic rules; second-person prose addresses the reader; quality gates run before any book ships. **Fourth, the companion song:** one MusicGen prompt per book, packaged with each release. Auto-rendering of the companion song to WAV is coming with Pearl Star Phase B.

## Two Types of Music-Mode Books

**Variant 1 — With Lyrics.** Lyrical content interleaved at chapter opens, closes, and one mid-chapter beat. For musicians whose word-craft is part of the work. The system drops in lyric blocks that match the chapter's emotional arc. It honors your `lyric_form` preference — free verse, rhymed, or verse-chorus. It respects your `explicit_content_ok` gate. The felt rhythm of your voice carries through every chapter.

**Variant 2 — No Lyrics.** Reflection essays, journal entries, or meditations on your music. For musicians whose work is instrumental, or for catalog rows where lyrics aren't the right fit. Choose your reflection form — essay, journal, meditation, or mixed. Choose perspective — musician, listener, critic, or mixed. The system reaches readers who came for the feeling, not the words. The companion song carries the lyrical absence.

## Second Person. To the Reader. From the Music.

The book speaks to the reader in second person. It does not impersonate you. It carries the felt sense of your music into the reader's day.

Example prose:

> *"You hear the morning. You feel the room get small. The track plays once, then again. You stop holding your breath."*

Three reasons this framing matters.

**No misrepresentation.** The book never claims to be you speaking. You don't own every nuance of every line. A reader who carries your sound into their life is sharing what it meant to them — not claiming to represent you perfectly.

**Authentic transmission.** Your themes, devices, and register go into the atoms. The book is recognizably yours without ever putting words in your mouth. Your sound's shape carries; your literal voice doesn't get spoofed.

**Refines over time.** More survey input + more atoms + more touchstones = closer fit between your music and the prose around it. The system learns your sound across releases. The catalog gets more recognizably yours as the catalog grows.

## Audio Freebies: Five Ways to Build the Relationship

A separate program, `MUSIC-MODE-FREEBIE-FUNNEL-V1-02`, adds music-native lead magnets — audio assets that live outside the book catalog and serve as top-of-funnel hooks that draw listeners toward becoming readers. These are not the books. They are the on-ramp.

**M1 — Companion Track Download (`companion_track_download_v1`)**
Full-quality companion song download, gated behind email capture. The first direct connection between musician and reader outside of a streaming algorithm. A reader who downloads this track has actively chosen to stay in the relationship.

*Example: "Download the studio version of 'Morning Room' — the track this chapter was written about."*

**M2 — 30-Second Preview Clip (`preview_clip_30s_v1`)**
A hook-packed 30-second clip with a lyric snippet on-screen and the audio hook that defines the track. Short enough to finish in any context; memorable enough to replay. Bridges the listener to album pre-save or a full release.

*Example: "Hear the opening 30 seconds of 'Drift' — with the Chapter 3 lyric overlaid."*

**M3 — Sample EP Bundle (`sample_ep_bundle_v1`)**
3–5 tracks delivered as a single download (zip) or streaming links per platform policy. The complete sonic world of a book — all in one place, before the book exists in the reader's hands.

*Example: "Get the 'Quiet Hours' EP — 4 tracks that form the sonic spine of this book."*

**M4 — Lyric Poster PDF (`lyric_poster_pdf_v1`)**
A printable art poster pairing the lyric with visual design. Tied to M1/M2 in the email sequence. Lives on walls and in inboxes — a physical-feeling artifact of a digital relationship. Readers print it. It stays.

*Example: "Download the 'Dissolve' lyric poster — the track referenced on page 47."*

**M5 — Behind-the-Song Interview (`behind_the_song_interview_v1`)**
Audio or long-form text. The musician explains what a track means, why it was written, what broke, what held. Ties the artist persona to the music's emotional meaning. The reader stops hearing a track and starts understanding an artist.

*Example: "8-min audio: 'The Distance' — why it was written, what the bridge means, what the last line costs."*

## From Free Track to Loyal Reader: The Funnel

The five lead magnets work in sequence. Each stage deepens the relationship until buying the book is the natural next step.

**Stage 1 — Listen (Top of Funnel).** Track page: title, album art, 30-second listen embed. First contact between your sound and someone who might become a reader.

**Stage 2 — Free Download (Lead Magnet).** Companion Track (M1) or Sample EP (M3) — full quality, no catch. Email captured. They chose to hear more.

**Stage 3 — The Story (Email Sequence).** Preview Clip (M2) arrives with the "why this track exists" email. Lyric Poster (M4) follows. The music starts to mean something personal to the reader — not just something they stream.

**Stage 4 — Behind the Song (Mid-Sequence).** Behind-the-song interview (M5): audio or long-form text. The musician's voice, unfiltered. The reader understands the artist. This is where the parasocial relationship deepens into something real.

**Stage 5 — The Book (Conversion).** Book CTA arrives naturally in the sequence. Companion song is bundled with it. The book is already familiar territory — the reader knows the sound. They buy it. They read it. The relationship continues.

Books are the catalog. Audio freebies are the top of funnel. Listeners who discover your music through these lead magnets become the most loyal readers — because they already know the feeling before they open the first page.
