## How a single quarter works

Three movements per quarter. They are the same shape across all four quarters.

**Penance** is ninety days of impeccable karma yoga in your pillar. Not austerity. Not punishment. "Penance" is the old word for what happens when work is done with full presence over a season — the inner pattern gets reorganized by the consistency of the doing. Twelve Sundays attended. Twelve shipped deliverables. Real work done, week after week. The penance is the work.

**Empowerment** is the quarterly special Sunday. One of the world masters in the alliance teaches in their own tradition's voice for thirty to forty-five minutes. The Karma Yogi receives, without interrupting. The teaching is not abstract — it is the teaching for *this* level, in *this* master's lineage frame.

**Alignment** is the moment, near the end of the special Sunday, when the master speaks the volunteer cohort into the level. The operator's working language for what happens here: *"The teacher is aligning the vessels of the world dharma."* The volunteer becomes a vessel for one specific world-domain — karma clearing, then ecological harmony, then the rising generations, then the universal good.

Then the new attainment-state name is conferred. Names are spoken aloud. The act of naming is part of the alignment.

That is one quarter. There are four.

## Three alternative paths

The operator may want to ship Plan A. The operator may also want to ship something else, or several somethings in parallel. V1.5 surfaces three full alternatives, each at the same depth.

**Plan B** is the Mahāyāna Six Pāramitā compressed ladder. Six perfections of the Bodhisattva path, gathered into four quarters: Q1 Dāna + Śīla (Generosity + Ethics), Q2 Kṣānti + Vīrya (Patience + Effort), Q3 Dhyāna (Meditation), Q4 Prajñā (Wisdom). The final attainment is *Pāramitā-Bodhisattva*. Plan B's strength is its uncontroversial canon — every Buddhist sangha member across schools knows the Pāramitās. Its weakness is that it doesn't carry the operator's Vairocana-cosmology gravity.

**Plan C** is the Vajrayāna 4-Initiation inspired ladder. The four Vajrayāna empowerments — Vase, Secret, Wisdom-Knowledge, Word — purify Body, Speech, Mind, and their union. The final attainment is *Vajrasattva-Aligned World Bodhisattva*. Plan C carries the deepest esoteric architecture — and the heaviest caveat. Actual Vajrayāna *abhiṣeka* requires strict guru-disciple lineage. Plan C is INSPIRED BY the four-initiation structure, not a transmission of it. If Plan C is shipped, Joshin Sensei's consent as Shingon lineage holder is mandatory, and the "inspired by" framing must be carried in all volunteer-facing language without exception. Pearl_Editor coordinates a full lineage-holder review before publication.

**Plan D** is the Tao-Buddha-Hindu-Pearl Polestar ladder — one tradition per quarter, ending with Ahjan's Pearl Transmission as the synthesis. Q1 Taoist (Pure-Qi cultivation; Master Feung or Master Wu), Q2 Buddhist (Compassion field; Joshin Sensei), Q3 Hindu (Dharma Body alignment; senior Hindu lineage holder in the alliance — Sai Maa via Acharya Dayananda Das is the strongest candidate from USLF #3), Q4 Ahjan's Pearl Transmission. The year is a polestar walk through the four streams of the master alliance. Plan D has the strongest alliance visibility — every master from their own tradition, the year ends in Ahjan's own offering — but it requires the most careful cross-tradition deference (Hindu naming conventions; Buddhist + Vajrayāna naming conventions; Pearl_Editor coordinates multi-lineage review).

The comparison matrix in the spec (§7) lays them side by side. Pearl_Architect's recommendation summary: Plan A for narrative coherence and lowest gates. Plan D for maximum alliance visibility. Plan B for Buddhist-canonical purity. Plan C for esoteric depth, only with Joshin consent. The operator picks via Q-OP-PROGRESSION-SHIP-01.

The digest's closing tagline tells them: *"These are the world's gifts this month — and your year of service is one of the conditions that lets them flourish."* Not "you did this." Not "your alignment caused this." But: one of the conditions. A reverent framing. The volunteer reads the digest and identifies — they ARE the kind of person whose presence in the field is one of the conditions for the world's flourishing. Over months, this identity settles deep.

The operator's framing: *"They create an identity of being a world helper of the ecology of the world."*

The Volunteer Digest runs on the existing Pearl News article corpus, filtered by per-level tags, with the closing tagline appended. Monthly send. Quiet. Reverent. No marketing copy. Per-volunteer opt-in by default on active status. Stops at departure-conversation.

## The empowerment ceremony

The quarterly special Sunday. Ninety minutes total. The same Zoom platform as the weekly Sunday coordination — but a different shape.

Ten minutes of pre-ceremony silence. A senior Dharma Steward names the alliance present in spirit. The lineage of the quarter's transmitting master is acknowledged. Thirty to forty-five minutes of teaching from the world master in their own voice, in their own tradition's frame. Translation as needed. The Karma Yogis receive without interrupting. Fifteen to twenty minutes of the empowerment / alignment moment — the ritual heart. The master speaks the volunteer cohort into the level. In Plan A Q4, this is Ahjan's Pearl Transmission. Ten minutes of naming — each Karma Yogi receives their attainment-level title for the next ninety days. Names are spoken aloud. Five to ten minutes of closing dedication and sealing.

Recording is yes with teacher consent per ceremony — so Karma Yogis who couldn't attend live can receive later.

The ceremony is not theater. The ceremony is the event. The Karma Yogi who emerges has been aligned. The next ninety days they walk in the new attainment-state's field.
