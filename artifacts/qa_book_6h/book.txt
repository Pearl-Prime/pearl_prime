There is a mechanism that activates more strongly at night: the mechanism that reviews the day, that looks ahead to the next day, that dwells on conversations and moments and things you said that were awkward. this mechanism is not more active at night because you are worse at managing your thoughts at night. The nervous system is also preparing to sleep, which involves a shift in neurochemistry. It is a sign that the conditions at night—the quiet, the lack of distraction, the shift in nervous system state—are conditions in which the anxiety mechanism activates more readily. The mechanism itself is very normal.

Completeness and incompleteness produce different stories from the same data.
