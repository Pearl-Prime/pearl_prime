Chapter 9

The achievement arrived. The promotion, the milestone, the thing you worked for. And yet here you are, already looking at the next horizon. The restlessness did not end when you reached the goal. It simply found a new destination.

The all-hands mentioned metrics and you exceed them. Nobody mentioned it to your face. Your open office desk achievement went unwitnessed. the commuter rail brings people who will be recognized. Your manager moved on to other business. You note this.

Notice a thought that repeats itself in your mind. Follow it back. Who told you
this was true? How long have you carried it? If you set it down right now, what
would be different? Not forever. Just for today. What changes when the story stops?

you use busy-ness, distraction, or sleep to not feel something? Not to change it,

The brightness has not moved. You have simply stopped covering it temporarily. The contraction you feel is real. It lives in your body. The distinction is becoming visible between the gesture of self-protection and the openness that was never absent. Feel your feet on the ground. Feel your spine. What was hidden becomes obvious when you stop the internal resistance. The body is the messenger. Listen to what it's telling you about the separation you've maintained. Your heart knows the truth. Your bones carry the knowledge of what was always real underneath the contracted gesture.
