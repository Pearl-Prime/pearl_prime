Before you keep reading, balance your breath. In and out, same length. The symmetry tells your system that nothing requires urgency right now.

This is equal breathing, also called Sama Vritti. By making the inhale and exhale exactly the same length, you remove the urgency signal from breathing. Your system stops scanning for which direction needs more emphasis and settles into balance.

Breathe in for four counts. Breathe out for four counts. No holds. Even in, even out. The balance is the point. Repeat for eight cycles. Let the breath be so even that you cannot tell where inhale ends and exhale begins.

Now, I want you to notice a settling that happened without a decision. You did not choose to calm down. You gave your system a balanced input and it adjusted accordingly. Regulation through rhythm, not willpower.

Now, as you return, equal breathing is the simplest practice to use anywhere. No one can tell you are doing it. Three breaths of four-four before a reply, before a meeting, before you check your phone.
