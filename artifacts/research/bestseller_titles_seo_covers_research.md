#### Title Formulas That Work in US Market

**FORMULA 1: "The [Concept Name]" Pattern**
- The Let Them Theory, The Mountain Is You, The Four Agreements, The Body Keeps the Score
- Works because: Creates a branded, shareable concept. TikTok/BookTok-friendly.
- Success rate: Dominates top 10 consistently

**FORMULA 2: "How to [Action] When/Without [Obstacle]" Pattern**
- How to Break Free from Overthinking
- How to Stop Doubting Your Greatness
- Works because: Directly addresses the reader's problem-state

**FORMULA 3: "[Number] [Timeframe/Things] to [Outcome]" Pattern**
- 7 Habits of Highly Effective People
- The Four Agreements
- 12 Rules for Life
- Works because: Promises finite, achievable structure

**FORMULA 4: "[Provocative/Emotional Hook]: [Practical Promise]" Pattern**
- Unfuck Yourself: Get Out of Your Head and Into Your Life
- You Are a Badass: How to Stop Doubting Your Greatness
- Works because: Thumb-stopping title + clear value proposition in subtitle

**FORMULA 5: "[Verb] Your [Inner State]" Command Pattern**
- Quiet Your Mind and Get to Sleep
- Master Your Emotions
- Unfu*k Your Brain
- Works because: Gives reader agency, implies immediate actionability

#### Subtitle Patterns That Convert

| Pattern | Example | When to Use |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **Benefit-first** | "A Practical Guide to Personal Freedom" | When the main title is abstract/branded |
| **Audience-first** | "A Millennial and Gen Z Guide to..." | When targeting a specific demographic |
| **Method-first** | "An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits" | When the method IS the selling point |
| **Transformation** | "Transforming Self-Sabotage Into Self-Mastery" | When targeting readers who know their problem |
| **Social proof** | "...That Millions Can't Stop Talking About" | When you have momentum/virality |

#### Title Length Sweet Spots (US Market)

- **Main title:** 2-6 words optimal (The Let Them Theory = 4 words; Atomic Habits = 2 words)
- **Main title character count:** 15-40 characters
- **Subtitle:** 6-15 words optimal
- **Total (title + subtitle):** Under 120 characters for best display across all platforms
- **Amazon hard limit:** 200 characters combined (title + subtitle)
- **Thumbnail readability:** Main title must be readable at 120px wide thumbnail

#### Words That SELL in Self-Help Titles

**High-converting words (human-authored bestsellers):**
- Purpose, Journey, Life, Love, Power, Freedom
- Heal, Transform, Rise, Break Free, Overcome
- Nervous System, Somatic, Body, Brain
- Courage, Worth, Enough, Worthy

**Words That Signal "AI-Generated" (AVOID):**
Based on Originality.ai study of 844 Amazon self-help titles (2025):
- Blueprint, Strategies, Master, Code, Build
- "Step into" (used by 67 AI books vs 1 human)
- "Practical guide" (overused by AI in summaries)
- "Build a better life" (AI-formulaic)
- "Personal growth" (too generic, AI-associated)
- Mindset (now so overused it signals low quality)
- Emojis in descriptions (87 AI books used them vs 5 humans)

**The 77% Problem:** 77% of books in Amazon's "Success" self-help subcategory are likely AI-generated. Human authors differentiate with emotional, aspirational, story-driven language rather than instructional/formulaic phrasing.

**KEY INSIGHT:** Our titles must sound HUMAN. Use emotional words, metaphors, and specific audience language. Avoid generic self-help jargon that now signals AI-generated content.

#### Japanese Title Conventions

1. **Title structure:** Main title is often 4-8 characters (kanji-dense). Subtitle (帯文 obi-style) can be much longer and descriptive
2. **Kanji vs Hiragana:** Mix is preferred. Pure kanji titles feel academic; pure hiragana feels juvenile. The sweet spot is kanji concepts with hiragana particles
3. **English loanwords:** Increasingly acceptable for concept names (e.g., マインドフルネス for "mindfulness"). Signal modernity
4. **The 超 (Super) prefix:** Very popular for self-help. 超睡眠力, 超集中力, etc.
5. **力 (Power/Ability) suffix:** Converts well. 雑談力, 伝え力, 聞く力
6. **Subtitle conventions:** Long explanatory subtitles are normal. Full sentences on the obi (book band) are standard
7. **Question titles:** Popular for addressing reader concerns directly (なぜ〜なのか)

### 2.1 Google Play Books

#### Required Metadata Fields

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Title | Yes | Must match content exactly |
| Subtitle | Optional | Strong SEO value; keywords here are heavily indexed |
| Description | Yes | No officially published character limit; recommend 500-2000 characters. First 2-3 lines visible in preview |
| Categories/Genres | Yes | Based on BISAC system; max 3 recommended but no hard limit |
| Language | Yes | Must match book content |
| ISBN | Recommended | Not strictly required for self-published |
| Cover Image | Yes | Min 640x1005px, recommended 2560x1600px |
| Publication Date | Yes | |
| Page Count | Yes (ebooks) | |

#### SEO Best Practices for Google Play

1. **Title + subtitle are the #1 search factor.** Google Play's algorithm gives particular importance to titles, subtitles, and descriptions
2. **Description should be SEO-rich** but readable. Use natural keyword placement, not stuffing
3. **Genre selection:** Genres in Play Store are based on BISAC. Use the most specific subcategory available
4. **No keyword stuffing.** Google penalizes obvious keyword manipulation
5. **Algorithm ranking for new books:** Factors include relevance to search query, user engagement (clicks, reads, ratings), price competitiveness, and metadata quality

### 2.2 Amazon KDP

#### 7 Backend Keywords — What Works (2025-2026)

**Structure:** 7 fields, each up to 50 characters. Amazon indexes each word across ALL 7 fields (no need to repeat words).

**Formula per field:** [Topic] + [Audience Modifier] + [Outcome/Format]

**Example keyword fields for an anxiety book targeting professionals:**

| Field | Keywords |
|-------|----------|
| 1 | anxiety relief for working professionals corporate |
| 2 | nervous system regulation burnout recovery healing |
| 3 | somatic exercises calm overwhelm workplace stress |
| 4 | women healthcare workers emotional resilience book |
| 5 | mindfulness techniques overthinking stop worrying |
| 6 | therapist recommended evidence based cbt workbook |
| 7 | self help mental health wellness audiobook 2026 |

**What NOT to do:**
- Never use competitor author names or book titles (trademark violation, books get removed)
- Don't repeat words across fields (wastes indexing opportunity)
- Don't use quotation marks, commas, or special characters
- Avoid single words that are too broad ("anxiety" alone is too competitive)

#### Category Selection (2025-2026)

**IMPORTANT CHANGE:** Amazon replaced BISAC codes with its own Amazon store categories in mid-2023. You now select directly from Amazon's browse categories.

- **3 categories per format** (ebook, paperback, hardcover) in KDP dashboard
- The 3-category limit is now firm (no more emailing support for extras)

**High-converting self-help subcategories (lower competition):**

| Category Path | Competition Level |
|--------------|-------------------|
| Self-Help > Anxieties & Phobias | Medium |
| Self-Help > Stress Management | Medium |
| Self-Help > Women's Personal Growth | Medium-Low |
| Self-Help > Neuro-Linguistic Programming | Low |
| Health, Fitness & Dieting > Mental Health > Anxiety | Medium |
| Self-Help > Emotions | Medium-Low |
| Self-Help > Death, Grief, Bereavement | Low |
| Medical Books > Psychology > Occupational | Low |

**Category influence weight (2025):**

| Factor | Influence Weight |
|--------|-----------------|
| BISAC Codes / Category Selection | 80% |
| Backend Keywords | 65% |
| Subtitle Language | 55% |
| Book Description | 50% |

#### Description Optimization

**Character limit:** 4,000 characters (including HTML tags)
**Critical zone:** First 300 characters (visible before "Read more" click)

**Supported HTML tags:**
- `<b>` for bold
- `<p>` for paragraphs
- `<br>` for line breaks
- `<h4>`, `<h5>`, `<h6>` for headings (H1-H3 NOT supported)
- `<ul>`, `<ol>`, `<li>` for lists

**Optimal description structure:**
1. **Hook** (first 300 chars): Emotional hook addressing the reader's pain point
2. **Problem validation:** Show you understand their struggle
3. **Solution promise:** What this book delivers
4. **Bullet list of benefits:** 4-6 specific outcomes using `<ul><li>` tags
5. **Social proof** (if available): Endorsements, credentials
6. **Call to action:** "Scroll up and click Buy Now" or similar

**Conversion impact:** A compelling description can increase conversion rate by 30-50%.

### 2.5 Spotify Audiobooks

#### Metadata Requirements
- Title and subtitle: Include main topic, benefit, or target emotion
- Keywords: Mix broad and long-tail (e.g., "nonfiction audiobook" + "motivational audiobook 2025")
- Description: Used for content embedding via Sentence-BERT
- Genre/Category: Required for recommendation algorithm

#### Discovery Algorithm
Spotify uses a sophisticated recommendation system combining:
1. **Content embeddings** from title + description (via Sentence-BERT)
2. **Heterogeneous Graph Neural Network (HGNN) embeddings** for user-item relationships
3. **Cross-domain data:** Podcast/music listening patterns influence audiobook recommendations
4. **Human curation:** Editorial playlists and recommendations supplement algorithmic discovery

#### New Features (2025-2026)
- Audiobook Charts (US and UK) for discovery
- AI-generated listener interaction (questions and reflections)
- Cross-domain recommendations based on overall audio preferences

**KEY INSIGHT for metadata:** Spotify's algorithm reads your title + description through AI embeddings. Write descriptions that capture the FEELING and AUDIENCE of your book, not just keywords.

### 3.2 Design Research — What Covers SELL in Self-Help

#### Color Trends 2025-2026

**Primary colors by topic:**

| Topic/Theme | Primary Color | Why It Works |
|------------|---------------|-------------|
| Anxiety / Mental Health / Calm | Blue (deep navy to sky blue) | Most universally trusted color; signals calm, stability |
| Personal Growth / Renewal | Green (sage, emerald) | Signals growth, health, natural balance |
| Energy / Motivation / Burnout Recovery | Orange / Warm Yellow | Conveys optimism, energy without aggression |
| Self-Worth / Empowerment | Purple / Violet | Signals depth, spirituality, premium |
| Financial Wellness | Green + Gold accents | Traditional prosperity signals |
| Somatic / Body Work | Warm earth tones (terracotta, sand) | Grounded, embodied, natural |
| Sleep / Rest | Deep blue + soft lavender | Signals nighttime, calm, rest |

**Color distribution formula:**
- 60-70% primary color
- 20-30% neutral (white, black, gray)
- 10% accent color

#### Typography Trends

1. **Title dominance:** Title should occupy 40-50% of visual space
2. **Two-font max:** Display font for title + clean complementary for subtitle/author
3. **Serif comeback:** Modern serifs signal premium, thoughtful content
4. **Handwritten/script accents:** Used sparingly for warmth (but can signal "indie" if overdone)
5. **Bold sans-serif:** Still dominant for action-oriented self-help
6. **Type-only covers:** Many bestselling self-help covers are entirely typographic (no imagery). Just title on colored background

#### What Actually Increases Click-Through

- **Minimalist covers outsell busy designs by 63% in self-help**
- **72% of self-help readers say cover influenced their purchase**
- **Dual-scale design is mandatory:** Must work at thumbnail (120px) AND full size
- **High contrast:** Title must be instantly readable at thumbnail size
- **White space:** Signal of quality and clarity
- Covers with a single strong focal point outperform multi-element designs

#### Faces vs. Abstract

- **Abstract/typographic:** Better for anxiety, burnout, sleep (reader doesn't want to see someone's face)
- **Silhouette/partial face:** Can work for identity/empowerment titles
- **Author photo on cover:** Only if author is a known brand (Mel Robbins, Brene Brown)
- **Illustration:** Works well for gentle/accessible topics; popular in Japan/Korea

### 3.4 Per-Format Cover Needs

#### Ebook Cover (Portrait)
- Standard portrait orientation (1:1.6 ratio)
- Title must be readable at thumbnail
- No spine needed
- Design for digital screens (RGB, vivid colors OK)

#### Audiobook Cover (Square)
- 1:1 ratio (completely different from ebook)
- Often needs redesign, not just cropping
- "Listen" / headphone / audio indicators sometimes added
- Must work at small sizes in podcast apps and Spotify
- Avoid placing critical elements in lower-right corner (Audible badge area)

#### Manga Volume Cover
- Japanese standard: B6 (128mm x 182mm) or Tankobon size
- Volume number prominently displayed
- Character illustration is standard
- Series logo/brand mark consistent across volumes
- Spine design critical (shelf browsing in bookstores)
- Dust jacket + obi band for physical editions

#### Pocket Guide Cover
- Simpler design, badge-style or icon-based
- Often uses series color-coding
- Smaller text acceptable (guides are usually known by series, not individual title)
- Format indicator on cover (e.g., "Pocket Guide" or "Quick Reference")

#### Series Cover System (7-14 Books)

**Design elements that must remain consistent across series:**
1. **Typography:** Same fonts, same placement for title/subtitle/author
2. **Layout template:** Same compositional structure
3. **Color system:** Either same base color with topic-variant accents, OR unique color per volume within a coherent palette
4. **Series branding:** Series name + book number always in same position
5. **Logo/mark:** Consistent brand mark
6. **Spine design:** Series name + volume number on every spine; consistent spine color system

**Volume numbering conventions:**
- Front cover: Top bar or bottom bar with series name + book number
- Spine: Book number at bottom of spine
- Number format: "#1" or "Book One" or "Volume 1" depending on market

**Recommended approach for 7-14 book series:**
- Design a master template with variable zones (color, topic icon/image, volume number)
- Lock the following: font family, font sizes, margins, series logo position, author name position
- Variable: background color/gradient, subtitle text, volume number, optional topic icon

## Appendix B: Quick Reference — Cover Spec Universal Template

```
EBOOK COVER:
  Dimensions: 2560 x 3840 px (1:1.5 ratio)
  Resolution: 72 DPI (digital) / 300 DPI (print)
  Color: RGB (digital) / CMYK (print)
  Format: JPEG (high quality, <10MB)
  Typography: Title readable at 120px thumbnail width

AUDIOBOOK COVER:
  Dimensions: 3200 x 3200 px (1:1 ratio)
  Resolution: 72 DPI
  Color: RGB
  Format: JPEG (<5MB)
  Safe zone: Avoid lower-right corner (Audible badge area)

PRINT COVER:
  Resolution: 300 DPI minimum
  Color: CMYK
  Bleed: 0.125" all sides
  Barcode area: 2" x 1.2" white/light rectangle, lower-right back cover
  Format: PDF (preferred) or TIFF

MANGA VOLUME:
  Physical size: B6 (128 x 182mm) standard tankobon
  Cover: Full-color illustration
  Spine: Series name + volume number
  Dust jacket + obi band for premium editions
```
