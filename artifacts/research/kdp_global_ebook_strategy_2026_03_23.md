## Executive Decision

**Strategy: Create KDP-exclusive ebooks that are NOT published on any other platform.**

This means:
- Audiobooks go to Google Play, Audible, Spotify, Kobo, Ximalaya, etc. (multi-platform)
- Ebooks go ONLY to Amazon KDP (exclusive via KDP Select)
- No cross-platform content overlap = no duplicate content risk
- Different content formats per channel = maximum platform optimization

## 2. KDP Select — Why It's The Right Choice for This Strategy

### What KDP Select Gives Us:
1. **Kindle Unlimited (KU)** — 4.8M subscribers paying $11.99/month
   - KU borrows often out-earn sales 3:1 in non-fiction
   - Page read rate: $0.0041–$0.0048 per page (KENPC)
   - Wellness/self-help readers browse KU heavily
2. **70% royalty in ALL markets** (including Japan, India, Brazil, Mexico)
3. **Kindle Countdown Deals** — time-limited discounts with royalty protection
4. **Free Book Promotions** — 5 free days per 90-day enrollment period
5. **Amazon Ads perform 40% better** inside KDP Select (lower CPC)

### What KDP Select Costs:
- Cannot sell ebook digitally on ANY other platform for 90 days
- **But we don't want to** — our audiobooks are on other platforms, ebooks are Amazon-only

### The Strategic Advantage:
Since we're making KDP-exclusive content BY DESIGN, the exclusivity requirement costs us nothing. We get all the KDP Select benefits (KU, better ads, 70% global royalty) with zero downside.

## 4. AI Content Disclosure Requirements

### What Amazon Requires (2025-2026):
- **AI-generated content**: Must be disclosed during upload (text, images, translations)
- **AI-assisted content**: No disclosure needed (brainstorming, grammar, editing)
- Disclosure is for Amazon's internal records — NOT shown to buyers
- Non-disclosure can result in book removal, account suspension, royalty withholding

### Key Distinction:
- If you wrote content yourself and used AI for editing/brainstorming → "AI-assisted" → no disclosure
- If AI created the actual content (even with substantial edits) → "AI-generated" → must disclose

### Our Approach:
- Disclose honestly as AI-generated where applicable
- Amazon allows AI-generated content — they just require disclosure
- Quality and originality standards still apply regardless of creation method

## 7. Manuscript Technical Requirements

### Supported Upload Formats:
- **EPUB** (recommended — best control over formatting)
- DOCX (Word — acceptable but less precise)
- KPF (Kindle Create output)
- PDF (not recommended — rigid formatting)
- HTML (advanced users)
- MOBI no longer accepted (deprecated 2023)

### Cover Image Requirements:
- Minimum: 625 × 1000 px
- **Recommended: 2560 × 1600 px** (1:1.6 ratio)
- Maximum file size: 50 MB
- Format: JPEG or TIFF
- **Different from audiobook covers** (audiobook = 1080×1920 / 9:16)

### Manuscript Best Practices:
- Use heading styles consistently (H1 for chapters, H2 for sections)
- Embed fonts if using non-standard typefaces
- Test on Kindle Previewer before publishing
- Include active table of contents
- For workbooks: use text-based layouts (avoid heavy image-based pages that don't reflow)

- Each book = 12-18 months editorial process
- Full editorial passes create natural uniqueness
- Published across all channels (not exclusive)

### What We Do Differently:
- KDP-exclusive ebooks = separate product line from audiobooks
- Faster production via AI-assisted creation (with honest disclosure)
- Workbook/interactive format = genuinely different from audio content

### Key Insight:
Traditional publishers succeed because each author writes unique material. Our system achieves similar uniqueness through teacher doctrine separation, per-teacher atom pools, and different content formats (audio vs. interactive ebook). The KDP-exclusive strategy removes the cross-platform parity problem entirely.

## 10. Risk Mitigation

### Duplicate Content Risk: ELIMINATED
- Ebooks are different products from audiobooks (workbook vs. narration)
- No cross-platform prose overlap
- Each teacher's ebook draws from different content pools

### AI Detection Risk: MANAGED
- Honest AI disclosure at upload
- Amazon allows AI-generated content with disclosure
- Quality and originality standards still apply
- Prose-level dedup between titles within KDP catalog (KDP Readiness Gate spec exists)

### Account Risk: LOW
- Stagger releases (no more than 1-2 titles per week)
- Each title genuinely different (different teacher, different topic, different exercises)
- Quality over quantity — aim for 100+ page workbooks, not thin content

### Platform Dependency Risk: ACCEPTABLE
- Ebooks are KDP-exclusive by design
- Audiobooks diversified across 5+ platforms
- If KDP account ever had issues, audiobook revenue stream is completely independent
