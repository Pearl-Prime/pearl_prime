# RCG-001 — Self-publishing market revenue / scale (HIGH)

**Source claim (Batch 1 prompt):** Revenue estimates for the self-publishing market.  
**Tooling:** Web search + public pages (2026-03-31). Industry sizing varies by definition (ISBN-registered titles vs. all indie output, geography).

## Claim

The self-publishing sector is a large and growing share of new title output, with **multi‑million annual ISBN-registered self-published titles** and **trade-report market sizing in the low single-digit billions USD** (exact headline number depends on the research firm and year).

## Sources

1. **Publishers Weekly — Bowker / ProQuest data (Oct 2024):** Reports **2.6 million self-published ISBN titles in 2023** (+7.2% YoY) vs. **563,019 traditionally published** titles; positions self-publishing as the dominant ISBN channel for new titles. https://www.publishersweekly.com/pw/by-topic/industry-news/publisher-news/article/96468-self-publishing-s-output-and-infuence-continue-to-grow.html (retrieved 2026-03-31)  
2. **Jane Friedman — Bowker “Self-Publishing in the United States” summary (Oct 2024):** Corroborates the **2.6M self-published ISBN registrations in 2023** framing used in trade press. https://janefriedman.com/bowker-reports-increase-in-isbns-issued-for-self-published-titles/ (retrieved 2026-03-31)  
3. **Industry analyst market reports (2024–2025):** Multiple firms publish **global “audiobooks / self-publishing services” market size and CAGR** forecasts with **widely divergent baselines** (methodology not harmonized). Treat any single **“$X billion TAM”** figure as **vendor-specific** unless tied to a named report table.
