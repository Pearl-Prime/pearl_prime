# RCG-013 — AI narration ~90% cheaper than human studio

**Source claim (Batch 2 prompt):** “AI narration costs 90% less than human studio” (MEDIUM).

## Claim

Synthetic / AI audiobook production is **an order of magnitude cheaper** than professional human narration + studio.

## Sources

1. **Narration Box (vendor blog, 2025):** example total-cost bands **~$3k–8k+ human** vs **~$40–250 AI** for an ~80k-word title — implies **~90–99%** savings depending on assumptions. https://narrationbox.com/blog/ai-voice-vs-human-narrator-cost-80k-words  
2. **TomeVox (vendor blog, 2026):** states **~95–98%** cost reduction vs traditional production in promotional framing. https://www.tomevox.com/blog-ai-vs-human-narrator  
3. **ACX / industry pricing norms (human PFH):** human rates commonly cited **$200–500 PFH** in producer guides — use as baseline when modeling comparisons (see ACX documentation / producer FAQs on https://www.acx.com/ ).  

## Verdict

**PARTIALLY CONFIRMED** — **Order-of-magnitude savings** are consistently claimed across vendor comparisons [1][2]. **“Exactly 90%”** is **not** a magic universal constant—depends on **word count, royalty share, revision policy, and quality tier**. Prefer ranges and disclose vendor bias.
