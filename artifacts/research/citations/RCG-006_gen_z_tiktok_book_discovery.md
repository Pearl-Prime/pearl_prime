# RCG-006 — Gen Z discovers books via TikTok (~40% framing)

**Source claim (Batch 2 prompt):** “40% of Gen-Z discovers books on TikTok” (MEDIUM).  
**Tooling:** Web search + public pages (2026-03-31). Not audit §A row 6 (different claim there).

## Claim

A substantial share of Gen Z book discovery/purchase is driven by TikTok / BookTok (exact “40% discovers” wording varies by survey question).

## Sources

1. **Book Riot — TikTok survey (May 2023, US/Canada, n=10,000 TikTok users):** 62% of US respondents said they had read a book based on a TikTok recommendation; 48% of US users reported reading more because of BookTok. https://bookriot.com/booktok-survey/  
2. **Nielsen Book UK — industry view of TikTok bookselling impact:** https://nielsenbook.co.uk/the-bookselling-power-of-tiktok/  
3. **Wiley *Literature Compass* narrative review (2024) — BookTok scale:** notes tens of millions of #BookTok posts and hundreds of billions of aggregate views in 2024-era reporting (methodology varies by platform analytics). https://compass.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/lic3.70012
