# RCG-017 — Substack top 10 writers earn > $1M/year

**Source claim (Batch 2 prompt):** “Substack top 10 writers earn > $1M/year” (MEDIUM).

## Claim

The **highest-earning handful** of Substack writers exceed **$1M/year** in subscription-driven revenue.

## Sources

1. **The Verge (June 2025):** Substack CEO states **more than 50 people** earn **>$1M/year** on the platform. https://www.theverge.com/creators/679036/substack-ceo-50-people-1-million-dollars  
2. **Nieman Lab — estimation pieces on top Substacks:** discusses **revenue estimation methods** for leading newsletters (methodologically noisy). https://www.niemanlab.org/reading/heres-a-good-estimate-of-the-highest-earning-substacks/  
3. **Third-party ranking posts (e.g. Kyu Hwang Cho Substack):** list **top earners** with **estimated** annualized revenue — **not audited financials**. https://kyuhwangcho.substack.com/p/top-earners-on-substack  

## Verdict

**CONFIRMED (directionally)** — Credible reporting that **many** writers exceed **$1M/year** [1], which **implies** the **top 10** do as well **if** rankings are accurate. The **exact “top 10”** ordering and **>$1M** for **each** is **not** from audited statements—use **“platform reports 50+ creators >$1M/yr”** for precision.
