# RCG-015 — #BookTok 842M views in 2024

**Source claim (Batch 2 prompt):** “BookTok drove 842M views on #BookTok in 2024” (MEDIUM).

## Claim

#BookTok hashtag view-count in **2024** was on the order of **842 million**.

## Sources

1. **Wikipedia — BookTok (accessed via search indexing):** states **#BookTok** surpassed **~309 billion** cumulative views by **October 2024** with **~52M posts** — **orders of magnitude above 842M** if “views” are lifetime cumulative. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/BookTok  
2. **Wiley *Literature Compass* (2024):** narrative review cites **>200 billion views** by mid-2024 for tagged posts globally — again **not 842M**. https://compass.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1111/lic3.70012  
3. **WriteStats / trade articles:** discuss **59M print unit** sales impacts — different metric (units, not hashtag views). https://writestats.com/booktok-for-authors-how-tiktok-is-driving-59-million-book-sales/  

## Verdict

**UNCONFIRMED / LIKELY INCONSISTENT** — Public secondary sources cite **hundreds of billions** of aggregate hashtag views in 2024-era reporting [1][2], which **contradicts** an **842 million** figure unless **842M** refers to a **narrow geography, time window, or non-view metric** not recovered here. **Do not use 842M** without the original primary release.
