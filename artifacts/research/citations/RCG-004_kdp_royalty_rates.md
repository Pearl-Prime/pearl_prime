# RCG-004 — “Self-pub authors earn ~70% on KDP vs 10–15% traditional” (HIGH)

**Source claim (Batch 1 prompt):** Self-published authors earn **~70% royalty on KDP** vs **10–15% traditional**.  
**Tooling:** Official KDP help + trade/author-guild contract primers (2026-03-31).

## Claim

**Amazon KDP** publishes **70% ebook royalties** for eligible price bands and territories (with conditions), and **35%** outside those bands. **Traditional trade royalties** are commonly described as **lower net percentages on list/retail** (often **roughly ~10–25%** depending on format, territory, and contract terms), so the **directional comparison** (“self-serve platform can pay out a higher list-based digital royalty than many legacy print contracts”) is **often true**, but **not universally** at exactly **70 vs 10–15** for every author.

## Sources

1. **Amazon Kindle Direct Publishing — eBook royalties (official help):** Documents **70% and 35% royalty options**, price thresholds, delivery costs, and **KDP Select / territory** conditions. https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/help/topic/G200735480 (retrieved 2026-03-31)  
2. **Amazon KDP — “Earn with KDP” overview:** High-level **royalty rate** marketing copy pointing to the same **70% / 35%** structure. https://kdp.amazon.com/en_US/earn (retrieved 2026-03-31)  
3. **Authors Guild — publishing contract / royalty explainers:** Describes typical **traditional royalty mechanics** as **fractions of list or net** and emphasizes **variation by format and negotiation** (use for **framing**, not a single fixed “10–15%” without your contract). https://www.authorsguild.org/industry-advocacy/ (contract resources index; retrieved 2026-03-31) — deep link example topic: “Understanding Royalties” style articles under industry advocacy.

## Verdict

**PARTIALLY CONFIRMED** — **KDP 70% (conditional)** is **CONFIRMED** from **Amazon’s own documentation** [1][2]. **Traditional 10–15%** is a **common shorthand** for **print/digital list royalties in many deals**, but **real contracts vary widely** [3]. Recommend copy: **“Up to 70% digital list royalty on KDP within stated bands vs. typical trade royalties often materially lower — see contract.”**
