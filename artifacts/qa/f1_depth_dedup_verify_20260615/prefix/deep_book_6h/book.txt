facts of your life. Not the size of your problem. The situation. The

This is the most ordinary teaching he gives, and also the one most people
underestimate. They think they need a thousand moments of noticing,

then you need to be willing to let one mean something."

Here is how he describes it. The body is tight. The mind is racing. The
pattern is running its familiar program. You are about to react the way
you always react. And then, somewhere in the middle of it, you notice.
You see the tightness in the chest. You see the racing thought. You see
the pattern getting ready to fire. The noticing takes about half a second.

awareness, even for half a second, is the seed of every freedom that
comes later.
