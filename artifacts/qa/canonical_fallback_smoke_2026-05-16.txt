INTERPRETATION
========================================================================
Pre-patch:   1 canonical body filled all 36 slots verbatim. After
             intra-chapter dedupe, ~16 verbatim repeats survived (the
             Tanya/Sarah leak documented in book_quality_report.json:54-56).
Post-patch:  36 slots receive 23 distinct bodies drawn from the same
             CANONICAL.txt's individual ## ARC vNN sections. The worst-case
             repeat drops from 16/36 (44%) to 4/36 (11%).

Why no Tanya marker in the post-patch run:
  sorted(topic_dir.iterdir()) hits the `comparison/` engine subdir before
  `spiral/`. With the per-(chapter,section,seed) selection, only the
  earliest engine subdir's atoms are reached in this smoke shape. The
  Tanya block lives in `spiral/CANONICAL.txt` (alphabetically later); under
  the patch, the resolver now also tags its output with the specific arc
  block so future telemetry can identify cross-engine duplication if it
  arises. The architectural fix — never paste the whole file blob — holds.
