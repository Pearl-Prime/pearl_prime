# Blind review instructions (give to judges)

You are evaluating **one vertical manga episode** (Candidate) against professional published work.

## What you will see

1. **Candidate** — a full episode (all panels in read order)
2. **Comparator A** — excerpt from a published series (same genre)
3. **Comparator B** — excerpt from a different published series (same genre)

You will **score only the Candidate** on the attached rubric. Comparators help you calibrate scores 4 and 5.

## Rules

- Do not discuss with other judges until submissions are complete.
- Do not research the candidate's origin during scoring.
- Score each of 8 dimensions from 1 (amateur) to 5 (top reference tier).
- Use the full scale; 3 = competent serial, 4 = professional publishable.

## Dimensions (summary)

1. Panel-flow readability
2. Page-turn / scroll rhythm
3. Character consistency
4. Lettering craft
5. Genre-trope fluency
6. Anatomy / hands
7. Tone / atmosphere
8. Dialogue register

Full anchors are in the rubric attachment.

## After scoring

Return the completed scorecard to the operator. Optional free-text: strongest dimension, weakest dimension, would you publish (yes/no/with revision).
