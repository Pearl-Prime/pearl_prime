## 2 · Body Memory · *The Body Speaks* (josei · Yui Tanaka)

### Page 1 — Cold open · Kyoto morning
**SHOTS:** Wide of a wooden machiya house in Higashiyama, dawn. A small brass shingle: *Tanaka Somatic Practice*. Inside, YUI (32) lays out futons in a treatment room, methodical. Her cat watches from a windowsill.
**NARRATION:** Yui is good with bodies that are not hers.

### Page 2 — The first session
**SHOTS:** A male client face-down on the futon. Yui's hands working a knot beneath his shoulder blade. Her face — skilled, distant. A clock — 9:47.
**CLIENT:** *(muffled)* You always know exactly where it is.
**YUI:** That part is easy.

### Page 3 — Lunch alone
**SHOTS:** Yui at a small café, soup untouched. Her phone buzzes — a new client referral. The name on the screen: NAOKO ITO, age 17.
**INTERNAL:** Naoko. The name lands somewhere I have been keeping locked.

### Page 4 — The client arrives
**SHOTS:** A teenage girl at the genkan, taking off her shoes. NAOKO (17) — small, dark-haired, the exact shape of someone Yui used to know. Yui's hand on the door frame — white-knuckled.
**NAOKO:** Tanaka-sensei? I'm sorry I'm early.
**YUI:** *(too long a pause)* Please come in.

### Page 5 — The session that doesn't work
**SHOTS:** Naoko on the futon. Yui's hands trembling slightly. A muscle in Yui's lower back beginning, for the first time in years, to throb in a way that is not muscle.
**INTERNAL:** This is not Naoko's pain. This is mine.

### Page 6 — After the client leaves
**SHOTS:** Yui sitting alone in the empty treatment room. Her hand against her own lower back. The pain is now speaking in full sentences.
**NARRATION:** It said: *I have been waiting twenty years for you to ask.*

### Page 7 — The phone call
**SHOTS:** Yui calling her mother. The mother in a kitchen in Osaka, taking the call. Both faces — careful.
**MOTHER:** Yui-chan. It's been three months.
**YUI:** I need to ask you something about Mariko.
**(silence — full panel)**

### Page 8 — The teacher's message
**SHOTS:** Yui's phone showing a message from her own teacher, OMOTE SENSEI. *I am in Kyoto next week. Come.* Yui staring at it. The pain in her back agreeing.

### Page 9 — End beat
**SHOTS:** Wide of the machiya at evening. The cat. Yui standing in the doorway of the treatment room. Light through the rice-paper screen.
**NARRATION:** The body had been holding the grief safely for twenty years. It would hold it a little longer — but it had stopped pretending the grief was not there.
**END CHAPTER 1.**

## 3 · Bright Presence (TW) · *The Gap* (seinen · Wei Lin)

### Page 1 — Press conference
**SHOTS:** A government auditorium in Taipei. WEI LIN (38), in the press pen — pen ready, recorder running. The minister at the podium. Wei has done this a thousand times.

### Page 2 — The lie
**SHOTS:** The minister speaking — confident, polished. Wei watching. A small, grey, not-quite-physical shape leaving the minister's mouth and dissolving into the air. Wei's pen, frozen mid-stroke.
**INTERNAL:** What.

### Page 3 — Confirmation
**SHOTS:** Other journalists scribbling. None of them seeing what Wei is seeing. The grey shapes, multiplying.
**INTERNAL:** I am not well.

### Page 4 — After
**SHOTS:** Wei on the MRT going home. Reflecting in the window. He is forty-eight in his eyes and eighteen in his exhaustion.
**INTERNAL:** Stress. Burnout. Optic migraine. A specific name will calm me.

### Page 5 — The street vendor
**SHOTS:** Wei walking past a tea stall in Wanhua. An OLD MAN — calligraphy face, sandals — looks up.
**OLD MAN:** You saw the gap today.
**WEI:** *(stopping)* What did you say?

### Page 6 — Tea
**SHOTS:** Wei sitting on the small stool the old man indicates. Tea poured. The old man pouring his own. Silence.
**OLD MAN:** It does not get easier from here. But it gets clearer.

### Page 7 — Home
**SHOTS:** Wei in his apartment. His daughter LIN-LIN (16) at the kitchen table doing homework. She does not look up.
**LIN-LIN:** You're home early.
**WEI:** Yeah.
**LIN-LIN:** You okay?
**WEI:** *(beat)* Lin-Lin — when was the last time I asked you a real question?

### Page 8 — The daughter looks up
**SHOTS:** Lin-Lin lifting her head. Surprised. Studying her father.
**LIN-LIN:** *(quiet)* Three years ago, I think.
**WEI:** *(slowly)* That is a long time to not be asked.
**LIN-LIN:** Yeah, Dad. It is.

### Page 9 — End beat
**SHOTS:** Wei at his desk, the half-written investigative piece in front of him. He closes the laptop. Picks up a pen. Writes one line in his notebook: *what is in the gap?*
**END CHAPTER 1.**

## 4 · Cognitive Clarity · *The Pause* (shonen · Kenji Mori)

### Page 1 — Tournament floor
**SHOTS:** A neon arena in Akihabara. KENJI (16) at his rig, headphones, focused. The crowd a blur. His APM counter spinning into the 280s. He is winning.

### Page 2 — The new rule
**SHOTS:** Tournament screen flashes: *NEW MATCH RULE — 5-SECOND MANDATORY PAUSE BETWEEN ROUNDS.* Kenji's coach, off-stage, paling. Kenji's hands, paused, going stiff.
**INTERNAL:** What do you do in five seconds. What do you DO.

### Page 3 — Loss
**SHOTS:** The opponent's match-point animation. The crowd's intake of breath. Kenji — first loss in two years.

### Page 4 — The opponent
**SHOTS:** Across the stage — SORA, 16, temple-cut hair, sitting calmly. He looks across at Kenji and — bows. Not in mockery. In respect.

### Page 5 — Backstage
**SHOTS:** Kenji in the locker room, controller still in hand, knuckles white. His mother, DAIRYUIN-NI, in plain robes, waiting.
**MOTHER:** I watched.
**KENJI:** You watch with the sound off.
**MOTHER:** I watch the silences. You do not have any.

### Page 6 — Sora arrives
**SHOTS:** Sora appears at the doorway. Polite. Holds out a card — temple address.
**SORA:** Train with us. One week. After that decide if you want the rematch.
**KENJI:** Why are you offering this.
**SORA:** Because the next tournament has the same rule.

### Page 7 — The temple
**SHOTS:** Mountain mist. A wooden gate. Kenji arriving with his gaming bag. Sora — already practicing zazen on a low platform.

### Page 8 — First sit
**SHOTS:** Kenji forced to sit. His knees screaming. His mind at 280 APM with nowhere to go.
**INTERNAL:** This is worse than losing.

### Page 9 — The question
**SHOTS:** Sora opening one eye after thirty minutes.
**SORA:** Where do you go when there's nothing to do?
**KENJI:** I don't know.
**SORA:** Yes. That's the problem.

### Page 10 — End beat
**SHOTS:** Kenji on the mat, alone after sora has left. Eyes closed. The mind beginning, almost against its will, to slow.
**NARRATION:** The pause was not the enemy. The pause was the technique.
**END CHAPTER 1.**

## 5 · Devotion Path · *The Kitchen* (shonen · Arjun Iyer)

### Page 1 — Mumbai, August
**SHOTS:** A community kitchen in Bandra-East. ARJUN (17), in donated kitchen whites, chopping onions badly. Steam everywhere. Other volunteers — older, faster.

### Page 2 — The supervisor
**SHOTS:** SUPERVISOR DIDI, 50, watching Arjun without expression. She picks up his pile of onions. Starts again, with him.
**DIDI:** The hand has to know it is not in a hurry.

### Page 3 — The family arrives
**SHOTS:** A displaced mother, ZARA, with two boys — eight and four. The four-year-old, RIZ, looking up at Arjun with full attention.
**ZARA:** Bhaiya, do you have rice?
**ARJUN:** Yes. *(plating, careful)* Yes, of course.

### Page 4 — The reach
**SHOTS:** Riz finishing his small plate. Looking up. Reaching one hand toward Arjun's hand on the counter. Arjun — frozen.
**INTERNAL:** I do not know what to do with this.

### Page 5 — Didi watches
**SHOTS:** Didi has seen. Says nothing. Hands Arjun a wet cloth. Arjun wipes the counter. The hand is still extended. Arjun does not take it.

### Page 6 — Night
**SHOTS:** Arjun in the volunteer dorm. Phone open to his IIT acceptance email. Cursor in the *defer extended* field.
**INTERNAL:** Grandmother said: *go to the kitchen for a year.* She did not say what would happen there.

### Page 7 — The teacher
**SHOTS:** Morning. A small visiting teacher, SAI MAA, in white. The kitchen volunteers gathering. Arjun watching from the back.
**SAI MAA:** *(addressing all)* Do not perform devotion. Be available to be opened.

### Page 8 — One look
**SHOTS:** Sai Maa's eyes scanning the room. Pausing on Arjun. A half-second. She moves on. Arjun feels something he cannot name.

### Page 9 — Riz again
**SHOTS:** Lunch service. Riz in line again. Arjun serving rice. The hand reaches up. This time, Arjun lets his own hand be taken.

### Page 10 — End beat
**SHOTS:** Arjun in the back of the kitchen, after service. Crying. Didi handing him the wet cloth without comment.
**NARRATION:** Devotion was not a series of correct gestures. Devotion was being available to be reached for.
**END CHAPTER 1.**

**SHOTS:** A small inn. Tea. Ra patient.

# Format notes (apply to all 12)

- **Reader-promise check:** each chapter closes on the brand profile's promise (e.g. body_memory's *"the body has been keeping the grief safe until you were ready"* surfaces explicitly on Page 9 of #2, etc.).
- **Teacher anchor:** named teacher appears in chapter 1 in-frame for every brand, dialogue limited to 1–3 short paragraphs per scene, no tradition word used without an in-narrative translation.
