## Reader promise

> You will find your way back to stillness even when the alarm is still ringing.

## Chapter logline

An ordinary morning. One notification. The whole nervous system goes off — and Mira learns, over a single cup of tea, that the alarm in her chest is not lying about danger. It is lying about *when*.
