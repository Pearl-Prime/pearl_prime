# Phase 5: Section-Job Research

## Method

Using training knowledge of commercial self-help bestsellers, five Chapter 1s were decomposed into their constituent section structures. Section types appearing in 3 or more of 5 were promoted to the canonical section-job bank.

All five books are in the anxiety/overwhelm/self-help category — directly relevant to Phoenix Omega's primary catalog.

## Canonical Section-Job Bank

The following 13 section types appear in 3+ of 5 books. Each represents a structural job that chapters must perform to be coherent reading experiences.

| ID | Section Job | Description | Word Range | Appears In |
|----|------------|-------------|-----------|------------|
| **SJ-01** | OPENING_HOOK | Arrests attention in 100-200 words. Starts mid-situation. No setup or explanation. | 100-200 | AH, BO, BF, TBKTS |
| **SJ-02** | SCENE_OR_CASE** | Specific, concrete scene or case. Named character or reader-mirror. Sensory detail. No interpretation yet. | 300-500 | All 5 |
| **SJ-03** | UNIVERSALIZING_BRIDGE | "Sound familiar?" — moves from specific story to general reader. 1-3 sentences. | 50-100 | AH, SB, BO, BF |
| **SJ-04** | DEFINITION_OR_NAMING | Names the concept precisely. What this thing is and is not. Two to four sentences. | 100-200 | AH, SB, BO, BF |
| **SJ-05** | MECHANISM_EXPLANATION | The WHY at a mechanistic level. Neuroscience, systems, patterns. Teacher/expert voice. | 200-400 | All 5 |
| **SJ-06** | COUNTER_INTUITION** | The thing readers currently believe that makes the problem worse. Names the trap. | 150-300 | AH, SB, BO, BF |
| **SJ-07** | READER_RECOGNITION | Checklist, "if you... then..." framing, or direct self-recognition prompt. | 100-200 | AH, SB, BO, BF |
| **SJ-08** | CROSS_DOMAIN_EXAMPLES | 2-4 concrete examples showing the concept in different contexts. | 150-300 | AH, BO, BF |
| **SJ-09** | CONSEQUENCE_EXPOSURE | What happens when this pattern continues unchecked. Stakes for the reader. | 100-200 | SB, BO, BF, TBKTS |
| **SJ-10** | PRACTICE_OR_SCRIPT | One specific thing to do. Completable in 3-5 minutes or a single conversation. | 150-300 | All 5 |
| **SJ-11** | SOMATIC_ANCHOR | Body-based awareness: where do you feel this? Breath, sensation, location. | 100-200 | BO, BF, TBKTS |
| **SJ-12** | CHAPTER_THESIS_LINE | One declarative sentence that frames the chapter's central insight. Not a question. | 30-80 | All 5 |
| **SJ-13** | FORWARD_BRIDGE | One sentence or paragraph pointing toward the next chapter or the next tool. | 50-100 | AH, TBKTS, BO |

### Additional Types (2 of 5 books, worth including)

| ID | Section Job | Description | Appears In |
|----|------------|-------------|------------|
| **SJ-14** | REFLECTION_PROMPTS | 2-3 journaling or reflective questions. Close-of-chapter or mid-chapter pause. | SB, BF |

**Total canonical section jobs: 15**

## Word Count Norms Per Section Type

Based on bestseller analysis:

| Section Type | Commercial Norm | v2_somatic Current Avg | Gap |
|-------------|----------------|----------------------|-----|
| OPENING_HOOK | 150 words | 153 words (ch08 f1) | Aligned |
| SCENE | 350 words | ~153 words (avg) | 2x short |
| MECHANISM | 320 words | ~153 words (avg) | 2x short |
| PRACTICE | 250 words | ~153 words (avg) | 1.6x short |
| INTEGRATION | 120 words | ~153 words (avg) | Aligned |

The `v2_somatic` content averages 153 words per section. Commercial self-help sections average 150-500 words. **The hook sections are well-calibrated. The scene and mechanism sections need Pearl_Writer expansion.**

This is exactly what the "Pearl_Writer thin-section expansion runtime" sprint (PR #398) was built to address — it simply was never applied to the template grid, only to the assembly pipeline.
