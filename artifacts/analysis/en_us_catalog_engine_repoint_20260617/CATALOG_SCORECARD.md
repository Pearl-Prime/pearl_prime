## Method (engine-driven)

Titles/subtitles are generated by the **canonical naming engine** (`phoenix_v4/naming`,
`angle_id = engine`), not hand-written. The generator requests the engine's 12 distinct candidates
per cell and selects the first well-formed, brand-unique one (salting the series_id the engine sees
to vary candidate-selection when a pool is exhausted — pure engine output, the canonical series_plan
filename is unchanged). Persona/topic copy (bisac, price, comps, character, avatar, voice markers,
keywords.primary) is preserved by cloning the per-(persona, topic) source plan; engine-mechanism
spans in cover_tagline / short_blurb / long_description are reframed from the new engine.
