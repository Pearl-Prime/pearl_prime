# Genre Vessel Sketches — teacher-mode XOR music-mode, per genre

Companion to `MANGA_MODE_WRAPPER_DESIGN.md`. For each genre: the **teacher-mode** vessel
(doctrine *earned*, reader is *taught*) and the **music-mode** vessel (sound *felt*, reader is
*anchored, never instructed*). Same wellness interior, opposite contract, never both in one series.
Full worked examples: dark_fantasy=teacher (`the_forest_beneath_the_skin`), mecha=music
(`the_cockpit_remembers_the_song`). The rest are sketched here.

---

### iyashikei  ·  *interior: sleep / somatic*
- **Teacher** — *the tea-house hands.* An old keeper who never gives advice; she only makes tea
  a certain way — warming the cup before the leaves, waiting for the steam to stop climbing. The
  cadet who can't sleep learns body-first by watching hands, not hearing words. The doctrine is the ritual.
- **Music** — *the wind-chime that keeps the season.* One small melody threads the daily world —
  a shop chime, a neighbor's humming. It returns each chapter, the same notes, until the night it
  finally sounds *different* — because the listener has changed. Nothing is said; the chime just lands softer.

### supernatural_mystery  ·  *interior: grief*
- **Teacher** — *the medium who reads the cold.* An elder who "sees ghosts" is really teaching the
  apprentice to feel the body's signal first — the cold spot arrives a breath before the apparition.
  Body-knows-first, dressed as second sight. The case is solved by feeling, not deducing.
- **Music** — *the song only the haunted can hear.* The dead don't speak; they leave a half-finished
  melody. Laying a spirit to rest = finishing its song. The grief-arc (held → released) is carried
  entirely by the phantom phrase recurring, completing, and at last going quiet.

### psychological_thriller  ·  *interior: anxiety / hypervigilance*
- **Teacher** — *the detective who reads bodies.* A mentor closes cases by the suspect's jaw, the
  witness's held breath — "the body confesses before the mouth does." The protégé learns their own
  panic is data, not malfunction. Doctrine as detection method.
- **Music** — *the motif that arrives one beat early.* A two-note figure plays just before every
  reveal — the reader's chest tightens before the clue lands. The dread is the body's alarm, scored.
  Resolution comes when the motif finally completes instead of cutting off.

### romance_josei_drama  ·  *interior: self_worth / social_anxiety*
- **Teacher** — *the beloved who rests.* No one lectures the guarded lead to soften; an older,
  unhurried partner simply *models* it — how she sets things down, how she lets a silence be. The
  doctrine is learned off another body's ease.
- **Music** — *the shared song.* One tune passes between two people; who hums it, and when, charts
  the whole romance. The walls-down feeling is never narrated — it's the night they finally sing the
  bridge *together*, unforced.

### workplace_drama  ·  *interior: burnout*
- **Teacher** — *the custodian who's seen the cycles.* A night-shift veteran teaches body-first in
  small acts — when to put the pen down, when the shoulders have already answered. The doctrine is
  earned across the grind, never in a meeting.
- **Music** — *the after-hours frequency.* The office has two tempos: the forced sprint and a slower
  beat someone hums by the copier. The burnout-arc is scored by which tempo the protagonist is moving
  to — and the chapter it finally drops to the sustainable one.

### sci_fi_cyberpunk  ·  *interior: overthinking / somatic*
- **Teacher** — *the old hacker / the wetware elder.* Teaches that the meat knows what the augments
  override; the body's alarm is truer than the optimized feed. Body-first as resistance to the system's
  edit.
- **Music** — *the pirate frequency — the soul in the hum.* A filtered-out signal the city scrubbed;
  re-finding it is re-finding the body the upgrades muted. The settling is felt as the static parting
  to let one warm analog tone through.

### psychological_horror  ·  *interior: trauma_recovery / shame*
- **Teacher** — *the survivor who turns toward it.* An elder teaches that the horror is the body's
  truth refusing to be silenced — the only way out is *through*, met not fled. Doctrine earned in the dark.
- **Music** — *the corrupted lullaby.* A tune the horror has warped; the protagonist must re-find it
  *clean*. Terror resolves to groundedness as the melody is, note by note, restored to the version
  that was safe.

### action_battle  ·  *interior: courage / burnout*
- **Teacher** — *the sensei of the settled strike.* The hardest blow comes from a quiet body, not a
  braced one. The student loses every bout they force and wins the one they flow — the doctrine, earned in defeat.
- **Music** — *the war-drum heart.* The fighter's own pulse is the score: ragged when forcing,
  steady when settled. The reader hears the body find its rhythm and *feels* the turn before the strike lands.

### historical_period  ·  *interior: self_worth / grief*
- **Teacher** — *the master of the craft.* A swordsmith / calligrapher whose discipline *is* the
  doctrine — patience with the body, the hand that knows before the plan. Earned in apprenticeship.
- **Music** — *the folk song down the eras.* One melody survives generations; the feeling of
  belonging and continuity is carried by the song outliving everyone who sang it — and reaching the lead at the end.

### isekai  ·  *interior: trauma_recovery / courage*
- **Teacher** — *the guide who trusts the old instincts.* A mentor in the new world teaches that the
  body's old-world alarms are the truest map; the body adapts faster than the mind can map. Doctrine for a stranger in a strange land.
- **Music** — *the tune that crosses worlds.* The hummed song from "home" is somehow *magic* here —
  the only thing that translates. Belonging is felt as the borrowed melody turning, slowly, into one
  that fits the new world.

### sports_competition  ·  *interior: imposter_syndrome / self_worth*
- **Teacher** — *the coach of the unclenched.* Teaches that the choke is bracing and the flow is
  release; drills the body until it stops auditioning. Earned through the loss that came from gripping too hard.
- **Music** — *the inner beat / the team chant.* The runner's stride has a tempo; under pressure it
  scatters. The race turns the instant the beat re-finds itself — scored, not explained.

### school_coming_of_age  ·  *interior: social_anxiety / self_worth*
- **Teacher** — *the senpai who pays quiet attention.* No speeches — just being *seen* accurately by
  someone older models the doctrine. The teaching is in the noticing.
- **Music** — *the song the year is set to.* One club tune, picked up in spring, mastered by winter;
  growing up is felt as the song maturing in the characters' hands.

### cultivation_martial  ·  *interior: somatic / courage*
- **Teacher** — *the master of the lower gate.* Cultivation *is* body-first — the dantian knows
  before the mind; force ruptures the meridians, settling opens them. Doctrine as the cultivation path itself.
- **Music** — *the sound-sect's flute.* A school that cultivates through tone; qi moves on the
  melody. Mastery is felt as the breath and the note becoming one line — no lesson, just the sound finding the body.

---

## Using the sketches
Each is one series' **spine**, not a scene. Pick the genre, pick the mode, instantiate the vessel,
run the 3-beat skeleton (`MANGA_MODE_WRAPPER_DESIGN.md`). The vessel is the only thing carrying the
mode — swap it and you've changed modes, never stacked them.
