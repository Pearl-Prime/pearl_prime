In the morning Nadia opens the banking app before her coffee is ready. The number is there. She knows the number. She closes the app. At 2:15 in the afternoon she opens it again between two meetings. The number is the same number. She knows this is what numbers do. She closes it.

At 8pm she opens the app and sets a savings goal of $400 by the end of May. She will not hit $400 by the end of May. She knows this while she is typing it. She taps save anyway. The app shows her a small progress bar at zero. It looks like something is happening. At midnight she opens the app again. She does not change the goal. She looks at the number. She closes it.
