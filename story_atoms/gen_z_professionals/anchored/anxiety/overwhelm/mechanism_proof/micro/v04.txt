The #wins channel posts a shoutout for the product launch. Four names.
Chris reads them twice. He was on the launch. He prepared three of the
five assets the post mentions. He is not one of the four names. He reacts
to the post with a fire emoji because the moment between seeing his absence
and responding is short enough that the response is already the safer
move. His manager likes his reaction. He watches the notification. He goes
back to his desk and opens a task he has been avoiding for two days and
works on it for ninety minutes without stopping, which is the longest
stretch of focus he has had all week.
