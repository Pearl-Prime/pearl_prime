Maya opens LinkedIn to find the name of a conference speaker she half-remembers. She finds the name in eleven seconds. She does not close the tab. She clicks through to the speaker's connections and then to a profile she does not recognize — Priya Nair, 26, Senior Product Designer at a studio Maya has an alert set for. She reads the bio twice. She closes the tab. She opens her own profile. She reads her title. Associate UX Designer. She reads it a second time as though the words might shift. They do not. She closes her own profile.

This is Tuesday. On Wednesday she opens LinkedIn to check a comment notification and is on Priya's profile within four minutes without deciding to go there. On Thursday she does not even have a reason. She opens LinkedIn. She finds the profile. She closes both tabs in the same motion and picks up her phone.
