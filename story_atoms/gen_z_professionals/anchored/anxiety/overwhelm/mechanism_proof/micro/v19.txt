Suki's preparation runs in a fixed order: green tea first, then the slides once through on her laptop, then ten minutes of silence with no phone, then the hallway. She added the hallway after the third talk because the tea and the slides and the silence were not enough on their own. They were not enough in the sense that she was still pacing — she just moved the pacing to before the hallway instead.

She knows the ritual does not make the before go away. She knows this because the before is still there every time, right up until she goes on. She does the ritual anyway because she cannot find out what would happen if she didn't. She would need to skip it and then give the talk and then know. She has never been willing to run that test. Without the data, the ritual stays.
