Aisha has four plans on her calendar this weekend — brunch at eleven, a gallery thing at two, a birthday dinner Saturday night, a hike Sunday morning. She made all of them in the same conference room on Thursday, when leaving felt like a statement.

It is Saturday at 9am. She opens a text to Maya. Types "Hey I'm so sorry but I don't think I can make—" and puts the phone face-down on the bed.

She picks it up again. Opens the text to Deja. Types "This week has been—" and stops. Deletes it.

Her shoulders are up near her ears. She does not notice this. She opens the text to Maya again. The draft is still there. She reads it. She closes the app.

All four plans are still on the calendar. The three texts are still unfinished. The brunch is in two hours.
