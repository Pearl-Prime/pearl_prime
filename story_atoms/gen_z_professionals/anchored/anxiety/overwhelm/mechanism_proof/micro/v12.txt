Tariq sits in the parking garage after Tuesday standup. He does not go back upstairs right away. He stays in the car with his hands in his lap and the engine off and he waits for something he cannot name to pass.

It started as a Friday thing. Now it is also Tuesdays. Last week, after the grocery run, he drove around the block twice before going inside. His roommate was on the couch and looked up and asked if he was okay. Tariq said yeah, just needed some air. His roommate nodded and looked back at his phone.

The grocery run had been sixteen minutes of nobody needing anything from him. He does not have a word for that yet. He only knows he did not want it to end.
