Daniel knows his manager's reply cadence. Under four minutes is the baseline. Under four minutes at 10am on a Tuesday means nothing. Over twelve minutes at 10am on a Tuesday, when the manager is online and has been in no meetings — that goes into the pattern.

He does not have a spreadsheet. He doesn't need one. The pattern is in his head in the same way a route home is in his head: he couldn't draw it but he would know immediately if something changed.

Something changed eight days ago. He has known for eight days. He has not decided what it means yet, which is a different thing from not noticing.
