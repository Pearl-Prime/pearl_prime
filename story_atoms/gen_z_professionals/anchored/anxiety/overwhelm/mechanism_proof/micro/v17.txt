His manager said "you're a natural" in the Q3 review. Leo nodded. He had done four hundred and twelve cold calls that quarter. Before each one, without exception: heart rate up, hands slightly cooler than normal. During the call: fine. After: the same release, the same drop back to baseline. Before the next one: the same rise.

He did not mention this to his manager. He has not mentioned it to anyone. He does not know if there is a word for it. He does not know if other people have a before, or if other people just have the call.
