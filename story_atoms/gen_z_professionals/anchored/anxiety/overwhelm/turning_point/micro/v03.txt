Aisha cancels on Saturday. She does this by text at 10pm on Friday,
which she knows is late enough to be an apology text and early enough
to not be a crisis. The friend responds with "no worries, rest up" and
a heart. Aisha puts the phone down. She had been planning to cancel
since Wednesday. She is not sick. She is not busy in a way she can
describe. She is tired in the way that makes other people's energy feel
like a thing she would have to perform for, and she does not have enough
left to perform. She will feel guilty about this tomorrow. Tonight she
turns the lights down and does not watch anything.
