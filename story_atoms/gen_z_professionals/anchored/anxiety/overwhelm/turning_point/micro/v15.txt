The fifth talk runs long in the green room briefing and Suki does not get to the hallway. She goes straight to the stage. She gives the talk. Same delivery, same timing, same body-before that she has not named but knows by now as the thing that lives in the twenty minutes before she goes on.

She stands at the back of the room while the next speaker gets introduced. The hallway was not what was managing the before. The hallway was managing her belief that the before needed managing. These are different. She knows this now because she has the data: one trial, skipped ritual, same outcome. The sample size is small. She will probably pace next time because the body does not update as fast as the conclusion. But she will know, while she is pacing, what the pacing is actually for.
