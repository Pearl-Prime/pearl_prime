HR sends a calendar invite Friday afternoon. Subject: "Company Update — All Hands." No body text.

Daniel spends the weekend in it. By Saturday evening he has two scenarios. By Sunday morning he has three, ranked. The first involves his entire division. The second involves contractors only. The third involves a reorg that leaves his role technically intact but reports to someone new.

The townhall is about a new benefits provider. Dental network change effective June 1.

He sits with that for a moment. His coffee is still warm. He picks up his phone and opens the calendar to check what else is scheduled in the next three weeks. There is a skip-level on the fourteenth he had not thought about until now.

He doesn't have a name for what he is doing. He knows it takes something out of him. He scrolls to the fifteenth anyway.
