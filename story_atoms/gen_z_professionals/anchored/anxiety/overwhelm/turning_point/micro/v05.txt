Zoë has been putting off the appointment for four months. Not because she
is afraid of what the doctor will say. Because the appointment requires
the portal, and the portal requires her insurance card, and the insurance
card is linked to her job, and her job is currently a thing she is trying
not to think too carefully about. She does not know if she is staying.
She does not know if she will be asked to stay. She knows that if the
answer to either question is no, there is a window between when that
happens and when she has coverage again, and the appointment costs money
she has calculated and does not want to calculate again. She closes the
portal tab.
