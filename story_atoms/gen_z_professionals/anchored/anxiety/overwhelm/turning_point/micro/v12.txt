Another calendar invite with no subject line. Sam reads it. He opens a new message to his manager and types: "Hey — quick question about Thursday's invite, what's it for?" He reads it over once. He sends it.

The reply comes in eleven minutes. "new vendor contract to sign, nothing exciting lol"

He reads this. He puts his phone face-down on the desk. He picks it up again. He thinks about what he did with the eleven minutes. He had opened the performance review rubric. He had read three paragraphs of the company's progressive discipline policy. He had drafted two versions of a response to a conversation that had not happened.

The invite was about a vendor contract. He knew that now. He sat with what he had built in eleven minutes anyway.
