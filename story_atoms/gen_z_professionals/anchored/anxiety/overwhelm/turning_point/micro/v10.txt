Diane asks the question at the end of their coffee, after the roadmap talk and the feedback on Maya's portfolio and the part where Maya explains where she wants to be in two years. Diane sets her cup down. She says: but what do you actually want — not what you're building toward, what do you want.

Maya opens her mouth. She has an answer ready. The answer is about the senior title and the studio she has bookmarked and the kind of work that would look correct on the profile she checks every Tuesday. She starts to say it. The words are there. But Diane is looking at her and not filling the space and the answer Maya has ready is not the answer to the question Diane asked.

Maya looks at the table. She says she has to think about it. Diane nods. They leave. Maya walks to her car with something open in her chest that she does not know how to close.
