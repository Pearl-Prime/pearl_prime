Chris's mother asks how work is going. He says it is going well. She
asks if he is eating. He says yes. She asks if he is sleeping. He says
mostly. She pauses in the way she pauses when she is deciding whether to
push. She decides not to. He can hear her deciding. She says she is
proud of him and he says thank you and he means it and it lands somewhere
he cannot reach. After the call he sits with his phone in his hand for
a moment that is longer than a moment. He does not know what he would
have said if she had pushed.
