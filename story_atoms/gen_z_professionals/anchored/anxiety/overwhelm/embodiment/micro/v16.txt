Keynote to sixty people, regional sales summit. Leo does the bathroom ritual anyway: cold water, list on the phone, three minutes. Then he walks out and takes the stage.

At the podium he notices the before is a little quieter than usual. Not gone. Quieter. He does not adjust anything because of this. He gives the presentation. He answers two questions from the floor. He thanks the moderator by name.

He still did the ritual. He still had the before. The difference is something he carries without stopping to name it.
