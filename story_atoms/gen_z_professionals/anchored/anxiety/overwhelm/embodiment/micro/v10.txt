Tariq blocks his Friday afternoons on the calendar. No title, no note. His team sees the block and nobody asks.

He does not use the time for anything that counts. He reads an article he bookmarked in February. He does not finish it. He starts a second one and reads halfway through and closes the tab. He sits with the laptop open and nothing running. He does not clear his inbox. He does not touch the backlog.

He does this for the full two hours. He closes the laptop when the block ends. He does not feel good about it exactly. He also does not move it.
