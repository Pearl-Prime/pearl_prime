Third calendar invite with no subject. Sam reads it. He tags it in his task manager under the client's name. Then he goes back to the brief he was writing.

He does not open the draft folder. He does not run the four-minute timeline. He types the next paragraph of the brief. He knows the invite is there. He knows he could check the chain for clues. He does not.
