Maya graduated with a specific idea of who she was going to be by twenty-
seven. She is twenty-six. The idea is still technically possible. She does
the math sometimes: if she gets a promotion in the next eight months, if
she moves to a company that pays what she knows the market pays, if she
starts the side project she has had in a notes app since March. The math
works on paper. What doesn't work is that the person who was going to do
those things had more energy than she currently does and she cannot
remember exactly when that changed or whether it is permanent.
