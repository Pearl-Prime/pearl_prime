The HR calendar invite appears at 9:02am with no subject line. Sam reads
it three times. The attendees are him, his manager, and a name from HR
he does not recognize. The time is Thursday at 4pm, which is a time when
nothing good gets scheduled. He types and deletes a message to his manager
asking what the meeting is about. He types it again and leaves it in
drafts. He opens the company handbook and reads the performance improvement
section and then closes it. He has not done anything he can identify. He
is also aware that this is not the same as having done nothing.
