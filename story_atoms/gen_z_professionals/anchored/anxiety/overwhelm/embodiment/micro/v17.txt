Amara sends the brief at 2:15pm on a Tuesday. She closes the doc. She does not open the activity panel. Not because she has stopped wanting to — she can feel that she wants to. She opens the next project file instead and works through the copy revisions until the light in the office shifts.

At 5:30 she opens the doc. There are two comments from the director, both specific, both useful. She responds to both with tracked changes. She closes her laptop at 6:05.

The four hours between sending and checking were used. That is the difference. They were not held in waiting.
