Dominique asks if Aisha can do Saturday. They are standing outside the office, the conversation winding down toward goodbye.

Aisha says, "I can't this weekend."

Dominique says, "No worries. Next time."

That is the whole thing.

Aisha takes the train home. She makes a bowl of rice and sits on her couch and puts on a documentary about deep-sea fish that she had saved for three months because watching it alone felt like something she was supposed to earn first. She watches the whole thing. She does not text anyone to explain why she said no. She does not open a calendar to propose an alternative. The documentary is ninety minutes. She watches until the credits.
