Deadline Friday. At 1pm Zara sets a timer on her phone: sixteen minutes. She gets up from the desk. She does not bring the laptop. She eats the lunch she packed at 7am. She does not check the channel. She does not think about the four deliverables because she is eating.

The timer goes off. She goes back to her desk. The four deliverables are still there. Nothing collapsed in sixteen minutes. She looks at the clock: 1:19pm.

At 3:30 she sets it again. Same amount of time. Same rules. She does not tell anyone she is doing this. It is a private experiment she is running on herself.

The deliverables are done at 4:47. She notices it took the same amount of time. She does not draw a conclusion from this. She is too tired. But she notices it.
