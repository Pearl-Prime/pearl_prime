Nadia is in the produce section when she notices the phone in her hand. She is already partway through the math. Rent, $200 more. Student loan on the 14th. She puts the phone in her bag before she reaches the end. The number would have been the same number.

She picks up the thing she was going to put back. Furikake, $6.40. She puts it in the basket. At checkout she takes the receipt even though she pays digitally and almost never keeps them. She folds it and puts it in her coat pocket. She is not sure what she will do with it. She walks home.
