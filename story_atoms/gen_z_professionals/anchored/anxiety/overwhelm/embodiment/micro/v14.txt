Daniel's manager replies eighteen minutes after his message. Daniel notes it. He writes it into the pattern the way he always does — time, day, what else was happening in the channel.

Then he goes back to the model he was building.

He finishes the section he was on. He checks the formatting. He sends it to the shared drive and writes the summary note. Then he looks at the timeline again.

Last month he would have checked at fourteen minutes. He would have checked at sixteen. The model would have waited. This time the model did not wait. He is not sure when that changed. He is still watching. The watching did not stop. It just came second today, and that is a different order of things than it was before.
