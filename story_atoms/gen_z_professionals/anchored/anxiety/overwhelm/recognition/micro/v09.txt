Nadia opens the email in the hallway outside her apartment. The subject line says lease renewal. She reads the new number. It is $200 more than what she is paying now. She closes the email. She opens it again to make sure the number is what she read. It is.

In the elevator she does the math. Rent, the student loan on the 14th, the Spotify she has not canceled because it is the one thing. The numbers add up to a number that works. She gets to her floor. She does the math again. The same items, the same order. Rent, student loan on the 14th, Spotify. It still works. It works the same way it worked thirty seconds ago. She puts her key in the door.
