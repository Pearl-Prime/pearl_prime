Nia keeps a log. It has four columns: date, trigger, a number from zero to ten she invented herself, and a field she labels "contributing factors." She reviews it every Sunday at 9am. She looks for patterns. The log tells her that Tuesdays are harder than Thursdays. It tells her that email from the senior partner before 8am is a nine. It tells her that the open floor plan is a six on its own baseline.

She adds new entries with precision. She uses the same syntax each time. There are fifty-three entries.

The log does not make Tuesdays easier. It makes her feel like she is doing something about the fact that Tuesdays are not easier. This is not the same thing. She knows this. She keeps the log anyway.

She has not told anyone the log exists.
