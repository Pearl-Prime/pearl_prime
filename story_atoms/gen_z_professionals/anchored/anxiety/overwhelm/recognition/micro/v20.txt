The reminder fires forty-one minutes before the stakeholder review and her body responds as if the meeting is already underway. She is still in the kitchen. The toast is not finished. Her hands go cold on the counter edge. She reads the notification again — forty-one minutes, not four — and her heart rate does not correct. She opens the deck on her phone to scan slide one while the kettle boils. The opening line is fine. She knows it is fine. Her voice tried it twice in the dry run yesterday and her manager said proceed. None of that information reaches her chest. The mobilization is running on its own schedule, ahead of the calendar, ahead of the coffee she has not poured yet. Priya stands in a kitchen that is not a conference room and feels the presentation in her throat.
