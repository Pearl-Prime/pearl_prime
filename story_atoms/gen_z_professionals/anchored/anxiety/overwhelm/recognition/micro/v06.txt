Marcus scrolls past the LinkedIn notification without opening it. He
already knows what it says. Jess from his cohort got promoted to senior
associate. They started the same month. He closes the app and opens his
email. He opens LinkedIn again. He reads Jess's post — three paragraphs,
grateful, humble, excited — and he feels something that is not exactly
jealousy and not exactly not jealousy and he does not have a word for it
that he would use in conversation. He likes the post. He means it and
he does not mean it.
