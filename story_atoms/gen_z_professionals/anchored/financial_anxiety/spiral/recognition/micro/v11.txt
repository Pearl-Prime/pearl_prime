Quinn's card gets declined at the coffee cart, which does not make sense, because payday was Friday. She steps out of line and opens the bank app on the sidewalk. The balance is forty-one dollars. She knows why — the annual thing, the insurance auto-draft she forgot renews in July. She refreshes anyway, thumb dragging the screen down until it bounces, as if the number might have been a loading error. It reloads to forty-one. She refreshes again. Then she gets back in line and orders a water.
