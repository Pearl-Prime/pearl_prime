Payday is a Friday, which means the money is real from about 6 a.m. until the autopays run. Wren knows the window. The deposit hits and Wren opens the banking app on the walk to the train, and the number is the biggest it will be all month, and Wren just looks at it. Rent leaves Monday. The loan servicer takes its piece Tuesday. Wren does the subtraction standing on the platform, then does it again, as if the second time it might land differently. The train comes. Wren refreshes the balance one more time before going underground, where it can't be checked.
