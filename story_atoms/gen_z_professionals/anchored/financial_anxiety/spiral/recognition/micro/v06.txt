Bex's phone buzzes with the same notification she has swiped away four times today. Pay in 4, it says, your next installment is coming up. She does not open it. She knows it's the boots, or the desk chair, or maybe the concert tickets, and the not-knowing sits easier than the math would. Her thumb moves the banner off the screen. She tells herself she'll add it all up on Sunday. It is Sunday. She opens Instagram instead.
