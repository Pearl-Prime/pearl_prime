Kofi's paycheck lands at 11:58 p.m. and by 12:03 he has already opened the transfer app. He does the conversion in his head before the screen even loads — the rate today, minus the fee, what it becomes on the other side. He sends the amount home first, always first, before rent, before the card, before he has looked at what stays. His thumb hovers over Send, holds, presses. Then he closes the app fast, like shutting a door on a room he doesn't want to count.
