Imani's loan payoff estimate is three years and seven months, and she has just closed the tab that told her so, and she is opening it again. The number has not moved since lunch. Her thumb knows the path without her eyes—portal, balance, back—the way it used to know a lock screen. She switches to the budgeting app with the pastel circles waiting to be filled, hovers over the first empty field, and her breath stops at the top of the scroll. She closes it. She opens the balance again.
