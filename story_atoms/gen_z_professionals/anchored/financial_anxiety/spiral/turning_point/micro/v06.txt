A coworker mentions, offhand, that she deleted all her buy-now-pay-later apps and just waits two weeks before buying anything now. Bex says that's smart, means it, and does not do it. But that night she is lying in the dark doing the 1am math again, and for the first time she lets the real number surface instead of swatting it away — says it, actual digits, into the quiet room. It is smaller than the monster and worse than the story. She lies there holding both and does not reach for the phone.
