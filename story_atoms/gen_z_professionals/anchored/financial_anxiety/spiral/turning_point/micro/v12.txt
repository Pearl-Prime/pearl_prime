The card reader declines, then on the second tap it clears. Nobody notices. Devon notices. He carries the coffees back to the table and the number is not running. For one strange second there is just the weight of two cups and the noise of the cafe. He sits. Priya asks if he is okay and he says the tap thing always does that. It is true. It is not the reason he has gone quiet. He cannot get the ledger to start again, and the silence where it was unnerves him.
