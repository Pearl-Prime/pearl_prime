A regular customer, an older woman she delivers to most Fridays, hands Nia the bag back with a twenty folded inside and says, "You look like you haven't stopped in a while." Nia laughs it off, says she's just hustling, and takes the next ping. But at the light she is sitting with the phone dark in the cupholder for once, not tapping it, and she realizes she cannot remember the last hour she was not earning or recovering from earning. The light turns green. She doesn't move right away.
