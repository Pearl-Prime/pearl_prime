The photo lands Sunday night — six of them on the porch, the lake behind. Sol is on her couch with a bowl of pasta going cold. Forty-one dollars until Friday; she knows this the way she knows her address. She heart-reacts fast, before the pause can show. Then she opens the banking app, sees the number she already knew, closes it, opens it again forty seconds later. She eats standing up so the sitting doesn't count as an evening.
