Theo has a system now. Every paycheck, he rebuilds the sheet from scratch, column by column, because the saved version feels like it might be lying. Tonight the total is the same as last night, and the night before. His jaw is tight enough that his back teeth ache. He tells himself once he sees the numbers clearly, once they finally resolve, he can close the laptop and sleep. So he adds a new tab to model rent going up. The clock says 2:40. He opens a fresh cell.
