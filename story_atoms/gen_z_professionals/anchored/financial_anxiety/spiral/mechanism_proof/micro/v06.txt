A card gets declined at the coffee place and Bex laughs it off, says try the other one, and the other one works. Walking back to her desk she starts the tally — Klarna, Afterpay, the one built into the shopping app, the fourth she can't name — and loses the thread by the third. The number she lands on has a question mark after it. Four apps, four due dates, none of them opened. She buys nothing the rest of the day and feels the restraint like it should count for something.
