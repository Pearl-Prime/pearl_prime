The rent clears and the paycheck is already gone—servicer, card minimum, the group order she said yes to. Imani opens the portal at 12:40 a.m. to watch a number that will not change before morning. A friend from her cohort posts a salary, base plus equity, and Imani's jaw sets. She thumbs back to the pastel app, the circles still empty three weeks running, because a plan needs money that stays. She adds nothing. She refreshes the balance instead, four times, as if looking hard enough could subtract from it.
