It is the fourth trip this month. Priya's boarding pass, Maldives, the little water bungalow. Dre sends the heart in under a second — that part is automatic and honest. Underneath it the tally runs: her rent is like his, so either she saves what he cannot or her parents cover the gap, and both of those answers cost him something to hold. His jaw is tight. He does the arithmetic on his own account without opening it because he already knows it. He mutes the chat. Twenty minutes later he unmutes it.
