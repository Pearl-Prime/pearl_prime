Bex opens the Klarna app — the first of the four, not all of them — and reads the balance, the whole number, and does not close it in the same breath. She screenshots it. Then she opens the notes on her phone, types the name of the app and the number beside it, and leaves a blank line for the next one, for tomorrow. Her chest is doing the tight thing. She writes the second line anyway. Two apps, two numbers, somewhere she can see them. She sets the phone face-up on the desk.
