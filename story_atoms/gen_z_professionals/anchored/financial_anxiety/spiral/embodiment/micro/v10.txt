The portal is open before Imani decided to open it, the balance already loading under her thumb. This time she does not close it and open it again. She reads the payoff date out loud, once, the real month, the far one, and lets it sit as a fact instead of a sentence passed on her. Then she opens the pastel app and types one number into one circle. Rent. Not a plan, not yet. Just the single thing she already knows, entered, and left there on the screen.
