A new inquiry lands on a slow morning — a good one, real budget. Ash's hand goes for the dashboard to run the months-of-rent math before replying. Halfway there Ash stops, sets the phone face-down on the desk, and writes back one line: "Let me check my calendar and get back to you tomorrow." Then Ash actually opens the calendar, sees the week already full, and does not say yes today. The balance goes unchecked until noon. It's a small thing. Ash notices it anyway.
