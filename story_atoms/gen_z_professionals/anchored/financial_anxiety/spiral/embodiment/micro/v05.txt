Payday lands. Kofi opens the transfer app the way he always does. This time, before he sends, he moves a small amount — less than a dinner — into the account he never funds, the one with his own name and nothing in it. Then he sends home, same as ever. It doesn't feel like triumph. It feels almost like theft, his heart going quick. He sits with the quick heart and does not undo it, and lets the small number stay.
