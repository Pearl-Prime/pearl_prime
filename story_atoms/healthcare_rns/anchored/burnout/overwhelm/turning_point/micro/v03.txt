Near the end of a night on yet another unit, an aide Elena has never met sets a coffee by her charting station: "You had the hard rooms tonight. I saw." Elena says thanks and reaches for the next order set. But her pen stops halfway to her palm. She looks at the coffee, still steaming, left by someone who noticed which patients were hers. She does not write the room number down. She holds the warm cup a second longer than the workflow allows, and her throat tightens.
