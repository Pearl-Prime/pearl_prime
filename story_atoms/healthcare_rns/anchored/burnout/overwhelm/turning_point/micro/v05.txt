A palliative doctor finds Tam charting in the stairwell instead of the station. He does not tell her she did everything right; she knows the everything, she can recite it. He says, "You are allowed to have known him and not be able to look at the picture yet. Both. For as long as it takes." Tam nods and goes back to her charting. But that night she stands in front of the fridge four seconds longer than it takes to open it, and she lets herself.
