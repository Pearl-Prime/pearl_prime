Tam is restocking the code cart when her hands stop. Not a decision — they just stop, both of them, holding a pack of pediatric electrodes over the drawer. Down the hall a monitor alarms and a different nurse answers it. The sticker on the electrode pack is a cartoon giraffe. She has opened this drawer four hundred times. Now she is standing in the supply room reading a giraffe, and somewhere a clock is running that she is not part of, and she does not move.
