Suz clocks out of her twelve on the floor and clocks straight into the education office, same badge, same building, one paycheck. There is a new-hire module due, so she opens the laptop while she is still in her scrubs. The cursor blinks in the empty field where the objective goes. She has taught this competency forty times. She reads the prompt again. She reads it a third time. Her hands are on the keys and nothing comes, and she does not notice she has stopped.
