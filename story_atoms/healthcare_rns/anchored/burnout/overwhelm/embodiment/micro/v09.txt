The listings are open before Coop has decided to open them, a contract two states away already under his thumb. This time he doesn't tap. He carries the duffel to the closet, unzips it, and puts three shirts on actual hangers — not all of it, just three, his hands unsure it is allowed. He does not book the far city. He leaves the empty bag open on the closet floor instead of by the door, and lets one week be a week he is staying in, not a gap to outrun.
