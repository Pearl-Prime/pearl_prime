Elena scans into a unit she has never worked, and before she copies the room numbers to her palm, she walks the hall once, slowly, on purpose. She finds the crash cart. She reads the charge nurse's name tag and says it out loud, once, so it will be a person and not a floor. It changes almost nothing about the twelve hours ahead. But at the med cart she writes her own name on the whiteboard under "RN today," where the last float left it blank.
