Third unit this week, and Elena's break is over before she finds the break room. She eats a granola bar at a med cart on a floor whose staff call her "the float" without looking up. A tech asks if she knows where the glucometer lives here. She does not. She has memorized four supply closets this week and each one is wrong the next morning. Her jaw stays set. She answers the call light, the phone, the pump alarm, keeping a running list on her palm because nothing here is allowed to get familiar.
