The family is crying at the bedside and Denise is already charting. She fills the boxes cleanly, times right, no gaps, and somewhere in the second paragraph the wife thanks her for being so kind. Denise says of course, honey, in the voice, and does not look up. She notices she has stopped checking whether she means it, the way she stopped tasting the break-room coffee. The chart is perfect. The kindness came out of her mouth fully formed and touched nothing on the way. She moves to the next room on time.
