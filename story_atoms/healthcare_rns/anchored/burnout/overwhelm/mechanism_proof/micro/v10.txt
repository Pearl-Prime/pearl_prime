The precepting is the part she used to love. Now, when the new grad asks why the protocol changed, Suz hears her own voice give the answer clean and correct, and feels nothing under it, the way a hand reports a countertop it cannot warm. She builds the slide anyway. She stays the extra hour off the clock because the module is due and she is the only educator, and she has begun to call this being reliable. Someone thanks her. She says no problem, and means don't ask for more.
