The new contract clears and Coop is packed in nine minutes, because he was never unpacked. Third city this year. He works the floor at a competent pace, eats standing at the med cart, learns no one's name — names are weight, and the point is to travel light. On the fourth night a mother thanks him for how gentle he was. He nods and cannot feel it land; it goes through him like water through mesh, and he is already scanning the board for the city after this one.
