Marcus is home by 7:40, scrubs still on, when the staffing text lights up the counter: OT available, ICU, tomorrow. His thumb has already typed I can take it before he has taken off his shoes. His wife is asleep down the hall. He stands at the counter reading the words he just sent. The reply comes back — Thank you!! — with the exclamation points that mean somebody is relieved it will not be them. He sets the phone down and does not sit.
