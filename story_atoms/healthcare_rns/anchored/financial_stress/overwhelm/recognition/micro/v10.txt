Beau sits in the truck in the last patient's driveway with the odometer photographed on his phone. He is doing the math again — 41 miles to the Hollis place, then 12 back to town, then home. The tank needle sits a hair above the E line where it has sat for three days. He does the arithmetic a fourth time, gets the same number, and does it a fifth. The engine is off. His hand is still on the key.
