The wedding invitation is magneted to Thanh's fridge, RSVP due Friday. Saturday is a double she already claimed on the swap board — twelve hours of oncology, time-and-a-half she has spent in her head three times. She stands in the kitchen doing the arithmetic of a plus-one gift against a shift she cannot un-take. Her jaw is tight. She tells herself she is being responsible. She does not notice she has stopped being invited to things.
