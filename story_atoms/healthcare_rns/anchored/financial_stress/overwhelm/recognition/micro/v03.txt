Renata's first real paycheck cleared four hours ago and she is already inside the loan portal, thumb hovering over the balance she has memorized. She opens it. The number is the number. She closes it. She opens the budgeting app instead, the one with the cheerful empty circles waiting for her to enter a plan, and she looks at the blank fields the way she looks at a chart she doesn't know how to fill in yet. She closes that too.
