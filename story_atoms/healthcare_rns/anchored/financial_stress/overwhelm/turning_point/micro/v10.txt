An older home-health nurse rides along on the Tuesday route and watches Beau photograph the odometer at every stop. She doesn't say he's cheap or careful. She says, "One hospital in this county and they know it — that's why you're doing their math on your phone." Beau says he doesn't mind and turns up the radio. But out past the Hollis place he catches himself not counting the miles for once, and he can't say what he traded to stop.
