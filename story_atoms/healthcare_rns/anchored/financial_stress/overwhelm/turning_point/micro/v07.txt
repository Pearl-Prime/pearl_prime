A traveler nurse two beds over lets an unknown number go to voicemail without flinching, then catches Amara's thumb already hovering over Decline. 'You screen the 800s too,' she says, not a question. Amara starts to explain the area codes, the system she's built, and finds the explanation won't hold its shape out loud. She says something about spam and turns back to her charting. But racking the next syringe she notices her jaw has come unclenched a half-inch, and she couldn't say when.
