A newer nurse asks Thanh how she does it, all the extra shifts, like it is a superpower. Thanh opens her mouth to say the money is good and hears herself say, "I don't actually know when I last had a Saturday." The sentence lands in the break room and just sits there. She laughs it off and clocks in. But scrubbing for the next case she notices she is scrubbing slower, and she can't say why her chest feels loose.
