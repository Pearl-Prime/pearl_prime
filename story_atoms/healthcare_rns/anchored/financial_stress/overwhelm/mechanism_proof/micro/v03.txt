The paycheck is spoken for before it arrives; Renata has watched the deposit land and leave in the same breath, loan servicer, rent, the minimum on the card. What it takes is the budgeting app she opens again tonight and cannot fill, because a plan requires money that stays long enough to be planned. Her jaw is tight. She scrolls the empty circles, adds nothing, and starts refreshing the loan balance instead, as if watching it might make it smaller.
