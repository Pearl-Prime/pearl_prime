The dinners are the first thing to go. When the group texts about splitting a check, Nora says she's on nights. She isn't, not that night. She has started leaving one drawer in the kitchen closed for days at a time. Every place she doesn't go is one less place the number can find her, and she has begun to call this being responsible. When people ask how she's doing she says fine, and the word has come to mean don't look.
