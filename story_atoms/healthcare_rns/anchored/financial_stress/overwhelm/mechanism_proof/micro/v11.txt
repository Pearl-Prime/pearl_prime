The unit posts the charge-nurse differential and Priyanka has already spent it against Danielle's second income before the meeting ends. It is never enough to close the gap; the gap is not made of dollars. She takes the extra Saturday. She takes the on-call. She is the one who stays, and staying has become the only answer she can give the question she keeps not-asking. Her sister calls; Priyanka lets it ring. There is nothing to report except the tally, and the tally never stops.
