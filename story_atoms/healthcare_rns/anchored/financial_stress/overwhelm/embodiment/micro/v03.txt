The loan portal is open again before Renata decided to open it. This time she does not close it and reopen it. She reads the payoff date at the top of the screen out loud, once, the real year, the far one, and lets it be a fact instead of a verdict. Then she opens the budgeting app and types a single number into one circle. Rent. Not a plan yet. Just the one thing she already knows, entered and left there.
