Priya sees the internal call for panelists for the leadership offsite land in her inbox. The ask is exactly her profile: five years in, cross-functional, done the work twice over. She opens the nomination form. She gets to the field that says describe your relevant experience and her hands go cold. She types a sentence. She deletes it. She types a smaller sentence. She minimizes the tab and tells herself she'll finish it after lunch. She does not finish it after lunch. She tells herself tomorrow, first thing. Tomorrow she opens the tab, rereads what she has, and closes it again without adding a word. The deadline is Friday at five. She knows the deadline. She has known it since Monday. Her chest is tight every time the tab is open, so mostly she keeps the tab closed.
