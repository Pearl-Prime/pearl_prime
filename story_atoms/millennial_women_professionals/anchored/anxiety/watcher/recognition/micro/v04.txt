Mia opens the launch announcement and reads it the way a forensic accountant reads a ledger. Subject: Shipping the new onboarding flow. Four names in the credit line. Hers is not among them. The stomach does its twist and Mia notes the twist, notes the timing, notes that this is the third launch this quarter where the body has done this exact thing in this exact order. She scrolls down. She is scrolling and she is also watching herself scroll. The postscript does not contain her. She rereads. The watcher logs: third reading, same data. The jaw tightens at the second reread. She opens the green-coded calendar to confirm she ran the working sessions. The confirmation arrives. The body does not relax at the confirmation. The watcher notes this too — the body's failure to be soothed by evidence. She closes the tab. She opens it again. She watches herself open it again. She does not have a name for the part of her that is doing the watching, only the data it produces.
