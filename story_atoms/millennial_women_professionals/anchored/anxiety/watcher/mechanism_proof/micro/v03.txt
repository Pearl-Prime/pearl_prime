Sarah's promotion announcement goes out on a Tuesday. Senior Director, Operations. The email lists no removals from her old portfolio. The watcher reads the email twice. It logs: title up one tier, salary up four percent, scope expanded by approximately forty percent, prior responsibilities zero subtracted. By Friday Sarah has thirty-one open Slack DMs and four overlapping decks. The chest is very tight by 11am every day this week. She is now managing three reports who report to her in her old role and were already overworked before she was their boss. She tries to walk one of them through a budget thread and finds she cannot remember the input numbers. She watches herself fail to remember. The watcher catalogs the failure in the same column it catalogs everything else: shoulders forward by two inches, jaw tight, breath shallow at the top, hands cool, attention split four ways, recall function compromised at threshold load. The promotion was supposed to feel like arrival. The watcher's data does not show arrival. It shows the same body in a larger box. Sarah eats half a granola bar at her desk at 2:45 because the watcher noticed she had not eaten and the watcher noticed she did not stop noticing this until something was done about it.
