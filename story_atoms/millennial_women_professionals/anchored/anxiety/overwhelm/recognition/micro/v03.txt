Sarah opens the email at 7:53am. The subject line is just: quick favor? She has not finished her coffee. She has not opened her actual inbox. The favor is a deck. The deck is for Friday. It is Tuesday. Her chest tightens. She types yes of course before she has read the second paragraph. She deletes of course. She types happy to. She sends it. She already has four open decks. She knows this. The throat closes a quarter inch. Her hands are cold around the mug. She opens her calendar. She finds an hour Thursday night between 9 and 10pm. She blocks it. She labels it deck. She does not tell anyone she blocked it. She picks up the coffee. It has gone lukewarm.
