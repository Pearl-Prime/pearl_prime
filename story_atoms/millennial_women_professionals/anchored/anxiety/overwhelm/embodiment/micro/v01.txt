She says, I built it over the last six months with input from these four people. She lists them. She does not list everyone in the room as a courtesy. Her chest is open. After, three people come up to ask follow-ups. She answers each one. She does not say it was nothing or that she got lucky. She says, here's how I was thinking about it. Her stomach is settled.
