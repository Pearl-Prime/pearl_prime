Sarah gets the promotion announcement in the Monday email. Senior Director. Her chest tightens. She reads the line about expanded scope twice. The team she was managing informally is now hers formally. Plus two other teams. Plus the budget. Plus the cross-functional council. Her shoulders hunch forward. The email does not mention what gets taken off her plate. She scrolls down looking for that paragraph. There is no paragraph. The throat closes. She counts the standing meetings she's now in: eleven. She was in seven. She still owns the four client accounts she was running before. Her hands are cold. She types out a thank-you reply. She makes it warm. She makes it grateful. She mentions she's looking forward to the new scope. She sends it. She sits at her desk and does not open the next email for a long time.
