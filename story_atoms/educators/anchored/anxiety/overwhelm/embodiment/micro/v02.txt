Marcus tells the chair he'll take testing coordinator only if yearbook
goes to someone else this year. It is the first trade he has ever
proposed instead of an addition he's absorbed. The chair agrees faster
than he expected, which makes him wonder how many of the other yeses
could have been trades too. He doesn't chase that thought all the way
down tonight. He drives home, makes the dinner reservation his girlfriend
asked about three weeks ago, and does not check his email once during
the meal.
