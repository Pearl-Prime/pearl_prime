Rachel closes the laptop on the seven open tabs and opens it again ten
seconds later. The IEP meeting is Tuesday. The observation is Wednesday.
The curriculum map is due Friday and she has not started it, not because
she doesn't know how, but because starting means picking one thing when
all one hundred twenty-seven papers are still ungraded on the passenger
seat of her car. She tells her sister on the phone that it's just a busy
week. It has been a busy week for four months. She says this like it is
new information and hangs up before her sister can point out that it
isn't.
