Tara's daughter sets her phone on the counter and says, "Did you watch my soccer game?" Tara watched the second half on the stream, on a phone propped against her water bottle, audio off, while she edited a deck. She did not see the goal. She has seen the clip her daughter posted. Her chest tightens. She says, "I saw the goal — beautiful." Her daughter watches her face for a beat longer than the answer requires. Then nods. Then turns back to the homework. Tara stands at the sink rinsing a plate that was already clean. Her hands grip the edge. The plate is cold. The water is warm. She does not move.
