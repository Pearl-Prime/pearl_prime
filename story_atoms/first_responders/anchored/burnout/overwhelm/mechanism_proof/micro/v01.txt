Fourth shift straight, and Reyes has stopped eating at the table. He takes his sandwich to the truck and sits with the door open, running the checklist he already ran. Every siren that is not his makes his jaw set. A rookie tells the shoe story back to him, laughing, and Reyes hears his own voice from a month ago say the exact words and feels nothing move in his chest. He checks the pediatric bag. The airways are there. He checks again.
