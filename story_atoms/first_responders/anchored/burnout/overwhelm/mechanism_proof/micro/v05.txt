The crew has stopped feeding him the setups. Reyes used to lob him the easy ones across the bay, and Iker would spike them before the sentence landed. Now the ribbing goes around him, a table he's still sitting at. He runs the scene at every call instead, reads the room the second he's through the door, exits already mapped, and the reading never shuts off, not at dinner, not at the good one about the lasagna, which he clocks as funny and lets pass.
