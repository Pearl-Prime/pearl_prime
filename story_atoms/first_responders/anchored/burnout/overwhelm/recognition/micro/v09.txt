The tones drop for the fourth structure fire since midnight and Marsh is already counting heads before his boots hit the bay floor. Rossi's limping. Delgado hasn't cracked a joke since the pediatric call. He clocks the new kid's hands shaking on the hose and files it, the way he files all of them, in a ledger only he reads. He can tell you the sleep debt of every man on his rig. He could not tell you the last time he tasted food.
