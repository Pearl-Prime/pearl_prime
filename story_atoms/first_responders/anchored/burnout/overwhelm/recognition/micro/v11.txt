Gray restocks the rig after the pediatric call the way he restocks it after a lift-assist. Same order, same drawers, gauze here, tape there, his hands doing it without him. A newer medic came out of that house with his jaw set wrong, went quiet by the bench seat. Gray notices the kid is not okay and notices, from a long way off, that he himself is fine. He clicks the last cabinet shut. He cannot remember the last call that reached his chest.
