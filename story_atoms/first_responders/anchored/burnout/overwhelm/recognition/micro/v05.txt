The kid at the back of the rig says the line about the burnt lasagna, the good one, and the whole crew goes. Iker hears it land. He knows it's funny. His own laugh arrives about a second later, a beat behind the others, loud enough that Reyes glances over. He plays it off, reaches for the strap like he meant the pause. On the drive back he catches himself timing it, waiting for the laugh to come the way you wait for a tone-out.
