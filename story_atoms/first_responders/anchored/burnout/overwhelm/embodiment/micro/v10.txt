The overdose is textbook and Odie works it clean, and afterward, restocking the jump bag on the tailgate, he does one new thing. He lets his face stay whatever it already is for the length of one exhale — doesn't wipe it, doesn't set it anywhere — and finds it is tired, and leaves it tired where Renner can see. Renner glances over, glances back at the kit. Says nothing. Odie zips the bag. His face is still his own when he climbs into the cab, and it is not a costume yet.
