Reyes is restocking at 3 a.m. again, counting the pediatric airways once. He gets to the number and lets his hand come off the zipper. The shoe surfaces — the light, the booster seat — and this time he says the kid's name out loud, just once, into the empty bay, and keeps counting the next pouch. A newer medic asks if he's alright.
