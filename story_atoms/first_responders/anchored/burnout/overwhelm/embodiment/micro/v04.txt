The next arrest is an old man, PEA, no shock advised, and it goes the way those go. In the truck after, Boone feels his hands reaching for the tape, the rewind, the better version. He lets them rest on his knees instead. He says it out loud to nobody: "Ran the call we had." Not the one he wanted. His jaw stays loose a second longer than usual. The mother from Tuesday is still in there. He drives without re-cutting her.
