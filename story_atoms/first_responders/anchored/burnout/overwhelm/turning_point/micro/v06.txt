A newer medic rides with her and won't stop talking, and forty minutes in he just says, "You went dead quiet after that last one. You okay?" Priyanka says she's fine, she's always fine. But at the next red light she notices her thumb is already on the radio dial, poised to kill a song that isn't playing, and her hand is shaking against the plastic. She puts both hands back on the wheel. The light goes green. She does not know why her eyes are stinging.
