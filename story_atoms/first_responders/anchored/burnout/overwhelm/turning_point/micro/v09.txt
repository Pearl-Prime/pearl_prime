Rossi finds Marsh alone in the bay at 0300, restocking a jump bag that is already stocked. He doesn't leave. He says, "Cap. You do roll call on all of us every shift. Who does yours?" Marsh starts to say he's fine and the word won't come up his throat. He stands there with a roll of gauze in his hand he doesn't remember picking up. He tells Rossi to get some sleep. But refilling the bag, his hands have gone still, and he can't say when they stopped.
