An old medic named Ruiz, forty years on the box, watches Boone re-run it out loud at the bay doors and doesn't correct the timeline. "You keep re-cutting it 'cause you think there's a version where you win. There isn't. That call was already lost when it came in." Boone says something about traffic and walks to the rig. But he doesn't rewind it on the drive. For eleven minutes he just watches the road, and the silence in his head is so loud it scares him.
