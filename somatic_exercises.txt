"Anxiety is just excitement" rebranding
Grounding through logic ("name 5 things you can see" — without the somatic component)
Medication as the only "real" solution

Sympathetic activation — your body's alarm system

Voice & Tone Guidelines for Writers
Sentence length: Average 8–12 words. Max 20.
Paragraphs: 2–4 sentences max.
Contractions: Always (you're, don't, can't, isn't)
Swearing: Occasional, strategic ("bullshit," "damn," not gratuitous)
Pop culture: Reference current Gen Z touchpoints (TikTok, doom-scrolling, "main character energy")
Academic language: Avoid. Translate everything. "Polyvagal theory" becomes "your body's three modes."
Humor: Dry, self-deprecating, meme-adjacent
Vulnerability: The author shares real failures, not just victories
Authority: Comes from lived experience + science, never from "I'm the expert"
Audiobook pacing: Include natural pause points indicated by "..." or "[pause]"
Direct address: "You" constantly. This is a conversation, not a lecture.
