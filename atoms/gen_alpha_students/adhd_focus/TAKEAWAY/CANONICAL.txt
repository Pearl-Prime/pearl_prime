## TAKEAWAY v03
---
---
Your attention goes toward what's loud, not what's important. Make your homework louder than the video.

## TAKEAWAY v04

Time is hard to feel without a timer. Put one where your eyes already are.

## TAKEAWAY v05

## TAKEAWAY v06

## TAKEAWAY v08

## TAKEAWAY v09

## TAKEAWAY v10

Checking the one spot you always forget beats searching your whole backpack again.
