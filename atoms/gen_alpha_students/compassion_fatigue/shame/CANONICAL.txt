MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 3

---
Asha talks to someone who has experienced compassion fatigue. The person says: everyone hits this limit. It's not weakness. It's the normal response to giving more than is sustainable. The permission to have limits is revolutionary. The fatigue doesn't go away. But the shame about the fatigue starts to dissolve. The shame was based on the belief that limits were failure.
