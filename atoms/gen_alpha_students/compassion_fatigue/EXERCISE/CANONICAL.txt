

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit down right now. You have been listening to everyone. Put your hands on your own knees. Feel your own body. Not theirs. Yours. Breathe into your own chest. Your own problems exist. Attend to yourself.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Close your eyes. Listen to your own breath. Count eight breaths where you think only of your breath. Not their problems. Your breath. In. Out. In. Out. Your oxygen. Your lungs. Your survival.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Feel where your body touches the seat. Feel that contact. That is you. Not the person you just helped. You are still here. You are still solid. You did not disappear into their crisis.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Tense all your muscles at once. Clench everything for three seconds. Release. Feel the relief. You are holding everyone else's feelings. Release that. Let their feelings be theirs. Your body gets lighter.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Press your hands on your thighs. Push down hard. Count to four. Your legs are yours. Your body is yours. You gave your listening. Now take your body back.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Splash cold water on your wrists if you can. Or imagine it. Cold brings you back to your body. Your nervous system resets. You are here. You are separate. You are not merged with their pain.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Make a small sound. A sigh. A groan. A hum. Let out sound that belongs to you. Your voice has been holding others. Your voice comes back to you.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Look in the mirror if you can. Or imagine your own face. This is you. Not them. See your face. See your eyes. Say hello to yourself. You are here too.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Shake out your hands like you are shaking off water. Shake hard for five seconds. Shake off their weight. Shake off their pain. Your hands are empty again.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Breathe in through your mouth. Breathe out through your nose. Switch it. Mouth out. Nose in. This strange breathing resets your nervous system. You step out of helper-mode. You come back to baseline.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
Notice one beautiful thing around you. A plant. Sunlight. A color. Look at it for five full breaths. Beauty exists when you are not listening. Beauty still happens. See it.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Feel your heart. Put your hand on your chest. Your heart beat even when you were busy listening to them. Your heart kept beating. Your heart is here. It is alive. It is yours.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
Stand if you can and stretch. Reach your arms up. Feel your body expand. You are big. You take up space. You are not small. You are not invisible. Stretch tall.
---

## EXERCISE v14
---

---
Hum a song you like. Just hum it. Your brain chooses a song. Your body makes the sound. This is about you. Your taste. Your voice. Hum it fully.
---

## EXERCISE v15
---

---
Feel your feet on the ground. Press each foot down separately. Left foot. Right foot. Both feet. You are planted. They are not pulling you up. You are here. Below.
---

## EXERCISE v16
---

---
Eat something or drink something. Taste it. Feel the flavor on your tongue. This is for you. This nourishment is yours. You do not give it to them. You keep it.
---

## EXERCISE v17
---

---
Roll your shoulders backward five times. Roll. Roll. Roll. Loosen the burden you are carrying. Your shoulders can release what is not yours to carry.
---

## EXERCISE v18
---

---
Touch something soft near you. A blanket. A stuffed animal. Your own arm. Feel the softness. You are allowed to feel soft things. You do not have to be hard for them.
---

## EXERCISE v19
---

---
Close your eyes and listen for one minute. Not to them. To the space around you. Wind. Cars. A fan. Ambient sound. Let your mind rest on these sounds. These are just sounds. No problems.
---

## EXERCISE v20
---

---
Say the phrase I am separate out loud three times. I am separate. I am separate. I am separate. Your voice confirms it. Your body believes it. You are not them.
---

## EXERCISE v21
---

---
Feel tension in your neck and shoulders. Where is it. Massage that spot gently for ten seconds. Breathe into it. The tension is from holding. Release it. That burden was not yours to hold.
---

## EXERCISE v22
---

---
Lie down if you can or lean back in your chair. Feel your whole back supported. The ground or the chair is supporting you. You do not have to support yourself alone. Let the surface hold you.
---

## EXERCISE v23
---

---
Blink slowly ten times. Each blink is a reset. Slow. Slow. Slow. Your eyes rest. Your brain rests. You reset between conversations.
---

## EXERCISE v24
---

---
Notice your hands. Do they ache from gesturing while listening. Flex your hands. Make fists. Release. Repeat five times. Your hands are tired from their problems. Give your hands a rest.
---

## EXERCISE v25
---

---
Breathe in a scent that makes you calm. Anything. Trace it with your smell. Your body gets to have preferences. Your body gets to feel good. This is allowed.
---

## EXERCISE v26
---

---
Say no silently inside yourself. No more listening right now. No more feeling for them. Your silence is okay. Your rest is okay. Say it.
---

## EXERCISE v27
---

---
Feel your jaw. Is it clenched from listening. Open it. Drop it loose. You have been making the work face. Make the rest face. Let your jaw relax.
---

## EXERCISE v28
---

---
Tap your chest lightly with your fist. Gentle taps. Three times. You are here. You survived their crisis. You made it through. You are still beating.
---

## EXERCISE v29
---

---
Move your head slowly in circles. One direction. Four circles. Then the other way. Four circles. Loosen the mental grip. The worry leaves through your neck.
---

## EXERCISE v30
---

---
Put both hands on your belly. Breathe into it. Feel it expand into your hands. This belly is yours. These organs are yours. You keep them. You feed them. You restore them.
---
