## PIVOT v01
---

---
You didn't cry at the news and the spiral started: I'm heartless, something's wrong with me, I'm becoming the kind of person I hate. The spiral has more feeling than the numbness did.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Compassion fatigue spirals into identity crisis: if I can't feel for others, who am I? The spiral mistakes emotional depletion for character failure. You're not less caring — you're overdrawn.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The spiral after the numbness is the cruelest part. You can't feel, and then you panic about not feeling. The compassion fatigue generates its own anxiety.
---
