

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Claim one accomplishment this week. Not to brag. Just to practice believing yourself. The doubt isn't evidence of incompetence. It's evidence of self-awareness that hasn't caught up to itself yet. The credential doesn't silence the doubt because the doubt isn't looking at the credential. It's looking for the exception.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
Your peers post about achievements you have not had. The honor roll. The acceptance. The performance. You read the posts and your stomach drops. The doubt arrives faster than you can interrogate it. The posts are the highlights. You are comparing your inside to their outside. You know this. The knowing does not stop the comparison.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Each accomplishment that should have made you feel solid did not. The grade. The award. The compliment from the teacher. They land for an hour. Then the doubt resumes. The doubt is not waiting for evidence. It has already filed a verdict and is waiting for the appeals to fail.
