## PIVOT v01
---

---
Every new challenge is another chance to be found out. The overwhelm isn't about the work — it's about the expanding territory where someone might discover you're not as smart as they think.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: you're overwhelmed by the material. The new frame: you're overwhelmed by the performance of understanding. The material is manageable. The act around it is exhausting.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Imposter overwhelm compounds with success: the better you do, the more you have to protect. Achievement becomes its own burden when every win raises the exposure stakes.
---
