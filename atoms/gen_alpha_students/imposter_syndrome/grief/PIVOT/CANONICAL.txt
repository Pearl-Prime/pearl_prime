## PIVOT v01
---

---
Underneath the imposter feeling is grief for never being able to enjoy what you've earned. The grades are real. The accomplishments are real. The feeling that you deserve them got lost in the noise.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You grieve the celebrations you couldn't feel. Every A, every acceptance, every compliment — experienced through the filter of someone who believes it's all a matter of time before they're caught.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The grief in imposter syndrome is for the experience of your own life. You were there for all of it and missed all of it because you were too busy waiting to be exposed.
