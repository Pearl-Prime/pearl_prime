---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Mira identifies the comparison's data source: curated feeds. The people she compares herself to are showing their best moments. Edited photos, top grades, winning games. The comparison takes curated highlights and measures them against Mira's unedited daily life. The mechanism: she is comparing her raw footage to everyone else's trailer. The trailer makes every movie look good. The raw footage includes the boring parts, the mistakes, the retakes. Her chest tightens. The comparison is rigged at the input stage. The data was never equal.
