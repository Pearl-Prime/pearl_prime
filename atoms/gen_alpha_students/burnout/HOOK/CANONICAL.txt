

## HOOK v01
---

---
Leo is slumped at his desk after school, Chromebook open on three unfinished tabs, shoulders collapsed toward the keyboard — he has been running on empty since October and nobody named it.
---

## HOOK v02
---

---
You open the Chromebook to three unfinished tabs and feel the specific tiredness of a kid who has been performing fine since fall, because fine is what keeps the questions away.
---

## HOOK v03
---

---
The assignment is not even hard, but you sit in front of it like it is a wall, because the part of you that used to just start things ran out somewhere around the third project.
---

## HOOK v04
---

---
You said you were okay so many times that the word stopped meaning anything, and now you are not sure how to find the sentence underneath it.
---

## HOOK v05
---

---
After school you do not play, you do not rest, you just power down in the chair with your shoes still on, a body too young to be this far past empty.
