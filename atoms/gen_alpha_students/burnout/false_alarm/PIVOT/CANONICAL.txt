---
You slept in on Saturday and felt okay by Sunday afternoon. Then you called it recovered. That's not recovery — that's the false alarm burnout uses to keep you from making real changes.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: you're not really burned out because you can still get the grades. The new frame: functioning and burned out aren't opposites. They're your specialty.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Your burnout tells you it's not that bad. It said the same thing three months ago when it was half this weight. The false alarm is the most dangerous feature of high-functioning exhaustion.
