## MECHANISM_PROOF v06

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Amara identifies the spiral's growth pattern. It does not spiral outward into new territory. It spirals inward, making the same territory feel larger. The same grade, the same test, the same fear — revisited with increasing intensity. Each pass adds emotional weight, not new information. His chest tightens. The mechanism: the spiral is an amplifier, not an explorer. It makes small things loud. It does not discover large things. The original concern is still the original size. The spiral added volume.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Theo sees the spiral's dependency: it needs uncertainty to run. Known outcomes stop the spiral. The spiral only operates in the gap between question and answer. A test is posted: spiral. Grades are unknown: spiral. Grades arrive: spiral stops. New test announced: spiral restarts. The mechanism: the spiral is powered by the unknown. It converts unknowns into worst cases, and the worst cases feel like certainty. Theo's chest tightens. The spiral is a machine that manufactures certainty from uncertainty. The certainty is always negative because the machine only builds downward.
