COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Zoe is in her work shift and everything feels hard. Getting coffee feels hard. Smiling feels hard. Existing in her body feels hard. She doesn't know why. She's been working here for six months. It's not new. But everything is slowly feeling impossible. She moves through the shift on fumes. She's ashamed. Other people do this without struggling. Asha works full-time and goes to nursing school. Leo works and takes eighteen credits. Zoe works fewer hours than both of them. Why is she struggling? The shame is in her chest. She shouldn't be struggling.
