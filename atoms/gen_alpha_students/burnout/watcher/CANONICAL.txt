Lina stands in the homework pile feeling the pattern of watcher pressing against the inside of the chest. The burnout has been building for weeks. Hands cold. Jaw locked. The old story — that this pattern is permanent, that it defines everything — starts to crack. Lina notices the crack. Does not fill it. Does not repair the old story. ## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Deshawn monitors her energy the way a pilot monitors fuel. Every activity depletes the gauge. She watches it drop through the school day: morning classes take forty percent. Lunch recovery adds five. Afternoon classes take thirty. She arrives home at twenty-five percent and homework needs fifty. The math does not work. The frame collapses: she has been managing an impossible equation. The equation is not solvable through monitoring. It is solvable through changing the inputs. She tells her parents she needs to drop one afternoon activity. The gauge rebalances.

Suki stops checking the teacher's face. Not forever. For this class period. She looks at her work. The problems on the page. The answers forming. The teacher walks past. Suki's eyes stay on the page. The watcher pulls toward the teacher's expression. Suki stays on the page. The answer she is writing is correct. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nolan maps the watcher's target list. It watches the teacher for disapproval. The popular kids for exclusion signals. The grades for decline. The parents for disappointment. The watcher has multiple feeds running simultaneously. Her neck is stiff. The mechanism: the watcher is not one system. It is several systems with different targets all running at once. Each system consumes attention. The total consumption exceeds Nolan's bandwidth. She is trying to run five surveillance cameras through one monitor. The monitor cannot hold it.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Amara sees the watcher's deepest cost: it has replaced her experience with her surveillance. She does not live moments. She monitors them. Every class, every interaction, every meal — she is watching the moment instead of being in the moment. The mechanism: the watcher has positioned itself between Amara and her own life. She experiences life at one remove. The removal is the watcher's product. Her chest aches. She is present everywhere and experiencing nothing. The watcher gave her coverage. It took her presence.
