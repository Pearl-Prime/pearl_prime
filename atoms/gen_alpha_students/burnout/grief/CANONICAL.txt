## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Nolan stands in the homework pile feeling the pattern of grief pressing against the inside of the chest. The burnout has been building for weeks. Hands cold. Jaw locked. The old story — that this pattern is permanent, that it defines everything — starts to crack. Nolan notices the crack. Does not fill it. Does not repair the old story. Just stands in the homework pile and watches the frame shift. The pattern is still running. But the frame around it is no longer solid. Something moved. Not much. Enough.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Amara finds an old drawing from when she was eight. It is messy and wonderful and made with zero concern for how it would be graded. She holds the paper and tears come. She is thirteen and she is grieving the eight-year-old who drew for joy. The burnout replaced joy with performance. The frame shatters: she picks up a pencil. Draws something terrible. On purpose. The terrible drawing is the most joyful act she has performed in months. It will not be graded. It will not go on the portal. It exists because she drew it.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/grief/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Zadie misses her best friend who moved away last year. The grief hits in the cafeteria — the empty seat that used to hold someone who understood. Her chest aches. She sits down anyway. Eats anyway. The grief does not leave but it does not control the lunch period. She talks to the person next to her. Not a replacement. A new person. The new person laughs at something Zadie says. The laugh is not the old friend's laugh. It is its own laugh. Zadie lets it be its own.

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/grief/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kai sits in the cafeteria at the empty seat. The grief for her friend is a permanent resident now. Not a visitor. She does not try to fill the seat. Does not try to replace the absence. She eats her lunch with the empty seat beside her and talks to the new people across from her. The grief and the new connections share the table. Neither cancels the other. The table is big enough. Her chest holds the ache and the lunch conversation simultaneously. Both are real. Both belong to her.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Lina sees how the grief for her friend compounds. Each new experience that the friend would have enjoyed becomes a grief trigger. A movie they would have watched together. A joke only the friend would get. A new student who does not know the friend existed. The grief is not fading because new triggers keep arriving. The mechanism: the grief is tied to the future, not just the past. Every new moment is a moment the friend misses. Her chest aches. The grief has an infinite supply of future moments.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Deshawn sees the grief's layered structure. The surface grief is for the friend who moved. Beneath it is grief for the stability the friendship represented. Beneath that is grief for the belief that good things last. The mechanism operates in layers: each grief reveals a deeper grief underneath. Deshawn's chest aches. The friend's departure is the top layer. The bottom layer is the understanding that everything Deshawn loves is temporary. The mechanism is not about one loss. It is about the architecture of loss itself.
