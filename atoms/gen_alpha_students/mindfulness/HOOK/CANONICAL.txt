## HOOK v01
---
---
Marcus is sitting at the dinner table, and his mom is telling him about her day, and his eyes are on her face, and he could not tell you a single word she just said, because his brain is still replaying the thing that happened at recess three hours ago.
---

## HOOK v02
---
---
Priya is reading the same page of her book for the fourth time, eyes moving across the words, and none of it is landing, because part of her is still in the classroom thinking about the test she has tomorrow.
---

## HOOK v03
---
---
Dana is playing a board game with her little brother, and she moves her piece, and he says "you skipped your turn last time too, are you even playing?" and she realizes she genuinely doesn't remember her last three turns.
---

## HOOK v04
---
---
Theo's teacher asks him a question he definitely knows the answer to, and he has to ask her to repeat it, because his body was in the classroom and his mind was still at home, worrying about something that happened this morning.
---

## HOOK v05
---
---
James is walking home from school, and he gets to his front door, and he cannot remember a single thing about the walk — not what street he crossed, not who he saw — like his body did the whole walk without him.
---

## HOOK v06
---
---
Renee is hugging her grandma goodbye, and she's already thinking about the video game she wants to play when she gets home, and some part of her knows the hug doesn't feel like a real hug because of that.
---

## HOOK v07
---
---
Diallo is doing his math homework, and his little sister asks him a question, and he answers her, and five minutes later he can't remember what she asked or what he said back, only that words happened between them.
---

## HOOK v08
---
---
Ms. Whitfield's friend is telling her something important at recess, something she can tell matters to her friend, and Ms. Whitfield is nodding, and thinking about what she's going to do at lunch, and her friend's voice starts to sound far away even though she's standing right there.
---

## HOOK v09
---
---
Priya is at soccer practice, ball at her feet, and her coach yells her name because she's standing still in the middle of a drill, mind somewhere else entirely, body just parked in the grass.
---

## HOOK v10
---
---
Marcus is watching his favorite show, the one he's been looking forward to all week, and halfway through he realizes he hasn't actually followed the plot for the last ten minutes, even though his eyes never left the screen.
---

## HOOK v11
---
---
Dana counts it once, for fun, how many times during class her mind leaves the room. She loses count before lunch.
---

## HOOK v12
---
---
Theo is being read a bedtime story by his dad, the same voice, the same warm light, and he catches himself thinking about tomorrow's spelling test instead of the story, and something in him feels a little sad about that, though he's not sure why.
---

## HOOK v13
---
---
James is mid-conversation with his best friend at lunch, and he answers a question a half-second too fast, on autopilot, and his friend gives him a look like he knows James wasn't really listening.
---

## HOOK v14
---
---
Renee's little cousin asks her to play, tugging her sleeve twice before she notices, because Renee was standing right there, eyes open, and nowhere at all.
---

## HOOK v15
---
---
Diallo is brushing his teeth and realizes, looking in the mirror, that he has no memory of walking into the bathroom, brushing the first half of his teeth, or picking up the toothbrush — his body did all of it while he was somewhere else.
---
