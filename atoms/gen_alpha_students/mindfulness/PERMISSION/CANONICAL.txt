## PERMISSION v01
---
---
You are allowed to let your mind wander during class or dinner and still call yourself a good listener, as long as you keep coming back.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---
---
You are allowed to ask someone to repeat something instead of pretending you heard it the first time.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---
---
You are allowed to tell a worry "I'll think about you later" and actually mean it, instead of trying to solve it right now.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---
---
You are allowed to feel a little scattered on a busy day and still be practicing this correctly.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---
---
You are allowed to use a tiny, one-second habit instead of waiting to feel calm enough to be present.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---
---
You are allowed to forget your "come back" habit for a while and pick it back up later without starting over.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---
---
You are allowed to feel worried or excited and still be paying real attention to what's happening right now.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---
---
You are allowed to not remember every single detail of every day, as long as you keep practicing landing in the next moment.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---
---
You are allowed to count a moment of coming back as a win, even on a day that still feels chaotic overall.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---
---
You are allowed to let the coming-back feel like effort; it gets easier with practice, not by getting it perfect right away.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---
---
You are allowed to give a hard feeling a "later" time instead of carrying it into everything you do for the rest of the day.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---
---
You are allowed to start practicing this on your hardest, most distracted day instead of waiting for an easier one.
---
