## TAKEAWAY v01
---
---
Being present isn't staying focused forever. It's coming back, over and over, a little faster each time.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---
---
You don't stop your mind from wandering. You just get quicker at noticing and coming back.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---
---
Half-listening doesn't look like a mistake. It looks like a normal day where you weren't quite there.
---

## TAKEAWAY v04
---
---
The goal was never calm. The goal is noticing what's actually real right now.
---

## TAKEAWAY v05
---
---
Every new moment is a chance to actually show up for it, even if you missed the last one.
---

## TAKEAWAY v06
---
---
A quick return costs almost nothing. An unnoticed drift costs the whole conversation.
---

## TAKEAWAY v07
---
---
You're not failing every time your mind wanders. You're only failing if you never notice.
---

## TAKEAWAY v08
---
---
Busy and scattered aren't the same. Doing one thing fully beats doing five things halfway.
---

## TAKEAWAY v09
---
---
Coming back gets easier with practice. It's not supposed to feel easy on day one.
---

## TAKEAWAY v10
---
---
A worry you park quiets down. A worry you fight just gets louder.
---

## TAKEAWAY v11
---
---
You don't need a calm day to practice this. You need a small cue you use often.
---

## TAKEAWAY v12
---
---
Count the times you come back, not the times you drift. The coming back is the whole practice.
---
