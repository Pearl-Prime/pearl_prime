## PIVOT v01
---

---
You grieve the ease you see in other people — the casual conversations, the effortless belonging. The social anxiety took something that should be natural and made it a battlefield.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Underneath the social anxiety is loss: the loss of spontaneity, the loss of showing up without a script, the loss of just being yourself without the running commentary of what could go wrong.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The grief in social anxiety is for connection itself. You want it desperately and the anxiety makes every attempt feel like defusing a bomb. The grief is for the simplicity you never had.
---
