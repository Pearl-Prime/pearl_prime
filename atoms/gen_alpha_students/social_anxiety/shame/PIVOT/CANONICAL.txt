## PIVOT v01
---

---
You rehearse a conversation three times before having it and you're ashamed that it takes this much preparation just to seem normal. The shame of social anxiety is the performance gap — the distance between how hard it is and how easy it should be.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Shame says everyone does this effortlessly. They don't. But your social anxiety adds a production budget to interactions that others do as improv.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The specific shame: you know the anxiety is disproportionate and you still can't override it. The awareness without the ability to change is its own torture.
---
