## PIVOT v01
---

---
The conversation went fine and your body still took an hour to calm down. The false alarm of social safety is your nervous system refusing to believe that fine is actually fine.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: it went well, so relax. The new frame: your system doesn't update in real time. The all-clear takes longer to arrive than the alert, and by then you're already bracing for next time.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Social anxiety's false alarm goes both ways: the danger was false, and the relief is false too. You know it'll feel exactly this bad next time, regardless of how well today went.
---
