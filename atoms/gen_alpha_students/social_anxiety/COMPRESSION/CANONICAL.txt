

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Before the next social moment, ask 'What do I want to know about this person?' instead of 'What will they think of me?' The performance anxiety doesn't mean you're doing it wrong. It means the stakes registered. They're real to you.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
The group chat is the room you cannot leave. Even when you put the phone down, the chat is happening. You will see what you missed. You will be expected to know what was said. The social interaction is no longer an event. It is a continuous condition. The continuous condition does not have a recovery period.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Your face is on a screen during every class, every video call, every TikTok. You learned to watch your own face the way other people watch you. The watching never turns off. The body cannot tell the difference between the practice mirror and the actual interaction. They are both monitored. Both are tiring.
