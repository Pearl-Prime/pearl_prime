## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v02 — ORDINARY TRIGGERS — EARLY
---
Jaden finds Max everywhere. The squeaky toy behind the couch—wave. The vet's number on the fridge—wave. The specific way 5pm used to mean walk time—wave. He gave away the food, the bowls, the bed. The triggers stayed. They're in the carpet dent. The hook by the door. The silence at 5pm. Max is gone. The ordinary things that held Max are still here, triggering.
