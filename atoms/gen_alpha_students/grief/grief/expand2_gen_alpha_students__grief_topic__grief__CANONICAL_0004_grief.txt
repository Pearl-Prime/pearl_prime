## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v04 — RELATIONSHIP LOSS — MID — CHEST ACHE
---
Leo sees Kenji in the hallway. They nod. Keep walking. Two years of being inseparable and now they nod. The friendship didn't end—it just stopped. No fight. No moment. Just less and less until this. Leo's chest aches watching Kenji laugh with someone else. The inside jokes are still in his head. The shorthand. The history. Kenji has it too, probably. They just don't use it anymore.
