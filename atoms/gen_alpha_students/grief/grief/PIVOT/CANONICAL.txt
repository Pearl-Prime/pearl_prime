## PIVOT v01
---

---
Sometimes you grieve the grief itself — the fact that this is something you have to carry now, that this weight exists and didn't exist before. The meta-grief is real.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You grieve the life that existed before this loss. Not just the person or the thing — the version of your world where this hadn't happened yet.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The hardest grief is grieving who you were before grief found you. That person is gone too, and nobody talks about that loss.
