## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v01 — WAVE DOESN'T DESTROY — MID
---
Maya expects the wave to flatten her. Grandma's birthday. She wakes up braced for devastation. The wave comes at breakfast—the empty chair, the cake they're not making. But she doesn't drown. She cries in the bathroom. Washes her face. Goes to school. The grief is huge. She's still standing. The day happens around the loss, not instead of it. She thought it would stop her. It didn't.
