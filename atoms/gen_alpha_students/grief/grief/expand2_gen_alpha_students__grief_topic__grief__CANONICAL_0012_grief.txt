## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v02 — MEMORY WITH WARMTH — LATE
---
Jaden braces when he finds the squeaky toy. The wave comes—but this time there's something else in it. Max destroying that toy on Christmas morning. The ridiculous squeaking. Mom yelling from the kitchen. Max's tail going crazy. He almost laughs. The grief is still there. But the memory brought Max, not just the absence. Max existed. Made ridiculous sounds. The wave passes. Something warm stays.
