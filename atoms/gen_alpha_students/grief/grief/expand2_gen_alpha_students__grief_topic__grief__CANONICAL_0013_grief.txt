## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v03 — SOMEONE MENTIONS THEM — EARLY
---
Zoe freezes when Dad says it. "Remember when your mom burned the turkey that year?" Mom's name, out loud. Zoe braces. But the wave doesn't drown her. Dad is half-smiling, remembering. Other people knew her too. Other people have memories. Mom exists in conversation, not just in absence. The missing is still there. It has company now.
