## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v04 — WALKS THE OLD PATH — MID
---
Leo takes the long way home. Past the park where he and Kenji used to sit. The bench is there. The tree. The path they wore into the grass. The friendship isn't there, but the path is. He walks it sometimes. Not to fix anything. Just to keep the route familiar. Memory walks. The person isn't there. The walking remembers anyway.
