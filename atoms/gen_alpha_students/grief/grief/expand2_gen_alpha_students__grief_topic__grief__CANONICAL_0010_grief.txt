## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — WAVES DON'T ASK PERMISSION — EARLY
---
Aisha is fine in class. Fine at lunch. Fine walking home. Then she touches her collarbone—the bare spot—and the wave arrives without warning. In public. On the sidewalk. Eyes stinging, throat closing. The necklace has been gone for two weeks. The waves don't care about time. Don't care about location. They arrive when they arrive. She keeps walking. The wave walks with her.
