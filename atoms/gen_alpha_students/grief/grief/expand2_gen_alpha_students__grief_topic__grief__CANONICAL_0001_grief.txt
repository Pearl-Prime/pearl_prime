## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v01 — PERSON LOSS — MID — THROAT TIGHTENING
---
Maya sees grandma's number still in her phone. Three months now. The contact exists. The person doesn't. Her throat tightens scrolling past it. She could delete it. She doesn't. The number sits there like a door to a room that's empty. Grandma answered that number for sixteen years. Now it just rings. Maya keeps scrolling. The number stays. The throat stays tight.
