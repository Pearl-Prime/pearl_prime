## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v05 — OBJECT LOSS — LATE — EYES STINGING
---
Aisha reaches for the necklace that isn't there. Grandma's necklace. Lost somewhere between school and home. She's checked everywhere. Retraced everything. It's gone. Her eyes sting in the bathroom mirror. The necklace was the last thing grandma touched. The clasp grandma closed. Now it's in a gutter somewhere, or a trash can, or nowhere. The absence sits on her collarbone where the weight used to be.
