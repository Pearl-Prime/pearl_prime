## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v02 — SAYS IT PRIVATELY — LATE
---
Jaden stands in the backyard at dusk. Max's favorite time. The time they'd throw the ball until it was too dark to see. He doesn't have a ball. Just stands there. "I miss you, buddy." Quiet. To no one. To Max. The words land in the empty yard. Max can't hear them. That's not the point. Jaden needed to say them. The saying is the point.
