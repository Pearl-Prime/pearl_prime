

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: inherited
tier_variant: C

The stoic continuation is both a gift and a curse. The gift is that you can function. The curse is that functioning while grieving is profoundly lonely. No one sees that you are broken because you are still showing up. The visible performance of okayness prevents visible grief. The mechanism allows you to survive. But the survival comes at a very high cost.
