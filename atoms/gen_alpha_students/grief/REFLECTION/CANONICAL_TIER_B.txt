

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: inherited
tier_variant: B

Grief becomes private because the world does not pause for it. You hold the loss in the spaces between classes, in quiet moments, in the night when you are alone. The mechanism is allowing you to survive. But the survival is incomplete. The mechanism is allowing you to function, but the functioning is hollow. You are here, but you are not fully here. You are present, but you are also absent. The mechanism is keeping you moving while your insides are still. What comes to matter is the performance of okayness. The grief becomes silent. No one knows about the breaking because you have become very good at keeping going. The cost is that no one sees. The cost is that you grieve alone.
