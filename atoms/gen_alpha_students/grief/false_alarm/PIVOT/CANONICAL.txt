You felt okay for a few days and thought the grief was done. Then a song played or a memory loaded and it was back at full volume. The grief wasn't gone — it was buffering.

## PIVOT v02

---
The old frame: you cried, you processed it. The new frame: grief doesn't have a loading bar. The false alarm of one clear week in a storm of feeling is grief's favorite trick.

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Grief's false alarm is cruel: it gives you a preview of normal, then revokes access. The preview wasn't recovery. It was an ad for a version of okay you're not subscribed to yet.
