## PIVOT v01
---

---
The grief sits on top of everything else you're already carrying — school, social, family, future. You don't have bandwidth for loss on top of a full load. Nobody does.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: be strong. The new frame: you're already strong. You've been carrying a full life and now grief is an uninvited addition. The overwhelm is the honest weight.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Grief plus the normal demands of being your age creates a specific overwhelm: the loss compounds because there's never a gap in the schedule to feel it.
