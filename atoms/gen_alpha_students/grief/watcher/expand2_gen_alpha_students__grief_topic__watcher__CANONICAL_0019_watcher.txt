## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v04 — HAND ON OBJECT — MID
---
Leo picks up the card from Kenji. The paper is in his hand. He feels it—thin, sharp at the edges. He's about to drift. He doesn't. Reads the words from here. "You meant a lot to me." The words are in his hands, not behind the glass. He reads them present. They land. Not fully, but more than before.
