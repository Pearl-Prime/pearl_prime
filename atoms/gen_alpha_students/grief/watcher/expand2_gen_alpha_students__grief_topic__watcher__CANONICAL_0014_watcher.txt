## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v04 — TEAR HAPPENS — MID
---
Leo is fine at Kenji's goodbye. Composed. Watching himself hug and wish him well. Then his eyes water without permission. The tear falls before he can observe it forming. He feels it on his cheek—wet, present, real. The tear wasn't performed. It happened to him. Through him. The watcher didn't make that tear. Leo did.
