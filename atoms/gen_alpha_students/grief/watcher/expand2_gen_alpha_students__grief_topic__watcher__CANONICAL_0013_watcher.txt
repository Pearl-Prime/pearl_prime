## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v03 — BODY RECLAIMS — EARLY
---
Zoe stubs her toe on a moving box. The pain arrives immediately—sharp, here, now. Not delayed. Not muted. She's in her body. The toe throbs. She's aware of the room. Of the boxes. Of the strange apartment. The watcher distance collapses for a moment. Then returns. But the pain was real-time. She was in it.
