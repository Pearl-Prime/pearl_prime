## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v02 — NAME LANDS — LATE
---
"Jaden." Mom says it twice before he realizes it's his name. Then something catches. Not just hearing—recognizing. He's Jaden. That's him. He's in the conversation about Max, not watching it. Mom is asking if he wants to keep the collar. Jaden answers—not from autopilot, from here. The moment fades. But for five seconds, he was inside the grief, not above it.
