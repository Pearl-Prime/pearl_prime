## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v05 — SMELL BREAKS THROUGH — LATE
---
Aisha opens grandma's jewelry box. The smell. Lavender and something else. Grandma's smell. It bypasses the glass completely. She's not watching herself smell it. She's in the smell. In the memory the smell carries. The watcher can't observe a smell from distance. It's too inside. For a moment, she's inside too.
