## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v02 — FEELS THE CRYING — LATE
---
Jaden is crying about Max. He notices the watcher trying to pull back—observe the crying from above. He doesn't let it. Stays in the tears. The wet. The pressure in his throat. He's crying. Not watching himself cry. The grief hurts and he's inside the hurting. Present for his own sadness. For now.
