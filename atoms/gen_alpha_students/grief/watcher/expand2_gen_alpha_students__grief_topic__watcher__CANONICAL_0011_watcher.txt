## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01 — CRACK IN GLASS — MID
---
Maya is putting away grandma's sweater when something shifts. The wool is soft. Not soft like a fact—soft like a feeling. She's here. In this moment. Holding this sweater. For three seconds, the glass isn't there. She's not watching herself grieve. She's grieving. Then it closes. But it opened. She felt the wool. From here.
