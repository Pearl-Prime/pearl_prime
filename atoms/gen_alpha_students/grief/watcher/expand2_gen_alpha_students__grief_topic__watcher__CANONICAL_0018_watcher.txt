## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v03 — WALKS, DOESN'T FLOAT — EARLY
---
Zoe is walking through dad's apartment. She catches the drift starting—the familiar lift where her body keeps moving and she leaves it. She comes back. Feet on floor. Weight shifting. The hallway is short. She walks it inside her body. Not watching from above. Walking. When she reaches her room, she knows she walked there.
