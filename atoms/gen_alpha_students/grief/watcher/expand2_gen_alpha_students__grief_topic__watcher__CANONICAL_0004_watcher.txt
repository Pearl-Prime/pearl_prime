## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v04 — EMOTIONAL LAG — MID
---
Leo hears that Kenji is moving away. For real this time. Different state. The words land and his face stays calm. He says "oh wow" and "that's big." Twenty minutes later, alone in his room, his throat closes. The feeling arrives late—like it took the long way. He wasn't sad in the conversation. He's sad now, when Kenji can't see it. The emotion missed its moment.
