## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v03 — MEMORY GAP — EARLY
---
Zoe can't remember the last week. She moved out of the house. She must have—her stuff is here, in dad's apartment. But the week itself is a gap. She was there. She packed boxes. The evidence exists. The memory doesn't. The grief took the moving away and left her standing in a new room with no recollection of arriving.
