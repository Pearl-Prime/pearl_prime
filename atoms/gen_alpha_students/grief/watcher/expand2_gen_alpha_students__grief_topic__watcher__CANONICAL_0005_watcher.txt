## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v05 — SENSORY MUTING — LATE
---
Aisha goes through grandma's things. The necklace drawer. The jewelry that's left. She's supposed to pick something. Her hands move through the pieces. The metal has texture—she knows this theoretically. Her fingers feel muted, like touching through gloves. She picks something. Puts it on. The weight is there, somewhere, not quite reaching her. The choosing happened. She wasn't entirely present for it.
