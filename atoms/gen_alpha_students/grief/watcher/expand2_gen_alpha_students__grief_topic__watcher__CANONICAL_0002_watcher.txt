## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v02 — DELAYED SENSATION — LATE
---
Jaden's mom hugs him. He sees it happen. Feels the pressure of her arms three seconds later, muted and distant. "I know you miss him," she says. He nods. The words land eventually, like sound through water. Everything about Max's death is reaching him on delay. The vet visit happened. The empty house happened. He's receiving the news of it after the fact.
