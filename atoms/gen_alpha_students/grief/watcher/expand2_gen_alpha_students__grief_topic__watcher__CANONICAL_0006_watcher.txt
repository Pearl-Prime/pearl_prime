## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v01 — DAYS BLUR — MID
---
The week after Max died looks like one long gray day. Jaden went to school. The motions happened. Teachers talked. He answered. The days don't separate in his memory. Tuesday has no shape distinct from Thursday. He was present for all of it—technically. He was somewhere else while it happened. Time moved. He floated alongside it.
