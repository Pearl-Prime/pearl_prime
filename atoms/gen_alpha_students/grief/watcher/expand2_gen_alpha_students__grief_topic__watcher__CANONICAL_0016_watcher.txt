## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v01 — STAYS PRESENT — MID
---
Maya is folding grandma's things. She notices herself starting to drift—the familiar float upward. She doesn't follow. Keeps her hands on the fabric. The texture. The buttons. She stays in her fingers. One sweater, fully present. The grief is here, in the folding. She folds it from inside. Then moves to the next item. That's enough for now.
