## PIVOT v01
---

---
Your worth is recalculated by every interaction — likes, grades, comments, reactions. The overwhelm of being perpetually evaluated by everyone, everywhere, always, is specific to your generation.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: just be yourself. The new frame: yourself is being assessed in real time by an audience you can't fully see. The overwhelm is the correct response to that level of exposure.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Self-worth overwhelm looks like trying to be enough in every arena simultaneously: school, social media, friend groups, family. The overwhelm is what happens when your value has no offline mode.
