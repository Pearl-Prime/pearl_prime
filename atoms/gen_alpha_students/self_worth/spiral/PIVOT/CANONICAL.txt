## PIVOT v01
---

---
One negative comment becomes proof of worthlessness becomes certainty that everyone secretly agrees. The self-worth spiral doesn't stop at one opinion — it crowdsources the judgment.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The spiral runs from a single rejection to a life sentence: this means I'm not enough, which means I was never enough, which means everyone who was nice was just being polite.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Self-worth spirals go total: from one bad interaction to universal unworthiness. The spiral doesn't do nuance. It does absolutes.
---
