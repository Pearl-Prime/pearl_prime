## RECOGNITION v05 - friend replied short
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/recognition/micro/v05.txt
Aisha stares at the message. 9:34pm. Just "ok." One word. From her best friend. The conversation was normal. She asked about tomorrow. Got "ok." Now her mind replays everything. The lunch table. The walk to class. Did she say something wrong? Her pulse quickens. Her palms go damp. She scrolls up. Reads the whole thread. Nothing bad. Nothing different. Her friend is probably just tired. Probably doing homework. But Aisha's body has already decided something is wrong. The phone feels heavy. The bed feels wrong. Everything is fine. Her body hasn't gotten the message.
