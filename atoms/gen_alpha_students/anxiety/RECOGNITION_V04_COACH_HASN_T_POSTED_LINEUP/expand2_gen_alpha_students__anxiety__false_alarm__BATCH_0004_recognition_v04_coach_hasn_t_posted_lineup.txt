## RECOGNITION v04 - coach hasn't posted lineup
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/recognition/micro/v04.txt
Tyler checks the team app. 7:08am. Then 7:12. Then 7:19. The lineup isn't posted yet. Practice was fine. He did everything right. Twenty-three drills. No mistakes. But his shoulders climb toward his ears. His breakfast sits heavy. The screen shows nothing new. Just yesterday's schedule. He locks the phone. Unlocks it. Checks again. His body braces for bad news that doesn't exist. The lineup will come when it comes. He knows this. His nervous system doesn't.
