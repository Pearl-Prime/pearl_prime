## EMBODIMENT v02 - message left alone
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
Aisha sends the text. 4:08pm. Her thumb hovers over the chat. Waiting for the read receipt. She catches herself. Sets the phone on the desk. Screen down. The urge to check pulls at her. She opens her notebook instead. Starts the first problem. The pull is still there. Quieter now. She finishes the problem. Then the next. At 4:31, she picks up the phone. Three messages waiting. Normal. Fine. She didn't need to watch it happen. It happened anyway.
