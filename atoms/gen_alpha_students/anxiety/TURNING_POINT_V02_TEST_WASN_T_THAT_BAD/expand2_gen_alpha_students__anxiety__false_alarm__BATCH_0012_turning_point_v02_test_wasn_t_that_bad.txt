## TURNING_POINT v02 - test wasn't that bad
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
Leo was certain he failed. The math test. He felt sick for three days waiting. Couldn't eat breakfast. Checked the portal forty times. The grade posted Thursday afternoon. Eighty-seven. He stared at the number. His body had been screaming emergency. The emergency was an eighty-seven. He'd worried himself hollow over a B-plus. Something shifts. Small. Uncomfortable. What if the feeling doesn't match the truth? What if his body is loud, but not right? He doesn't like the question. But he can't unfeel it now.
