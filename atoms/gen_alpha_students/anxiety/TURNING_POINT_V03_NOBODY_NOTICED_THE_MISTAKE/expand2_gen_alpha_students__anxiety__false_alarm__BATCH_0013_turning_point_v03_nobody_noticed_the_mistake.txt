## TURNING_POINT v03 - nobody noticed the mistake
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v03.txt
Maya tripped during the presentation. Just a small stumble over a word. She felt her face burn. Knew everyone saw. Knew they'd remember forever. She sat down shaking. Waited for the whispers. Nothing came. The next person presented. Class moved on. Nobody mentioned it at lunch. Or after school. Nobody remembered because it wasn't worth remembering. Her body had screamed catastrophe. Reality shrugged. She wonders now: how many catastrophes were just this? Just moments that passed and meant nothing? The thought makes her dizzy. Like the ground moved.
