## PIVOT v01
---

---
One awkward comment becomes social ruin becomes total isolation. Your mind speedruns the worst-case scenario like it's going for a world record. That's not thinking — that's anxiety on autopilot.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The spiral runs the same speedrun every time: small mistake to catastrophic consequence. What if the speedrun itself is the pattern worth interrupting? The path isn't inevitable just because it's fast.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You think you're preparing for the worst when you spiral. You're actually living every worst case in real time. The cost: you experience disasters that never happen, and the real ones still surprise you.
---
