## MECHANISM_PROOF v04 - door lock
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v04.txt
Zoe locks the front door. Her parents aren't home yet. She tests the handle. Locked. Walks to the kitchen. Comes back. Tests again. Locked. She sits on the couch. Gets up. Tests the handle. Still locked. The door hasn't unlocked itself. Doors don't do that. She knows this. She tests it anyway. Seven times in twelve minutes. Each test proves it's locked. Each test creates the need for another test. The door is always locked. She never believes it.
