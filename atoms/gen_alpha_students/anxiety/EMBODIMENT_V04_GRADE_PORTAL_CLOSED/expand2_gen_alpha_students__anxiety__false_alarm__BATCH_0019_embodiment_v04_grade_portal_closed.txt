## EMBODIMENT v04 - grade portal closed
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v04.txt
Maya finishes the test. Submits it. The grade will post when it posts. Her fingers drift toward the bookmark. The portal. She stops. Closes the laptop lid instead. Makes a snack. Watches something short on the screen in the living room. The itch to check sits in her stomach. She doesn't scratch it. The number will exist whether she watches for it or not. Waiting won't change it. At 7pm, she looks once. Ninety-one. She nods. Closes it. Eats dinner. The world didn't end in the hours she wasn't watching.
