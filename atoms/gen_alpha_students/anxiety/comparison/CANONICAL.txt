COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Maya finishes her math test and looks left. The girl next to her is already done—pencil down, paper flipped, hands folded. Maya has three problems left. The comparison fires: she's faster, you're slower, faster means smarter. The comparison doesn't account for the girl skipping two problems. The comparison doesn't account for Maya checking her work. The comparison takes one data point—pencil down versus pencil still moving—and converts it into a ranking. The ranking has two positions: better and worse. Maya is in the worse position. She rushes through the last three problems. The rushing is the comparison's product. The checking was the better strategy.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Maya maps who she compares herself to. Not the kids who struggle more than her—never downward. Always upward. The girl who finishes first. The boy who gets the highest grade. The friend with the neatest handwriting. The comparison selects the person who's best at the specific thing and measures Maya against that person. The measurement always finds Maya lacking because the benchmark is always the top performer. She's comparing her math to the best math student, her writing to the best writer, her art to the best artist. She's competing against a composite person who doesn't exist—a person who's best at everything simultaneously.

The volume difference is the comparison's operating system. Others' wins are signals. His wins are anomalies.

## TURNING_POINT v01

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Maya finishes the test at her own pace. The girl next to her has been done for five minutes. The comparison fires: slower, worse, behind. Maya looks at her paper. Every problem is answered. Every answer is checked. She counts: zero skipped problems, two corrections from checking. The corrections are the product of the pace the comparison called slow.
