## TURNING_POINT v05 - they didn't cancel plans
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v05.txt
Zoe was sure they'd cancel. The weekend plans. Every silence felt like proof. Every short reply a sign. She spent Friday night waiting for the text. The apology. The excuse. Saturday morning, her phone buzzed. "On my way." They came. They showed up. Laughing. Normal. Like nothing was wrong. Because nothing was wrong. Zoe stood in her doorway, heart still braced for rejection that never arrived. Maybe, she thinks, my body sends warnings about things that aren't real. Maybe the feeling isn't the same as the truth.
