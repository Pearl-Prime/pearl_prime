Maya starts counting. Every time her phone buzzes and her stomach drops, she checks: was the message actually bad? Monday: three buzzes, three drops, zero bad messages. Tuesday: four buzzes, four drops, zero bad messages. By Friday she has nineteen drops and zero bad messages. The alarm fired nineteen times and was wrong nineteen times. Maya's phone buzzes. Stomach drops. She reads the text: "can you bring chips tomorrow." She looks at the drop and the text and speaks to the gap between them: "That's chips." The speaking is new.
