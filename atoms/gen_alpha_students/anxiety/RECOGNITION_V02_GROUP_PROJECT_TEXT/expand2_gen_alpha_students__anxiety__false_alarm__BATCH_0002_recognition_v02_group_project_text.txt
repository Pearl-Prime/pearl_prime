## RECOGNITION v02 - group project text
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/recognition/micro/v02.txt
Jaden sees the notification. 11:23pm. The group chat. Someone asking about the project. He hasn't done anything wrong. His part is finished. Sitting in his folder. Ready. But his stomach drops anyway. His jaw tightens. He reads the message four times. It's just a question. A normal question. His thumb hovers over the keyboard. Fifty-two seconds pass. He puts the phone face down on the bed. His heart races like he's in trouble. He's not in trouble. The project is fine. Everything is fine. His body doesn't know that.
