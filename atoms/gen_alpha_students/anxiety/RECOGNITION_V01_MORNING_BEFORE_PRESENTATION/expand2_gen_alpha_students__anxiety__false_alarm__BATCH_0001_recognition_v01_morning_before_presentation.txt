## RECOGNITION v01 - morning before presentation
---
path: gen_alpha_students/anxiety/false_alarm/recognition/micro/v01.txt
It's 6:47am. Maya is already awake. The presentation isn't until fourth period. Nothing is wrong yet. But her chest is tight. Her hands are cold. She checks the slides again. Nineteen times since yesterday. The screen glows in the dark room. Her body screams danger. There is no danger. Just a Tuesday. Just a presentation she's done three times already. She pulls the blanket up. Her heart pounds like something is chasing her. Nothing is chasing her. The door is closed. The house is quiet. Her body doesn't believe it.
