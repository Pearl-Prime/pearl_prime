COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Theo hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: chest locks. The system overload pattern has been running for so long Theo stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Theo's palms sweat. The old narrative about somatic healing says this is weakness. But Theo looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. Sees how the frame keeps Theo locked in a loop that feels like safety but functions as a cage. The frame shifts. Not dramatically. A hairline fracture in the foundation. Theo notices the fracture. Does not repair it. Lets it widen. The system overload pattern stumbles. For the first time, Theo sees the pattern from outside.

Lila breaks. The system overload pattern reaches maximum intensity and something gives. A shiver runs through Lila. Every system fires at once. The somatic healing is no longer background noise. How the system overload feeds the somatic healing feeds the system overload feeds the collapse. Lila sees the loop from inside the loop's core. The frame doesn't shift gently. It shatters. What replaces it is not comfort. It is clarity. Lila stands in the wreckage of the old story and feels the ground beneath it. Solid. Different. The old frame is debris now. Lila does not rebuild it.

Dex stands in the heat and does not flinch. The system overload pattern demands the old response. Dex's breastbone aches. The somatic healing presses hard. Dex's feet root. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Dex makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Dex pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Dex is now. The system overload pattern fades to background noise.

Lila's fists clench. The somatic healing activates and the system overload pattern responds on schedule. But this time Lila traces the mechanism back to its origin. Sees the protective logic. The system overload started as defense. Lila learned that controlling everything prevented collapse. The defense became automatic. The automatic became identity. Lila's stomach drops. The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Dex's jaw drops. The somatic healing and the system overload pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. A shiver runs through Dex. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Dex sees the entire architecture: how the system overload creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Dex sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Dex that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
