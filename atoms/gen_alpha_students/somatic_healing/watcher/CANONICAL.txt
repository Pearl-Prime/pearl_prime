---
Ezra hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: gut twists. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern has been running for so long Ezra stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Ezra's hair stands. The old narrative about somatic healing says this is weakness. But Ezra looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Everything collapses at once. Elke's knees weaken. The somatic healing detonates and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern amplifies it past capacity. Elke's palms sweat. The crisis does not resolve. But the frame through which Elke sees the crisis transforms completely. There is no going back to the old interpretation. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern has burned through its own container.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The hypervigilance and scanning pattern runs hot and Elke watches it run. Elke's shoulders curl forward. The mechanism is clear: the somatic healing activates the hypervigilance and scanning, the hypervigilance and scanning generates a protective response, the protective response costs more than doing nothing. Elke's grip tightens. Elke sees why the pattern persists. It persists because it works. Not well. Not efficiently. But it works. It reduces the immediate threat by creating a long-term drain. Elke understands this trade-off for the first time while the pattern is active. The heat doesn't diminish. The understanding doesn't soothe. But Elke sees the logic. The pattern is not irrational. It is a survival strategy that has outlived its context.

The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Arlo's grip tightens. The somatic healing and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. Arlo's upper back seizes. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Arlo sees the entire architecture: how the hypervigilance and scanning creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Arlo sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Arlo that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
