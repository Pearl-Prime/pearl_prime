## PIVOT v01
---

---
The thing you need to do feels enormous because you're seeing all the steps at once. Courage doesn't need a complete map. It needs one honest step. The overwhelm wants you to believe otherwise.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You see every possible consequence of being brave and it paralyzes you. The overwhelm is real. But it's also the mind's way of keeping you in the safe zone.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Courage doesn't require you to solve everything at once. The overwhelm says it does because overwhelm is the guardian of staying the same.
---
