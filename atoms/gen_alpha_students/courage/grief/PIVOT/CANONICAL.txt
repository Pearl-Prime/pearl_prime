## PIVOT v01
---

---
The courage to change means grieving what you'll lose: the group, the dynamic, the version of yourself that was safe because it was small. Change requires a funeral first.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Underneath the fear of being real is grief for all the times you weren't. All the moments you stayed small to stay safe. Courage asks you to mourn those before you can move forward.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The grief in courage is specific: it's for the comfort of the familiar. Even when the familiar is hurting you, leaving it means losing something you know how to navigate.
---
