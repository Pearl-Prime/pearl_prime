## PIVOT v01
---

---
Everything feels equally heavy. Homework and getting dressed carry the same impossible weight. The overwhelm of depression isn't about the tasks — it's about the energy it takes to exist.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: just start small. The new frame: when your brain is running on 10% battery, even small feels enormous. The overwhelm isn't about size. It's about capacity.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Depression overwhelm looks like paralysis. Not because nothing matters, but because everything costs the same amount of energy you don't have.
