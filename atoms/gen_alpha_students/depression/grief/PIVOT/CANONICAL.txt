## PIVOT v01
---

---
Underneath the flatness is grief for the version of you that felt things fully. The excitement, the curiosity, the caring. Depression didn't delete them — it muted them.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You're not just depressed. You're mourning the aliveness you used to have. The version of you who could get excited about a new game, a new friend, a new anything.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The grief in depression is for the feelings themselves. You miss missing things. You miss caring. The absence of feeling is its own kind of loss.
---
