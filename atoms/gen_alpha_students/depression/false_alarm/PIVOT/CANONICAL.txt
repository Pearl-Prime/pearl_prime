## PIVOT v01
---

---
You had one good day and thought the gray was gone. It wasn't gone — it was buffering. The false alarm of one clear day in a fog is depression's cruelest feature.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: if you can still laugh, you're fine. The new frame: laughing and being depressed coexist all the time. The ability to perform okay is the false alarm that delays getting help.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
One good weekend doesn't mean the depression was a phase. It means the depression took a weekend off. The false alarm keeps you from building the support you actually need.
---
