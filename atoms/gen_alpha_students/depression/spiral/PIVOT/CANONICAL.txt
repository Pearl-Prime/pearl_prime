## PIVOT v01
---

---
The flatness becomes evidence that something is fundamentally wrong with you becomes certainty that nothing will ever change. Depression spirals are quiet — they don't scream, they whisper permanently.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Depression spirals don't look like anxiety spirals. They're slow, heavy, and certain. Each thought closes a door: this won't change, nothing helps, this is just how it is now.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral into permanence: I'll always feel this way. The spiral is depression's marketing department, selling you on hopelessness as a fact instead of a symptom.
---
