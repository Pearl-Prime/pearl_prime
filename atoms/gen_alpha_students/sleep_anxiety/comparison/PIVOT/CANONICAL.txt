---
Your friend falls asleep in minutes. You've been staring at the ceiling for two hours. The comparison turns sleep into another thing you're failing at, which makes it harder to sleep.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: normal people just sleep. The new frame: your nervous system was trained by late-night scrolling, notification alerts, and a world that's always on. The comparison is between a phone in the drawer and a phone under the pillow.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You compare your restless nights to everyone who seems to sleep effortlessly. Sleep anxiety loves comparison because every comparison adds another layer of wakefulness.
