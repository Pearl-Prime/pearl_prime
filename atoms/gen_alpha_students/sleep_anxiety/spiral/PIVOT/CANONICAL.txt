## PIVOT v01
---

---
It's midnight. You calculate: if I fall asleep now, I get six hours. Five. Four. The countdown is the spiral, and the spiral is what keeps you counting.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The sleep spiral runs a familiar loop: can't sleep, tomorrow will be terrible, the terrible-ness makes me more anxious, the anxiety keeps me awake. The loop is the problem and the product.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral about the sleep itself: one bad night becomes a pattern becomes a permanent condition becomes a ruined life. The spiral takes one night and makes it forever.
---
