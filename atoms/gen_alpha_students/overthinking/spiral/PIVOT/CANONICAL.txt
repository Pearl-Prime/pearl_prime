## PIVOT v01
---

---
One thought spawns ten and those ten spawn a hundred. The overthinking spiral doesn't escalate in intensity — it escalates in volume. More thoughts, not bigger ones.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The overthinking spiral is different from the anxiety spiral. Anxiety goes catastrophic. Overthinking goes fractal — every thought splits into more thoughts until the original question is buried.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You started with a simple question and now you're twelve layers deep in hypotheticals. The spiral isn't going anywhere — it's going everywhere, and that's the problem.
