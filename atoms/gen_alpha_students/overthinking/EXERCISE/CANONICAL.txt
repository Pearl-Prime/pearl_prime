## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Brain spinning about what-ifs? Stop. Look around. What do you see? Hear? Actually feel right now. That's real.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Get up. Jump, dance, run around. Move your body. Interrupt the thought loop physically. Your brain stops spiraling.
---

---
The conversation happened. You can't redo it. Right now you're fine. Future hasn't happened yet. Stay in now.
