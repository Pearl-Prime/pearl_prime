## PIVOT v01
---

---
One unexpected expense becomes can't cover it becomes falling behind becomes everything collapsing. The financial spiral runs on the logic of scarcity, where every setback is existential.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The stress spiral treats every money problem as evidence of total failure. One tight week becomes this is how it'll always be. The spiral doesn't check the calendar — it only checks the balance.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral from one cost to total catastrophe. The spiral's math has no resilience built in, no recovery timeline, no base rate for things working out. Just worst case, always.
