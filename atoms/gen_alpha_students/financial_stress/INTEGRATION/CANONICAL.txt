You know something. You've seen a parent worried about money in a way that lands in your nervous system. You've felt the financial alarm that lives in your house. You've heard the conversations that weren't meant for you. And your body absorbed it. Turned it into its own kind of background threat. Into the knowledge that there isn't enough. More aware of the cost of things. More afraid of taking up space.
That weight in your body. That's not you being dramatic. That's you absorbing the real stress of the people who raised you.
Your body carries the family stress.
