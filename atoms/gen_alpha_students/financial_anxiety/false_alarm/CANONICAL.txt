Zoe gets a bill in the mail. Her body responds like it's an emergency. Her chest tightens. Her hands go cold. She opens it. The bill is reasonable. It's expected. But her nervous system treated it like disaster. The false alarm is about money. Every financial notification triggers crisis. A receipt from the bookstore. An email about tuition. A notification that rent is due. All false alarms. All treated as catastrophe. Her hands shake.
