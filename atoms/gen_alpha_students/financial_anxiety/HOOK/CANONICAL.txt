

## HOOK v01
---

---
Jordan is in the school hallway between classes, phone in hand, stomach dropping at the group chat about the trip — the family account balance is visible in the banking app and the number does not clear the deposit.
---

## HOOK v02
---

---
You see the price of the trip in the group chat and your stomach does the drop it has learned to do, because you already know the answer at home and you already know how it will feel to say it.
---

## HOOK v03
---

---
Everyone is talking about the new thing and you are doing the quiet kid math — what it costs, what is left, what you will pretend you did not want.
---

## HOOK v04
---

---
You overheard the conversation through the wall again, the one about bills with the voices kept low, and now you carry it to school in a backpack no one can see.
---

## HOOK v05
---

---
The teacher says just ask your parents and you nod, because explaining that asking is the hard part would take a kind of grown-up you did not choose to become this early.
