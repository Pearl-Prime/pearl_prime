Leo's brain offers the thought: That look meant something bad. He feels the familiar pull downward. The invitation to build a story. He lets the thought exist. Doesn't add to it. Doesn't confirm or deny. Just: that's a thought. He goes back to his book. The thought fades like a sound he stopped paying attention to. It might come back. Probably will. But right now, it's just noise. Not truth.
