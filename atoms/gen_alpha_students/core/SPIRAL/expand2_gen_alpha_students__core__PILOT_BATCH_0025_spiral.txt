## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/spiral/recognition/micro/v05.txt
Aisha got a question wrong. In front of everyone. 2:15pm. The teacher moved on immediately. No one laughed. But Aisha's mind hasn't moved on. The wrong answer plays again. And again. Her face was hot. Is still hot. What if everyone thinks I'm stupid now? What if the teacher thinks I don't try? What if this goes on my record somehow? Her pen taps the desk. The next lesson is happening. She's not in it. She's still in the moment from forty minutes ago.
