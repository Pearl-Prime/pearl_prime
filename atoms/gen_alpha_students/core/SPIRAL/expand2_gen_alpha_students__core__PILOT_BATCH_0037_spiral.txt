Aisha notices the first thought. They didn't laugh at my joke. The second thought tries to arrive: They think I'm— She catches it. Pauses. Takes a breath. The chain breaks. Not completely. The echo is still there. But she doesn't follow it. She asks her friend a question instead. The conversation moves on. The spiral had a doorway. This time, she didn't walk through.
