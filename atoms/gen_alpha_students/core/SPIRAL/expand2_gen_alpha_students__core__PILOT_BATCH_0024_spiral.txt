Tyler made a mistake in practice. 4:47pm. Missed one catch. Just one. Now he can't stop seeing it. The ball slipping. His hands too slow. His brain loops it: What if the coach noticed? What if I get benched? What if I'm not as good as I thought? His shoulders tense. He's been sitting in the car for eight minutes. His mom is waiting. He can't stop replaying the moment. The ball falling. His hands empty. One mistake, but his mind made it a thousand.
