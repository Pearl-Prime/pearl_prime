Aisha catches herself mid-loop. 11:17pm. She's been replaying the same moment for an hour. The same what-ifs. The same conclusions. Then something shifts. She notices she's thought this exact sequence before. Last week. About something different. And that thing turned out fine. The thought interrupts the pattern. Just barely. What if the loop isn't finding truth? What if it's just... looping? She doesn't have an answer. But the question cracks something open.
