## SPIRAL / EMBODIMENT v04
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/spiral/embodiment/micro/v04.txt
Maya catches the loop at thought three. Earlier than before. She recognizes the shape. The way one worry grabs the next. She puts her hand on her chest. Feels her heartbeat. Fast, but slowing. The thoughts don't disappear. But she stops feeding them. Stops building. The spiral collapses without new material. She finishes dinner. The thoughts are background noise now. Present but not in charge.
