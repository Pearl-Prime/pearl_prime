Zoe says it out loud. "I think I'm making this up." Her voice sounds strange in the quiet room. The thought was so loud inside. Out loud, it sounds like what it is: a guess. Not evidence. Not proof. Just her brain filling silence with stories. She doesn't know if the story is true. But saying it out loud made it smaller. Made room for other possibilities.
