## SPIRAL / MECHANISM_PROOF v04
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v04.txt
Zoe sees her friends laughing without her. Just for a moment. That becomes: They're closer without me. Which becomes: I'm the outsider. Which becomes: I've always been the outsider. Which becomes: Maybe I don't really have friends at all. By dinner, she can't remember what they were laughing about. She only remembers the story she made from it. The story that started small and swallowed the afternoon.
