## SPIRAL / RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/spiral/recognition/micro/v01.txt
It's 11:43pm. Maya lies in bed. One thought arrives: What if I said something weird at lunch? The thought doesn't leave. It invites another. What if they noticed? What if they talked about it after? What if that's why the group chat was quiet? Each thought pulls the next. Her chest tightens. She turns over. The thoughts keep coming. Faster now. What if tomorrow is worse? What if everyone knows something she doesn't? Her body is tired. Her mind won't stop. The ceiling stares back.
