## SPIRAL / TURNING_POINT v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/spiral/turning_point/micro/v03.txt
Maya has been spinning for forty minutes. Same loop. Same conclusions. Then her phone buzzes. A meme from the friend she was sure hated her. Just a stupid meme. But it doesn't fit the story she's been building. The story said this friend was done with her. The meme says otherwise. She stares at it. The loop stutters. Maybe the story was wrong. Maybe she made it up entirely.
