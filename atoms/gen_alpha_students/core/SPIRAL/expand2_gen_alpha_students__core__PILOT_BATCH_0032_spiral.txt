Leo writes down the thought. Just to see it. "Everyone is tired of me." He stares at the words. They look different on paper. Smaller. More like a guess than a fact. He remembers thinking the same thing a month ago. Before the birthday party where everyone showed up. Before the group project where they picked him first. The thought felt true then too. But it wasn't. Maybe it isn't now either.
