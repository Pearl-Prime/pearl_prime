## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v02
---

Leo stubs his toe on the doorframe. 6:33pm. Sharp pain. Immediate. For two seconds, there's no distance. Just pain. Just him in his body, feeling it. Then the watching starts again. But those two seconds stay with him. He was there. Actually there. Not observing. Just being. If pain can pull him back, maybe other things can too.
