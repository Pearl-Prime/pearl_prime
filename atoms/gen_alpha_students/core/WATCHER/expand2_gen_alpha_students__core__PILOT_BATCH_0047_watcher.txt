## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
Maya watches herself do homework. The pen moves. Notes appear. She sees it happening. Observes the process. Three hours pass. The homework is done. She doesn't remember doing it. Just watched it get done. Watched her hand write. Watched pages turn. Tomorrow she'll watch herself turn it in. Watch the teacher collect it. Watch herself sit back down. The watching never stops. The doing never starts.
