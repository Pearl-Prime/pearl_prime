Tyler watches himself celebrate the win. The team cheers. He cheers too. He sees his arms go up. Hears his voice join the noise. But he's watching from somewhere else. The joy is happening to his body. His face is smiling. His heart should be full. He watches it all from behind the glass. Everyone else seems inside the moment. He's watching the moment from outside. Same win. Different experience.
