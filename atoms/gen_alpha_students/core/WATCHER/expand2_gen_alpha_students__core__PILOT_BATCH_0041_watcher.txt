## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/recognition/micro/v01.txt
It's 7:22pm. Maya sits at the table. Her family is talking. She can hear them. See their mouths moving. But she's not really there. She's watching herself sit there. Watching herself nod. Watching herself push food around the plate. Like she's behind glass. Present but not participating. Her brother asks her something. She answers. The words come out right. But she doesn't feel like she said them. She's just watching it happen.
