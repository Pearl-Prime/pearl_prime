## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v04
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/recognition/micro/v04.txt
Tyler is at practice. 5:08pm. Running drills. His body knows what to do. It does the movements. He watches from somewhere else. Sees his arms, his legs, his teammates. It all looks familiar. But distant. Like watching through fog. The coach yells something. Tyler responds. The right response. But he doesn't feel connected to it. Just a witness to his own actions.
