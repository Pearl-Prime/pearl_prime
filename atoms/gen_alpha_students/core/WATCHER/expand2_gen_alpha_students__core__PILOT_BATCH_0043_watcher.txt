## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/recognition/micro/v03.txt
Zoe is in the group project meeting. 3:45pm. Her screen is on. Camera on. She's nodding at the right times. Adding comments when expected. But she's not really in the conversation. She's watching herself be in the conversation. Noticing her own face in the corner. Hearing her own voice like it belongs to someone else. The meeting ends. She closes the laptop. She was there the whole time. She doesn't remember any of it.
