## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
Aisha is watching herself read when something shifts. 8:14pm. A sentence in the book. A character describing exactly this. The distance. The glass wall. She stops. Reads it again. Someone else felt this too. Enough to write it down. She's been watching herself for so long, she forgot there might be another option. The book sits in her hands. For a second, she's not watching herself hold it. She's just holding it.
