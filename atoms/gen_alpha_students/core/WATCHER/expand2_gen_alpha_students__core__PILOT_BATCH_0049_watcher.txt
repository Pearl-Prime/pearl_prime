## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v04
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v04.txt
Zoe goes to her friend's house. Watches herself knock. Watches herself smile when the door opens. Watches herself sit on the familiar couch. Her friend talks. Zoe watches herself respond. The conversation flows. Laughter happens. She sees it all. From a step back. A layer removed. The visit ends. She watches herself wave goodbye. Later, she'll barely remember what they talked about. She just remembers watching.
