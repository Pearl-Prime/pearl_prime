## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
Jaden hugs his mom. 6:12pm. He decides to stay in it. Not watch. Just feel. Her arms. The smell of dinner on her sweater. The pressure of the hug. Five seconds of actual contact. No distance. No glass. When he pulls back, the watching tries to return. But something is softer now. The door is easier to find. He was there. He can be there again.
