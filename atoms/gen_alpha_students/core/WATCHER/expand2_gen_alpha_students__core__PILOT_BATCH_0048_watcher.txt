## WATCHER / MECHANISM_PROOF v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v03.txt
Jaden attends three classes. Watches himself take notes. Watches himself raise his hand. Watches himself answer. The day happens around him. He's in all of it. None of it touches him. At 3pm, the bell rings. He watches himself pack up. Walk out. The whole day played like a movie. He was the main character. But he was also in the audience. Watching from behind his own eyes.
