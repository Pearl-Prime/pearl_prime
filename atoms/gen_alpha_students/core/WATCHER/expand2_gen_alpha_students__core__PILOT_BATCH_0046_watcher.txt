Leo watches himself get ready for school. Brushing teeth. Packing bag. He's seen this scene before. Every morning. From the same distance. He watches himself eat breakfast. Watches himself say goodbye. Watches himself walk to the bus. The routine plays out. He's in every frame. And behind every frame, watching. Days blur together. Same motions. Same distance. He's always watching. Never quite arriving.
