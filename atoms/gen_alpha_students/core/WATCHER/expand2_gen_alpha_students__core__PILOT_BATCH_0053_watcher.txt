Maya laughs. Really laughs. 7:41pm. Something her brother said. For six seconds, she's not watching herself laugh. She's just laughing. Body shaking. Eyes watering. Then it passes. The watching returns. But the memory stays. There's a place where she's not behind the glass. She's been there. Just now. Just for a moment. The glass isn't permanent.
