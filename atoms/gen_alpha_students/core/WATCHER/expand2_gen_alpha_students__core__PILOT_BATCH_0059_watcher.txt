## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v04
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/embodiment/micro/v04.txt
Maya walks home. 3:47pm. She notices her feet on the sidewalk. The rhythm. Left. Right. Left. She stays with it. Doesn't narrate. Doesn't observe from above. Just walks. Feels her legs move. Her lungs breathe. The air on her face. Two blocks of just walking. Present and accounted for. She reaches her door. The watching returns. But she walked here. Actually walked. Not watched herself walk.
