Aisha takes a bite of the apple. 12:22pm. Notices the crunch. The sweetness. The cold from the fridge. She stays with it. Not watching herself eat. Just eating. Tasting. Being a person with an apple. The lunch table noise fades. For a minute, she's not behind glass. She's just here. Eating an apple. When she finishes, the distance returns. But gentler now. She found the door. She can find it again.
