Jaden is petting his dog when he notices. 5:52pm. The fur under his fingers. The warmth. The weight against his leg. He's not watching himself pet the dog. He's just... there. In the moment. With the dog. No narration. No distance. It lasts maybe thirty seconds. Then the watching returns. But now he knows: the watching isn't all there is.
