Zoe cries. 10:08pm. Something cracked open. For a few minutes, she's not watching herself cry. She's just crying. The sadness moves through her without observation. Without commentary. Raw and present. When it slows, the watching comes back. But something's different. She felt that. Actually felt it. Not from a distance. Maybe the glass has cracks she didn't notice before.
