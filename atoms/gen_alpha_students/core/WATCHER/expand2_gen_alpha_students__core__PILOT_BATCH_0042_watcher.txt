## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v02
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/watcher/recognition/micro/v02.txt
Jaden walks through the hallway. 12:14pm. People everywhere. Laughing. Talking. He sees it all. Registers it. But he's floating above it somehow. Not in his body. More like watching a video of himself walking. His legs move. His face makes expressions. But he's not steering. Just observing. Someone waves. He waves back. The motion feels automatic. Like watching his own hand from very far away.
