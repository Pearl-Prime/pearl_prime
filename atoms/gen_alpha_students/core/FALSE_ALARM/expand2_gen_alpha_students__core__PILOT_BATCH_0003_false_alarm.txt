## FALSE_ALARM / RECOGNITION v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/recognition/micro/v03.txt
Zoe refreshes the page. 3:14pm. Still nothing. The assignment was three days ago. She knew the material. She studied for six hours. But her hands shake when she clicks. Her breath catches. The screen loads slow. White screen. Then numbers. Other classes. Not this one. Not yet. She closes the laptop. Opens it again. Refreshes. Her body acts like something terrible is coming. Like the number will change who she is. She knew the answers. Her chest doesn't care what she knows.
