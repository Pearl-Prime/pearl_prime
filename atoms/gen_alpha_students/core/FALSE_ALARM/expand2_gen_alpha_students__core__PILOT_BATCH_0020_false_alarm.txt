## FALSE_ALARM / EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
Jaden gets the text. 8:47pm. "k." One letter. His mind starts building the story. She's mad. Something's wrong. He notices it building. Stops. Reads the message again. Just "k." Maybe she's busy. Maybe she's tired. Maybe it means exactly nothing. He doesn't spiral. Just sets the phone aside. Finishes his game. At 9:12, she sends a meme. A normal meme. He laughs. Sends one back. The "k" was just a "k." His brain had almost made it a crisis. This time, he let it be small.
