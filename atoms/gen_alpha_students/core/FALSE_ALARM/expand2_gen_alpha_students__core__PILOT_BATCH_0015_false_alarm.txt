## FALSE_ALARM / TURNING_POINT v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v05.txt
Zoe was sure they'd cancel. The weekend plans. Every silence felt like proof. Every short reply a sign. She spent Friday night waiting for the text. The apology. The excuse. Saturday morning, her phone buzzed. "On my way." They came. Laughing. Normal. Like nothing was wrong. Because nothing was wrong. Zoe stood in her doorway, heart still braced for rejection that never arrived. Maybe the feeling isn't the same as the truth.
