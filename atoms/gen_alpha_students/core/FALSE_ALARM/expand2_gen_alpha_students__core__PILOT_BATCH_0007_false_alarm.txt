Maya zips the backpack. Homework inside. She saw it go in. She unzips. Checks. It's there. Zips again. Walks to the door. Stops. Unzips. Checks. Still there. The paper hasn't moved. It can't move. She zips the bag. Takes three steps. Her hands reach for the zipper again. Fourth time. Fifth. The homework is exactly where she put it. It will still be there tomorrow. She checks one more time. Just to be sure. Just once more.
