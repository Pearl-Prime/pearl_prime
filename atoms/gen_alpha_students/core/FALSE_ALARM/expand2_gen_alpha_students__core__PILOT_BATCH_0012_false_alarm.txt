## FALSE_ALARM / TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
Leo was certain he failed. The math assessment. He felt sick for three days waiting. Couldn't eat breakfast. Checked the screen forty times. The score posted Thursday afternoon. Eighty-seven. He stared at the number. His body had been screaming emergency. The emergency was an eighty-seven. He'd worried himself hollow over a B-plus. Something shifts. Small. Uncomfortable. What if the feeling doesn't match the truth? What if his body is loud, but not right?
