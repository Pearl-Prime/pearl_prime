## FALSE_ALARM / EMBODIMENT v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v03.txt
Leo puts the homework in the backpack. Zips it closed. Walks to the door. The old pull comes. Check it. Make sure. He pauses. Feels the strap on his shoulder. The weight is there. The paper is inside. He knows this. He doesn't open the bag. Just walks to the kitchen. Eats breakfast. The urge fades to background static. Still there, but smaller. The homework didn't disappear. It was never going to.
