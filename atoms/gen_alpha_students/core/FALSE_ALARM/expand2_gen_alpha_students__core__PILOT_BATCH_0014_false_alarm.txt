## FALSE_ALARM / TURNING_POINT v04
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v04.txt
Jaden dreaded telling his dad about the missing assignment. Built it up for days. Practiced apologies. Imagined the disappointment. The lecture. Finally said it at dinner, braced for impact. His dad just nodded. "Okay. Get it done tomorrow." That was it. Jaden's whole body had prepared for devastation. Got a nod instead. He sits in his room after, confused. The fear was so real. So certain. But it made something up. It guessed wrong.
