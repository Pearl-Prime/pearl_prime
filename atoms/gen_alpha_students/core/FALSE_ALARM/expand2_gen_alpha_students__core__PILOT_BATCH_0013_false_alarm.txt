## FALSE_ALARM / TURNING_POINT v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v03.txt
Maya tripped during the presentation. Just a small stumble over a word. She felt her face burn. Knew everyone saw. Knew they'd remember forever. She sat down shaking. Waited for the whispers. Nothing came. The next person presented. Class moved on. Nobody mentioned it at lunch. Or after. Nobody remembered because it wasn't worth remembering. Her body had screamed catastrophe. Reality shrugged. She wonders now: how many catastrophes were just moments that passed?
