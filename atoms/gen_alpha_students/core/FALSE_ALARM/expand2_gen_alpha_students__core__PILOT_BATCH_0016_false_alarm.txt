## FALSE_ALARM / EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
Tyler sets the alarm. 6:30am. He watches the red numbers lock in. His hand moves toward the phone to check again. Then stops. He breathes. The alarm is set. He saw it. He lets the phone stay on the nightstand. The urge to check buzzes in his chest. He lets it buzz. Doesn't answer it. Twenty minutes later, he's still awake. But he hasn't checked. The alarm will go off. It doesn't need his worry to work. He closes his eyes.
