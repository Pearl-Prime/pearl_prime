Maya finishes the assignment. Submits it. The score will post when it posts. Her fingers drift toward the bookmark. The screen. She stops. Closes the laptop lid instead. Makes a snack. Watches something short. The itch to check sits in her stomach. She doesn't scratch it. The number will exist whether she watches for it or not. Waiting won't change it. At 7pm, she looks once. Ninety-one. She nods. Closes it. The world didn't end in the hours she wasn't watching.
