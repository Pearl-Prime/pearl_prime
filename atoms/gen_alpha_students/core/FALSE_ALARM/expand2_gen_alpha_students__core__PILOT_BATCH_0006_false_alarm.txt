## FALSE_ALARM / MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

Leo checks the alarm. Set for 6:30. He checks again at 10:14pm. Still set. Checks at 10:31. Still there. The red numbers glow. He knows it's set. He watched himself set it. But he checks at 10:47. And 11:02. Each check brings relief for maybe forty seconds. Then the doubt creeps back. What if he turned it off by accident? What if the power goes out? He checks at 11:15. The alarm is set. It has always been set. He checks again.
