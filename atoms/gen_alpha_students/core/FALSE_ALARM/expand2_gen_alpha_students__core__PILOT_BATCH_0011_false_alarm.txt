## FALSE_ALARM / TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_alpha_students/core/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
Aisha spent all week waiting for the confrontation. The friend group thing. She rehearsed what to say. Planned escape routes. Lost sleep over it. Then Friday came. Nothing happened. Everyone ate lunch together. Laughed at the same jokes. No confrontation. She sat with her tray, heart ready for impact. Impact never came. Her body had prepared for a war that wasn't real. She wonders, just for a second: what if her warning system doesn't know the difference? What if the alarm is just noise?
