Zoe is at a college party and she feels suddenly sad. Not about this moment. About another moment that's not happening. The senior spring she didn't get. COVID took it. She's grieving something she never had. Everyone's having the college experience now. She is too. But there's a ghost experience in the shadows. The version where she had normal high school. The version where college was new, not a continuation of emergency. Her chest is heavy. The party is loud. She's mourning for a timeline that no longer exists.
