## PIVOT v01
---

---
Setting a boundary cost you something. Maybe a friendship, maybe a position in the group. The grief is real — and it doesn't mean the boundary was wrong.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The grief isn't about saying no. It's about realizing you needed to — that the people or spaces you gave your energy to wouldn't protect your energy the way you needed them to.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You drew the line and felt a door close. That's grief. The old dynamic — the one where you said yes to everything — is ending, and endings hurt even when they're necessary.
---
