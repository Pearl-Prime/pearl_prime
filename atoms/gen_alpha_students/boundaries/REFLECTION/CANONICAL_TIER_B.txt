

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: protective
tier_variant: B

You cannot choose to have boundaries you have never experienced. The capacity develops through having it modeled, reinforced, and respected. If no one ever showed you what a boundary looks like, you learned a different architecture instead. Everything is available. Everything is negotiable. Everything can be demanded of you. The mechanism absorbed this lesson through watching the adults around you. When they said yes to everything, you learned yes was the only option. When they did not protect their own boundaries, they could not teach you to protect yours. Now when someone asks for something, your first response is yes. You do not even realize refusal was an option. The mechanism never developed the internal sense that says: this is mine, this is not mine. It only knows: everything is available.
