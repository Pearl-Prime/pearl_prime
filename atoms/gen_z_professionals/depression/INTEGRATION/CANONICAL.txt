

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: BODY_LANDED
reframe_type: BODY_FACT
weight: standard
carry_line: "You showed up anyway."
---
The day happened and you moved through it like a watcher. Your body was there. Your hands typed. Your face made the shapes that mean professional. And somewhere behind all of that was a you that couldn't feel much of anything.
This isn't failing at being a person. This is what depression does when the external circumstances are technically fine. Everything looks right from the outside. The salary cleared. The project's on track. The LinkedIn profile is acceptable. And inside there's a flatness that doesn't match any of that.
Notice your feet on the floor. Your breath somewhere in your chest. That heaviness you feel — that's depression in your body. Not a failure. A fact.
You showed up anyway.
