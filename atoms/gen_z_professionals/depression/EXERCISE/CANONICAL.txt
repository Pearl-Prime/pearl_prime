

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Notice where heaviness lives in your body right now. Usually: chest, shoulders, belly. Locate it. Sit upright. Press both feet into the floor firmly. Count to three. Feel the ground beneath you. The ground is solid. You are less solid. This is depression. Heaviness pulls you down. Resist it. Keep pressing your feet down. Feel resistance. This resistance is life. Do this three times. Press, hold, release. You are fighting gravity with your body. You are alive.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You are on the couch. Depression wants you there. Stand up anyway. Stand up slowly. Feel your legs. They still work. Step to a window. Look outside. Count five things you see. Tree. Sky. Person. Car. Building. These things exist outside. You exist inside. You are part of the world. The world did not disappear. Feel this. Walk away from the window. You have proved it.

## EXERCISE v03
---

---

Your lungs still move. Your body still functions. Open your eyes. Look at something in front of you. Your vision works. Your sight is evidence of life. You are alive.
