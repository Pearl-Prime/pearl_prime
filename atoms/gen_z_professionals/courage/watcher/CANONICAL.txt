Priya is sitting with the email draft. The request is simple: present the quarter's work to leadership. Three slides. She's done this three times already. But this time the watcher activates before she can decide. The watcher projects: the presentation will start and her mind will go blank. The manager's face will show disappointment. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v01

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Priya starts to notice the watcher's method. It's not prediction. It's simulation. The watcher doesn't see the future. It invents futures based on the worst-case scenario template. And the template is specific: she will be exposed as inadequate. The details change but the structure never does. The watcher runs the scenario so vividly that the nervous system responds as though it already happened. The body goes into shutdown, not because the presentation is actually dangerous but because the watcher has convinced the nervous system that it is. And here's the strange part: the watcher's prophecy creates its own truth. The shutdown makes her less present, less sharp, less able to actually handle the presentation if it happens. So when the watcher shows her the future where she stumbles, the watcher has already set the conditions for stumbling. The jaw is tight with a different kind of recognition: the watcher doesn't predict failure. It engineers failure by preventing the attempt.

Marcus sees the pattern. The watcher always activates in social situations. Always projects race into the outcome. The white people in the room are navigating normal social anxiety. Marcus is navigating normal social anxiety plus the watcher's detailed scenario about how his presence is being read, misread, coded as threatening or incompetent or both. The watcher adds a layer that wasn't there before. It's not just the general fear of social failure. It's the specific terror of social failure as a Black man in a white-majority space. The watcher uses his race as a plot point in the catastrophe. And Marcus can't stop watching. The nervous system is stuck in the watcher's theatre and the theatre only shows him futures where his Blackness is the problem. The shoulders drop slightly, not from relief but from the exhaustion of carrying this additional weight. The watcher has taken something that could be manageable anxiety and made it something loaded with meaning about his body, his presence, his right to be in the space.
