Jordan identifies the shame's core message: you aren't the kind of person who does this. Not "you can't" or "you shouldn't"—"you aren't." The message is about identity, not ability. Priya notices the shame activates specifically on acts of self-advocacy. She can advocate for her team without shame. She can pitch someone else's idea without shame. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v03

COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Kai calculates the compound interest of shame-blocked courage. Each time he stays silent instead of contributing, he reinforces the silence. Each silence makes the next one easier. Ease is the wrong word—more automatic. The shame doesn't need to fire as hard each time because the silence has become default. The courage required to break the default increases with each cycle. Year one: he could have spoken with moderate effort. Year three: speaking requires overriding three years of precedent. The shame compounds. The cost of delayed courage is that the delay makes the courage more expensive.
