BAND: 2

COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Kai is sitting at the desk at 8 p.m. on a Wednesday thinking about the mentorship conversation that just happened. The mentor asked: are you actually happy? And Kai realized that the question is unanswerable because the version of Kai that would know the answer to that question doesn't exist anymore. That version was the one who thought that doing well in school would mean doing well in life. The one who thought that the right job would feel meaningful instead of just necessary. The one who thought that working hard would mean security. That version is gone. Kai is aware now of the loss of that version, but there's no funeral for a belief system. There's no place to mark it. The chest is heavy. The throat is tight. Kai is grieving something that no one else can see.
