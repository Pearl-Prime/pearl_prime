

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: protective

The boundary-setting machine in your nervous system was never installed because the environment where you learned to work never required it. You learned responsiveness instead. You learned that being valuable meant being available, that saying yes was the mechanism by which you proved you were serious, that the three-day turnaround was the speed that mattered. The logic is baked in.
