--- variant: v01
The spiral has an entry point -- a specific thought or sensation that starts the loop. Catching the entry point is different from stopping the spiral. One is possible in the first three seconds. The other usually isn't. We're going for the one that works.

--- variant: v02
Inside the spiral, every thought feels equally important. That's the spiral's trick -- it removes the filter that ranks relevance. There's a way to reinstall that filter while the spiral is running, and it doesn't require stopping the loop first.

--- variant: v03
The spiral runs on a specific kind of fuel: unanswered questions about the future. Not all futures -- a particular kind. Identifying which future your spiral fixates on reveals something about what actually needs attention. It's usually not what the spiral says it is.
