

## COMPRESSION v01
---

---
You are not solving the problem. You are rehearsing the feeling of having a problem. The loop stays open because finishing it would mean stopping, and stopping feels like negligence.
---

## COMPRESSION v02
---

---
One more lap through the same thought feels like diligence. It is repetition wearing a highlighter. Nothing new enters; you just get louder inside your own head.
---

## COMPRESSION v03
---

---
Overthinking is motion that looks like work. You burn real hours on imaginary court cases where you already know the verdict. The case never closes because you keep re-opening it for sport.
---

## COMPRESSION v04
---

---
You treat "longer" as if it meant "truer." Time in the spiral does not sharpen the answer; it thickens the fog. The mind mistakes rumination for rigor.
---

## COMPRESSION v05
---

---
The story you tell about what might go wrong is easier to run than the small move that would make things real. Overthinking is how you postpone the discomfort of acting while still feeling busy.
---
