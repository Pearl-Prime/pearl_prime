Zara replays the three sentences from the 1:1 meeting for the seventh time. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Zara's temples throb. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Zara watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Zara tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the neck. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Zara examines the story. The story has no evidence. The temples has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Zara holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Leo has been staring at the same paragraph for forty minutes. The words mean nothing. The thoughts mean everything. The alarm system hits maximum volume. Leo's fingers tap. Leo's jaw grinds. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Leo stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Leo hears it. For the first time, Leo hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Leo's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Leo separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The alarm sounds and the body recognizes it as noise. The frame cracks open.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nia documents the analysis loop: decide, doubt, re-analyze, decide again, doubt again. Nia's forehead tightens. The heat is on. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Nia tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is threat detection without actual danger. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Nia sees the cost clearly now. Nia's temples throb as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the alarm's false positives never get corrected because Nia avoids the situation before learning it was safe. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Nia.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan has spent four hours on a two-paragraph email. The loop has consumed the entire morning. Peak intensity. Jordan's fingers tap. Jordan's jaw grinds. The alarm system runs at full power. Jordan watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ambiguous stimulus, threat interpretation, body mobilization, avoidance or escape, relief, reinforcement. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Jordan's body absorbs the volume. Jordan's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Jordan sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
