## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Stop. Your mind is spinning into the future.
Name three things you can see right now.
Name three things you can touch right now.
Name one thing you can hear right now.
Your body is in the present moment.
Your anxiety is imagining something that isn't here.
Feel the ground beneath your feet.
Press your feet down into it.
This is what's actually real right now.
Not your spiral. This moment.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You're overthinking something that hasn't happened yet.
Write down the exact fear on paper.
Now write down one thing you can't control.
Overthinking doesn't prevent bad things from happening.
It just steals today's peace.
Stand up. Do one physical thing.
Walk. Stretch. Get water. Move.
Your brain will quiet down when your body moves.
It can't spiral and exercise simultaneously.
Use your body to interrupt the loop.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Notice: you've spiraled into this thought loop before.
The worst thing didn't happen then.
It probably won't happen now either.
Your anxiety is recycling the same fears.
You have proof that you survived before.
Set a timer for ten minutes.
Let yourself think about it if you must.
When the timer ends, stop.
Do something else with complete intention.
Your brain is trained to spiral. Retrain it.
