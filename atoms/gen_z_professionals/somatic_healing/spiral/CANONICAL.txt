## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/somatic_healing/spiral/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Sam catches the moment when the spiral begins. The chest is tight. The mind reaches for the interpretation. And Sam pauses. Sam asks: what if the chest tight is just the chest tight? Not a signal. Not data about the call. Just a sensation that a body sometimes has. The pause creates a space. The chest is still tight but the spiral stops accelerating. The call continues. The outcome is not different but the interior experience is different. Sam has noticed that there is a difference between the sensation and the spiral. The turning point is recognizing that the chest's tightness and the mind's story about the tightness are separate things.
