Alex lies on the massage table and the therapist's hands touch the upper back. The tissue responds. The tissue is tight in a way that seems old. The tightness has a quality of time to it. Alex's mind never named this. The hands press slightly and Alex feels something in the chest rise. Not quite tears but something close. The breath catches. The therapist does not comment. The tissue is holding something. Alex recognizes the recognition: the body has been carrying something the mind has not looked at. The tightness is old. The body is older than the moment.

Priya is in a breathwork session and something shifts. The session is supposed to be about regulation. Priya is hyperventilating in a controlled way and suddenly Priya is crying. The crying is not about anything current. It is not about the job or the partner or any situation. It is raw crying. Priya does not understand what it is. The facilitator lets it continue. Jordan leaves the breathing session and the proof deepens. The shallow breathing was not a choice. It was a survival mechanism. The shallow breathing kept the grief at a distance. The breath was containing something. The breathing coach has opened the breath and the grief that the breath was containing is moving through the system. The proof is in the body.
