## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Stand with your feet hip-width apart.
Feel all four corners of your feet.
Press each corner into the ground deliberately.
Feel the earth responding under your weight.
Notice where your body feels tense right now.
Shake out just your hands for ten seconds.
Shake out just your legs for ten seconds.
Roll your shoulders backward five times.
Roll them forward five times.
Your body is a living, sensing thing.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Lie down on your back or sit.
Start at the top of your head.
Is there any tension? Just notice it.
Don't try to fix it. Just observe.
Move your attention down to your face.
Then your neck. Your shoulders. Your arms.
Track each part without judgment.
Don't force anything to change.
When you notice tension, breathe toward it.
One simple breath per body part.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Put on music that makes you feel something.
Don't overthink which song. Pick one.
Close your eyes and move however feels right.
You're not dancing. You're moving your feelings.
Don't think about how you look.
Think about how your body wants to move.
Let one part of you move at a time.
Then let another part join.
Move until something releases in you.
Your body knows what it needs.
---
