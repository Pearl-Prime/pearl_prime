--- variant: v01
You're allowed to see someone else's success and feel the sting. That sting doesn't mean you're petty or ungrateful. It means the comparison engine activated, and you're human enough to feel the output. Feeling it and being run by it are different things. You can hold the sting and still choose your own direction.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to unfollow, mute, and curate your inputs without guilt. Protecting your mental bandwidth isn't avoidance. It's maintenance. You don't owe your nervous system unlimited access to other people's highlight reels just because the platform makes it easy.

--- variant: v03
It's okay to feel behind. It's okay to know intellectually that comparison is irrational and still feel it in your chest. The gap between knowing and feeling isn't failure. It's the normal lag between insight and integration. You're not supposed to think your way out of a feeling in real time.
