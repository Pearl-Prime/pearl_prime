## TURNING_POINT v06

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Maya notices the tension in the jaw during the standup and lets it soften. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Maya's shoulders drop. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Maya watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Maya tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the warmth. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Maya examines the story. The story has no evidence. The shoulders has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Maya holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dev drops to the floor mid-meeting, knees hitting carpet, body finally refusing to pretend. The overwhelm flood hits maximum volume. Dev's muscles release. Dev's breath deepens. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Dev stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Dev hears it. For the first time, Dev hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Dev's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Dev separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The flood reveals it is many small streams, not one wall of water. The frame cracks open.

COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Zara notices the tension between the shoulder blades during the standup. Breathes into it. The tension softens. The standup continues. The overwhelm flood activates. Zara's breath deepens. The heat rises. Zara recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Zara picks one stream and follows it, letting the rest run alongside. The old pattern fires and Zara acts from the new understanding anyway. Zara's jaw softens, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Zara does while the pattern runs. Zara does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Zara also exists. They are not the same thing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Suki documents the body's truth sequence: tension builds, mind ignores, tension escalates, body speaks louder. Suki's jaw softens. The heat is on. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Suki tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is volume flattening into paralysis. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Suki sees the cost clearly now. Suki's shoulders drop as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the overwhelm prevents the sorting that would reduce the overwhelm, creating its own fuel. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Suki.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marco's body shuts down mid-sprint. Migraine, nausea, trembling. The somatic debt comes due all at once. Peak intensity. Marco's muscles release. Marco's breath deepens. The overwhelm flood runs at full power. Marco watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: task accumulation, flattening into mass, paralysis, self-criticism for paralysis, more accumulation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Marco's body absorbs the volume. Marco's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Marco sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
