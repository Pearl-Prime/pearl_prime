---
Kai gets a project assignment. A good project. Visible project. Kai's body responds with immediate alert. The alarm goes off: you're going to be seen doing this and they're going to figure out you don't actually know how to do this. The chest tightens. The hands get cold. The jaw tightens. Kai knows logically that Kai has completed similar projects. Kai has the skills. But the body doesn't know this. The body only knows: competence is being assessed and competence is dangerous because if you're competent it means you're a fraud. The logic is circular but the body doesn't care about logic. The alarm is firing.
