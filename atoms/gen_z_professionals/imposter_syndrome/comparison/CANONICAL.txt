## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Zara gets praised in the all-hands and waits for the correction. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Zara's face flushes. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Zara watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Zara tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the stomach. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Zara examines the story. The story has no evidence. The face has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Zara holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Leo gets the promotion email and the first feeling is terror. Pure, physical terror. The comparison engine hits maximum volume. Leo's hands shake. Leo's heart races. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Leo stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Leo hears it. For the first time, Leo hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Leo's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Leo separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The metric collapses under its own selectivity. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Suki accepts the compliment. Does not deflect, correct, or minimize. The comparison engine activates. Suki's heart races. The heat rises. Suki recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Suki holds both someone else's achievement and their own without ranking. The old pattern fires and Suki acts from the new understanding anyway. Suki's mouth dries, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Suki does while the pattern runs. Suki does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Suki also exists. They are not the same thing.

---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marco leads the critical meeting when the manager is out. No safety net. No understudy. Maximum intensity. Marco's stomach sinks. Marco's hands shake. The comparison engine roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Marco does not yield. Marco stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Marco's body carries both. The crisis does not define Marco. Marco's response defines Marco. Marco moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Marco walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nia maps the fraud cycle: receive praise, dismiss evidence, wait for exposure, receive praise again. Nia's mouth dries. The heat is on. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Nia tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is relative positioning against curated highlights. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Nia sees the cost clearly now. Nia's face flushes as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each comparison produces a momentary relief—at least Nia checked—followed by a deeper deficit. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Nia.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan gets asked a question in front of the CEO and the mind goes completely blank. Peak intensity. Jordan's hands shake. Jordan's heart races. The comparison engine runs at full power. Jordan watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: trigger, selective data pull, asymmetric weighting, deficit verdict, temporary relief, repeat. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Jordan's body absorbs the volume. Jordan's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Jordan sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
