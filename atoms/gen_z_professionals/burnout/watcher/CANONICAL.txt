## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jordan stares at the same screen that greeted the morning eight hours ago. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Jordan's legs go heavy. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Jordan watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Jordan tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the eyelids. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Jordan examines the story. The story has no evidence. The legs has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Jordan holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya opens the laptop and the screen blurs. The cursor blinks. Priya cannot remember what the task is. The watcher hits maximum volume. Priya's arms feel hollow. Priya's ribs ache. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Priya stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Priya hears it. For the first time, Priya hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Priya's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Priya separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The watcher's scorecard turns out to be fiction built on selective memory. The frame cracks open.

## EMBODIMENT v05

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kai closes the laptop at 5:30. The inbox is not empty. Kai is. The watcher activates. Kai's ribs ache. The heat rises. Kai recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Kai acts while the watcher watches, refusing to pause for its commentary. The old pattern fires and Kai acts from the new understanding anyway. Kai's neck locks, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Kai does while the pattern runs. Kai does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Kai also exists. They are not the same thing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev documents the burnout cycle: push harder, feel less, push harder, feel nothing. Dev's neck locks. The heat is on. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Dev tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is self-surveillance that scores without participating. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Dev sees the cost clearly now. Dev's legs go heavy as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the surveillance feels protective—as long as Dev watches for failure, failure might be prevented—but the watching is the cost. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Dev.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Zara cannot feel the keyboard under the fingers. The numbness has reached the hands. Peak intensity. Zara's arms feel hollow. Zara's ribs ache. The watcher runs at full power. Zara watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: action taken, surveillance activation, performance scoring, deficit finding, behavioral inhibition, withdrawal. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Zara's body absorbs the volume. Zara's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Zara sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
