## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Leo stares at the same screen that greeted the morning eight hours ago. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Leo's eyelids drag. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Leo watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Leo tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the arms. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Leo examines the story. The story has no evidence. The eyelids has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Leo holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Suki opens the laptop and the screen blurs. The cursor blinks. Suki cannot remember what the task is. The grief wave hits maximum volume. Suki's ribs ache. Suki's neck locks. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Suki stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Suki hears it. For the first time, Suki hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Suki's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Suki separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The wave arrives and doesn't need to be stopped to be survived. The frame cracks open.

## EMBODIMENT v05

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marco closes the laptop at 5:30. The inbox is not empty. Marco is. The grief wave activates. Marco's neck locks. The heat rises. Marco recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Marco carries the weight and moves through the day with it, not despite it. The old pattern fires and Marco acts from the new understanding anyway. Marco's legs go heavy, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Marco does while the pattern runs. Marco does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Marco also exists. They are not the same thing.

Nia does not yield. Nia stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Nia's body carries both. The crisis does not define Nia. Nia's response defines Nia. Nia moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Nia walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan documents the burnout cycle: push harder, feel less, push harder, feel nothing. Jordan's legs go heavy. The heat is on. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Jordan tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is loss surfacing through sensory memory. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Jordan sees the cost clearly now. Jordan's eyelids drag as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the grief stores in the body because the mind keeps routing around it, and stored grief compounds. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Jordan.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Priya cannot feel the keyboard under the fingers. The numbness has reached the hands. Peak intensity. Priya's ribs ache. Priya's neck locks. The grief wave runs at full power. Priya watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ordinary trigger, sensory memory activation, body response, cognitive catch-up, suppression attempt, residual storage. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Priya's body absorbs the volume. Priya's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Priya sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
