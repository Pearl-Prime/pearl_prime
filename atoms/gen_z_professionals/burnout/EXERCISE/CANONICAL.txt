## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Stop checking Slack and email right now.
Close the laptop lid completely.
Stand up and stretch your arms overhead.
Walk to the window or outside.
Breathe in for four counts.
Breathe out for six counts.
Repeat two more times.
Tell yourself: work will exist tomorrow.
Set a timer for your break.
Return when the timer ends.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Notice one task you finished today.
Write it down or say it aloud.
This happened. You did this.
Feel the weight of that completion.
Close your work tabs on purpose.
Don't reopen them before tomorrow.
Put your phone in another room.
Give yourself permission to not think.
Rest isn't laziness. It's refueling.
You'll work better when you're recharged.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Set a specific time to stop working.
Write that time on your calendar.
Treat it like a client meeting.
When that time comes, close everything.
Don't check work email after this point.
Replace work with one thing you enjoy.
Watch a show. Play a game.
Call a friend. Read something.
Your brain needs to switch modes.
Sleep tonight without work in your head.
---
