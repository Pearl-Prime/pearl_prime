Kai is pitching the product strategy. The pitch is good. Kai can feel it. The room is tracking. The energy is landing. And simultaneously, Kai is outside the room, noting the energy, measuring it, judging it as insufficient. The two realities don't collapse. They run parallel. Pitch-reality and Watching-reality.
