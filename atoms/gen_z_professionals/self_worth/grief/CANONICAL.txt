---
Sam is at the coffee shop. There's a moment of happiness. The coffee tastes good. The sun is hitting the table. Then the grief interrupts. Memory of two years ago when Sam would have been too anxious to sit still at a coffee shop. Two years of anxiety that no longer exists. But the years still exist. They're still gone. The mechanism is this: grief has a way of collapsing time. Every good moment now has a shadow version. A version from the years when Sam was trapped in the anxiety.
