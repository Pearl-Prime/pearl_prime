

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
After receiving performance feedback, pause immediately. Place both feet flat on the floor. Feel the ground beneath you. Your worth is not the feedback. Your feet are real. The ground is real. Your worth is real. The feedback is words. Words are not more real than your body. Feel your feet. Press down. Count to three. You are grounded in your own worth.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Look at your hands. Really look at them. These hands worked. These hands created. These hands are proof of capability. Say: "My hands matter." Say it twice. Now look at your face in a mirror. Say: "My face matters." Say it twice. Your being matters separate from your performance. Your existence is worthy. Feel this. Believe this.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
At your desk, close your eyes. Say silently: "My worth is not my metrics." Say it five times. Feel each statement settle. Your worth is not your productivity. Your worth is not your sales numbers. Your worth is not your engagement metrics. Your worth is inherent. You have worth because you exist. Say: "I exist, therefore I matter." Say it three times.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
On your couch, lie down. Place both hands on your belly. Breathe into your belly slowly. Count four as you breathe in. Count six as you breathe out. Do this five times. Your belly is your center. Your center knows: you are worthy. Your worth is not performed. Your worth is located in your core. Feel it. Breathe into it.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
In a bathroom, splash your face with cool water. Feel the shock. Feel yourself present. Your presence is valuable. Not your productivity. Not your perfection. Your presence. Pat your face dry. Look at yourself. Say: "I am here." Say it three times. Your presence is enough. You are here. This is enough.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Stand and place your feet hip-width apart. Feel both feet equally grounded. You are standing on your own. Your own weight. Your own strength. Feel this independence. You do not need someone else's approval to be standing. You are standing. You are worthy. Feel the worthiness in your feet. Feel it in your legs.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Before checking social media metrics or engagement, take three conscious breaths. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for four counts. Breathe out for six counts. Do this three times. Tell yourself: "My worth is not in the numbers." Feel this truth. Numbers are abstract. You are concrete. You are more real than numbers.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Sit upright in a chair. Feel your spine. Your spine holds you upright. Your spine is strong. Your worthiness is like your spine: structural, holding you, real. Feel your spine. Feel its support. Feel the strength in your back. This strength is yours. This worthiness is yours.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Place one hand on your heart. Feel your heartbeat. Count ten beats. Your heart beats without approval. Your heart beats because you exist. Your heartbeat is proof of your worth. You do not need to earn your heartbeat. You do not need to perform for your heart. Your heart loves you automatically. Feel this. Believe this.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Close your eyes. Recall a moment when you felt worthy without metrics. Remember the feeling. Maybe you were with someone you love. Maybe you were doing something you enjoyed. Remember this feeling in your body. Feel it now. This feeling is available to you always. Not only when metrics are good. Feel this truth.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
On a transit seat, focus on your breathing. Breathe normally. No one is judging your breathing. Your breathing is worthwhile. Your breathing is valuable. You exist. People exist. Existence is valuable. Feel this in your lungs. Feel your lungs working. Feel worthiness in function.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
At your desk, hold your phone without checking metrics or notifications. Just hold it. Feel its weight. Your weight is more real than digital numbers. Your body is more real. Your body is more valuable. Put the phone down. Feel lighter. Feel free from the numbers.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
After criticism, stand up. You do not need to sit with criticism. Stand tall. Feel your full height. Criticism tries to make you small. Standing makes you large. Hold this tall posture for two minutes. Feel your strength. Criticism is one person's opinion. Your strength is real.
---

## EXERCISE v14
---

---
In a bathroom mirror, look directly at yourself. Do not look away. This person looking back is worthy. Say: "You are worthy." Say it to yourself. Say it three times. Hear yourself say it. Believe it. Your face is worthy. Your existence is worthy.
---

## EXERCISE v15
---

---
Place both hands on your thighs. Press down firmly. Your legs carry you. Your legs work. Your work is worthy. Your existence is worthy. Your thighs are strong. Feel this strength. Feel the worthiness in your strength.
---

## EXERCISE v16
---

---
On your couch, lie back. Place your arms at your sides, palms up. This is the vulnerable position. Vulnerability is not weakness. Vulnerability is worthiness. You are worthy enough to be vulnerable. Feel your vulnerability. Feel your worthiness. They exist together.
---

## EXERCISE v17
---

---
Before receiving feedback, ground yourself. Sit down. Place both feet on the floor. Say: "My worth is separate from this feedback." Say it three times. Feel the separation. Feedback is information. Your worth is fundamental. They are not the same. Feel this. Hear the feedback with this knowing.
---

## EXERCISE v18
---

---
Hum for five seconds. Feel the vibration in your chest. Stop. Hum again. Feel it. Your voice is worthy. Your voice matters. You are worthy of being heard. Hum five more times. Each hum is a declaration of worth. Your voice declares: I exist. I matter.
---

## EXERCISE v19
---

---
Stand in front of a window. Look at your reflection. See yourself as the world sees you: a living person. A person worthy of love. A person worthy of rest. A person worthy of existence. Feel this seeing. Feel worthy. Feel seen as worthy.
---

## EXERCISE v20
---

---
At your desk, close your eyes. Say silently: "My mistakes do not define me." Say it five times. Say: "My failures do not diminish me." Say it five times. Say: "My worth is constant." Say it five times. Feel each statement. Feel the truth. Your worth is not earned. It is inherent.
---

## EXERCISE v21
---

---
Place one hand on your shoulder. Squeeze gently. You are supporting yourself. You are worthy of self-support. Move to your other shoulder. Squeeze gently. Repeat five times, alternating shoulders. You are giving yourself care. You are worthy of self-care. Feel this care. Accept it.
---

## EXERCISE v22
---

---
On a transit seat, press your feet into the floor. Press for five seconds. Your feet are powerful. Your whole body is powerful. Power is worth. You are worthy of your power. Release. Your power remains inside you. Feel it.
---

## EXERCISE v23
---

---
Take a piece of paper. Write one thing you did today that required effort, regardless of outcome. One thing. Your effort matters. Your effort is worthy. Fold the paper. Keep it. Your effort is evidence of your worth. Not the outcome. The effort.
---

## EXERCISE v24
---

---
In a bathroom, wash your hands slowly. Feel the water. You are washing away the day's shame, if any. You are washing clean. Say: "I am clean." Say it three times. You are worthy of cleanliness. You are worthy of renewal. You are worthy.
---

## EXERCISE v25
---

---
Sit upright. Close your eyes. Place one hand on your chest, one on your belly. Breathe normally. Feel the breath. Feel the functioning. Your body functions. Your body is worthy of care. Feel the worthiness in your breath. Feel it in your functioning body.
---

## EXERCISE v26
---

---
Before bed, lie down. Say silently: "I did my best today." Say it three times. Feel the truth. Regardless of metrics, you did your best. Your best was enough. Your best was worthy. Sleep knowing this. Your effort was worthy. You are worthy.
---

## EXERCISE v27
---

---
Stand and stretch your arms overhead slowly. Feel the stretch. Feel the expansion. You are expanding into your worthiness. Hold the stretch for five seconds. Feel your full size. Your size is worthy. Your presence takes up space. Space is yours. Feel worthy in your space.
---

## EXERCISE v28
---

---
Look at your face in a mirror. Close your eyes. Say: "I am worthy of love." Say it five times. Open your eyes. The person in the mirror is worthy of love. That person is you. You are worthy of love. Not conditional love. Pure love. Feel worthy.

## EXERCISE v29
---

---
Place both palms together at your chest. Press them together firmly for five seconds. Your hands are holding your heart. Your heart is worthy. Your heart is safe. Your heart matters. Release your hands. Your heart is held by you. You hold yourself as worthy.
---

## EXERCISE v30
---

---
Before sleep, lie in bed. Place both hands on your heart. Say silently: "I am worthy because I exist." Say it five times. Feel it settle. Say: "My worth is not earned; it is inherent." Say it five times. Feel the truth. Say: "I am worthy." Say it ten times slowly. Feel complete worthiness. Sleep in it.
