

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before opening your banking app, pause. Place both feet flat on the floor. Feel the ground beneath you. The ground is real. Your body is real. Numbers on a screen are not more real than your body. Feel your feet. Press down. Count to three. You are grounded in this moment. Open the app now. You will not disappear into numbers. You have grounded yourself first.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
After checking your account and feeling panic, go to a sink. Run warm water over your hands slowly. Feel the warmth. Count to ten. Warm water is life. Numbers are abstract. Your hands are concrete. Feel your hands. Pat them dry. Your hands work. Your body works. Your body is capable. Numbers are not capable. You are.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Sit at your desk. Place one hand on your heart. Feel your heartbeat. Count ten beats. Your heart does not care about your account balance. Your heart keeps beating. Your body keeps living. Your body's needs are real. Your body needs shelter, food, water. These are real needs. A number in an app is abstract. Feel the difference. Feel your heart's realness.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
On a transit seat, place both feet flat. Feel the motion beneath you. You are moving toward your workplace where you earn. You are moving toward income. Feel the forward motion. You are not stuck. You are moving. The vehicle is moving. Motion is change. Your financial situation can change too. Feel the possibility in the motion. You are moving forward.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Before paying a bill, take one full breath cycle. Breathe in through your nose for four counts. Hold for four counts. Breathe out through your mouth for six counts. This breath means: I am here. I am alive. Bills are real. You are more real. You are living. A bill is a piece of paper or data. You are flesh and blood. You are more real. Pay with presence.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
In a bathroom, splash your face with cool water. Feel the shock. Feel the aliveness. Financial stress takes you out of your body. Cold water brings you back in. Pat your face dry. Look at yourself. Say: "My body is real." Say it twice. Your body is the only thing you truly own. Everything else is temporary. Feel your body. Believe in it.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
At your desk, place both hands on the surface in front of you. Press down firmly. The desk is solid. Your hands are solid. The world is solid. Your financial situation is not as solid as the desk. The desk will hold you. Money comes and goes. The desk remains. Feel this stability. Find stability in solids: desk, chair, walls, floor. Feel them.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Sit upright in a chair. Feel your spine against the back. Your spine is your strength bone. It holds you upright even when afraid. Financial fear bends your spine. Straighten it. Feel your full height. You are strong. Your spine knows strength. Hold this posture for two minutes. Feel the strength. Feel the straightness. Your body is strong even when money is scary.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
After financial worry, stand and shake your entire body for ten seconds. Shake your arms, legs, torso. Feel the vibration. Feel the movement. Worry is stagnant. Shaking mobilizes it. Shake for five more seconds. Stop. Feel the stillness after shaking. Feel lighter. Worry has been mobilized and shaken out. It is gone. Stand still. You are clear.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Close your eyes. Count backward from twenty to one slowly. This stops financial spiraling. Count: twenty, nineteen, eighteen. Slow count. By one, notice: you are still okay. You have completed twenty breaths without the world ending. Open your eyes. You are still here. You will be still here by tomorrow.

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
On a transit seat, press your feet into the floor of the vehicle firmly. Count to five. Feel the pressure. Your feet are strong. Your feet work. Your feet carry you to work where you earn. Feel gratitude for your feet. Feel gratitude for their strength. Your body has strength. Your body works. Your body generates income. Feel this truth in your feet.

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Place one hand on your shoulder. Squeeze gently. You are carrying financial weight. Now you carry yourself gently. Move your hand to your other shoulder. Squeeze gently. You are not alone in this. You are giving yourself care. Repeat five times, alternating shoulders. Each squeeze says: "I am taking care of myself." By the fifth squeeze, feel the care. Feel less alone.

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
At your desk, close your eyes. Say silently: "My value is not my net worth." Say it three times. Feel it settle. Your worth is not measured in dollars. Your worth is in your being. Open your eyes. Look at your hands. Say: "My hands are valuable." Say it twice. Your hands work. Your hands are valuable. Your hands generate value regardless of account balance.

## EXERCISE v14

---

Drink the water slowly. Count the swallows: one, two, three. Feel the water hydrate you. You are replenishing yourself. You are capable of self-care despite financial stress.

---

Shift back to center. Do this five times. Your weight shifts. Your financial situation shifts. You shift between stability and fear. But right now, you are standing. You are stable.

## EXERCISE v22
---

---
Before checking your bank account again, take three full breaths. Breathe in for four, hold for four, breathe out for six. Do this three times. Three breaths of intentional calm. Now check. You have prepared your nervous system. The number will not shock you. You are ready. Check with calm.

---

Say it three times. Feel the promise. You will work. You will earn. You will survive. Say: "I am capable." Say it five times. Feel capability settle in your bones. Sleep as someone capable.
