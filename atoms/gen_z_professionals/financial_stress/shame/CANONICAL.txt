Priya is twenty-six and making a reasonable salary and she is also living paycheck to paycheck. Her student loans are nine hundred dollars per month. Her rent is fifteen hundred dollars per month. Her car payment is four hundred dollars per month. Food, insurance, utilities, phone: two thousand dollars more per month. She makes four thousand dollars per month. The shame is that she is doing it right and she is still failing.

Priya has stopped going out with colleagues because she cannot afford to go out with colleagues. She is using the excuse that she is tired. The truth is that she is tired and she is also poor.
