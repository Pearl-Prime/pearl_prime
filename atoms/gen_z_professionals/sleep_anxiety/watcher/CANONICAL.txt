Marco maps the insomnia spiral: check clock, calculate hours, panic about lost sleep, lose more sleep. Marco's stomach flutters. The heat is on. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Marco tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external.
