Alex tries the breathing technique from the app. Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4. The breath pattern is regulated. But the watcher is observing the breath. Is it working? Am I getting sleepy? ## Mechanism_Proof v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/sleep_anxiety/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus calculates the sleep math obsessively. Nine hours until the presentation. If he falls asleep now, he'll get seven hours. If he falls asleep in 10 minutes, he'll get 6 hours 50 minutes. The numbers are precise and meaningless. Sleep is not a function of minutes counted backward from deadline. But the watcher doesn't understand this. The watcher is running scenarios. Each scenario includes an outcome. The presentations haunts all of them. Marcus's breathing becomes irregular without his input. The body is responding to the threat simulation in his mind. The watcher is now not observing reality — it's constructing worst-case scenarios and examining them like evidence. The chest feels hollow. The worst case becomes more real than the present moment. Sleep is impossible when the threat is still being constructed.

---
Marcus stops calculating. The math doesn't make sleep appear. The stopping itself is an experiment. What if the presentation outcome isn't determined by tonight's sleep? The thought is dangerous and relieving simultaneously. Marcus's chest expands. Without the threat simulation running, there's suddenly nothing to defend against.
