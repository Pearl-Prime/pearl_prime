--- variant: v01
The grief hiding under your sleeplessness has a shape. Not metaphorically -- it takes up specific space in your body and your schedule. Finding where it lives is the first step, and it lives somewhere most people don't look.

--- variant: v02
There's an unfinished conversation between your sleeplessness and the grief beneath it. The sleeplessness speaks louder, but the grief has the older claim. What happens when you let the quieter voice go first is worth examining.

--- variant: v03
You've been carrying two things and calling them one. The moment you separate the grief from the sleeplessness, the total weight doesn't change. But something shifts in how you carry it. That shift has consequences.
