## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Nia lies awake calculating how many hours of sleep remain before the alarm. The thought spiral chains one worry to the next in an accelerating loop. Nia's chest constricts. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Nia watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Nia tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the pulse. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Nia examines the story. The story has no evidence. The chest has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Nia holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jordan has been awake for thirty-six hours. The body begs for sleep. The mind refuses. The thought spiral hits maximum volume. Jordan's legs twitch. Jordan's eyes burn. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Jordan stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Jordan hears it. For the first time, Jordan hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Jordan's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Jordan separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The spiral loses speed when one link gets examined instead of chained. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Priya turns off the alarm calculation and lies in the dark without counting hours. The thought spiral activates. Priya's eyes burn. The heat rises. Priya recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Priya notices the spiral starting and stays at the first thought without following. The old pattern fires and Priya acts from the new understanding anyway. Priya's stomach flutters, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Priya does while the pattern runs. Priya does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Priya also exists. They are not the same thing.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kai faces the worst insomnia night of the year and gets up at 6 AM anyway. Goes to work. Does the work. Maximum intensity. Kai's pulse quickens. Kai's legs twitch. The thought spiral roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Kai does not yield. Kai stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Kai's body carries both. The crisis does not define Kai. Kai's response defines Kai. Kai moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Kai walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Maya maps the insomnia spiral: check clock, calculate hours, panic about lost sleep, lose more sleep. Maya's stomach flutters. The heat is on. The thought spiral chains one worry to the next in an accelerating loop. Maya tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is each thought breeding a worse thought without pause. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Maya sees the cost clearly now. Maya's chest constricts as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each thought in the chain validates the next thought, and validation feels like progress instead of repetition. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Maya.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev has not slept in forty-eight hours. The anxiety about not sleeping is the only thing awake. Peak intensity. Dev's legs twitch. Dev's eyes burn. The thought spiral runs at full power. Dev watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: initial worry, extrapolation, catastrophic endpoint, new worry from endpoint, further extrapolation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dev's body absorbs the volume. Dev's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dev sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
