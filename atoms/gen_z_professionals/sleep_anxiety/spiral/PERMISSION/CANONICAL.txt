--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be in the spiral and not have an exit strategy yet. The demand to immediately stop spiraling is itself a kind of pressure that feeds the loop. Sometimes the most you can do is notice you're in it. That's not nothing. That's the first honest data point.

--- variant: v02
Permission to not have the answer right now. The spiral insists that if you just think one more round, clarity will arrive. You can let go of that promise. Clarity rarely arrives through the spiral. It arrives after. You're allowed to wait for after.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to spiral and still be okay. The spiral feels like falling, but you've spiraled before and you're still here. The spiral lies about its own danger. It says this time is different. It's usually not. You're allowed to trust your track record over the spiral's predictions.
