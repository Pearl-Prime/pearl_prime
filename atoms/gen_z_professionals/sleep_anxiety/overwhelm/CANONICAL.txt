## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev lies awake calculating how many hours of sleep remain before the alarm. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Dev's chest constricts. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Dev watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Dev tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the pulse. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Dev examines the story. The story has no evidence. The chest has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Dev holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Zara has been awake for thirty-six hours. The body begs for sleep. The mind refuses. The overwhelm flood hits maximum volume. Zara's legs twitch. Zara's eyes burn. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Zara stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Zara hears it. For the first time, Zara hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Zara's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Zara separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The flood reveals it is many small streams, not one wall of water. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Leo turns off the alarm calculation and lies in the dark without counting hours. The overwhelm flood activates. Leo's eyes burn. The heat rises. Leo recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Leo picks one stream and follows it, letting the rest run alongside. The old pattern fires and Leo acts from the new understanding anyway. Leo's stomach flutters, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Leo does while the pattern runs. Leo does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Leo also exists. They are not the same thing.

Suki faces the worst insomnia night of the year and gets up at 6 AM anyway. Goes to work. Does the work. Maximum intensity. Suki's pulse quickens. Suki's legs twitch. The overwhelm flood roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Suki does not yield. Suki stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Suki's body carries both. The crisis does not define Suki. Suki's response defines Suki. Suki moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Suki walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marco maps the insomnia spiral: check clock, calculate hours, panic about lost sleep, lose more sleep. Marco's stomach flutters. The heat is on. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Marco tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is volume flattening into paralysis. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Marco sees the cost clearly now. Marco's chest constricts as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the overwhelm prevents the sorting that would reduce the overwhelm, creating its own fuel. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Marco.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nia has not slept in forty-eight hours. The anxiety about not sleeping is the only thing awake. Peak intensity. Nia's legs twitch. Nia's eyes burn. The overwhelm flood runs at full power. Nia watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: task accumulation, flattening into mass, paralysis, self-criticism for paralysis, more accumulation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Nia's body absorbs the volume. Nia's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Nia sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
