## COMPRESSION v01
---

---
The effort to sleep is the thing keeping you awake. Trying to force the switch flips your body into audit mode — breath, pulse, clock — and audits are not how you drift.
---

## COMPRESSION v02
---

---
Every glance at the time turns the night into a test you are already losing. The scoreboard glows in the dark; your nervous system reads "behind" instead of "horizontal."
---

## COMPRESSION v03
---

---
Your mind promotes bedtime to a second shift with the same deadlines as your day. The ceiling becomes a desk. Rest was never meant to run on the same KPIs as your inbox.
---

## COMPRESSION v04
---

---
Sleep is not a thing you grab; it is a thing that sneaks in when you quit grabbing. The chase keeps the gate shut. Stillness starts when you stop auditioning for unconsciousness.
---

## COMPRESSION v05
---

---
What spikes is not always danger — sometimes it is the fear of being the only one still awake while the building pretends the world is soft and off. The loneliness of the hour feeds the wakefulness.
---
