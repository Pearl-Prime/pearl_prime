## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Maya opens the banking app and watches the number not move. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Maya's ribs contract. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Maya watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Maya tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the throat. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Maya examines the story. The story has no evidence. The ribs has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Maya holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev stares at the overdraft notification. The number is red. The rent is due tomorrow. The shame contraction hits maximum volume. Dev's gut churns. Dev's scalp prickles. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Dev stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Dev hears it. For the first time, Dev hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Dev's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Dev separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The contraction loosens when the moment separates from the person. The frame cracks open.

---
Zara opens the budget spreadsheet and looks at the numbers without flinching. The shame contraction activates. Zara's scalp prickles. The heat rises. Zara recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Zara stands inside the shame without shrinking, body upright, voice even. The old pattern fires and Zara acts from the new understanding anyway. Zara's fingers tremble, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Zara does while the pattern runs. Zara does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Zara also exists. They are not the same thing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Suki tracks the money-dread cycle: check account, panic, avoid planning, check account again. Suki's fingers tremble. The heat is on. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Suki tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is a moment becoming a permanent verdict on selfhood. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Suki sees the cost clearly now. Suki's ribs contract as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the shame prevents examination because examination requires looking at the thing that shames. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Suki.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marco gets the collections call during the team standup. The two worlds collide at maximum volume. Peak intensity. Marco's gut churns. Marco's scalp prickles. The shame contraction runs at full power. Marco watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: event recall, identity fusion, contraction, hiding behavior, isolation, deeper fusion. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Marco's body absorbs the volume. Marco's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Marco sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
