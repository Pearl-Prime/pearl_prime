## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nia opens the banking app and watches the number not move. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Nia's throat tightens. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Nia watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Nia tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the gut. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Nia examines the story. The story has no evidence. The throat has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Nia holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan stares at the overdraft notification. The number is red. The rent is due tomorrow. The overwhelm flood hits maximum volume. Jordan's scalp prickles. Jordan's fingers tremble. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Jordan stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Jordan hears it. For the first time, Jordan hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Jordan's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Jordan separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The flood reveals it is many small streams, not one wall of water. The frame cracks open.

COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Priya opens the budget spreadsheet and looks at the numbers without flinching. The overwhelm flood activates. Priya's fingers tremble. The heat rises. Priya recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Priya picks one stream and follows it, letting the rest run alongside. The old pattern fires and Priya acts from the new understanding anyway. Priya's ribs contract, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Priya does while the pattern runs. Priya does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Priya also exists. They are not the same thing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Maya tracks the money-dread cycle: check account, panic, avoid planning, check account again. Maya's ribs contract. The heat is on. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Maya tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is volume flattening into paralysis. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Maya sees the cost clearly now. Maya's throat tightens as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the overwhelm prevents the sorting that would reduce the overwhelm, creating its own fuel. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Maya.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev gets the collections call during the team standup. The two worlds collide at maximum volume. Peak intensity. Dev's scalp prickles. Dev's fingers tremble. The overwhelm flood runs at full power. Dev watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: task accumulation, flattening into mass, paralysis, self-criticism for paralysis, more accumulation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dev's body absorbs the volume. Dev's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dev sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
