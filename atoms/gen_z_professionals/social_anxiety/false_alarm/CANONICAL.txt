Leo enters the happy hour and immediately calculates the nearest exit. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Leo's hands sweat. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Leo watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Leo tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the voice. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Leo examines the story. The story has no evidence. The hands has real sensation. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

---
Suki stands in the elevator with six coworkers. The doors close. The air disappears. The alarm system hits maximum volume. Suki's knees lock. Suki's heartbeat pounds. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Suki stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Suki hears it. For the first time, Suki hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Suki's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Suki separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The alarm sounds and the body recognizes it as noise. The frame cracks open.

Marco recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Marco lets the alarm ring and acts anyway, body buzzing but hands steady. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan traces the avoidance architecture: invitation arrives, threat calculates, excuse manufactures, isolation wins. Jordan's face heats. The heat is on. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Jordan tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is threat detection without actual danger. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Jordan sees the cost clearly now. Jordan's hands sweat as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the alarm's false positives never get corrected because Jordan avoids the situation before learning it was safe. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Jordan.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Priya has a full panic attack in the conference room. Visible. Public. The mechanism exposed. Peak intensity. Priya's knees lock. Priya's heartbeat pounds. The alarm system runs at full power. Priya watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ambiguous stimulus, threat interpretation, body mobilization, avoidance or escape, relief, reinforcement. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Priya's body absorbs the volume. Priya's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Priya sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
