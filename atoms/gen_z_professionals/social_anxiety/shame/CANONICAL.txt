Jordan enters the happy hour and immediately calculates the nearest exit. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Jordan's face heats. Priya stands in the elevator with six coworkers. The doors close. The air disappears. The shame contraction hits maximum volume. Priya's voice tightens. Priya's knees lock. COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev traces the avoidance architecture: invitation arrives, threat calculates, excuse manufactures, isolation wins. Dev's heartbeat pounds. The heat is on. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Dev tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is a moment becoming a permanent verdict on selfhood. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Dev sees the cost clearly now. Dev's face heats as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the shame prevents examination because examination requires looking at the thing that shames. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Dev.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Zara has a full panic attack in the conference room. Visible. Public. The mechanism exposed. Peak intensity. Zara's voice tightens. Zara's knees lock. The shame contraction runs at full power. Zara watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: event recall, identity fusion, contraction, hiding behavior, isolation, deeper fusion. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Zara's body absorbs the volume. Zara's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Zara sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
