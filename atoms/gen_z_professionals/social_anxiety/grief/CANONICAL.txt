Alex stops trying to outrun the grief. In a moment of exhaustion, he stops resisting. He sits with the sadness. It's bigger up close. It fills the room. He feels it in his ribs, his throat. ---
Suki traces the avoidance architecture: invitation arrives, threat calculates, excuse manufactures, isolation wins. Suki's voice tightens. The heat is on. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Suki tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is loss surfacing through sensory memory. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Suki sees the cost clearly now.
