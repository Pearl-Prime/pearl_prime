IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Leo enters the happy hour and immediately calculates the nearest exit. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Leo's hands sweat. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Leo watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Leo tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the voice. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Leo examines the story. The story has no evidence. The hands has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Leo holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Suki stands in the elevator with six coworkers. The doors close. The air disappears. The comparison engine hits maximum volume. Suki's knees lock. Suki's heartbeat pounds. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Suki stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Suki hears it. For the first time, Suki hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Suki's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Suki separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The metric collapses under its own selectivity. The frame cracks open.

Marco enters the networking event and walks to the nearest person. Introduces. The voice shakes. The introduction lands. The comparison engine activates. Marco's heartbeat pounds. The heat rises. Marco recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Marco holds both someone else's achievement and their own without ranking. The old pattern fires and Marco acts from the new understanding anyway. Marco's face heats, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Marco also exists. They are not the same thing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/social_anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan traces the avoidance architecture: invitation arrives, threat calculates, excuse manufactures, isolation wins. Jordan's face heats. The heat is on. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Jordan tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is relative positioning against curated highlights. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Jordan sees the cost clearly now. Jordan's hands sweat as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each comparison produces a momentary relief—at least Jordan checked—followed by a deeper deficit. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Jordan.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/social_anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Priya has a full panic attack in the conference room. Visible. Public. The mechanism exposed. Peak intensity. Priya's knees lock. Priya's heartbeat pounds. The comparison engine runs at full power. Priya watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: trigger, selective data pull, asymmetric weighting, deficit verdict, temporary relief, repeat. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Priya's body absorbs the volume. Priya's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Priya sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
