Jordan opens Slack on Monday morning. There are 43 unread messages. Not all are directed at Jordan. Most are ambient — conversations Jordan could join or could ignore. The choice is exhausting. The presence of choice is itself demanding. Jordan's throat tightens. The week hasn't started yet, but it's already full. There's a mandatory standup at 10. A team lunch is scheduled. After work, there's a networking happy hour that Jordan's boss mentioned. The calendar exists as a kind of threat. Jordan's body recognizes the threat before the conscious mind does. The shoulders rise slightly. The inbox is not just information — it's demand. The recognition is: there is more than I can do.

Maya arrives at the company event. It's a mixer. The point is to network. Maya is supposed to be good at this. Marketing people are good at events. Maya is supposed to know people and people are supposed to like Maya. The expectation sits on Maya's shoulders. The room has 100 people. Maya knows maybe 5 of them. The expectation is to expand the network. To be present with many people. To make each person feel seen. Maya's body is already tired. It hasn't started yet, but the anticipation of the performance has depleted resources. Maya's jaw is tight. The throat is slightly constricted. The overwhelm is not just the crowd but the requirement to perform ease in the crowd. Maya's body knows it doesn't have enough social energy for this. The overwhelm is the gap between who people expect Maya to be and who Maya actually is.

## Mechanism_Proof v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/social_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jordan stays through the happy hour and the depletion worsens. Each conversation requires a reset. Each joke requires energy. Each transition between conversation partners requires a moment of gathering. Jordan is running on reserves. The body shows this. Jordan's smile is smaller. Jordan's voice is quieter. Jordan's presence is reduced. But Jordan stays because leaving early is noticed. Not leaving is the social priority. Jordan's body is shutting down from the inside while the outside remains compliant. This is the mechanism: social demand overrides physical capacity, so the body depletes in service of social obligation. Jordan drives home in silence. The silence is recovery, but there's no time to recover because tomorrow is another social day. The mechanism is that the overwhelm is continuous.

## Mechanism_Proof v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/social_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v03.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sam skips the coffee date. The guilt is immediate. The guilt is physical. Sam's chest tightens. This is a former colleague. They've worked together. The coffee is supposed to be maintenance of relationship. By not going, Sam is failing at relationship maintenance. The overwhelm is not eliminated by skipping one thing — it's replaced by guilt about skipping. The mechanism is that overwhelm produces either depletion (by saying yes) or guilt (by saying no). There's no third option. Sam's body carries both. Sam says yes to the next thing to compensate for the skipped coffee. The overwhelm increases. The mechanism is that each decision to manage overwhelm creates new overwhelm.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity

---
Maya stands in the mixer room and the performance machinery activates. Maya's body knows what to do. Maya's face arranges itself. Maya's voice pitches itself for the room. The mechanism is that Maya's nervous system generates a version of Maya that is designed for social success. This version is not fully Maya. The version is competent and charming. The version is missing something. The version is missing the actual interior. The real Maya is being left behind in service of the performance. Maya's chest is tight with this split. Maya is watching herself perform. The watching is another layer of separation. The mechanism is that the overwhelm includes this splitting. Maya can't be fully present because the performance requires a version that's been extracted from the actual person.

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jordan leaves the happy hour early. The leaving is an experiment. The leaving is also a small failure of the social expectation. But Jordan's body cannot continue. The decision comes from below the neck, from the exhausted nervous system. Jordan acknowledges the exhaustion as real. The acknowledgment is the turning point. Jordan doesn't ignore the body's limits. Jordan honors them. The body releases slightly when the honoring happens. Jordan's shoulders drop. Jordan's face softens. The guilt arrives immediately — guilt about leaving early, guilt about not being the person who stays. But underneath the guilt is something else: relief. The relief is that the body's limits are being recognized as legitimate. The mechanism is reversing. The social obligation is no longer overriding the physical reality.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 2

---
Sam stops trying to do all the things. The stop is an experiment in disappointment. Sam is going to disappoint some people. Some relationships will wane. Some opportunities will be missed. The peace comes from acknowledging that all the demands cannot be managed. Sam will choose some things and let other things go. The guilt that arrives is expected. MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3

COST_INTENSITY: 2

---
Maya admits that she can't perform for everyone. The admission is quiet and serious. Maya is not going to be the version of herself that everyone expects. Maya is going to be a more limited version.
