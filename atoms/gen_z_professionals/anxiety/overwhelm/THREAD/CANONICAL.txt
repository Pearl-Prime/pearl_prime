
## THREAD v01
---

---
The overwhelm has a threshold -- a specific point where your system switches from coping to flooding. That threshold isn't fixed. It moves based on something most people never track. Once you track it, the overwhelm becomes less random.
---


## THREAD v02
---

---
There's a difference between being at capacity and being over capacity. Your system has been in one of those states long enough that you've lost the reference point for the other. Recalibrating that distinction changes what you do next.
---


## THREAD v03
---

---
The first thing the overwhelm takes is your ability to prioritize. Once everything feels equally urgent, you're in the overwhelm's operating system, not yours. There's a way to get your own system back online, and it's smaller than you'd expect.
---
