
## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You're allowed to be overwhelmed. Full stop. Not because you're weak. Because the load is genuinely too much, and admitting that is the first honest thing you've said to yourself in a while. The overwhelm isn't evidence against you. It's evidence about the situation.
---


## PERMISSION v02
---

---
Permission to drop a ball. Not every ball is glass. Some are rubber. They'll bounce. You've been treating everything as fragile because the overwhelm makes prioritization feel impossible. Let something be less important today. The world will adjust.
---


## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You don't have to earn the right to say "this is too much" by first trying every optimization, hack, and workaround. You can say it now. You can say it before you collapse. The overwhelm being real doesn't require proof of exhaustion as evidence.
---
