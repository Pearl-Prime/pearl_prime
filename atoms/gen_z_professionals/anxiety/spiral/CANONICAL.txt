Jordan maps the spiral's architecture. Every chain has the same structure: ambiguous event, first interpretation (negative), extrapolation, catastrophe, identity threat. Priya notices the spiral has a time signature. Morning spirals are short—ten minutes, interrupted by work demands. Evening spirals run unchecked—forty minutes, an hour. Dev finds the spiral has a physical entry point. It doesn't start with a thought. It starts with a sensation—chest tightness, usually. The thought arrives to explain the sensation.
