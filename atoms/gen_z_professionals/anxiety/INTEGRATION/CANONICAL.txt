mode: BODY_LANDED
reframe_type: BODY_FACT

carry_line: "You're still here."
---
The alarm ran today. It ran during the call. It ran when the reply didn't come. It ran when the to-do list sat open and nothing on it moved. This is what the alarm does when it mistakes urgency for danger. Your nervous system can't distinguish the deadline from the threat. It fires the same response either way. So it ran.
Notice your hands now. Still. The call is over. The reply will come or it won't. The list is there and you are not currently in it. Chest rising. Breath somewhere in your ribs. The alarm ran through the day and the day moved anyway.
What fired this week was not a failure. It was a system doing its job in the wrong conditions.
The system ran. You're still here.
