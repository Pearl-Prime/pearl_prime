
## PERMISSION v01
---

---
Your alarm system going off does not mean you are weak. It means you are wired to protect yourself, and the wiring is sensitive. You are allowed to feel the full surge of a false alarm and still recognize, afterward, that you are safe. Recognition after the fact still counts.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You do not need to justify the adrenaline. You do not need to explain why a text message made your heart race or why a calendar notification triggered a cortisol spike. Your body has reasons that do not require your conscious approval. Accepting the response without judgment is part of the recalibration — not bypassing it.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
Permission to be tired from alarms that were not real. The energy was real. The body does not care about the source — it spent the same resources. You are allowed to need recovery from threats that never materialized. The exhaustion is valid even when the danger was not.
---
