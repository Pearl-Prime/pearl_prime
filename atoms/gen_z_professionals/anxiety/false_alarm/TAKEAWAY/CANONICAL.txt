
## TAKEAWAY v01
---

---
Your body cannot tell the difference between a real threat and a remembered one. That is not a flaw. That is biology running yesterday's update on today's situation.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---

---
The false alarm costs the same adrenaline as the real thing. Your system does not do refunds. Honor that expense even when the danger was not real — the body spent something genuine.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---

---
Not every alarm means fire. But every alarm means your system cared enough to check. The calibration is off. The intention is sound.
---

## TAKEAWAY v04
---

---
You do not need certainty before you move. You need a move small enough to survive uncertainty. The nervous system learns from action, not from reassurance.
---
