Jordan maps the shame trigger: it fires on any moment of unscripted visibility. Planned visibility—presentations, scheduled updates—he can manage. The shame is contained because the performance is prepared. Unscripted visibility—impromptu questions, being called on, off-agenda comments—lights up the circuit without warning.
