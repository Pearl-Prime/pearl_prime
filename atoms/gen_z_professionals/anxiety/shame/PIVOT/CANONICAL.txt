
## PIVOT v01

---
The old frame: you should be over this by now. Everyone else seems fine. The new frame: shame is the anxiety about having anxiety. It's the meta-loop that makes the original feeling twice as heavy. You're not failing because you're anxious. You're carrying two loads -- the anxiety and the judgment about it.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Shame says your anxiety proves something is wrong with you. Here's what shame won't tell you: the fact that you feel ashamed of struggling means you internalized a standard that was never real. Nobody's feed shows the panic attack before the presentation. Shame runs on the gap between the curated version and the lived version.

## PIVOT v03

---
