Priya sits with a patient in the clinic and feels nothing. The patient is describing pain. Describing fear. Describing a health crisis. And Priya's body has no response. No activation. No empathic resonance. Just exhaustion. And underneath the exhaustion is shame. Priya became a nurse because she cared. Because she wanted to help. Now she's sitting with a person who needs care and Priya is completely numb. The thought arrives: I'm a bad nurse. I'm a bad person. I got into this work for pure reasons and now I've become someone who can't feel empathy for someone in pain. The shame is about the loss of capacity. Not about the patient. The patient is just the mirror that shows Priya what Priya has become. Empty. Incapable of the very thing that made Priya choose this work. The numbness itself is evidence of failure.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_professionals/anchored/compassion_fatigue/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Priya starts to understand the mechanism. The compassion fatigue isn't about moral failure. It's about nervous system depletion. Empathy requires nervous system availability. When the nervous system is completely depleted, the circuits for empathy literally don't activate. The numbness isn't a choice. It's a symptom. But understanding this doesn't change the shame because the shame is about the visible symptom, not the invisible cause. The mechanism is that compassion fatigue looks like coldness. So even though the coldness isn't moral failure, it appears like moral failure. And the appearance creates the shame. Priya knows intellectually that the emptiness comes from the pace. But watching herself be numb with a suffering patient produces shame regardless of the cause. The mechanism is that compassion fatigue converts an occupational hazard into a character flaw. Priya is being depleted by the job. But Priya is being shamed for the depletion. The shame ensures Priya keeps the depletion hidden. Which means the depletion gets worse.
---
