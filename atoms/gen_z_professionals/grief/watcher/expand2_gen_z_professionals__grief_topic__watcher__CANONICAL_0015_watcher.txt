## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v05 — SONG BREAKS THROUGH — LATE
---
Nadia hears it in the coffee shop. The song from her first day. The one playing when she walked in nervous and new. It bypasses the glass completely. She's not watching herself react. She's in the memory. The nerves. The hope. The person she was. The watcher can't observe a song from distance. For a moment, she's inside too.
