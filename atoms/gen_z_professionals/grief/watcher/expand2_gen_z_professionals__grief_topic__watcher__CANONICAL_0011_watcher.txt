## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01 — CRACK IN GLASS — MID
---
Kai is cleaning out the Slack channel Maya started. Archiving it. Then something shifts. Maya's first message. "Hey team! Excited to be here." The words are real. Not real like information—real like feeling. They're here. In this moment. Reading this message. For three seconds, the glass isn't there. They're grieving. Not watching. Then it closes. But it opened.
