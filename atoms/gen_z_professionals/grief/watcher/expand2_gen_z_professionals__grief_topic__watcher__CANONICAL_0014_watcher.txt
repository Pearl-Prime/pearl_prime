## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v04 — TEAR HAPPENS — MID
---
Marcus is fine at Nadia's going-away dinner. Composed. Watching himself toast and smile. Then his eyes water without permission. The tear falls before he can observe it forming. He feels it on his cheek—wet, present, real. The tear wasn't performed. It happened to him. The watcher didn't make that tear. Marcus did.
