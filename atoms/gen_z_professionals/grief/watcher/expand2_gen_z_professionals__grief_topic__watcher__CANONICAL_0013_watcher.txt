## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v03 — BODY RECLAIMS — EARLY
---
Priya drops her coffee. It splatters on the floor. The sound arrives immediately—sharp, here, now. Not delayed. Not muted. She's in her body. Aware of the mess. The heat through her socks. The apartment around her. The watcher distance collapses. Then returns. But the spill was real-time. She was in it.
