## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v03 — MEMORY GAP — EARLY
---
Priya can't remember the last office day. She was there—the photos prove it. The goodbye cake. The awkward group shot. But the day itself is a gap. She packed her desk. Said the words. The evidence exists on her phone. The memory doesn't. The grief took the ending and left her standing in her apartment with boxes she doesn't remember carrying.
