## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v04 — EMOTIONAL LAG — MID
---
Marcus hears that Nadia got the job in Singapore. Really leaving. Different continent. The words land and his face stays calm. He says "amazing opportunity" and "so happy for her." Twenty minutes later, in the bathroom, his eyes sting. The feeling arrives late. He wasn't sad in the meeting. He's sad now, in the stall, where Nadia can't see it.
