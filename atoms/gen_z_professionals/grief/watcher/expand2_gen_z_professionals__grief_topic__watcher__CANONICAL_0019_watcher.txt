## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v04 — HAND ON CARD — MID
---
Marcus picks up Nadia's card. The paper is in his hand. He feels it—thick, textured. He's about to drift. He doesn't. Reads the words from here. "Miss you already." The words are in his hands, not behind the glass. He reads them present. They land. Not fully. But more than before.
