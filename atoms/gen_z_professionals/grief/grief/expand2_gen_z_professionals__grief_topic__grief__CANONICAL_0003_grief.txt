## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v03 — PLACE LOSS — EARLY — HEAVINESS
---
Priya gets the email. The office is closing permanently. Remote forever. She stands in the empty floor one last time. Her desk. The kitchen where she ate sad salads. The conference room where she bombed her first presentation. The heaviness fills her chest. She hated this commute. She's going to miss this commute. The building will be something else next year. Condos, probably.
