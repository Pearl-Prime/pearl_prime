## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v05 — WAVES DON'T ASK PERMISSION — EARLY
---
Nadia is fine in the meeting. Fine on the call. Fine until she sees a new hire with the same eager energy she used to have. The wave arrives without warning. In the conference room. Eyes stinging, chest tight. For who she used to be. For the energy that's somewhere in the past. The loss of her former self doesn't ask permission.
