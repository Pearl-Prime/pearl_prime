## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v04 — FRIEND LOSS — MID — CHEST ACHE
---
Marcus sees Nadia at the conference. They wave. Keep walking. Three years of being inseparable in the early days—same start date, same onboarding cohort, same complaints. Now they wave. The friendship didn't end. She got promoted. Different floor, different schedule, different lunch crowd. Marcus's chest aches watching her laugh with her new team. The inside jokes are still in his head.
