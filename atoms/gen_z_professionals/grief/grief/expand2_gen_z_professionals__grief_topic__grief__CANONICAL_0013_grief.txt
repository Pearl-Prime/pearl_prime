## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v03 — SOMEONE MENTIONS THEM — EARLY
---
Priya freezes when Marcus says it. "Remember when the office printer jammed every Monday? Like clockwork." The office, out loud. Priya braces. But the wave doesn't drown her. Marcus is half-smiling, remembering. Other people miss it too. The office exists in conversation, not just in absence. The missing has company.
