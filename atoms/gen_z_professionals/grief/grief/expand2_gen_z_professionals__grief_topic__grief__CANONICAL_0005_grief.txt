## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v05 — SELF LOSS — LATE — EYES STINGING
---
Nadia finds her old notebook. From her first job. The handwriting is eager. Stars next to ideas. Underlines everywhere. "Change the industry" written on page one. That version of her is gone. She's still working. She's not that worker. Her eyes sting looking at the person who thought ambition would feel different. The notebook is real. The hope isn't anymore.
