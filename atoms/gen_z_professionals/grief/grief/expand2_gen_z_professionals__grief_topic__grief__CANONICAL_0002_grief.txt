## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v02 — RELATIONSHIP LOSS — LATE — HOLLOWNESS
---
Jordan walks past the coffee shop. The one where they met Alex every Saturday. Alex is in Chicago now. Different city, different life. The table by the window has someone else sitting in it. The hollowness opens. Two years of Saturdays. Gone somewhere between a long-distance phone call and a text that said "I think we should talk." The coffee shop didn't change. Everything else did.
