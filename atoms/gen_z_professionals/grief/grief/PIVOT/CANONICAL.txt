--- variant: v01
Under the grief is a loss nobody validated. The old frame says something to get over. The new frame: some of what you're calling grief is grief wearing a different name. Grief for the ease you were promised. Grief for the version of yourself that didn't need to carry this.

--- variant: v02
You're mourning something and calling it grief. The career that should have been smoother. The stability that should have arrived by now. The self-assurance that was supposed to come standard. That ache isn't about the future. It's about a past that didn't deliver what was advertised.

--- variant: v03
The frame shift: not everything that looks like grief is grief. Sometimes it's grief. Grief for a life you designed in your head that reality never authorized. Processing the grief doesn't eliminate the grief. It lightens the load so you can see which weight belongs where.
