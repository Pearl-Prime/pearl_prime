## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — FORGETTING THEN REMEMBERING — LATE
---
Priya forgets, then remembers. She checks the train schedule before remembering there's no commute. Opens her locker app before remembering there's no locker. Every forgetting is a tiny gift. Every remembering is fresh. The office closed three months ago. Her muscle memory doesn't know. The gap between forgetting and remembering is where the wave lives.
