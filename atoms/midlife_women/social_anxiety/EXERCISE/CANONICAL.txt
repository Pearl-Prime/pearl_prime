

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before you walk into the room, plant both feet shoulder-width apart. Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. You do not need to explain why you look different than you did ten years ago.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Put your phone away before you scroll the group photos. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. Their highlight reel is not a record of your worth this week.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Unclench your hands at your sides. Let your shoulders drop. Count to five. You are allowed to enter a room without performing that everything in your life is fine.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Pick one person to make eye contact with first. Hold it for three seconds. Say one true sentence. Let that be the whole opening move.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Press your feet into the floor before you speak in the group. Count to three. Your voice does not need to compete with the younger women in the room to be worth hearing.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. Say silently: "I do not have to be the version of myself from fifteen years ago to belong here."
---
