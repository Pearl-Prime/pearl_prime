


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that the social ease you performed for twenty years was a performance, and that the performance took something from you, and that you do not have the same reserves for it that you once did.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to leave the party earlier than expected without an elaborate explanation. The headache is real, even when its name is exhaustion.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel socially anxious even though you have been reading rooms and managing other people's comfort for decades. Competence in a skill does not protect you from the cost of using it.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to choose smaller social circles, slower social pace, fewer obligations that require you to perform ease you do not actually feel.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to stop apologizing for needing to recover after social interactions. Introversion and anxiety are not the same thing, and sometimes neither label fully captures what is true for you.
---
