

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit up straight at your desk. Place both feet flat on the floor. Say silently: "I have done this work for twenty years." Repeat twice before the meeting starts.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Write down one thing you know that the room does not. Read it silently. Fold the paper. Carry it into the meeting without showing anyone.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Unclench your jaw before you speak. Let your shoulders drop an inch. Take one breath before your first sentence. You returned to this field; you did not arrive new to it.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Place one hand flat on the table before you answer a question. Press down for three counts. This steadies the voice that thinks it forgot how to belong here.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Stand in the hallway before you walk in. Roll your shoulders back twice. Lift your chin slightly. The gap on your resume is not evidence you are unqualified.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. Say silently: "Being the oldest one in the room is not the same as being the least capable." Repeat once more.
---
