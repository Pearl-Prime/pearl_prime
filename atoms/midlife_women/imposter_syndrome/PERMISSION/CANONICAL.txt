


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to name what it cost you to be underestimated for twenty years — the promotion not pursued, the opinion swallowed, the idea you presented quietly so it wouldn't be dismissed.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to believe in your own expertise even though people younger than you are now in positions above you. The path was not fair. Your knowledge is real.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to take up space in the meeting even though the internalized message of your career has been to take less of it. Taking up space is not arrogance. It is accuracy.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to stop downplaying your credentials before you speak. The qualifying language protected you once. You do not owe it forever.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to feel the impostor feeling and speak anyway. The feeling is old. What you know is current and earned.
---
