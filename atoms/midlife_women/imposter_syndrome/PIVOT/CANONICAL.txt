## PIVOT v01
---

---
Twenty years of being interrupted, underestimated, and having her ideas attributed to others left a trace. The internal voice that says she doesn't quite belong is not an accurate assessment of her competence. It is the sedimented residue of how she has been treated — accurate data about the environment, mislabeled as data about herself.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
She has been preparing three times as hard for half as much credit for so long that she has confused rigor with inadequacy. Her preparation is not evidence that she is insufficient. It is evidence that she learned, correctly, that she was operating in a system that required more from her than from others. That is the system's failure, not hers.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The voice that says she doesn't belong has not updated to include the last two decades of evidence. It is running on the data from the beginning of her career, when she was young and female and underestimated and trying to prove herself in rooms that hadn't decided whether to take her seriously. She has proven herself. The voice missed the memo.
---
