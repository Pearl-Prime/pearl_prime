## PERMISSION v01
---
---
You are allowed to do the tiny, unimpressive version of the task instead of the whole thing, and let that be enough for today.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---
---
You are allowed to need your important tasks to look different, brighter, or newly placed before your brain will actually notice them.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---
---
You are allowed to talk your way into a task out loud before you can write your way into it alone.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---
---
You are allowed to put a deadline somewhere physical and early instead of trusting your own internal sense of time to warn you.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---
---
You are allowed to ask someone to simply be present while you do a task, without that meaning you couldn't have done it "properly" alone.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---
---
You are allowed to say a task's real size out loud and believe the plain sentence over the accumulated dread.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---
---
You are allowed to question whether the hard version of a task in your head is even the current, accurate version.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---
---
You are allowed to make your private plans social and witnessed instead of expecting your own willpower to hold them alone.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---
---
You are allowed to sign the form standing up, in the car, in the hallway, without waiting for the moment you'd originally imagined.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---
---
You are allowed to schedule your real working session for the night urgency actually arrives, instead of shaming yourself for the calm week that never worked.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---
---
You are allowed to borrow someone else's first draft, spoken or written, and make it yours in your own hand.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---
---
You are allowed to track your own avoidance lightly, even find it a little funny, instead of only ever meeting it with shame.
---
