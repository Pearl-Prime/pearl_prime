


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to wake at 3am with your heart already running and not have a reason ready. The cortisol surge is real. The body is not lying to you, and it does not require a justification.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel more anxious than you did at thirty, even though you know more and have survived more. Perimenopause is rewriting your nervous system's thresholds. That is biology, not failure.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to still be scanning for threats. You learned that skill when small people depended on you staying alert. It kept them safe. You are allowed to also learn, slowly, how to put it down.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to tell the therapist that the anxiety feels different now — heavier, more physical, less connected to any specific fear. That difference is information worth saying out loud.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to cancel the social obligation because your nervous system has already given everything it had by Thursday evening.
---
