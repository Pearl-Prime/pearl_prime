

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Stand tall. Place both feet firmly on the ground. Say one true sentence out loud about what you want next. Let your voice fill the room. You are allowed to want something new at this age.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Write down one decision you have been postponing. Read it out loud once. Fold the paper. You do not have to act today. You are only naming it without flinching.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Roll your shoulders back. Lift your chin an inch. Hold this posture for ten counts. This is the posture of the decision you are considering, not the one you already made years ago.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Place your hand on your chest. Feel your own pulse. Say silently: "I am allowed to start over." Repeat three times. Starting over in midlife is not a failure of timing.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Stand in front of a mirror. Look directly at your own eyes for ten counts. Do not look away first. This is the same steadiness the next hard conversation will require.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Take one small action toward the change today. Send the email. Make the call. Book the appointment. Do only this one thing. Let it be enough for today.
---
