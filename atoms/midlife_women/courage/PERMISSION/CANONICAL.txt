


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to leave the career that made financial sense. The financial sense was real. So is the cost of staying somewhere that is taking years you will not get back.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to say the thing you have been not saying in the marriage. The saying will cost something. The not-saying is costing you more.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to choose yourself at forty-seven, even though you were taught that choosing yourself was the selfish option, and even though the selfless version of you built everything you have.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to be afraid of what comes after the courageous act and do it anyway. Fear and courage inhabit the same moment. Only one of them defines what you do next.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to want a different second half. The first half was real and mattered. So does the wanting.
---
