

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Stand in front of a mirror. Look at your own face for ten counts. Do not evaluate what you see. Just let yourself be looked at by you.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Write one sentence about something you did today that had nothing to do with caretaking. Read it silently. Let it count on its own.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Place a hand on your own chest. Say silently: "I am allowed to matter outside of what I provide." Repeat twice, slowly.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Sit up straight. Roll your shoulders back. Hold this posture for ten counts without apologizing for taking up the space.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Name one skill you have that predates your role as mother, wife, or daughter. Say it out loud once. Let it stand without qualifying it.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. You have spent decades measured by what you give others. Let this breath be measured by nothing.
---
