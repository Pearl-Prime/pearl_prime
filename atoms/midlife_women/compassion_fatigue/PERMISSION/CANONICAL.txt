


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel nothing when your teenager comes to you with a problem tonight. The nothing is not cruelty. It is the limit of a capacity that has been giving without replenishing for twenty years.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to admit that you are tired of being the person everyone else comes to. You are allowed to want that to be someone else's role for a while.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel grief about the loss of your own capacity to care. You became the family's emotional center because you were good at it, and now your own good work has cost you.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to tell your therapist that you don't want to talk about anyone else's feelings today. You get to have a session that is only about yours.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to recognize that what looks like selfishness from the outside is what survival looks like from the inside.
---
