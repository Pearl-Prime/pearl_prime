


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to not have it all figured out right now. The brain that planned everyone else's life for twenty years can take longer than a month to figure out what it wants for itself.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to sit with an unanswered question without forcing it toward a decision. Not every loop needs to be resolved tonight.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to notice that the overthinking is partly grief: you are thinking about who you were supposed to become, and the gap between that and now is real and deserves some mourning, not just more planning.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to turn the planning brain toward your own life — your own desires, your own timeline, your own definition of what the second half looks like — even if that feels unfamiliar and even slightly terrifying.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to feel confused about what you want. Decades of orienting toward everyone else's needs can leave your own wants genuinely unclear. That is not failure. It is where you are starting from.
---
