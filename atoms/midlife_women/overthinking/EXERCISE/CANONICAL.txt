

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Write down the decision looping in your head. Write only one sentence. Set the pen down. The choice between your mother's care options does not need to resolve in this second.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place both hands flat on your thighs. Press down. Count to five. Release. Your mind has been running the same three scenarios for an hour. Let your hands interrupt it.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Say out loud the one fact you actually know right now. Stop there. Do not add the next five hypotheticals. One fact is enough for this breath.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. Count backward from twenty, one number per exhale. This gives the looping mind a single, simple task.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Stand up. Walk to another room. Stand still there for ten seconds. The physical distance interrupts the loop about your child's school, your job, your parent's next appointment.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Name three of your senses right now: what you hear, what you see, what you feel against your skin. This pulls attention out of the spiral and into this room.
---
