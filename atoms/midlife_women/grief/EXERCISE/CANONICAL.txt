

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit somewhere quiet. Place both hands in your lap, palms up. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. Let the missing be here without fixing it. Repeat five times.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Hold an object that belonged to the parent you lost, or place your hand where it would be. Breathe once, slowly. You do not have to feel ready for this.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Place one hand on your chest. Say silently: "She is gone, and I am still here." Repeat twice. Let both parts of the sentence stay true at once.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Cry if it comes. Let your shoulders shake if they need to. Set a hand on your own knee. You do not need to explain this grief to anyone in the room.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Look at a photograph from before the loss. Hold it for ten seconds. Set it down gently. This is one visit, not the whole grief, today.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Hold for two. Breathe out for six. You are also grieving the version of yourself before this loss. Let that grief have its own breath too.
---
