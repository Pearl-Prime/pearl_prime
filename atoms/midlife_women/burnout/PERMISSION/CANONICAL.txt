


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be depleted even though, from the outside, your life looks like it is working. Depletion is not visible in a functioning calendar.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to name that you are being pulled in six directions simultaneously — parent, child, employer, partner, patient, yourself — and that this is not a sustainable arrangement.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel resentment toward the people you love most. Resentment is what happens when giving continues past the point where it was freely given.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to put your own medical appointment on the calendar before anyone else's and not feel like that makes you selfish.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to want something back. You are allowed to notice how long it has been since you received care that asked nothing in return.
---
