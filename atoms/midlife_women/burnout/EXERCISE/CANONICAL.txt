

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit in the parked car before you go inside. Turn the engine off. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. This is the only transition your day gives you. Take it fully before you open the door.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand at the kitchen counter. Place both hands flat on it. Press down for five counts. Release. You have been the family's administrative center since this morning. Let the counter hold your weight instead.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Close the laundry room door behind you. Stand still for thirty seconds. Breathe in for four. Out for four. No one needs anything from you in this specific room, right now.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Sit on the edge of the bed. Let your shoulders drop fully. Count to ten. You have been holding the household's schedule in your head all day. Set it down for these ten counts.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Run cool water over both wrists for fifteen seconds. Feel the temperature travel upward. This interrupts the flat, wired feeling of running on empty since your mother's last hospital call.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Lean your head back against the chair. Let your eyes close. Set no timer in your mind. Two minutes of nothing is recovery, not indulgence, after a day of managing everyone else's crises.
---
