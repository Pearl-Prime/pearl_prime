## PIVOT v01
---

---
This is not depletion from one source — it is depletion from six simultaneous sources over a decade. The body can sustain one major drain and recover. It cannot sustain the career, the marriage, the aging parent, the growing children, the perimenopause, and the financial pressure at the same time, indefinitely, without something starting to go quiet inside.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
She is not tired because she is weak. She is tired because the load was genuinely too heavy for too long, and she carried it anyway, and now the body is presenting the bill. Burnout is not a character flaw. It is a biological response to sustained overextension.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The numbness is the last thing before the body stops cooperating entirely. It is not indifference — it is the system protecting itself from further injury. She didn't fail to care enough. She cared so much, for so long, that caring itself became the wound.
