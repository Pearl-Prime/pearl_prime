

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Open the curtains fully. Stand in the light for thirty seconds. Do not evaluate how you feel. Just let the light land on your skin.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place both feet flat on the floor. Press down slowly. Count to ten. You do not have to feel motivated to do this. Just do the pressing.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Drink one full glass of water slowly. Notice the temperature. Notice your throat swallowing. This is one concrete task you can complete today, fully, without anyone else's approval.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Stand up from the chair you have been sitting in for hours. Walk to the nearest window. Look outside for one full minute. Let your eyes rest on something distant.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Put one hand flat on the wall. Press for five counts. Release. The flatness you feel today is not a verdict on your whole life. It is today's weather in your body.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Name the day of the week out loud. Name the room you are in. Name one object within reach. This is enough orienting for this moment. You do not need to solve anything else right now.
---
