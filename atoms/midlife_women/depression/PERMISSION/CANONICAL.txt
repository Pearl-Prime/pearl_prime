


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to name what is happening as depression even though you are still functioning, still showing up, still getting things done. Depression has a version that looks fine from the outside.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the self you lost so gradually you didn't notice the leaving. She didn't disappear all at once. She went quietly, one compromise at a time.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to want more than this — more feeling, more aliveness, more recognition that your life is actually yours — without it meaning you are ungrateful for what you have built.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to tell your doctor about the flatness, the low-level nothing, the sense that the color turned down several years ago and has not come back. That is information she needs.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to need help. Midlife depression is not weakness dressed up as a phase. It is a signal that something important in you needs attention.
---
