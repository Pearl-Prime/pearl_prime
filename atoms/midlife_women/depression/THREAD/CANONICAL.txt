


## THREAD v01
---

---
The version of yourself you lost did not leave in a single event you can point to. She went in increments — a preference not pursued, a desire set aside, a part of you that stopped being asked about and then stopped speaking up. Finding her requires going back through those increments.
---

## THREAD v02
---

---
Midlife depression that hides inside a functioning life is especially hard to treat because the evidence against it is everywhere — look how much you have, look how much you do. The emptiness underneath is real despite the evidence.
---

## THREAD v03
---

---
The aliveness you are looking for is not ahead of you in some different life. It is available in this one, in smaller increments than you expect, starting with telling the truth about what you have actually been feeling.
