


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel crushed by the weight of your parent's medical costs arriving at the same time as your child's college costs. This is not a personal failing. This is a structural trap that nobody warned you about.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the career pause that cost you years of earnings and compounding that cannot be recovered. The loss is real. Naming it is not complaint — it is accuracy.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to say out loud that the financial stress is affecting your health, your sleep, your marriage, your ability to be present. The stress does not live only in the spreadsheet.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to ask for help navigating the financial pressure without it meaning you are not capable. Competence does not require solving this alone.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to feel the full weight of what carrying this financial burden has taken from you — the trips not taken, the repairs deferred, the version of yourself that had space to breathe.
