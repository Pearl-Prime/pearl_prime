

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Push the stack of bills to one side of the table. Place both hands flat where they were. Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. This pile is not due this exact second.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Press your feet firmly into the floor. Count to six. Release. Press again. The tuition bill and the parent's copay are both real. Your feet are still under you.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Roll your shoulders back three times. Let them drop lower each time. You have been carrying two households' finances in your shoulders since this morning. Set some of that weight down.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Say out loud: "This is a math problem, not a character problem." Repeat once more, slower. Let the sentence land before you return to the numbers.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Drink a full glass of water before you open the spreadsheet again. Notice the temperature. This pause belongs to you before the numbers take the room back.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Place one hand over your heart. Feel it beating for eight counts. The strain of covering two generations' costs is heavy. Your heart is still doing its steady work underneath it.
---
