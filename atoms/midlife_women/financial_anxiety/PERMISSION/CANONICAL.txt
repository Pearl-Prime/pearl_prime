


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel genuine fear about retirement savings that are behind where you planned they would be. The fear is proportionate. It is also not the whole story.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to resent the years when your income went entirely to other people's needs — the college tuition, the parent's care, the household — and left nothing to compound for you. That resentment is fair.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to tell your financial planner the truth: that your security feels precarious and that you are afraid of what the numbers actually show. The truth is the beginning of the plan.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to say no to a financial request from your adult child even though you have always found a way to say yes. Protecting your retirement is not a failure of love.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the version of the future you planned for and now have to revise. The plan was not foolish. The circumstances changed.
---
