

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit at the table where the bills live. Place both hands flat on the surface. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. You do not have to solve the retirement math tonight.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Close the banking app. Set the phone down. Press your palm against your chest. Count to ten. The number on the screen is not happening to you this second.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Write down one number you are afraid of. Read it silently. Fold the paper away. You are naming the fear, not solving it, in this exercise.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Unclench your hands slowly. Spread your fingers. Hold for four counts. Your hands have been gripping the calculator, the spreadsheet, the college tuition bill. Let them open now.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Stand and stretch both arms overhead. Breathe in as you reach. Hold for four counts. Lower slowly. The number in your retirement account did not move while you took this breath.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Place one hand on your stomach. Breathe until the hand rises. Repeat five breaths. The dread about supporting parents and children on one income lives here first.
---
