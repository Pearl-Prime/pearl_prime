

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before you answer the family group chat, put your phone down. Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. You do not owe an instant reply to every request. Wait sixty seconds before you respond.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Say the word "no" out loud, alone, once. Notice your jaw. Notice your stomach. Let the sound finish completely. You are practicing the shape of the word before you need it with your mother.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Place one hand on your stomach. Press gently. This is where you feel the guilt before you have even said no. Breathe into that spot for four full breaths. The guilt is a feeling, not a verdict.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Roll your shoulders back three times. Let them drop an inch each time. Your shoulders rise every time your adult child calls needing something. Let them come down now, on your own time.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Write one sentence on paper: "I am not available right now." Read it silently. Fold the paper. You do not have to send it today. You are only practicing that the sentence exists.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Press down through both feet. Count to five. This is your ground, not your sister's expectations or your husband's assumptions. Feel your own weight here.
---
