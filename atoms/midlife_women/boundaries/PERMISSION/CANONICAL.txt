


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to say no to your adult child without it meaning you have stopped loving them. The love is not in the yes. The love was there long before the yes, and it remains in the no.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to stop answering work emails after 7pm even though you have answered them after 7pm for fifteen years. A pattern is not a contract.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to tell your aging parent that you cannot come this weekend. You are allowed to feel guilty and go anyway on the weekend you choose. You are allowed to feel guilty and not go.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to protect one hour of your day for something that is only yours, with no explanation offered to anyone in the household.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to hold the line even when the person pushing it is someone you love. Love does not require you to collapse.
---
