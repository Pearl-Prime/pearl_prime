## PIVOT v01
---

---
She learned yes as a survival skill — yes was how a woman stayed safe, stayed valued, stayed connected. The body learned it before she had words for it. What looks like a lack of boundaries is not weakness; it is an old strategy that no longer serves the life she is actually in.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The guilt she feels when she doesn't answer the phone is not evidence that she is a bad daughter. It is evidence of how long she was trained to believe that her presence was owed, that her availability was love. The guilt is a reflex, not a verdict.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
She has been giving from the same well for twenty years and calling it generosity. What a boundary does is not take something away from others. It creates the conditions under which she can keep giving at all.
