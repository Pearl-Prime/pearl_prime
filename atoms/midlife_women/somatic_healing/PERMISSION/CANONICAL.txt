


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to give your body the attention it has been waiting for. It kept everyone else alive for forty-plus years. It is not too late for it to also be cared for.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel the anger stored in your shoulders, the grief stored in your chest, the vigilance stored in your jaw — and to let those things move through you without immediately translating them into a task or an apology.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to treat body-based practices as legitimate healing rather than indulgence. Yoga, breathwork, somatic therapy — these are not luxury items. They are the method that matches the problem.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to learn, slowly, what your body needs — not what it can endure, not what it can deliver, but what it actually needs to feel safe and whole.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to cry when the bodyworker finds the knot you have been carrying for eight years. The crying is not embarrassment. It is forty years of accumulated service finally having somewhere to go.
---
