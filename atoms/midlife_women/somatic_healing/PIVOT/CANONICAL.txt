## PIVOT v01
---

---
The body held what the mind managed. Every crisis she got through without breaking, every year she ran on less sleep than she needed, every need she absorbed without asking for help — the body kept the account. What she feels in her shoulders, her jaw, her gut is not weakness. It is the record of a life spent in service with insufficient recovery. The body is not failing. It is presenting evidence.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
She has spent decades managing her body — controlling its appetites, pushing past its limits, overriding its signals when they were inconvenient. What somatic healing asks is the opposite: to listen before acting, to feel before managing, to let the body's information reach her before the fixing instinct cuts it off. This is not a small thing for a woman who has been managing everything including herself for twenty years.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The healing is slow because the pattern is old and the pattern is structural. She did not create the tension in her body in a week and she will not release it in a week. What she can do is start making a different agreement: that her body's signals are data, not interruptions. That her body is something to tend, not something to overcome.
