

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Place both hands on your stomach. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. Your body has been running hotter and stranger than it used to. Meet it here without correcting it.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Sit still through the heat wave when it rises. Do not fight it. Breathe in for four counts. Out for four. Let it crest and pass through you.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Press your palm flat against your chest. Feel your heart's own rhythm, separate from the racing your hormones cause today. Count ten beats slowly.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Roll your neck slowly in one direction, five times. Reverse. Your body is not betraying you. It is changing on its own timeline, and it still deserves this attention.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Run cool water over your wrists and the back of your neck. Count to fifteen. This is not vanity. This is caring for a body doing unfamiliar work.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Lie down flat. Let your spine fully touch the floor. Breathe into your ribs for six breaths. This body carried children, work, and grief. It is allowed this rest now.
