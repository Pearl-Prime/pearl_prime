

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
If you wake at three, keep the lights off. Place one hand on your stomach. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. Repeat ten times before you check the clock.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Uncurl your toes. Unclench your jaw. Let your tongue rest low in your mouth. Your body braced for the day before your mind woke up. Let it stand down now.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Name five things you can feel against your skin right now: sheets, air, pillow, your own hands, the mattress. This grounds the mind before it starts listing tomorrow's tasks.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Press your feet against the end of the bed. Hold for five counts. Release. The heat and the racing mind at 3am are not the same signal as danger.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Breathe in through your nose for four counts. Out through your mouth for six. Repeat until your next inhale slows on its own. Do not check the clock during this count.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Place a pillow between your knees or against your chest. Feel the pressure evenly. Let your full weight sink into the mattress. You do not have to solve anything at this hour.
---
