


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be furious that your body wakes you at 2am in a sweat and then hands you three hours of worst-case thinking. The body is not doing this to you on purpose. And you are allowed to be furious anyway.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the sleep you used to have — the deep, reliable sleep that felt like yours and now isn't. That loss is real and it affects everything downstream.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to tell your doctor that the sleep disruption is affecting your work, your patience, your ability to show up as the person you want to be. That is medical information, not complaint.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to stop treating 3am like a productivity opportunity. The inbox will still be there when your nervous system is actually equipped to handle it.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to take the sleep problem seriously. It is not laziness to protect your rest. It is the daily resource your entire life runs on.
---
