## PERMISSION v01
---
---
You are allowed to miss a slide, a sentence, or a beat of the meeting while you find your way back to it, without treating the drift as proof you're failing at your job.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---
---
You are allowed to write the worry down instead of carrying it silently, and trust the paper to hold it as well as your head was pretending to.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---
---
You are allowed to need more than one breath, more than one return, more than one meeting before this gets easier, and still call the practice working.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---
---
You are allowed to keep your phone out of reach during the one call that matters most today, even if every other habit in your life stays exactly the same.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---
---
You are allowed to notice you were "somewhere else" in a meeting without spiraling into what that says about you as a mother, an employee, or a person who's supposed to have it together.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---
---
You are allowed to count your catches instead of your failures, and let a rising tally mean you're getting better, not worse.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---
---
You are allowed to feel your feet on the floor for one full second before answering a hard question, even if no one in the room will ever know you did it.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---
---
You are allowed to let the good review, the kind comment, the actual praise land fully, instead of doing pickup math underneath it.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---
---
You are allowed to give a replayed comment fifteen minutes of your full attention on purpose, so it stops taking it from you unannounced for the rest of the week.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---
---
You are allowed to be in the room you're actually in, instead of performing competence from a few feet outside your own body.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---
---
You are allowed to return badly, late, and imperfectly, and still count it as presence.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---
---
You are allowed to protect one single meal, one single call, one single hour today, without having fixed every other hour first.
---
