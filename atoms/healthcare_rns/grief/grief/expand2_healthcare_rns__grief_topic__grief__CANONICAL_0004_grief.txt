## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v04 — RELATIONSHIP LOSS — MID — CHEST ACHE
---
Derek sees Elijah at the station. They nod. Keep charting. Four years of being inseparable and now they nod. The friendship didn't end—Elijah transferred to nights. No fight. Just schedules. Derek's chest aches hearing Elijah laugh with the night shift. The inside jokes are still in his head. The shorthand. They just don't share shifts anymore.
