## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v03 — SOMEONE MENTIONS THEM — EARLY

Sofia freezes when Elijah says it. "Remember Margaret's face when the computer crashed?" Margaret's name, out loud. Sofia braces. But the wave doesn't drown her. Elijah is half-smiling, remembering. Other people knew her too. Margaret exists in conversation, not just in retirement. The missing has company.
