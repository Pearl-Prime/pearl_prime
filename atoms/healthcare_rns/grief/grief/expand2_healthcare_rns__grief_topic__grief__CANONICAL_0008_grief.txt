## GRIEF / MECHANISM_PROOF v03 — FORGETTING THEN REMEMBERING — LATE
---
Sofia forgets, then remembers. She walks toward the old break room before remembering it's gone. Reaches for the locker that doesn't exist. Every forgetting is a tiny gift. Every remembering is fresh. She's been doing this for a year. The muscle memory doesn't update. The break room does. The gap between them is where the wave lives.
