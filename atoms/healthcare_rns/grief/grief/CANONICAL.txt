COST_INTENSITY: 1
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
A patient Maria has been caring for dies. Not unexpectedly. The trajectory was clear. Yet the death still lands. Maria feels it in her chest. There's no formula that makes this easier. The patient had a name. A family who loved them. A life that will no longer be lived. Maria feels the weight of what's been lost. Not loss in the abstract. Loss of this specific person who was alive this morning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/grief/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism is: I can care for dying people and not be affected by their dying. The mechanism produces a certain compartmentalization. Maria's professional capacity is real. She can manage the medical tasks. Yet she cannot avoid the reality that the person dies. The mechanism tries to keep feeling separate from function. But the separation is never complete.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism proves itself through the way grief cannot be scheduled. Chen can't grieve during her shift. She must continue working. The mechanism says: You can feel later. Yet later becomes tomorrow, next week, never. The grief accumulates because the mechanism doesn't allow for processing. The proof is the backlog of ungrieved losses.
