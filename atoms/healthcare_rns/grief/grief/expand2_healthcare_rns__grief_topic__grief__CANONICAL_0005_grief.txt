## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v05 — SELF LOSS — LATE — EYES STINGING
---
Anika finds her first badge in a drawer. The photo is eight years old. New grad. Eager face. Believed she'd change healthcare. That version of her is gone—not dead, just somewhere in the past. She's still a nurse. She's not that nurse. Her eyes sting looking at the woman who thought this work would feel different. The badge is laminated. The hope wasn't.
