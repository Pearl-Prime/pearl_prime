## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v05 — GRIEF HAS A NAME — LATE
---
Anika realizes what the exhaustion is. Not burnout. Grief. For the nurse she used to be. For the energy that's gone. Naming it doesn't fix it. But the size makes sense now. She's mourning someone she was. That's allowed. The tiredness is real. So is the loss. Both things wearing the same face.
