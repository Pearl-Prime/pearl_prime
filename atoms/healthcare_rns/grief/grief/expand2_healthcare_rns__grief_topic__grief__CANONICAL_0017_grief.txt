## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v02 — SAYS IT PRIVATELY — LATE
---
James stands outside 412 at shift change. New patient, new name. He doesn't go in. Just stands in the hallway. "I remember you," he says. Quiet. To no one. To Mr. Patterson. The words land in the empty hallway. Mr. Patterson can't hear them. That's not the point. James needed to say them.
