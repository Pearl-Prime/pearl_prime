## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v03 — PLACE LOSS — EARLY — HEAVINESS
---
Sofia passes the old break room. Supply closet now. They renovated last year. Her locker was there. The couch where she cried after her first code. The microwave that burned everything. The heaviness fills her chest. The room exists—four walls, same location. Her room doesn't. She ate lunch there for six years. Now it holds bedpans.
