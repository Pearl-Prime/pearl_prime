## GRIEF / EMBODIMENT v05 — LOOKS AT THE PHOTO — LATE
---
Anika keeps her old badge in her locker. Not to use—just to have. She looks at it sometimes. The eager face. The different posture. She can't get that nurse back. She can acknowledge she existed. The badge is proof. That version of her was real. Did this work with different energy. The photo doesn't change. She does. Both things in the same locker.
