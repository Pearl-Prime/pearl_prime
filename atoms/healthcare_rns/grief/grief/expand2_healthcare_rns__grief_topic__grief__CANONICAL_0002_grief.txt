## GRIEF / RECOGNITION v02 — PATIENT LOSS — LATE — HOLLOWNESS
---
James walks past room 412. Different name on the board now. Mr. Patterson was there for eight weeks. James knew his coffee order, his daughter's name, his fear of needles. Now the room has someone else's vitals on the monitor. The hollowness opens in his chest. Mr. Patterson died Tuesday. The room filled Wednesday. The shape of him is still there, underneath the new patient.
