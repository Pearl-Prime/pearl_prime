## GRIEF / TURNING_POINT v01 — WAVE DOESN'T DESTROY — MID
---
Rachel expects Mr. Patterson's anniversary to flatten her. One year since he died. She braces for devastation. The wave comes during rounds—his room number, someone else's name. But she doesn't drown. She excuses herself. Breathes in the supply room. Returns. The grief is huge. She's still working. The shift happens around the loss.
