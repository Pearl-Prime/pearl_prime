## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v03 — BODY RECLAIMS — EARLY
---
Sofia drops her badge. It clatters on the floor. The sound arrives immediately—sharp, here, now. Not delayed. Not muted. She's in her body. She's aware of bending down. Of the floor. Of the unit sounds. The watcher distance collapses for a moment. Then returns. But the clatter was real-time. She was in it.
