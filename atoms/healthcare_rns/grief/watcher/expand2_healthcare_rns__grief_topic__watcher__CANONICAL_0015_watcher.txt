## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v05 — SMELL BREAKS THROUGH — LATE
---
Anika opens the locker. Margaret's perfume. Still there, in the metal. The smell bypasses the glass completely. She's not watching herself smell it. She's in the smell. In the memory the smell carries. Margaret getting ready for shift. The watcher can't observe a smell from distance. It's too inside. For a moment, she's inside too.
