## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v02 — DELAYED SENSATION — LATE
---
James's colleague hugs him after the code. "I know that was hard." He feels the pressure of the hug three seconds later, muted and far away. He nods. The words land eventually. Everything about Mrs. Chen's death is reaching him on delay. The code happened. The time of death was called. He's receiving the news of it after the fact.
