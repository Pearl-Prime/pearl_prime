## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v04 — HAND ON OBJECT — MID
---
Derek picks up the gift from Elijah. A coffee mug. "World's Tolerable Coworker." He feels it—ceramic, cool, heavy. He's about to drift. He doesn't. Holds the mug from here. Reads the joke from here. The gift is in his hands, not behind the glass. The humor lands. Not fully, but more than before.
