## WATCHER / TURNING_POINT v01 — CRACK IN GLASS — MID
---
Rachel is filing Mr. Patterson's memorial card when something shifts. The paper is thick. Not thick like a fact—thick like a feeling. She's here. In this moment. Holding this card. For three seconds, the glass isn't there. She's not watching herself grieve. She's grieving. Then it closes. But it opened. She felt the paper. From here.
