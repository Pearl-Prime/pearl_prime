## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/grief/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace traces the patient loss circuit: patient dies, debrief happens, next patient arrives, grief stores in the body. Grace's eyes burn. The heat is on. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is self-surveillance that scores without participating. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's ribs hollow as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the surveillance feels protective—as long as Grace watches for failure, failure might be prevented—but the watching is the cost. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.

Dante loses two patients in one shift. The grief mechanism processes neither. Both store in the shoulders. Peak intensity. Dante's chest caves. Dante's throat swells. The watcher runs at full power. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
