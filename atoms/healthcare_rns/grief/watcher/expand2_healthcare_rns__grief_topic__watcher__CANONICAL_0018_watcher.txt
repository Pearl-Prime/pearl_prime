## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v03 — WALKS, DOESN'T FLOAT — EARLY
---
Sofia is walking to Margaret's old station. One last time. She catches the drift starting—the familiar lift where her body keeps moving and she leaves. She comes back. Feet on floor. Weight shifting. The hallway is familiar. She walks it inside her body. Not watching from above. When she reaches the station, she knows she walked there.
