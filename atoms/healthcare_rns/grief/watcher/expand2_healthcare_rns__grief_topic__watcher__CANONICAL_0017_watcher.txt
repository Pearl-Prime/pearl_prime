## WATCHER / EMBODIMENT v02 — FEELS THE CRYING — LATE
---
James is crying in the break room. About Mrs. Chen. About all of them. He notices the watcher trying to pull back—observe the crying from above. He doesn't let it. Stays in the tears. The wet. The pressure. He's crying. Not watching himself cry. The grief hurts and he's inside it. Present for his own loss.
