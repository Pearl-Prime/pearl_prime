## WATCHER / RECOGNITION v04 — EMOTIONAL LAG — MID
---
Derek hears that Elijah is leaving the hospital. Really leaving. Different city. The words land and his face stays calm. He says "that's a big move" and "good for him." Twenty minutes later, in the supply room, his throat closes. The feeling arrives late. He wasn't sad at the station. He's sad now, among the gauze, where Elijah can't see it.
