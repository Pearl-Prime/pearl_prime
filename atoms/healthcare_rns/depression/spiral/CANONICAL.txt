IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Aisha parks in the hospital lot and cannot open the car door. The thought spiral chains one worry to the next in an accelerating loop. Aisha's mouth dries. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Aisha watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Aisha tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the skull. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Aisha examines the story. The story has no evidence. The mouth has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Aisha holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

Claire sits in the hospital parking garage after the shift. The engine is off. An hour passes. The thought spiral hits maximum volume. Claire's feet drag. Claire's limbs weigh down. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/depression/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ravi maps the parking lot delay: shift ends, sit in car, dread tomorrow, eventually drive home. Ravi's chest sinks. The heat is on. The thought spiral chains one worry to the next in an accelerating loop. Ravi tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is each thought breeding a worse thought without pause. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Ravi sees the cost clearly now. Ravi's mouth dries as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each thought in the chain validates the next thought, and validation feels like progress instead of repetition. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Ravi.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/depression/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace calls in sick for the third shift. The phone call takes everything left. Peak intensity. Grace's feet drag. Grace's limbs weigh down. The thought spiral runs at full power. Grace watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: initial worry, extrapolation, catastrophic endpoint, new worry from endpoint, further extrapolation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Grace's body absorbs the volume. Grace's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Grace sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
