## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Depression is a heaviness. You don't need to fight it.
One tiny action. That's all. Just one.
Can you drink one sip of water?
Can you stretch one arm above your head?
Can you feel the ground under you?
You did something. That's enough for right now.
You don't need to accomplish anything today.
You don't need to feel better.
You just need to be gentle with yourself.
That's the whole practice right now.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Lie down. You're allowed to rest.
Depression says "you're lazy." Depression is lying.
Your body needs to reset. Let it.
Feel one part of your body at a time.
Your left hand. Your right foot.
Just notice. No judgment. No pushing.
When you're ready (even five seconds from now),
try one small movement. Wiggle one finger.
That's all. Rest is enough.
You don't have to earn recovery time.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Depression whispers that nothing will change.
But you've felt this before and survived.
You're still here. That's proof.
Don't try to think positive thoughts.
Just feel your body existing right now.
Feel your breath. Feel your heartbeat.
You're alive. That's everything right now.
No performance needed. No fixing required.
Be gentle with yourself like you'd treat a friend.
The gentleness itself is the medicine.
---
