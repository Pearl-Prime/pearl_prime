Claire parks in the hospital lot and cannot open the car door. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Claire's skull presses. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Claire watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Claire tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the feet. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace maps the parking lot delay: shift ends, sit in car, dread tomorrow, eventually drive home. Grace's mouth dries. The heat is on. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is relative positioning against curated highlights. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's skull presses as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each comparison produces a momentary relief—at least Grace checked—followed by a deeper deficit. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante calls in sick for the third shift. The phone call takes everything left. Peak intensity. Dante's limbs weigh down. Dante's chest sinks. The comparison engine runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: trigger, selective data pull, asymmetric weighting, deficit verdict, temporary relief, repeat. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
