## MECHANISM_PROOF v01

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism is: Overwhelm forces me to keep going. The depression is what happens when I stop. The mechanism creates a false choice. Either stay in constant motion and avoid depression, or stop and be consumed by it. The mechanism proves itself through the way stillness brings sadness and motion keeps it at bay. Tomas learned this early. Stay busy or fall apart.
