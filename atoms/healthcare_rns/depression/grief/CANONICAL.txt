## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/depression/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism is: I have experienced too much loss. I cannot continue to be affected by it. The mechanism produces depression as a protective response to cumulative grief. Chen's inability to feel delight is the mechanism at work. She has lost too many patients. She has felt too much pain. The system has learned that feeling leads to pain, so it shuts feeling down. The proof is the flatness of her experience.
