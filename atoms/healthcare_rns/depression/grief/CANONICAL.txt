COST_INTENSITY: 2

---
Aisha maps the parking lot delay: shift ends, sit in car, dread tomorrow, eventually drive home. Aisha's mouth dries. The heat is on. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Aisha tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is loss surfacing through sensory memory. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Aisha sees the cost clearly now. Aisha's skull presses as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the grief stores in the body because the mind keeps routing around it, and stored grief compounds. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Aisha.
