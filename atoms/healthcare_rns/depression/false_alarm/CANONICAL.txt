---
Tomás parks in the hospital lot and cannot open the car door. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Tomás's chest sinks. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Tomás watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Tomás tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the mouth. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Tomás examines the story. The story has no evidence. The chest has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Tomás holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/depression/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Elena maps the parking lot delay: shift ends, sit in car, dread tomorrow, eventually drive home. Elena's limbs weigh down. The heat is on. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Elena tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is threat detection without actual danger. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Elena sees the cost clearly now. Elena's chest sinks as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the alarm's false positives never get corrected because Elena avoids the situation before learning it was safe. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Elena.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/depression/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ravi calls in sick for the third shift. The phone call takes everything left. Peak intensity. Ravi's skull presses. Ravi's feet drag. The alarm system runs at full power. Ravi watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ambiguous stimulus, threat interpretation, body mobilization, avoidance or escape, relief, reinforcement. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Ravi's body absorbs the volume. Ravi's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Ravi sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
