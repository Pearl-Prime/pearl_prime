Your shoulders are tight. Your hands feel it. Your body is still at work even though the shift ended hours ago. Your nervous system is still in clinical mode.

Notice your hands right now. Still. The shift is over. The patient is stable or they're not, and it won't change by replaying it.

Your hands are finished. Your brain doesn't know.
