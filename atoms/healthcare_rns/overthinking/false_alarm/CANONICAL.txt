## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Marcus rechecks the medication calculation that was correct the first time. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Marcus's forehead tightens. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Marcus watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Marcus tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the temples. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Marcus examines the story. The story has no evidence. The forehead has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Marcus holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Elena rechecks the IV rate for the ninth time. It has been correct every time. The alarm system hits maximum volume. Elena's neck stiffens. Elena's fingers tap. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Elena stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Elena hears it. For the first time, Elena hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Elena's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Elena separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The alarm sounds and the body recognizes it as noise. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ravi checks the medication once. Administers. Walks to the next room without circling back. The alarm system activates. Ravi's fingers tap. The heat rises. Ravi recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Ravi lets the alarm ring and acts anyway, body buzzing but hands steady. The old pattern fires and Ravi acts from the new understanding anyway. Ravi's jaw grinds, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Ravi does while the pattern runs. Ravi does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Ravi also exists. They are not the same thing.

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Grace makes the judgment call on the deteriorating patient without waiting for the lab confirmation. The call saves time. The time saves the patient. Maximum intensity. Grace's temples throb. Grace's neck stiffens. The alarm system roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Grace does not yield. Grace stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Grace's body carries both. The crisis does not define Grace. Grace's response defines Grace. Grace moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Grace walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante documents the charting re-check spiral: chart, doubt, re-read, change one word, doubt again. Dante's jaw grinds. The heat is on. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Dante tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is threat detection without actual danger. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Dante sees the cost clearly now. Dante's forehead tightens as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the alarm's false positives never get corrected because Dante avoids the situation before learning it was safe. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Dante.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Keiko rechecks every order for every patient. The shift takes three extra hours. The re-checking saved nothing. Peak intensity. Keiko's neck stiffens. Keiko's fingers tap. The alarm system runs at full power. Keiko watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ambiguous stimulus, threat interpretation, body mobilization, avoidance or escape, relief, reinforcement. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Keiko's body absorbs the volume. Keiko's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Keiko sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
