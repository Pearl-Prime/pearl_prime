--- variant: v01
You are allowed to leave a shift without reviewing every decision you made. The chart is closed. The next nurse has it. Your brain is allowed to clock out too.

--- variant: v02
You are allowed to have an imperfect shift and not dissect it for the next forty-eight hours. Some shifts are just hard. The hardness doesn't require forensic analysis.

--- variant: v03
You are allowed to let a thought pass without following it to its worst-case conclusion. Not every thread needs to be pulled. Some can just be noticed and released.
