## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/watcher/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante rechecks the medication calculation that was correct the first time. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Dante's jaw grinds. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Dante watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Dante tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the forehead. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Dante examines the story. The story has no evidence. The jaw has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Dante holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/watcher/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Keiko rechecks the IV rate for the ninth time. It has been correct every time. The watcher hits maximum volume. Keiko's temples throb. Keiko's neck stiffens. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Keiko stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Keiko hears it. For the first time, Keiko hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Keiko's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Keiko separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The watcher's scorecard turns out to be fiction built on selective memory. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/watcher/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nora checks the medication once. Administers. Walks to the next room without circling back. The watcher activates. Nora's neck stiffens. The heat rises. Nora recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Nora acts while the watcher watches, refusing to pause for its commentary. The old pattern fires and Nora acts from the new understanding anyway. Nora's fingers tap, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Nora does while the pattern runs. Nora does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Nora also exists. They are not the same thing.

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/watcher/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Tomás makes the judgment call on the deteriorating patient without waiting for the lab confirmation. The call saves time. The time saves the patient. Maximum intensity. Tomás's forehead tightens. Tomás's temples throb. The watcher roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Tomás does not yield. Tomás stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Tomás's body carries both. The crisis does not define Tomás. Tomás's response defines Tomás. Tomás moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Tomás walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Aisha documents the charting re-check spiral: chart, doubt, re-read, change one word, doubt again. Aisha's fingers tap. The heat is on. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Aisha tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is self-surveillance that scores without participating. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Aisha sees the cost clearly now. Aisha's jaw grinds as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the surveillance feels protective—as long as Aisha watches for failure, failure might be prevented—but the watching is the cost. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Aisha.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Claire rechecks every order for every patient. The shift takes three extra hours. The re-checking saved nothing. Peak intensity. Claire's temples throb. Claire's neck stiffens. The watcher runs at full power. Claire watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: action taken, surveillance activation, performance scoring, deficit finding, behavioral inhibition, withdrawal. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Claire's body absorbs the volume. Claire's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Claire sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
