## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C5
Try this: at the end of a shift, write down the one thing you want to release. Not solve. Release. The chart that bothered you. The conversation that was not finished. The patient you did not have time to sit with. Naming it is not closing it. It is telling your nervous system the shift has actually ended.
