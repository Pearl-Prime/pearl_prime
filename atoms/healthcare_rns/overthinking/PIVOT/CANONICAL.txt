Now that same brain won't stop running the analysis after the shift ends. Overthinking in a nurse isn't a personality flaw. It's a clinical skill that lost its off switch.

The old frame treats overthinking as rumination. In nursing, it started as vigilance — the kind that catches the subtle change before the code. The problem isn't that you think too much. It's that the thinking doesn't know the shift is over.

--- variant: v03
You replay the shift in your car, in the shower, at 2 AM. Not because you made an error, but because your brain is running one more assessment — just in case. That 'just in case' is the loop that's stealing your off-duty hours.
