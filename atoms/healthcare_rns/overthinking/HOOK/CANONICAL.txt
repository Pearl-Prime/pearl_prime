

## HOOK v01
---

---
You can assess your own nervous system with clinical accuracy and still can't calm it. You know what's happening—activation, threat response, burnout markers—and the knowing doesn't change the happening.

## HOOK v02
---

---
You made a clinical decision about someone's care. The decision was right. You're still replaying the interaction, the delivery, whether the patient understood, whether they felt cared for.

## HOOK v03
---

---
The shift rotation is destroying your sleep. You're overthinking at 2am on a night shift about something that's not urgent because your circadian rhythm is broken. The overthinking is partly insomnia, partly the job.

## HOOK v04
---

---

## HOOK v05

---

## HOOK v06

---

## HOOK v10

---
