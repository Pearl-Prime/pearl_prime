## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/spiral/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Grace rechecks the medication calculation that was correct the first time. The thought spiral chains one worry to the next in an accelerating loop. Grace's fingers tap. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Grace watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Grace tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the jaw. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Grace examines the story. The story has no evidence. The fingers has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Grace holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/spiral/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante rechecks the IV rate for the ninth time. It has been correct every time. The thought spiral hits maximum volume. Dante's forehead tightens. Dante's temples throb. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Dante stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Dante hears it. For the first time, Dante hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Dante's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Dante separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The spiral loses speed when one link gets examined instead of chained. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/spiral/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Keiko checks the medication once. Administers. Walks to the next room without circling back. The thought spiral activates. Keiko's temples throb. The heat rises. Keiko recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Keiko notices the spiral starting and stays at the first thought without following. The old pattern fires and Keiko acts from the new understanding anyway. Keiko's neck stiffens, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Keiko does while the pattern runs. Keiko does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Keiko also exists. They are not the same thing.

## EMBODIMENT v02
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nora makes the judgment call on the deteriorating patient without waiting for the lab confirmation. The call saves time. The time saves the patient. Maximum intensity. Nora's jaw grinds. Nora's forehead tightens. The thought spiral roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Nora does not yield. Nora stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Nora's body carries both. The crisis does not define Nora. Nora's response defines Nora. Nora moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Nora walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Tomás documents the charting re-check spiral: chart, doubt, re-read, change one word, doubt again. Tomás's neck stiffens. The heat is on. The thought spiral chains one worry to the next in an accelerating loop. Tomás tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is each thought breeding a worse thought without pause. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Tomás sees the cost clearly now. Tomás's fingers tap as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each thought in the chain validates the next thought, and validation feels like progress instead of repetition. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Tomás.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/overthinking/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Aisha rechecks every order for every patient. The shift takes three extra hours. The re-checking saved nothing. Peak intensity. Aisha's forehead tightens. Aisha's temples throb. The thought spiral runs at full power. Aisha watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: initial worry, extrapolation, catastrophic endpoint, new worry from endpoint, further extrapolation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Aisha's body absorbs the volume. Aisha's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Aisha sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
