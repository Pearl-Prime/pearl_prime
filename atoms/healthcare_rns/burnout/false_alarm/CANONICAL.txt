COST_TYPE: internal

---
Tyler is at home on his night off. His body is asleep. His nervous system is not. At 2am he wakes suddenly. Code alarm. The hospital code alarm. His heart is racing. His hands are reaching for his phone. Then the awareness: He's not at work. There's no code. The pager was in his dream. His stomach is still elevated with adrenaline. He gets up. He cannot go back to sleep. His mind is at the hospital. His body thinks there's an emergency. But there's just his bedroom and his sleeping wife. His nervous system has learned that the shift is never over. Even on his days off. The body is still listening for the emergency. It will take an hour to settle. Then maybe he can sleep again.
