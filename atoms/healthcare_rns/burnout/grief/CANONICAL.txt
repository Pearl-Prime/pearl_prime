## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Claire clocks in for the third double shift this week. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Claire's ribs ache. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Claire watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Claire tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the neck. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Claire examines the story. The story has no evidence. The ribs has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Claire holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus sits in the supply closet between rooms. The pager buzzes. The body does not move. The grief wave hits maximum volume. Marcus's legs go heavy. Marcus's eyelids drag. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Marcus stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Marcus hears it. For the first time, Marcus hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Marcus's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Marcus separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The wave arrives and doesn't need to be stopped to be survived. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/grief/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Elena finishes the shift. Does not check the schedule for tomorrow. Drives home in silence. The grief wave activates. Elena's eyelids drag. The heat rises. Elena recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Elena carries the weight and moves through the day with it, not despite it. The old pattern fires and Elena acts from the new understanding anyway. Elena's arms feel hollow, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Elena does while the pattern runs. Elena does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Elena also exists. They are not the same thing.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5

---
Ravi cries in the supply closet and then walks out and gives the best handoff of the shift. Maximum intensity. Ravi's neck locks. Ravi's legs go heavy. The grief wave roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Ravi does not yield. Ravi stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Ravi's body carries both. The crisis does not define Ravi. Ravi's response defines Ravi. Ravi moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Ravi walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace documents the depletion curve: give care, absorb stress, skip break, give more care, absorb more stress. Grace's arms feel hollow. The heat is on. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is loss surfacing through sensory memory. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's ribs ache as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the grief stores in the body because the mind keeps routing around it, and stored grief compounds. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante walks past the patient call light and does not stop. The burnout has reached the reflex level. Peak intensity. Dante's legs go heavy. Dante's eyelids drag. The grief wave runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ordinary trigger, sensory memory activation, body response, cognitive catch-up, suppression attempt, residual storage. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
