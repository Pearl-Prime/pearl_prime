## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Nora clocks in for the third double shift this week. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Nora's legs go heavy. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Nora watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Nora tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the eyelids. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Nora examines the story. The story has no evidence. The legs has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Nora holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tomás sits in the supply closet between rooms. The pager buzzes. The body does not move. The overwhelm flood hits maximum volume. Tomás's arms feel hollow. Tomás's ribs ache. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Tomás stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Tomás hears it. For the first time, Tomás hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Tomás's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Tomás separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The flood reveals it is many small streams, not one wall of water. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Aisha finishes the shift. Does not check the schedule for tomorrow. Drives home in silence. The overwhelm flood activates. Aisha's ribs ache. The heat rises. Aisha recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Aisha picks one stream and follows it, letting the rest run alongside. The old pattern fires and Aisha acts from the new understanding anyway. Aisha's neck locks, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Aisha does while the pattern runs. Aisha does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Aisha also exists. They are not the same thing.

Claire cries in the supply closet and then walks out and gives the best handoff of the shift. Maximum intensity. Claire's eyelids drag. Claire's arms feel hollow. The crisis does not define Claire. Claire's response defines Claire. Claire moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Claire walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus documents the depletion curve: give care, absorb stress, skip break, give more care, absorb more stress. Marcus's neck locks. The heat is on. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Marcus tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is volume flattening into paralysis. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Marcus sees the cost clearly now. Marcus's legs go heavy as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the overwhelm prevents the sorting that would reduce the overwhelm, creating its own fuel. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Marcus.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/burnout/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Elena walks past the patient call light and does not stop. The burnout has reached the reflex level. Peak intensity. Elena's arms feel hollow. Elena's ribs ache. The overwhelm flood runs at full power. Elena watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: task accumulation, flattening into mass, paralysis, self-criticism for paralysis, more accumulation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Elena's body absorbs the volume. Elena's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Elena sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
---
