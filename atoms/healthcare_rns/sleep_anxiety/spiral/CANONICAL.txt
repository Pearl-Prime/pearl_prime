## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
His stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. Marcus is staring at the ceiling before the alarm when he watches the thought breed and the offspring breed before the first resolves. The operating assumption — thinking harder will eventually solve the problem — meets a fact it cannot absorb. His jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held his world in place tilts on its axis. Marcus does not fall. He stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around his new position. The next thought does not contain the answer any more than the last one did. He does not grasp for a replacement frame. He breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Marcus stands in bed with the phone face-down on the nightstand. Sound drops away. Only the pulse remains, massive and deafening. He feels the spiral accelerates past the point where intervention is possible and the noise becomes total. The frame that has held everything in place — the answer lives somewhere in the next thought if the thinking continues — strains under the weight of what Marcus sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Marcus feels the fracture travel through his chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The spiral is motion without destination — no thought in the chain reaches ground. Marcus does not reach for a replacement. He stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Mei pauses while staring at the ceiling before the alarm. A tremor moves through her hands. Mei presses them flat against the surface. The spiral has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The spiral manufactures urgency from hypothetical scenarios, treating each imagined outcome as already real. Mei traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Mei watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
At 3 am after a double shift. Her hands go completely numb. Pins and needles race up both forearms. The spiral operates in plain view for the first time. Rosa catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. Each thought treats possibility as certainty and uses that false certainty to generate the next link in the chain. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Rosa watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
