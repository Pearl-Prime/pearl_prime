## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Pressure mounts behind her eyes like a wave building against a wall. Luz is at 3 AM after a double shift when she feels another demand land on top of a pile that already wobbles. The operating assumption — falling behind means failing entirely — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Luz does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. Falling behind is a position, not a verdict — movement forward begins with one step. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Staring at the ceiling before the alarm. Brianna feels input exceeds all bandwidth — the queue locks and the screen goes white. Every nerve fires simultaneously. Her skin burns from scalp to soles. For months the frame has held: everything is equally urgent and nothing can wait. Now Brianna watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Sequence exists even when the panicking mind denies it — one thing, then the next. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Brianna stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.

## EMBODIMENT v01

COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Chen moves through at 3 AM after a double shift. A tremor moves through her hands. Chen presses them flat against the surface. The overwhelm arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Chen puts everything down except the next single action and begins there. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Chen acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Chen is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Staring at the ceiling before the alarm. Pressure mounts behind her eyes like a wave building against a wall. The overwhelm operates in plain view for the first time. Alicia catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The pile presents itself as one massive indivisible object when it is actually made of individual, addressable items. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Alicia watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Priya pauses while staring at the ceiling before the alarm. The air evacuates the room. Her ribs lock. The diaphragm freezes. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The system conflates volume with urgency, treating everything as equally critical when triage is the actual need. Priya traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Priya watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
