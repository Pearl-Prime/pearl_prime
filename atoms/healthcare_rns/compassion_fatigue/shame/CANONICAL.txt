BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Amy sits with a terrified child. She's supposed to feel compassion. She feels tired. Her chest is heavy from years of holding children's fear. She goes through the motions of soothing but the real comfort isn't accessible. The shame arrives immediately. What kind of pediatric nurse has worn out her capacity to comfort? The shame is that she's built this role on being able to hold children's fear and she can't anymore. Her throat is tight. She's fatigued in a way that cost her the feeling she was trained to offer. The child is soothed by her presence. The shame is that the child doesn't know the presence is empty.
