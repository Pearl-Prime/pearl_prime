Maria arrives at work and already feels the weight. Seven patients. Three are new admissions. One is coded overnight. The charge nurse is short-staffed. The weight is not theoretical. It's in her shoulders, her breath, the tightness across her chest. She hasn't even begun her shift. The overwhelm arrives before the work does. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism is: I must function despite overwhelming circumstances. This mechanism proves itself through the continued ability to move, to chart, to medicate, to care. Maria can work a shift while completely overwhelmed. The mechanism doesn't require that the overwhelm disappear. It requires that the overwhelm be managed, suppressed, tolerated. The mechanism proves itself through function. Yet function in the face of overwhelming circumstances creates a fracture between what's demanded and what's sustainable.
