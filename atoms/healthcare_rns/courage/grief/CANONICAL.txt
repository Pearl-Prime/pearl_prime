## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace watches the silence pattern: see the error, fear the hierarchy, stay quiet, carry the weight home. Grace's voice cracks. The heat is on. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is loss surfacing through sensory memory. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's lungs expand as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the grief stores in the body because the mind keeps routing around it, and stored grief compounds. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.

---
Dante watches the unsafe practice happen again and the silence pattern runs its fastest circuit. Peak intensity. Dante's pulse hammers. Dante's knees wobble. The grief wave runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ordinary trigger, sensory memory activation, body response, cognitive catch-up, suppression attempt, residual storage. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
