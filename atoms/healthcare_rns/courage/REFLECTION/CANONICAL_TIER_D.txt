

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: automatic
tier_variant: D

I want to name something about courage in nursing that is specific and different from what people usually mean by courage. Courage in most contexts means doing something difficult or dangerous despite fear. Running into a burning building. Standing up to an oppressor. Taking on an obvious enemy. The stories we tell about courage are stories about people overcoming external threats. The system does not get to tell me I did not see what I saw.
