## MECHANISM_PROOF v06

COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nora watches the silence pattern: see the error, fear the hierarchy, stay quiet, carry the weight home. Nora's pulse hammers. The heat is on. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Nora tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is self-surveillance that scores without participating. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Nora sees the cost clearly now. Nora's knees wobble as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the surveillance feels protective—as long as Nora watches for failure, failure might be prevented—but the watching is the cost. The persistence is not mysterious.
