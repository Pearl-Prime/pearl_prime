

## RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/courage/comparison/recognition/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Chris watches another charge nurse manage a crisis with apparent calm. Chris's own crisis is chaotic. The comparison arrives: that nurse is braver than I am. That nurse doesn't seem afraid. Chris's chest is tight. The fear is visible to Chris. It's not visible to others. But it's there. The courage would be to acknowledge the fear and lead anyway. The comparison is suggesting that courage means fearlessness. Chris's shoulders drop.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/courage/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chris has learned that courage looks different on different people. The mechanism is that the comparison created shame. Chris's fear was being interpreted as lack of courage. The mechanism suggested: Chris just isn't brave like the other charge nurse. But Chris is making decisions anyway. Chris is leading anyway. The fear is present. The leading is happening. The mechanism is starting to shift. Chris's courage looks like fear. That's still courage.
---
