

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Claim one accomplishment this week. Not to brag. Just to practice believing yourself. The doubt isn't evidence of incompetence. It's evidence of self-awareness that hasn't caught up to itself yet. The credential doesn't silence the doubt because the doubt isn't looking at the credential. It's looking for the exception.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
You watched the senior nurse handle the code without flinching. You assumed she felt confident. Now you are her. You handle the code. You also feel exactly what she felt — the second-guess that runs alongside the action. The confidence you saw was not internal. It was the discipline of acting before the doubt could land.
