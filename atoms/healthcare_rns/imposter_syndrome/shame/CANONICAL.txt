## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/shame/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Claire accepts the preceptor assignment and immediately doubts the qualifications. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Claire's heart races. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Claire watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Claire tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the mouth. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Claire examines the story. The story has no evidence. The heart has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Claire holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/shame/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Marcus gets assigned the highest-acuity patient and the charge nurse says: you're ready. Marcus's hands say otherwise. The shame contraction hits maximum volume. Marcus's face flushes. Marcus's stomach sinks. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Marcus stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Marcus hears it. For the first time, Marcus hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Marcus's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Marcus separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The contraction loosens when the moment separates from the person. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/shame/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Elena orients the new nurse without disclaimers. Teaches the IV technique without prefacing it with how long it took to learn. The shame contraction activates. Elena's stomach sinks. The heat rises. Elena recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Elena stands inside the shame without shrinking, body upright, voice even. The old pattern fires and Elena acts from the new understanding anyway. Elena's hands shake, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Elena does while the pattern runs. Elena does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Elena also exists. They are not the same thing.

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ravi takes the charge role on the worst night of the month. Three admits, two rapids, one code. Ravi runs the board. Maximum intensity. Ravi's mouth dries. Ravi's face flushes. The shame contraction roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Ravi does not yield. Ravi stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Ravi's body carries both. The crisis does not define Ravi. Ravi's response defines Ravi. Ravi moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Ravi walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace maps the competence doubt loop: succeed at procedure, attribute to luck, dread next procedure, succeed again. Grace's hands shake. The heat is on. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is a moment becoming a permanent verdict on selfhood. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's heart races as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the shame prevents examination because examination requires looking at the thing that shames. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante makes a near-miss medication error. The fraud narrative has its proof now. Peak intensity. Dante's face flushes. Dante's stomach sinks. The shame contraction runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: event recall, identity fusion, contraction, hiding behavior, isolation, deeper fusion. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
