## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Marcus accepts the preceptor assignment and immediately doubts the qualifications. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Marcus's mouth dries. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Marcus watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Marcus tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the face. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Marcus examines the story. The story has no evidence. The mouth has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Marcus holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Elena gets assigned the highest-acuity patient and the charge nurse says: you're ready. Elena's hands say otherwise. The comparison engine hits maximum volume. Elena's stomach sinks. Elena's hands shake. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Elena stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Elena hears it. For the first time, Elena hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Elena's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Elena separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The metric collapses under its own selectivity. The frame cracks open.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ravi orients the new nurse without disclaimers. Teaches the IV technique without prefacing it with how long it took to learn. The comparison engine activates. Ravi's hands shake. The heat rises. Ravi recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Ravi holds both someone else's achievement and their own without ranking. The old pattern fires and Ravi acts from the new understanding anyway. Ravi's heart races, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Ravi does while the pattern runs. Ravi does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Ravi also exists. They are not the same thing.

## EMBODIMENT v02
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Grace takes the charge role on the worst night of the month. Three admits, two rapids, one code. Grace runs the board. Maximum intensity. Grace's face flushes. Grace's stomach sinks. The comparison engine roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Grace does not yield. Grace stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Grace's body carries both. The crisis does not define Grace. Grace's response defines Grace. Grace moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Grace walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante maps the competence doubt loop: succeed at procedure, attribute to luck, dread next procedure, succeed again. Dante's heart races. The heat is on. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Dante tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is relative positioning against curated highlights. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Dante sees the cost clearly now. Dante's mouth dries as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each comparison produces a momentary relief—at least Dante checked—followed by a deeper deficit. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Dante.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Keiko makes a near-miss medication error. The fraud narrative has its proof now. Peak intensity. Keiko's stomach sinks. Keiko's hands shake. The comparison engine runs at full power. Keiko watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: trigger, selective data pull, asymmetric weighting, deficit verdict, temporary relief, repeat. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Keiko's body absorbs the volume. Keiko's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Keiko sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
