---
Nora maps what the comparison actually measures. She compares herself to Elena's speed, to Raj's calm, to the charge nurse's authority. Each comparison uses a single variable. Speed. Calm. Authority. She's never compared using her own strongest variables: thoroughness, patient education, family communication.
