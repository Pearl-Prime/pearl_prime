## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nora realizes the spiral has a unique feature in nursing: the catastrophes are real possibilities. The chain from medication to anaphylaxis to death is an actual clinical pathway. The spiral's power comes from its medical accuracy. Each link is a real thing that could happen. The could is the mechanism. In most contexts, spirals exaggerate. In nursing, the spiral uses real risk as fuel. The distinction between "could happen" and "is happening" collapses because the nurse has seen the could become the is. Once. Maybe twice. The spiral uses those experiences as permanent evidence.

Claire maps the spiral's relationship to experience. New nurses spiral on everything—they don't know what's normal yet. Experienced nurses spiral on specific things—the patterns they've seen go wrong. Claire has ten years. Her spiral is precise: decelerations that look like the one from 2019. The one that ended badly.
