## COMPRESSION v32

Outside the unit, social interactions feel slower than the body knows how to read. People take a long time to get to the point. Nobody is dying. The pace mismatch creates a low-grade discomfort that gets misread as social anxiety. The discomfort is real. The cause is recalibration.
