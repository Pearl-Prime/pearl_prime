COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Keisha stands at the staff meeting with the attending. Her throat closes around the words. The vocal cords tighten. She sees the colleague's promotion announcement. The frame that has held everything in place — worth depends on relative position — strains under the weight of what Keisha sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Keisha feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The scoreboard tracks a game nobody consented to play. Keisha does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Elijah pauses in the elevator beside the charge nurse. His stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. The comparison has run for hours without interruption. Now he sees the machinery laid bare. The ranking system uses rigged criteria — it selects only data that confirms inferiority and discards everything else. Elijah traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. His stomach clenches as he maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Elijah watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
