COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Claire tracks the overtime dependency: need money, take shifts, lose health, need money for health. Claire's ribs contract. The heat is on. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Claire tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is relative positioning against curated highlights. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Claire sees the cost clearly now. Claire's throat tightens as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each comparison produces a momentary relief—at least Claire checked—followed by a deeper deficit. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Claire.

Marcus calculates that the student loan will outlast the career at current pace. The math is a wall. Peak intensity. Marcus's scalp prickles. Marcus's fingers tremble. The comparison engine runs at full power. Marcus watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: trigger, selective data pull, asymmetric weighting, deficit verdict, temporary relief, repeat. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Marcus's body absorbs the volume. Marcus's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Marcus sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
