Marcus calculates whether the overtime check covers the car payment. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Marcus's throat tightens. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante tracks the overtime dependency: need money, take shifts, lose health, need money for health. Dante's ribs contract. The heat is on. The grief wave crashes without warning from ordinary triggers. Dante tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is loss surfacing through sensory memory. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Dante sees the cost clearly now. Dante's throat tightens as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the grief stores in the body because the mind keeps routing around it, and stored grief compounds. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Dante.

Keiko watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ordinary trigger, sensory memory activation, body response, cognitive catch-up, suppression attempt, residual storage. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Keiko's body absorbs the volume. Keiko's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Keiko sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
