## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus tracks the overtime dependency: need money, take shifts, lose health, need money for health. Marcus's throat tightens. The heat is on. The watcher monitors from a distance, recording failures but never engaging. Marcus tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is self-surveillance that scores without participating. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Marcus sees the cost clearly now. Marcus's gut churns as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the surveillance feels protective—as long as Marcus watches for failure, failure might be prevented—but the watching is the cost. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Marcus.

---
Elena calculates that the student loan will outlast the career at current pace. The math is a wall. Peak intensity. Elena's fingers tremble. Elena's ribs contract. The watcher runs at full power. Elena watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: action taken, surveillance activation, performance scoring, deficit finding, behavioral inhibition, withdrawal. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Elena's body absorbs the volume. Elena's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Elena sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
