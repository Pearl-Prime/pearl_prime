

## HOOK v01
---

---
You're at the kitchen table in scrubs still damp from shift, pay stub and rent bill spread out — your fingers freeze on the ratio that has not moved since you became an RN.
---

## HOOK v02
---

---
It is 5:40 AM and you are scrubbing in, but the number from last night's banking app is still scrolling behind your eyes — the overtime you picked up barely closed the gap the car repair opened.
---

## HOOK v03
---

---
You count the meds twice because your hands are unsteady, and it is not the caffeine; it is the rent that went up again while the differential stayed exactly where it was three years ago.
---

## HOOK v04
---

---
The patient asks if you are okay and you say of course, smiling, while a part of you is doing arithmetic you cannot stop — shifts times rate minus what is already spoken for.
---

## HOOK v05
---

---
On the drive home you pass the hospital billboard advertising your own unit as a great place to grow, and you laugh once, dry, because growth has a salary band and you have been pinned to the floor of it.
---
