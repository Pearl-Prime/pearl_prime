## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Tomás calculates whether the overtime check covers the car payment. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Tomás's scalp prickles. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Tomás watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Tomás tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the fingers. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Tomás examines the story. The story has no evidence. The scalp has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Tomás holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Aisha counts the per-diem shifts needed to cover the student loan payment and the number is higher than the available days. The shame contraction hits maximum volume. Aisha's ribs contract. Aisha's throat tightens. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Aisha stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Aisha hears it. For the first time, Aisha hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Aisha's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Aisha separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The contraction loosens when the moment separates from the person. The frame cracks open.

## EMBODIMENT v01

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Claire declines the overtime. The bank account protests. The body needs rest more than money. The shame contraction activates. Claire's throat tightens. The heat rises. Claire recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Claire stands inside the shame without shrinking, body upright, voice even. The old pattern fires and Claire acts from the new understanding anyway. Claire's gut churns, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Claire does while the pattern runs. Claire does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Claire also exists. They are not the same thing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Elena tracks the overtime dependency: need money, take shifts, lose health, need money for health. Elena's gut churns. The heat is on. The shame contraction collapses identity into a single failure point. Elena tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is a moment becoming a permanent verdict on selfhood. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Elena sees the cost clearly now. Elena's scalp prickles as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the shame prevents examination because examination requires looking at the thing that shames. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Elena.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ravi calculates that the student loan will outlast the career at current pace. The math is a wall. Peak intensity. Ravi's ribs contract. Ravi's throat tightens. The shame contraction runs at full power. Ravi watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: event recall, identity fusion, contraction, hiding behavior, isolation, deeper fusion. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Ravi's body absorbs the volume. Ravi's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Ravi sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
