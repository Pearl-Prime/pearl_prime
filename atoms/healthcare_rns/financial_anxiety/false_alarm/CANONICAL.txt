Claire calculates whether the overtime check covers the car payment. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Claire's ribs contract. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace tracks the overtime dependency: need money, take shifts, lose health, need money for health. Grace's fingers tremble. The heat is on. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is threat detection without actual danger. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's ribs contract as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the alarm's false positives never get corrected because Grace avoids the situation before learning it was safe. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante calculates that the student loan will outlast the career at current pace. The math is a wall. Peak intensity. Dante's gut churns. Dante's scalp prickles. The alarm system runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ambiguous stimulus, threat interpretation, body mobilization, avoidance or escape, relief, reinforcement. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
