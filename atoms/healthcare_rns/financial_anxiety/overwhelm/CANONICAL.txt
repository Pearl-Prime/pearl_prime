## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Claire calculates whether the overtime check covers the car payment. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Claire's ribs contract. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Claire watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Claire tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the throat. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Claire examines the story. The story has no evidence. The ribs has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Claire holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus counts the per-diem shifts needed to cover the student loan payment and the number is higher than the available days. The overwhelm flood hits maximum volume. Marcus's gut churns. Marcus's scalp prickles. Everything narrows to this moment—collapse or breakthrough, no middle ground. Marcus stands inside the peak. The pattern says: this is who you are. Marcus hears it. For the first time, Marcus hears it as a sentence, not a fact. A sentence can be wrong. Marcus's body shakes with the intensity. The shaking is real. The sentence is interpretation. Marcus separates the shaking from the sentence. The body still trembles. The identity claim dissolves. The flood reveals it is many small streams, not one wall of water. The frame cracks open.

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Elena declines the overtime. The bank account protests. The body needs rest more than money. The overwhelm flood activates. Elena's scalp prickles. The heat rises. Elena recognizes the heat. Does not turn away from it. Elena picks one stream and follows it, letting the rest run alongside. The old pattern fires and Elena acts from the new understanding anyway. Elena's fingers tremble, but the hands keep moving. The identity is not the pattern. The identity is what Elena does while the pattern runs. Elena does the next right thing with the pattern screaming in the background. The screaming fades to a hum. The hum fades to a fact: the pattern exists. Elena also exists. They are not the same thing.

Ravi tells the partner: we need to change our plan. The conversation is the one they have been avoiding for months. Maximum intensity. Ravi's throat tightens. Ravi's gut churns. The overwhelm flood roars at peak volume. Every nerve says yield. Ravi does not yield. Ravi stands at the center of the storm and acts from the new identity—not against the pattern, but through it. The pattern is noise. The action is signal. Ravi's body carries both. The crisis does not define Ravi. Ravi's response defines Ravi. Ravi moves forward with the full weight of the moment pressing down. The weight is real. The collapse is not. Ravi walks through the peak and comes out the other side still standing. Still choosing.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace tracks the overtime dependency: need money, take shifts, lose health, need money for health. Grace's fingers tremble. The heat is on. The overwhelm flood collapses all tasks into one impossible mass. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is volume flattening into paralysis. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's ribs contract as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the overwhelm prevents the sorting that would reduce the overwhelm, creating its own fuel. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante calculates that the student loan will outlast the career at current pace. The math is a wall. Peak intensity. Dante's gut churns. Dante's scalp prickles. The overwhelm flood runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: task accumulation, flattening into mass, paralysis, self-criticism for paralysis, more accumulation. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
