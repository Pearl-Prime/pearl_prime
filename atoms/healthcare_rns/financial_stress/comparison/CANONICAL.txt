## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_stress/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Grace charts the paycheck-to-crisis cycle: deposit arrives, bills consume, anxiety returns, next deposit feels distant. Grace's neck tenses. The heat is on. The comparison engine measures against someone else's visible success. Grace tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is relative positioning against curated highlights. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Grace sees the cost clearly now. Grace's vision narrows as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because each comparison produces a momentary relief—at least Grace checked—followed by a deeper deficit. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Grace.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante discovers the health insurance premium increase on the same day as the car repair estimate. Peak intensity. Dante's shoulders bunch. Dante's stomach drops. The comparison engine runs at full power. Dante watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: trigger, selective data pull, asymmetric weighting, deficit verdict, temporary relief, repeat. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Dante's body absorbs the volume. Dante's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Dante sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
