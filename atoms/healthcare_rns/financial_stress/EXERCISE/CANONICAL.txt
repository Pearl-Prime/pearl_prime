## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You just looked at an unexpected bill.
Your nervous system is hijacked right now.
Before you do anything, breathe deliberately.
Breathe in through your nose for four counts.
Hold for two counts.
Out through your mouth for six counts.
Repeat this five times. Don't skip this.
Your nervous system is in survival mode.
You can't think clearly from survival mode.
Once you've breathed, then look at the bill.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Separate urgent money problems from not-urgent ones.
You have a bill due Friday. Urgent.
You might need a new car someday. Not urgent.
Write down what's actually pressing right now.
Focus on one urgent thing only.
Don't let not-urgent things steal your attention.
Your nervous system treats them all as emergencies.
You have to choose what's actually emergency.
Make a plan for just the urgent item.
Everything else can wait until it becomes urgent.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You're stressed about money. This is normal.
But spiraling doesn't fix money problems.
Take one concrete action toward one problem.
Maybe it's calling about a payment plan.
Maybe it's checking your account balance.
One action. Just one. Do it now.
When the action is done, you can rest.
You don't solve money by panicking nonstop.
You solve it by taking small actions.
This action matters. You're doing something.
---
