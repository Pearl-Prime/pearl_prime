## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_stress/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Marcus picks up the extra shift not because of the patients but because of the rent. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Marcus's hands clench. The pattern demands attention now—hot, insistent, refusing to wait. Marcus watches it demand. This time, instead of obeying, Marcus tracks where the demand originates. It starts in the shoulders. It travels to the thoughts. The thoughts build a story. The story says: act now or lose everything. Marcus examines the story. The story has no evidence. The hands has real sensation. The story has fabricated urgency. Marcus holds the sensation without following the story. The sensation stays. The story loses its audience. The frame shifts: the pattern is loud, but loudness is not truth.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_stress/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dante charts the paycheck-to-crisis cycle: deposit arrives, bills consume, anxiety returns, next deposit feels distant. Dante's vision narrows. The heat is on. The alarm system fires danger signals when no real threat exists. Dante tracks the sequence this time: trigger, body response, thought cascade, behavioral pull. The trigger is external. The body response is automatic. The thought cascade is threat detection without actual danger. The behavioral pull is the part that costs. Dante sees the cost clearly now. Dante's hands clench as the pattern runs its circuit. The pattern persists because the alarm's false positives never get corrected because Dante avoids the situation before learning it was safe. The persistence is not mysterious. The persistence is mechanical. Seeing the machine does not stop the machine. Seeing the machine reveals the machine is not Dante.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/financial_stress/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Keiko discovers the health insurance premium increase on the same day as the car repair estimate. Peak intensity. Keiko's stomach drops. Keiko's neck tenses. The alarm system runs at full power. Keiko watches it run. Even at maximum volume, the mechanism follows the same sequence: ambiguous stimulus, threat interpretation, body mobilization, avoidance or escape, relief, reinforcement. The sequence is identical at band five as at band two. Only the amplitude changes. The pattern does not become smarter at higher intensity. It becomes louder. Keiko's body absorbs the volume. Keiko's mind maps the circuit. The circuit has no exit built in—it loops. The loop persists because it feeds on its own output. Keiko sees the feed. Seeing the feed does not stop the loop. Seeing the feed reveals the loop has a structure. Structure can be interrupted.
