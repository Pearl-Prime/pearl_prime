
## RECOGNITION v01
---

BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 1
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Maria watches Keisha work. Keisha is efficient. Patients love her. She never seems rushed. She has energy at the end of her shift. Maria compares herself. Maria feels rushed all day. She feels behind. She leaves exhausted. The comparison creates a gap. Keisha is the nurse Maria thinks she should be. Maria is something less. The comparison is happening without words, just in the body.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/self_worth/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism is: My worth is determined by how I compare to other nurses. Maria's constant comparison is the mechanism at work. Keisha is better. Keisha has worth. Maria is worse. Maria has less worth. The mechanism uses comparison as the metric for self-evaluation. The proof is everywhere. Every interaction becomes data in the comparison.
