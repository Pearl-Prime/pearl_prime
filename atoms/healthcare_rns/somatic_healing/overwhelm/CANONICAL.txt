## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Heat climbs through her sternum and spreads across her collarbones. Alicia is washing hands between patients for the fortieth time when she feels another demand land on top of a pile that already wobbles. The operating assumption — the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Alicia does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. The pile is made of individual items that can be addressed one at a time. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
In the supply closet between codes. Luz feels input exceeds all bandwidth — the queue locks and the screen goes white. Every nerve fires simultaneously. Her skin burns from scalp to soles. For months the frame has held: the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole. Now Luz watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Falling behind is a position, not a verdict — movement forward begins with one step. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Luz stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
In the parking garage after a twelve-hour shift. Weight presses his chest flat. Breathing becomes a conscious act. The overwhelm pushes with full force against his sternum. Elijah puts everything down except the next single action and begins there. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Elijah moves from the new ground he has claimed. His hands tremble and his hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in his muscles, in the steadiness of his spine, in the way his feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

## EMBODIMENT v02

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rosa moves through in the parking garage after a twelve-hour shift. Every nerve fires simultaneously. Her skin burns from scalp to soles. The overwhelm arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Rosa picks up one item from the pile and sets the rest aside with steady hands. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Rosa acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Rosa is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus pauses washing hands between patients for the fortieth time. His skin prickles with electric heat. Every follicle stands. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now he sees the machinery laid bare. The system conflates volume with urgency, treating everything as equally critical when triage is the actual need. Marcus traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. His stomach clenches as he maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Marcus watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
