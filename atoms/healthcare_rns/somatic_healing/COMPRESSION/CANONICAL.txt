Try this: Lower your shoulders every time you notice them raised. Small practice. Nervous system relearning. The body records what the mind edits out. The record doesn't disappear just because it isn't being read. The tension that lives in the body as posture, as bracing, as held breath — it arrived for a reason. It stays for the same reason.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
You learned to stand on your feet for hours without complaint. The complaint was filed somewhere — knees, hips, lower back, plantar fascia. The body kept showing up because the patients did. The receipt for that showing up is still being sent to your body now. It will keep being sent until someone agrees to read it.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Nurses' bodies carry the choreography of the unit. The lift that should have been three people but was one. The bend over the bed at the wrong angle. The seven hours without sitting. The body memorizes these. The memorization shows up as chronic pain, as the back that locks at home, as the headache that arrives the moment you stop moving. The body is filing what the schedule never paid for.
