## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Your body has been holding trauma from work.
Give it permission to release right now.
Shake out your hands for fifteen seconds.
Shake them like you're flinging water off.
Shake out your legs. Big movements. Vigorous.
Your body wants to discharge the activation.
Let it. Don't be gentle right now.
Stomp your feet. Clench and release your fists.
Feel the stagnant energy moving out.
This is release. Your body is healing.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Lie down on your back or sit.
Notice one part of your body that's tight.
Your shoulders. Your jaw. Your belly.
Put your hand there and breathe into it.
Don't try to release it. Just track it.
Feel what sensation lives there.
Is it heat? Cold? Pressure?
Just observe without judgment.
Your awareness itself is healing.
Your body knows how to soften when witnessed.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Gentle movement. No rushing. No exercise.
Slow neck rolls. Forward and back.
Feel your shoulders. Roll them slowly.
Gentle arm circles. Slow and deliberate.
Stretch your side body. One side at a time.
Notice: your body is becoming present.
Work mode was tense and goal-focused.
This is presence. This is gentle.
Let each stretch feel restorative.
Your body is learning to trust again.
---
