## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tanya stands in the parking garage after a twelve-hour shift. Her jaw locks. Teeth press together hard enough to ache. She feels the surveillance tighten until every gesture carries the weight of an audition. The frame that has held everything in place — self-monitoring prevents failure and keeps things safe — strains under the weight of what Tanya sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Tanya feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The observation is what disrupts the signal it claims to guard. Tanya does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Her heartbeat fills her entire skull. Nothing else is audible. Rosa is washing hands between patients for the fortieth time when she feels the surveillance becomes so total that the self disappears into the act of monitoring itself. The operating assumption — the watching is necessary because without it everything falls apart — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Rosa does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. The watching is the interference itself — the monitor is the source of the static, not the cure. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
In the supply closet between codes. The base of her skull throbs. The tension radiates forward into her forehead. The watcher pushes with full force against her sternum. Keisha does the thing without watching the doing, and discovers the doing is better without the audience. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Keisha moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The muscles in his back seize from the base of the skull downward. David stands in the supply closet between codes. The watcher runs its program at full speed. David watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the watcher creates a permanent split between performer and judge, and the judge never once approves — the observation itself is what contaminates the performance. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. David sees this now. His ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But David stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not his name.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/healthcare_rns/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Keisha pauses in the supply closet between codes. Her body shakes — not a tremor but a quake that starts in the core. The watcher has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The watcher creates a permanent split between performer and judge, and the judge never once approves — the observation itself is what contaminates the performance. Keisha traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Keisha watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
