David's eyes sting in a meeting. The grief surfaces without permission — something about the agenda item triggers the loss. Vision narrows. The shame fires instantly: professionals do not cry at work. Leaders do not show this. David blinks it back. The grief retreats behind the shame and calcifies there. Then David notices: the grief has been calcifying for months because every time it surfaces, the shame pushes it back down. The frame cracks — the shame is not protecting David's image. It is preventing David's healing. Every suppressed tear is a gram of unprocessed grief hardening into depression. Chest loosens The shame is the dam. The grief needs to flow.

David is exposed in the context of grief. Fully, publicly, undeniably. The thing the shame was guarding — the perceived deficiency at the center — is visible to everyone. Shoulders lock. Throat closes. David waits for annihilation. The room continues. The world does not end. People see the flaw and respond with humanity instead of exile. Everything David hid, every performance of competence, every mask — all to prevent a moment that turned out to be survivable. Vision narrows. The shame's power depended on David never testing its predictions. The predictions were just tested. They failed.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David catches the shame's trigger mechanism around grief. The shame does not respond to actual failure — it responds to the possibility of being perceived as failing. Vision narrows. A meeting where David might not know the answer. An email that might contain criticism. A review that might reveal a gap. The shame fires on "might." It does not wait for evidence. It preempts evidence by generating its own conviction of inadequacy. Chest hollow. The pattern persists because the shame is prophylactic — it punishes David before the world can, creating the illusion that self-punishment prevents external punishment. But self-punishment does not prevent anything. It just arrives first.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David traces the shame about grief to its origin. The shame was not born in this role or this career. It was born in a moment — specific, remembered, formative — when a younger David was seen as insufficient and believed it. The professional shame is a professional costume on a personal wound. Hands grip the desk. Every activation at work is the same activation from that original moment. The boss's frown is the parent's disappointment. The missed target is the failed test. The professional review is the childhood judgment. Face burns. The pattern persists because the shame is pre-professional. It arrived before the job. It will outlast the job. Fighting it at the professional level is fighting the symptom. The wound is underneath, in a younger body, in an earlier room, where someone who mattered said or implied that David was not enough. David's eyes are wet. The career just gave the old wound a new vocabulary.
