## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David grieves the grief itself. The heaviness of carrying loss has become its own burden — separate from the original loss, a meta-grief about being a person who cannot stop mourning. Stomach drops. Then David realizes the grief has layers: the original loss underneath, and on top, the exhaustion of grieving. The exhaustion is not the same as the grief. It is the cost of the grief. The frame cracks — addressing the exhaustion does not dishonor the loss. Resting from grief is not the same as forgetting. Breath deepens The two layers separate. The loss remains. The exhaustion becomes manageable.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David's grief grief breaks open completely. Not gradually — all at once, in a public space, without warning. The tears come or the voice breaks or the body simply stops cooperating. Chest tight. The loss that has been carried in the body finally exceeds the body's capacity to contain it. David is undone in front of witnesses. The shame is secondary to the grief. The grief is so large it overwhelms even the shame. Something shifts in the breaking — the grief, when it finally flows, carries with it the toxic weight of containment. The frame shatters: containing the grief was more destructive than releasing it. The breaking is not the problem. The holding was the problem. Hands cold. David sits in the aftermath, emptied and raw, and the rawness is the first honest sensation in months.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/grief/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Sarah carries the loss related to grief openly now. It shows up in quiet moments — a memory triggered by a familiar phrase, an absence noticed at a meeting. Shoulders lock. The grief passes through and Sarah continues. Not over it. Through it. The loss does not define Sarah's capacity. Sarah works with the loss as a companion rather than an obstacle. Breathing stays even Sarah is a person who has lost something real and carries the loss without being consumed by it. The carrying is honest. The continuing is deliberate. Both are true.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/grief/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
The deepest loss arrives. Sarah's grief situation produces a grief that goes all the way down — not surface sadness but the kind that restructures the landscape. Chest hollow. Sarah does not resist it. Does not perform composure. Sits with the full weight. The grief flows through and Sarah is still standing when it passes. Not unmarked. Changed. But standing. Hands grip the desk. Sarah has become a person who can hold maximum grief without being annihilated. The grief is enormous and Sarah is also enormous — large enough to contain the loss without being consumed by it. The identity shift is complete: Sarah is not a person who avoids grief. Sarah is a person who carries it. Chest is open and aching and alive

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David notices that grief about grief surfaces at predictable triggers — a date, a name, a place, a song. The grief is not random. It has architecture. It waits at specific locations and activates when David passes through. Jaw clenches. David sees the machinery: the grief has embedded itself in the landscape of daily life. The conference room where the last meeting happened. The calendar date that marks the anniversary. The protective logic is clear — the grief marks the places that mattered, ensuring they are never forgotten. But the marking also ensures they are never just places again. Stomach drops. The pattern persists because the grief is the price of having cared, and the caring was real, and the places where the caring happened still exist.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David realizes the grief about grief has been compounding for years. Not one loss but an accumulation — each small grief adding to the pile because none were fully processed. The current grief is not about the current loss. It is about every loss that preceded it. Shoulders lock. David sees the full machinery: each ungrieved loss adds weight to the next. The system has no outlet. Grief enters and never leaves. It accumulates in the chest, in the shoulders, in the jaw. Throat closes. The pattern persists because grieving requires time and space and both are in short supply. The unprocessed grief stays stored, and stored grief compounds into depression, and depression prevents the processing that would relieve the accumulation. The system is a grief warehouse with no exits.
