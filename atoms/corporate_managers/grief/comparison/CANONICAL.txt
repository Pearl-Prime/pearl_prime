---
David watches a colleague process loss with visible grace — tears at the memorial, a speech that lands, the kind of mourning that looks whole. David's grief is messier. Private. Stuck in the body instead of flowing through words. The comparison fires hot: other people grieve properly. David grieves wrong. Chest tight. Then David sees the colleague afterward, alone in the hallway, face collapsed, hands shaking. The grace was performance. The real grief is ugly for everyone. The frame cracks. There is no proper grieving. There is only grieving. David's version — silent, somatic, stuck — is not inferior. It is different. Shoulders drop The comparison was measuring surfaces while the grief lives underground.

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/comparison/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David's grief reaches a breaking point. The comparison has been running for so long and so hard that the body refuses to continue. Hands grip the desk. Face burns. David stands in the wreckage of a metric system that collapsed under its own weight — measuring against others until the measurement consumed all the energy that should have gone to the actual work. Then the person David has been comparing against reveals their own collapse. The carefully maintained exterior crumbles. David's chest cracks open. The comparison was between two performances of adequacy, not between adequacy and inadequacy. Both were performing. Both were breaking. The frame shatters because the person who seemed to have it all together was held together by the same kind of wire. Nobody wins the comparison because nobody was competing. They were all just surviving.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/grief/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David catches the comparison operating in real time within grief. The comparison fires before the thought completes — automatic, pre-rational, faster than analysis. Chest crushes. David watches the machinery: a neutral data point enters the system and exits as a deficit. The peer's action becomes evidence of David's inadequacy. The conversion happens in milliseconds. Hands shake. The protective logic is visible now: if David tracks who is better, David can never be surprised by falling short. But the tracking IS the falling short. The comparison creates the deficit it claims to measure. The pattern persists because the comparison feels like assessment when it is actually assault.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David lies awake at night and the comparison runs its full program within grief. Every peer. Every gap. Every dimension on which David falls short. The comparison has become autonomous — it needs no trigger, no input, no event. It generates its own material from memory and projection. Chest tight. David sees the full architecture in the dark: the comparison is not a response to external data. It is an internal program that creates its own data. It selects the peers. It chooses the dimensions. It constructs the gaps. The entire comparison is self-authored. Hands cold. The pattern persists because the comparison feels like it is about other people when it is entirely about David's relationship with David's own adequacy. Other people are just the raw material. The deficiency is pre-existing. The comparison just gives it a face.
