


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to grieve at work in the way that is available to you. The hallway, the bathroom, the car.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to take more than three days. The policy is a minimum, not a prescription.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to cancel the meeting because the grief arrived mid-morning.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to let the small things feel enormous. Grief changes scale.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to not be fine in the role that requires you to look fine.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to tell your team that you lost someone. They can hold it.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to need the big things at work to feel meaningless for a while. That feeling is temporary.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to cry in the office. The office is wrong if it doesn't have room for that.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to manage at half capacity and call it enough.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to let the performance review be about performance and not about the grief you're hiding.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to grieve on a timeline that has nothing to do with the org's timeline.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to let the loss change what you want from this job. Changed priorities are not betrayals.
