## EXERCISE v01
---

---
The loss hits during a meeting. Give yourself permission. Excuse yourself. Sit alone. Let it move through. Don't analyze. Just feel. This is allowed.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Working after loss feels wrong. Before the day begins, acknowledge it. Say: I am here. Part of me is elsewhere. Both can be true.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
At day's end, sit quietly. Say the name or remember what is gone. Place your hand on your heart. Hold it there. Let yourself be sad. That honors it.
---
