## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before the company event, ground your body. Feel your feet on the floor. Press them down. Feel your shoulders drop. You don't have to perform. Just show up.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
At the event, feeling observed and awkward. Find one person. Ask them one question. That is the assignment. Don't impress. Just connect on one thing.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
After social time, you're drained from performing. Go home. Sit alone. Notice what your body needs. Quiet. Water. Rest. Give yourself that. You showed up.
---
