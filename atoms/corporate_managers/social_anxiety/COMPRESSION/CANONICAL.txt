

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Before the next social moment, ask 'What do I want to know about this person?' instead of 'What will they think of me?' The performance anxiety doesn't mean you're doing it wrong. It means the stakes registered. They're real to you.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
Leadership is a social role performed in public. Every meeting is a stage. Every offsite is a test of presence. You learned to read the room before you learned to be in it. The watching never turned off. It just learned to wear a confident face.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Notice the difference between meetings where you contribute and meetings where you perform contributing. In one, you are tracking the substance. In the other, you are tracking how you appear while tracking the substance. The second one is twice the work and half the value.
