

## RECOGNITION-EMBODIMENT v01-v05

---
David feels anxious in a meeting and spirals: I'm failing socially means people see me means I'm exposed means I'm done. His chest is heavy. Sarah feels awkward and spirals: I'm not handling this means people judge means I don't belong. Her hands are cold. Kevin feels tense and spirals: I'm failing at leadership means the team sees means they don't respect me. His shoulders are tight. Anthony feels out of place and spirals: I don't fit means executives know means I'm not qualified. His throat is tight. Tanya feels uncomfortable and spirals: I'm failing at presence means people notice means I'm out. Her stomach is heavy. Social moment becomes existential crisis. David stops: "I felt anxious. That's just anxiety." His chest opens. Sarah interrupts: "I felt awkward. People didn't judge." Her hands warm. Kevin breaks in: "Tension doesn't determine competence." His shoulders release. Anthony pauses: "Discomfort doesn't determine fit." His throat opens. Tanya stops: "Social anxiety is just anxiety." Her stomach calms. Anxiety stays anxiety.
