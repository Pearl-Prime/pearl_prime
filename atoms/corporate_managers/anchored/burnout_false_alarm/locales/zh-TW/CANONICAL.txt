This sequence effectively maps the psychological progression of corporate managers misidentifying acute stress or fatigue as chronic burnout, and the subsequent correction of that narrative. Here is a summary of the structural logic captured in these atoms:

### **Core Narrative Arc**
1.  **Recognition (Pre-awareness):** The nervous system triggers a "false alarm." Physical symptoms (tight chest, cold hands, tight shoulders/throat/stomach) are misinterpreted as permanent depletion (burnout) rather than temporary states (stress, fatigue, difficult quarters).
2.  **Mechanism Proof (Destabilization):** Acting on the false diagnosis creates real problems. Managers make unnecessary life/work overhauls, communicate crisis to teams, or create internal disruption based on a misdiagnosis. The cost shifts from purely internal to social/operational.
3.  **Turning Point (Experimentation):** A cognitive check occurs. The individual distinguishes between *intensity* (hard work/stress) and *depletion* (burnout). They realize rest or completion of the current cycle is the solution, not a systemic overhaul. Physical tension begins to release.
4.  **Embodiment (Self-claim):** The new understanding is integrated. The manager responds appropriately to stress vs. burnout. Physical symptoms resolve (open chest, warm hands, relaxed shoulders), indicating a regulated nervous system and accurate self-assessment.

### **Key Differentiators Identified**
*   **Timeframe:** Stressful week/hard month/difficult quarter vs. chronic condition.
*   **Physiology:** Temporary tension (tightness/cold) vs. permanent depletion.
*   **Action:** Overreaction (overhaul/boundary changes) vs. Appropriate Response (rest/finish project).

*   **Identity Stage Progression:** `pre_awareness` → `destabilization` → `experimentation` → `self_claim`.
*   **Cost Type:** Primarily `internal`, with some `social` impact during the destabilization phase when communication spreads the false alarm.
*   **Band/Mechanism Depth:** Recognition starts at Band 2/Depth 1, deepens to Band 3/Depth 2 during Mechanism Proof and Turning Point, and settles back to Band 1-2/Depth 1 in Embodiment as the response becomes automatic/integrated.
