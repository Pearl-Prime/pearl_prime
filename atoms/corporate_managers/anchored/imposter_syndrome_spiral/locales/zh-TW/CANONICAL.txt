### 🛠️ Suggestions for v06+ Expansion

#### 1. **Add a "Recognition Cue" Step**
   Before stopping the spiral, add a brief *awareness* phrase to help users catch the spiral earlier:
   - *"I notice I’m spiraling."*  
   - *"My body is reacting to a story, not reality."*  
   This creates a pause between trigger and response.

#### 2. **Differentiate Intervention Types**
   Not all minds respond to the same reframing. Consider offering options:
   - **Normalization**: *"Everyone doubts themselves sometimes."*
   - **Evidence-Based**: *"I’ve handled X before; I can handle this."*
   - **Compassionate**: *"It’s okay to feel unsure. I’m still learning."*
   - **Action-Oriented**: *"What’s one small step I can take right now?"*

#### 3. **Include a "Relapse Protocol"**
   Spirals often return. Add a quick reset for when doubt creeps back:
   - *"Doubt is visiting again. I don’t need to invite it in."*
   - *"My body is safe. My mind is clear. I am enough."*

#### 4. **Expand Somatic Release Techniques**
   Pair the verbal intervention with a micro-action:

   - **Tanya**: Place hand on stomach → breathe into it.

#### 5. **Contextual Triggers**

   - Executive meetings → Anthony’s belonging anxiety
