1.  **RECOGNITION (v01–v05):** The initial trigger where a single data point (criticism, bad interaction, underperformance, variance, bad metric) collapses into an identity threat due to lack of boundaries.

3.  **TURNING_POINT (v01–v05):** The intervention where the manager consciously sets a cognitive boundary to separate the *event* from the *identity/system*.

    *   *Key State:* `self_claim`, low cost, physical ease (open chest, warm hands, relaxed shoulders).
