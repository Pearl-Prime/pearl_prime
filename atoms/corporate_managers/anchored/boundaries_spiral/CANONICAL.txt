---
David gets one piece of criticism and immediately spirals. The criticism activates his fear that he's failing. His chest tightens. He starts reviewing everything he's done through the lens of the criticism. One comment becomes evidence of systematic failure. Without a boundary between the comment and the identity threat, the spiral consumes him. He's no longer processing feedback. He's catastrophizing.
