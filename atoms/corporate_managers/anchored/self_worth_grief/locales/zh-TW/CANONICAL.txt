### **Core Theme**
> **"Grief erodes self-worth." → "Grief integrated with stable worth."**

The transformation is not about eliminating grief, but about **reclaiming self-worth *within* the grieving process**, resulting in physical release and emotional stabilization.

     - **Shoulders** (凱文): Burden/responsibility.
     - **Throat** (安東尼): Voice/expression.
     - **Stomach** (譚雅): Gut instinct/security.
   - Resolution is marked by **physical release**: opening, warming, relaxing, calming.

   - **Discovery-based**: 安東尼 (strategic value), 莎拉 (new sources).
   - This suggests multiple valid pathways to healing—no single "correct" way to rebuild self-worth.

3. **Narrative Structure:**
   - The text follows a clear **before → after** structure.
