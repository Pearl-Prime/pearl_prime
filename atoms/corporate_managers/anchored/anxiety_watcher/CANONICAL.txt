

## RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/anxiety_watcher/recognition/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
David sits in his own meeting and watches himself deliver feedback. Part of him is present in the conversation and part of him is hovering above it, evaluating. How does he sound? Is he being clear? Is the engineer taking this well? Is he coming across as competent? The watcher divides his attention. He's not fully in the feedback conversation; he's partially monitoring his own performance. His chest feels both tight and distant. He's managing the other person and simultaneously managing the impression he's making.
