

## RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/burnout_shame/recognition/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
David is depleted. His chest is always heavy. He used to enjoy solving engineering problems. Now the thought exhausts him. He's burned out. The shame fires immediately: good leaders don't burn out. Good leaders manage their energy. He should be able to do this. His inability to sustain feels like personal failure. His hands are cold. The burnout is happening and the shame about the burnout is doubling it.
