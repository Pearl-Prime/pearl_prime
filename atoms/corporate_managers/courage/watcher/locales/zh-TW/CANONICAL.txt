### 4. The Tragedy of the Map
**MECHANISM_PROOF v03** offers a haunting summary of the opportunity cost:
> *"He has a perfect map of every room he was ever afraid in and zero moments of having been brave. His eyes fill. The map is worthless."*

This speaks to your reflection on **"the inherited nature of management burnout."** Many leaders inherit this "map-making" behavior—thinking that if they just analyze enough, predict enough, and prepare enough, they can eliminate risk. *   **Old Model:** Scan constantly → Identify potential threat → Suppress action to maintain safety → Burnout from sustained tension.
