## TAKEAWAY v03

Your wiring does not feel time. Put a clock where your eyes already are.

## TAKEAWAY v04

## TAKEAWAY v05

## TAKEAWAY v06

## TAKEAWAY v07

## TAKEAWAY v08

## TAKEAWAY v09

## TAKEAWAY v10

## TAKEAWAY v11
