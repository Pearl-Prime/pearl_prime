---
David lies awake and watches his inability to sleep. His chest is tight. Sarah watches herself not sleeping and evaluates failure. Her hands are cold. Kevin watches sleep elude him. His shoulders tense. Anthony watches the night pass. His throat tightens. Tanya watches insomnia. Her stomach is tight. Watching prevents sleep. David stops watching and practices sleep hygiene. His chest opens. Sarah stops evaluating and sleeps. Her hands warm. Kevin lets go and rests. His shoulders relax. Anthony stops monitoring and sleeps. His throat opens. Tanya releases the watch and sleeps. Her stomach calms. Releasing watching allows sleep.
