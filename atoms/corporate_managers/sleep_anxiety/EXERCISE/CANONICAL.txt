## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Pre-sleep: tomorrow's list runs in your head. Write it down. Every task. Get it outside your mind. Now it is on paper.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
In bed, anxiety spirals about tomorrow. Close your eyes. Slowly scan your body from head down. Foot. Ankle. Calf. Notice each part.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Can't sleep? Don't force it. Lie still. Feel the weight of your body in bed. The bed is holding you. Rest will come.
---
