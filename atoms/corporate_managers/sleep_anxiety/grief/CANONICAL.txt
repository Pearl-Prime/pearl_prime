## MECHANISM_PROOF v01

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David notices that grief about sleep anxiety surfaces at predictable triggers — a date, a name, a place, a song. The grief is not random. It has architecture. It waits at specific locations and activates when David passes through. Jaw clenches. David sees the machinery: the grief has embedded itself in the landscape of daily life. The conference room where the last meeting happened. The calendar date that marks the anniversary. The protective logic is clear — the grief marks the places that mattered, ensuring they are never forgotten. But the marking also ensures they are never just places again. Stomach drops. The pattern persists because the grief is the price of having cared, and the caring was real, and the places where the caring happened still exist.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/sleep_anxiety/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David realizes the grief about sleep anxiety has been compounding for years. Not one loss but an accumulation — each small grief adding to the pile because none were fully processed. The current grief is not about the current loss. It is about every loss that preceded it. Shoulders lock. David sees the full machinery: each ungrieved loss adds weight to the next. The system has no outlet. Grief enters and never leaves. It accumulates in the chest, in the shoulders, in the jaw. Throat closes. The pattern persists because grieving requires time and space and both are in short supply. The unprocessed grief stays stored, and stored grief compounds into depression, and depression prevents the processing that would relieve the accumulation. The system is a grief warehouse with no exits.
