MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David catches the shame's trigger mechanism around sleep anxiety. The shame does not respond to actual failure — it responds to the possibility of being perceived as failing. Vision narrows. A meeting where David might not know the answer. An email that might contain criticism. A review that might reveal a gap. The shame fires on "might." It does not wait for evidence. It preempts evidence by generating its own conviction of inadequacy. Chest hollow. The pattern persists because the shame is prophylactic — it punishes David before the world can, creating the illusion that self-punishment prevents external punishment. But self-punishment does not prevent anything. It just arrives first.
