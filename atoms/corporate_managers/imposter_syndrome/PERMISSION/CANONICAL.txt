


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel like an imposter and still be the right person for the job. The feeling and the fact coexist.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to ask the question in the meeting. The question will not expose you. It will inform you.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to accept the praise as yours, not the role's.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to prepare normally instead of over-preparing to compensate for doubt.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to be in the room without constantly auditioning for the right to be there.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to let the success count as evidence. Not as luck.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to not know everything and still lead effectively.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to feel uncertain and keep the feeling to yourself or share it. Both are valid.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to trust your track record even when your body doesn't.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to stop waiting for the correction that isn't coming.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to be seen as competent and believe it at the same time.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to have gotten here on merit. The merit is real even when the doubt is loud.
---
