*   **Your Reality:** You calculate worst-case financial scenarios constantly. You feel the ground is unstable. *   **The Cost:** You are paying the emotional tax of a crisis that hasn’t happened. Your muscle tension, shaking hands, and jaw tightness are the physical receipt for this preemptive punishment. You are working harder than others not because you need to, but because your shame insists that *vigilance* is the only thing preventing annihilation.
