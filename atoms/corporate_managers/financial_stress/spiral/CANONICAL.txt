Linda opened a savings account statement at midnight. The balance triggered a thought: "What if this runs out?" From there, her mind spiraled. How long would it last if she lost her job? What if her salary gets cut? What if the kids' needs increase? She's now imagining scenarios three to five years out. Each scenario is darker. Each scenario leads to another scenario. By 2 AM she's convinced herself that her financial security is fragile. By 3 AM she's planning for disaster. The plan isn't preventing disaster. It's amplifying her sense that disaster is coming. She's exhausted and hasn't solved anything.
