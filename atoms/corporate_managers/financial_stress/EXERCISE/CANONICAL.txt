## EXERCISE v01
---

---
The numbers feel catastrophic. Ask: what must happen today. Write it down. Everything else is future thinking. Focus on what you can do.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
In the financial review meeting, ground yourself. Feel your chair. Distinguish urgent from catastrophic. Urgent: deadline this week. Catastrophic: what never happens.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
After staring at spreadsheets, step back. Place both hands on your desk. Feel the solidity. You have navigated this before. Name one time.
