## EXERCISE v01
---

---
After a difficult one-on-one meeting, separate. Go to a different room. Place your hand on your chest. Feel your heartbeat. You are here. Your heart.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
After absorbing someone else's crisis, sit alone. Notice what you carry that is not yours. Name it. Say: this is theirs, not mine.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
At day's end, stand before a mirror. Look at your face. Say one kind thing to yourself. Not to the team. To you.
---
