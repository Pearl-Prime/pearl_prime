## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit with both feet flat on the floor. Press down. Feel the ground push back. That resistance is real. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for two. Exhale for six. Let your jaw unclench. Notice what changed.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place one hand on your sternum. Feel it rise and fall. Name the tightness there. Don't fix it. Breathe into that spot for three full breaths. Release your hand. Notice the warmth left behind.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Stand up from your chair. Plant your feet hip-width apart. Press your heels down hard. Feel your spine lengthen. Drop your shoulders. Take one slow breath. Sit back down deliberately. Carry that posture forward.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Clench both fists tight. Hold for five counts. Release completely. Feel the blood return. Shake your hands loose. Notice the difference between grip and open. That difference is available to you all day.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Close your eyes. Locate where tension lives right now. Chest. Jaw. Neck. Name it aloud or silently. Breathe directly into that location. Exhale slowly. Repeat twice more. Open your eyes.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Roll your shoulders forward slowly. Then back. Then down. Stop there. Feel the space across your upper chest. Take one breath from that open position. Hold it. Release. Keep your shoulders where they are.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Press your palms flat on your desk. Feel the surface temperature. Feel the pressure in your wrists. Breathe in. Push down slightly as you exhale. Notice your spine straighten automatically. Lift your palms. Pause.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Unclench your back teeth. Let your tongue drop from the roof of your mouth. Feel the space created. Breathe in through your nose. Let your belly lead. Exhale through your mouth. Repeat three times.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Before your next meeting, stop outside the door. Put both feet on the floor. Feel your weight. Breathe in for four counts. Exhale for four counts. Walk in. You enter differently now.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Sit back fully in your chair. Let the chair hold you. Stop bracing. Feel the difference between holding yourself up and being supported. Take three breaths from that supported position. Notice your shoulders drop.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
Lift your gaze from the screen. Focus on something ten feet away. Let your eyes soften. Feel the muscles around your eyes release. Take one slow breath. Blink deliberately. Return to work from here.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Place both hands on your thighs, palms down. Feel the warmth. Feel your legs solid beneath you. Press down gently. Breathe in. On the exhale, release all grip in your hands. Stay here. Breathe once more.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
After a hard conversation, find a wall. Press your back against it. Feel the full surface contact. Let the wall take your weight. Breathe in. Breathe out slowly. Push back gently. Feel yourself push back.
---
