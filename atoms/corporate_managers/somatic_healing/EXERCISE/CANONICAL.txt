## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit upright. Breathe in through your nose slowly. Feel your ribs expand. Feel your belly rise. Hold. Now exhale completely. Feel the release.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Standing, shake out your shoulders. Roll them back. Feel the shift in your chest. Feel upper back. Press feet down. Notice stability.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Close your eyes. Scan your body. Notice where you hold tension. Your jaw. Your neck. Your shoulders. Don't change it. Just notice.
---
