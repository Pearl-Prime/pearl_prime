## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Your next meeting starts in ten minutes. Stop. Plant both feet flat on the floor. Feel the pressure. Breathe out fully. Count to four. You exist outside your title.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Before you open your inbox, pause. Put one hand on your sternum. Notice your chest rise. Count three breaths. Do not check the subject lines yet. Three breaths first.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You just left a difficult conversation. Find a wall. Press your back against it. Feel the solid surface. Stay there. Count to five. The wall holds. You do not have to.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Pick up your coffee mug. Hold it with both hands. Feel the warmth. Notice the weight. Drink one slow sip. Taste it. That moment belonged to no one else.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Close your office door or face away from your screen. Drop your shoulders deliberately. They rose without permission. Let them fall. Breathe in for four counts. Out for six. Repeat once.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Write one name on a notepad. Someone on your team. Not a task. Just a name. Set the pen down. That person is real. So are you.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Stand up from your chair. Walk to a window. Look at something distant. Not a screen. Blink slowly. Stay there for sixty seconds. Count them if you need to.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Before your next skip-level, sit still. Notice your jaw. Unclench it. Notice your hands. Open them flat on your thighs. Take one full breath. You walked in. That counts.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Find the heaviest object near you. A stapler. A book. Hold it. Feel the actual weight in your palm. Set it down slowly. Weight is real. So is this moment.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
After reading the reorg email, do not forward it yet. Put both palms on your desk surface. Press down lightly. Feel the resistance. The desk pushes back. You are still here.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
Name one task you completed today. Say it aloud, quietly. Not in your head. Out loud. One word or three. Spoken things become real. You did something. It happened.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
In the elevator, do not look at your phone. Stand still. Feel the floor vibrate. Notice the hum. Count floors as they pass. Arrive knowing you were present for the ride.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
At the end of your workday, close one application. Just one. Watch the window disappear. Exhale once, fully. That closure was yours. You chose it. Nothing else did.
