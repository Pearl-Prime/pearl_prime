


## PIVOT v01
---

---
The exhaustion isn't from the work. It's from the performance of being fine while doing the work.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You didn't burn out because you worked too hard. You burned out because the work stopped meaning anything and you kept going.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The problem isn't that you're tired. It's that rest doesn't fix what's actually broken.
---

## PIVOT v04
---

---
You thought the burnout was about the hours. It's about the gap between what you give and what comes back.
---

## PIVOT v05
---

---
The weekend doesn't recharge you because the battery isn't low. The battery is a different shape now.
---

## PIVOT v06
---

---
You're not lazy. You're depleted in a way that effort can't reach. The depletion is structural.
---

## PIVOT v07
---

---
The cynicism isn't a personality change. It's your system's way of protecting itself from caring about something that doesn't care back.
---

## PIVOT v08
---

---
You held it together for your team. Your team didn't notice because holding it together was invisible. The invisibility is the cost.
---

## PIVOT v09
---

---
The recognition never came. But the burnout isn't about recognition. It's about a system that kept giving without receiving until the system changed.
---

## PIVOT v10
---

---
You thought saying yes was loyalty. Now the yeses have no space left and the loyalty is a cage.
---

## PIVOT v11
---

---
The dread before Monday isn't about Monday. It's about a body that learned Mondays are where the depletion begins again.
---

## PIVOT v12
---

---
You're not burned out from doing too much. You're burned out from doing too much of what doesn't matter to you.
---

## PIVOT v13
---

---
The efficiency that saved you is the thing that's killing you. The machine you built runs perfectly and it runs on you.
---

## PIVOT v14
---

---
The numbness isn't apathy. It's the circuit breaker your system tripped when caring became unsustainable.
---

## PIVOT v15
---

---
You stopped enjoying the wins. Not because you're ungrateful. Because the wins stopped registering in a body that ran out of room to receive them.
---

## PIVOT v16
---

---
The vacation didn't help because the thing you needed a vacation from came back with you. The thing is the pattern, not the place.
---

## PIVOT v17
---

---
You built the systems. You trained the team. You documented everything. And now you feel replaceable. The replaceability is the burnout talking.
---

## PIVOT v18
---

---
The output stayed high while the person producing it got smaller. The gap between output and person is the burnout.
---

## PIVOT v19
---

---
You didn't lose your passion. Your system redirected it to survival. Survival is not the same as purpose.
---

## PIVOT v20
---

---
The problem isn't the workload. The problem is that you stopped being a person who does work and became a person who is the work.
---
