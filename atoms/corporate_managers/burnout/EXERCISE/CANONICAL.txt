## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before your next one-on-one, sit down first. Place one hand flat on the desk. Press down until you feel the surface. Breathe out completely. Now open the calendar.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Pick one task on your list right now. Write it on paper. Draw a box around it. That box is the only thing that exists. Work only inside it for twenty minutes.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Stand up from your chair. Walk to a window. Find one fixed point outside. Hold your eyes there for thirty seconds. Let your shoulders fall. Return to your desk.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
After your next performance conversation, go somewhere alone. Press your back flat against a wall. Feel the wall hold you. Count five slow exhales. Then move on.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Open your email. Close it. Do not open it again for fifteen minutes. Set a timer now. Use those minutes for nothing urgent. Sit with the discomfort.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Write down three things your team finished this week. Not tasks remaining. Finished ones only. Read each item aloud. Pause after each one. Then put the paper away.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Before your skip-level meeting, find a bathroom. Run cold water over your wrists for ten seconds. Dry your hands slowly. Roll your neck once to each side. Walk in.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
At the end of today, write one sentence. Finish this: "Today I held." One sentence. No explanation. Fold the paper. Leave it on your desk overnight.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Notice your jaw right now. Is it clenched. Release it. Let your tongue drop from the roof of your mouth. Breathe in through your nose for four counts. Out for six.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Block thirty minutes on Friday. Label it "closed." Tell no one what it is for. When it arrives, do not fill it. Sit in it. Let it stay empty.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
After your next reorg meeting, do not check Slack. Go outside the building. Walk one full block. Notice three things you can hear. Return through a different door.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Right now, unclench your hands. Spread your fingers wide. Hold that for five seconds. Let them close slowly. Feel the difference in your palms. Return to work.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
At lunch today, eat without a screen. Put your phone face down. Chew slowly enough to taste the food. When your mind pulls toward your inbox, bring it back. Eat.
