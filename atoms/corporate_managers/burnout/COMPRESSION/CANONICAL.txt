

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
You know how to recover from failure. You know how to recover from loss. But you don't know how to recover from success when success means you've become the lynchpin of something that can't pause. And that not knowing is its own kind of exhaustion. The exhaustion of being trapped inside your own achievement.

## COMPRESSION v32
---
compression_family: C2
---
Your team needs you and you have nothing left. The one-on-one is in ten minutes. Your direct report is struggling. You know exactly what to say. You know exactly how to coach this moment. And you cannot summon the energy to care. The caring muscle has been overworked. It has seized. You will say the right words. The words will be empty. Your report will leave the meeting feeling helped. You will remain in your chair feeling hollow.

## COMPRESSION v33
---

---
Three open headcount. Six months unfilled. The work did not decrease because the headcount decreased. The work redistributed to the people who remained. To you. The math is simple. The math is ignored. The organization operates as though bandwidth is infinite. Your bandwidth ended four months ago. You are now operating on deficit energy. The deficit compounds like interest.

## COMPRESSION v34
---
compression_family: C4
---
You got the promotion and felt nothing. The email arrived. Congratulations. Your title changed. Your scope expanded. Your compensation increased. And the feeling you expected, the sense of accomplishment, the reward for years of effort, did not arrive. The feeling that arrived instead was dread. More scope means more demand. More compensation means more expectation. The promotion was not a reward. It was an expansion of the thing that is destroying you.

## COMPRESSION v35
---
compression_family: C5
---
Your doctor asked about your energy levels. You said: I'm tired. Your doctor asked how long you've been tired. You calculated. The answer shocked you. Two years. You have been tired for two years. Not sleepy. Not overworked-tired. Bone-tired. The kind of tired that sleep does not fix because the tired is not in your muscles. It is in your capacity to care. The fatigue is motivational, not physical.

## COMPRESSION v36
---
compression_family: C1
---
You catch yourself fantasizing about minor injuries. A broken ankle. Something that would require six weeks of recovery. Not because you want pain. Because you want permission. Permission to stop. Permission to be unavailable. Permission to lie on a couch without the guilt of choosing it. The fantasy of injury is the burnout's way of telling you that you cannot give yourself permission to rest unless the rest is imposed by force.

## COMPRESSION v49
---

---
Your body has started saying no on your behalf. The migraines that cancel meetings. The back spasm that requires you to work from home.
