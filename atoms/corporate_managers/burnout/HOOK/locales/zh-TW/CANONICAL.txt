Here are 30 persona-specific hooks tailored for **Corporate Managers** dealing with **Burnout**. These are designed to stop the scroll by addressing specific pain points like decision fatigue, imposter syndrome, the "always-on" culture, and the isolation of leadership.

### HOOK v01
**The "High-Functioning" Trap**
"You’re not ‘high-functioning.’ You’re just really good at hiding how much you’re drowning. Here’s the difference."

### HOOK v02
**The Sunday Scaries**
"If your heart rate spikes at 4 PM on Sunday, you don’t have a time management problem. You have a boundary problem."

### HOOK v03
**Decision Fatigue**
"Stop making decisions after 8 PM. Your brain isn’t wired for it, and your team doesn’t need your ‘best’ work at midnight—they need your rested work at 9 AM."

### HOOK v04
**The Promotion Lie**
"They promoted you because you were a great individual contributor. Now they’re watching you burn out because nobody taught you how to lead without doing everything yourself."

### HOOK v05
**Slack Anxiety**
"Turn off your Slack notifications. No, seriously. The world won’t end, but your cortisol levels might if you don’t."

### HOOK v06
**Imposter Syndrome**
"Feeling like a fraud? Good. It means you’re growing. But feeling exhausted? That’s not growth. That’s negligence."

### HOOK v07
**The "Quick Question"**
"There is no such thing as a ‘quick question’ from a direct report at 9:30 PM. Stop rewarding after-hours availability."

### HOOK v08
**Meeting Hell**
"If you have more than 4 hours of meetings a day, you aren’t managing. You’re attending. And you’re too tired to actually lead."

### HOOK v09
**The Guilt Trip**
"Taking a mental health day isn’t ‘slacking off.’ It’s maintenance. You wouldn’t drive a car for 100k miles without an oil change. Why do you expect your brain to?"

### HOOK v10
**Performance vs. Presence**
"Your team doesn’t need you to be present 24/7. They need you to be present *when it matters*. Stop confusing visibility with value."

### HOOK v11
**The Email Reflex**
"Delete the email app from your phone. If it’s an emergency, they’ll call. If it’s not, it can wait until Monday. You are not on call for life."

### HOOK v12
**Leading by Example**
"You tell your team to prioritize work-life balance, then you send emails at midnight. They aren’t listening to your words. They’re watching your actions."

### HOOK v13
**The "Busy" Badge**
"Being ‘busy’ isn’t a status symbol. It’s a sign that you’ve failed to delegate, prioritize, or say no."

### HOOK v14
**Emotional Labor**
"Managing emotions is part of the job. Managing *everyone else’s* emotions while suppressing your own is a one-way ticket to burnout."

### HOOK v15
**The Vacation Myth**
"Checking email ‘just once’ on vacation doesn’t keep you in control. It keeps you in chains. Disconnect completely, or don’t bother leaving."

### HOOK v16
**Perfectionism**
"Perfect is the enemy of done. And ‘done’ is the enemy of your sanity. Aim for ‘effective,’ not ‘flawless.’"

### HOOK v17
**The Isolation**
"Leadership is lonely. But suffering in silence isn’t ‘strong.’ It’s unsustainable. Find a peer group. Talk. Vent. Heal."

### HOOK v18
**The Morning Rush**
"If your first act of the day is checking email, you’ve already lost. You’re reacting, not leading. Reclaim your morning."

### HOOK v19
**Delegation Fear**
"You’re afraid to delegate because you think it’ll take longer to explain. It will. Once. Then you get your life back. Do the math."

### HOOK v20
**The "Rockstar" Culture**
"Stop hiring ‘rockstars’ who burn out in 18 months. Start building sustainable teams who stay for 5 years. Consistency beats intensity."

### HOOK v21
**Physical Symptoms**
"Headaches. Insomnia. Irritability. These aren’t ‘stress.’ They’re your body’s eviction notice. Listen before you get kicked out."

### HOOK v22
**The Feedback Loop**
"You’re terrified to give hard feedback because you want to be liked. So you let issues fester, causing more stress later. Clarity is kindness."

### HOOK v23
**The Weekend Work**
"Working weekends doesn’t make you dedicated. It makes you inefficient. Fix your workflow, not your calendar."

### HOOK v24
**Identity Crisis**
"You are not your job title. When the laptop closes, who are you? If you don’t know, you’re already halfway gone."

### HOOK v25
**The "No" Power**
"The most important word in a manager’s vocabulary isn’t ‘Yes.’ It’s ‘No.’ Say it more often. Your team will thank you."

### HOOK v26
**Micromanagement**
"You micromanage because you’re anxious. Your team resents it because they’re capable. Trust them. It lowers their stress and yours."

### HOOK v27
**The Energy Audit**
"Track your energy for a week, not your time. What drains you? What fuels you? Stop doing the things that drain you without purpose."

### HOOK v28
**The Comparison Trap**
"Stop comparing your behind-the-scenes struggle with everyone else’s highlight reel on LinkedIn. You’re playing a different game."

### HOOK v29
**The Exit Interview**
"Don’t wait for your exit interview to admit you’re burned out. By then, it’s too late. Fix it now, or plan your escape."

### HOOK v30
**The Legacy**
"Do you want to be remembered as the manager who never slept? Or the leader who built a team that thrived? Choose wisely."
