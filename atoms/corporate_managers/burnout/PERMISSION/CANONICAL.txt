


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be burned out and still good at your job. The burnout is not a performance issue.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to stop saying yes without an explanation that sounds like something is wrong.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel nothing about a win you worked months to achieve. The numbness is real.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to need more than a vacation. You are allowed to need something structural to change.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to resent the work without feeling guilty about the paycheck.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to admit that you gave more than was asked and received less than was fair.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to feel tired in a way that sleep cannot fix.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to stop being the person who holds everything together. The everything was never yours to hold alone.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to want to quit without being ready to quit. Wanting is information.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to need your work to mean something. That need is not entitlement.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to feel cynical about the mission statement. The cynicism is earned.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to take a mental health day without calling it a stomach bug.
---

## PERMISSION v13
---

---
You are allowed to have given your best and still feel like it wasn't enough. That's the burnout, not the truth.
---

## PERMISSION v14
---

---
You are allowed to close the laptop at 5pm and mean it.
---

## PERMISSION v15
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the version of yourself that used to love this work.
---

## PERMISSION v16
---

---
You are allowed to feel angry that nobody noticed how much you were carrying.
---

## PERMISSION v17
---

---
You are allowed to set a boundary that inconveniences the team. Boundaries are not betrayals.
---

## PERMISSION v18
---

---
You are allowed to stop proving that you can handle more. You've proven it. The proof is the problem.
---

## PERMISSION v19
---

---
You are allowed to do less well for a while. A while is not forever.
---

## PERMISSION v20
---

---
