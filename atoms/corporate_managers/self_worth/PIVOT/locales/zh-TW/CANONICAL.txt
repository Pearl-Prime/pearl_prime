### The Pivot Point

You are currently stuck in a loop:
1.  **Financial Stress** creates bodily dread.
2.  **Imposter Syndrome** drives over-performance to secure the income.
3.  **Over-performance** drains the energy needed to process the stress or plan an exit.
4.  **Lack of Cushion** prevents you from leaving, reinforcing the trap.

**The question these pivots raise is not "How do I stop feeling like an imposter?"**

**The right question is:**

Given your financial constraints ($140k vs $170k market, no cushion), you cannot simply "quit." But you can stop paying the **Performance Tax**.
