


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel like a placeholder and still do the job well. Feeling and performing are separate.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to not absorb your team's failures as evidence of your worth.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to receive positive feedback and believe it. Not just note it.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel insufficient in a role that is designed to exceed any one person.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to let the quarterly review measure the quarter, not you.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to lead without feeling like you have to be the best person in the room.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to want recognition and not feel petty for wanting it.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to stop comparing yourself to the peer who got promoted. Their path is theirs.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to have doubts about your capability and still show up as the manager.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to separate your team's outcomes from your personal value.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to be seen as good enough even when you don't feel it.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to exist as a person who manages, not as a manager who occasionally remembers to be a person.
---
