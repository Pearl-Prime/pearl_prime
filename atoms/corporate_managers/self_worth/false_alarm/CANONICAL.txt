## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David tracks the alarm's behavior around self-worth. It fires at the same intensity for genuine concerns and trivial irritants. A significant deadline and a routine email produce identical physiological responses — same cortisol, same chest pressure, same cold hands. Chest tight. David sees the machinery: the alarm has no calibration mechanism. It runs a single program at a single volume for all inputs. The pattern persists because the alarm's inability to distinguish intensity means David is always at maximum alert. Hands cold. There is no resting state. Every stimulus is emergency-grade. The body never recovers because recovery requires a period of non-emergency and the alarm never permits one.
