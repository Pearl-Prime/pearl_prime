---
David gets one piece of negative feedback and spirals into worthlessness. His chest tightens. Sarah makes one mistake and thinks she has no value. Her hands are cold. Kevin gets one critical comment and spirals into believing he's worthless. His shoulders tense. Anthony hears one doubt and spirals into believing he's valueless. His throat tightens. Tanya gets one criticism and spirals into self-worth crisis. Her stomach is tight. One feedback becomes total judgment. David separates feedback from worth. His chest unclenches. Sarah recognizes one mistake doesn't determine value. Her hands warm. Kevin understands criticism is about action, not worth. His shoulders release. Anthony separates feedback from identity. His throat releases. Tanya learns criticism is data, not judgment of her. Her stomach settles. Feedback stays as feedback.
