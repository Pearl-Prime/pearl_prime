## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/overthinking/spiral/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David enters the spiral about overthinking. First loop: the situation is concerning. Second loop: the situation is dire. Third loop: the situation is irreversible. Fourth loop: everything connected to the situation will also fail. Jaw clenches. Stomach drops. By loop five, David has constructed a complete catastrophe from an incomplete data set. Then David writes down loop one: the situation is concerning. That is the fact. Loops two through five are fiction. The frame cracks — the spiral was not analyzing the situation. It was decorating the situation with fear until the fear became the situation. Jaw releases The fact is manageable. The fiction was not.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/overthinking/spiral/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David spirals about overthinking for so long that the body gives out. The spiral ran continuously for hours, building catastrophe upon catastrophe, consuming cognitive resources until there was nothing left. Chest hollow. David hits the floor of the spiral — the place where even the spiral cannot continue because the body has no fuel. In the silence at the bottom, something shifts. The catastrophe did not arrive. Hours of prediction and the predicted future did not materialize. David sits in the gap between the spiral's reality and actual reality and the distance is infinite. The frame shatters — the spiral was not preparing for catastrophe. It was manufacturing catastrophe inside David's body while the actual world continued normally. Hands grip the desk. The old way is no longer survivable because the spiral consumed the body it was supposed to protect.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Sarah feels the spiral begin about overthinking — the familiar first loop, the pull toward the second. Sarah catches it. Writes down the first thought. Sits with the first thought. Does not build loop two. Chest crushes. The first thought is manageable. The spiral wanted to make it unmanageable. Sarah chose the manageable version. Hands warm Sarah is a person who can sit with a concern without converting it into a catastrophe. The concern is real. The catastrophe was invented. Sarah keeps the concern and releases the catastrophe.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/overthinking/spiral/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
The biggest overthinking decision arrives and the spiral tries to consume it. Every option generates six counter-arguments. Every path forward triggers twelve catastrophic projections. Shoulders lock. Sarah sits in the storm of the spiral and makes the decision anyway. Imperfect information. Incomplete analysis. The spiral screams that more thinking is needed. Sarah acts. Throat closes. The decision lands. The consequences unfold. Some are good. Some are hard. All are real. The spiral's catastrophic projections were wrong — as they always are — because reality is more nuanced than fear. Sarah is a person who can act inside the spiral without waiting for the spiral to stop. The spiral may never stop. Sarah acts anyway. Hands are shaking and decisive This is the final identity: a person who carries the spiral and moves forward simultaneously.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/overthinking/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David maps the spiral's structure around overthinking on paper. The first thought is reasonable: a concern about a real situation. The second thought adds interpretation. The third adds projection. The fourth adds certainty. By the fifth, the concern has become a conviction of doom. Hands grip the desk. Each loop adds one thing: certainty. The spiral does not add information. It adds confidence to the same fear. By loop five, David is certain about a catastrophe that loop one merely wondered about. Face burns. The pattern persists because certainty feels like knowledge. The spiral produces the feeling of knowing something terrible, and the feeling is so convincing that David treats the feeling as evidence.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/corporate_managers/anchored/overthinking/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David has been spiraling about overthinking for so long that the spiral has consumed the subject it was processing. David can no longer distinguish between the original concern and the spiral's elaboration of it. The concern and the catastrophe have merged. Chest crushes. David sees the full machinery: the spiral does not process problems. It metabolizes them into fuel for more spiraling. Each problem that enters the spiral is converted into material for the next loop. The spiral feeds on its own output. Hands shake. The pattern persists because the spiral has become the primary activity. David spirals instead of acting. The spiraling feels like work — effortful, exhausting, productive-seeming. But it produces nothing except more spiraling. It is a machine that manufactures its own input from its own output, a perpetual motion engine of suffering that will run until the body cannot sustain it.
