

## HOOK v01

---
You're responsible for 12 people and their development, their engagement, their performance. You're also trying to hide that you yourself are struggling. The hiding creates the overthinking—you're internally processing what you can't externally express.

---

---
The email you sent to your team could be read two ways. One is confident leadership. One is concern. You're replaying it trying to decide which reading they had. The reading determines whether you've communicated strength or weakness.

---

## HOOK v06
