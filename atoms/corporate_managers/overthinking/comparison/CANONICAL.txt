David watches a peer handle overthinking with apparent ease. The comparison fires hot — David struggles while others seem to navigate the same terrain effortlessly. Chest tight. Hands cold. The gap between David's experience and the peer's performance feels like proof of deficiency. Then something cracks open. ---
David's overthinking reaches a breaking point. The comparison has been running for so long and so hard that the body refuses to continue. Hands grip the desk. Face burns. David stands in the wreckage of a metric system that collapsed under its own weight — measuring against others until the measurement consumed all the energy that should have gone to the actual work. Then the person David has been comparing against reveals their own collapse. The carefully maintained exterior crumbles. David's chest cracks open. The comparison was between two performances of adequacy, not between adequacy and inadequacy. Both were performing. Both were breaking. The frame shatters because the person who seemed to have it all together was held together by the same kind of wire. Nobody wins the comparison because nobody was competing. They were all just surviving.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
David catches the comparison operating in real time within overthinking. The comparison fires before the thought completes — automatic, pre-rational, faster than analysis. Chest crushes. David watches the machinery: a neutral data point enters the system and exits as a deficit. The peer's action becomes evidence of David's inadequacy. The conversion happens in milliseconds. Hands shake. The protective logic is visible now: if David tracks who is better, David can never be surprised by falling short. But the tracking IS the falling short. The comparison creates the deficit it claims to measure. The pattern persists because the comparison feels like assessment when it is actually assault.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02

COST_TYPE: internal

---
David lies awake at night and the comparison runs its full program within overthinking. Every peer. Every gap. Every dimension on which David falls short. The comparison has become autonomous — it needs no trigger, no input, no event. It generates its own material from memory and projection. Chest tight. David sees the full architecture in the dark: the comparison is not a response to external data. It is an internal program that creates its own data. It selects the peers. It chooses the dimensions. It constructs the gaps. The entire comparison is self-authored. Hands cold. The pattern persists because the comparison feels like it is about other people when it is entirely about David's relationship with David's own adequacy. Other people are just the raw material. The deficiency is pre-existing. The comparison just gives it a face.
