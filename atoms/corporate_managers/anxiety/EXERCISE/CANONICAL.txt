## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before the difficult meeting, stand. Feel both feet on the floor. Breathe in through your nose. Count: one, two, three, four. Exhale slowly through your mouth. You have been in this room before.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
At your desk, push back from the monitor. Place feet flat on the floor. Press your hands down on your thighs. Notice the pressure. Hold it. You are here. You are solid.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Between back-to-back calls, stop. Close your eyes for five seconds. Name one fact that is true now. Not future. Not the worst case. What is actually happening today.
