## EXERCISE v01
---

---
When the anxiety spirals about future money, pause. Look at your account right now. What is the number today. Not next month. Today.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
At the budget meeting, feel your feet. Breathe out fully. Stay with this present moment number. Not the projected disaster. What is now.
