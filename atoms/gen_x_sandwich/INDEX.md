## Engine Mechanics Integrated

Each file contains narrative atoms demonstrating one psychological/somatic mechanism:

### SHAME
The shame of not doing enough for parents/children/work/self. The shame of needing help when raised to handle alone.

### FALSE_ALARM
Every parent's fall or unusual symptom reads as the beginning of the end. Every teen's silence reads as crisis.

### OVERWHELM
Too many people needing them. The math has never worked. They are one person holding up multiple households.

### COMPARISON
Siblings who moved away and didn't take on caregiver role. Friends whose parents are healthy. Peers who seem to have more capacity.

### SPIRAL
Missed call from parent → something's wrong → should have called sooner → they're not doing enough → all the other things they're not doing.

### WATCHER
Watching themselves manage crises. Watching themselves be competent. Knowing they're running on empty and watching themselves perform energy they don't have.

### GRIEF
Grief for the parent being lost in real time. Grief for the retirement that can't be imagined. Grief for the self that got lost in the middle of everything.

## Writing Standards

✓ Specific body sensations (exact tension location)
✓ Character names from cast (Carol, Tom, Diane, Mike, Janet, Steve, Paula, Ray)
