## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel numb. After years of absorbing everyone's emotions -- your parents', your children's, your own -- the numbness is not apathy. It is a system that gave everything it had and is now running on reserves.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to not feel compassion today and still be a compassionate person. One does not cancel the other. Fatigue is temporary. The caring underneath it is structural.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to stop being the emotional first responder for every person in your orbit. Stepping back from that role is not abandonment. It is the only way to return to it with anything left to offer.
---
