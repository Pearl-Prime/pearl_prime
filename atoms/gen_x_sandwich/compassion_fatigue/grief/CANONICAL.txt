## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Phil stands in the middle of the pattern and sees it. The loss and absence runs hot—ribcage constricts. Phil has carried this story about compassion fatigue for so long the weight became invisible. Now the weight announces itself. Goosebumps erupt across Phil's skin. The old frame says: endure. Push through. Keep performing. But the frame cracks. Phil watches the crack spread. The story that held everything together—the one about being strong enough, disciplined enough, good enough—stops making sense. Not because it was wrong. Because it was incomplete. Phil feels the incompleteness in the body. Something shifts behind the sternum. The old story doesn't fit the evidence anymore. Phil stands in the gap between what was believed and what is true.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Mike breaks. The loss and absence pattern reaches maximum intensity and something gives. Mike's knees weaken. Every system fires at once. The compassion fatigue is no longer background noise. It is the entire room. Mike's chest locks. And then—in the center of the breaking—Mike sees the architecture. The whole structure. How the loss and absence feeds the compassion fatigue feeds the loss and absence feeds the collapse. Mike sees the loop from inside the loop's core. The frame doesn't shift gently. It shatters. What replaces it is not comfort. It is clarity. Mike stands in the wreckage of the old story and feels the ground beneath it. Solid. Different. The old frame is debris now. Mike does not rebuild it.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Keith stands in the heat and does not flinch. The loss and absence pattern demands the old response. Keith's vision narrows. The compassion fatigue presses hard. Keith's shoulders climb. But Keith has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Keith acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Keith makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Keith pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Keith is now. The loss and absence pattern fades to background noise.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The loss and absence pattern runs hot and Mike watches it run. Mike's vision narrows. The mechanism is clear: the compassion fatigue activates the loss and absence, the loss and absence generates a protective response, the protective response costs more than doing nothing. Mike's hands go cold. Mike sees why the pattern persists. It persists because it works. Not well. Not efficiently. But it works. It reduces the immediate threat by creating a long-term drain. Mike understands this trade-off for the first time while the pattern is active. The heat doesn't diminish. The understanding doesn't soothe. But Mike sees the logic. The pattern is not irrational. It is a survival strategy that has outlived its context.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Peak intensity strips everything to structure. Keith's voice dies. The compassion fatigue hits maximum. The loss and absence pattern runs at capacity. Keith's belly knots. In the crisis, the mechanism becomes naked. Keith sees it without narrative, without justification, without the story that makes it make sense. Raw machinery. The loss and absence pattern takes the pain of compassion fatigue and converts it into exhausting busyness. The conversion is lossy. More energy goes in than comes out. The deficit accumulates in the body. Keith feels years of deficit in this single moment. The mechanism has been running at a loss for so long the debt is staggering. Keith sees the balance sheet. The numbers do not lie. The pattern costs more than it saves.
