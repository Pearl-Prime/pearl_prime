

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit down. Place both hands on your heart. Feel your heartbeat. This heart has been giving. Giving to your mother. To your child. To everyone. Now give to yourself. Press your hands into your heart. Hold for five counts. Release. Do this ten times. Each time you press, you're sending care to yourself. Each time you release, you're receiving that care. Your heart deserves care too. You deserve care. Feel this. After ten presses, rest your hands on your heart. Feel your own heartbeat. Feel yourself alive. Feel yourself worthy of care.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand up. Shake your hands vigorously. Shake them hard. Feel the release. Feel the energy moving out of you. Not into you. Out. Now shake your feet. Shake hard. Shake the exhaustion out. Now your whole body. Shake. Move. Feel the energy releasing. After thirty seconds, stand still. Feel the lightness. You've released some of the burden. You can do this anytime. You can shake out the exhaustion. You're allowed to release. You're allowed to lighten your load.
