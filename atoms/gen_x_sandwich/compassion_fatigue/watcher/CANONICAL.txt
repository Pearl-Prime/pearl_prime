## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Keith stands in the middle of the pattern and sees it. The hypervigilance and scanning runs hot—throat closes. Keith has carried this story about compassion fatigue for so long the weight became invisible. Now the weight announces itself. Keith's shoulders lock. The old frame says: endure. Push through. Keep performing. But the frame cracks. Keith watches the crack spread. The story that held everything together—the one about being strong enough, disciplined enough, good enough—stops making sense. Not because it was wrong. Because it was incomplete. Keith feels the incompleteness in the body. Something shifts behind the sternum. The old story doesn't fit the evidence anymore. Keith stands in the gap between what was believed and what is true.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Lisa breaks. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern reaches maximum intensity and something gives. Lisa's stomach drops. Every system fires at once. The compassion fatigue is no longer background noise. It is the entire room. Lisa's words jam. And then—in the center of the breaking—Lisa sees the architecture. The whole structure. How the hypervigilance and scanning feeds the compassion fatigue feeds the hypervigilance and scanning feeds the collapse. Lisa sees the loop from inside the loop's core. The frame doesn't shift gently. It shatters. What replaces it is not comfort. It is clarity. Lisa stands in the wreckage of the old story and feels the ground beneath it. Solid. Different. The old frame is debris now. Lisa does not rebuild it.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Andrea stands in the heat and does not flinch. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern demands the old response. Andrea's palms sweat. The compassion fatigue presses hard. Andrea's skin prickles. But Andrea has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Andrea acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Andrea makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Andrea pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Andrea is now. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern fades to background noise.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Lisa's knuckles whiten. The compassion fatigue activates and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern responds on schedule. But this time Lisa traces the mechanism back to its origin. Sees the protective logic. The hypervigilance and scanning started as defense. Lisa learned that vigilance prevented surprise. The defense became automatic. The automatic became identity. Lisa's chest caves inward. The cost structure is visible now: every activation of the hypervigilance and scanning pattern spends energy, narrows options, reinforces the loop. Lisa sees the reinforcement happening. Watches the pattern strengthen itself through use. The mechanism is elegant in its dysfunction. It solves the wrong problem perfectly. Lisa stands in the heat and sees every moving part.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Andrea's thighs tremble. The compassion fatigue and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. Andrea's chest locks. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Andrea sees the entire architecture: how the hypervigilance and scanning creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Andrea sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Andrea that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
