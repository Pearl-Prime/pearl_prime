## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Joan hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: gut twists. The system overload pattern has been running for so long Joan stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Joan's shoulders climb. The old narrative about compassion fatigue says this is weakness. But Joan looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. Sees how the frame keeps Joan locked in a loop that feels like safety but functions as a cage. The frame shifts. Not dramatically. A hairline fracture in the foundation. Joan notices the fracture. Does not repair it. Lets it widen. The system overload pattern stumbles. For the first time, Joan sees the pattern from outside.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Everything collapses at once. Beth's jaw drops. The compassion fatigue detonates and the system overload pattern amplifies it past capacity. Beth's sternum burns. The body cannot sustain this. The mind cannot sustain this. Something has to break. What breaks is the story. The story that tells Beth to hold this. The story that makes holding the only job. The story shatters and beneath it Beth finds something unexpected: the ground. Not the ground of the old narrative. Raw ground. Unnarrated. Beth stands on it and feels it hold weight. The crisis does not resolve. But the frame through which Beth sees the crisis transforms completely. There is no going back to the old interpretation. The system overload pattern has burned through its own container.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Vince stands in the heat and does not flinch. The system overload pattern demands the old response. Vince's stomach turns to stone. The compassion fatigue presses hard. Vince's cheeks burn. But Vince has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Vince acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Vince makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Vince pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Vince is now. The system overload pattern fades to background noise.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

Beth sees the machinery in real time. The system overload pattern fires and Beth catches the sequence: trigger, body response, narrative, behavioral loop. Beth's skin goes cold. The compassion fatigue is hot. Active. But Beth traces the wiring. The system overload protects by adding tasks to manage the overload. It has always protected by adding tasks to manage the overload. The protection costs more than the threat. Beth's shoulders lock. Beth watches the cost accumulate in the body. Watches the pattern spend resources defending against something that is already happening. The mechanism is visible now. Not as theory. As lived architecture. Beth feels every gear turn. The understanding doesn't reduce the heat. But it names the fire.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

Peak intensity strips everything to structure. Vince's stomach drops. The compassion fatigue hits maximum. The system overload pattern runs at capacity. Vince's knuckles whiten. In the crisis, the mechanism becomes naked. Vince sees it without narrative, without justification, without the story that makes it make sense. Raw machinery. The system overload pattern takes the pain of compassion fatigue and converts it into paralysis. The conversion is lossy. More energy goes in than comes out. The deficit accumulates in the body. Vince feels years of deficit in this single moment. The mechanism has been running at a loss for so long the debt is staggering. Vince sees the balance sheet. The numbers do not lie. The pattern costs more than it saves.
