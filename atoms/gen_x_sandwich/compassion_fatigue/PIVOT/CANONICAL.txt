## PIVOT v01
---

---
You used to feel everything -- your mother's pain, your kid's disappointment, the neighbor's bad news. Now you feel a flatness where empathy used to live and you have decided something is wrong with you. Nothing is wrong with you. Compassion fatigue is what happens when the empathy account has been overdrawn for years with no deposits.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: a good caregiver never runs dry. The new frame: you have been the emotional first responder for your parents' decline and your children's growing pains simultaneously. The numbness is not a character failure. It is a circuit breaker doing its job.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You feel guilty for not feeling more. That guilt is the last soldier standing -- proof that your caring has not died, it has just been buried under the weight of caring for everyone on both ends of the age spectrum. The pivot is not to care harder. It is to let someone care for you.
---
