

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit down. Feel your spine. This is your support. Your core strength. Now stand up slowly. Feel this strength moving through you. Stand tall. Feel yourself tall. Feel yourself present. Feel yourself taking up space. You're allowed to be here. You're allowed to take up space. You're allowed to be visible. Stand for thirty seconds. Feel your strength. Feel your presence. You matter. You're here.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place your feet firmly on the ground. Feet shoulder-width apart. Now press down. Press firmly. Feel the ground pressing back. You're connected. You're supported. You're not falling. Press down for five counts. Release. Do this ten times. Each time you press, you're grounding yourself in your own power. Each time you release, you're practicing trust. After ten presses, stand still. Feel the groundedness. You're powerful. You're here.
---
