## TAKEAWAY v01
---

---
You are grieving people who are still here and a life that never arrived. Both of those losses are real even if neither one fits on a sympathy card.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---

---
Sandwich grief does not wait for a funeral. It shows up in the parking lot after the care facility, in the quiet after the kids go to bed.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---

---
You do not need permission to grieve what you have lost while everyone around you is still alive. Loss is not only death. It is also disappearance.
---
