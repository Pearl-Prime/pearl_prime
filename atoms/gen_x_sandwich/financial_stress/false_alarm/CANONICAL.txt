

## RECOGNITION v01
---

---
Carol is financially stressed. She's spending on her mother. Money is tight. Her chest is heavy.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

---
Carol's financial stress is creating shame. She feels ashamed of her financial situation. The mechanism is that shame prevents her from asking for help or making changes. Her chest stays heavy.
---

## TURNING_POINT v01
---

---
Carol stops being ashamed of her financial reality. She adjusts her budget. Her chest opens slightly.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

---
Carol is managing financial stress without shame now. She's adjusted. Her chest is lighter. She's learning that financial constraint is not a personal failure.
---
