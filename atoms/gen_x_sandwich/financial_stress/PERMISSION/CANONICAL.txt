## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be stressed about money even if the bills are technically paid. Technically paid and sustainably funded are different things, and your body knows the difference even when the spreadsheet does not show it.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to ask for help with the financial load. Siblings, community resources, professional guidance -- using them is not failure. It is what people do when the weight exceeds what one person was built to carry.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to stop pretending the numbers work when they do not. The performance of financial composure is costing you sleep, health, and honesty. You deserve to put that mask down.
---
