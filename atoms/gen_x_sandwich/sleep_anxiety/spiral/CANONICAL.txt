## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dan hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: forehead tightens. The recursive thought loops pattern has been running for so long Dan stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Dan's skin prickles. The old narrative about sleep anxiety says this is weakness. But Dan looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. Sees how the frame keeps Dan locked in a loop that feels like safety but functions as a cage. The frame shifts. Not dramatically. A hairline fracture in the foundation. Dan notices the fracture. Does not repair it. Lets it widen. The recursive thought loops pattern stumbles. For the first time, Dan sees the pattern from outside.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Peak. Dean's gut twists. The recursive thought loops mechanism runs so hot it melts its own tracks. Dean's teeth clench. The sleep anxiety becomes total. There is no outside. No perspective. No distance. Just the raw force of it. Dean is inside the center of the storm and the center is silent. In that silence, the old frame stops. Just stops. Like a clock that runs out. Dean waits for the old story to restart and it does not. The silence stretches. In the absence of the old frame, Dean notices what remains: a body, breathing. A floor, holding weight. A present moment with no narrative attached. The recursive thought loops pattern cannot operate without a story to run on. Dean stands in the gap.

Hank stands in the heat and does not flinch. The recursive thought loops pattern demands the old response. Heat crawls up Hank's neck. The sleep anxiety presses hard. Hank's fingertips tingle. But Hank has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Hank acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Hank makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Hank pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Hank is now. The recursive thought loops pattern fades to background noise.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
A shiver runs through Dean. The sleep anxiety activates and the recursive thought loops pattern responds on schedule. But this time Dean traces the mechanism back to its origin. Sees the protective logic. The recursive thought loops started as defense. Dean learned that thinking harder meant thinking better. The defense became automatic. The automatic became identity. Dean's nausea rises. The cost structure is visible now: every activation of the recursive thought loops pattern spends energy, narrows options, reinforces the loop. Dean sees the reinforcement happening. Watches the pattern strengthen itself through use. The mechanism is elegant in its dysfunction. It solves the wrong problem perfectly. Dean stands in the heat and sees every moving part.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Everything is visible now. Hank's throat seals. The recursive thought loops mechanism fires at peak capacity and Hank sees through it completely. Hank's vision narrows. The sleep anxiety at this intensity burns away every excuse, every rationalization, every reason the pattern seemed necessary. What remains is the bare mechanism: a loop that feeds itself. The recursive thought loops creates more thoughts to think about, which activates more recursive thought loops, which creates more thoughts to think about. Perpetual motion machine of suffering. Hank sees the engine running and understands it is not broken. It is working exactly as designed. The design is the problem. The crisis makes the design obvious. Hank cannot unsee what the peak has revealed.
