## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to dread bedtime. When nighttime is the only window where the unsolved problems come flooding in, dreading it is rational. You are not failing at sleep. You are carrying a daytime load that does not fit in daytime hours.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be angry that you cannot even rest without your responsibilities following you. Sleep was supposed to be the one safe place. It is not your fault it became another shift.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to take the 2 a.m. thoughts seriously without acting on them immediately. They are not emergencies. They are the unprocessed material of a life that gives you no margin to process during the day.
---
