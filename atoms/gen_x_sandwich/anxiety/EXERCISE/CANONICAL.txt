

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Find a chair. Sit down. Plant both feet on the ground. Press your feet firmly downward. Feel the ground beneath you. Hold this for thirty seconds. Breathe in slowly through your nose. Count four beats as you breathe in. Hold the breath for four beats. Exhale through your mouth for four counts. Do this four times. Your body is here. Your body is safe. Notice your shoulders. Drop them away from your ears. Let your jaw unclench. You're holding too tight. Release the tension. Your chest can soften. Your stomach can release. Do this breathing pattern five more times. You're slowing down. You're anchoring here. You're present in this body. You're not in the hospital. You're not in the waiting room. You're here. In this chair. Breathing. Alive. Safe enough.
