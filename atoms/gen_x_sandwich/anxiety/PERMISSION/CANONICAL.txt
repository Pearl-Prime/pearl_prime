## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be anxious without having a tidy reason for it. When you are holding up two generations, the reason is the situation itself. You do not owe anyone an explanation for the weight you carry.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel the anxiety and still be a good parent, a good child, a good employee. Anxiety does not disqualify you from any of those roles. It is evidence of how seriously you take them.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to admit the anxiety out loud without it meaning you cannot handle your life. Naming it is not weakness. It is the first honest thing you have said about your load in months.
---
