## PIVOT v01
---

---
You were taught that anxiety means something is wrong with you. But when you are holding up two generations at once, anxiety is your nervous system doing math on an impossible equation. The new frame: this is not a flaw. It is an accurate reading of an unsustainable load.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old story says worry means you care. The truth is you have been running threat-detection for everyone in the house -- your mother's medications, your teenager's silence, your own chest tightness last in line. What if the anxiety is not a feeling to manage but a signal that the ratio of giving to receiving has been broken for years?
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You have been treating the anxious hum like background noise -- something to push through between the school pickup and the pharmacy run. But background noise that never stops is not background. It is the main track. The pivot: stop asking how to quiet it and start asking what would need to change for it to have a reason to quiet down.
