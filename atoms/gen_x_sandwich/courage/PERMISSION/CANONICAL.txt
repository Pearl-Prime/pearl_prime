## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to call what you do courage even if it does not feel dramatic. Waking up into a life that demands more than one person can give and showing up anyway -- that is not ordinary. It is extraordinary in ordinary clothing.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be brave and terrified in the same breath. The shaking hands that still show up at the hospital, the care meeting, the school conference -- those hands are not weak. They are human, and they keep showing up.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to be proud of yourself for surviving this season even if it does not look like thriving. Survival in the sandwich is not a consolation prize. It is the prerequisite for everything else.
