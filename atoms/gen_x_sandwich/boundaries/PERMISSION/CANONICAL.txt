## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to say no to someone you love. Loving them and limiting what you give them are not opposites. They are the only combination that keeps you here long enough to keep loving them.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to disappoint someone today in order to still exist for them tomorrow. The guilt you feel is not proof you did the wrong thing. It is the aftershock of doing the right thing for the first time.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to draw a line even when nobody around you understands why you need one. You see the full picture -- the elder care, the kids, the career, the disappearing self. Their confusion does not erase your clarity.
---
