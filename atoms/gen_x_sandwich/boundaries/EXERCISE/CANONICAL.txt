

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit down. Close your eyes. Imagine your phone ringing. Your mother calling. Notice your immediate response. The tension. The obligation. Now imagine a boundary. A fence between you and the phone. A boundary that is made of love. Not rejection. Just a boundary. Feel this. After thirty seconds, open your eyes. You can set a boundary. A boundary is not cruelty. A boundary is self-respect. Practice this image tomorrow. Feel the fence. Feel the boundary. Feel the love within it. You are allowed to have boundaries.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand up. Feet shoulder-width apart. Extend your arms out to your sides. Feel the space around you. This is your space. Your personal space. Feel it. Now imagine a circle around you. Now extend your arms again. You can open. You can close. This is your choice. Feel this power. You have the right to boundaries.
