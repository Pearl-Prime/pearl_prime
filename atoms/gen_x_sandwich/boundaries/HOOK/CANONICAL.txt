

## HOOK v01
---

---
Your mother called at 9pm on a worknight with a small problem that would take three hours to solve. Your throat closed because you already knew your answer before she finished speaking: yes. You have become the person who absorbs what won't fit anywhere else. Your ribs know the shape of that accommodation now.

## HOOK v02
---

---
The boundary you tried to set lasted 72 hours. Your father's voice on the phone did something to your resolve that you don't have words for. You backed down and you'll back down again because the alternative is carrying guilt instead of carrying him. Your shoulders chose the heavier weight.

## HOOK v03
---

---

## HOOK v04
---

---
You said no to your boss for the first time in three years and the silence that followed was worse than any argument. Your gut tightened. You spent the next four hours mentally preparing your apology. You didn't mean it. You both knew you didn't mean it. Your spine felt the reversal before your mouth made it official.

---

---

---

---

## HOOK v08
---

---

---
