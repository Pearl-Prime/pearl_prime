

## RECOGNITION v01
---

---
Carol grieves the woman she could be with boundaries. She's recognizing that her boundarylessness is the structure of her current life. If she sets boundaries, she becomes a different woman. Her chest is tight. The recognition is that boundaries require loss.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

---
Carol's grief about losing her boundless identity is preventing her from setting boundaries. She grieves who she is while simultaneously hating who she is. The mechanism is that grief is keeping her stuck.
---

## TURNING_POINT v01
---

---
Carol decides that grieving her old identity is acceptable. She sets boundaries anyway. The turning point is allowing grief while also changing.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

---
Carol has grieved her boundless identity and set boundaries. She's a different woman now. Her chest is open. She's alive in a new way.
---
