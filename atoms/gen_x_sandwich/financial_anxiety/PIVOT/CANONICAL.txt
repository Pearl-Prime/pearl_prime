## PIVOT v01
---

---
The old frame says financial anxiety is about money. Your version is about time multiplied by obligation. College tuition on one side. Assisted living on the other. Your own retirement a number you stopped looking at. The new frame: this is not poor planning. This is one income generation absorbing the costs of three.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You were told to save, invest, plan ahead. Nobody mentioned the part where your parents would need care that insurance barely covers while your kids need things that scholarships barely reach. Financial anxiety in the sandwich is not about spreadsheets. It is about being the only bridge between two cliffs and feeling the bolts loosen.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Everyone has advice about money. Nobody has your particular math -- the math where helping your father means not helping your future self, and helping your kid means another year before you can breathe. The pivot: stop comparing your finances to people carrying one generation. You are carrying three.
