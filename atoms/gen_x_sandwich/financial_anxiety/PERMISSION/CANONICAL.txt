## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel terrified about money even if you make a decent living. When one salary is funding three generations, decent is not the same as enough. The fear is proportional to the math, not your character.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to admit the financial pressure out loud without it meaning you have failed as a provider. You are providing for more people across more needs than any single income was designed to cover.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to be angry that this landed on you -- the elder care costs nobody planned for, the tuition nobody subsidizes, the retirement you keep postponing. The anger is not bitterness. It is an accurate response to an unfair distribution.
---
