---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The confrontation arrives without warning. Wendy's knees weaken. The system overload mechanism fires and Wendy catches it mid-launch. Sees the trajectory. Sees where it lands every time: back in the same financial anxiety loop. Wendy's neck stiffens. This time, Wendy refuses the landing. The refusal is physical. Feet plant. Spine straightens. The old frame—the one that says this is how things work, this is who Wendy is—wobbles. Wendy watches it wobble. Does not stabilize it. The frame tilts and light enters from an angle Wendy has never seen before. The financial anxiety looks different from this angle. Smaller. More mechanical. Less like identity and more like a pattern that can be interrupted.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Mike breaks. The system overload pattern reaches maximum intensity and something gives. Mike's shoulders brace. Every system fires at once. The financial anxiety is no longer background noise. It is the entire room. Mike's throat closes. And then—in the center of the breaking—Mike sees the architecture. The whole structure. How the system overload feeds the financial anxiety feeds the system overload feeds the collapse. Mike sees the loop from inside the loop's core. The frame doesn't shift gently. It shatters. What replaces it is not comfort. It is clarity. Mike stands in the wreckage of the old story and feels the ground beneath it. Solid. Different. The old frame is debris now. Mike does not rebuild it.

---
Barb stands in the heat and does not flinch. The system overload pattern demands the old response. Barb's legs brace. The financial anxiety presses hard. Barb's gut clenches. But Barb has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Barb acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Barb makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Barb pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Barb is now. The system overload pattern fades to background noise.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Mike sees the machinery in real time. The system overload pattern fires and Mike catches the sequence: trigger, body response, narrative, behavioral loop. Mike's knuckles whiten. The financial anxiety is hot. Active. But Mike traces the wiring. The system overload protects by adding tasks to manage the overload. It has always protected by adding tasks to manage the overload. The protection costs more than the threat. Mike's words jam. Mike watches the cost accumulate in the body. Watches the pattern spend resources defending against something that is already happening. The mechanism is visible now. Not as theory. As lived architecture. Mike feels every gear turn. The understanding doesn't reduce the heat. But it names the fire.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Everything is visible now. Barb's eyes sting. The system overload mechanism fires at peak capacity and Barb sees through it completely. Barb's palms sweat. The financial anxiety at this intensity burns away every excuse, every rationalization, every reason the pattern seemed necessary. What remains is the bare mechanism: a loop that feeds itself. The system overload creates more inputs to manage, which activates more system overload, which creates more inputs to manage. Perpetual motion machine of suffering. Barb sees the engine running and understands it is not broken. It is working exactly as designed. The design is the problem. The crisis makes the design obvious. Barb cannot unsee what the peak has revealed.
