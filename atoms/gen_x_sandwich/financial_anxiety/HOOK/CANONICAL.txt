

## HOOK v01
---

---
Linda is at the dining table with the parent-care estimate and the tuition portal both open, fingers shaking on the trackpad — the numbers land in the same minute and her stomach drops before she finishes reading.
---

## HOOK v02
---

---
You are on hold with your mother's insurance and your kid's college portal is open in the next tab, and the two numbers want the same dollar and neither of them will wait.
---

## HOOK v03
---

---
The estimate for the care facility arrives the same week as the tuition deposit, and you stand in the hallway between two rooms that both need you and do the arithmetic that has no answer.
---

## HOOK v04
---

---
You used to think you would have margin by now, and instead you are the margin — the give in the system, the part that absorbs what the spreadsheet cannot.
---

## HOOK v05
---

---
At 2 AM you are awake again, not solving anything, just holding the weight of two generations and the quiet fear that there is not enough of you to go around.
---
