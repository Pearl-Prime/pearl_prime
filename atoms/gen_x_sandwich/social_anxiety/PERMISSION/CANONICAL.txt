## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to cancel plans without guilt. When your social energy has been consumed by caregiving before you ever reach the restaurant, canceling is not rudeness. It is self-preservation.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel lonely in a crowded room. When nobody there knows about the hospital visits or the school calls or the weight of your actual life, connection feels performative because it is. You deserve at least one space where it is not.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to need people and also not have the bandwidth for people. Those two truths live side by side in the sandwich. Neither one cancels the other, and neither one makes you broken.
---
