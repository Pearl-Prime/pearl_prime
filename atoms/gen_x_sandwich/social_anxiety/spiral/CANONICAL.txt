

## RECOGNITION v01
---

---
Carol is socially anxious. She avoids friends because she's too busy. She's isolated. Her chest is tight. The recognition is that she has no social connections anymore.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

---
Carol's social anxiety is creating overwhelm. Being social feels like too much. The mechanism is that she's too drained to maintain friendships. Her chest stays tight.
---

## TURNING_POINT v01
---

---
Carol texts a friend. She says: I'm struggling. The friend responds. Her chest opens slightly.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

---
Carol is connecting socially again. She's letting people know she's struggling. Her chest is lighter. She's learning that isolation is a choice she can change.
---
