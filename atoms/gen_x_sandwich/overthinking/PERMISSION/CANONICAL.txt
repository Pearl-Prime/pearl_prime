## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to have a brain that will not stop. When you are responsible for people on both ends of the age spectrum, the vigilance is not irrational. It is proportional. You are not overthinking. You are thinking about an actual situation that has too many moving parts.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to not have the answer at 2 a.m. The problems circling your mind were not designed to be solved alone in the dark. They were designed for daylight, for support, for shared weight. Your brain just does not know that yet.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to admit that the mental load is too much without it meaning you are too little. The load was designed for a team. You have been carrying it solo. The exhaustion is not weakness. It is arithmetic.
---
