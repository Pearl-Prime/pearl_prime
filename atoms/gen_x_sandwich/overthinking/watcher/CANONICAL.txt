The confrontation arrives without warning. Gail's fists clench. The hypervigilance and scanning mechanism fires and Gail catches it mid-launch. Sees the trajectory. Sees where it lands every time: back in the same overthinking loop. Heat crawls up Gail's neck. This time, Gail refuses the landing. The refusal is physical. Feet plant. Spine straightens. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern has burned through its own container.

Tom's chest tightens. The overthinking activates and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern responds on schedule. But this time Tom traces the mechanism back to its origin. Sees the protective logic. The hypervigilance and scanning started as defense. Tom learned that vigilance prevented surprise. The defense became automatic. The automatic became identity. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

Peak intensity strips everything to structure. Dan's core seizes. The overthinking hits maximum. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern runs at capacity. Heat crawls up Dan's neck. In the crisis, the mechanism becomes naked. Dan sees it without narrative, without justification, without the story that makes it make sense. Raw machinery. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern takes the pain of overthinking and converts it into constant tension. The conversion is lossy. More energy goes in than comes out. The deficit accumulates in the body. Dan feels years of deficit in this single moment. The mechanism has been running at a loss for so long the debt is staggering. Dan sees the balance sheet. The numbers do not lie. The pattern costs more than it saves.
