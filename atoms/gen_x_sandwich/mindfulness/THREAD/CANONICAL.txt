## THREAD v01

---
Marcus keeps his one-breath pause before every conversation now, out of habit more than intention. Tomorrow a hard update from his mother's doctor will land in the middle of his daughter's exam week, and the habit will get tested harder than it ever has on an easy Tuesday. He doesn't know yet that it holds.

## THREAD v02
---
---
Denise's "okay, here" has become so automatic that her husband asked if she was talking to herself. She laughed it off. What she didn't say was that the mutter is the only thing keeping her present through a stretch of overlapping appointments that isn't easing up any time soon.

## THREAD v03

## THREAD v04
