## TAKEAWAY v01
---
---
Presence isn't a state you hold. It's a return you make, over and over, faster each time.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---
---
You don't stop the drifting between generations. You just get better at noticing it, and quicker at coming back.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---
---
Half-presence doesn't look like a mistake. It looks like a normal day where nobody, on either side, was fully seen.
---

## TAKEAWAY v04
---
---
The goal was never calm. The goal is accurate — attention on what's real, right now.
---

## TAKEAWAY v05
---
---
Every conversation is a chance to close the last one and open the next one clean.
---

## TAKEAWAY v06
---
---
A fast return costs almost nothing. An unnoticed drift costs the whole conversation.
---

## TAKEAWAY v07
---
---
You're not failing every time you leave the room. You're only failing if you never notice.
---

## TAKEAWAY v08
---
---
Busy and scattered aren't the same thing. One conversation fully beats two at half-attention.
---

## TAKEAWAY v09
---
---
The return gets cheaper with practice. It was never supposed to feel easy on day one.
---

## TAKEAWAY v10
---
---
A parked worry quiets down. A suppressed one just runs louder underneath everything else.
---

## TAKEAWAY v11
---
---
You don't need a lighter caregiving season to practice this. You need a cue you use at every single threshold.
---

## TAKEAWAY v12
---
---
Count the returns, not the drifts. The reps are the whole practice.
---
