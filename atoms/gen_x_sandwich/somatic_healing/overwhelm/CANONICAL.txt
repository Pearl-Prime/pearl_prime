COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
The confrontation arrives without warning. Diane's core seizes. The system overload mechanism fires and Diane catches it mid-launch. Sees the trajectory. Sees where it lands every time: back in the same somatic healing loop. Diane's sternum burns. This time, Diane refuses the landing. The refusal is physical. Feet plant. Spine straightens. The old frame—the one that says this is how things work, this is who Diane is—wobbles. Diane watches it wobble. Does not stabilize it. The frame tilts and light enters from an angle Diane has never seen before. The somatic healing looks different from this angle. Smaller. More mechanical. Less like identity and more like a pattern that can be interrupted.

---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Peak. Heat crawls up Keith's neck. The system overload mechanism runs so hot it melts its own tracks. Keith's eyes sting. The somatic healing becomes total. There is no outside. No perspective. No distance. Just the raw force of it. Keith is inside the center of the storm and the center is silent. In that silence, the old frame stops. Just stops. Like a clock that runs out. Keith waits for the old story to restart and it does not. The silence stretches. In the absence of the old frame, Keith notices what remains: a body, breathing. A floor, holding weight. A present moment with no narrative attached. The system overload pattern cannot operate without a story to run on. Keith stands in the gap.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The system overload pattern runs hot and Keith watches it run. Keith's cheeks burn. The mechanism is clear: the somatic healing activates the system overload, the system overload generates a protective response, the protective response costs more than doing nothing. Keith's throat closes. Keith sees why the pattern persists. It persists because it works. Not well. Not efficiently. But it works. It reduces the immediate threat by creating a long-term drain. Keith understands this trade-off for the first time while the pattern is active. The heat doesn't diminish. The understanding doesn't soothe. But Keith sees the logic. The pattern is not irrational. It is a survival strategy that has outlived its context.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Carl's words jam. The somatic healing and the system overload pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. Heat crawls up Carl's neck. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Carl sees the entire architecture: how the system overload creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Carl sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Carl that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
