## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Rita hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: core seizes. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern has been running for so long Rita stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Rita's calves lock. The old narrative about somatic healing says this is weakness. But Rita looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. Sees how the frame keeps Rita locked in a loop that feels like safety but functions as a cage. The frame shifts. Not dramatically. A hairline fracture in the foundation. Rita notices the fracture. Does not repair it. Lets it widen. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern stumbles. For the first time, Rita sees the pattern from outside.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Susan breaks. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern reaches maximum intensity and something gives. Susan's knees weaken. Every system fires at once. The somatic healing is no longer background noise. It is the entire room. Susan's jaw drops. And then—in the center of the breaking—Susan sees the architecture. The whole structure. How the hypervigilance and scanning feeds the somatic healing feeds the hypervigilance and scanning feeds the collapse. Susan sees the loop from inside the loop's core. The frame doesn't shift gently. It shatters. What replaces it is not comfort. It is clarity. Susan stands in the wreckage of the old story and feels the ground beneath it. Solid. Different. The old frame is debris now. Susan does not rebuild it.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Dan stands in the heat and does not flinch. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern demands the old response. Dan's gut twists. The somatic healing presses hard. Dan's shoulders curl forward. But Dan has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Dan acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Dan makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Dan pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Dan is now. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern fades to background noise.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Susan sees the machinery in real time. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern fires and Susan catches the sequence: trigger, body response, narrative, behavioral loop. Susan's belly knots. The somatic healing is hot. Active. But Susan traces the wiring. The hypervigilance and scanning protects by scanning for threats that create the tension they scan for. It has always protected by scanning for threats that create the tension they scan for. The protection costs more than the threat. Susan's temples pulse. Susan watches the cost accumulate in the body. Watches the pattern spend resources defending against something that is already happening. The mechanism is visible now. Not as theory. As lived architecture. Susan feels every gear turn. The understanding doesn't reduce the heat. But it names the fire.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Dan's sternum burns. The somatic healing and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. Dan's shoulders brace. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Dan sees the entire architecture: how the hypervigilance and scanning creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Dan sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Dan that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
