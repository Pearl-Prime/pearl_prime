## PIVOT v01
---

---
Your back went out on a Tuesday. Your jaw has been clenched since March. The headache lives behind your right eye like a tenant. The old frame says these are separate physical complaints. The new frame: your body has been keeping the score of every generation you have held up, and it is running out of places to store the tally.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You have been to the doctor. The tests came back normal. But the tension in your shoulders, the knot in your stomach, the fatigue that sleep does not fix -- those are not nothing. The pivot: your body is not malfunctioning. It is accurately reflecting the weight you have been carrying without anyone offering to share the load.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The old frame says emotions stay in your head. The new frame: every unspoken frustration with your parent, every swallowed reaction to your teenager, every time you said I am fine when you were not -- all of it had to go somewhere. Your body volunteered. Somatic healing is not about fixing the body. It is about listening to what it has been holding.
---
