## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to have a body that hurts even when the doctors say nothing is wrong. The scans cannot see the weight of two generations sitting in your shoulders. The pain is real. It just does not have a billing code.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to listen to your body even when there is no time for it. The tension, the headaches, the fatigue -- they are not inconveniences to push through. They are messages from the part of you that never gets a vote.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to need healing that is not productive. Not yoga that makes you a better caregiver. Not meditation that makes you more patient. Healing that exists purely because you deserve to feel okay in your own skin again.
