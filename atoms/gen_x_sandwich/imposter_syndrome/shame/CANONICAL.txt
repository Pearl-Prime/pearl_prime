## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Ed hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: teeth clench. The exposure and hiding pattern has been running for so long Ed stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Ed's neck stiffens. The old narrative about imposter syndrome says this is weakness. But Ed looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. Sees how the frame keeps Ed locked in a loop that feels like safety but functions as a cage. The frame shifts. Not dramatically. A hairline fracture in the foundation. Ed notices the fracture. Does not repair it. Lets it widen. The exposure and hiding pattern stumbles. For the first time, Ed sees the pattern from outside.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Everything collapses at once. Dean's cheeks burn. The imposter syndrome detonates and the exposure and hiding pattern amplifies it past capacity. Dean's fists clench. The body cannot sustain this. The mind cannot sustain this. Something has to break. What breaks is the story. The story that tells Dean to hold this. The story that makes holding the only job. The story shatters and beneath it Dean finds something unexpected: the ground. Not the ground of the old narrative. Raw ground. Unnarrated. Dean stands on it and feels it hold weight. The crisis does not resolve. But the frame through which Dean sees the crisis transforms completely. There is no going back to the old interpretation. The exposure and hiding pattern has burned through its own container.

Dean sees the machinery in real time. The exposure and hiding pattern fires and Dean catches the sequence: trigger, body response, narrative, behavioral loop. Dean's hair stands. The imposter syndrome is hot. Active. But Dean traces the wiring. The exposure and hiding protects by hiding what needs exposure. It has always protected by hiding what needs exposure. The protection costs more than the threat. Dean's legs brace. Dean watches the cost accumulate in the body. Watches the pattern spend resources defending against something that is already happening. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

Everything is visible now. Ellen's eyes sting. The exposure and hiding mechanism fires at peak capacity and Ellen sees through it completely. Ellen's skin goes cold. The imposter syndrome at this intensity burns away every excuse, every rationalization, every reason the pattern seemed necessary. What remains is the bare mechanism: a loop that feeds itself. The exposure and hiding creates more material to hide, which activates more exposure and hiding, which creates more material to hide. Perpetual motion machine of suffering. Ellen cannot unsee what the peak has revealed.
