## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel out of your depth at work while managing a household that would overwhelm a small team. The imposter feeling is not evidence of your inadequacy. It is evidence of your impossible workload.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to not have it all together. The myth of the person who excels at career, parenting, and elder care simultaneously was written by someone who has never done all three. You are doing something no brochure prepared you for.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to take up space at the table even when half your brain is on a parent's appointment or a kid's crisis. Your divided attention does not make your contributions less valuable. It makes them harder won.
