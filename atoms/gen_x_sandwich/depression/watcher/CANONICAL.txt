## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/watcher/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
The confrontation arrives without warning. Mark's gut clenches. The hypervigilance and scanning mechanism fires and Mark catches it mid-launch. Sees the trajectory. Sees where it lands every time: back in the same depression loop. Mark's shoulders lock. This time, Mark refuses the landing. The refusal is physical. Feet plant. Spine straightens. The old frame—the one that says this is how things work, this is who Mark is—wobbles. Mark watches it wobble. Does not stabilize it. The frame tilts and light enters from an angle Mark has never seen before. The depression looks different from this angle. Smaller. More mechanical. Less like identity and more like a pattern that can be interrupted.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Everything collapses at once. Neil's ribcage constricts. The depression detonates and the hypervigilance and scanning pattern amplifies it past capacity. Neil's legs go heavy. The body cannot sustain this. The mind cannot sustain this. Something has to break. What breaks is the story. The story that tells Neil to hold this. The story that makes holding the only job. The story shatters and beneath it Neil finds something unexpected: the ground. Not the ground of the old narrative. Raw ground. Unnarrated. Neil stands on it and feels it hold weight. The crisis does not resolve. But the frame through which Neil sees the crisis transforms completely. There is no going back to the old interpretation. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern has burned through its own container.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Neil sees the machinery in real time. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern fires and Neil catches the sequence: trigger, body response, narrative, behavioral loop. Neil's skin prickles. The depression is hot. Active. But Neil traces the wiring. The hypervigilance and scanning protects by scanning for threats that create the tension they scan for. It has always protected by scanning for threats that create the tension they scan for. The protection costs more than the threat. Neil's legs brace. Neil watches the cost accumulate in the body. Watches the pattern spend resources defending against something that is already happening. The mechanism is visible now. Not as theory. As lived architecture. Neil feels every gear turn. The understanding doesn't reduce the heat. But it names the fire.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Peak intensity strips everything to structure. Roy's forehead tightens. The depression hits maximum. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern runs at capacity. Roy's hands go cold. In the crisis, the mechanism becomes naked. Roy sees it without narrative, without justification, without the story that makes it make sense. Raw machinery. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern takes the pain of depression and converts it into constant tension. The conversion is lossy. More energy goes in than comes out. The deficit accumulates in the body. Roy feels years of deficit in this single moment. The mechanism has been running at a loss for so long the debt is staggering. Roy sees the balance sheet. The numbers do not lie. The pattern costs more than it saves.
