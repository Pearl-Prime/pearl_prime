## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be depressed and still love your family. Depression does not mean you have stopped caring. It means the caring has been costing more than you have, and the deficit finally showed up.

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel nothing after a day of doing everything. That flatness is not ingratitude. It is the sound of a system that has been on high output with no input for too long.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to not be okay right now. Not next week okay, not after-the-holidays okay. Right now. The people around you can handle your honesty better than they can handle your quiet disappearance.
