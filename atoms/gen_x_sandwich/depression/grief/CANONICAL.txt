## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The confrontation arrives without warning. Brian's skin prickles. The loss and absence mechanism fires and Brian catches it mid-launch. Sees the trajectory. Sees where it lands every time: back in the same depression loop. Brian's sternum burns. This time, Brian refuses the landing. The refusal is physical. Feet plant. Spine straightens. The old frame—the one that says this is how things work, this is who Brian is—wobbles. Brian watches it wobble. Does not stabilize it. The frame tilts and light enters from an angle Brian has never seen before. The depression looks different from this angle. Smaller. More mechanical. Less like identity and more like a pattern that can be interrupted.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Karen breaks. The loss and absence pattern reaches maximum intensity and something gives. Karen's words jam. Every system fires at once. The depression is no longer background noise. It is the entire room. Karen's abdomen tightens. And then—in the center of the breaking—Karen sees the architecture. The whole structure. How the loss and absence feeds the depression feeds the loss and absence feeds the collapse. Karen sees the loop from inside the loop's core. The frame doesn't shift gently. It shatters. What replaces it is not comfort. It is clarity. Karen stands in the wreckage of the old story and feels the ground beneath it. Solid. Different. The old frame is debris now. Karen does not rebuild it.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Susan stands in the heat and does not flinch. The loss and absence pattern demands the old response. Susan's face flushes. The depression presses hard. Susan's chest locks. But Susan has changed the relationship with this pressure. Not by eliminating it. By owning a different position inside it. Susan acts from identity, not from emergency. The difference is physical. The old response collapsed the body inward. The new response keeps the spine vertical. Susan makes a decision that the old self would not have made. The decision costs something. Susan pays it. The payment is not sacrifice. It is investment in who Susan is now. The loss and absence pattern fades to background noise.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Karen sees the machinery in real time. The loss and absence pattern fires and Karen catches the sequence: trigger, body response, narrative, behavioral loop. Karen's calves lock. The depression is hot. Active. But Karen traces the wiring. The loss and absence protects by converting absence into action items. It has always protected by converting absence into action items. The protection costs more than the threat. Karen's throat seals. Karen watches the cost accumulate in the body. Watches the pattern spend resources defending against something that is already happening. The mechanism is visible now. Not as theory. As lived architecture. Karen feels every gear turn. The understanding doesn't reduce the heat. But it names the fire.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Susan's face flushes. The depression and the loss and absence pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. Susan's palms sweat. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Susan sees the entire architecture: how the loss and absence creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Susan sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Susan that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
