## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/gen_x_sandwich/anchored/depression/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The system overload pattern runs hot and Nancy watches it run. Nancy's calves lock. The mechanism is clear: the depression activates the system overload, the system overload generates a protective response, the protective response costs more than doing nothing. Nancy's cheeks burn. Nancy sees why the pattern persists. It persists because it works. Not well. Not efficiently. But it works. It reduces the immediate threat by creating a long-term drain. Nancy understands this trade-off for the first time while the pattern is active. The heat doesn't diminish. The understanding doesn't soothe. But Nancy sees the logic. The pattern is not irrational. It is a survival strategy that has outlived its context.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

---
Everything is visible now. Rita's fists clench. The system overload mechanism fires at peak capacity and Rita sees through it completely. Rita's jaw drops. The depression at this intensity burns away every excuse, every rationalization, every reason the pattern seemed necessary. What remains is the bare mechanism: a loop that feeds itself. The system overload creates more inputs to manage, which activates more system overload, which creates more inputs to manage. Perpetual motion machine of suffering. Rita sees the engine running and understands it is not broken. It is working exactly as designed. The design is the problem. The crisis makes the design obvious. Rita cannot unsee what the peak has revealed.
