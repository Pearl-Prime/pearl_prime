## PIVOT v01
---

---
You call it being tired. Or overwhelmed. Or just not yourself lately. But the flatness that greets you before the alarm goes off has a real name. Depression in the sandwich generation hides behind competence. You keep the machine running and nobody notices the operator went dark.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: depression means you have given up. The new frame: you have not given up a single thing. You are still driving to physical therapy, still signing permission slips, still making dinner. Depression for you looks like doing everything while feeling nothing. That is not laziness wearing a mask. It is a system screaming for repair while the owner keeps working.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You think you should be grateful -- you have both parents alive, healthy kids, a roof. And you are grateful. And you are depressed. Those two things can occupy the same body at the same time. The pivot is not choosing between them. It is admitting that holding two generations up does not protect you from sinking.
