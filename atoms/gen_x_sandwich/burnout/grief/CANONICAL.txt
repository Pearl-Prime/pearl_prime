Lisa hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: trapezius hardens. The loss and absence pattern has been running for so long Lisa stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. Lisa's temples pulse. The old narrative about burnout says this is weakness. But Lisa looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The loss and absence pattern runs hot and Nancy watches it run. Nancy's gut twists. The mechanism is clear: the burnout activates the loss and absence, the loss and absence generates a protective response, the protective response costs more than doing nothing. Nancy's ribcage constricts. Nancy sees why the pattern persists. It persists because it works. Not well. Not efficiently. But it works. It reduces the immediate threat by creating a long-term drain. Nancy understands this trade-off for the first time while the pattern is active. The heat doesn't diminish. The understanding doesn't soothe. But Nancy sees the logic. The pattern is not irrational. It is a survival strategy that has outlived its context.

The mechanism reveals itself at maximum intensity. Roy's hair stands. The burnout and the loss and absence pattern fuse into a single overwhelming force. Roy's swallow catches. And in the overwhelm, the structure becomes transparent. Roy sees the entire architecture: how the loss and absence creates the conditions it claims to prevent. How the protective mechanism generates the exact threat it guards against. The loop is perfect. Self-sustaining. Self-generating. Roy sees it running at full speed and understands why it never stops. It never stops because stopping feels like the danger. The mechanism's deepest trick: convincing Roy that the mechanism itself is safety. The body shakes with the recognition. The pattern is the problem wearing the mask of the solution.
