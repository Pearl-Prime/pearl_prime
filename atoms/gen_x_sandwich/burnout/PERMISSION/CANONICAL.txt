## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be burned out even though other people have it worse. Comparison is not a disqualifier. Your nervous system does not grade on a curve. The exhaustion is real because the load is real.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to need help even though you have always been the one who helps. Admitting the tank is empty is not a failure of character. It is the most honest inventory you have taken in years.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to stop performing resilience. The people who depend on you do not need you to be invincible. They need you to still be here. Those are different projects.
