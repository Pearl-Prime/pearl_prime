## PIVOT v01
---

---
You think you are tired. Tired has a fix -- sleep, a weekend, a vacation. What you have is the compound interest on years of being the only adult in the room for two generations. Burnout is not a battery problem. It is a load-distribution problem, and you are the only node on the network.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: push through, you have handled worse. The crack in that frame: you have handled worse, and that is exactly why your system has stopped believing rest is coming. Burnout in the sandwich generation is not dramatic collapse. It is the quiet moment when you realize you cannot remember what you actually want.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Everyone sees someone who has it together. Doctor appointments scheduled, homework supervised, meals on the table. What nobody sees is that the competence is running on fumes. The new frame: burnout is not what happens when you stop caring. It is what happens when caring is the only gear you have.
