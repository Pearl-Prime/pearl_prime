Wendy hits the wall. Not metaphorically. The body announces it: shoulders climb. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern has been running for so long Wendy stopped noticing the cost. Now the cost is visible. A shiver runs through Wendy. The old narrative about burnout says this is weakness. But Wendy looks at the narrative and sees the machinery. Sees how the story protects by constraining. Sees how the frame keeps Wendy locked in a loop that feels like safety but functions as a cage. The frame shifts. Not dramatically. For the first time, Wendy sees the pattern from outside.

Sees the protective logic. The hypervigilance and scanning started as defense. Ellen learned that vigilance prevented surprise. The defense became automatic. The automatic became identity. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Peak intensity strips everything to structure. Jim's calves lock. The burnout hits maximum. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern runs at capacity. Jim's hands shake. In the crisis, the mechanism becomes naked. Jim sees it without narrative, without justification, without the story that makes it make sense. Raw machinery. The hypervigilance and scanning pattern takes the pain of burnout and converts it into constant tension. The conversion is lossy. More energy goes in than comes out. The deficit accumulates in the body. Jim feels years of deficit in this single moment. The mechanism has been running at a loss for so long the debt is staggering. Jim sees the balance sheet. The numbers do not lie. The pattern costs more than it saves.
