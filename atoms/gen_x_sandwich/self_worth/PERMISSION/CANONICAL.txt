## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to matter to yourself. Not after the kids are settled. Not after your parents are taken care of. Now. Your needs are not a line item that gets funded after everyone else's budget is met. They are the foundation the whole structure depends on.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to want something that has nothing to do with being useful to someone else. A desire that exists purely because you are a person, not because you are a parent or a caregiver or an employee. That wanting is not selfish. It is alive.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel invisible and still demand to be seen. The sandwich generation runs on your labor, and the least it owes you is acknowledgment -- from the people around you and especially from yourself.
