

## RECOGNITION v01
---

---

---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

---
Carol's collapsed self-worth is creating false alarms. Every mistake seems to confirm her worthlessness. The mechanism is that she can only see failure. Her chest stays compressed.
---

## TURNING_POINT v01
---

---
Carol looks at actual evidence of her worth. She's managing multiple households. She's doing hard things. Her chest opens slightly.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

---
Carol's self-worth is rebuilding. She's recognizing her actual capacity. Her chest is less compressed. She's learning that worth isn't determined by perfect performance.
