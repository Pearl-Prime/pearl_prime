## EXERCISE Sample: Boundaries × Phone Turn Off Ritual

```
Pick up your phone. Look at it. This device. Has stolen your evenings. 
Now. Power it off. Completely off. Not silent. Off. Feel the button press. 
Hold it. Count to three. Phone is dark. Keep it dark. For the next 
four hours. The world will continue. You will be okay. The phone is off. 
This is your boundary.
```

**Specs met:** Second-person imperative. ≤10 words per instruction. Explicit action sequence. Somatic grounding. Persona relevant.

## INTEGRATION Sample: Boundaries × Still-Here Mode

```
Five p.m. came. I closed my laptop. I left work. Not perfectly. 
Not without worry. But I left. And now it's seven p.m. and I'm still fine. 
The panic about all unfinished things hasn't destroyed me. I'm here. I'm breathing. 
I'm separate from work. The boundary held. Not because it was easy. 
Because I held it. Even while uncomfortable.

Carry: I can be uncomfortable and boundaried.
```

**Specs met:** First-person author voice. 120-200 words. Concrete body state. 1 reframe. 1 carry line ≤8 words. No reassurance.

## TOPIC-SPECIFIC DETAIL

### Anxiety: Actual Triggers
- Slack notification sounds
- Standing desk discomfort
- Code compile errors
- Performance review documents
- Calendar invites
- Oncall alerts
- Notification badges
- Meeting preparation

### Boundaries: Real Violations
- Phone at 11pm
- Weekend Slack messages
- Timezone-spanning meeting scheduling
- Meeting requests without agendas
- Lunch blocks getting overridden
- Response time expectations
- Out of office disrespect
- Calendar takeover

### Compassion Fatigue: Actual Depletion
- Junior developers seeking constant help
- Three direct reports in crisis simultaneously
- Being team's emotional anchor
- Mentee dependency
- Listening to repeated problems
- Mentoring at cost to sleep
- No reciprocal support
- Resentment building

### Courage: Honest Risks
- Speaking against momentum
- Naming system problems
- Escalating upward
- Saying no to projects
- Code review criticism
- Admitting mistakes publicly
- Taking unpopular positions
- Being seen as difficult

### Depression: Embodied Reality
- Heaviness in bones
- 4pm energy crash
- Flatness of voice
- Inability to taste food
- Sleep disruption
- Numbness to achievements
- Isolation preference
- Gray internal weather

### Financial Stress: Math That Doesn't Work
- $300K salary + $5K rent = perpetual scarcity
- RSU vesting cliff taxes halving value
- Options underwater and expiring
- Student loans at 15 years
- Credit card minimum payments feeding interest
- 401K that won't fund retirement
- Emergency fund insufficient for one emergency
- Lifestyle inflation trap

### Grief: Actual Losses
- Culture change after acquisition
- Team scatter from reorg
- Product shutdown
- Mentor departure
- Leadership replacement
- Technical debt rewrite of own code
- Launch cancellation
- Company closure

### Self-Worth: Imposter Syndrome Persistence
- $300K salary + imposter syndrome coexisting
- FAANG prestige + fake feeling
- Promotion without feeling promotion-worthy
- Code review corrections as evidence of inadequacy
- Others knowing more at same level
- Mistake as proof of fraud
- Success attributed to luck
- Comparison to people's highlights

- Insight without lecture
- Present tense

- Wisdom without wisdom-ness
- Pithy but true

## EVIDENCE OF SPECIFICITY

**Not abstract:**
- Not "manage stress" → Specific: Close Slack. Turn off phone. Ground feet in floor.
- Not "take care of yourself" → Specific: Your lunch block is being overridden. Set it non-negotiable.
- Not "you're enough" → Specific: You completed something. The completion happened. You did it.

**Not toxic positivity:**
- Not "it will get better" → "Spirals are weather, not identity" (present-moment truth)
- Not "you're not alone" → "Millions have felt this before. The club is real." (system truth)
- Not "you can do this" → "You've survived every day this bad so far. That's math." (evidence-based)

**Not cognitive only:**
- Every exercise includes physical action
- Every integration includes body state
- Every scene includes sensory detail
- Every compression includes observable fact
