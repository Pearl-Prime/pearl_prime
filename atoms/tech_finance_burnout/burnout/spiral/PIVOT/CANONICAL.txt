## PIVOT v02

---
The burnout spiral has a familiar shape: exhaustion to incompetence to exposure to ruin. Every step feels inevitable. None of them are — but your depleted brain can't see the exits.

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral about the burnout itself. I'm too tired to perform, which means I'll slip, which means they'll notice. The spiral is burnout metabolizing itself.
