Leo pauses while staring at the same ticket for the third consecutive day. His stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. The watcher has run for hours without interruption. Now he sees the machinery laid bare. The watcher creates a permanent split between performer and judge, and the judge never once approves — the observation itself is what contaminates the performance. Leo traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. His stomach clenches as he maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Leo watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
