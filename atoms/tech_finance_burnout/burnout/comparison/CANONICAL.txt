## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/burnout/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ryan's comparison with his peer is deepening his burnout. The mechanism is: burnout produces exhaustion, exhaustion makes him function less well, functioning less well triggers comparison with peers, comparison produces shame, shame prevents asking for help, preventing help deepens burnout. The comparison is intensifying the cycle. He is not just burned out. He is burned out and convinced he is weaker than his peer.
