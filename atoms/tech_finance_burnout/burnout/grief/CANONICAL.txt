## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
At the ide at 11 pm on a friday with no one else online. Yuki touches the object that belonged to what was lost. Her fingers dig into her own palms. Crescents form in the skin. For months the frame has held: letting go means forgetting what mattered. Now Yuki watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Setting the weight down is not abandonment — it is an act of trust that the memory holds on its own. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Yuki stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/burnout/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Leo stands in the fluorescent hum of the empty office at midnight. Nausea rolls through like a rogue wave. Leo grips the counter to stay vertical. He the grief arrives total and undivided, demanding everything at once. The frame that has held everything in place — putting down the weight means it never mattered in the first place — strains under the weight of what Leo sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Leo feels the fracture travel through his chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. Carrying the weight is not the same as honoring the memory — the memory survives without the ache. Leo does not reach for a replacement. He stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

---
Pressure mounts behind her eyes like a wave building against a wall. Priya stands at the IDE at 11 PM on a Friday with no one else online. The grief screams at peak volume, demanding the old compliance. Priya releases the grip on the grief and finds the love still present underneath. Not despite the sensation — she feels every degree of it. Her body carries the heat. Her choices carry her name. She does not wait for the feeling to pass before acting. She acts while the feeling burns at full intensity. The acting is the embodiment. The body and the choice exist in the same moment. Priya owns this. The ownership lives in her hands, in her breath, in the ground beneath her feet.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/burnout/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Dev pauses at the IDE at 11 PM on a Friday with no one else online. His stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. The grief has run for hours without interruption. Now he sees the machinery laid bare. Carrying the pain has become the last remaining thread to what was, and setting it down feels like severing that thread. Dev traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. His stomach clenches as he maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Dev watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
