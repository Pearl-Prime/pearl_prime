## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ryan's shame about his capacity has become pervasive. He is ashamed that he is tired. Ashamed that he cannot maintain focus. Ashamed that he needs breaks. The mechanism is: burnout degrades capacity, degraded capacity produces shame, shame prevents asking for help, shame prevents rest, preventing rest worsens burnout. He is locked. The shame is both the consequence of burnout and the thing preventing recovery.
