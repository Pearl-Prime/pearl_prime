## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Your stock portfolio notification arrived with bad news.
The catastrophic future is playing in your head.
Pause. You cannot know what will happen.
Name three specific things you are certain about.
You have a job. You have food. You.
You have handled financial difficulty before in life.
You have always found a way through it.
This moment's panic is not tomorrow's reality.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Startup equity. The dream. Also the terror.
Your worth is not your net worth.
What would be actually true in worst case?
Say it out loud. Sit with it.
You would survive. You have survived before.
This catastrophizing is your anxiety, not your future.
Open your banking app. Look at reality.
What is actually true right now today?
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Layoff fears are running through your system now.
Your brain is modeling impossible worst cases.
Stop. Return to facts this actual moment.
You have money in the bank. How much?
What are your actual monthly expenses right now?
How many months could you cover expenses?
The gap between fact and fear is large.
Sit with the facts. Not the predictions.
