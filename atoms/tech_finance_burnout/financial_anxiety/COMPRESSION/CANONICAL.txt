

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Stop trying to be certain about money and start being honest about fear. That honesty is where something real can shift. The number never feels safe because safety was never what the number was actually tracking. The stress response doesn't care about your actual bank balance. It responds to the story about the bank balance.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
You make more than your parents made in a lifetime. You also feel one bad quarter from disaster. The number on the offer letter is not the number running the anxiety. The anxiety is running on the gap between your lifestyle and your runway. The lifestyle moved up faster than the runway grew.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Total comp is a number that includes vested equity, unvested equity, options that may never strike. Half of it is theoretical money you cannot spend. The other half is gone the moment you change jobs. The anxiety is correctly tracking the difference between liquidity and net worth. The brain just keeps using the wrong number.
