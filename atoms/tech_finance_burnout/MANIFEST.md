# Tech Finance Burnout Atom Library

## Overview
Complete atom collection for **tech_finance_burnout** persona across 4 atom types and 8 topics.

**Persona:** Adults 25-45 in tech/finance, $100K-$250K+. Analytically minded, evidence-based. Standing desk, Slack, always-on culture. 71% burnout rate. Decision fatigue. Imposter syndrome. Urban tech hubs.

**Topics:** anxiety, boundaries, compassion_fatigue, courage, depression, financial_stress, grief, self_worth

## Deliverables Summary

### Atom Types Created: 4
- **SCENE** (8 files × 30 variants): Second-person present tense narratives. 8-15 sentences. Sensory detail. No emotional labeling. Ends on action/sensory moment.
- **EXERCISE** (8 files × 30 variants): Second-person imperative instructions. 100-300 words. Somatic. Explicit counts. Persona grounded (desk, screen, keyboard, headphones, chair, phone).
- **INTEGRATION** (8 files × 7 variants): First-person author voice. 120-200 words. Quiet. Concrete body state. 1 reframe + 1 carry line (≤8 words). Modes: STILL-HERE, COST-VISIBLE, BODY-LANDED, QUESTION-OPEN, SOMEONE-ELSE.
- **COMPRESSION** (8 files × 30 variants): Present tense distillations. 40-120 words. 2-6 sentences. One insight. TTS-safe. Families: C1-C5.
