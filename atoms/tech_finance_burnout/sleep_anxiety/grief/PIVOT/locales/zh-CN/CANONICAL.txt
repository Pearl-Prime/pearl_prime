### Why PIVOT v03 hits hardest

1.  **It validates the grief of identity loss:** You mentioned grieving the "loss of intrinsic value and sense of self outside of professional output." 3.  **It bridges the somatic and the analytical:** Your analytical mind needs a reason for the pain. "Mourning" is a concept the intellect can grasp, but "missing them at 2am" is a somatic, felt experience. It meets you where you are: in the body, in the dark, with the mind racing.
