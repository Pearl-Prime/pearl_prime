## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Midnight. Your brain is still running code.
The system design problem will not release.
Acknowledge: work is solved for today.
You cannot solve it in the dark.
Put phone down. Close all screens.
Lie down. Start at your feet.
Feet. Shins. Knees. Thighs. Release each.
Work will exist tomorrow. Rest is priority.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Sleep time. But your brain runs tomorrow.
Spreadsheets. Meetings. Decisions. Email. All waiting.
Your nervous system cannot sleep while planning.
Acknowledge: you have done enough work.
Tomorrow will happen whether you plan now.
Ground in your mattress. Feel it supporting.
Say: "Tomorrow is not happening now."
Return to your body. That is task.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Anxiety is keeping you awake at one.
Your mind is catastrophizing about next week.
You must interrupt the thinking loop now.
Tense every muscle in your body hard.
Hold for five seconds. Everything. Maximum.
Release completely. Feel the wave of release.
Your body knows it is safe to rest.
The catastrophe is not happening right now.
---
