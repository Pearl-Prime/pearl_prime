## EXERCISE v01
---

---
The networking event starts in thirty minutes.
Your brain is predicting judgment and rejection.
Stand. Feel your feet firmly on ground.
Your body exists in this room too.
You do not have to perform here.
Name one genuine thing about your work.
You can say that one true thing.
You do not need more than that.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
All-hands meeting. Everyone will be looking.
Your performance anxiety is high right now.
You are not on stage. You are.
Breathe. Place both hands on your legs.
Feel the stability of your body here.
You belong to this group through work.
Your value is not measured by appearance.
Speak one thing if you can. Enough.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
The networking event. The voice in head.
"Everyone here is more impressive than you."
That is not data. That is anxiety.
Before you go: name three things.
One accomplishment. One skill. One contribution.
These are facts about your actual value.
Walk in carrying your own evidence here.
You do not need anyone's permission here.
