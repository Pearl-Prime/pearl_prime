## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
The performance review system trained you to track every interaction. The 1:1 became a status update. The off-site became a calibration session. The body could not turn off the tracking just because the calendar said "social." Now even the dinner with friends runs an internal evaluator. The evaluator is the residue of the system.
