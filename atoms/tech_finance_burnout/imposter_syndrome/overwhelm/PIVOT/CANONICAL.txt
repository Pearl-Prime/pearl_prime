## PIVOT v01
---

---
Every new responsibility is another surface area for exposure. The overwhelm isn't about capacity — it's about the expanding territory where someone might discover you don't belong.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame: you're overwhelmed by the work. The new frame: you're overwhelmed by the performance of competence. The work is manageable. The act around it is exhausting.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Imposter overwhelm has a specific shape: the more you succeed, the more you have to protect. Success becomes its own burden when every win raises the stakes of being exposed.
---
