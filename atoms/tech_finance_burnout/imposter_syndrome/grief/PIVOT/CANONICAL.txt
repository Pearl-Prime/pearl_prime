## PIVOT v01
---

---
Underneath the imposter syndrome is grief: for never being able to enjoy what you've earned. The achievements are real. The enjoyment was stolen by the certainty you'll be found out.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You grieve the celebrations you couldn't feel. Every promotion, every ship, every recognition — experienced through the filter of someone who believes it's all borrowed time.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The grief in imposter syndrome is for the career you've actually had but couldn't experience. You were there for all of it and missed all of it.
---
