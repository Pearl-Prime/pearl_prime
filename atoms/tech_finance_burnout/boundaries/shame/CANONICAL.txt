BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Closing the laptop at 9 pm and feeling the guilt arrive. Leo feels the raw self surface where others can see it. His shoulders climb toward his ears and lock there. For months the frame has held: the shame is a verdict, delivered by the body, and the body does not lie. Now Leo watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. His body registers the shift before his mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of his spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. The flaw is an event, bounded and specific — not a self, permanent and total. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Leo stands in that silence. His hands open at his sides.

Her legs buckle. Mei catches the doorframe before going down. Mei is when Slack pings at midnight with an urgent tag when she feels the exposure reaches the bedrock of self-concept and there is nowhere deeper to hide. The operating assumption — the flaw is not a behavior — it is the identity itself — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Mei does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. The exposure is a single moment in time, not a life sentence. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Yuki pauses while declining the Saturday deploy for the first time. Her stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. The shame has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The shame converts specific, bounded events into proof of fundamental deficiency while skipping every piece of contrary evidence. Yuki traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Yuki watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 5

COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
His vision narrows to a single point. The periphery dissolves. Nolan closes the laptop at 9 PM and feeling the guilt arrive. The shame runs its program at full speed. Nolan watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the mechanism treats every single error as confirmation of a permanent verdict that was decided long ago. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Nolan sees this now. His ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Nolan stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not his name.
