COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3

---
A tremor moves through his hands. Soren presses them flat against the surface. Soren is declining the Saturday deploy for the first time when he feels the siren fire in a quiet, safe room. The operating assumption — the body never lies about threat — meets a fact it cannot absorb. His jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held his world in place tilts on its axis. Soren does not fall. He stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around his new position. The siren and the threat occupy different timelines entirely. He does not grasp for a replacement frame. He breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

Declining the saturday deploy for the first time. Nolan the body hits full emergency protocol with nothing to flee from. His body shakes — not a tremor but a quake that starts in the core. For months the frame has held: if the sensation says danger then danger is here. Now Nolan watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. His body registers the shift before his mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of his spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. The alarm is signal without source — electricity without event. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Nolan stands in that silence. His hands open at his sides.

The base of her skull throbs. The tension radiates forward into her forehead. Mei declines the Saturday deploy for the first time. The alarm screams at peak volume, demanding the old compliance. Mei holds still in the noise and lets the wave pass through without obeying. Not despite the sensation — she feels every degree of it. Her body carries the heat. Her choices carry her name. She does not wait for the feeling to pass before acting. She acts while the feeling burns at full intensity. The acting is the embodiment. The body and the choice exist in the same moment. Mei owns this. The ownership lives in her hands, in her breath, in the ground beneath her feet.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2

---
His throat closes around the words. The vocal cords tighten. Soren declines the Saturday deploy for the first time. The alarm runs its program at full speed. Soren watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the alarm has lost the ability to distinguish remembered threat from present fact. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Soren sees this now. His ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Soren stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not his name.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Kiran pauses when Slack pings at midnight with an urgent tag. Nausea rolls through like a rogue wave. Kiran grips the counter to stay vertical. The alarm has run for hours without interruption. Now he sees the machinery laid bare. The alarm has lost the ability to distinguish remembered threat from present fact. Kiran traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. His stomach clenches as he maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Kiran watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
