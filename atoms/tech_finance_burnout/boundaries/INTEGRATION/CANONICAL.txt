

## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: BOUNDARY-FACT
---
My phone is in another room. I can't see it. I can't reach it. And I notice: I'm fine. My hands aren't shaking. My breath is normal. I expected catastrophe. No catastrophe arrived. The urgent thing either got handled without me or it's still waiting. My job didn't disappear. My value didn't vanish. I just created distance. Physical distance. It's remarkable how physical distance changes my nervous system. I'm not being unreachable. I'm just choosing when I reach. The boundary is concrete. The boundary is a room. That's all.
Carry: Boundaries are just physical distance.
