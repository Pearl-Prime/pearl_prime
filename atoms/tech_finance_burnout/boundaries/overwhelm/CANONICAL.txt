## MECHANISM_PROOF v01

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3

---
Ryan's lack of boundaries is producing increasing overwhelm. Without containment, the work is infinite. The mechanism is: work expands to fill available time plus some.
