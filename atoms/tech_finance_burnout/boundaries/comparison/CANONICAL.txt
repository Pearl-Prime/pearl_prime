COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tara stands when Slack pings at midnight with an urgent tag. Weight presses her chest flat. Breathing becomes a conscious act. She sees the colleague's promotion announcement. The frame that has held everything in place — worth depends on relative position — strains under the weight of what Tara sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Tara feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The metric measures nothing real — it is a machine for producing inadequacy. Tara does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

Heat climbs through his sternum and spreads across his collarbones. Dev closes the laptop at 9 PM and feeling the guilt arrive. The comparison runs its program at full speed. Dev watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the ranking system uses rigged criteria — it selects only data that confirms inferiority and discards everything else.
