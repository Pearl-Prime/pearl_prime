**1. Decoupling Feeling from Failure (v01)**

Just because colleagues appear "fine" at standup does not mean they are free of struggle, nor does their apparent stability invalidate your very real somatic and emotional burden. Your experience of being the "emotional infrastructure" and the "designated processor" for trauma is a heavy, invisible load that others may not carry in the same way. **A Gentle Suggestion:**
