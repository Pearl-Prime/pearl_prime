You had a good quarter and thought the depression was gone. It wasn't gone — it was dormant. The false alarm keeps you from building the scaffolding you actually need.

## PIVOT v02

---
The old frame: if you can still ship, you're fine. The new frame: shipping while depressed is your specialty, and the ability to function is the false alarm that delays your reckoning.

## PIVOT v03

---
