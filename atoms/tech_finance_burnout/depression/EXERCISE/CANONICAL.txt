## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Everything feels gray. No energy exists right.
You do not have to be productive.
Notice one small thing near your hand.
A pen. A coffee cup. Your phone.
Touch it. Feel its temperature and texture.
You are still in your body right.
That is enough for this moment today.
You do not have to fix anything.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
The drive to perform has left body.
Nothing matters. The screens all look same.
Stand. Move to a window if possible.
Look outside for two minutes without thinking.
Feel the air on your skin when.
You exist. Your body exists. That is.
One step. That is all required today.
The rest can wait until you rest.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Your brain is offline. The code.
Permission: you do not have to today.
Lie down. Place a pillow under head.
Feel the support holding you from beneath.
Breathe. Notice you are still alive here.
Your depression lies. You are not worthless.
Tomorrow may be different. Today you exist.
That existing is complete in itself today.
---
