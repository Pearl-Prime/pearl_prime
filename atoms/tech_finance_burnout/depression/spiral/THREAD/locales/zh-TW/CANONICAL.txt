The shift is from **resistance** to **witnessing**.

When you stop trying to fix the spiral, you stop fighting the part of you that is trying to keep you safe. The "next room" is not a place of solutions, but a place of **information**.

In that room, the spiral is no longer an enemy to be defeated, but a messenger. It is speaking in the only language it has left: repetition, intensity, and exhaustion. It is saying, *"I am holding this so you don't have to feel what is underneath."*

The shift is realizing that the energy you’ve been using to suppress the spiral—or to shame yourself for having it—can now be used to **listen** to it.

What does the spiral sound like when you are not trying to silence it?
