## PIVOT v01
---

---
The flatness becomes evidence of fundamental brokenness becomes certainty that you'll never feel anything again. The depression spiral is quiet — it doesn't panic, it just closes doors.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Depression spirals don't look like anxiety spirals. They're slow, heavy, certain. Each thought is a door closing: this won't change, nothing helps, this is just how it is now.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral into permanence: this grayness is forever. The spiral is the depression's PR department, selling you on hopelessness as a fact instead of a symptom.
---
