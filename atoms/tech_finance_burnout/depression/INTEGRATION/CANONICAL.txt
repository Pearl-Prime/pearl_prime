

## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: MOVEMENT-FACT
---
I walked today. Just walked. My legs were heavy. My body moved anyway. The walk didn't fix the depression. But my legs are less heavy now. That's all. That's enough. Movement changes the body state. Not the mind state. Not yet. But the body moves first. The mind follows sometimes.
Carry: Movement is the only treatment I control.
---

## INTEGRATION v02
---
integration_mode: STILL-HERE
reframe_type: SURVIVAL-FACT
---
I survived today. That's the whole report. I survived. I didn't accomplish anything else. I didn't solve anything. I just kept existing. And that counts. Survival is success right now.
Carry: Survival is the only success today.
---

## INTEGRATION v03
---
integration_mode: COST-VISIBLE
reframe_type: REALITY-FACT
---
The cost of depression is real. I can see it. One meal skipped. One friendship postponed. One project delayed. This is what depression actually costs. Not abstract suffering. Concrete cost. And the cost keeps being paid. I notice it.
Carry: Depression has a measurable price.
---

## INTEGRATION v04
---
integration_mode: QUESTION-OPEN
reframe_type: POSSIBILITY-FACT
---
What if tomorrow is just slightly less heavy? What if I'm not waiting for sunshine but for shade? What if I'm not waiting for happiness but for numbness to shift even slightly? I'm asking smaller questions now. Different questions.
Carry: Smaller questions might be answerable.
---

## INTEGRATION v05
---
integration_mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
reframe_type: WITNESS-FACT
---
I watched someone else be depressed. They were functional. They existed. They did small things. They weren't fixed but they moved. And I'm thinking: I can do that. That's a model of surviving depression that isn't about fixing it.
Carry: Functionality coexists with depression.
---

## INTEGRATION v06
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: SIGNAL-FACT
---
My body is telling me: slow down. Rest. Stop fighting. I can feel that instruction in my heaviness. In my slowness. In my resistance to everything. My body knows something. I'm listening instead of fighting.
Carry: Heaviness might mean stop, not fail.
---

## INTEGRATION v07
---
integration_mode: STILL-HERE
reframe_type: ENDURANCE-FACT
---
I've been here before. This state. This grayness. And it eventually shifted. Not because I did something special. Because time happened and the state changed. I'm sitting in the knowledge that change happens. Even when I can't see how.
Carry: Every gray day had an ending eventually.
