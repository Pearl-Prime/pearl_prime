## EXERCISE v01
---

---
A colleague you respected just left company.
Your immediate reaction: push it down, continue.
Stop. You can feel this loss right.
Sit. Let the sadness sit with you.
Do not analyze it. Do not fix.
Place your hand on your chest and.
Breathe. You are allowed to miss them.
That missing is love showing up as.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
The layoff was yesterday. Your team gone.
Your brain wants to problem-solve the sadness.
You cannot. This is not a problem.
Sit somewhere where no one can see.
Let yourself feel the weight of loss.
You do not have to be fine.
Five minutes of grieving is healing work.
After: return to work. Grief still there.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You are mourning the life before burnout.
That freedom. That excitement in the work.
Now it is all meals and meetings.
Sit quietly. Place both hands over heart.
Do not fix this feeling. Hold gently.
Say: "I miss that version of me."
That loss is real. It deserves sadness.
You can grieve and rebuild at once.
---
