---
The loss connects to every other loss. One grief becomes a cascade: you lost this, and before that you lost that, and underneath it all is the life you thought you'd have. The spiral makes every grief total.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Grief spirals in tech are quiet: they happen in the ten minutes between meetings, in the bathroom before the all-hands. The spiral runs the full loss inventory in the time it takes to compose yourself.

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral from the current loss to the accumulated cost of years in this industry. The grief isn't just about what you lost — it's about what you gave up without realizing it was a trade.
