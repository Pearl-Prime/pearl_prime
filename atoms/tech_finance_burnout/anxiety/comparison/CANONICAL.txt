## MECHANISM_PROOF v01
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ryan has now built a mental model of his peers' trajectories. He is tracking them. Every promotion, every role change, every company switch is data. He is running a constant calculation: where am I relative to where they are? The calculation is eating his attention. He is less focused on his own work because he is comparing his work to their work. His achievement looks smaller next to their achievement. He is spiraling into a narrative where he is falling behind. The mechanism is: comparison creates anxiety, anxiety makes him feel behind, feeling behind makes him wonder if he actually is behind, so he compares more.
