This permission reminds you that **their composure is not a reflection of your inadequacy.** Their ability to mask their struggle does not invalidate your very real, physical, and emotional toll. Your anxiety is not a deviation from the norm; it is a rational response to an irrational load. You are allowed to be the one who is struggling, even if no one else admits it.
