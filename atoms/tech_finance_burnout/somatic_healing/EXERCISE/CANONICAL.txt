## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You have been sitting for ten hours.
Your body is compressed, aching, disconnected.
Stand. Reach both arms high above head.
Feel your spine extending and lengthening upward.
Hold for three seconds. Feel your height.
Release. Now reach down toward your toes.
Let your spine release and decompress downward.
Repeat three times. Your body needs this.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Your neck and shoulders are locked tight.
The desk has been your cage hours.
Stand. Roll your shoulders backward slowly.
Feel each joint moving through its full.
Slow and deliberate. Eight rotations backward.
Then eight rotations forward. Feel the release.
Your shoulders now know they can move.
Return. Your nervous system has been reset.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
The pain in your lower back sharp.
Sitting for too long. Postural compression.
Stand. Put hands on lower back area.
Gently arch backward. Feel your front open.
Hold three seconds. Feel the gentle stretch.
Release. Hands to front of your thighs.
Forward fold. Let gravity lengthen your spine.
Your body is signaling: movement is necessary.
---
