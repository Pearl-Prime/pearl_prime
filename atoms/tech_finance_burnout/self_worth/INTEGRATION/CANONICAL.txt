

## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: EVIDENCE-FACT
---
My imposter syndrome still talks to me. My brain still questions my competence. And I notice: I have evidence against it. My promotion. My salary. My completed projects. Carry: Evidence is louder than imposter voice.
