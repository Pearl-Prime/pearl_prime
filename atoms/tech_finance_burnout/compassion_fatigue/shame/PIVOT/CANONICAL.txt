## PIVOT v01
---

---
You used to genuinely care about your team's problems. Now you catch yourself calculating the time cost of listening. The shame of that calculation is a second wound.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Shame says a good leader wouldn't feel empty when someone brings them a problem. The truth: a good leader has been absorbing everyone's problems for so long their reservoir needs its own support.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The shame of compassion fatigue is specific: you feel like a fraud for going through the motions of caring. But going through the motions while depleted is its own form of effort.
