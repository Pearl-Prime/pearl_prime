## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Her jaw locks. Teeth press together hard enough to ache. Mei is listening to another team member describe their own burnout when she watches the list grow while the clock shrinks. The operating assumption — falling behind means failing entirely — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Mei does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. Falling behind is a position, not a verdict — movement forward begins with one step. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Listening to another team member describe their own burnout. Nolan the system hits total capacity and no new input can be processed at all. Cold sweat sheets across his back. His shirt clings to the skin. For months the frame has held: everything is equally urgent and nothing can wait. Now Nolan watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. His body registers the shift before his mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of his spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Sequence exists even when the panicking mind denies it — one thing, then the next. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Nolan stands in that silence. His hands open at his sides.

Her jaw locks. Teeth press together hard enough to ache. Yuki stands after mentoring the third junior developer this week who is struggling. The overwhelm screams at peak volume, demanding the old compliance. Yuki chooses one task, moves it forward, and lets the pile exist without attending to it. Not despite the sensation — she feels every degree of it. Her body carries the heat. Her choices carry her name. She does not wait for the feeling to pass before acting. She acts while the feeling burns at full intensity. The acting is the embodiment. The body and the choice exist in the same moment. Yuki owns this. The ownership lives in her hands, in her breath, in the ground beneath her feet.

Ezra absorbs another round of one-on-ones that all end the same way. A roar fills his ears and blots out the ambient sound of the room. The overwhelm arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Ezra chooses one task, moves it forward, and lets the pile exist without attending to it. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in his body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Ezra acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries his name. This is who Ezra is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
After mentoring the third junior developer this week who is struggling. His fingers dig into his own palms. Crescents form in the skin. The overwhelm operates in plain view for the first time. Nolan catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The overwhelm creates the illusion that everything must be handled simultaneously, hiding the fact that sequence is always possible. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Nolan watches the loop close and reopen. His body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/tech_finance_burnout/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Mei pauses after mentoring the third junior developer this week who is struggling. The walls close in. Her lungs refuse to expand against the compression. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The pile presents itself as one massive indivisible object when it is actually made of individual, addressable items. Mei traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Mei watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
---
