## PIVOT v01
---

---
You felt nothing when a teammate shared their struggle. Then the spiral: I'm becoming cold, I'm a bad leader, I'll be found out, no one will trust me. The spiral has more emotion than the original moment did.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Compassion fatigue spirals into identity crisis: if I can't feel for others, who am I? The spiral mistakes depletion for character. You're not less caring — you're overdrawn.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The spiral after the numbness is the cruelest part. You can't feel, and then you catastrophize about not feeling. The compassion fatigue generates its own anxiety.
