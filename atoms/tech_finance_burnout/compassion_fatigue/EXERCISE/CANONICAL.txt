## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You have been the person everyone vents.
Their stress has become your internal stress.
Stop. Your nervous system has absorbed their fear.
Place both hands on your own chest.
Feel your heartbeat. This is your body.
Take three breaths where you breathe for you.
You cannot fix their problems or feelings.
Return to your own body and work.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Emotional labor today: team problems, anxiety, response.
You absorbed everyone's distress into your system.
Notice where you feel that burden now.
Shoulders? Chest? Stomach? Name the location.
That weight is not yours to carry.
Shake your hands like water off them.
Say out loud: "That is their feeling."
Give their worry back to them respectfully.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
A coworker vented about job security today.
Their fear is now living in your.
Wash your hands with cold water slowly.
Feel the temperature wake up your senses.
Notice: your hands belong to you alone.
Your job security is separate from fear.
Their struggle is real and not yours.
Return to something you actually control now.
---
