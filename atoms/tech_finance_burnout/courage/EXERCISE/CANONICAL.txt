## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You need to speak up in meeting.
The fear is: they will judge you.
Stand. Press both feet into the ground.
Feel the earth holding you up completely.
Your fear is a prediction, not fact.
Say the true thing you want to.
They can disagree. You still exist after.
Speak. You have earned the right to.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Before the difficult conversation with manager starts.
Name the specific fear without minimizing it.
Place one hand on your chest right.
This is your life. Your one life.
What do you actually need from talk?
Say it out loud three times clearly.
You are not asking for permission to.
You are asking for something real and fair.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
The code review revealed something hard.
Your stomach drops. You want to defend.
Wait. Breathe. This feedback is not worth.
Stand. Clench your fists. Hold three seconds.
Release. Feel the strength still there after.
You can hear hard feedback and survive.
You can disagree without disappearing completely.
Respond to the feedback today or tomorrow.
