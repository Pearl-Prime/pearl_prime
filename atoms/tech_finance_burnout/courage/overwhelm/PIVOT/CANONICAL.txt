## PIVOT v01
---

---
The list of things you'd need to change to live differently is so long it paralyzes you. Overwhelm disguises itself as realism when courage needs a single first step.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You see every obstacle between here and a different life. The overwhelm is real. But it's also a strategy for staying put — and staying put has its own costs you're not counting.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Courage doesn't need you to solve every problem at once. The overwhelm wants you to believe it does, because overwhelm is the guardian of the status quo.
---
