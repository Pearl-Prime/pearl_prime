

## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: FEAR-FACT
---
My hands were shaking when I said it. My heart was racing. My voice wavered slightly. This is what courage looks like in my body. Not absence of fear. Presence of fear plus action anyway. I said the true thing. My hands shook. Both are real. The tremor didn't make me stop. It just made me human.
Carry: Courage physically shakes sometimes.
---

## INTEGRATION v02
---
integration_mode: STILL-HERE
reframe_type: RISK-FACT
---
I risked the relationship by speaking truth. The relationship might break. I accept that. I also notice: it hasn't broken. The person is processing. The conversation is open. Truth didn't destroy. It created possibility instead.
Carry: Truth breaks bad relationships only.
---

## INTEGRATION v03
---
integration_mode: COST-VISIBLE
reframe_type: PRICE-FACT
---
There was a cost to speaking. Social cost. Relational cost. Someone was upset. I felt that. I didn't take it back. I just accepted the upset as the price of integrity. The price was real. I paid it. And I'm still here.
Carry: Integrity has a real price.
---

## INTEGRATION v04
---
integration_mode: QUESTION-OPEN
reframe_type: WORTH-FACT
---
What if speaking truth is more important than being liked? What if my integrity matters more than my approval rating? I'm sitting with that question. Not answering it. Just thinking about it. And I notice I'm calmer since I stopped trying to be liked universally.
Carry: Universal likeability is actually impossible.
---

## INTEGRATION v05
---
integration_mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
reframe_type: WITNESS-FACT
---
I saw someone else say a hard truth. Their voice didn't crack. They sounded calm. Powerful. And I realized: that could be me. I can do that. Not perfectly. But I can. The model exists.
Carry: I can borrow courage temporarily.

## INTEGRATION v06
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: GROUNDING-FACT
---
When I said the truth my feet were on the floor. I felt them press down. That physical grounding held me. My body knew what to do even when my mind was afraid. The grounding was real.
Carry: Ground first, speak second, worry third.

## INTEGRATION v07
---
integration_mode: STILL-HERE
reframe_type: SURVIVAL-FACT
---
I said something true and hard. The world didn't end. I didn't get destroyed. The feared thing didn't happen. I'm still here. Still employed. Still myself. Still capable. Actually more capable now. I know I can survive that risk.
Carry: Courage is just surviving the risk.
