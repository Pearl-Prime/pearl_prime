## PIVOT v01
---

---
The courage to change requires grieving the path you're on. You built this career with real sacrifice. Leaving isn't just brave — it's a loss, even when it's the right move.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Underneath the fear of leaving is grief for the identity you built here. Senior, respected, compensated. Courage asks you to mourn that before you can move.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The grief in courage is underrated. You're not just afraid of the unknown — you're sad about what the known has cost you and what walking away means about those costs.
---
