## PIVOT v01
---

---
One market dip and your mind runs the full sequence: portfolio down, retirement delayed, kids' college fund short, spouse finds out you were wrong. The spiral outpaces the actual loss by orders of magnitude.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The financial spiral has a specific flavor: numbers multiplying into catastrophe. One layoff rumor becomes no income becomes losing the house. The math is wrong but the fear is real.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You spiral from a budget variance to financial ruin in under a minute. The spiral's spreadsheet has no error bars, no base rates, no historical context. Just worst case, always.
