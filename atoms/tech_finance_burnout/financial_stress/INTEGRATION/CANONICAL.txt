

## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: REALITY-FACT
---
My stomach tightens when I open my bank account. This is the body reporting stress. Real stress. Not imaginary. The numbers are what they are. My reaction to them is what it is. I can see both. I can breathe through the stomach tightness. The numbers stay the same. But my nervous system doesn't have to match the panic.
Carry: Numbers are true, panic is optional.
---

## INTEGRATION v02
---
integration_mode: STILL-HERE
reframe_type: ACCEPTANCE-FACT
---
I make good money. And I'm still stressed about money. Both are true. The stress isn't proportional. It's disproportionate. And I notice: I've survived this before. Financially. I've gotten through tight months. I'm getting through this one. Still employed. Still housed.
Carry: Survival despite financial stress is possible.
---

## INTEGRATION v03
---
integration_mode: COST-VISIBLE
reframe_type: TRADEOFF-FACT
---
Everything costs something. Staying in the expensive apartment costs peace of mind. Moving costs energy and risk. Not solving it costs sleep. There's no cost-free option. I'm choosing which cost I can actually afford to pay. That's the only real choice.
Carry: Every option has a real cost.
---

## INTEGRATION v04
---
integration_mode: QUESTION-OPEN
reframe_type: ASSUMPTION-FACT
---
What if I don't need to be rich to be okay? What if I'm confusing affluence with security? What if I'm waiting for a fantasy when actual sustainability is possible here? I'm questioning the assumption that I need to make more. Maybe I need to spend less. Or maybe I just need to accept uncertainty.
Carry: Uncertainty doesn't mean disaster is coming.
---

## INTEGRATION v05
---
integration_mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
reframe_type: WITNESS-FACT
---
I talked to someone else about money stress. They make similar money. They have similar stress. We're not uniquely bad at money. We're part of a system designed to create this stress. That changes something. I'm not the problem. The math is.
Carry: The system stresses, not just me.
---

## INTEGRATION v06
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: NERVOUS-FACT
---
My nervous system tightens around money. Shoulders. Jaw. Stomach. My body has learned to fear scarcity. And I can feel that learning in my physiology. Relaxation is harder than fear. But my body can change. Not instantly. But with practice.
Carry: My body learned fear, can unlearn it.
---

## INTEGRATION v07
---
integration_mode: STILL-HERE
reframe_type: AGENCY-FACT
---
I have some control. Not much. But some. I control spending. I control how much I earn. I control what I ask for. Those aren't small things. I can't control housing prices. But I can control my response. That's where my actual power lives.
Carry: Agency exists within these constraints.
