## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Numbers on the screen are triggering alarm.
Slow down. Look at each number separately.
Which of these require action this week?
Write down: urgent action items only today.
Breathe before doing anything with the list.
Which ones can wait until next week?
Your nervous system cannot solve everything once.
Choose one action. Do that one action.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Financial stress is tightening your chest.
Notice the physical grip in your body.
Breathe in for four. Hold for four.
Out for six. Slow and deliberate breathing.
Numbers are not your safety or worth.
What is in your control this moment?
List three small financial actions you can.
Start with the smallest action right now.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
The spreadsheet is sending your system panic.
You are confusing important with urgent now.
Close the spreadsheet for five minutes complete.
Drink water. Move your body around.
Return. Open the file. Look at facts.
Which items truly need attention this week?
Which are you catastrophizing about unnecessarily?
Focus only on what needs action today.
