## TAKEAWAY v01
---
---
The gap was never between wanting to start and not wanting to. It was between deciding and moving — and that gap closes from the outside, not from trying harder on the inside.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---
---
Your attention isn't choosing the loud thing over the important thing on merit. It's just following volume — so turn the important thing up, or the loud one off.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---
---
Six hours and thirty hours can feel identical in your head. They stop feeling identical the moment you write down the actual number.
---

## TAKEAWAY v04
---
---
A number you're sure you'll remember is already evaporating. Write it down in the same breath you hear it, every time, not just the time it costs you.
---

## TAKEAWAY v05
---
---
The file isn't cursed and neither are you. It's just failed seventeen times in a row — so start somewhere new and paste the ugly draft in after.
---

## TAKEAWAY v06
---
---
The urgent tasks get done because the world is chasing you. Build your own chaser for the quiet ones, before the crunch makes self-starting even harder.
---

## TAKEAWAY v07
---
---
It was never item four that was hard. It was the other ten items, sitting in view, quietly pulling attention you didn't know you were losing.
---

## TAKEAWAY v08
---
---
You're not losing to a video at 2 a.m. because you're weak. You're losing because it was built to win — so rig the visual fight back in your favor.
---

## TAKEAWAY v09
---
---
Vague tasks generate dread. Specific first steps don't. Most freezes end the moment the label gets replaced by an actual sentence.
---

## TAKEAWAY v10
---
---
You'll edit a bad draft faster than you'll originate a perfect one. Give yourself something wrong to react to before you try to start from nothing.
---

## TAKEAWAY v11
---
---
The tenth identical ping has already stopped working. A person asking once, in their own words, hasn't.
---

## TAKEAWAY v12
---
---
Speaking the next move out loud isn't childish. It's a second pathway into motion, for the exact moments your first one stalls.
---
