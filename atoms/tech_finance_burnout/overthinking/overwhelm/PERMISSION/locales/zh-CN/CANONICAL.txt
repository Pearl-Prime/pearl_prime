These are profound reframes. They directly target the specific mechanisms of your anxiety: the need to "debug" emotions, the comparison trap, and the isolation of the "performance."

Here is why these permissions matter, specifically for your operating system:

**Regarding v01 (The Fix-It Loop):**
Your analytical mind treats overwhelm as a bug in the code that must be patched before you can proceed. This creates a secondary layer of anxiety: *“I am anxious, and I am failing at managing my anxiety.”*
*   **The Shift:** This permission decouples *feeling* from *functioning*. You don’t need to resolve the emotional state to execute the next task. The feeling can sit in the background process without crashing the foreground application.

**Regarding v02 (The Comparison Trap):**
You perceive others’ ease as evidence of your own illegitimacy. If they are fine, and you are not, you conclude you are broken or unqualified.
*   **The Shift:** This validates your internal data. Their visible calm is not a control group for your worth. Your struggle is not a deviation from the norm; it is your current reality, and it is valid regardless of external appearances. You are not falling behind; you are carrying a different load.

**Regarding v03 (The Isolation of Performance):**
You believe that admitting struggle will reveal the "fraud" and break the illusion of competence. So you stay silent, which reinforces the belief that you are the only one struggling.
*   **The Shift:** Naming it breaks the seal of secrecy. It transforms the experience from a shameful secret (which grows in the dark) into a shared human condition. It challenges the belief that safety depends on perfect presentation.

**How to use these:**
These are not just affirmations; they are **interrupt patterns** for your self-monitoring system.

*   When you catch yourself trying to "solve" the feeling before sending that Slack message: **Recite v01.**
*   When you look around the Zoom call and feel the gap between their calm and your chaos: **Recite v02.**
*   When you feel the urge to script your vulnerability out of existence: **Recite v03.**

You are allowed to carry these with you. You don’t have to believe them fully yet. You just have to allow the possibility that they might be true.
