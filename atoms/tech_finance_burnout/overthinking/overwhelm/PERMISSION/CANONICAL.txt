## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel overwhelm inside your overthinking without fixing it before the next sprint. Feeling it is not failing at it.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to struggle with overthinking and overwhelm even when everyone at the standup seems fine. Your experience is valid regardless of their performance.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to name the overwhelm out loud. Saying it doesn't make it more real. It makes you less alone in the golden handcuffs.
---
