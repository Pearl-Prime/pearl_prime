## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Your brain has been running the same.
Two hours. Three hours. Same thoughts repeat.
Stop. Stand. Move your body immediately.
Walk to the window or outside possible.
Feel the air. Look at something living.
Your brain is stuck in a loop.
Physical movement breaks the neural loop.
Return when your body feels different.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
It is eleven PM. Code runs.
You are replaying decisions made ten hours.
You cannot change them from the couch.
Close laptop. Stand. Shake your body.
Hands up. Shake them hard for ten.
Your body is now in different state.
Overthinking requires the same neural state.
You have interrupted it through action.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
The analysis loop started two hours ago.
You are modeling futures that don't exist.
Interrupt pattern: name five things you.
Right now. Not tomorrow. Not past decisions.
The chair. The light. Your breath. Sound.
What is actually true in this moment?
Your analysis is future-focused. Return to now.
Now is the only time you act.
