

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to need sleep as a physiological requirement and not as something you must earn by achieving enough before bed.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that the body running at performance-level cortisol for a decade has genuinely forgotten what baseline feels like. That is not metaphor. That is neurobiological fact.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to lie awake before the board meeting without making the wakefulness mean something is wrong with you.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to recognize that the mind reviewing the agenda at 4am is not being thorough. It is being afraid.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to put the phone across the room without treating that act as a professional risk.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to have a body that does not decompress on command just because you changed the setting from office to bedroom.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to take the insomnia seriously as a symptom rather than pushing through it as an inconvenience your competitors probably also have.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to stop rehearsing tomorrow's decisions tonight. The decisions will be better tomorrow with eight hours than worse tonight with none.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to create conditions that support sleep even if those conditions feel like lost productivity time.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to accept that a rested brain outperforms a depleted one and then actually act on that knowledge instead of just knowing it.
---
