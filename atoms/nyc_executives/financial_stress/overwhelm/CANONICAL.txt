The compensation is structured: base salary, bonus based on quarterly performance, equity vesting over four years, options that may or may not be exercised. He's tracking market movements because the investments depend on it. He's tracking interest rates because the mortgage depends on it. He's tracking his children's education costs because the tuition depends on it. Everything depends on everything else. The system is running in his head, always.
