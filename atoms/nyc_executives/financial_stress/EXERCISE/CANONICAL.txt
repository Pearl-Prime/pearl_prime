

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before opening the spreadsheet, sit and place both feet flat. Press down for five counts. The ground is solid. Numbers move. The ground does not move. Breathe in for four counts. Breathe out for four counts. Repeat three times. You will remain solid while numbers change.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Close your eyes. Clench your fists. Hold the clenching for six counts. Release your hands. Repeat five times. The numbers are clenching. You are releasing. You are separate from the numbers. Your body will not follow them down.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Place both hands on your desk. Press down firmly. The desk is holding your weight. The desk is solid. The numbers are on the desk. The desk is holding everything. Breathe in and feel supported. Breathe out. Repeat three times. The desk will not collapse.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Stand and shake out your hands. Shake as if shaking off water. Shake for six counts. Stop. Your hands are clear. Your hands are empty. Sit down. Your hands are ready for the numbers. Repeat twice. You can handle the numbers.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Hold for four counts. Breathe out for four counts. Hold for four counts. This is box breathing. The box is stable. The numbers are not. Repeat five times. You will stay in the box.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Place one hand on your heart. Feel your heartbeat. Your heart is beating at a steady pace. The numbers do not control your heartbeat. Your heartbeat is automatic. It will not change based on the numbers. Feel it beating. Repeat three times. Your body is separate from the numbers.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Close your eyes. Scan your body for tension. Find one area. Place your attention there. Breathe into that area for four counts. Move to another area. Repeat from head to feet. You are locating the stress. Breathing moves it out.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Sit and press your feet into the floor. Press with increasing pressure. Count to five as you press harder. Release. Your feet are the only thing pressing into the ground. The numbers are not. Repeat five times. You are grounded. Numbers float.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Place both hands in your lap. Interlock your fingers. Squeeze your hands together. Count to six. Release. Your hands are holding each other. They are not holding fear. Repeat five times. Your hands are holding comfort.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Hum for the entire exhale. Breathe in in silence. Hum out. Repeat six times. Humming signals to your nervous system that you are safe. The numbers are not dangerous. They are just numbers. You are safe.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
Stand and place your feet shoulder-width apart. Feel the ground under both feet. Shift your weight from foot to foot. The ground shifts with you. The ground does not collapse. Shift five more times. You are shifting. You are not collapsing.

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. As you hold the breath, count your heartbeats. Feel your heart. Count to ten. Your heart is beating. Your heart is not panicking. Your heart knows this is not an emergency. Breathe out. Repeat three times. Your heart is steady.

## EXERCISE v13
---

---

## EXERCISE v14
---

---

## EXERCISE v15

---

---

---

---

---
