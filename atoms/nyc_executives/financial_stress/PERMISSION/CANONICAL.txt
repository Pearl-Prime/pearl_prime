


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel stressed about money even when you earn well. The stress is real regardless of the number.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to stop checking the account balance today. It will still be there tomorrow.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to spend on something that has no return except your own enjoyment.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel the scarcity even though the scarcity doesn't match the spreadsheet.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to admit that money carries emotions for you that go back further than this job.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to want financial security and acknowledge that the definition of security keeps moving.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to not compare your financial position to your peers. Their numbers are not your business.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to feel afraid of losing what you have. That fear is not ingratitude.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to earn what you earn without guilt about earning more than your parents.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to have a complicated relationship with money. Complicated is not broken.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to not have it all figured out financially. The figuring out is ongoing.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to separate your financial worth from your human worth. They were never the same thing.
