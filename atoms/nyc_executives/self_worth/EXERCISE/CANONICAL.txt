

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit at your desk. Close your eyes. Feel your heartbeat. Your heartbeat does not change based on rankings. Your heartbeat does not know your job title. Your heartbeat is beating for you, not for your performance. Count ten heartbeats. Your heart values you equally at all times.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place both hands on your thighs. Feel the muscle underneath. This muscle exists whether you are promoted or not. This muscle is the same. Your body is the same regardless of your rank. Press down for five counts. Your body does not rank itself.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Stand and place your feet shoulder-width apart. Feel the ground under both feet. The ground does not know your salary. The ground does not know your title. The ground is holding you equally as a human. Feel both feet on the ground. The ground is fair.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Close your eyes. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for four counts. Breathe out for four counts. Hold for four counts. This is box breathing. The box is stable. Your worth is stable. Worth does not move. Repeat five times. Your worth is constant.

## EXERCISE v05
---

---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
