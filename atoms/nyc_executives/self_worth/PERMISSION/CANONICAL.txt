


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to not know who you are without the title. That confusion is honest.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel hollow after the promotion. The hollowness is information, not ingratitude.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to stop earning your right to exist. You already have it.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel afraid of who you are without the credentials. That fear is real.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to care about the ranking and also know it doesn't define you. Both are true.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to be tired of performing confidence you don't feel.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---

## PERMISSION v10

---

## PERMISSION v12

## PERMISSION v13

## PERMISSION v14

## PERMISSION v15

## PERMISSION v17

## PERMISSION v18
