## MECHANISM_PROOF v01

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
David tracks the triggers: voices, certain laughs, the smell of pipe tobacco in the lobby of the older building. Each one fires the same response—throat closes, eyes sting, a dropping sensation in the stomach. The triggers have no logic. They don't cluster around anniversaries or holidays. They arrive embedded in ordinary moments. A Tuesday meeting. A Thursday elevator ride. The grief doesn't follow a schedule. The grief follows sensory associations the conscious mind hasn't catalogued.

Sarah notices she avoids certain conversations. When colleagues mention their mentors, their advisors, their sponsors—she changes the subject. Not dramatically. A slight redirect. A new question. Grief doesn't fit in a conference room. So she tucks it behind redirection and keeps moving.

## EMBODIMENT v01
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David keeps a note on his phone: "Grief appeared at 2pm. Conference call. Voice trigger." He adds to it weekly. The log doesn't prevent the grief. The log gives it coordinates. Date, time, trigger, body location. The grief becomes data without becoming less real. He reads the log sometimes. The pattern is clear: ordinary moments, extraordinary responses. The log doesn't explain why. The log proves that it happens, and that he survived each one.
