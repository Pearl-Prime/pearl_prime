Before the shareholder call with the cfo watching. Helena feels a new request arrive and the entire system tilt. Weight presses her chest flat. Breathing becomes a conscious act. For months the frame has held: the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole. Now Helena watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. The pile is made of individual items that can be addressed one at a time. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Helena stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.

COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Helena pauses while walking into the conference room where the seats are already taken. The muscles in her back seize from the base of the skull downward. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The pile presents itself as one massive indivisible object when it is actually made of individual, addressable items. Helena traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Helena watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.

Before the shareholder call with the cfo watching. Nausea rolls through like a rogue wave. Elliott grips the counter to stay vertical. The overwhelm operates in plain view for the first time. Elliott catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Elliott watches the loop close and reopen.
