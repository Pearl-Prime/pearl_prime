


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel unsafe in a room where nothing is wrong. That feeling is a signal, not a verdict.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to close the laptop without checking one more time. The checking is the anxiety talking.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to let the deal sit overnight without running scenarios until 2am.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel the relief and not immediately replace it with the next worry.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to be successful and still feel afraid. Those are not contradictions.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to stop scanning the room for threats that aren't forming.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to say you don't know without treating it as a failure of preparation.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to leave a meeting without replaying every sentence you said.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to rest without earning it first with another deliverable.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to feel your shoulders drop and not treat the drop as letting your guard down.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to admit the anxiety is louder than the evidence. Admitting it is not weakness.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to stop being the person who handles everything without showing strain.
---

## PERMISSION v13
---

---
You are allowed to take the weekend without monitoring your inbox for fires that aren't burning.
---

## PERMISSION v14
---

---
You are allowed to feel the win land. Not just in your head. In your chest.

## PERMISSION v15
---

---
You are allowed to need help with something your title says you should handle alone.

## PERMISSION v16
---

---
You are allowed to stop rehearsing the worst case. The rehearsal is costing you the present.
---

## PERMISSION v17
---

---
You are allowed to have a body that reacts even when your mind says everything is fine.
---

## PERMISSION v18
---

---
You are allowed to decline the optional meeting that your anxiety has labeled mandatory.
---

## PERMISSION v19
---

---
You are allowed to be still without being productive. Stillness is not a gap in your output.

## PERMISSION v20
---

---
