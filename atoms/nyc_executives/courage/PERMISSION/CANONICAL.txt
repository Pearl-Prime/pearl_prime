


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be afraid and do it anyway. The fear is not a stop sign.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to say the thing that risks the room's approval. The room is not your keeper.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to show the vulnerability you've been hiding. It will not destroy what you've built.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to walk away from the safe path if the safe path is making you smaller.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to admit you were wrong in a room full of people who expect you to be right.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to start before you're ready. Readiness is a myth your fear invented.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to ask for what you need without wrapping it in business language.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to be seen without the armor. The armor was always optional.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to choose the hard conversation over the comfortable silence.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to risk looking foolish in pursuit of something true.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to trust someone with the unpolished version of what you're thinking.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to be courageous in small ways that nobody notices. Small courage still counts.
---
