David has identified the acquisition target. The target is sound. The fit is strategic. The presentation is prepared. He could bring this to the partners tomorrow. He has spent ninety minutes reaching the catastrophe. The catastrophe is complete. His mind is now at career damage. The proposal hasn't moved. The thought machinery has moved it all the way to ruin.

David has mapped the spiral through enough cycles to recognize it as machinery. The structure is mechanical: action → imagined negative reaction → escalating consequence → career threat. The structure is identical across different scenarios. Propose an idea. Push back on a direction. Ask for something. Each one runs through the same machine. The machine has one output: catastrophe. David can see the machine. He can see how it works. And he can feel it work. The spiral runs at speed. His careful analysis runs at thought-speed. The spiral runs at feeling-speed. By the time he could articulate the first step, steps two through ten have already concluded. The machinery is faster than understanding.

## TURNING_POINT v01
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
David decides to test the machinery. He writes down the spiral: Propose acquisition → partners push back → judgment questioned → partnership track at risk → career damaged. Then he examines each link. What's the evidence that pushback means judgment questioned? What evidence exists? He looks. There is none. Pushback on a specific target doesn't mean judgment on the partner. The link is assumed, not connected. He looks at the next link: partnership track at risk. What's the evidence? The evidence is: nothing specific. The cascade is built from assumptions. The turning point is this: the spiral is a prediction, not a certainty. The prediction has been running unchallenged. He's going to propose the acquisition. The spiral will run. He's going to notice that the spiral is running and that it's a prediction. He's going to act despite the prediction's certainty.
