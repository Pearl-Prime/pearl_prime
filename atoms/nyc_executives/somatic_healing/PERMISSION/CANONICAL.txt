

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that the body held what the career required and that the holding has a cost that is now presenting itself.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to give the body what it needs even though the body's needs are not on the calendar and do not produce deliverables.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to discover that the body has a different timeline than the career does and to respect that difference.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel in a session what you have not allowed yourself to feel in a decade of board rooms. That is not breakdown. That is finally completing a circuit.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to let somatic work be slow even though slow is not a register you have operated in professionally.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to treat the jaw tension, the shoulder lock, the chest tightness as language rather than inconvenience.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to need physical recovery from professional performance. Athletes understand this. You are allowed to apply the same logic.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to work with a body that has been sustained by ambition and adrenaline for years and is now asking for something different.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to feel the physical cost of the success without that cost negating the success.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
