


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel nothing for the colleague's crisis right now. The nothing is not permanent.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be depleted by caring. That depletion is proof, not failure.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to step back from someone else's pain without feeling guilty about the distance.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to need your empathy refilled before you can give it again.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to stop being the person everyone brings their problems to.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to admit that absorbing other people's emotions has changed how you feel your own.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to redirect a conversation that is taking more from you than you have to give.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to protect what remains of your capacity to care. Protecting it is how it comes back.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to feel the cynicism without judging yourself for it. The cynicism is a symptom.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to close the door and not feel for anyone for an hour. The hour is medicine.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to be the strong one who is currently not strong. Both versions are real.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the version of yourself who used to feel everything. That version is resting, not gone.
---
