

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
After the meeting, close your office door. Sit in your chair. Place both feet firmly on the floor. Feel the pressure under your feet. Breathe in for four counts. You have absorbed their stress. Breathe out for four counts. The stress is leaving your body with each exhale. Repeat five times. You are emptying the meeting from your body.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand and shake your entire body for eight counts. Shake your arms, legs, torso. Let everything vibrate. Stop suddenly. Feel the sensation. The meeting stress is being shaken off. Repeat this twice. Shake and settle. Shake and settle. Your body is separate from their stress.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Sit and place your hands on your chest. Feel your heartbeat. Your heartbeat is yours. It is not their heartbeat. Breathe in for four counts. Breathe out for four counts. Repeat six times. Your heart is beating for you. Not for the meeting. Not for their problems. For you.

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
In the bathroom, splash cold water on your face. Feel the shock. Cold is present. The meeting is past. Dry your face. Look in the mirror. You are still here. You are intact. The meeting did not take you. You have returned.

## EXERCISE v05
---

---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Sit at your desk. Press your palms down into the desk surface. Press firmly for six counts. Release. Your palms are pressing the meeting into the desk. The desk absorbs it. Not your body. Repeat eight times. The desk is solid. Your body is not the container.

---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---

## EXERCISE v14

---

## EXERCISE v15

---

## EXERCISE v16

---
