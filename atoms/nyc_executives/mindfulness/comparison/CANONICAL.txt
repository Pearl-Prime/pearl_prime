COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Julian tries something during a partner meeting where a rival firm's name comes up favorably: instead of letting his attention leave to run the comparison, he names it silently — that is their result, this is my room — and brings himself back to the actual agenda item in front of him. He does this four times in one meeting. Each time it works for about ninety seconds before the pull returns, but ninety seconds of actual presence, four times, is ninety seconds more than he had the meeting before.
