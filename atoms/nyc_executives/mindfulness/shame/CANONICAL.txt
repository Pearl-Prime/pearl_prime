Andre tries something after a costly mistake he cannot stop replaying: instead of continuing to relitigate it in every subsequent meeting, he names it once, silently, to himself — I made an error, it is being handled, it does not need to be re-tried in every room I enter today — and lets the sentence be the end of the loop rather than the start of another lap.
