

## PIVOT v01
---

---
The anxiety isn't about money. The money is the language your nervous system borrowed to express something older: the belief that safety is always one reversal away from being revoked. The number changing doesn't reach that belief.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You're not afraid of losing the wealth. You're afraid of losing the identity the wealth confirmed. The money proved something. The anxiety is about what happens to that proof if the markets correct.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
High earners are supposed to feel secure. The fact that you don't feel secure with this much feels like a malfunction. But the nervous system learned what safety felt like long before the compensation arrived — and it learned it without any money in the equation.
---
