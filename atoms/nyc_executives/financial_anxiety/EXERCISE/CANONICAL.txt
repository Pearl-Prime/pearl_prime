

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit at your desk before opening your portfolio app. Place both feet on the floor. Breathe in for four counts. The numbers on the screen will still be there in four seconds. Breathe out for four counts. Open the app only after the breath is complete.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place one hand over your sternum. Feel your heartbeat. Your net worth does not determine whether your heart continues beating. Count ten heartbeats. Your body is stable regardless of what the market did today.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Before a compensation conversation, stand and press your feet into the floor. Feel the floor holding your weight. You do not need the number to hold you up. The floor already is. Breathe in for four counts. Exhale for four counts.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Close your eyes at your desk. Say silently: I have provided today. Breathe in for four counts. I will provide tomorrow too. Breathe out for four counts. This is not a guarantee. It is a grounding sentence.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Place both hands on your desk. Press down firmly for five counts. The pressure is real, unlike the hypothetical scenarios your mind is running about the market. Release. Repeat three times.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Before opening your banking app, roll your shoulders back twice. Notice the tightness across your chest. That tightness is the anxiety, not the account balance. Breathe into the tightness for four counts.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Sit back fully in your chair. Let it hold your weight. Say silently: this feeling will pass through me like weather. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for two. Breathe out for six.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Stand and shake out both hands for six counts. The shaking moves the adrenaline the anxious thought produced. Stop. Notice the stillness after. Repeat once more.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Place your palm flat against your desk or the wall beside you. Press for five counts. This is something solid you own outright. It does not fluctuate. Release and notice your breath has slowed.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Before checking your portfolio at night, close your laptop and place both hands on your thighs. Breathe in for four counts. The market is closed. Checking again will not change tonight's number. Breathe out for four counts.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
At your desk, count five objects you can see that are yours and have nothing to do with money, a photo, a plant, a mug. Naming them interrupts the spiral toward the ledger.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Before a bonus conversation, splash cool water on your wrists in the bathroom. Hold for ten seconds. Notice the temperature change traveling up your arm. This interrupts the physical cascade before you walk into the room.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
Sit and press your feet flat, alternating heel lifts, right heel up, down, left heel up, down, eight times. Feel the pressure shift with each lift. This is discreet enough to do under a conference table before a budget review.
---

## EXERCISE v14
---

---
Place one hand on your chest and breathe in for four counts, hold for four, out for four, hold for four. Box breathing steadies the nervous system before a negotiation. Repeat four cycles.
---

## EXERCISE v15
---

---
At the end of the day, name one number that is true and stable, hours worked, meetings completed, a task finished. Breathe out slowly as you say it silently. Let this fact anchor you instead of the volatile one.
---
