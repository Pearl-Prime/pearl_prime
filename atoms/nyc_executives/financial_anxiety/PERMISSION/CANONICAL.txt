

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel financially anxious even though your net worth is more than your parents ever imagined possible. The math does not resolve the fear.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be afraid of losing what you built. That fear is not irrational. It is the weight of what contingency actually means at this level.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that the lifestyle you constructed now constructs you — and that awareness is not the same as being trapped.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel exposed by the equity cliff, the market position, the leverage you carry. The exposure is real.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to want financial security even at a level most people would call already secure. The threshold moves with the stakes.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to be uncomfortable with how dependent your sense of safety has become on a number.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to notice that wealth did not produce the safety you expected it would. That is worth staying with.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to feel anxiety about money even in the years when the money is good. The good years carry the memory of what they could become.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to separate the financial facts from the financial fear. They are not the same document.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge the specific terror of having built something and watching a single market move threaten its foundation.
---
