

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to find client dinners exhausting even though you appear to thrive in them. The performance is real. The cost of the performance is also real.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that the body running threat calculations under a very expensive suit is working harder than anyone in the room can see.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel socially anxious even though your role requires social performance. The requirement does not eliminate the anxiety. It just hides it.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to find the conference floor draining and not pathologize yourself for it. Sustained performance of ease is not ease.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to manage the anxiety through the role while also knowing that management is not resolution.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to step out of the reception without it being a professional failure. Recovery is not weakness.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to be seen without it meaning you are being evaluated. Not every room is an assessment center.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to notice the social anxiety underneath the executive composure without that noticing undoing the composure.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to need preparation time before high-exposure events. That need does not mean you are unsuited for the role.

## PERMISSION v10
