

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit in your chair. Place both feet on the floor. Press your feet down hard. Count to eight. Feel the pressure. This is aliveness. The pressure is proof. Release. Rest. Press again. Repeat five times. Your feet are alive. Your feet can press.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand and shake your entire body for ten counts. Shake your arms, legs, torso. Let every part vibrate. Stop and feel the sensation in your body. This is aliveness moving through you. Sit down. Stand again. Shake again. Repeat three times. You can activate yourself.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Close your eyes and listen for sounds. Identify one sound. A hum. A voice. Traffic. One real sound outside your body. Listen for another. And another. Repeat five times. You are connecting to the world. The world is alive. You are part of it.

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
