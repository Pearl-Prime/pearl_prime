

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: BODY_LANDED
reframe_type: BODY_FACT
weight: standard
carry_line: "Achievement doesn't lift this heaviness."
---
The deal closed. The revenue target was exceeded. The partnership came through. By every external measure, this quarter was the best. And inside, there's nothing. The lightness that used to come with wins doesn't arrive. The satisfaction doesn't land. Instead there's a flatness. A heaviness. The sensation of watching your own life happen from behind glass. The body knows this isn't the response to circumstances. The body knows this is something else. The weight in the chest. The limbs that feel heavier than they should. The energy that doesn't return with sleep. The motivation that flatlines regardless of what's at stake. This isn't laziness. This isn't lack of ambition. The body is showing you something. The absence of feeling is also information.
---

## INTEGRATION v02
---
mode: COST_VISIBLE
reframe_type: COST_UNCOUNTED
weight: standard
carry_line: "The losses don't appear on any report."
---
The marriages that dissolved while you were building the practice. The relationships with your children that became transactions around pickup times and quarterly visits. The friendships that eroded because you didn't have presence. The curiosities that died—the music you used to listen to, the books you used to read, the hobbies that required engagement. The person you were before the drive to perform became everything. Those losses accumulate as a low-grade grief that has no occasion. There's no funeral for the self you traded away. There's no memorial for the version of you that had interests unrelated to revenue. The cost appears as the heaviness. As the sense that something inside has been hollowed out. As the achievement that should have meant something and doesn't.
---

## INTEGRATION v03
---
mode: STILL_HERE
reframe_type: TIME_SHIFT
weight: light
carry_line: "Still here. The flatness is not forever."
note: STILL-HERE mode — final chapter only, one per book
---
You remember the last time you felt this. How it seemed permanent. How the flatness felt like the truth about who you actually are without the achievement covering it. How the heaviness felt like it would never lift. And then something shifted. Not because the circumstances changed. Because the nervous system found its way back. Because rest actually came. Because someone said something that landed differently. Because a moment appeared where you were present for it. The flatness is real. The flatness is not forever. You've been here before. You've found your way out before. The path back is slower than the descent. It requires things you don't know how to do. Stillness. Presence. Vulnerability. The slowness is part of the return.
---

## INTEGRATION v04
---
mode: QUESTION_OPEN
reframe_type: QUESTION_REFRAME
weight: standard
carry_line: "What are you performing that you've forgotten why."
---
The partnership track. The revenue targets. The hours billed. The deals closed. The reputation built. Ask yourself when you chose these things. Ask yourself what they were meant to bring you. Ask yourself what you were trying to prove, or protect, or build. The answers might surprise you. The answers might reveal that you've been chasing something that doesn't fill what's empty. The flatness might be coming from the gap between what you're building and what would actually mean something. The question isn't how to fix the heaviness. The question is what you've been performing without noticing you're no longer connected to why.
---

## INTEGRATION v05
---
mode: SOMEONE_ELSE
reframe_type: OTHER_PERSON
weight: heavy
carry_line: "The partner you admire carries the same weight."
---
The one who has everything. The title. The office. The compensation package. The prestige. The one whose career is exactly what you thought would mean something. You see it in their face sometimes. The absence of light. The way they go through the motions with a kind of disconnection. The way they seem to be watching their own life happen. They've made it all the way. They've become the thing they were supposed to become. And inside, there's still nothing. The flatness doesn't disappear when you win.
---

## INTEGRATION v06
---
mode: BODY_LANDED
reframe_type: BODY_FACT
weight: interrupt_landing
carry_line: "The body begins to soften when you stop."
note: interrupt_landing — use after The Interrupt story, quietest atom in the book
---
The work pauses. The emails wait. The momentum halts. The body doesn't immediately lighten. The heaviness remains. But something shifts. The constant motion stops pressing against the flatness. The possibility of feeling something else emerges in the silence. This is not healing. This is the moment before healing can begin.

## INTEGRATION v07
---
mode: COST_VISIBLE
reframe_type: COST_UNCOUNTED
weight: heavy
carry_line: "The cost is the self you lost."
---
The version of you that had opinions beyond the deal. The version that could be surprised. The version that wanted things for reasons beyond reputation. The version that felt joy without calculating its value. That cost is permanent. That cost is real. The flatness that came with the performance is the evidence that something was traded. Something true for something that looked right from the outside. The cost is paid in the slow dissolution of the capacity to feel.
