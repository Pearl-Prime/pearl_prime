COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Sweat breaks along his hairline. His shirt collar dampens. Theo is staring at the skyline from the forty-second floor when he feels the mask slip and the inadequacy become visible to witnesses. The operating assumption — the shame is a verdict, delivered by the body, and the body does not lie — meets a fact it cannot absorb. His jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held his world in place tilts on its axis. Theo does not fall. He stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around his new position. The flaw is an event, bounded and specific — not a self, permanent and total. He does not grasp for a replacement frame. He breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

In the corner office at 8 pm with the lights off. Sweat breaks along her hairline. Her shirt collar dampens. The shame pushes with full force against her sternum. Katherine holds ground while seen, without flinching, without performing, without defending. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Katherine moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
In the corner office at 8 pm with the lights off. Pressure mounts behind his eyes like a wave building against a wall. The shame operates in plain view for the first time. Rajan catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The shame machinery feeds on particular incidents to fuel a general indictment against the entire self. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Rajan watches the loop close and reopen. His body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
