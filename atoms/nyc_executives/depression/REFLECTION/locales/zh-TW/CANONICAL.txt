## PRACTICE v01

### SOURCE: The Three Questions

---

Find a quiet moment. A cup of tea. Five minutes where nobody needs anything from you.

Then sit with these three questions. Don't answer them fast. Let them settle.

**First: What am I actually doing with my time?**

Not the story you tell people. Not the version that sounds noble or impressive. What are you actually spending your days on? Where do your hours really go?

**Second: Does this align with what matters to me?**

Be honest. If there's a gap between how you spend your time and what actually matters to you, you've found something important.

**Third: What small thing could I change this week?**

Not everything. Not your whole life. One small redirect. One honest choice that moves you closer to what actually matters.
