

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: inherited
---
The invisibility-of-continuity mechanism operates when high-performers continue producing while internal engagement has dimmed. The executive is still delivering results, but the felt meaning has gone offline. This asymmetry is particular to those trained to function regardless of internal state. The skill that used to serve them has become a liability. The work continues seamlessly while something fundamental has shifted. Nobody else can see the internal change because the external performance has not faltered. I recognize this in the gap between what appears to be true and what actually is.
