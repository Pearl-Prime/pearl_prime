

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to sit at the table and still carry the voice that says you do not belong there. Both can be true.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be the first in the room without needing to be proof of anything. You earned the seat. You are allowed to just sit in it.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that the internal evidence file — the one cataloging every doubt, every near-miss, every thing you do not know — is not an accurate record. It is a selected one.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to be genuinely competent and privately terrified of exposure at the same time. That is the standard experience at your level.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to stop auditing yourself during the meeting. The audit is costing you the presence you need.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to have gotten here partly by luck without that fact erasing every ounce of skill that kept you here.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to perform confidence while privately uncertain. That performance is not fraud. It is leadership.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to be the person who does not yet know and to be treated as if you do. That gap is universal. It just feels uniquely yours.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to acknowledge that being first carries a weight that the people you report to have never had to carry.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to stop trying to be twice as prepared as everyone else in the room. You are allowed to arrive as a peer.
