


## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to grieve at work even though the work doesn't make room for it.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to not be over it on anyone else's timeline.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to cancel the meeting because the grief showed up uninvited.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel the loss in the elevator, in the cab, in the middle of a sentence.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to tell one colleague what happened. Carrying it alone is not strength.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---

---
You are allowed to be less productive for as long as the grief requires.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---

---
You are allowed to cry in a building where nobody cries. The building is wrong, not you.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---

---
You are allowed to feel angry that the world kept going. The going feels like an insult.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---

---
You are allowed to miss someone during a presentation. The missing and the presenting can coexist.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---

---
You are allowed to not compartmentalize today. The compartment is full.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---

---
You are allowed to take the time off without calling it anything professional.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---

---
You are allowed to let the grief change what work means to you. Changed meaning is not lost meaning.
---
