

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Claim one accomplishment this week. Not to brag. Just to practice believing yourself. The doubt isn't evidence of incompetence. It's evidence of self-awareness that hasn't caught up to itself yet. The credential doesn't silence the doubt because the doubt isn't looking at the credential. It's looking for the exception.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
You hire people who are smarter than you for specific things. That is good leadership. It also creates new doubt: what is your specific thing. The thing you actually did was bet on a future that was not obvious. That bet is the skill. The bet is also harder to point at than any of the credentials your team carries.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Every funding round, every press hit, every milestone — they create new evidence that you might still be the wrong person to be running this. The evidence stops mattering at a certain altitude. You did not get here by accident. You got here by making the calls that everyone else thought were too risky. The risk was the call. That is the credential.
