MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

COST_INTENSITY: 4

---
Lila catches the spiral's propagation method. It does not generate new content. It takes one thought and copies it, adding a slight variation with each copy. The original thought: the pitch might fail. Copy one: the pitch will fail. Copy two: the pitch will fail and the company will suffer. Copy three: the company will suffer and people will lose jobs.
