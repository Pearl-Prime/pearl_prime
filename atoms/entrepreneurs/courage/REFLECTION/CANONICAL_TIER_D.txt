I want to talk about something I've noticed in myself and in business owners I've worked with: a mechanism that makes changing direction harder than it logically should be. But there's a mechanism that operates against that logic.
The mechanism calculates a cost to changing direction that goes beyond the actual business cost. The mechanism reads changing direction as a kind of failure. The mechanism makes it feel like pivoting means you were wrong. The mechanism is automatic.
