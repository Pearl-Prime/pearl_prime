

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: STILL-HERE

You're just here. In the dark. Awake. That's enough.

## INTEGRATION v07
---
mode: COST-VISIBLE
Calculate what a rested version of you can accomplish versus what a sleep-deprived version can do. The rested version closes deals. The sleep-deprived version second-guesses the deals they should close. The rested version sees opportunity. The depleted version sees only risk. Sleep isn't a luxury. It's a business asset. Every hour you steal from sleep, you're stealing from your own performance. The math is that simple.
