--- variant: v01
You're allowed to sleep. That sounds obvious and it's the permission your body has been requesting while your mind keeps overriding the request. You have permission to be unconscious, to be unavailable, to miss whatever happens between midnight and morning.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to need sleep without it meaning you're soft, uncommitted, or less of a founder. The glorification of sleeplessness in startup culture is a badge of honor that's actually a warning label. You're allowed to opt out of that particular competition.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to not solve the sleep problem tonight. The pressure to fall asleep is its own kind of productivity demand. You have permission to lie there, awake, without that being a failure. Sometimes the permission to not sleep is the thing that lets sleep come.
