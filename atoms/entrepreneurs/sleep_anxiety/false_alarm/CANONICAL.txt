## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante jolts awake at 2 AM. Heart pounding. Something is wrong. He runs through the inventory: servers up, no emergency emails, no missed calls. Nothing is wrong. His body does not believe the evidence. His chest is still hammering. The false alarm fires in the dark where there is nothing to check, nothing to verify, nothing to resolve. The frame shifts: the alarm is not responding to reality. It is responding to the habit of crisis. His nervous system has no off switch because he never built one. His breath slows. The alarm is a recording of yesterday's stress playing on tonight's speakers.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Lila wakes in full panic. Sheets soaked. Heart rate dangerous. She reaches for her phone — the reflex is faster than thought. No emergencies. No messages. No fires. Her body is in fight-or-flight in a dark, quiet room. The false alarm is total: every system activated for nothing. Her hands shake. She cannot convince her body that the room is safe. The frame collapses: she has trained her nervous system to treat sleep as a vulnerability window. Rest equals exposure. The alarm is not about any specific threat. It is about the terror of being unconscious while the company exists. She puts the phone on the dresser, across the room. Lies back down with her chest still pounding. Safety is not the absence of the alarm. It is the decision to stay horizontal after it fires.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren receives a vague email from a client. Heart rate spikes. Hands go cold. The false alarm fires its full sequence: they are leaving, the contract is dead, the revenue is gone. Ren sits with the alarm for thirty seconds. Does not respond. Does not draft the crisis email. Opens the message. Reads it. The client wants to discuss expanding the contract. Ren closes the email and notices the alarm is still running even though the information is positive. The alarm is not about the email. It is about the wiring. Ren knows the wiring now. Lets it run. Responds to the email, not the alarm.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Sasha receives a legal letter. Her hands shake. Her chest compresses. Every false alarm in her history fires simultaneously: this is the one, this is real, this will end everything. She opens the letter with trembling hands. Reads every word. It is a standard trademark notice — routine, not hostile. Her body is still in full alarm. She sets the letter down. Picks up the phone. Calls the lawyer. The alarm is screaming. She dials anyway. The action is not brave. It is practiced. She has learned to act inside the alarm instead of waiting for the alarm to stop. The alarm does not stop. She acts anyway. That is the identity.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Wei tracks the false alarm's activation pattern. Every ambiguous input — a vague email, an unexpected call, a delayed response — enters the same processing pipeline: assume worst case, activate body, prepare for crisis. The pipeline does not have a branch for neutral or positive inputs. Everything routes through worst case. Wei's chest tightens. She has been running a system with one pathway. The pathway is threat. The mechanism is not broken. It is limited. It has one road. Every traveler takes that road because there is no other road. Her hands warm slightly as she sees the bottleneck. The solution is not faster processing. It is more roads.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ava identifies the false alarm's ultimate function: it keeps her in a perpetual state of readiness that prevents rest, reflection, or joy. The alarm is not protecting her from danger. It is protecting her from vulnerability. Rest is vulnerable. Joy is vulnerable. Reflection reveals pain. The alarm keeps her activated so she never has to be still. Ava's chest pounds. The mechanism is not about threat detection. It is about stillness avoidance. The alarm is a movement machine. As long as she is responding to alarms, she does not have to sit with herself.
---
