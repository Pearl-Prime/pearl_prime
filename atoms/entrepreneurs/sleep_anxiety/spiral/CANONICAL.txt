## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya starts with one thought: the pitch is tomorrow. The thought branches: what if the pitch fails? What if the pitch fails and they pull funding? What if they pull funding and he has to lay off the team? What if the layoff destroys the company? Each branch spawns two more. His chest tightens with every fork. The spiral is a fractal of worst cases built on a single future event. The frame shifts when he traces the spiral back to its root: the pitch is tomorrow. One event. One room. One hour. His jaw releases. The spiral built a disaster movie from a calendar entry. The calendar entry is still just a time and a place.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante has been running the same failure scenario for four hours in the dark. The scenario starts with a missed deadline and ends with bankruptcy. Every time it completes, it restarts with a new detail added. Her body is exhausted but her mind has infinite energy for catastrophe. Her chest is a cage. Her hands grip the sheets. The frame ruptures: the spiral is not protecting her from failure. It is simulating failure so vividly that her body experiences it as real. She has been bankrupt six times tonight — in her mind, in her body — and none of it has happened. She opens her eyes in the dark. The room is quiet. The company exists. The failure is fiction. She is the only audience. She stops watching.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marcus notices the spiral starting. The thought hooks into the next thought, which hooks into the next. Marcus names it aloud: 'Spiral.' The word is a speed bump. The spiral does not stop but it loses velocity. Marcus picks up a pen and writes the original question — the one before the spiral hijacked it. The question is simple. The answer takes five minutes. The spiral wanted five hours. Marcus gave it five minutes and a name. The spiral will return. Marcus knows what to do when it does.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren feels the spiral build to peak speed. Every thought chain is running simultaneously: finance, product, team, market, self-worth, mortality. The spiral is a centrifuge. Her chest is compressed. Her vision narrows. She does not fight the centrifuge. She sits in the center of it. Closes her eyes. The spiral spins around her. She is still. The stillness is not the spiral stopping. It is Ren finding the motionless point inside the spin. The point exists. She found it before. She finds it now. The spiral loses its center when Ren claims it.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sasha catches the spiral's propagation method. It does not generate new content. It takes one thought and copies it, adding a slight variation with each copy. The original thought: the pitch might fail. Copy one: the pitch will fail. Copy two: the pitch will fail and the company will suffer. Copy three: the company will suffer and people will lose jobs. Each copy adds one link. The chain grows because each link creates the next. Sasha's chest tightens. The spiral is not creative. It is a copier with an increment function. The original thought is still just: the pitch might fail. Everything after it is duplication.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Wei sees the spiral's deepest truth: it is a substitute for feeling. Every rotation converts emotion into thought. Grief becomes analysis. Fear becomes scenario planning. Anger becomes strategic assessment. The spiral takes raw emotional material and processes it into cognitive product. Wei's chest is tight. The mechanism: the spiral is not maladaptive thinking. It is emotional avoidance disguised as intellectual engagement. She is not thinking too much. She is feeling too little, and the thinking fills the gap.
---
