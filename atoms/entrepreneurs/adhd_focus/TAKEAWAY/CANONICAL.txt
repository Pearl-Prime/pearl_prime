---
The freeze breaks at the verb, not the vision. Name the smallest physical action and start.

---
Six proposals are not one task — they're six initiation decisions. Give the folder a shrinking edge and let it carry you.

## TAKEAWAY v03
---
---
Your attention follows what's loud, not what's important. Make the investor update louder than the Notion board.

## TAKEAWAY v04
---
---
Time doesn't announce itself to an ADHD brain. Put a clock where your eyes already are.
---

## TAKEAWAY v05
---
---
Working memory drops what isn't written down. Write it the instant it occurs to you, not at day's end.

## TAKEAWAY v06

## TAKEAWAY v07

## TAKEAWAY v08

## TAKEAWAY v09

## TAKEAWAY v10
