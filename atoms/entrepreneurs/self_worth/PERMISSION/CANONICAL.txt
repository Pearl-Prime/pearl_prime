--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be valuable on a day when the company lost value. Your worth doesn't fluctuate with the metrics. You have permission to be enough regardless of the dashboard. That's not delusion. That's the basic human right you forfeited when you fused your identity with the business.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to exist as more than a founder. To have interests, relationships, and parts of your identity that have nothing to do with the company. Those parts aren't distractions. They're the scaffolding that keeps you human when the business tries to consume you entirely.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to be proud of yourself without a quarterly result to justify it. You have permission to feel good about who you are -- not what you've built, not what you've earned, not what you've proven. Just who you are, standing there, without a slide deck to validate it.
