--- variant: v01
Your worth isn't a KPI. It doesn't have a dashboard, it doesn't need quarterly review, and it doesn't depreciate with a bad month. The project of separating your value as a human from your value as a founder is the most important work you'll never put on a slide deck.

--- variant: v02
Every founder who ties their self-worth to the company is building on a foundation that could liquidate at any time. That's not a sustainable architecture for a human psyche. The work is building something inside you that survives regardless of what happens to the thing you built outside you.

--- variant: v03
You are not your cap table. You are not your revenue. You are not your last round. You are the person underneath all of that, and that person has been neglected in favor of the metrics for so long that meeting them again might feel like meeting a stranger. Do it anyway.
