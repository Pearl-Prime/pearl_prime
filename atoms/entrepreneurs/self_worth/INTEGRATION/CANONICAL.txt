

## INTEGRATION v01
---

---
I read a testimonial from a client who said I changed their business.
I almost deleted it. The voice said they were just being nice.
I read it again. They were specific. They were real.
My worth wasn't determined by comparison. It was determined by actual impact.
Carry line: Impact is worth. Comparison is noise.
I'm counting impact now.

---

---

Carry line: Shipped is better than perfect.

## INTEGRATION v03
---

---
I stopped following the person I'd been comparing myself to.
Three weeks without comparison. My mood improved noticeably.
Comparison was the habit that cost me my worth.
Breaking it cost me a feed but gained me peace.
Carry line: Comparison is a thief I'm refusing.
I'm keeping my peace instead.
