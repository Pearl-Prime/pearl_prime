## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Ava's body carries the overwhelm in his neck. He cannot turn his head past forty-five degrees. Three months of restricted range of motion. Three months of the overwhelm living in his cervical spine. His hands massage the muscles uselessly — the tension is deeper than his fingers reach. The frame shifts when he stops trying to fix the neck and starts listening to it. The restriction is not an injury. It is a boundary his body set. The neck says: you cannot look at everything at once. You have to choose a direction. His jaw unclenches. The overwhelm wanted him to see every threat simultaneously. His body limited the view. The limitation is wisdom.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Nadia's hands go numb during a meeting. Both hands. Complete loss of sensation. She stares at her fingers. Moves them. They move but she cannot feel them moving. The overwhelm has become neurological. Her body is shutting off the inputs it cannot process. Her chest is a vise. The frame ruptures: her body is doing what her mind would not — reducing the incoming data. The numbness is not pathology. It is a circuit breaker tripping because the load exceeded capacity. She stands up from the meeting. Walks to the window. Presses her palms against the cold glass. Sensation returns slowly. The cold is information her body can process. One input. One sensation. The overwhelm retreats to the edges of her hands. The center holds.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Priya opens the inbox: forty-seven messages. The overwhelm presses on the chest. Priya counts three that are urgent. Forty-four that are not. Priya opens the first urgent message. Not all forty-seven. Not the overwhelm's preferred method: everything at once. One message. Priya has built the practice of subtraction — removing the non-urgent to see the urgent. The overwhelm protests. Priya works the three. The forty-four wait. They are still there when the three are done. They are manageable now.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Dante is in the hardest week of the year. Everything is due. Everything is urgent. Her chest is a vise. Her hands are numb. The overwhelm is at peak intensity. She does not try to solve the overwhelm. She does the next task. Then the next. The overwhelm is the weather. The tasks are the path. She walks the path in the weather. She does not wait for the weather to change. The weather does not change. She changes her relationship to walking in it. Each completed task is a step. The overwhelm is still the sky. She is still walking.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Lila sees the overwhelm's architecture. It does not respond to actual volume. It responds to perceived simultaneity — the belief that everything must happen at once. Twenty tasks spread across a month are manageable. The same twenty tasks perceived as simultaneous are paralyzing. The overwhelm converts sequential demands into parallel ones. Lila's chest compresses. She sees the conversion happening in real time: the task list does not say 'all at once.' Her mind does. The overwhelm is an editing error. Her mind removes the timestamps and presents every deadline as now.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Marcus sees the overwhelm's terminal stage: it has become the operating system, not the error message. She does not experience overwhelm as a state. She experiences it as reality. The world is too much. Always. The overwhelm is not a response to conditions. It is the lens through which all conditions are perceived. Marcus's chest is compressed. The mechanism: the overwhelm installed itself as the default filter. Every input is pre-overwhelmed before it reaches her. She does not get overwhelmed by things. She receives things through overwhelm.
---
