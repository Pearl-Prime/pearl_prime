--- variant: v01
You're allowed to feel things in your body without understanding them. Not every sensation needs a strategy. You have permission to notice the tightness, the heat, the pressure without immediately trying to fix it or figure it out. Observation is the first step. It doesn't need to be followed immediately by action.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to be a founder who does body work. Whatever that looks like -- yoga, therapy, breathwork, just sitting and paying attention to what your shoulders are doing. It's not soft. It's the maintenance schedule your hardware has been requesting since you were too busy building software to listen.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to prioritize your body over your business for an hour. For a day. For a season. The somatic healing isn't a detour from the work. It's the foundation the work was always supposed to be built on. You have permission to return to it.
