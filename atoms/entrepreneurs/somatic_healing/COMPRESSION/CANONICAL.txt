

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Lower your shoulders every time you notice them raised. Small practice. Nervous system relearning. The body records what the mind edits out. The record doesn't disappear just because it isn't being read. The tension that lives in the body as posture, as bracing, as held breath — it arrived for a reason. It stays for the same reason.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
The body has been compensating for a strategy. You learned to push past the no-energy day, the cold-coming-on day, the mind-too-loud day. The pushing made the company. It also taught the body that its signals would be overruled. That training takes a while to undo.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Founders carry stress in specific places. Forward-shoulder posture from the laptop. Jaw clench from the meetings. Lower-back tension from the chair you bought in year two and have not replaced. The places are not random. They are the topography of the role. Knowing the topography is the first step to changing it.
