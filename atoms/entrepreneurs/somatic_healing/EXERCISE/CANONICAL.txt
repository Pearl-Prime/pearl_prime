## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Your body holds the weight of every decision. Lie down. Feel where you touch the ground. Your back. Your legs. This contact is healing. Just notice.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand. Roll your shoulders back slowly. Feel your spine lengthen. Press your feet down. Your body carries the business. Honor it. Move it consciously.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Close your eyes. Scan from your head down. Where is your tension. Your jaw. Your neck. Stomach. Don't fix it. Just notice. That awareness itself releases it.
---
