## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya's neck is permanently rotated fifteen degrees to the right — the direction of his office door. His body has been orienting toward the entry point of potential threats for so long that his muscles have adapted. His right shoulder is higher than his left. The watcher is not just a mental state. It is a postural deformity. The frame shifts during a chiropractic session when the doctor asks: what are you watching for? Priya does not know the answer. The watching has outlived its reason. His body is guarding a door from a threat that has no name. He turns his head to center. The muscles protest. He holds center anyway. The watcher loses its fixed orientation.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante cannot sit with her back to a room. Restaurants, coffee shops, meetings — she positions herself facing the door. Always. Her back against the wall. Her nervous system requires the full visual field or it will not allow her body to rest. Her shoulders are granite from the constant readiness. The frame shatters when her massage therapist works the erector spinae and asks: 'When did you last feel safe with your eyes closed?' She cannot remember. The watcher has made eye contact with the world a survival requirement. Closing her eyes is surrender. She lies face-down on the table. Eyes closed. Back exposed. The vulnerability is unbearable for forty seconds. Then bearable. Then her shoulders release for the first time in years. The watcher did not need the visual field. It needed permission to stop.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marcus walks into the office and catches himself scanning faces. The watcher activates. Marcus notices the activation — the neck tightening, the eye movement, the chest bracing. Then Marcus chooses: today, I look at the work, not the faces. The faces are not data. They are people who are thinking about their own problems. Marcus opens the laptop. Begins the first task. The watcher protests from the periphery. Marcus types. The watcher fades to background noise. It is still there. Marcus is louder.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren is in the highest-stakes meeting of his career. Acquisition offer. Every face in the room matters. The watcher wants to scan every expression, decode every glance, interpret every throat-clear. Ren looks at the term sheet. Just the term sheet. The faces are peripheral. The numbers are central. He reads the numbers. Asks a question about the numbers. The watcher screams that someone frowned. Ren reads the next clause. The meeting proceeds on the content, not the facial theater. The watcher has lost the steering wheel. Ren drives.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sasha identifies the watcher's energy source: uncertainty. The watcher does not activate on known threats. It activates on unknown ones. The space between data points is where the watcher lives. Between emails, between meetings, between check-ins — the gaps are the watcher's fuel. Sasha's shoulders are rigid. She sees the mechanism: the watcher cannot be satisfied by information because information creates new gaps. Each answered question reveals two unanswered ones. The watcher is not searching for resolution. It is powered by the impossibility of resolution.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/somatic_healing/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Wei identifies the watcher's final form: it has consumed his capacity for trust. Trust requires the watcher to stand down. The watcher cannot stand down because standing down means being unguarded. Being unguarded means being betrayable. The mechanism is circular: the watcher prevents trust, the absence of trust validates the watcher, the validation strengthens the watcher. Wei's shoulders are granite. The cycle is self-sustaining. The watcher does not need external threats. It generates its own justification by preventing the relationships that would prove it unnecessary.
---
