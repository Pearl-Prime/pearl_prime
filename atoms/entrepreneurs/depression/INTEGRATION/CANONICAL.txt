

## INTEGRATION v01
---

---
I stopped fighting the darkness and it became smaller.
I sat with it one evening. Just sat. Didn't resist.
By sitting, I learned it wasn't infinite. It had edges.
My body could survive it.
Carry line: Surrender is stronger than resistance.
I'm surviving now.
---

## INTEGRATION v02
---

---
The numbers looked fine from the outside.
Inside, I was hollow. The success felt empty.
I finally told someone. My therapist. A friend. My partner.
The saying-it-out-loud made it real enough to address.
Depression lies about being shameful. It's just chemistry.
Carry line: Silence feeds the lie that you're alone.
Speaking breaks the lie.
---

## INTEGRATION v03
---

---
I moved my body for five minutes. That was the whole goal.
Not exercise. Just movement.
My nervous system needed to know I could still move.
Five minutes became easier by day three.
Carry line: Movement tells your body it's safe.
My body is learning it's safe.
---

## INTEGRATION v04
---

---
I wrote down what was true when depression said it was all false.
The company existed. I existed. That was enough.
By writing it, I made it real enough to believe.
Carry line: Truth written down is harder to deny.
I'm writing myself toward believing.
---

## INTEGRATION v05
---

---
I sat in the sunshine for ten minutes. No agenda.
Just sat and felt the warmth.
Something in my chemistry shifted. Not cured. But shifted.
Carry line: The body remembers how to receive.
My body is remembering.
---

## INTEGRATION v06
---

---
I called someone I love. I didn't know what to say.
I said nothing. They listened.
That listening was enough.
Depression tells you that you're too broken to connect.
Connection is the evidence that you're not.
Carry line: Connection is the only antidote I've found.
I'm staying connected.
---

## INTEGRATION v07
I survived another day. That was the whole win.

Not success. Not breakthrough. Survival.

By month's end, I'd survived thirty days.

Thirty days is enough proof that recovery is possible.

**Carry line: Survival today is victory today.**

I'm counting victories smaller now.
