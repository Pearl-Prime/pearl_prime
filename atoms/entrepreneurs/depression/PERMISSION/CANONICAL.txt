--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be depressed and still be a founder. Those identities can coexist. You don't have to choose between admitting the depression and maintaining credibility. The permission you need isn't from your board or your team. It's from yourself, to be a person who is struggling while also being a person who is capable.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to not be okay. Not temporarily, not strategically, not as a prelude to a comeback narrative. Just not okay, right now. The depression doesn't need a timeline or a resolution plan to be valid. It needs acknowledgment before anything else.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to seek help for this. Not a life coach. Not a founder peer group where everyone performs resilience. A professional who treats depression as the clinical reality it is. You have permission to stop treating a mental health condition as a mindset problem.
