## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tomás walks into a conference room full of potential partners. His body floods with adrenaline. Threat. Exposure. Danger. His hands go cold. His throat tightens. Every face in the room is neutral but his nervous system reads them as hostile. He sits down. The meeting begins. The partners are friendly. Engaged. Interested. The false alarm fired on the walk through the door and never corrected itself. The frame shifts: his body's threat assessment is not about the room. It is about rooms in general. Every room with people is a threat room. His jaw unclenches as the first partner smiles. The room is not the threat. The anticipation of the room is the threat.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya stands backstage at a pitch competition. Her heart is at 140 BPM. Her vision is tunneling. She is certain she will freeze on stage. Blank out. Humiliate herself in front of three hundred people. She considers leaving. Her legs are shaking. She walks onstage. Her voice works. The slides work. The audience laughs at her joke. The pitch lands. She walks offstage with her hands still trembling. The false alarm was total — every system in her body predicted catastrophe, and the catastrophe did not occur. The frame collapses: her nervous system is not a predictor. It is an alarm. Alarms do not know the difference between fire and a fire drill. She just completed a fire drill. Her body still thinks the building was burning.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Lila receives a vague email from a client. Heart rate spikes. Hands go cold. The false alarm fires its full sequence: they are leaving, the contract is dead, the revenue is gone. Lila sits with the alarm for thirty seconds. Does not respond. Does not draft the crisis email. Opens the message. Reads it. The client wants to discuss expanding the contract. Lila closes the email and notices the alarm is still running even though the information is positive. The alarm is not about the email. It is about the wiring. Lila knows the wiring now. Lets it run. Responds to the email, not the alarm.

## EMBODIMENT v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marcus receives a legal letter. Her hands shake. Her chest compresses. Every false alarm in her history fires simultaneously: this is the one, this is real, this will end everything. She opens the letter with trembling hands. Reads every word. It is a standard trademark notice — routine, not hostile. Her body is still in full alarm. She sets the letter down. Picks up the phone. Calls the lawyer. The alarm is screaming. She dials anyway. The action is not brave. It is practiced. She has learned to act inside the alarm instead of waiting for the alarm to stop. The alarm does not stop. She acts anyway. That is the identity.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ren tracks the false alarm's activation pattern. Every ambiguous input — a vague email, an unexpected call, a delayed response — enters the same processing pipeline: assume worst case, activate body, prepare for crisis. The pipeline does not have a branch for neutral or positive inputs. Everything routes through worst case. Ren's chest tightens. She has been running a system with one pathway. The pathway is threat. The mechanism is not broken. It is limited. It has one road. Every traveler takes that road because there is no other road. Her hands warm slightly as she sees the bottleneck. The solution is not faster processing. It is more roads.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sasha identifies the false alarm's ultimate function: it keeps her in a perpetual state of readiness that prevents rest, reflection, or joy. The alarm is not protecting her from danger. It is protecting her from vulnerability. Rest is vulnerable. Joy is vulnerable. Reflection reveals pain. The alarm keeps her activated so she never has to be still. Sasha's chest pounds. The mechanism is not about threat detection. It is about stillness avoidance. The alarm is a movement machine. As long as she is responding to alarms, she does not have to sit with herself.
