## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante stumbles through an introduction at a pitch event. His name comes out wrong. He corrects himself. His face is on fire. The shame is instant and total. He has failed at the most basic social act: saying his own name. His chest constricts. He excuses himself to the bathroom. The frame shifts in the mirror: he said his name wrong because his mouth was dry from anxiety. Not because he is defective. The shame converted a dry mouth into a character flaw. He splashes water on his face. Returns to the room. Says his name correctly. The shame protests. He overrules it.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Lila freezes mid-sentence at a client dinner. Complete blank. Seven people staring at her. The silence is three seconds that feel like three minutes. Her face is white. Her hands are under the table, gripping her knees. Someone fills the silence with a joke. The table laughs. The moment passes for everyone except Lila, who will replay the freeze for the next six weeks. The frame shatters: the shame is not about the freeze. The freeze was three seconds. The shame will spend six weeks on it. The freeze is a moment. The shame is a sentence. She forces her hands onto the table. Picks up her fork. Continues eating. The shame wanted her to disappear. She stays visible. The staying is the rupture.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren presents the real numbers at the board meeting. No sanitizing. No editing. The numbers are not good. The room is quiet. Ren's face is warm but the voice is steady. The shame says: they will see you as a failure. Ren says the numbers aloud anyway. The saying is the identity. A founder who presents real numbers, even the bad ones. The board asks questions. The questions are practical, not damning. The shame expected execution. The reality is a working meeting. Ren's hands unclench under the table.

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Sasha is asked point-blank at a public event: 'Did your last company fail?' The shame detonates. Face hot. Hands ice. Every cell wants to disappear. Sasha answers: 'Yes.' One word. The word hangs in the air. The audience does not flinch. Sasha continues: 'And this is what I built from the wreckage.' The shame wanted the yes to be the end. Sasha made it the beginning of the next sentence. The audience leans in. The shame recedes behind the story. Not gone. Behind.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Wei identifies the shame's core logic: visibility equals vulnerability. Every act of being seen — presenting numbers, sharing struggles, asking for help — routes through the shame processor, which tags it as exposure and triggers the hide response. The shame does not evaluate the content of the visibility. All visibility is dangerous. His face warms as he sees the mechanism. The shame treats a board presentation and a confession of failure identically: both are visibility, both trigger hiding. The mechanism has no granularity. His chest loosens slightly. The shame is not wise. It is indiscriminate.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/social_anxiety/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ava identifies the shame's terminal architecture: it has become the foundation of his decision-making. Every choice passes through the shame filter: will this expose me? Every action is evaluated not for its merit but for its risk of visibility. The shame has become the executive function. Ava's face is burning. The mechanism is complete: he does not make decisions. The shame makes decisions. He executes them. The decisions protect the shame, not the company. Not the person. The shame.
---
