--- variant: v01
Burnout isn't the opposite of hustle. It's the final product of it. You didn't fail at working hard enough. You succeeded at it so completely that there's nothing left. Recovery isn't about getting back to where you were. It's about questioning whether where you were is worth getting back to.

--- variant: v02
The rest you need isn't a weekend. It's a fundamental renegotiation of the terms under which you've been operating. Burnout is a breach of contract between your ambition and your body. The body isn't wrong. The contract was.

--- variant: v03
You can't sprint a marathon. That's not a motivational quote -- it's a physics equation your body has been trying to show you. The takeaway from burnout isn't 'try harder.' It's 'the strategy of trying harder was the strategy that brought you here.'
