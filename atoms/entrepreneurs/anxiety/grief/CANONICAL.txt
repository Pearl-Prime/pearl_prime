

## RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/anxiety/grief/recognition/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Alex watches a friend get promoted to VP at his old company. Stable salary. Benefits package. Title. Respect. The friend is thrilled. Alex feels his chest tighten. He gave that up. Deliberately. He made a choice to build something instead. The choice was intentional. But watching others get the security he abandoned creates a specific kind of grief. His hands feel cold. He's not regretting the choice exactly. But he's grieving what he chose not to have. The friend has a paycheck. Alex has uncertainty. The friend has a defined role. Alex has everything. The trade feels real and permanent.
