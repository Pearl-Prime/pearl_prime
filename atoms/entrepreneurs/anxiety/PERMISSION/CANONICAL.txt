You're allowed to be anxious and still be a good founder. Those aren't contradictory. You're allowed to have a nervous system that runs hot without interpreting it as evidence that you can't handle this. You can handle this and be anxious about it at the same time.

You have permission to stop performing calm. The energy spent pretending you're fine is energy that could go toward actually dealing with what's happening. You don't owe anyone a relaxed version of yourself -- especially not at the expense of your own honesty.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to need help with the anxiety. Not a podcast recommendation. Not a breathing exercise link from someone who's never had their chest tight before a board meeting. Real help. Professional help. The kind that requires admitting you can't optimize this alone.
