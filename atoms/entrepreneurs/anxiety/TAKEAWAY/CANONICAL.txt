--- variant: v01
The anxiety isn't a flaw in your founder operating system. It's the overhead cost of running a human being at startup speed without ever scheduling maintenance. You can't optimize it away. But you can stop treating it like a bug and start treating it like a signal that something in the system needs attention.

--- variant: v02
Naming the anxiety doesn't make it stop. But it does stop it from running your decisions while wearing a disguise labeled 'gut instinct.' The first step isn't fixing it. It's seeing it clearly enough to stop letting it steer.

--- variant: v03
You don't need to be anxiety-free to build something great. You need to stop letting anxiety be the silent co-founder making half your decisions. Awareness is the first term sheet revision. It doesn't solve everything, but it changes the cap table of what's actually running the show.
