--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be in financial trouble without it meaning you're in trouble as a person. The financial situation is one dimension of your life. You have permission to stop letting it speak for all the other dimensions.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to ask for help. From an advisor, a mentor, a professional who's seen worse. The financial stress has convinced you that asking is admitting defeat. It's not. It's accessing resources. That's what good founders do.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to make the hard financial decisions -- the layoffs, the cuts, the conversations -- and still feel terrible about them. You don't have to be ruthless to be responsible. You have permission to be a founder who makes tough calls and grieves what they cost.
