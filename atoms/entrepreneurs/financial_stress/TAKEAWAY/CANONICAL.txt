--- variant: v01
Financial stress and financial anxiety aren't the same thing. The stress is about the real situation. It's about payroll, creditors, and decisions that have material consequences. Acknowledging the weight of that reality isn't weakness -- it's the first honest conversation you've had about what you're carrying.

--- variant: v02
You can't think your way out of financial stress at 3 AM. The midnight math sessions aren't problem-solving -- they're rumination dressed as responsibility. The actual problem needs daylight, a plan, and possibly help you've been too proud to ask for.

--- variant: v03
The financial stress is real, but the story you're telling yourself about what it means about you isn't. Bad finances don't make you a bad founder. They make you a founder in a bad financial situation. Keeping those separate is how you make decisions from clarity instead of shame.
