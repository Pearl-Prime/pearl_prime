I call this the client absorption mechanism. It's the way that professional relationships get metabolized through your own nervous system until the client's problem becomes your problem, the client's stress becomes your stress, the client's anxiety becomes your anxiety.
A client reaches out with concern, and before you've even thought about it, your nervous system is already sympathizing. It's the nervous system that never gets to fully discharge because it's constantly matching and mirroring and taking in.
