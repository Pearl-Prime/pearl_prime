## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Alex sits with the loss that compassion fatigue created — not the acute pain but the chronic ache of something that will never return to what it was. Vision narrows. The grief arrives in the body first: Chest hollow., a heaviness that has no specific weight but fills every space. Then Alex stops fighting the grief and lets it exist alongside the day's work. The frame cracks — grief does not require resolution to be carried. It does not require understanding to be felt. Alex can function inside the ache. The ache is not the absence of function. It is the presence of caring about something that changed. Stomach settles The caring is the grief's engine and the grief is the caring's cost.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Alex's compassion fatigue grief breaks open completely. Not gradually — all at once, in a public space, without warning. The tears come or the voice breaks or the body simply stops cooperating. Chest tight. The loss that has been carried in the body finally exceeds the body's capacity to contain it. Alex is undone in front of witnesses. The shame is secondary to the grief. The grief is so large it overwhelms even the shame. Something shifts in the breaking — the grief, when it finally flows, carries with it the toxic weight of containment. The frame shatters: containing the grief was more destructive than releasing it. The breaking is not the problem. The holding was the problem. Hands cold. Alex sits in the aftermath, emptied and raw, and the rawness is the first honest sensation in months.
---

Maya carries the loss related to compassion fatigue openly now. It shows up in quiet moments — a memory triggered by a familiar phrase, an absence noticed at a meeting. Shoulders lock. The grief passes through and Maya continues. Not over it. Through it. The loss does not define Maya's capacity. Maya works with the loss as a companion rather than an obstacle. Breathing stays even Maya is a person who has lost something real and carries the loss without being consumed by it. The carrying is honest. The continuing is deliberate. Both are true.

The deepest loss arrives. Maya's compassion fatigue situation produces a grief that goes all the way down — not surface sadness but the kind that restructures the landscape. Chest hollow. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Alex notices that grief about compassion fatigue surfaces at predictable triggers — a date, a name, a place, a song. The grief is not random. It has architecture. It waits at specific locations and activates when Alex passes through. Jaw clenches. Alex sees the machinery: the grief has embedded itself in the landscape of daily life. The conference room where the last meeting happened. The calendar date that marks the anniversary. The protective logic is clear — the grief marks the places that mattered, ensuring they are never forgotten. But the marking also ensures they are never just places again. Stomach drops. The pattern persists because the grief is the price of having cared, and the caring was real, and the places where the caring happened still exist.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/compassion_fatigue/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Alex realizes the grief about compassion fatigue has been compounding for years. Not one loss but an accumulation — each small grief adding to the pile because none were fully processed. The current grief is not about the current loss. It is about every loss that preceded it. Shoulders lock. Alex sees the full machinery: each ungrieved loss adds weight to the next. The system has no outlet. Grief enters and never leaves. It accumulates in the chest, in the shoulders, in the jaw. Throat closes. The pattern persists because grieving requires time and space and both are in short supply. The unprocessed grief stays stored, and stored grief compounds into depression, and depression prevents the processing that would relieve the accumulation. The system is a grief warehouse with no exits.
---
