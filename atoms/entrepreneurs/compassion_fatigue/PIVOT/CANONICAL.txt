--- variant: v01
You started this because you cared. About the mission, about your team, about the problem you're solving. Now caring feels like a liability on your balance sheet. Compassion fatigue doesn't mean you've become a bad person. It means you've been giving from a fund with no deposits.

--- variant: v02
Your team comes to you with problems and you feel... nothing. Or worse, resentment. Not because you don't care, but because you've been the emotional Series A for everyone else's crises while running on fumes yourself. The well isn't dry because you're broken. It's dry because nobody thought to refill it.

--- variant: v03
Every founder pitch includes the word 'passion.' Nobody mentions that passion has a burn rate too. You've been so busy caring about your team, your customers, your mission that you forgot to check whether you had any compassion left for the person in the mirror.
