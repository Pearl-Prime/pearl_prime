## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/watcher/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tomás scans the market daily for signs that his old company's failure was inevitable. He reads industry reports, competitor earnings, economic indicators — all filtered through one question: was it my fault? His eyes are dry from screen time. His shoulders are concrete. The watcher is not gathering intelligence. It is gathering evidence for a verdict. The frame shifts when he notices he skips any data that suggests the failure was market-driven. He only saves the data that implies personal responsibility. The watcher is not objective. It is the grief's prosecutor. His hands leave the keyboard. The prosecution rests.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya monitors her former employees' LinkedIn profiles. Every update is a data point. Hired. Promoted. Thriving. Her chest aches. Her throat is a knot. She watches their recoveries the way she used to watch dashboards — constantly, compulsively, looking for information that will make the grief make sense. Her stomach drops. The frame collapses: she is not watching them succeed because she is happy for them. She is watching to measure how much her failure delayed their lives. The watching is self-punishment disguised as caring. Her hands close the browser. Their stories are theirs. Her grief is hers. The two are not connected by a dashboard.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/watcher/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Lila walks into the office and catches himself scanning faces. The watcher activates. Lila notices the activation — the neck tightening, the eye movement, the chest bracing. Then Lila chooses: today, I look at the work, not the faces. The faces are not data. They are people who are thinking about their own problems. Lila opens the laptop. Begins the first task. The watcher protests from the periphery. Lila types. The watcher fades to background noise. It is still there. Lila is louder.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/watcher/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marcus is in the highest-stakes meeting of his career. Acquisition offer. Every face in the room matters. The watcher wants to scan every expression, decode every glance, interpret every throat-clear. Marcus looks at the term sheet. Just the term sheet. The faces are peripheral. The numbers are central. He reads the numbers. Asks a question about the numbers. The watcher screams that someone frowned. Marcus reads the next clause. The meeting proceeds on the content, not the facial theater. The watcher has lost the steering wheel. Marcus drives.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ren identifies the watcher's energy source: uncertainty. The watcher does not activate on known threats. It activates on unknown ones. The space between data points is where the watcher lives. Between emails, between meetings, between check-ins — the gaps are the watcher's fuel. Ren's shoulders are rigid. She sees the mechanism: the watcher cannot be satisfied by information because information creates new gaps. Each answered question reveals two unanswered ones. The watcher is not searching for resolution. It is powered by the impossibility of resolution.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sasha identifies the watcher's final form: it has consumed his capacity for trust. Trust requires the watcher to stand down. The watcher cannot stand down because standing down means being unguarded. Being unguarded means being betrayable. The mechanism is circular: the watcher prevents trust, the absence of trust validates the watcher, the validation strengthens the watcher. Sasha's shoulders are granite. The cycle is self-sustaining. The watcher does not need external threats. It generates its own justification by preventing the relationships that would prove it unnecessary.
---
