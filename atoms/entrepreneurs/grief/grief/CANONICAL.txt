## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tomás drives past the building where his office used to be. New signage. Different company. His hands tighten on the wheel. His throat closes. He built something in that space. It is gone. The grief is not abstract — it has an address, a parking spot, a window he used to look out of while thinking. His chest aches. The frame shifts when he realizes he has been driving this route deliberately, not by habit. He has been visiting the grief like a grave. His jaw unclenches. The building is not his anymore. The grief is. He can carry the grief without circling the block.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/grief/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya finds the original pitch deck on an old hard drive. Slide one: the vision. Her vision. She built every slide with hands that shook from excitement, not dread. Now her hands shake differently. Her eyes sting. She reads the market analysis she wrote at 2 AM with absolute conviction. The company is gone. The conviction is gone. The slides remain. Her chest breaks open. The frame collapses: she has been grieving the company. But the deeper loss is the person who made these slides — someone who believed without evidence, who leapt without calculating the fall. That person is not dead. That person is buried under the grief. She closes the laptop gently. Not to forget. To stop punishing herself for having believed.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/grief/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Lila drives past the old office building. The grief rises. Chest heavy. Throat tight. Lila does not drive faster. Does not look away. Holds the grief while driving at a steady speed. The building passes. The grief remains but it is in the passenger seat, not behind the wheel. Lila has learned to carry loss without being steered by it. The next block arrives. Then the next. The grief does not demand a destination. It just rides along.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/grief/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marcus holds the old pitch deck one more time. The grief is immense. Every slide is a version of himself that no longer exists. His chest is a cavern. His eyes burn. He does not close the laptop this time. He reads every slide. Lets the grief land on each one. The vision. The team page. The financial projections that were pure fiction and pure hope. He closes the laptop and the grief does not close with it. It stays open, in his chest. He carries it into the next thing. The next thing is a new project. Different slides. Different hope. The grief is not opposition to the new hope. It is the foundation under it.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ren sees how the grief maintains itself. It does not just arrive and wait. It recruits. It finds reminders — a song, a street, a calendar date — and converts them to grief triggers. The grief is not static. It is an active system with a supply chain. Each reminder restocks the grief. Ren's chest is heavy. The mechanism is not the loss itself. The mechanism is the infrastructure that keeps the loss current. His jaw tightens as he maps the supply chain: places he avoids, people he has not called, music he has stopped listening to. Every avoidance is a maintained trigger. The grief feeds on the avoidance as much as the memories.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/grief/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sasha sees the grief's deepest mechanism: it has become his identity. He is the founder who lost. Every introduction, every conversation, every mirror shows him the person who failed. The grief is not something he carries. It is something he became. The mechanism: the loss consumed his self-concept and replaced it with itself. He is not a person who experienced grief. He is the grief. Sasha's chest is a cavern. Separating himself from the grief means rebuilding an identity from scratch. The mechanism is not the loss. It is the merger of self and loss.
---
