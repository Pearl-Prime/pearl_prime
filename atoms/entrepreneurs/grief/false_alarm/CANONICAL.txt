---
Dante tracks the false alarm's activation pattern. Every ambiguous input — a vague email, an unexpected call, a delayed response — enters the same processing pipeline: assume worst case, activate body, prepare for crisis. The pipeline does not have a branch for neutral or positive inputs. Everything routes through worst case. Dante's chest tightens. She has been running a system with one pathway.
