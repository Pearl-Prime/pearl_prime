--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be terrified about money without it meaning you're bad with money. Financial anxiety and financial competence are separate variables. You can be excellent at managing finances and still have a nervous system that panics about them. Both things are real.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to stop checking. The bank balance, the dashboard, the runway calculator. The compulsive monitoring isn't making you safer. It's feeding the anxiety cycle. You're allowed to designate specific times for financial review and be unavailable to the panic between them.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to feel the financial anxiety without solving it in this moment. Not every anxious feeling requires an immediate action plan. Sometimes the permission you need is simply to feel scared about money without the feeling being an assignment to fix something right now.
