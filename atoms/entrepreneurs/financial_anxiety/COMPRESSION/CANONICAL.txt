

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Revenue isn't security. Security is security. Build security. That's the work that actually matters. The number never feels safe because safety was never what the number was actually tracking. The stress response doesn't care about your actual bank balance. It responds to the story about the bank balance.
