--- variant: v01
The relationship between your bank balance and your nervous system isn't rational, and treating it rationally won't fix it. Financial anxiety in founders is personal survival instinct wearing a business suit. You can address the numbers. But you also need to address the part of you that equates solvency with safety and insolvency with annihilation.

--- variant: v02
No amount of runway eliminates the anxiety because the anxiety was never really about the runway. It's about what running out would mean about you. Separating your financial reality from your identity reality is the accounting no spreadsheet can do.

--- variant: v03
Financial planning helps with financial problems. Financial anxiety is a nervous system problem that happens to be denominated in dollars. You need both kinds of solutions, and pretending the spreadsheet is enough is how founders stay trapped in a cycle of check-the-balance, feel-the-panic, check-again.
