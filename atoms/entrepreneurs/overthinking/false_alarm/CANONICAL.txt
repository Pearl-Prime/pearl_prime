## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya receives a routine audit email and his mind explodes into scenarios. Tax penalties. Legal exposure. Criminal negligence. His chest is a drum. His hands are ice. He drafts three responses, deletes all three, and starts researching tax law. Two hours later, his accountant calls it a standard filing confirmation. The overthinking converted a form letter into an existential crisis. The frame shifts: his mind does not distinguish between routine and emergency. Every input receives maximum analysis. His jaw softens. The audit email is still in his inbox. It is still a form letter. His nervous system is the only thing that changed it into a weapon.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante's biggest client goes silent for forty-eight hours. No response to emails. No Slack messages. Dante's mind builds the scenario: they are leaving. They found a competitor. They are drafting the termination notice right now. Her stomach is in her throat. She rewrites the follow-up email eleven times. Crafts a retention offer. Builds a churn prevention deck. The client replies: 'Sorry, was at my kid's school play. What's up?' The overthinking produced twelve hours of crisis response for a parent attending a school event. The frame ruptures: her mind cannot tolerate ambiguity. It fills silence with catastrophe. She sits with the relief and notices how much of her week the overthinking consumed. The silence was never information. It was absence. Her mind cannot tell the difference.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Marcus receives a vague email from a client. Heart rate spikes. Hands go cold. The false alarm fires its full sequence: they are leaving, the contract is dead, the revenue is gone. Marcus sits with the alarm for thirty seconds. Does not respond. Does not draft the crisis email. Opens the message. Reads it. The client wants to discuss expanding the contract. Marcus closes the email and notices the alarm is still running even though the information is positive. The alarm is not about the email. It is about the wiring. Marcus knows the wiring now. Lets it run. Responds to the email, not the alarm.

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren receives a legal letter. Her hands shake. Her chest compresses. Every false alarm in her history fires simultaneously: this is the one, this is real, this will end everything. She opens the letter with trembling hands. Reads every word. It is a standard trademark notice — routine, not hostile. Her body is still in full alarm. She sets the letter down. Picks up the phone. Calls the lawyer. The alarm is screaming. She dials anyway. The action is not brave. It is practiced. She has learned to act inside the alarm instead of waiting for the alarm to stop. The alarm does not stop. She acts anyway. That is the identity.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Sasha tracks the false alarm's activation pattern. Every ambiguous input — a vague email, an unexpected call, a delayed response — enters the same processing pipeline: assume worst case, activate body, prepare for crisis. The pipeline does not have a branch for neutral or positive inputs. Everything routes through worst case. Sasha's chest tightens. She has been running a system with one pathway. The pathway is threat. The mechanism is not broken. It is limited. It has one road. Every traveler takes that road because there is no other road. Her hands warm slightly as she sees the bottleneck. The solution is not faster processing. It is more roads.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Wei identifies the false alarm's ultimate function: it keeps her in a perpetual state of readiness that prevents rest, reflection, or joy. The alarm is not protecting her from danger. It is protecting her from vulnerability. Rest is vulnerable. Joy is vulnerable. Reflection reveals pain. The alarm keeps her activated so she never has to be still. Wei's chest pounds. The mechanism is not about threat detection. It is about stillness avoidance. The alarm is a movement machine. As long as she is responding to alarms, she does not have to sit with herself.
