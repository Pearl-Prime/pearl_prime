## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Caught in the spiral about that decision. Stop. Ask: can I change it today. If no, write it down. Let your mind rest.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
The what-ifs are running the show. Stand up. Walk around your space. Name five things you see. Table. Wall. Plant. This grounds you.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Before hitting send on something big, ground yourself. Close your eyes. Feel your chair. Feel your feet. The spiral always lies.
---
