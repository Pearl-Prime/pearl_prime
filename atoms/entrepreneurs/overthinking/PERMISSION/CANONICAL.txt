--- variant: v01
You're allowed to make a decision without certainty. You have permission to act on good-enough information rather than perfect information. The overthinking is searching for a guarantee that doesn't exist. You're allowed to stop the search and move with what you have.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to think less. That sounds counterintuitive for a founder, but the overthinking isn't adding value. It's adding anxiety disguised as thoroughness. You're allowed to trust the first instinct, make the call, and deal with what follows. That's not reckless. That's human.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to let some decisions be wrong. Not every choice is existential. The overthinking treats them all as if they are. You have permission to lower the stakes in your own mind, even when the stakes in reality are high. You'll make better calls from less pressure, not more.
