## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/watcher/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dante monitors every metric with the intensity of someone defusing a bomb. DAU. MRR. Churn. NPS. Each number gets fifteen minutes of analysis. His shoulders are by his ears. His breathing is shallow. He is watching the numbers the way a hypochondriac watches their heartbeat — constantly, fearfully, converting normal fluctuation into pathology. The frame shifts when his CTO says: the numbers are fine. You are not fine. Dante's chest loosens. The watching is not about the metrics. It is about the anxiety that lives between data points. He logs out of the dashboard. The metrics continue existing without his observation.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/watcher/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Lila's screen has seven analytics dashboards tiled across two monitors. She watches them update in real time. Every dip triggers analysis. Every spike triggers skepticism. Her eyes are dry. Her jaw is locked. She has not left this chair in nine hours. The watcher and the overthinking have fused: she is simultaneously monitoring everything and analyzing everything she monitors. The frame collapses: she is processing more data than her nervous system can hold. The watching is not producing insight. It is producing cortisol. She stands up. The dashboards continue their cycles without her. The numbers do not need a witness. They need an operator who sleeps.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/watcher/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren walks into the office and catches himself scanning faces. The watcher activates. Ren notices the activation — the neck tightening, the eye movement, the chest bracing. Then Ren chooses: today, I look at the work, not the faces. The faces are not data. They are people who are thinking about their own problems. Ren opens the laptop. Begins the first task. The watcher protests from the periphery. Ren types. The watcher fades to background noise. It is still there. Ren is louder.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/watcher/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Sasha is in the highest-stakes meeting of his career. Acquisition offer. Every face in the room matters. The watcher wants to scan every expression, decode every glance, interpret every throat-clear. Sasha looks at the term sheet. Just the term sheet. The faces are peripheral. The numbers are central. He reads the numbers. Asks a question about the numbers. The watcher screams that someone frowned. Sasha reads the next clause. The meeting proceeds on the content, not the facial theater. The watcher has lost the steering wheel. Sasha drives.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Wei identifies the watcher's energy source: uncertainty. The watcher does not activate on known threats. It activates on unknown ones. The space between data points is where the watcher lives. Between emails, between meetings, between check-ins — the gaps are the watcher's fuel. Wei's shoulders are rigid. She sees the mechanism: the watcher cannot be satisfied by information because information creates new gaps. Each answered question reveals two unanswered ones. The watcher is not searching for resolution. It is powered by the impossibility of resolution.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/entrepreneurs/anchored/overthinking/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Ava identifies the watcher's final form: it has consumed his capacity for trust. Trust requires the watcher to stand down. The watcher cannot stand down because standing down means being unguarded. Being unguarded means being betrayable. The mechanism is circular: the watcher prevents trust, the absence of trust validates the watcher, the validation strengthens the watcher. Ava's shoulders are granite. The cycle is self-sustaining. The watcher does not need external threats. It generates its own justification by preventing the relationships that would prove it unnecessary.
---
