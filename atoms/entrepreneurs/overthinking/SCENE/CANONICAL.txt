---

## SCENE v04
---

---
A meal with a co-founder, ostensibly relaxing, but you're both half-present. You're discussing the business because the business is what you both have in common. But underneath is monitoring—evaluating how the other person is holding up, whether they still believe, whether the stress is showing. You're never fully present because part of you is always scanning for threats.
