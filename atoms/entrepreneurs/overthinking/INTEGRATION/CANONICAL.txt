

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: STILL-HERE
You're the founder. The weight is real. The decisions matter. The thinking is happening. And you're also still here, still functioning, still moving forward even with the thinking in the background. The decision got made. You're executing on it. The thinking is running in the other thread. That's fine. You don't have to shut it off. You just have to not let it paralyze you.

## INTEGRATION v03
---
mode: BODY-LANDED
Your body is carrying the weight of the business. The tension is real. Your shoulders are tight. Your sleep is poor. Your nervous system is activated. The body is telling you something: this load is heavy. Listen to the body. It's not lying about the cost, even if your mind is in denial about it.

## INTEGRATION v05
---
mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
If a founder you respected was doing what you're doing—second-guessing a solid decision, unable to move past it—what would you tell them? You'd tell them: commit. Execute. The decision was good. The overthinking is the enemy now, not the decision. You'd tell them to trust themselves. Tell yourself the same thing.

## INTEGRATION v07
---
mode: COST-VISIBLE
Every hour you spend overthinking a past decision is an hour not spent building the future. You're bleeding time toward something immutable. The opportunity cost is enormous. See it. Let it drive your decision to stop.
