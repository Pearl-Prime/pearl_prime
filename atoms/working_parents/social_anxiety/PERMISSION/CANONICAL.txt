## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to cancel the plans. When your social energy has been consumed by small humans and conference calls before noon, canceling is not rudeness. It is an honest reading of what you have left.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel lonely and also not have the energy for people. Those two things coexist in working parenthood. Neither cancels the other, and neither means something is wrong with you.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to need connection and also need it to show up on your terms -- pajamas, couch, no babysitter required. The old version of social life was for the old version of you. This version deserves a format that fits.
---
