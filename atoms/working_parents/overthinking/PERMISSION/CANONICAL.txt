## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to have a brain that will not stop. When you are the project manager for both a career and a household, the vigilance is not irrational. It is the cognitive cost of being the only person holding the full picture.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to not solve it at 2 a.m. The problems circling your mind were designed for daylight, for conversation, for shared weight. Your brain running the night shift is not a flaw. It is a system that needs a second operator.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to be overwhelmed by the mental load without it meaning you are not smart enough or organized enough. The load was designed for a team. You have been carrying it solo, and the exhaustion is arithmetic, not inadequacy.
---
