## PIVOT v01
---

---
Your brain runs two simultaneous simulations -- one at the office, one at home -- and neither ever turns off. You call it overthinking. The pivot: it is not overthinking. It is dual-processing in a system that pretends you only have one job. The mental load is not a habit. It is a job requirement for a role nobody posts.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame says stop worrying so much. The new frame: you are the project manager for a household and an employee for a company, and both expect you to track every detail. The overthinking is not a personality flaw. It is the cognitive load of two full-time roles pretending to fit in one schedule.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You lie awake running through tomorrow -- the presentation, the school permission slip, the grocery list, the pediatrician follow-up. The old frame says you need to relax. The new frame: you are the single point of failure for an entire operation, and your brain knows it. The loop is not the problem. The single point of failure is.
