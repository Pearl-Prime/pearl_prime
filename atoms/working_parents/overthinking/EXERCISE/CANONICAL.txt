## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You just snapped at your kid.
Your mind is replaying it on.
They are fine. You are spiraling.
Stop. Stand. Move your body.
Do five jumping jacks or walk.
Physical movement stops the mental loop.
Return. Your child has likely moved.
You are the one still in.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
The argument with your partner runs.
You are in the next room.
Your brain cannot solve this thinking.
Interrupt: what is actually true now?
The children are safe. They're sleeping.
The argument happened. Now it's over.
You cannot change it by rehearsing.
Name three things in your present.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Your child said something that hurt.
You are replaying their words for.
Your brain is trying to problem.
Interrupt: that is your anxiety, not.
Your child loves you. That's true.
One harsh moment is not whole.
Move your body. Do something tactile.
Fold laundry. Wash dishes. Feel.
