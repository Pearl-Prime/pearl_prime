## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: BODY_LANDED
reframe_type: BODY_FACT
weight: standard
carry_line: "Your hands shook when you spoke too loud."
---
You snapped at your kid this morning. It was nothing. It was a small thing. Your hands shook a little when you realized what happened. Your voice came out sharper than you meant. They retreated. And now your hands are still tight thinking about it.

You're replaying the moment. The exact tone. Their face. The way they went quiet. You're trying to figure out if you damaged something or if they've already forgotten or if this is the moment you broke your own kid.

Your stomach carries this. Your shoulders carry this. Your body knows you did something you didn't want to do.

But the moment is done. They're in school. The damage is either done or it isn't and replaying it doesn't change it.

Notice your hands right now. Still. You're here. They're okay. The moment happened but the child survived the snapping.

That's what actually happened.
---

## INTEGRATION v02
---
mode: COST_VISIBLE
reframe_type: COST_UNCOUNTED
weight: standard
carry_line: "This anxiety hours add to the exhaustion."
---
You snapped because you're exhausted. But now you're exhausted from the guilt. The replay is costing you focus. The worry about the damage is costing you work time. The guilt is costing you peace.

You're a tired parent who got sharp with a kid and now you're spending the afternoon replaying it. The original moment was five seconds. The guilt is an entire afternoon.

Your kid is probably fine. They probably forgot. They probably just moved on to whatever's next. But you're still there, still replaying, still worried.

This is what the guilt costs you — the hours of replay you can't get back. Hours you could have used for actual work or rest instead of anxiety.

The snap happened. The guilt is something you're still doing.

## INTEGRATION v04

---

The sharp voice is one moment. The actual parenting is years and years of moments. One snapped sentence doesn't rewrite the whole thing.

You're the only one doing the full audit right now.
