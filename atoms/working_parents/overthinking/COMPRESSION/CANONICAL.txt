## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
The evening is when the rumination starts. The kids are asleep. The dishes are done. The brain that held it together all day starts running through what it skipped. Did you notice the change in your kid's tone at dinner. Did you respond well. Did you respond at all. The day is over. The audit is starting.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C5
Try this: each night, name one thing that worked. Not one thing you optimized. One thing that worked. The kid laughed at the silly thing. You caught the small win. The brain that audits tonight needs evidence the day held something good. Otherwise it will only catalogue what you missed.
