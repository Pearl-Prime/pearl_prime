BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Andre tries something after snapping at his kids over something small: instead of continuing to relitigate it through the rest of the evening, he names it once, out loud, to them — I was short with you, I'm sorry, that's on me — and lets the sentence end the loop rather than start another lap. It doesn't erase the moment. It gives him back the rest of the evening, which he'd otherwise have half-missed.

Tom sets a rule after a public parenting mistake at his kid's practice: the moment he notices himself replaying it mid-conversation with someone else, he says, silently, to himself, that already happened, and brings his attention back to the actual person in front of him.
