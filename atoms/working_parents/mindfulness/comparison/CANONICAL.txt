MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Julian tries something during a moment when another parent's apparent ease comes up in conversation: instead of letting his attention leave to run the comparison, he names it silently — that's their family, this is mine — and brings himself back to the actual moment with his own kid in front of him. He does this three times in one afternoon. Each time it works for about ninety seconds before the pull returns, but ninety seconds of actual presence, three times, is more than he had the afternoon before.
