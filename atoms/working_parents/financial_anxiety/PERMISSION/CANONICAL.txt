## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be terrified about money even if you have a good job. When childcare costs rival rent and the future gets more expensive every year, the terror is proportional. It is not about your earning. It is about the gap between earning and needing.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be angry that the financial setup penalizes working parents. The daycare costs, the lost promotions, the invisible tax on having children in a career economy -- your anger is not bitterness. It is accurate.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to talk about the money pressure without it meaning you regret your choices. You can love your family and hate the math that comes with them. Both things live in the same bank account.
