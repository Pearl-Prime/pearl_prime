

## HOOK v01
---

---
You're at the table with the childcare invoice and the mortgage statement side by side, breath shallow — you work to pay for care so you can work and the math loops without landing.
---

## HOOK v02
---

---
You pack the lunches and refresh the account in the same two minutes, and the number that has to cover both the field-trip fee and the gas is a number you have already counted three times today.
---

## HOOK v03
---

---
The daycare raises its rate by email, casually, and you read it twice standing in the kitchen, because somewhere in that paragraph is a piece of you that was supposed to be for something else.
---

## HOOK v04
---

---
Your kid asks for the thing the other kids have and you say maybe, brightly, while a quieter calculation runs underneath the word and lands, again, on not yet.
---

## HOOK v05
---

---
After bedtime you open the budget spreadsheet like a wound you keep checking, and the cells are honest in a way the daytime does not have room to be.
---
