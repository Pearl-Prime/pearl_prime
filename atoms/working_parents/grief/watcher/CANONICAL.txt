COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
The watcher slams into Keiko during the pediatrician's waiting room. Shoulders hunch. Every signal says the loss means what the surveillance mechanism claims — the absence that fills every room. Keiko holds still. The body burns. Skin prickles. The narrative falters because Keiko does not feed it the next link. For the first time, Keiko separates the sensation from the verdict. Lighter. Owned.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Tara's watcher about the loss runs hot. Spine stiffens. The mechanism is visible now: loss triggers the surveillance mechanism, the surveillance mechanism produces a verdict, the verdict intensifies the loss. The loop is closed and self-sustaining. Skin prickles. Tara sees the permission slip and the pattern fires again. Automatic. Relentless. The watcher is not insight. It is machinery. It needs no external evidence because it generates its own. Each cycle tightens the eye. Tara watches the loop complete itself and recognizes that the conviction it produces is manufactured, not earned. The surveillance mechanism is a closed system wearing the mask of truth.
