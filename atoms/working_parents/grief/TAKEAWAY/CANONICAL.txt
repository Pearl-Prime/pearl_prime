## TAKEAWAY v02

---
Working-parent grief lives in small moments -- the recital you watched on video, the first word you heard secondhand. Each one is tiny. Together they form a quiet ache nobody names.

## TAKEAWAY v03

---
Calling it a trade-off implies a choice. You did not choose between the meeting and the bedtime. You chose between the paycheck and the guilt. Those are not the same thing.
