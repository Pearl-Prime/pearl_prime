## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to grieve the moments you missed. The meeting ran long. The recital started without you. Neither failure defines you, and the ache you feel about it is not weakness. It is love registering a loss.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to miss the version of parenthood you thought you would have. The one with unhurried mornings and afternoons at the park. That version was real to you, and its absence deserves to be mourned.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel the loss even while being grateful for what you have. Gratitude and grief are not opposites. They are neighbors, and in working parenthood they share a wall.
---
