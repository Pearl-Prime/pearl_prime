## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to call what you do brave. Dropping off a crying child and walking into a conference room with a smile is not ordinary. It is a specific kind of courage that deserves to be named.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be proud of surviving this season even if it does not look like thriving. Survival under these conditions is not a consolation prize. It is the prerequisite for everything else.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to be exhausted by your own courage. The bravery of doing both every day does not come free. Its price is the tiredness you feel, and that tiredness does not make the bravery less real.
---
