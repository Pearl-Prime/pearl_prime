## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You need to ask your partner help.
The fear: you should be able manage.
That is a lie. Stand. Feel strength.
You are already doing two full-time.
Asking for help is not weakness here.
Say what you specifically need out loud.
"I need you to manage bedtime Wednesday."
Then ask. You have earned this request.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Your boss wants to discuss schedule flexibility.
You want to ask for part-time.
Fear: they will say no or judge.
Remember: you have done this job.
Place both feet on the ground right.
You are asking for what family needs.
That is courage. That is strength here.
Ask for what would actually work for.

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You want to decline an optional commitment.
Guilt arrives because you always say yes.
Feel your chest. That is your boundary.
Your time has real limits. Say that.
"I cannot take this on right now."
You are not failing at work here.
You are being honest about capacity now.
Your limit is an act of courage.
