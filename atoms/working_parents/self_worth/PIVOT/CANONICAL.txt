## PIVOT v01
---

---
Your worth got split between two identities -- the professional one and the parent one -- and neither feels like it gets enough of you to count. The old frame says you need to do more. The new frame: you are doing the work of two people and calling yourself half of each. That math was always wrong.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You measure yourself at work by the standard of someone without bedtime duty and at home by the standard of someone without a commute. Neither ruler fits. The pivot: your worth is not in how you compare. It is in the fact that you show up for both when nobody designed a life that accommodates both.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
The old frame says your value is in what you produce -- deliverables and well-adjusted children, ideally both on schedule. The new frame: you are a whole person underneath the roles. The self-worth that survives is the one that does not need a performance metric.
---
