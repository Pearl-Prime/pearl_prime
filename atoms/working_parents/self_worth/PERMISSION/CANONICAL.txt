## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to matter to yourself with the same urgency you matter to your kids and your employer. Your needs are not a luxury. They are the foundation the entire operation depends on.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to want something that has nothing to do with being a good parent or a good employee. A desire that exists because you are a person. That wanting is not selfish. It is proof you are still in there.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel invisible and still insist on being seen. The working-parent life runs on your labor -- paid and unpaid -- and the least it owes you is acknowledgment. Starting with your own.
