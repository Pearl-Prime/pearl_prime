## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/working_parents/anchored/self_worth/shame/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tara stands in the kitchen counter at 6 AM. Throat constricts. The shame fires hard — the doubt about deserving to exist as-is, and the shame mechanism demanding its usual conclusion. Tara catches it mid-sequence. Vision drops to the floor. The value questioning is real. The body holds both the intensity and a new recognition: the shame converts action into identity verdict, but the conversion is not truth. It is habit. Tara feels the heat in the chest and does not follow the narrative to its endpoint. The frame cracks. Tara checks the babysitter's text. Something underneath the old interpretation breathes. The shame still fires. The story it tells no longer lands as fact.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/working_parents/anchored/self_worth/shame/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Everything peaks at once. Shoulders cave. Javier cannot outrun the shame. The value questioning fills every cell — the doubt about deserving to exist as-is. The body reaches maximum intensity. Muscles contract inward. In the center of the crisis, Javier sees the entire structure of the shame mechanism. Sees how it manufactures the verdict from raw sensation. Sees that the verdict is manufactured, not discovered. The frame breaks at its foundation. The value questioning remains but the shame's interpretation of it falls away. Javier stands in the wreckage and does not rebuild the old structure. Cannot. Will not.
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Keiko enters the intensity with open eyes. Stomach drops to the floor. The shame demands the old response to the value questioning — the doubt about deserving to exist as-is. Keiko gives it a new one. Acts from self-knowledge, not from the shame mechanism. The body registers the heat. Blood rushes to the face. Keiko registers the choice. Sees the stack of unfolded laundry and responds as the person who survived seeing the pattern, not as the person the pattern describes. Identity belongs to Keiko now. Not to the shame. The body still feels everything. Keiko no longer translates feeling into the shame's verdict. The translation stops here.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/working_parents/anchored/self_worth/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The shame fires at high intensity. Gut twists. Javier traces the mechanism in real time — the doubt about deserving to exist as-is. The value questioning feeds the shame mechanism. The shame mechanism produces certainty about failure. The certainty deepens the value questioning. Vision drops to the floor. Round and round. Javier sees the half-eaten dinner and watches the loop complete itself without any new information entering. The pattern is self-sustaining. It needs no evidence because it treats its own output as input. Javier puts the phone face-down. The shame is the verdict that the shame converts action into identity verdict. Seeing the conversion does not stop it. But the seeing changes what the conviction means.
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2

COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization

Everything at maximum. Skin crawls. The shame completes its loop about the value questioning in seconds — the doubt about deserving to exist as-is. Muscles contract inward. Sensation to narrative to verdict to deeper sensation. Keiko tracks each conversion in real time. The shame mechanism operates at peak efficiency during crisis. This is when the pattern is most powerful — and most transparent. Every gear visible. Every false conclusion traceable to its source in sensation. Keiko scans the school email. The verdict completes another cycle. Keiko sees it complete. The seeing does not stop the machinery. The seeing changes who is operating it.
