## PIVOT v01
---

---
The old frame says working-parent anxiety means you are not handling it well. The new frame: you are handling two full-time roles that were never designed to be held by one person. The anxiety is not a failure of coping. It is an accurate reading of an impossible job description.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You think the worry means something is wrong with you. But when the meeting runs long and the daycare closes at six and the gap between those two facts is your child waiting, the worry is doing math -- not malfunctioning. The pivot: this is not your anxiety. This is the system's anxiety, and you are the one absorbing it.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You were taught that good parents do not worry this much and good employees do not get distracted. You are both, and the anxiety lives in the gap between those two roles. The new frame: the gap is not yours to close. It was built into a world that never planned for you to be both.
