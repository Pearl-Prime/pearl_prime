## EXERCISE v01
---

---
A work call just ended. Kids need.
Your body is still in stress mode.
Feet on ground. Name three things hear.
Voices. Traffic. Breathing. Anchor to present.
Inhale for four. Exhale for six.
One breath. That is all you need.
You are safe. Your kids are safe.
Reset complete. You can shift modes.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You are in the car picking up.
Work stress is living in your body.
Hands tight on wheel. Shoulders by ears.
Stop. Shake your hands hard at light.
Feel the release. Breathe out work stress.
Rolling down window. Cold air on face.
You are transitioning. Work is separate now.
Meet your child from a present place.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Work anxiety is making you snap.
Pause before reacting to their complaint.
Feel your feet on the floor here.
Count three things: child. Breathing. This room.
Your anxiety is about work, not them.
Take one slow breath before you respond.
Return to your face and your voice.
Your child needs you present, not perfect.
---
