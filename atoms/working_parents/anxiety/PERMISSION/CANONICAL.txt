## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be anxious without it meaning you are failing at either job. The worry exists because you are doing two things that each demand your full attention. That is not a coping problem. It is a math problem.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel the anxiety without fixing it before the next meeting or the next pickup. Sitting with it does not make you less competent. It makes you honest.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to name the worry out loud -- to a partner, a friend, a therapist -- without it meaning you cannot handle your life. Handling it and struggling with it coexist. They always have.
---
