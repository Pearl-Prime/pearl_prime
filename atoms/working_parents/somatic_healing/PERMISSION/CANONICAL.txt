## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to have a body that aches even when nothing is clinically wrong. The scans cannot see the weight of dual roles sitting in your neck and shoulders. The pain is real. It just does not photograph well.

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to need healing that is not productive. Not stretching so you can work better. Not meditating so you can parent better. Healing that exists because you are a body that has been absorbing impact and deserves repair.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to listen to the tension, the headache, the fatigue. They are not interruptions to your schedule. They are the one part of you that cannot fake being fine, and that honesty deserves your attention.
