## PIVOT v01
---

---
Your neck has been tight since the baby was born. Your jaw clenches during the commute. The tension headache starts at pickup time like clockwork. The old frame says these are stress symptoms to manage. The new frame: your body has been absorbing the collision between work and parenthood, and it is showing you where the impact lands.

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The tests say you are fine. But the exhaustion that sleep does not fix, the shoulder pain that stretching does not reach, the stomach that knots before Monday morning -- those are not nothing. They are the body's record of what it costs to hold two worlds together with no seam showing.

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You have been carrying your child on one hip and your career on the other. The body eventually responds to that asymmetry. Somatic healing is not about relaxation. It is about acknowledging that your body has been the shock absorber for a structural problem, and it deserves more than being told to stretch.
