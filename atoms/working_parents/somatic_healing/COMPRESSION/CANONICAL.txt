

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Lower your shoulders every time you notice them raised. Small practice. Nervous system relearning. The body records what the mind edits out. The record doesn't disappear just because it isn't being read. The tension that lives in the body as posture, as bracing, as held breath — it arrived for a reason. It stays for the same reason.

Your body learned to be useful. Picking up the kid. Carrying the grocery bags up three flights. Holding the squirming toddler while answering the work call. The body was never not needed. It also stopped getting consulted. The body is now sending invoices for years of unconsulted use.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
The lower back. The shoulder. The neck. The hip. Each of these holds a piece of the daily choreography. The pickup. The car-seat lift. The crouch. The stairs at midnight with a sleeping kid. The body does not file complaints in real time. It accumulates. The accumulation is what eventually gets your attention.
