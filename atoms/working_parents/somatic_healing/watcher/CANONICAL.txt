MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tara stands in the grocery aisle. Spine stiffens. The watcher fires hard — the pain the body holds when the mind cannot, and the surveillance mechanism demanding its usual conclusion. Tara catches it mid-sequence. Muscles freeze mid-motion. The body-stored tension is real. The body holds both the intensity and a new recognition: the watcher converts experience into performance review, but the conversion is not truth. It is habit. Tara feels the heat in the chest and does not follow the narrative to its endpoint. The frame cracks. Tara packs the bag one-handed. Something underneath the old interpretation breathes. The watcher still fires. The story it tells no longer lands as fact.

---
The watcher fires at high intensity. Eyes dart. Javier traces the mechanism in real time — the pain the body holds when the mind cannot. The body-stored tension feeds the surveillance mechanism. The surveillance mechanism produces certainty about failure. The certainty deepens the body-stored tension. Muscles freeze mid-motion. Round and round.
