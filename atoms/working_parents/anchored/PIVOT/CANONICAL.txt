## PIVOT v01
---

---
The old frame says balance is the goal. But balance implies a still point, and nothing about working parenthood is still. The new frame: you are not balanced. You are anchored. There is a difference -- anchored things still move with the waves. They just do not drift out to sea.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
You have been told to find your center. But your center moves -- between the office and the school, between the deadline and the bedtime story. The pivot: being anchored does not mean standing still. It means knowing what you will not let go of no matter how hard the current pulls.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Everyone is selling you equilibrium. The truth is your life does not have equal sides. Some weeks the work wins. Some weeks the kids win. The reframe: anchored does not mean even. It means there is something at your core that holds steady even when the proportions shift.
---
