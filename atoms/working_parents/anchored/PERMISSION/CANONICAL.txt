## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to have a week where work wins and a week where the kids win. Anchored does not mean even. It means the core holds. The proportions will always shift, and that shifting does not make you a bad parent or a bad professional.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to stop chasing balance. The people selling you equilibrium have never lived your schedule. What you have -- messy, uneven, stubbornly held together -- is enough.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to feel anchored and overwhelmed at the same time. Knowing what matters does not make the days easier. It just makes sure the hard days do not drift into the wrong priorities.
---
