## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to run out of patience by 6 p.m. without it meaning you are a bad parent. You spent eight hours performing care for other people's problems. The tank does not magically refill in the car.

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel nothing when your child shows you the drawing. The numbness does not mean the love is gone. It means the emotional bandwidth was already spent before you got home.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to need a transition between work-you and parent-you. The expectation that you walk through the door fully present is designed for someone who had a different kind of day than you did.
