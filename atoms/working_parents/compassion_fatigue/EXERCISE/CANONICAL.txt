## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You have been absorbed in everyone's today.
Kids' feelings. Coworkers' feelings. Your partner's too.
Your nervous system is carrying emotional weight.
Place both hands on your own chest.
Feel your heartbeat. This is your body.
Shake your hands gently. Release their feelings.
Say: "That is their feeling."
You can care and still protect yourself.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
A crying kid. Demanding work. Nothing left.
You are empty because you poured out.
Cold water on your wrists slowly.
Feel the temperature wake up your senses.
Your body is yours. Not a vessel.
Notice: you still exist after caring.
You have emotional reserves if you protect.
Step away for two minutes to restore.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
The day is over. You absorbed much.
Everyone's needs. Everyone's emotions. Everyone's expectations.
Imagine pouring out everything you are holding.
Feel the space opening inside your body.
Place one hand on your heart.
Say: "I can only carry mine."
Their emotional journey is not your burden.
You can love them and have boundaries.
---
