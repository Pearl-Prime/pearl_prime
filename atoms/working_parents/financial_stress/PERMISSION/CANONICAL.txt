## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be stressed about money even when the bills are paid. Paid and sustainable are different things, and your body knows the difference even when the bank statement does not show it.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to ask for help with the financial load -- from a partner, a family member, a professional. Using support is not failure. It is what people do when the weight exceeds what one person was designed to carry.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to stop pretending the numbers work when they do not. The performance of financial ease costs you sleep and honesty and peace of mind. You deserve to put that performance down.
---
