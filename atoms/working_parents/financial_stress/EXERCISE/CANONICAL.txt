## EXERCISE v01
---

---
The bill for medical visit arrived.
Your nervous system spikes. Too much.
Breathe. Slow down. You cannot solve.
Count to four in. Count six out.
Which bills are actually urgent this week?
List them. Separate from catastrophic thinking.
Choose one action. Just one. Do it.
Your system needs time to manage numbers.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You are checking the account compulsively now.
Each check sends your body into mode.
Stop checking. You have checked enough times.
The number will not change in.
Walk away from the screen right now.
Move your body. Feel something other.
Your nervous system cannot think straight.
Return when you have calmed down.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Financial stress is tightening your body.
Shoulders up. Jaw clenched. Fists tight.
This is your nervous system responding.
Shake your entire body hard for ten.
Feel the movement. Notice the release.
Now breathe. What is the one.
Not all of it. Just step.
Do that step. The rest tomorrow.
