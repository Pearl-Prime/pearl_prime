### EXERCISE v04: The Clinical Judgment Trust
*(Tailored to your Imposter Syndrome & Dependence on Authority)*

---
You encounter a patient situation that feels ambiguous.
Your impulse: Check the protocol. Ask the doctor. Verify.
Pause. You have thirty years of pattern recognition.
The protocol is a guide, not a replacement for eyes.
What does your gut say before you look it up?
Name one observation that only you noticed.
Trust that observation. It is data, not guesswork.
You are not an imposter. You are experienced.
Act on your judgment. Document why.
