Because admitting you were finite felt like admitting you weren't "good enough."

You are spending your internal currency faster than you can earn it back, borrowing against a future self who may not have the strength to pay the debt. The mechanism tells you that if you stop giving, you disappear. But the opposite is true. If you continue this infinite descent, you *will* disappear. You are allowed to be finite. You are allowed to have limits. In fact, your limits are what make your giving real. When you give from a place of abundance, it is a gift. When you give from a place of depletion without floor, it is a sacrifice that costs you your soul.
