## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
The shame fires at high intensity. Gut twists. Javier traces the mechanism in real time — the erosion of every line drawn. The limit dissolution feeds the shame mechanism. The shame mechanism produces certainty about failure. The certainty deepens the limit dissolution. Blood rushes to the face. Round and round. Javier sees the laptop left open on the dining table and watches the loop complete itself without any new information entering. The pattern is self-sustaining. It needs no evidence because it treats its own output as input. Javier puts the phone face-down. The shame is the verdict that the shame converts action into identity verdict. Seeing the conversion does not stop it. But the seeing changes what the conviction means.
