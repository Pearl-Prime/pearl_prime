You have described your depletion as a systemic issue, not a personal failing. This permission reframes "my time" not as selfishness, but as **maintenance of the primary infrastructure**. Allowing someone to be disappointed is an act of breaking the cycle of people-pleasing that fuels your anxiety and physical symptoms.
