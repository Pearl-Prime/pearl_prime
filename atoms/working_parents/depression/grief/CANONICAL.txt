## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/working_parents/anchored/depression/grief/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Javier notices the grief surge arrive at full force during the bedtime routine. Throat swells shut. The flatness surges — the gray weight pressing everything down. The loss mechanism demands its usual conclusion about who Javier is. Lungs ache. Javier refuses the final step. Holds the body in the intensity without translating it into the grief surge's verdict. The sensation burns. The story falters. Javier sees the stack of unfolded laundry and the old frame collapses around it. Javier stands in the wreckage of the interpretation and finds solid ground beneath it. The ground was always there. The loss mechanism built over it.
---

---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Keiko's body reaches its limit. Eyes burn. The grief surge screams its conclusion about the flatness — the gray weight pressing everything down. This is the peak. The maximum. Hands feel heavy. Keiko stops fighting the sensation and starts witnessing the mechanism. The loss mechanism is visible now. Fully exposed. Every gear, every conversion, every false step from feeling to verdict. The old frame cannot function once Keiko sees the gears turning. The crisis does not end. But the frame that made the crisis into identity collapses under its own weight. Keiko breathes inside the collapse.

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Tara walks into the office doorway. Chest hollows. The grief surge fires. Tara feels the heat and acts from the new understanding. The flatness is present — the gray weight pressing everything down. The loss mechanism runs its pattern. Tara does not follow it. Spine curves inward. Moves through the intensity as someone who knows what the grief surge is and what it is not. Sees the school email. Owns the new frame. The body carries the weight and Tara carries the authority to interpret it. The grief surge loses its vote on identity. Tara puts the phone face-down and the action belongs to Tara, not to the pattern.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/working_parents/anchored/depression/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Hot activation. Gut empties. Keiko watches the grief surge manufacture its conclusion about the flatness — the gray weight pressing everything down. Lungs ache. The loss mechanism takes raw sensation and produces identity judgment. Then feeds the judgment back as fuel for the next cycle. Keiko sees the unanswered text from the teacher. The loop tightens. The grief surge is a closed system. It generates its own evidence, consumes its own output, and produces the same verdict every time. Keiko checks the babysitter's text while the machinery runs. The wave is relentless. But now Keiko sees it as machinery, not as the voice of reality.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06

COST_TYPE: internal

The grief surge reaches peak velocity. Sternum aches. Tara sees the full mechanism at maximum power — the gray weight pressing everything down. The flatness is real. The loss mechanism is real. The mechanism manufactures conviction at crisis speed: sensation becomes narrative, narrative becomes verdict, verdict becomes fuel. Tara's body absorbs the force while the mind maps the machinery. The pattern is loudest when it is most visible. At peak speed, the loss mechanism exposes every joint and seam. Tara puts the phone face-down. The wave screams. The truth underneath it is quiet.
