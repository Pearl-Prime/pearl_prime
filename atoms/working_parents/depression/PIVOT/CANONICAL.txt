## PIVOT v01
---

---
You call it being drained or running on fumes. But the flatness that arrives before the alarm and follows you through the carpool has a different name. Depression in working parents hides behind busyness. You can be deeply depressed and still make every meeting and every school lunch. Functioning is not the same as being okay.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
The old frame says if you were really depressed you would not be getting things done. The new frame: your depression is wearing a productivity costume. The tasks get completed. The joy that used to live inside them walked out months ago and nobody noticed because the output stayed the same.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
You feel guilty for feeling nothing when your kid shows you their drawing. The guilt proves the caring is still there. The nothing proves the system is overtaxed. The pivot: depression for you is not about giving up. It is about the cost of never being allowed to stop.
---
