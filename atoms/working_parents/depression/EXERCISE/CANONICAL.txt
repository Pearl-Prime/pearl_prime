## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Everything feels gray. The kids are.
You can hear them playing. Feel nothing.
No energy. No joy. Just emptiness.
You do not have to be today.
Sit somewhere they cannot see you.
Feel the chair supporting you from beneath.
You are still alive. That is enough.
Tomorrow may be different. Today you exist.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You are going through the motions.
Feeding. Dressing. Playing. No joy anywhere.
You are not broken. You're exhausted.
One small thing: drink water. That's.
Not for kids. For your own body.
Notice you still exist after serving.
Your depression will pass. You will.
Just do the next small thing now.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Parenting feels pointless. Work feels pointless.
You have not slept well in weeks.
Depression lies. So does sleep deprivation.
One act: lie down for five.
Not productive. Not for kids. For you.
Feel your body supported by mattress.
You will return to yourself slowly.
This heaviness does not last forever.
---
