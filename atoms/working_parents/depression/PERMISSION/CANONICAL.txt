## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel empty after a full day. That emptiness is not ingratitude. It is the emotional invoice for a day that asked for everything and offered no space to feel any of it.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be depressed and still show up for your family. Depression does not disqualify you from good parenthood. Some of the best parents you know are doing it while feeling nothing, and that deserves compassion, not judgment.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to not be okay right now. Not after-the-project okay, not after-the-summer okay. Right now. Your children can handle your honesty better than they can handle your slow disappearance.
