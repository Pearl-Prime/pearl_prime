## MECHANISM_PROOF v05

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Tara's overwhelm about the flatness runs hot. Skull presses. The mechanism is visible now: flatness triggers the flooding mechanism, the flooding mechanism produces a verdict, the verdict intensifies the flatness. The loop is closed and self-sustaining. Ears ring. Tara sees the school email and the pattern fires again. Automatic. Relentless. The overwhelm is not insight. It is machinery. It needs no external evidence because it generates its own. Each cycle tightens the wave. Tara watches the loop complete itself and recognizes that the conviction it produces is manufactured, not earned. The flooding mechanism is a closed system wearing the mask of truth.
