## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Kids in bed. You have silence.
You do not need to check email.
You do not need to clean anything.
Sit. Feel your body in chair.
Breathe slowly. Let your shoulders drop down.
This silence is not wasted time.
Rest is the task. That is everything.
You will parent better tomorrow from rest.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You are running on empty for weeks.
Permission: something does not get done.
The dishes can wait. The email waits.
Your body and mind need actual rest.
Choose one thing you are releasing now.
Not forever. Just for today or tomorrow.
Your presence with kids is the priority.
Everything else is secondary to functioning.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Both jobs are demanding everything at once.
You cannot pour from an empty cup.
After bedtime: do the minimum only.
Make tea. Sit. Let your mind go.
No planning. No productivity. No forward thinking.
Just being alive right now is work.
Your kids need you rested more.
Give yourself permission to stop being productive.
---
