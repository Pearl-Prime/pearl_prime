## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to be burned out even though you love your kids and your career. Burnout is not ingratitude. It is what happens when two full-time jobs share one body and zero recovery time.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to say I cannot do this today without it meaning you cannot do this at all. One bad day is not a verdict. It is a signal.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to need help even though you have always been the one who handles everything. The people around you can absorb more than you think -- but only if you let them see what you are carrying.
---
