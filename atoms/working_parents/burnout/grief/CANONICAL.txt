COST_INTENSITY: 3

---
The grief surge fires at high intensity. Throat swells shut. Javier traces the mechanism in real time — the depletion that rest does not fix. The exhaustion accumulation feeds the loss mechanism. The loss mechanism produces certainty about failure. The certainty deepens the exhaustion accumulation. Spine curves inward. Round and round. Javier sees the calendar with double-bookings and watches the loop complete itself without any new information entering. The pattern is self-sustaining.
