## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to feel out of your depth in both roles. The job description for working parent was never written because it cannot be done by one person. You are not inadequate. The role is impossible. You are doing it anyway.

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to show up at the meeting with a sick-kid text buzzing in your pocket and still call yourself a professional. Your divided attention does not make your contributions less valuable. It makes them harder won.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to not have it all together. Nobody does. The ones who look like they do are either pretending or have resources you do not see. Your imperfect version is not lesser. It is more honest.
