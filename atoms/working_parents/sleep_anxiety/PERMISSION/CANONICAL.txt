## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to dread bedtime. When the night is the only window where both jobs debrief at once, dreading it is rational. You are not failing at sleep. You are carrying a dual-track day that gives you no time to process until the lights go off.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to be angry that you cannot rest without the responsibilities following you. Sleep was supposed to be neutral territory. It is not your fault that both jobs set up camp there.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to take the midnight thoughts seriously without acting on them. They are not emergencies. They are the unprocessed material of a life that runs on zero margin.
