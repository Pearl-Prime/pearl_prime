## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Kids are finally asleep. Exhausted.
Your brain is running tomorrow's schedule.
You cannot sleep while planning ahead.
Acknowledge: tomorrow will happen without you.
Close your eyes. Feel your pillow.
Name five things you hear right.
No planning. Just sounds. Just.
Your nervous system will calm down.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Bedtime. Your body is tired. Brain.
Worried about the sick kid. Meeting.
About whether the sitter was okay.
Stop. Ground in your body right.
Tense every muscle in your body.
Hold five seconds. Everything. Maximum.
Release. Feel the heaviness in limbs.
Your body knows how to rest.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You are lying down but mind.
Child who needs something. Work waits.
Everything that could go wrong playing.
Your nervous system is in threat.
Place both hands on chest.
Slow breath. Long exhale. Slow.
Your children are safe and sleeping.
The threat your mind is making.
---
