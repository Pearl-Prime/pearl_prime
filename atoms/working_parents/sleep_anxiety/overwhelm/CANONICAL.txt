BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Javier notices the overwhelm arrive at full force during the grocery aisle. Lungs refuse full breath. The sleep dread surges — the fear of the dark hours. The flooding mechanism demands its usual conclusion about who Javier is. Ears ring. Javier refuses the final step. Holds the body in the intensity without translating it into the overwhelm's verdict. The sensation burns. The story falters. Javier sees the laptop left open on the dining table and the old frame collapses around it. Javier stands in the wreckage of the interpretation and finds solid ground beneath it. The ground was always there. The flooding mechanism built over it.

Keiko breathes inside the collapse.

Tara sets a timer for the meeting and the action belongs to Tara, not to the pattern.
