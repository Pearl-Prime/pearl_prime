
## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: replenishment_practice
reframe_type: giving_to_receiving
---
This week, before you respond to anyone's need, you check your own first. Not as a rule you follow perfectly but as a question you ask: what am I starting from? Full, half-full, running on empty?

If you are running on empty: respond briefly, honestly, and without performance. "I care about you and I'm not in a place to hold much tonight" is a complete and honest sentence. It preserves the relationship. It protects you.

If you have something: offer it from what is actually there. The quality of presence you can give from a real reserve is more useful than an extended performance from a depleted one.

At the end of the week: identify one thing that replenished you, even slightly. A walk. A meal. Thirty minutes without being needed. Double it next week. You cannot give from empty indefinitely. Refilling is not selfishness. It is maintenance of the person other people rely on.
