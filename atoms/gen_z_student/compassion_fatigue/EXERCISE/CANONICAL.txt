
## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before you respond to the next message asking for something, pause for thirty seconds. Place one hand on your chest. Ask yourself: do I have something real to offer right now, or am I about to borrow from a deficit? The answer informs the response. If you have nothing, a brief honest reply is more sustainable than a full performance.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Write down three things you received today that were not about someone else's needs. A good meal. Quiet. A lecture you found interesting. A text from someone checking on you. If the list is empty, that is the data. Your week has been outflow without inflow. That imbalance has a direction.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
For one hour today, your phone goes on silent and face-down. You are not on call. You are not available. The hour belongs to something that restores rather than depletes. Notice the guilt that surfaces during the hour. The guilt is not an emergency. The hour is an experiment. Let it finish.
