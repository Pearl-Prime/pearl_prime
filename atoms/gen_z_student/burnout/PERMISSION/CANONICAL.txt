## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You have permission to do less this week. Drop one obligation that carries no grade. Skip the optional lecture. Cancel the coffee meeting. Your calendar does not own your cortisol. Delete one entry. Feel the space open in your chest. Protect it. Fill it with nothing.

You have permission to lie on the couch without producing anything. Your body resting flat is not your body wasting time. Your muscles release. Your heart rate drops. Your nervous system repairs. The couch is a recovery station, not a failure monument. Lie there without guilt.

You have permission to reply to the messages tomorrow. Your friends survived before your reply. They survive after. One day of silence costs nothing. Your thumbs rest. Your screen stays dark. The social debt carries zero interest. Pay it when your balance replenishes. Not before.

You have permission to eat without earning the meal. Your body requires fuel regardless of output. You did not write enough today. Eat anyway. Your cells divide, your blood circulates, your lungs expand. Those processes earned the meal. Sit down. Eat the warm food. Your body does not bill by productivity.

You have permission to cry in the shower. The water covers the sound. The heat loosens your chest. The tears carry cortisol out of your body through a biological exit route. Crying is not breaking down. It is draining the overflow. Your eyes clear afterward. Your breath deepens. Let the water do its work.

You have permission to tell one person you are burned out. Three words: "I am spent." The sentence costs almost nothing to speak. Your throat releases. Your shoulders drop. The secret depletes you faster than the burnout itself. Name it to someone safe. The naming halves the weight on the spot.

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You have permission to skip the meal prep ritual and order takeout. Open the app. Tap the order. Feel your thumb release. The pretty containers are a wellness performance. Calories are the actual goal. Get them by any route.

You have permission to take a sick day from class without medical proof. Email the professor: "I am unwell." Press send. Most professors do not require a doctor's note. The note culture trained you to over-document. Stop documenting.

You have permission to attend zero events this weekend. Open your calendar. Decline everything. Feel the empty grid open up. The empty weekend is a balance-sheet correction. Spending you cannot afford has been the default setting.

You have permission to use the elevator instead of the stairs. Press the button. Feel the cab rise. Your daily step count is not the metric your burned-out body needs to hit. Save the energy for the work that requires it.

You have permission to wear yesterday's clothes to a 9 AM class. Pull on the same hoodie. Smell it briefly. The dress code in your head is harsher than the actual dress code. The shirt is fine. Your body needs the morning the laundry took.

You have permission to skip your usual workout for a week. Lay down on the floor. Feel the ground hold you. Movement helps when the system has fuel. With no fuel, movement extracts what is not there. Rest first. Move when reserves return.

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You have permission to email your advisor and say "I need to reduce my course load." Sit at the desk. Type the words. Most schools have a partial-withdrawal option. The option exists because students like you needed it before. Use it.

You have permission to tell your boss you cannot take a shift this week. Stand by your locker. Open the schedule. The shift gets covered. The covering is somebody's job, not yours. Their schedule is not the priority. Your survival is.

You have permission to call the campus mental health line at 2 AM. Pick up the phone. Dial. The line exists for the hour you cannot make it through. The person on the other end is paid to be there. Use the resource that exists.

You have permission to spend a Saturday in bed and call it medicine. Pull the covers up. Feel them settle. Bed-as-medicine is a dosage your body is currently prescribing. The prescription does not require validation from anyone outside the bedroom.

You have permission to break up with the gym membership. Cancel online. Click confirm. Feel the small relief in your chest. The membership was guilt as monthly autopay. Cancelling does not end fitness. It ends the bill for fitness you did not use.

You have permission to apply for an incomplete in your hardest class. Walk to the registrar. Fill out the form. The incomplete is a finite extension. It buys your body the recovery weeks the semester denied you. Use the mechanism the institution built for this.
