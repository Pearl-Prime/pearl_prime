Week twelve hits and the screen blurs. Jordan stares at the blinking cursor on a half-finished essay, fingers locked above the keyboard. The library empties around her. Her chest tightens. ---

## HOOK v03
---

---
The caffeine hits but the focus never follows. Priya sits in the campus coffee shop, laptop burning her thighs, and reads the same sentence for the ninth time. Her fingers twitch on the trackpad. The hollowness behind her sternum spreads. Midterms ended two weeks ago. Finals start in ten days. Nothing in between feels real.

## HOOK v04
---

---
Two hundred unread notifications. Kai scrolls past every one. The bus lurches toward campus and his stomach lurches with it. His shoulders lock against the seat. He wrote four words on his thesis draft this morning. Four. His temples pound. The semester grinds forward and his body drags behind it like dead weight.

## HOOK v05
---

---
Sana sets three alarms and sleeps through all of them. She wakes at noon, heart hammering, mouth dry. The assignment deadline passed at midnight. Her phone screen floods with group chat messages she cannot open. Her hands shake as she reaches for the laptop. The lid stays closed. Her arms drop to her sides.

## HOOK v06
---

---
The library fluorescents buzz at one a.m. and Devon cannot feel his legs. He sat down at six. Seven hours and ninety words. His neck locked two hours ago. The campus empties outside the window. His vision tunnels to a single blinking cursor on a white page that stays white.

## HOOK v07
---

---
Lena types a sentence. Deletes it. Types it again. Deletes it again. The loop runs for forty minutes before she notices. Her throat tightens. The dorm desk lamp flickers. She grips the edge of the desk until her knuckles pale. Week twelve owns her now. Her brain runs on fumes and returns nothing.
---

## HOOK v08
---

---
The professor asks for questions and Theo's mouth stays shut. His tongue sits heavy and useless behind his teeth. He knew this material in September. Now the words dissolve before they reach his lips. His hands grip the desk edge. His chest caves inward. The lecture ends. He sits frozen while the room empties around him.
