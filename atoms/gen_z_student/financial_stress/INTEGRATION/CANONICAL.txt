
## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: resource_mapping
reframe_type: scarcity_to_strategy
---
This week, you map your resources instead of your worries. The map has two columns: what you have (income sources, financial aid, campus resources, people you can ask for help) and what you need (the actual non-negotiables, the upcoming costs, the gaps). The map is not a solution. It is a full picture, which is different from the partial picture the anxiety uses.

When the stress activates — notification, calculation, social situation where money is visible — you return to the map. The map is an actual account of your situation. The activation is a response to threat-feeling. They are not the same thing.

At the end of the week: notice one place where you managed the constraint without letting it manage you. You chose not to go to the expensive dinner and you did not spiral. You asked for the extension and the world did not end. You picked up the extra shift and the next week was easier. These moments are evidence that you are a person who operates inside real constraints with real skill. The skill is invisible to most people around you. It belongs to you.
