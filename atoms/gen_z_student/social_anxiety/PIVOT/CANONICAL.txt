## PIVOT v01
---

---
Your stomach drops at the door. Notice it. Name it. That drop is data, not destiny. Walk in anyway. Your body sends alerts — you decide which ones to obey.
---

## PIVOT v02
---

---
Your face flushes before you speak. The heat lies — it says danger where none exists. One sentence. Release it rough and imperfect. Perfection is the lock. "Good enough" is the key.
---

## PIVOT v03
---

---
Your voice thins in the group. So type it. Text it. Post it in the doc. Contribution has no single frequency. Show up in the channel that lets your brain breathe.
---

## PIVOT v04
---

---
Your hands grip the phone at the party door. Give them a different job. Hold a cup. Touch the wall. Small anchors break the circuit. The grip is habit, not need.
---

## PIVOT v05
---

---
The dining hall feels like a stadium. Shrink the mission. Tray, food, seat, eat. Four steps. Finish those four and leave. Scope is the lever that makes impossible rooms possible.
---

## PIVOT v06
---

---
Your voice thins and the moment passes. Write the thought down. Email the professor. Post after class. The conversation does not end when the room empties. Find the door that stays open.
---

## PIVOT v07
---

---
Your face flushes at the threshold. Set a timer — thirty minutes, then permission to leave. A boundary turns an open-ended threat into a contained experiment. Ninety minutes of dread shrinks to thirty.
---

## PIVOT v08
---

---
Your hands grip your phone under the table. Put one sentence in the group chat before the meeting starts. Arrive with words already on the record. That first message lowers the cost of the second.
---
