## HOOK v03

---
Mia closed the laptop, opened it, closed it again. Her professor's one-line email sat glowing on screen: "Let's discuss your paper." Four words. Her stomach dropped through the chair. She drafted nine replies in her Notes app. Sent none. Her fingers trembled against the trackpad.

---
