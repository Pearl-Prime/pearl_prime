## TAKEAWAY v01
---

---
Your worth does not fluctuate with your GPA. Press your palms together right now. Feel the heat between them. That warmth exists whether you ace the exam or bomb it. Your hands generate heat because you are alive, not because you performed.

Comparison borrows a metric that never belonged to your life. Plant both feet on the floor. Feel the solid ground hold you without checking your resume. The earth does not rank its inhabitants. Neither does your body.

A rejection email carries information, not identity. Place one hand on your chest. Your heart beats at its own rhythm. No admissions committee sets that tempo. Your pulse belongs to you alone.

The number of likes on a post measures attention, not substance. Grip the edge of your desk. Feel the hard surface push back against your fingers. Resistance proves presence. You exist beyond any screen.

Belonging does not require an audition. Rest your back against your chair. Feel the support press into your shoulder blades. The chair holds you without a transcript. So does the room. So does the day.

Achievement unlocks doors. It does not build the person who walks through them. Touch the base of your throat. Feel the small hollow there. You carried that body through every hard semester. That endurance speaks louder than any grade.
