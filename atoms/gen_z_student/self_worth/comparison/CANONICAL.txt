
## RECOGNITION v01

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Priya looks at the list of departmental award recipients and her name is not on it. A classmate she studied alongside all semester is. She doesn't begrudge her classmate the award. She also can't stop the comparison that follows: they studied the same material, attended the same seminars, submitted assignments to the same professors. The gap between their names on the list is not legible to her as random variance. It reads as judgment. Her value as a student—as a person in this department—adjusts downward in her own accounting without her permission. She knows this accounting is flawed. She closes the department newsletter tab. She opens her current paper. Her hands are cold. The paper has not changed since she last looked at it. She is reading the same paragraph she started four minutes ago. The words carry the same meaning. She cannot access that meaning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_student/self_worth/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Remy's roommate gets an internship at a company Remy applied to and wasn't selected for. The roommate is excited. Remy says congratulations and means it and also immediately recalibrates his self-assessment downward. His resume looks worse than his roommate's now, in his own accounting. His interview must have gone worse. His writing sample must have been weaker. He finds eleven differences between their applications and lands on each one as evidence of his own deficiency. The roommate orders takeout to celebrate and invites Remy and Remy goes and smiles and asks good questions about the company. Afterward, he lies on his bed and his chest is a low steady pressure. He is happy for his roommate. He is also measuring himself against his roommate and arriving at a number that feels like a verdict. He will apply again in the spring. He doesn't know what the number will be when he does.

## TURNING_POINT v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_student/self_worth/comparison/turning_point/micro/v03.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Dani writes down what she accomplished this week independent of how it compares to anyone else. She has been in a comparison cycle for two weeks and she knows the cycle doesn't let her see her own work clearly. The list: submitted three assignments, went to four classes, made progress on a chapter outline, covered a shift for a coworker, called her mother. She reads the list. Her chest doesn't immediately loosen. The comparison is still running in the background, still measuring her list against an imagined superior list somewhere. But the list exists. It is hers. No one else's output is on it. She folds it and puts it in her jacket pocket. The comparison will run again. When it does, she'll pull out the list and read it before she answers the comparison's claim. Her hands are still cold. The week happened. It is in her pocket.
