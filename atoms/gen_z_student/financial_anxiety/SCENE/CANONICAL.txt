
## SCENE v01
---

---
You check your bank balance at 11 p.m. and the number is smaller than you planned. Your chest tightens. You open the spreadsheet you maintain and re-run the month. The numbers confirm what the notification said. Your shoulders press toward your ears. You close the spreadsheet. You open it again. You close your laptop. The number is still there.

The group chat fills with plans for a weekend trip. You do the math silently at your desk while the messages arrive. The cost is just within reach if nothing unexpected happens this month. Nothing unexpected always happens. You type that you'll check your schedule and set your phone face-down. Your stomach is a fist.

Financial aid disbursement was supposed to hit three days ago. You check the portal on the campus bus. Still pending. Your rent is due in five days. Your palms are damp against the phone. The bus hums. The other passengers scroll their phones. No one knows about the five days or the portal or your hands.
