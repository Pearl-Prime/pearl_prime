
## RECOGNITION v01

Kofi watches himself do the body scan. He is lying on his back at the instruction of a wellness app and the voice is telling him to notice his feet, then his calves, then his knees. He notices them. He also notices himself noticing. He observes his body from the familiar remove: there is a tightness in the left hip, there is a held breath somewhere around the lower ribs, there is a jaw that won't loosen. He files these observations. He is good at observing. What the practice is asking—not observing from a distance but inhabiting the observation, being inside the hip rather than watching it—he cannot do. He watches himself fail to inhabit himself. His chest doesn't tighten. It stays flat, the way it does when the watcher has no commentary because it is watching the watching. He finishes the scan. He felt very little. He noticed everything.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v02
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: social
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nadia goes to the student wellness workshop on somatic awareness. There are eight students and a facilitator and the room smells like diffuser oil. The facilitator asks them to put one hand on their chest and one on their stomach and just feel. Nadia places her hands. She watches herself place her hands. She feels the warmth through the fabric of her shirt. She also observes herself feeling the warmth—notes that she is doing the exercise correctly, notes that she has not cried, notes that the person to her left is crying a little, notes that she is not. The facilitator says "stay with what's here." Nadia tries. The watching is what's here. The watching is always what's here. She presses her palms more firmly against her sternum. There is something underneath—not a feeling exactly, more like a pressure, a density. She stays with the pressure for thirty seconds before the watcher names it and the naming moves her back outside.

## TURNING_POINT v03
---
path: story_atoms/gen_z_student/somatic_healing/watcher/turning_point/micro/v03.txt
BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Arjun is walking across campus in November cold and his hands are in his pockets and for about forty seconds he is simply cold. He is not observing the cold from a distance. He is inside it—the bite on his cheeks, the sting at the tips of his ears, the warmth his chest generates that doesn't reach his fingers. It is a minor sensation. He is in it. Then the watcher notices he's in it and moves back to the balcony and the next thirty seconds are observed. He is aware of the difference. He stops walking and stands on the path. His hands are still cold in his pockets. He tries to stay inside the cold rather than observe it. He manages about fifteen seconds before the commentary resumes. Fifteen seconds is more than he's managed in months. His hands are still cold. He walks to his building. Fifteen seconds is something to practice with.
