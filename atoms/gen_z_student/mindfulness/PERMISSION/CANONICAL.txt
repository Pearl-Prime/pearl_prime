## PERMISSION v01
---
---
You are allowed to notice your mind has wandered off in the middle of a lecture without treating it as proof you're broken.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---
---
You are allowed to say "sorry, I got distracted, can you repeat that" instead of faking that you heard.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---
---
You are allowed to have a mind that drifts constantly and still call yourself someone who's working on being present.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---
---
You are allowed to measure progress by how fast you return, not by whether you ever stop drifting.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---
---
You are allowed to put your phone somewhere you can't see it during one meal without making it a whole lifestyle overhaul.
---

## PERMISSION v06
---
---
You are allowed to leave a conversation, mentally, and come back without confessing every single lapse out loud.
---

## PERMISSION v07
---
---
You are allowed to be gone for most of a lecture and still count the last five minutes of real attention as a genuine win.
---

## PERMISSION v08
---
---
You are allowed to skip the self-criticism when you catch yourself distracted and just quietly return instead.
---

## PERMISSION v09
---
---
You are allowed to try one small anchor tomorrow and not fix your entire attention span in a single sitting.
---

## PERMISSION v10
---
---
You are allowed to admit, even to your roommate, that you weren't really listening a minute ago.
---

## PERMISSION v11
---
---
You are allowed to treat every noticed drift as a rep instead of a failure.
---

## PERMISSION v12
---
---
You are allowed to be a person whose mind wanders and still be present more often than you think you can.
---
