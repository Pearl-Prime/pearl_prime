

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Before you check your account, sit down. Place your feet flat on the floor. Press down hard. Count to six. Release. Your feet are here. The ground is real. What you discover is information, not threat. Press down again. Count to six.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Breathe in for four counts. Hold for two. Breathe out for six. Repeat eight times. Money triggers your threat response. Calm your nervous system first. In for four. Hold. Out for six. Do this eight times before looking.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Close your eyes. Say silently: "I can handle this number." Breathe in. Breathe out. Say it again. Repeat three times. You have survived financial hardship before. You will survive this moment.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Roll your shoulders backward five times. Let them drop. Your shoulders carry financial weight high and tight. Release them. They can soften. Roll and drop.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Make a tight fist. Count to three. Release. Repeat six times. Your hands may clench when facing money numbers. Release them. They are not responsible for the number. Release.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Place your hand on your heart. Feel your heartbeat. Say silently: "My worth is not my salary." Breathe in. Breathe out. Say it again. Repeat three times. This is truth even if the number is small.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Press both feet down hard. Count to six. Release. Your feet know the ground. The ground does not move based on your account balance. Press and plant yourself.

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Hum as you exhale. Breathe in silently through your nose. Hum on the exhale. Do this eight times. The vibration settles your nervous system. Hum away the dread.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Clench your jaw. Count to three. Release completely. Repeat six times. You may be clenching your teeth about money. Release the jaw. You can unclench your whole body.

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Press your back against a wall or chair firmly. Count to six. Release. Press and count to six. Release. Repeat five times. You are supported. You are not falling. The chair holds you.

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
Feel the temperature of the air on your face. Breathe in and notice it. Breathe out and notice it. Repeat four times. You are here. The number will not change based on your dread. Face it present.

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Tense every muscle in your body. Count to four. Release. Tense and count to four. Release. Repeat five times. Money stress activates full-body tension. Release it before you look. Release it.

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
Touch your thumb to each fingertip on one hand slowly. Thumb to index. Middle. Ring. Pinky. Reverse. Repeat three times. Your mind is focusing on small, present sensations. Not on future catastrophe. Stay with your fingers.
---

## EXERCISE v14

---

## EXERCISE v15
---

---
Shake your hands out hard for five seconds. Stop. Shake them again. Stop. Three times total. Your hands may want to close in fear. Shake them open. They are alive and separate from the number.

## EXERCISE v16
---

---
Lean your upper body forward. Let your arms hang. Count to four. Rise slowly. Count to four. Repeat four times. You are moving out of contraction. Rising toward clarity.

## EXERCISE v17

---

## EXERCISE v18

## EXERCISE v19

---

## EXERCISE v20
