

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to not know the answer to a student's question. Not-knowing in front of the class is not proof that you don't belong there.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel like a fraud walking into the observation even though you've written the lesson plan three times. The feeling is not evidence.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to sit in the staff meeting and feel like everyone else is more capable, more current, more confident — and still be the one these students need.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel the gap between what you know and what the IEP requires you to know. The gap is real. The gap is also smaller than the panic makes it feel.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to teach the lesson even while doubting yourself. The doubt and the teaching can happen at the same time.
---
