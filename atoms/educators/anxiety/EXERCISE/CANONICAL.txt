

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Sit with your feet flat on the floor. Press your feet down hard. Count to four, pressing. Release and breathe out slowly. Repeat five times total. Notice your feet touching the ground. Press again and hold for four counts. Release. Your body is here, not in the future meeting. Press one more time, four counts. Release and feel the chair supporting you.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Close your eyes and find your breath. Breathe in for a count of four. Hold it for a count of four. Breathe out for a count of six. The longer exhale calms your nervous system. Repeat eight times. Your mind will drift to the meeting. Bring it back to the counting. In for four. Hold for four. Out for six. Do this eight times total.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Place both hands on your desk or lap. Spread your fingers wide. Press them flat and firm. Count to five, pressing hard. Release completely. Your hands hold tension first. Press again and count to five. Release. Feel the difference between tension and rest. Press a third time for five counts. Release and shake your hands out gently. Your nervous system shifts with this release.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Roll your shoulders backward slowly. Count to four as you roll back. Let them drop. Count to four as they settle. Lift them to your ears. Count to four. Drop them down. Count to four. Repeat the full cycle four times. Your shoulders carry meeting-anxiety high and tight. This movement interrupts that pattern. Feel them drop each time. Breathe as you move.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
