

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to sit down during the lesson. Your back has been absorbing the physical demands of this work for years and it is allowed to have a chair.

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to rest your voice. The vocal cord strain is information your body has been sending for a while, and rest is the appropriate response.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to come home and do nothing for an hour. The body that performed and managed and stood all day has earned the floor.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to feel how physically demanding teaching is. The cultural story that teaching is intellectual work makes the body's cost invisible, but the cost is real.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to tell a colleague that you are not okay without performing recovery. The depletion is physical and it is honest and it does not need to be managed for their comfort.
