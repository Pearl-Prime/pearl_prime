What he hadn't noticed was that the efficiency had required a suspension of sensation that extended now past the school day. He didn't feel his feet on the floor anymore. She placed her hand on her sternum for the first time in a long time. Just to see. The classroom had needed her hands for so long — for writing on boards, for calming shoulders, for directing and redirecting and building — that they'd stopped belonging to her in any simple way. She sat in that. Let the hand just be a hand. Found it strange. Found it, after a while, something closer to hers.
