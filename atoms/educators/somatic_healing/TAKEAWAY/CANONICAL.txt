

## TAKEAWAY v01
---

---
Teaching is a physical profession. Your body has been the instrument, and instruments require maintenance.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---

---
The back pain is years of bending toward desks that were built for smaller bodies than yours.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---

---
You come home depleted because you were present — fully present — for hours. That kind of presence costs something in the body.
---

## TAKEAWAY v04
---

---
Your voice carries the class all day. The hoarseness is not a side effect. It is a wound that keeps reopening.
---

## TAKEAWAY v05
---

---
The body that has stood and performed and managed now needs something other than more performance. It needs a witness.
---
