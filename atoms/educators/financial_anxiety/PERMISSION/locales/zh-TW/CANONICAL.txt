---

---

---

---

## 憑藉 v04
---

---
你可以希望賺更多錢，而不必覺得渴望更多的收入會讓你失去作為一名教師的價值。 dedication 和 compensation 幾乎不是對立的概念。

(Note: The word "dedication" and "compensation" are kept in English as they might have specific connotations in this context that could be lost in translation.)
