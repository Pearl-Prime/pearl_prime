

## HOOK v01

---
Elena is at her kitchen table on Sunday at 9 PM, lesson plan folder open, hands stopped above the page — she does not want to and she does not have anything left to give it.

She opens the lesson plan folder on Sunday night and her hands stop moving. She doesn't want to. She doesn't have anything left.

## HOOK v03

## HOOK v04

---

## HOOK v05
---

---
She submitted her resignation letter once. Deleted it. Submitted it again. Deleted it again. She opens the document a third time and her hands stay still. The question is no longer whether she wants to leave. It's whether she has anything left to stay for.
