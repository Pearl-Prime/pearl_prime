

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to admit that the summer is not enough anymore. One season of recovery cannot undo nine months of depletion.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to stop being the person who stays late. The students need a teacher who returns tomorrow, not a hero who burns out by March.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to grieve who you were before the job became everything. The teacher who had a life outside the classroom was not less dedicated — she was more sustainable.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to stop performing enthusiasm for an initiative you don't believe in. Compliance is enough. It does not have to look like joy.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to not take on the extra duty, the committee, the after-school program. The word no is not a violation of your contract.
---
