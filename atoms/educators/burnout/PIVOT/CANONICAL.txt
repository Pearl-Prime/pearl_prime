## PIVOT v02

## PIVOT v03

---
He thought the empty feeling meant he was lazy. What he was, was a container that had been full for too many years with not enough time to refill.

## PIVOT v04
---

---
The institution runs on the belief that one more passionate teacher can absorb one more structural failure. She absorbed them for eleven years. What she's feeling now isn't burnout — it's the cost coming due.
---

## PIVOT v05
---

---
He used to know the difference between a hard week and a broken system. He stopped knowing that somewhere around year five. The discovery that the system was the problem is not comforting. It is, finally, clarifying.
