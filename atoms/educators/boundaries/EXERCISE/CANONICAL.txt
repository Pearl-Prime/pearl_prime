

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Put your phone in another room. Feel your hands empty. Sit down. Close your eyes. Breathe in for four counts. Hold for two. Breathe out for six. Repeat this breathing six times. Notice the silence. Your nervous system begins releasing work urgency. No messages are entering your body right now. You are off the clock.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand at your door or window. Look outside at something far away. Notice one color you see. Notice one sound you hear. Notice the temperature on your skin. Return to this present place three times. You have left the building and the work. Your body is here now, not in the school.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Run cold water over your wrists and hands. Hold them under for a count of ten. Feel the cold wake your body. Shake them dry. This signals an ending. The work day has ended. Your body has shifted locations.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Change one piece of clothing immediately. Shoes off. Sweater off. Different shirt. One deliberate change. As you change, feel the boundary. You have left work. Your body is now in a different state.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Sit with your feet flat. Press down hard. Count to five. Release. Press and count to five. Release. Do this eight times. Say silently, "My time is my own." Feel the ground beneath you supporting this truth.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Place your hand on your heart. Say silently, "The day is complete." Breathe in while touching your heart. Count to four. Breathe out. Count to six. Repeat five times. Your work is done. The children are safe. You have done enough.
---

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Stand and shake your whole body. Shake your arms hard. Shake your legs. Make sound if you can. Shake for ten seconds. Stop. Stand still. Feel the new settling. You have physically released the day's activation.
---

## EXERCISE v08
---

---
Look at your phone screen off. Say, "I am not checking this tonight." Put it face down. Turn away. Walk to another room. Your boundary stands even though no one is monitoring it. You are monitoring it.
---

## EXERCISE v09
---

---
Tense every muscle in your body. Count to three. Release. Repeat this four times. On the final release, feel how much tension you were carrying from work. That tension is not your responsibility to carry into evening.
---

## EXERCISE v10
---

---
Close your eyes. Imagine a door closing behind you. The school is behind that door. Count to four. Open your eyes. You are on this side of the door. The door stays closed tonight.
---

## EXERCISE v11
---

---
Place both hands on your abdomen. Breathe in and feel your belly expand. Count to four. Breathe out and feel it settle. Count to six. Repeat six times. Your belly knows the difference between work stress and rest. Let it rest now.
---

## EXERCISE v12
---

---
Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Press both feet down hard into the ground. Count to six. Release. Your feet are planted. You are not going back. You are staying here.
---

## EXERCISE v13
---

---
Turn your head slowly left. Breathe in. Breathe out. Turn right. Breathe in. Breathe out. Repeat three times each direction. You are turning away from the day. Facing toward your evening.
---

## EXERCISE v14
---

---
Make two tight fists. Count to three. Release and spread fingers wide. Count to three. Repeat six times. Each release is a moment of letting go. The day, the emails, the tasks. Release.
---

## EXERCISE v15
---

---
Hum as you exhale. Breathe in silently through your nose. Hum as you breathe out through your mouth. Do this eight times. The vibration tells your body the threat is past. Hum the day away.
---

## EXERCISE v16
---

---
Press your back against a wall or chair. Push firmly. Count to five. Release. Push and count to five. Release. Repeat five times. You are held by something other than work. You are supported.
---

## EXERCISE v17
---

---
Touch your thumb to each fingertip slowly. Thumb to index. Middle. Ring. Pinky. Reverse. Repeat this sequence three times. Your mind is leaving work. It is touching something small and present. It is here.
---

## EXERCISE v18
---

---
Breathe in for five counts. Breathe out for seven. Repeat six times. The longer exhale signals safety. Do this while sitting in a chair that is not at work. In for five. Out for seven.
---

## EXERCISE v19
---

---
Roll your shoulders backward five times. Feel them drop. Let them drop lower. They have been lifted high from work worry. Drop them now. Feel the release in your neck and upper back.
---

## EXERCISE v20
---

---
Close your eyes. Place your hand on your forehead. Breathe in for four counts. Breathe out for six. Repeat five times. Your mind is released from the problems that wait tomorrow. Breathe them out tonight.
---

## EXERCISE v21
---

---
Clench your jaw. Count to three. Release completely. Repeat six times. Your jaw holds every word unsaid at work. Release it. You are not at work. You do not need to perform speech right now.
---

## EXERCISE v22
---

---
Cup your hands over your eyes without touching them. Sit in the darkness for a count of ten. Breathe. Remove your hands. You have created a boundary between work vision and evening vision. See what is in front of you now.
---

## EXERCISE v23
---

---
Tense your legs and press your feet down. Count to five. Release. Press and count to five. Release. Repeat six times. Your legs have carried you out of the building. They can rest now.
---

## EXERCISE v24
---

---
Say out loud or silently: "Work is done. I am home." Place your hand on your heart. Breathe in for four. Breathe out for six. Repeat three times. The statement is true. Your body can believe it.
---

## EXERCISE v25
---

---
Stand and lean your upper body forward slightly. Let your arms hang. Count to four. Rise slowly. Count to four. Repeat this four times. You are bending and releasing the day. Standing tall into evening.
---

## EXERCISE v26
---

---
Breathe in through your nose for four counts. Hold for two. Breathe out through your mouth for six. Repeat eight times. Nasal breathing in calms. Mouth breathing out releases. In through nose. Out through mouth.
---

## EXERCISE v27
---

---
Press your palms together at your chest firmly. Count to five. Release. Press and count to five. Release. Repeat five times. This gesture closes the book on work. The pages are sealed until tomorrow.
---

## EXERCISE v28
---

---
Feel the temperature of the room on your face. Notice where you feel warm. Notice where you feel cool. Turn your head slightly and notice the change. You are sensitizing yourself to here, to now, to home. Not to work.
---

## EXERCISE v29
---

---
Tense your face completely, including forehead, eyes, jaw. Count to three. Release. Repeat four times. Your face carries performance and masks from work. Release it. Relax your face completely.
---

## EXERCISE v30
---

---
Press your heels down hard into the floor. Keep your toes light. Count to six. Release. Press and count to six. Release. Repeat six times. You are grounded here. Work cannot pull you back. The boundary holds.
