

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: invisible
tier_variant: C

What I notice is a particular binding of self-worth to the outcomes of teaching. A student succeeds and I feel valuable. A student struggles and I feel inadequate. A class goes well and I feel worthy. A class goes badly and I feel like a failure. The mechanism that activates is what I think of as outcome-dependent-worth. The mechanism is the way that my sense of value is entirely dependent on things that are not fully in my control. The mechanism has tied my worth to student outcomes, test scores, parent satisfaction, administrative evaluation. The mechanism has made my worth into something external. When outcomes are uncertain, worth is uncertain.
