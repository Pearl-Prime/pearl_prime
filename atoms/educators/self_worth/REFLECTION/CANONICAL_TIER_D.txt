

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: invisible
tier_variant: D

What I notice is a particular binding of self-worth to the outcomes of teaching. A student succeeds and I feel valuable. A student struggles and I feel inadequate. A class goes well and I feel worthy. A class goes badly and I feel like a failure. The mechanism that activates is what I think of as outcome-dependent-worth. The mechanism is the way that my sense of value is entirely dependent on things that are not fully in my control.
