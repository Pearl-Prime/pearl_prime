

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: protective
tier_variant: D

What I notice is a particular kind of numbness that emerges after prolonged emotional labor. A student shares something difficult and I respond appropriately. I listen. I validate. I hold space. But there is something absent in my own internal experience. I am going through the motions of compassion while experiencing a kind of emptiness beneath it. The mechanism I recognize is what I think of as compassion-depletion.
