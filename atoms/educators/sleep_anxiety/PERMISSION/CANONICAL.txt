

## PERMISSION v01
---

---
You are allowed to put the rollbook down at 10pm. The names will still be there in the morning. Your sleep is required for them to have a present teacher.
---

## PERMISSION v02
---

---
You are allowed to feel the Sunday dread without treating it as a problem to solve. Sometimes the dread just needs to move through, not be fixed.
---

## PERMISSION v03
---

---
You are allowed to wake at 4am and not open your email. The inbox will wait. The hour between 4 and 5 is allowed to be yours.
---

## PERMISSION v04
---

---
You are allowed to have a bad night before a difficult day and still be enough. You do not have to arrive perfect.
---

## PERMISSION v05
---

---
You are allowed to tell the truth that your classroom does not close when the bell rings. That truth is the first step toward understanding what is actually keeping you awake.
