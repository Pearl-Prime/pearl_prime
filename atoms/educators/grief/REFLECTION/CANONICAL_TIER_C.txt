

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: inherited
tier_variant: C

What I notice is a particular quality of loss that accumulates across a career. Students who disappeared. Students whose trajectories shifted. Students who I thought would make it and did not. The mechanism that activates is what I think of as cumulative-student-loss. The mechanism is the way that losses accumulate. Each year brings new students. Each year some of those students become students I worried about. Each year some of those students are no longer there. The mechanism has been tracking these losses for years. What I have come to understand is that the losses are not usually dramatic. A student stops coming to school. A student drops out. A student moves schools.
