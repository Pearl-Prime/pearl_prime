## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez walks into the department holiday party and his body activates. Every face is a potential conversation he will fail at. His hands are cold. His chest pounds. He scans for an exit. Then his partner waves him over to a group that includes three people he trains with every week. People he knows. People who know him. The false alarm fired on the room, not the people in it. The frame shifts: he is not entering a room of strangers. He is joining people who already know his name.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Kim reaches the breaking point. The false_alarm pattern at peak social anxiety intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Kim watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Kim stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nowak hears a sound at home — a crash from the garage. Her body launches into response mode. Heart rate doubled. Hands ready. Then she pauses. Holds the doorframe. Checks: is this a real emergency or is this my training? She walks to the garage at a normal pace. The cat knocked over a paint can. She picks up the can. Her heart rate is still elevated but her response was measured. The false alarm fired. She chose not to obey it. The choosing is the identity: someone who can feel the alarm and select the response.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rivera wakes up at 3 AM in his own bed, heart slamming, body ready for a call that is not coming. The false alarm is total. His nervous system is in the fire. He lies still. Puts his hands on his chest. Feels the heart rate. Does not fight it. Does not check his phone. Does not verify that the world is safe. He lies in the alarm and lets his body discharge the activation. Three minutes. Five. The heart rate drops. He did not need to check. He needed to wait. The waiting is the identity: someone who knows the alarm from the inside and trusts it to pass.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen maps the false alarm's conditioning. Every tone drop trained her nervous system to expect the worst. After hundreds of calls, the worst-case pathway is the strongest neural pathway. The pathway fires before thought engages. The mechanism is not fear — it is training. Her body was trained to activate on every stimulus. The training was effective. Too effective. It eliminated the delay between stimulus and response that would allow assessment. Her chest tightens. The false alarm is not a malfunction. It is an over-trained response. The muscle is too strong.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/social_anxiety/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Morales sees the false alarm's deepest function: it has replaced intuition. He no longer has a gut feeling that distinguishes between real threats and false ones. Every feeling is the same feeling — alarm. The alarm replaced the nuance. The mechanism: years of genuine emergencies burned out the calibration between levels. Everything is maximum. A tone drop is maximum. A doorbell is maximum. His child's shout is maximum. Morales's chest is pounding. The mechanism is not hypervigilance. It is the death of graduated response.
---
