## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Nowak hears a name on the radio that sounds like the name of the partner she lost. Her body goes electric. Hands cold. Chest slamming. For three seconds, her dead partner is alive on the radio. Then the dispatcher clarifies — different name, different unit. The false alarm is her grief's nervous system, still tuned to the frequency of a person who no longer transmits. The frame shifts: her body has not accepted the death. Her mind has. Her nervous system is still listening for a voice that will not come. She turns down the radio. The silence is the grief acknowledging what the body has not.

Rivera reaches the breaking point. The false_alarm pattern at peak grief intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. ## EMBODIMENT v06

COST_TYPE: identity

IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Morales hears a sound at home — a crash from the garage. Her body launches into response mode. Heart rate doubled. Hands ready. Then she pauses. Holds the doorframe. Checks: is this a real emergency or is this my training? She walks to the garage at a normal pace. The cat knocked over a paint can. She picks up the can. Her heart rate is still elevated but her response was measured. The false alarm fired. She chose not to obey it. The choosing is the identity: someone who can feel the alarm and select the response.

COST_TYPE: identity

---
Gutierrez wakes up at 3 AM in his own bed, heart slamming, body ready for a call that is not coming. The false alarm is total. His nervous system is in the fire. He lies still. Puts his hands on his chest. Feels the heart rate. Does not fight it. Does not check his phone. Does not verify that the world is safe. He lies in the alarm and lets his body discharge the activation. Three minutes. Five. The heart rate drops. He did not need to check. He needed to wait. The waiting is the identity: someone who knows the alarm from the inside and trusts it to pass.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Patel maps the false alarm's conditioning. Every tone drop trained her nervous system to expect the worst. After hundreds of calls, the worst-case pathway is the strongest neural pathway. The pathway fires before thought engages. The mechanism is not fear — it is training. Her body was trained to activate on every stimulus. The training was effective. Too effective. It eliminated the delay between stimulus and response that would allow assessment. Her chest tightens. The false alarm is not a malfunction. It is an over-trained response. The muscle is too strong.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
O'Brien sees the false alarm's deepest function: it has replaced intuition. He no longer has a gut feeling that distinguishes between real threats and false ones. Every feeling is the same feeling — alarm. The alarm replaced the nuance. The mechanism: years of genuine emergencies burned out the calibration between levels. Everything is maximum. A tone drop is maximum. A doorbell is maximum. His child's shout is maximum. O'Brien's chest is pounding. The mechanism is not hypervigilance. It is the death of graduated response.
