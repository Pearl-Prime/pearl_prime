## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien watches other first responders process a line-of-duty death with visible emotion. Tears. Hugs. Open mourning. O'Brien feels nothing. His chest is hollow. The comparison fires: they grieve properly. He grieves wrong. The frame shifts when the chaplain says: there is no proper grief. There is only your grief. O'Brien's grief is silence and hollow chest and nothing. That is a form. Not an absence. His jaw unclenches. The comparison wanted his grief to look like theirs. His grief looks like his.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak grief intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Vasquez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Vasquez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

Nowak runs the worst call of her career alongside the paramedic she always compared herself to. The patient is critical. The comparison fires: the other medic is faster, smoother, more certain. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Rivera against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Rivera measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Chen's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
