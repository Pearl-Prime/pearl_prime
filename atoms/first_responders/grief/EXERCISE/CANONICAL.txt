## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Feel what you feel. No fixing it. No understanding it yet. Let tears come if they come. You're allowed.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Place one hand on your chest. Feel your heart beating. You survived this moment. Breathe once. And again. Again.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Name the person or thing lost. Say their name once. Feel the loss in your body. Sit with it. Breathe.
