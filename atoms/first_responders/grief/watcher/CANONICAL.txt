## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/watcher/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Kim scans every call for the conditions that killed his partner. Structure type, fire load, ventilation status, floor integrity. His neck rotates constantly on scene. His body is cataloging threat data that will not prevent a death that already happened. His shoulders are rigid. The frame shifts when his new partner says: you are scanning for a fire that is three months old. The watcher is not protecting the current crew. It is trying to prevent the past. The past is done. The watcher can protect the present. Different fire. Different conditions. Kim's jaw unclenches. He looks at this fire. This building. This crew.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/watcher/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jackson reaches the breaking point. The watcher pattern at peak grief intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Jackson watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Jackson stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/watcher/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rivera sits in the restaurant with his family. His back is to the door. He chose this seat deliberately. His body protests — the watcher wants visual access to the entrance. Rivera stays. Eats. His neck is stiff for twenty minutes. Then it softens. The watcher is running but Rivera is not obeying it. The meal continues. His daughter tells a story. He hears the story. Not the watcher. The daughter. The watcher does not need his attention right now. His daughter does.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/watcher/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Chen is off duty at a crowded stadium with her family. The watcher is running at maximum: exits, threats, crowd density, choke points. Her neck is rotating. Her daughter pulls her hand: look, the mascot. Chen looks at the mascot. For thirty seconds, the watcher loses the feed. The mascot is ridiculous. Her daughter is laughing. Chen laughs. The watcher reclaims the feed after the laugh fades. But the thirty seconds existed. Chen was a parent for thirty seconds inside the watcher's shift. She will build more seconds. The seconds accumulate.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Morales identifies the watcher's origin story. It began on a specific call — the one where she was not watching and something went wrong. That call installed the watcher permanently. The mechanism: one moment of missed attention became a lifetime of hyperattention. The watcher is a security system installed by a single breach. The breach was years ago. The security system has not been updated. It still protects against the exact threat from the exact call. Her shoulders are rigid. The watcher is guarding a door that was breached once, in a building that no longer exists.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/grief/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Gutierrez sees the watcher's deepest paradox: it makes him less safe. The constant scanning depletes the attention needed for actual threat response. He is so busy watching for everything that he cannot focus on anything. The watcher has reduced his effectiveness while convincing him it is the source of his effectiveness. His neck is stone. The mechanism: the watcher is a net negative. It consumes more safety than it produces. The vigilance is not protective. It is expensive. It costs more than the threats it theoretically prevents.
