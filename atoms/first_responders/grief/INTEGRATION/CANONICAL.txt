

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: STILL-HERE
The losses are real. The people who left. The person you were before. The future that won't happen. The calls that replay. You're still here carrying all of it. The station is still standing. The crew is still here even as the crew changes. You're still responding even though responding costs. That still-here-ness is everything. You're not broken by the loss. You're shaped by it. That's different. That's the whole thing.

## INTEGRATION v03
---
mode: BODY-LANDED
Your body carries the grief. The tightness in your chest when the anniversary comes. The way your shoulders tense when you pass the call location. The sleep that won't come when the replays start. Your body knows every loss. It's all stored there. Feel into it. Don't fix it. Just feel it. That's how you know you're still alive. You're still capable of grief. That's not weakness. That's the sign that you loved or believed or trusted. That's the real thing.

## INTEGRATION v05
---
mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
There's someone on the crew who's grieving too. A call they can't shake. A person they miss. A version of themselves that's gone. You don't have to talk about it. But knowing someone else is in it changes how alone you feel. You're all carrying. That's what the crew is. It's people carrying together. You don't have to say it out loud. But you're not alone in the weight.

## INTEGRATION v07
---
mode: BODY-LANDED
Right now you're still here. The losses are real but you're still breathing. The grief is real but you're still showing up. You're standing in the station that's different because people are gone. You're responding to calls that hurt. You're carrying losses that don't get funerals. And you're still here. That's the whole thing. That's where the healing lives.
