## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/shame/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez is embarrassed by how much the kind words from a patient's family affect him. He should not need external validation this badly. His face burns. The shame is about the need, not about the words. His chest tightens. The frame shifts: needing to be seen doing good work is not weakness. It is human. The words landed because he has been running on empty. The shame wanted him to be self-sustaining. Nobody is fully self-sustaining. His jaw unclenches.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/shame/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Kim reaches the breaking point. The shame pattern at peak self worth intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Kim watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Kim stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/shame/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nowak tells his crew he needs a minute after a bad call. In front of everyone. His face warms. The old shame says: weakness. Nowak walks to the bay door. Breathes for sixty seconds. Returns. The crew does not look at him differently. One of them nods. The minute was not weakness. It was modeling. Nowak took the minute he needed and the crew saw that taking a minute is allowed. The shame wanted him to perform invulnerability. He performed honesty. The honesty is the identity now.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/shame/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rivera tells her crew she is going to therapy. In the kitchen. At breakfast. Out loud. Her face is warm. The shame is present — a first responder admitting she needs help. She says it clearly and does not soften it with a joke. The crew is quiet for three seconds. Then one of them says: who is your therapist? I need one too. The shame expected isolation. It got company. The announcement is not easy. But it is honest. The honest is the identity now.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen identifies the shame's enforcement mechanism in first responder culture. The culture permits physical injury but not emotional injury. A broken bone gets sympathy and sick leave. A broken spirit gets silence and suspicion. The shame is not personal. It is institutional. The mechanism: the culture rewards stoicism and punishes vulnerability. Chen has been following a script written by the institution, not by her own values. Her face warms as the mechanism becomes visible. The shame is a cultural product, not a personal failing.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Morales sees the shame's generational transmission. His captain learned it from his captain. The silence, the stoicism, the punishment of vulnerability — it passes through the ranks like an inherited disease. The mechanism: shame is not felt. It is taught. It is modeled. The new recruit learns it from the veteran's silence. The veteran learned it from the captain's disapproval. The captain learned it from the culture's mythology of the unbreakable hero. Morales's face burns. He is not carrying his own shame. He is carrying decades of institutional shame that was handed down like equipment.
---
