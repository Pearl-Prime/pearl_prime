## TURNING_POINT v06

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez receives a positive evaluation and immediately begins questioning it. Was the supervisor being generous? Is the evaluation inflated? Would it hold up under scrutiny? Her chest tightens with each question. The spiral takes a positive input and dissolves it into doubt. The frame shifts: she runs the same analysis on negative feedback and accepts it instantly. The spiral is directional — it only questions the good. She holds the evaluation without questioning it. The discomfort of accepting praise is the self-worth stretching.

Kim reaches the breaking point. The spiral pattern at peak self worth intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. ## EMBODIMENT v06

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nowak feels the spiral begin after a call. The what-ifs arrive: what if she had done X, what if Y happened instead. She catches the spiral at the second rotation. Writes the facts on a napkin: patient found at this time, interventions started at this time, outcome at this time. Facts. Not hypotheticals. The napkin is small. The facts are small. The spiral was trying to make the call infinite. The napkin makes it finite. She folds the napkin. Puts it in her pocket. Done.

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/spiral/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rivera finishes the worst call of the year and the spiral launches immediately. What if. What if. What if. The spiral has maximum material — the call provided a hundred decision points. Rivera lets the spiral run for five minutes. Watches it. Then says to his partner: I need to debrief. Not the spiral. The debrief. The debrief has structure: what happened, what we did, what the outcome was. The spiral is infinite. The debrief is finite. He chooses the finite version. The spiral does not stop. But he stops following it into the infinite.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen tracks the spiral's fuel source: incomplete debriefs. Every call that is not fully processed becomes material for the spiral. The unprocessed calls stack in memory and the spiral cycles through them looking for the decision that could have changed the outcome. The mechanism: the spiral is not overthinking. It is under-processing. The calls were never completed in Chen's nervous system. The spiral is the nervous system's attempt to finish them. His chest tightens. The spiral is not the problem. The incomplete processing is the problem.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07

MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Morales sees the spiral's deepest layer: it is a grief response disguised as analysis. Every spiral about a call is really about the calls she cannot undo. The spiral runs because the calls are irreversible. The analysis is the mind's attempt to reverse what cannot be reversed. The mechanism: the spiral is not about the future. It is about the past wearing the future's clothes. Her chest aches. The spiral replays old calls under the disguise of reviewing current ones. The current call is the surface. The old call is the engine.
