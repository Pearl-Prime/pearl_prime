## TURNING_POINT v06

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien cannot triage her own value. Every task feels like a test she is failing. Patient care: not good enough. Paperwork: behind. Physical training: declining. Community engagement: insufficient. The overwhelm is the self-worth converting a normal workload into a scorecard. The frame shifts when she completes one task well: a clean intubation on a critical patient. The intubation is enough. BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity

IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez reaches the breaking point. The overwhelm pattern at peak self worth intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Vasquez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Vasquez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

Jackson is on her third consecutive call. The first two are still in her body: the chest pain patient in her shoulders, the MVA in her hands. The third call begins. She compartmentalizes not by ignoring the first two but by acknowledging them: I am carrying two calls. I am running a third. Three is my load right now. The acknowledgment creates the capacity. Nowak is on a mass casualty incident. Multiple patients. Limited resources. Maximum chaos. The overwhelm is total — too many needs, too few hands, too little time. Nowak triages. Not perfectly. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees the overwhelm's cumulative structure. It is not one call that overwhelms. It is the stack. Call on call on call without discharge. Each call adds a layer. The layers compress. The body has no protocol for decompression between calls. The mechanism: the job creates accumulation and provides no subtraction. The overwhelm is an accounting problem — all deposits, no withdrawals. Rivera's chest compresses. The solution is not fewer calls. It is deliberate discharge between them.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/self_worth/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen sees the overwhelm's systemic root: it is not personal capacity failure. It is institutional design failure. The shifts are too long. The breaks are too few. The emotional load is unaccounted for. The mechanism: the institution measures bodies on rigs, not nervous systems in recovery. Chen is not overwhelmed because she is weak. She is overwhelmed because the system was designed for machines and staffed with humans. Her chest compresses. The mechanism is structural. No amount of personal resilience fixes structural insufficiency.
