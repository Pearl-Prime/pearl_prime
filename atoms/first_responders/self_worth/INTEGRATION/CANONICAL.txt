

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: STILL-HERE
You're still here. You showed up at the station today. Your skills are still intact. Your capability is still real. The person wearing the uniform is the same person who exists without it. The uniform doesn't create the value. It reveals it. The value was there before the badge and it will be there after. You're learning that. You're building that knowledge now.

## INTEGRATION v03
