## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Kim watches a paramedic recite drug dosages from memory during a code. Perfect recall. Kim needs to check the protocol book every time. His face burns. The comparison says: real medics know this by heart. His hands are cold. The frame shifts when the training officer tells the group: the protocol book exists to be used. The medic who memorized the dosages is impressive. The medic who checks the book is safe. Kim's imposter syndrome measured competence by memorization. The system measures competence by outcomes. His outcomes are excellent.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jackson reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak imposter syndrome intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Jackson watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Jackson stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rivera runs a call alongside the medic whose competence used to shrink Rivera's confidence. The comparison fires: they are smoother, faster, more certain. Rivera lets the comparison run and focuses on the patient. Rivera's IV lands on the first stick. The patient stabilizes. Rivera does not look at the other medic for validation. The patient's vitals are the validation. Rivera is no longer performing against a benchmark. Rivera is performing for the patient. The comparison has lost its audience.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Chen runs the worst call of her career alongside the paramedic she always compared herself to. The patient is critical. The comparison fires: the other medic is faster, smoother, more certain. Chen pushes the comparison out of the treatment space. Not later. Now. She works the airway. Her hands are steady because the patient needs steady hands, not because the comparison gave her permission. The patient stabilizes. Both medics contributed. The comparison wanted a ranking. The patient needed a team. Chen was the team.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Morales identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Morales against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Morales measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/imposter_syndrome/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Gutierrez sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Gutierrez's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
---
