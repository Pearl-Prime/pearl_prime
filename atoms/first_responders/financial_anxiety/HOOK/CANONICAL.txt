

## HOOK v01
---

---
Marcus is at the kitchen table in uniform still damp from rain, calculator and insurance bill spread out — his jaw is clenched before the overtime math finishes.
---

## HOOK v02
---

---
You finish the shift report and open the banking app in the apparatus bay, and the balance does the thing it always does now — it looks fine for exactly as long as you do not think about the month ahead.
---

## HOOK v03
---

---
Overtime got you through last winter, but the body keeps a ledger too, and yours has started charging interest in the form of the dread that arrives before the alarm does.
---

## HOOK v04
---

---
Your partner jokes about the pension like it is a finish line, and you laugh, but on the drive home you do the real math and the line keeps moving the closer you get to it.
---

## HOOK v05
---

---
The mortgage cleared, the truck payment cleared, and you sat in the driveway a moment longer than you needed to, because clearing is not the same as having room to breathe.
---
