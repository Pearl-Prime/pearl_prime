You can run into danger but you can't look at a spreadsheet without your hands shaking. That's what I'm learning to name: the mechanism that turns financial numbers into a form of threat.

So the automatic response is financial anxiety. The mechanism is called trauma-matched deprivation—you've been through enough to know what real scarcity looks like. You've seen people with nothing. And the understanding that you could be them at any moment creates the anxiety.

You're one job loss away from collapse. The salary you earn is contingent on your ability to keep showing up, and the work damages your ability to show up.
