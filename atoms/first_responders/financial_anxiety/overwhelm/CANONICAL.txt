## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Chen stares at a stack of bills she sorted by due date. The stack is organized but the feeling is not. Every bill represents a demand on money she has already allocated elsewhere. Her chest compresses. The overwhelm is not disorganization — it is the gap between income and obligation. The frame shifts when she picks up the first bill and pays it. One. Just one. The stack shrinks by a single envelope. The overwhelm needed the full stack. The paid bill is subtraction. She picks up the second.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Morales reaches the breaking point. The overwhelm pattern at peak financial anxiety intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Morales watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Morales stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Patel is on her third consecutive call. The first two are still in her body: the chest pain patient in her shoulders, the MVA in her hands. The third call begins. She compartmentalizes not by ignoring the first two but by acknowledging them: I am carrying two calls. I am running a third. Three is my load right now. The acknowledgment creates the capacity. Naming the weight does not remove it but it organizes it. She runs the third call. Clean. Competent. Full.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
O'Brien is on a mass casualty incident. Multiple patients. Limited resources. Maximum chaos. The overwhelm is total — too many needs, too few hands, too little time. O'Brien triages. Not perfectly. Not without anguish. But he triages. The overwhelm does not paralyze him because he has built the practice of choosing in impossible conditions. He chooses who gets care first. The choosing costs something. He feels the cost in his sternum. He chooses anyway. The overwhelm is the environment. The choosing is the character.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Vasquez sees the overwhelm's cumulative structure. It is not one call that overwhelms. It is the stack. Call on call on call without discharge. Each call adds a layer. The layers compress. The body has no protocol for decompression between calls. The mechanism: the job creates accumulation and provides no subtraction. The overwhelm is an accounting problem — all deposits, no withdrawals. Vasquez's chest compresses. The solution is not fewer calls. It is deliberate discharge between them.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Kim sees the overwhelm's systemic root: it is not personal capacity failure. It is institutional design failure. The shifts are too long. The breaks are too few. The emotional load is unaccounted for. The mechanism: the institution measures bodies on rigs, not nervous systems in recovery. Kim is not overwhelmed because she is weak. She is overwhelmed because the system was designed for machines and staffed with humans. Her chest compresses. The mechanism is structural. No amount of personal resilience fixes structural insufficiency.
---
