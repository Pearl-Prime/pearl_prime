## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez hears a colleague talk about a vacation — beach, flights, hotel. Gutierrez calculates the cost in his head. His chest tightens. He could not afford that vacation on his salary. The comparison between their spending and his earning produces a verdict: he is failing at providing. The frame shifts when the colleague mentions the trip is on credit. Debt. The comparison was between Gutierrez's anxiety and someone else's debt. Neither is financial health. His jaw unclenches. He is not behind. They are not ahead. They are both struggling with different symptoms.

---
Patel reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak financial anxiety intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Patel watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Patel stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Jackson against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Jackson measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Nowak's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
