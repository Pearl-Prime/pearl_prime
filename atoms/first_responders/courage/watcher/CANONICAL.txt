## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jackson scans every scene for the thing that will require maximum courage. The collapsed structure. The active threat. The trapped victim. His neck rotates constantly. His eyes never rest. The watcher is pre-loading courage for a crisis that has not arrived. His shoulders are granite. The frame shifts when his partner says: we're on a medical assist. There is no fire. The watcher was preparing for a war that was not happening. Jackson's body was spending courage credits on an anticipated threat. The actual call requires competence, not heroism. His jaw unclenches. He adjusts his response to the actual scene.

Nowak reaches the breaking point. The watcher pattern at peak courage intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Nowak watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. Morales is off duty at a crowded stadium with her family. The watcher is running at maximum: exits, threats, crowd density, choke points. Her neck is rotating. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Gutierrez identifies the watcher's origin story. It began on a specific call — the one where she was not watching and something went wrong. That call installed the watcher permanently. The mechanism: one moment of missed attention became a lifetime of hyperattention. The watcher is a security system installed by a single breach. The breach was years ago. The security system has not been updated. It still protects against the exact threat from the exact call. Her shoulders are rigid. The watcher is guarding a door that was breached once, in a building that no longer exists.

---
Patel sees the watcher's deepest paradox: it makes him less safe. The constant scanning depletes the attention needed for actual threat response. He is so busy watching for everything that he cannot focus on anything. The watcher has reduced his effectiveness while convincing him it is the source of his effectiveness. His neck is stone. The mechanism: the watcher is a net negative. It consumes more safety than it produces. The vigilance is not protective. It is expensive. It costs more than the threats it theoretically prevents.
