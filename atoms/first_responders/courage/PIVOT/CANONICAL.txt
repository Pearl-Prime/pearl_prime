--- variant: v01
You run into what everyone else runs from. That's the headline. Here's the part nobody writes: courage isn't the absence of fear. It's the accumulation of fear that you carried forward anyway, shift after shift, and the weight of that accumulated courage has a cost that nobody debriefs.

--- variant: v02
The old definition says courage is doing the dangerous thing. New definition: courage is showing up the day after the dangerous thing didn't go well. The entry is brave. The return is braver.

--- variant: v03
Everyone celebrates the big save. Nobody celebrates the courage it takes to drive back to the same intersection where you couldn't save someone last month. That quiet return doesn't make the news. It makes the career.
