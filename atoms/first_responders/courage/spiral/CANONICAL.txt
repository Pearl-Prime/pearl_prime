## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/spiral/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien replays the moment she decided to enter the building. The decision took one second. The spiral has spent forty hours on that second. What if the floor had failed? What if the exit was blocked? What if she had not come out? Her chest constricts with each hypothetical. The spiral is punishing a successful decision by surrounding it with possible failures. The frame shifts: she made the right call. The floor held. The exit was clear. She came out. The spiral cannot undo the success. It can only surround it with ghosts. She lets the ghosts dissipate. The success remains.
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez reaches the breaking point. The spiral pattern at peak courage intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Vasquez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Vasquez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Jackson feels the spiral begin after a call. The what-ifs arrive: what if she had done X, what if Y happened instead. She catches the spiral at the second rotation. Writes the facts on a napkin: patient found at this time, interventions started at this time, outcome at this time. Facts. Not hypotheticals. The napkin is small. The facts are small. The spiral was trying to make the call infinite. The napkin makes it finite. She folds the napkin. Puts it in her pocket. Done.

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/spiral/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nowak finishes the worst call of the year and the spiral launches immediately. What if. What if. What if. The spiral has maximum material — the call provided a hundred decision points. Nowak lets the spiral run for five minutes. Watches it. Then says to his partner: I need to debrief. Not the spiral. The debrief. The debrief has structure: what happened, what we did, what the outcome was. The spiral is infinite. The debrief is finite. He chooses the finite version. The spiral does not stop. But he stops following it into the infinite.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera tracks the spiral's fuel source: incomplete debriefs. Every call that is not fully processed becomes material for the spiral. The unprocessed calls stack in memory and the spiral cycles through them looking for the decision that could have changed the outcome. The mechanism: the spiral is not overthinking. It is under-processing. The calls were never completed in Rivera's nervous system. The spiral is the nervous system's attempt to finish them. His chest tightens. The spiral is not the problem. The incomplete processing is the problem.
---

---
Chen sees the spiral's deepest layer: it is a grief response disguised as analysis. Every spiral about a call is really about the calls she cannot undo. The spiral runs because the calls are irreversible. The analysis is the mind's attempt to reverse what cannot be reversed. The mechanism: the spiral is not about the future. It is about the past wearing the future's clothes. Her chest aches. The spiral replays old calls under the disguise of reviewing current ones. The current call is the surface. The old call is the engine.
