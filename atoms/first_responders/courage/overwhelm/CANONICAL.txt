## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez's courage is not failing. It is being spent in too many directions. Courage to enter. Courage to lead the team. Courage to make the call. Courage to absorb the outcome. Courage to go home and be normal. Her chest is compressed from the spending. The overwhelm is not about fear. It is about the finite nature of bravery used without replenishment. The frame shifts when she recognizes that courage, like any resource, requires rest. She does not need more courage. She needs fewer demands on the courage she has.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel reaches the breaking point. The overwhelm pattern at peak courage intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Patel watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Patel stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Vasquez is on her third consecutive call. The first two are still in her body: the chest pain patient in her shoulders, the MVA in her hands. The third call begins. She compartmentalizes not by ignoring the first two but by acknowledging them: I am carrying two calls. I am running a third. Three is my load right now. The acknowledgment creates the capacity. Naming the weight does not remove it but it organizes it. She runs the third call. Clean. Competent. Full.

## EMBODIMENT v07

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kim is on a mass casualty incident. Multiple patients. Limited resources. Maximum chaos. The overwhelm is total — too many needs, too few hands, too little time. Kim triages. Not perfectly. Not without anguish. But he triages. The overwhelm does not paralyze him because he has built the practice of choosing in impossible conditions. He chooses who gets care first. The choosing costs something. He feels the cost in his sternum. He chooses anyway. The overwhelm is the environment. The choosing is the character.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson sees the overwhelm's cumulative structure. It is not one call that overwhelms. It is the stack. Call on call on call without discharge. Each call adds a layer. The layers compress. The body has no protocol for decompression between calls. The mechanism: the job creates accumulation and provides no subtraction. The overwhelm is an accounting problem — all deposits, no withdrawals. Jackson's chest compresses. The solution is not fewer calls. It is deliberate discharge between them.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/courage/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak sees the overwhelm's systemic root: it is not personal capacity failure. It is institutional design failure. The shifts are too long. The breaks are too few. The emotional load is unaccounted for. The mechanism: the institution measures bodies on rigs, not nervous systems in recovery. Nowak is not overwhelmed because she is weak. She is overwhelmed because the system was designed for machines and staffed with humans. Her chest compresses. The mechanism is structural. No amount of personal resilience fixes structural insufficiency.
