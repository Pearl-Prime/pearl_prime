Rivera sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Rivera's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
