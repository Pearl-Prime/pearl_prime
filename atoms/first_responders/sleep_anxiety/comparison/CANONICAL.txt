COST_TYPE: opportunity

IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel shares a bunk room with a firefighter who sleeps through everything. Snoring within minutes. Patel is awake, listening for the tone, body rigid. The comparison: that person can sleep in a fire station and Patel cannot. His jaw clenches. The frame shifts when the sleeping firefighter tells him the next morning: I take medication. O'Brien reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak sleep anxiety intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/sleep_anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Nowak against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Nowak measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/sleep_anxiety/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Rivera's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
