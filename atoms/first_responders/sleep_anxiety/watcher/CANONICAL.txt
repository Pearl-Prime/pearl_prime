MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez keeps one ear on the speaker even in sleep. His body has trained itself to half-sleep — aware enough to respond, unconscious enough to rest slightly. Except the rest is not rest. It is surveillance with closed eyes. His shoulders never fully relax. The frame shifts during a night with no calls. His body remains at half-sleep anyway. The watcher does not need actual calls. It needs the possibility of calls. He cannot eliminate the possibility. But he can notice that tonight, no calls came. His jaw unclenches at 5 AM. One night of evidence that the watcher's vigil was unnecessary.

Patel reaches the breaking point. The watcher pattern at peak sleep anxiety intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. Kim is off duty at a crowded stadium with her family. The watcher is running at maximum: exits, threats, crowd density, choke points. Her neck is rotating. Her daughter pulls her hand: look, the mascot. Kim looks at the mascot. For thirty seconds, the watcher loses the feed. The mascot is ridiculous. Her daughter is laughing. Kim laughs. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson identifies the watcher's origin story. It began on a specific call — the one where she was not watching and something went wrong. That call installed the watcher permanently. The mechanism: one moment of missed attention became a lifetime of hyperattention. The watcher is a security system installed by a single breach. The breach was years ago. The security system has not been updated. It still protects against the exact threat from the exact call. Her shoulders are rigid. The watcher is guarding a door that was breached once, in a building that no longer exists.

---
Nowak sees the watcher's deepest paradox: it makes him less safe. The constant scanning depletes the attention needed for actual threat response. He is so busy watching for everything that he cannot focus on anything. The watcher has reduced his effectiveness while convincing him it is the source of his effectiveness. His neck is stone. The mechanism: the watcher is a net negative. It consumes more safety than it produces. The vigilance is not protective. It is expensive. It costs more than the threats it theoretically prevents.
