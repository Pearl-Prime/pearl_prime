## TURNING_POINT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien grieves the body he had before the job. Twenty years of hauling, climbing, carrying, absorbing impact. His knees, his back, his shoulders — each one carries a history. His chest aches. The grief is not self-pity. It is accurate accounting. The job took something physical from him. The frame shifts when he notices what the job gave: reflexes, endurance, strength in patterns unique to the work. The body changed. Changed is not broken. Changed is adapted. His jaw softens.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez reaches the breaking point. The grief pattern at peak somatic healing intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Vasquez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Vasquez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

Nowak speaks at the memorial for a colleague. Her voice breaks. Her chest is shattered. She reads the words she wrote through tears that make the paper blurry. She does not apologize for the tears. Does not pause to compose herself. She reads through the breaking. The grief is in every word and every word is true. The room holds the grief with her. She finishes the speech. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/somatic_healing/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees how grief operates in a culture that does not permit it. The department has no ritual for ongoing grief. There is the funeral. There is the memorial. Then there is silence. The grief continues but the container ended. Rivera's chest is heavy. The mechanism: the institution processes grief as an event, not a condition. The grief becomes unauthorized after the official period. So it goes underground — into insomnia, irritability, isolation. The mechanism is not the grief itself. It is the absence of a container that allows the grief to exist in the open.

Chen sees the grief's institutional suppression. The department has no language for ongoing grief. The words available are: move on, stay strong, honor the memory. Each one closes the conversation. The grief has no sanctioned expression after the funeral. The mechanism: the institution's vocabulary creates the suppression. Without words, the grief goes into the body — chronic pain, sleep disruption, emotional flatness. Chen's chest is heavy. The mechanism is linguistic. The body holds what the language cannot express.
