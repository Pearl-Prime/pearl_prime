## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Nowak's phone buzzes on her day off. Her body activates before she reads the message. Heart rate spikes. Hands cold. She reaches for the phone with the certainty that she is being called in. The message is from her mother. Dinner plans. The false alarm converted a family text into a shift recall because her body cannot distinguish between personal and professional notifications. The frame shifts: she changes the notification sound for work. The separation is small. A different tone. But her nervous system needs the different tone. Her shoulders drop when the personal sound plays. The boundary is a ringtone.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/false_alarm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Rivera gets a call at 2 AM on his day off. The captain needs coverage for a sick crew member. Rivera's body says yes before his mind engages. His feet are on the floor. His hand is reaching for the uniform. Then he stops. The false alarm: every request is an emergency. This is not an emergency. The captain has other options. Rivera's hands are shaking from the adrenaline of almost saying yes automatically. The frame collapses: his body's boundary does not exist. His mind has to build one. He calls back: I cannot cover tonight. The captain says: understood. Rivera sits on the bed. His body wanted to go. His boundary held.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Morales hears a sound at home — a crash from the garage. Her body launches into response mode. Heart rate doubled. Hands ready. Then she pauses. Holds the doorframe. Checks: is this a real emergency or is this my training? She walks to the garage at a normal pace. The cat knocked over a paint can. She picks up the can. Her heart rate is still elevated but her response was measured. The false alarm fired. She chose not to obey it. The choosing is the identity: someone who can feel the alarm and select the response.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/false_alarm/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Gutierrez wakes up at 3 AM in his own bed, heart slamming, body ready for a call that is not coming. The false alarm is total. His nervous system is in the fire. He lies still. Puts his hands on his chest. Feels the heart rate. Does not fight it. Does not check his phone. Does not verify that the world is safe. He lies in the alarm and lets his body discharge the activation. Three minutes. Five. The heart rate drops. He did not need to check. He needed to wait. The waiting is the identity: someone who knows the alarm from the inside and trusts it to pass.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Patel maps the false alarm's conditioning. Every tone drop trained her nervous system to expect the worst. After hundreds of calls, the worst-case pathway is the strongest neural pathway. The pathway fires before thought engages. The mechanism is not fear — it is training. Her body was trained to activate on every stimulus. The training was effective. Too effective. It eliminated the delay between stimulus and response that would allow assessment. Her chest tightens. The false alarm is not a malfunction. It is an over-trained response. The muscle is too strong.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/false_alarm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
O'Brien sees the false alarm's deepest function: it has replaced intuition. He no longer has a gut feeling that distinguishes between real threats and false ones. Every feeling is the same feeling — alarm. The alarm replaced the nuance. The mechanism: years of genuine emergencies burned out the calibration between levels. Everything is maximum. A tone drop is maximum. A doorbell is maximum. His child's shout is maximum. O'Brien's chest is pounding. The mechanism is not hypervigilance. It is the death of graduated response.
---
