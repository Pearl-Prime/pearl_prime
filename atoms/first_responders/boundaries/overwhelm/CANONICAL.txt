COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Sofia comes home from a shift and her wife asks: how was work? Sofia starts talking. Three hours later, she's still talking. She's downloaded everything. Every call. Every moment. Her wife is overwhelmed. Sofia realizes: I have no boundary between the job and home. The job comes home completely. No filter. She's overwhelmed her wife with the day. Her wife is tired. Sofia didn't notice the moment when it became too much. She has no internal boundary about what to share.

## TURNING_POINT v01
---

BAND: 3
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Sofia sets a boundary. She tells her wife: I need to talk about work for thirty minutes. Then I need to leave it at the door. Her wife agrees. Sofia practices. The boundary is artificial at first. But it creates space. After thirty minutes, Sofia stops talking. She sits with the overwhelm but doesn't pour it on her wife. The boundary is protecting both of them. The overwhelm doesn't disappear. But it becomes manageable.
