MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity

IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez is covering for two colleagues, running double the patient load, and fielding texts from her captain about a staffing shortage. Her body is humming with exhaustion. Her hands shake from the caffeine keeping her upright. The overwhelm is not from the work. It is from the absence of a line she will not cross. She has no boundary between possible and acceptable. Everything asked is accepted. The frame shifts when she tells the captain: I cannot take the extra shift tomorrow. The captain says: okay. That is the entire conversation. One sentence. Her chest releases. The boundary was one sentence wide. She thought it was a wall.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson sees the overwhelm's cumulative structure. It is not one call that overwhelms. It is the stack. Call on call on call without discharge. Each call adds a layer. The layers compress. The body has no protocol for decompression between calls. The mechanism: the job creates accumulation and provides no subtraction. The overwhelm is an accounting problem — all deposits, no withdrawals. Jackson's chest compresses. The solution is not fewer calls. It is deliberate discharge between them.

Nowak sees the overwhelm's systemic root: it is not personal capacity failure. It is institutional design failure. The shifts are too long. The breaks are too few.
