## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel watches another officer decline overtime without guilt. The officer goes home at shift end. Clear. Clean. Patel has not declined overtime in three years. His chest tightens with the comparison: that officer has boundaries. Patel has compliance. The frame shifts when he realizes the comparison is between someone who says no and someone who forgot no existed. He has not been saying yes. He has been failing to locate the word no. His jaw unclenches. The word exists. It lives where his exhaustion intersects his permission. He finds it.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien watches a colleague set a boundary and get praised for it by the captain. Self-care, the captain calls it. O'Brien set the same boundary last month and got a cold shoulder. The comparison is between two identical actions with different receptions. O'Brien's chest burns. The frame shatters when she realizes: the difference is not the boundary. It is the relationship. The colleague has cultivated the captain's respect through years of visible reliability. O'Brien has too — but the watcher did not let her see that. She sets another boundary. This time she does not compare the reception. She owns the boundary regardless.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/comparison/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kim runs a call alongside the medic whose competence used to shrink Kim's confidence. The comparison fires: they are smoother, faster, more certain. Kim lets the comparison run and focuses on the patient. Kim's IV lands on the first stick. The patient stabilizes. Kim does not look at the other medic for validation. The patient's vitals are the validation. Kim is no longer performing against a benchmark. Kim is performing for the patient. The comparison has lost its audience.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/comparison/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Jackson runs the worst call of her career alongside the paramedic she always compared herself to. The patient is critical. The comparison fires: the other medic is faster, smoother, more certain. Jackson pushes the comparison out of the treatment space. Not later. Now. She works the airway. Her hands are steady because the patient needs steady hands, not because the comparison gave her permission. The patient stabilizes. Both medics contributed. The comparison wanted a ranking. The patient needed a team. Jackson was the team.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Nowak against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Nowak measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Rivera's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
---
