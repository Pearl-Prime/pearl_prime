## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Vasquez sees how grief operates in a culture that does not permit it. The department has no ritual for ongoing grief. There is the funeral. There is the memorial. Then there is silence. The grief continues but the container ended. Vasquez's chest is heavy. The mechanism: the institution processes grief as an event, not a condition. The grief becomes unauthorized after the official period. So it goes underground — into insomnia, irritability, isolation. The mechanism is not the grief itself. It is the absence of a container that allows the grief to exist in the open.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07

COST_TYPE: internal

---
Kim sees the grief's institutional suppression. The department has no language for ongoing grief. The words available are: move on, stay strong, honor the memory. Each one closes the conversation. The grief has no sanctioned expression after the funeral. The mechanism: the institution's vocabulary creates the suppression. Without words, the grief goes into the body — chronic pain, sleep disruption, emotional flatness. Kim's chest is heavy. The mechanism is linguistic. The body holds what the language cannot express.
