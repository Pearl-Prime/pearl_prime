## EXERCISE v01
---

---
When asked something, pause. Breathe before answering. Ask yourself: "Do I want this?" Honest answer only. Say it.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Name one thing you've said yes to. Did you actually want it? Notice. Next time, take time. Then choose.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Someone asks something of you. Feel your body. If tight, say "Let me think." Don't decide immediately. You can say no.
---
