

## RECOGNITION v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/boundaries/spiral/recognition/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 2
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 1
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: pre_awareness
---
Jake didn't set a boundary with a senior officer. The officer asked him to do something. Jake did it. No issue. But his mind started spiraling. What if I messed that up? What if the officer thinks I'm incompetent? The spiral started because he didn't have a boundary about saying no. He just did what was asked. Now he's replaying it, analyzing it, finding evidence of failure. The spiral is feeding on the lack of boundary between appropriate ask and internal responsibility.
