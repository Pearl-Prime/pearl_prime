Patel tracks the spiral's fuel source: incomplete debriefs. Every call that is not fully processed becomes material for the spiral. The unprocessed calls stack in memory and the spiral cycles through them looking for the decision that could have changed the outcome. The mechanism: the spiral is not overthinking.
