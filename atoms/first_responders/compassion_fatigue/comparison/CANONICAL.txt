## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel watches a newer medic cry after a bad call. Genuine tears. Patel felt nothing during the same call. The comparison fires: that medic still feels things. Patel is broken. Her chest is numb. Not tight — numb. The frame shifts when she realizes the comparison is backwards. The newer medic cries because the calls are still new. Patel does not cry because the calls are not new. The difference is exposure, not capacity. Her compassion is not gone. It is exhausted. The newer medic will arrive where Patel is eventually. The comparison was measuring burnout against freshness and calling freshness superior.

O'Brien reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak compassion fatigue intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Nowak against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Nowak measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Rivera's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
