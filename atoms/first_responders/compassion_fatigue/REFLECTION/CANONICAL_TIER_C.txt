

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: automatic
---
Compassion fatigue is the slow numbing of your empathy. The mechanism dials it down so gradually you don't notice. By year five, you're numb. By year ten, you're mechanical.
You show up but you're not there. The mechanism has protected you by erasing your capacity to feel.

## REFLECTION v02
---
family: F2
voice_mode: companion
mechanism_emphasis: protective
---
The protective mechanism dials down your empathy because the full dose is unsustainable. Your nervous system says: I can't keep feeling this much.
So it numbs you. The cost is your humanity.
---

## REFLECTION v03
---

voice_mode: mirror
mechanism_emphasis: invisible
---
