## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Press your hand on your heart. Feel your own heartbeat. You are alive. Your pain is yours alone. Breathe.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Shake out your whole body for one minute. Imagine releasing what isn't yours. Heavy breath out. Light breath in. Done.
---

## EXERCISE v03

---
Take a warm shower. Feel the water only on your skin. Not their pain. Yours. Just you. Warmth returns.
