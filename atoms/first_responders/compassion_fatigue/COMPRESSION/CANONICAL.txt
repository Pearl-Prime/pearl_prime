## COMPRESSION v31

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C5
Try this: at the end of a shift, name one thing that mattered. Not the win. Not the save. The detail that landed. "The grandfather thanked me by name." "The kid's hand stopped shaking." Naming the detail rebuilds the channel between the work and the meaning. The numbness was a circuit breaker, not a permanent state.
