## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez monitors her patients' families for emotional reactions she should be having. The wife cries. Gutierrez notes the crying. Does not feel it. The husband asks a question with a trembling voice. Gutierrez notes the tremor. Does not resonate. She is watching emotion happen without participating in it. Her neck is rigid from the observation. The frame shifts when she realizes watching emotion is not the same as being incapable of emotion. She is guarding against feeling by converting it to data. The watcher is protecting her from her own compassion. She does not need the protection anymore. The protection is the problem.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel reaches the breaking point. The watcher pattern at peak compassion fatigue intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Patel watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Patel stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Vasquez sits in the restaurant with his family. His back is to the door. He chose this seat deliberately. His body protests — the watcher wants visual access to the entrance. Vasquez stays. Eats. His neck is stiff for twenty minutes. Then it softens. The watcher is running but Vasquez is not obeying it. The meal continues. His daughter tells a story. He hears the story. Not the watcher. The daughter. The watcher does not need his attention right now. His daughter does.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kim is off duty at a crowded stadium with her family. The watcher is running at maximum: exits, threats, crowd density, choke points. Her neck is rotating. Her daughter pulls her hand: look, the mascot. Kim looks at the mascot. For thirty seconds, the watcher loses the feed. The mascot is ridiculous. Her daughter is laughing. Kim laughs. The watcher reclaims the feed after the laugh fades. But the thirty seconds existed. Kim was a parent for thirty seconds inside the watcher's shift. She will build more seconds. The seconds accumulate.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson identifies the watcher's origin story. It began on a specific call — the one where she was not watching and something went wrong. That call installed the watcher permanently. The mechanism: one moment of missed attention became a lifetime of hyperattention. The watcher is a security system installed by a single breach. The breach was years ago. The security system has not been updated. It still protects against the exact threat from the exact call. Her shoulders are rigid. The watcher is guarding a door that was breached once, in a building that no longer exists.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak sees the watcher's deepest paradox: it makes him less safe. The constant scanning depletes the attention needed for actual threat response. He is so busy watching for everything that he cannot focus on anything. The watcher has reduced his effectiveness while convincing him it is the source of his effectiveness. His neck is stone. The mechanism: the watcher is a net negative. It consumes more safety than it produces. The vigilance is not protective. It is expensive. It costs more than the threats it theoretically prevents.
