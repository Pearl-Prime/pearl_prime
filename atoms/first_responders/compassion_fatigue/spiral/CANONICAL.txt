BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Morales loops on whether he chose the wrong profession. The loop starts with a call where he felt nothing, moves to questioning his identity, moves to imagining a different life, moves to guilt about imagining a different life, and returns to the call where he felt nothing. His chest aches with each rotation. The spiral is not career planning. It is the compassion fatigue questioning his fundamental choice. The frame shifts when he asks himself: do I want to leave, or do I want to feel again? The answer is immediate — feel again. The spiral was asking the wrong question. He does not need a new career. He needs recovery.

Writes the facts on a napkin: patient found at this time, interventions started at this time, outcome at this time. Facts. Not hypotheticals. The napkin is small. The facts are small. ## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/spiral/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Vasquez finishes the worst call of the year and the spiral launches immediately. What if. What if. What if. The spiral has maximum material — the call provided a hundred decision points. Vasquez lets the spiral run for five minutes. Watches it. Then says to his partner: I need to debrief. Not the spiral. The debrief. The debrief has structure: what happened, what we did, what the outcome was. The spiral is infinite. The debrief is finite. He chooses the finite version. The spiral does not stop. But he stops following it into the infinite.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/compassion_fatigue/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Kim tracks the spiral's fuel source: incomplete debriefs. Every call that is not fully processed becomes material for the spiral. The unprocessed calls stack in memory and the spiral cycles through them looking for the decision that could have changed the outcome. The mechanism: the spiral is not overthinking. It is under-processing. The calls were never completed in Kim's nervous system. The spiral is the nervous system's attempt to finish them. His chest tightens. The spiral is not the problem. The incomplete processing is the problem.

Jackson sees the spiral's deepest layer: it is a grief response disguised as analysis. Every spiral about a call is really about the calls she cannot undo. The old call is the engine.
