## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/burnout/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Morales watches a colleague walk away from the job after fifteen years. Clean exit. The colleague looks relieved. Morales is still here at year eighteen, running on empty. The comparison fires: that person had the courage to leave. Morales does not. His chest tightens. The frame shifts when the colleague calls him a week later, crying. The relief was performance. The exit was terrifying. The comparison between Morales's stuck and the colleague's free was fiction. Both are struggling. One is struggling inside the job. One is struggling outside it. Neither is easier.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/burnout/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak burnout intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Gutierrez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Gutierrez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
O'Brien runs a call alongside the medic whose competence used to shrink O'Brien's confidence. The comparison fires: they are smoother, faster, more certain. O'Brien lets the comparison run and focuses on the patient. O'Brien's IV lands on the first stick. The patient stabilizes. O'Brien does not look at the other medic for validation. The patient's vitals are the validation. O'Brien is no longer performing against a benchmark. O'Brien is performing for the patient. The comparison has lost its audience.

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5

---
Vasquez runs the worst call of her career alongside the paramedic she always compared herself to. The patient is critical. The comparison fires: the other medic is faster, smoother, more certain. Vasquez pushes the comparison out of the treatment space. Not later. Now. She works the airway. Her hands are steady because the patient needs steady hands, not because the comparison gave her permission. The patient stabilizes. Both medics contributed. The comparison wanted a ranking. The patient needed a team. Vasquez was the team.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/burnout/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Kim identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Kim against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Kim measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/burnout/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Jackson's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
