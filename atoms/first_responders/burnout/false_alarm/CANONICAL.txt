IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien feels a wave of dread pulling into the station parking lot. Her body reads the building as a threat — adrenaline, chest compression, hands cold on the steering wheel. There is no active call. No emergency. Just the building. The false alarm is the burnout expressing itself as location-based anxiety. The frame shifts: her body is not afraid of the station. They are not the same thing.

COST_TYPE: identity

---
Jackson hears a sound at home — a crash from the garage. Her body launches into response mode. Heart rate doubled. Hands ready. Then she pauses. Holds the doorframe. Checks: is this a real emergency or is this my training? She walks to the garage at a normal pace. The cat knocked over a paint can. She picks up the can. Her heart rate is still elevated but her response was measured. The false alarm fired. She chose not to obey it. The choosing is the identity: someone who can feel the alarm and select the response.

Nowak wakes up at 3 AM in his own bed, heart slamming, body ready for a call that is not coming. The false alarm is total. His nervous system is in the fire. He lies still. Puts his hands on his chest. Feels the heart rate. Does not fight it. Does not check his phone. Does not verify that the world is safe. The waiting is the identity: someone who knows the alarm from the inside and trusts it to pass.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera maps the false alarm's conditioning. Every tone drop trained her nervous system to expect the worst. After hundreds of calls, the worst-case pathway is the strongest neural pathway. The pathway fires before thought engages. The mechanism is not fear — it is training. Her body was trained to activate on every stimulus. The training was effective. Too effective. It eliminated the delay between stimulus and response that would allow assessment. Her chest tightens. The false alarm is not a malfunction. It is an over-trained response. The muscle is too strong.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07

---
Chen sees the false alarm's deepest function: it has replaced intuition. He no longer has a gut feeling that distinguishes between real threats and false ones. Every feeling is the same feeling — alarm. The alarm replaced the nuance. The mechanism: years of genuine emergencies burned out the calibration between levels. Everything is maximum. A tone drop is maximum. A doorbell is maximum. His child's shout is maximum. Chen's chest is pounding. The mechanism is not hypervigilance. It is the death of graduated response.
