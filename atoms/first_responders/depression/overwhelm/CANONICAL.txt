## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez cannot get out of bed. Not because the day is too hard. Because the day exists. The sheer fact of another day — with its hours and tasks and interactions — pins her to the mattress. Her chest is heavy. Her limbs are concrete. The overwhelm is not about volume. It is about the expectation of engagement. The frame shifts when she makes one decision: put feet on floor. Just that. Not the day. Not the shift. Not the calls. Feet on floor. The decision is tiny. The overwhelm needed the whole day to be impossible. Feet on floor is possible. She puts her feet on the floor.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Kim reaches the breaking point. The overwhelm pattern at peak depression intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Kim watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Kim stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nowak is on her third consecutive call. The first two are still in her body: the chest pain patient in her shoulders, the MVA in her hands. The third call begins. She compartmentalizes not by ignoring the first two but by acknowledging them: I am carrying two calls. I am running a third. Three is my load right now. The acknowledgment creates the capacity. Naming the weight does not remove it but it organizes it. She runs the third call. Clean. Competent. Full.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Rivera is on a mass casualty incident. Multiple patients. Limited resources. Maximum chaos. The overwhelm is total — too many needs, too few hands, too little time. Rivera triages. Not perfectly. Not without anguish. But he triages. The overwhelm does not paralyze him because he has built the practice of choosing in impossible conditions. He chooses who gets care first. The choosing costs something. He feels the cost in his sternum. He chooses anyway. The overwhelm is the environment. The choosing is the character.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen sees the overwhelm's cumulative structure. It is not one call that overwhelms. It is the stack. Call on call on call without discharge. Each call adds a layer. The layers compress. The body has no protocol for decompression between calls. The mechanism: the job creates accumulation and provides no subtraction. The overwhelm is an accounting problem — all deposits, no withdrawals. Chen's chest compresses. The solution is not fewer calls. It is deliberate discharge between them.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Morales sees the overwhelm's systemic root: it is not personal capacity failure. It is institutional design failure. The shifts are too long. The breaks are too few. The emotional load is unaccounted for. The mechanism: the institution measures bodies on rigs, not nervous systems in recovery. Morales is not overwhelmed because she is weak. She is overwhelmed because the system was designed for machines and staffed with humans. Her chest compresses. The mechanism is structural. No amount of personal resilience fixes structural insufficiency.
---
