## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/grief/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel grieves his own aliveness. He remembers being someone who wanted things. Wanted the next shift. Wanted the next challenge. Wanted dinner. The depression has converted wanting into a memory rather than a present-tense experience. His chest aches for desire itself. The frame shifts when he notices he wants the aching to stop. The wanting is not gone. It is pointed at relief instead of achievement. But wanting is wanting. His jaw softens. The depression said he cannot want. He is wanting right now.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/grief/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien reaches the breaking point. The grief pattern at peak depression intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. O'Brien watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. O'Brien stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/grief/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kim visits the memorial wall. His partner's name is there. The grief arrives, heavy, expected. Kim stands with it. Does not speak. Does not try to make meaning. Just stands with the weight in his chest and the cold in his hands. Five minutes. Then he returns to shift. The grief goes with him — not as a burden but as a companion. He runs the next call with his partner's name in his chest and his own skills in his hands. Both are present. Neither is in charge.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/grief/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Jackson speaks at the memorial for a colleague. Her voice breaks. Her chest is shattered. She reads the words she wrote through tears that make the paper blurry. She does not apologize for the tears. Does not pause to compose herself. She reads through the breaking. The grief is in every word and every word is true. The room holds the grief with her. She finishes the speech. The grief does not finish. But the speech does. She returns to her seat with the grief fully visible on her face. The visibility is the identity. She grieves openly, in uniform, without diminishment.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak sees how grief operates in a culture that does not permit it. The department has no ritual for ongoing grief. There is the funeral. There is the memorial. Then there is silence. The grief continues but the container ended. Nowak's chest is heavy. The mechanism: the institution processes grief as an event, not a condition. The grief becomes unauthorized after the official period. So it goes underground — into insomnia, irritability, isolation. The mechanism is not the grief itself. It is the absence of a container that allows the grief to exist in the open.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/grief/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees the grief's institutional suppression. The department has no language for ongoing grief. The words available are: move on, stay strong, honor the memory. Each one closes the conversation. The grief has no sanctioned expression after the funeral. The mechanism: the institution's vocabulary creates the suppression. Without words, the grief goes into the body — chronic pain, sleep disruption, emotional flatness. Rivera's chest is heavy. The mechanism is linguistic. The body holds what the language cannot express.
