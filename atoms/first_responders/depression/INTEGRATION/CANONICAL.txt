

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: STILL-HERE
The flatness isn't proof you left. It's proof you're still standing watch. Your nervous system locked down to protect you and it did its job so well you can't remember why you were protecting yourself from. You're still the person they trust. You're just not trusting the person you are right now. Stay still long enough to let your own body remind you it's real.

## INTEGRATION v03
---
mode: BODY-LANDED
Your body is not broken. It's remembering how to exist when nothing's on fire. That's harder than crisis. Crisis is the design it knows. This slow time is a language it forgot. Feel for it in your hands. In the way your breath lands. In the ground underneath you. You're not waiting for feeling to come back. You're learning to recognize the feeling you already have even when it's quiet.

## INTEGRATION v05
---
mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
There's someone on your crew who knows what flatness is and won't name it that way. They'll just say the shift hit different. They'll ask if you're okay and you can say no and they won't try to fix you. They'll just stand there with you in the not okay and that's enough. That's crew. That's where you start.

## INTEGRATION v07
---
mode: BODY-LANDED
Right now you're still here. You showed up. Your body remembered how to do the job even when your mind checked out. That's not nothing. That's proof the machine is still running. Stand with that. The feeling will follow but not today. Today you're just here. That's the hold. That's the station. That's enough.
