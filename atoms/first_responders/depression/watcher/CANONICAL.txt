## TURNING_POINT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/watcher/turning_point/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Morales monitors his own mood the way he monitors vital signs on a patient. Every hour: am I worse? Am I better? Any change? The watching converts the depression into a patient to be assessed rather than an experience to be had. His neck is stiff from the internal surveillance. The frame shifts when his therapist says: stop checking your mood and start describing what you notice. The difference is subtle. Checking is clinical. Describing is personal. He describes: heavy. Dark. A weight behind my sternum. The description is the frame shifting. He is inside the experience now. Not monitoring it from above.
---

## TURNING_POINT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/watcher/turning_point/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez reaches the breaking point. The watcher pattern at peak depression intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Gutierrez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Gutierrez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v01
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/watcher/embodiment/micro/v01.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
O'Brien sits in the restaurant with his family. His back is to the door. He chose this seat deliberately. His body protests — the watcher wants visual access to the entrance. O'Brien stays. Eats. His neck is stiff for twenty minutes. Then it softens. The watcher is running but O'Brien is not obeying it. The meal continues. His daughter tells a story. He hears the story. Not the watcher. The daughter. The watcher does not need his attention right now. His daughter does.
---

## EMBODIMENT v02
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/watcher/embodiment/micro/v02.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Vasquez is off duty at a crowded stadium with her family. The watcher is running at maximum: exits, threats, crowd density, choke points. Her neck is rotating. Her daughter pulls her hand: look, the mascot. Vasquez looks at the mascot. For thirty seconds, the watcher loses the feed. The mascot is ridiculous. Her daughter is laughing. Vasquez laughs. The watcher reclaims the feed after the laugh fades. But the thirty seconds existed. Vasquez was a parent for thirty seconds inside the watcher's shift. She will build more seconds. The seconds accumulate.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Kim identifies the watcher's origin story. It began on a specific call — the one where she was not watching and something went wrong. That call installed the watcher permanently. The mechanism: one moment of missed attention became a lifetime of hyperattention. The watcher is a security system installed by a single breach. The breach was years ago. The security system has not been updated. It still protects against the exact threat from the exact call. Her shoulders are rigid. The watcher is guarding a door that was breached once, in a building that no longer exists.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/watcher/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson sees the watcher's deepest paradox: it makes him less safe. The constant scanning depletes the attention needed for actual threat response. He is so busy watching for everything that he cannot focus on anything. The watcher has reduced his effectiveness while convincing him it is the source of his effectiveness. His neck is stone. The mechanism: the watcher is a net negative. It consumes more safety than it produces. The vigilance is not protective. It is expensive. It costs more than the threats it theoretically prevents.
