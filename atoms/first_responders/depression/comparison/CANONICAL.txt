## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Gutierrez watches his crew joke in the kitchen. They are laughing. He is sitting among them, silent, his coffee going cold. The comparison fires: they feel things. He is hollow. His chest is heavy with the absence of feeling, which is its own kind of weight. The frame shifts when one of the crew, still laughing, catches Gutierrez's eye and gives a small nod. The nod says: I see you. The comparison assumed their laughter meant they were okay. The nod suggests at least one of them is performing too. The heaviness does not lift. But the isolation cracks.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Patel reaches the breaking point. The comparison pattern at peak depression intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Patel watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Patel stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/comparison/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Vasquez runs a call alongside the medic whose competence used to shrink Vasquez's confidence. The comparison fires: they are smoother, faster, more certain. Vasquez lets the comparison run and focuses on the patient. Vasquez's IV lands on the first stick. The patient stabilizes. Vasquez does not look at the other medic for validation. The patient's vitals are the validation. Vasquez is no longer performing against a benchmark. Vasquez is performing for the patient. The comparison has lost its audience.

---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Kim runs the worst call of her career alongside the paramedic she always compared herself to. The patient is critical. The comparison fires: the other medic is faster, smoother, more certain. Kim pushes the comparison out of the treatment space. Not later. Now. She works the airway. Her hands are steady because the patient needs steady hands, not because the comparison gave her permission. The patient stabilizes. Both medics contributed. The comparison wanted a ranking. The patient needed a team. Kim was the team.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Jackson identifies how the comparison operates in the station. It selects one trait from one person and measures Jackson against it. Strength from this officer. Speed from that medic. Calm from the captain. The comparison builds a composite — someone who has the best trait of everyone simultaneously. Jackson measures himself against this impossible person and fails every time. His chest tightens as the mechanism clicks into focus: he is competing against a team disguised as a single individual. No one can beat a composite.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Nowak sees the comparison's deepest cut: it is not about competence. It is about permission. The comparison to the calm veteran, the steady captain, the unshakeable medic — all of it is really asking: am I allowed to struggle? The answer the comparison gives is always no. Others do not struggle. Therefore struggling is not permitted. The mechanism: the comparison is a permission gate. It grants permission to perform and denies permission to suffer. Nowak's chest breaks open. She has been seeking permission from a system that only grants one kind.
