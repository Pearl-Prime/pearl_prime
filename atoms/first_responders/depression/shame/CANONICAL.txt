## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/shame/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Rivera is ashamed of the depression itself. He is a first responder. He responds. He does not lie on couches staring at ceilings. His face burns even in the empty room. The shame says strong people do not have depression. His chest tightens. The frame shifts when he reads a department bulletin about mental health support: 30 percent of first responders report depressive symptoms. Thirty percent. He is not weak. He is statistically normal. The shame needed him to be the only one. He is one of three in ten.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/shame/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Chen reaches the breaking point. The shame pattern at peak depression intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Chen watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Chen stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/shame/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Gutierrez tells his crew he needs a minute after a bad call. In front of everyone. His face warms. The old shame says: weakness. Gutierrez walks to the bay door. Breathes for sixty seconds. Returns. The crew does not look at him differently. One of them nods. The minute was not weakness. It was modeling. Gutierrez took the minute he needed and the crew saw that taking a minute is allowed. The shame wanted him to perform invulnerability. He performed honesty. The honesty is the identity now.
---

## EMBODIMENT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/shame/embodiment/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Patel tells her crew she is going to therapy. In the kitchen. At breakfast. Out loud. Her face is warm. The shame is present — a first responder admitting she needs help. She says it clearly and does not soften it with a joke. The crew is quiet for three seconds. Then one of them says: who is your therapist? I need one too. The shame expected isolation. It got company. The announcement is not easy. But it is honest. The honest is the identity now.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
O'Brien identifies the shame's enforcement mechanism in first responder culture. The culture permits physical injury but not emotional injury. A broken bone gets sympathy and sick leave. A broken spirit gets silence and suspicion. The shame is not personal. It is institutional. The mechanism: the culture rewards stoicism and punishes vulnerability. O'Brien has been following a script written by the institution, not by her own values. Her face warms as the mechanism becomes visible. The shame is a cultural product, not a personal failing.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/depression/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Vasquez sees the shame's generational transmission. His captain learned it from his captain. The silence, the stoicism, the punishment of vulnerability — it passes through the ranks like an inherited disease. The mechanism: shame is not felt. It is taught. It is modeled. The new recruit learns it from the veteran's silence. The veteran learned it from the captain's disapproval. The captain learned it from the culture's mythology of the unbreakable hero. Vasquez's face burns. He is not carrying his own shame. He is carrying decades of institutional shame that was handed down like equipment.
---
