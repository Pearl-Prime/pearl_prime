## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_stress/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
O'Brien sits at the kitchen table with bills fanned out like a losing poker hand. Mortgage, utilities, car payment, insurance, the kid's braces. Each one is payable. Together they are a wall. His chest compresses. His hands are flat on the table. The overwhelm is the sum, not the parts. The frame shifts when his partner sits down and picks up one bill. They split the stack. His half is three bills. Three bills are possible. The overwhelm needed the whole table. Half the table is a different calculation.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_stress/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vasquez reaches the breaking point. The overwhelm pattern at peak financial stress intensity has stripped everything back to bone. Chest cracking. Hands shaking. The old frame is not bending. It is shattering. Vasquez watches the pieces fall and does not try to reassemble them. The frame told a story about why this pain was the price of the work. The story was wrong. The pain is real. The price was manufactured by a system that confuses endurance with character. Vasquez stands in the wreckage of the old explanation. Something new is visible in the gap. Not a solution. A direction. The first direction that does not lead back to the same station, the same silence, the same acceptance of the unacceptable.

Jackson is on her third consecutive call. The first two are still in her body: the chest pain patient in her shoulders, the MVA in her hands. The third call begins. She compartmentalizes not by ignoring the first two but by acknowledging them: I am carrying two calls. I am running a third. Three is my load right now. The acknowledgment creates the capacity. Nowak is on a mass casualty incident. Multiple patients. Limited resources. Maximum chaos. The overwhelm is total — too many needs, too few hands, too little time. Nowak triages. Not perfectly. Not without anguish. But he triages. The overwhelm does not paralyze him because he has built the practice of choosing in impossible conditions. He chooses who gets care first. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_stress/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Rivera sees the overwhelm's cumulative structure. It is not one call that overwhelms. It is the stack. Call on call on call without discharge. Each call adds a layer. The layers compress. The body has no protocol for decompression between calls. The mechanism: the job creates accumulation and provides no subtraction. The overwhelm is an accounting problem — all deposits, no withdrawals. Rivera's chest compresses. The solution is not fewer calls. It is deliberate discharge between them.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v07
---
path: story_atoms/first_responders/anchored/financial_stress/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Chen sees the overwhelm's systemic root: it is not personal capacity failure. It is institutional design failure. The shifts are too long. The breaks are too few. The emotional load is unaccounted for. The mechanism: the institution measures bodies on rigs, not nervous systems in recovery. Chen is not overwhelmed because she is weak. She is overwhelmed because the system was designed for machines and staffed with humans. Her chest compresses. The mechanism is structural. No amount of personal resilience fixes structural insufficiency.
