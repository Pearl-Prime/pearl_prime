

## INTEGRATION v01
---
mode: STILL-HERE

You can survive the next part too.

## INTEGRATION v03
---
mode: BODY-LANDED
The money problem is real but it's not in your mind alone. It's in your nervous system. It's in your shoulders at the end of the shift. It's in the way you check your account. It's in the sleep that doesn't come because the math's running all night. Your body's carrying this. Your body knows this is unsustainable. Listen to it. Not to panic. Just to information. Your body's telling you something's got to shift.

## INTEGRATION v05
---
mode: SOMEONE-ELSE
There's someone on the crew who's figured out how to make the money feel slightly less impossible. Not because they make more. But because they've made different choices or they're just further along the acceptance curve or they've built something outside the job. Find that person. Not to copy them. But to understand that different ways through exist. They're on the same path. They just found a rhythm that works slightly better. That's possible for you too.

## INTEGRATION v07

mode: BODY-LANDED
Your nervous system knows the financial stress isn't safe. It knows you're not secure. It knows the family's not as protected as it should be. It's right.
