--- variant: v01
You're allowed to not solve anything between 11 PM and 7 AM. Whatever the mind wants to review, reorganize, or resolve can wait until morning. You have permission to let the night be for rest, not for the unpaid overtime of mental processing.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to go to bed with things unfinished. The dishes, the emails, the emotional conversation that didn't fully resolve. You're allowed to sleep with loose ends. Perfectionism at midnight is still perfectionism. You're allowed to release it.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to need help with the sleep. Not more melatonin. Real help. The kind that addresses why your nervous system won't let you rest. You have permission to treat the sleeplessness as a real problem that deserves real attention, not just another thing you manage on your own.
