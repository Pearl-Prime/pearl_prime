--- variant: v01
The moment you lie down, the second shift starts. Not the work emails -- the review. Did you handle the meeting correctly? Did the kids get enough attention? Did you remember to reply to your mother? Sleep anxiety for women carrying dual loads isn't about sleep. It's about the audit that starts when the doing stops.

--- variant: v02
You're exhausted but wired. The body is begging for sleep while the mind is running tomorrow's logistics, last week's unresolved conversation, and the low-grade guilt of not being more present today. Sleep anxiety is the collision between physical depletion and a mind that was trained to never be off duty.

--- variant: v03
You've been going since 6 AM and it's midnight and you still can't sleep because there's a feeling you've been outrunning all day that finally caught up. The anxiety at night isn't new anxiety. It's the day's accumulated emotional debt presenting the invoice the moment your body stops moving.
