## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Layla watches the clock with her chest tight. Her fingers dig into her own palms. Crescents form in the skin. She watches the thought breed and the offspring breed before the first resolves. The frame that has held everything in place — thinking harder will eventually solve the problem — strains under the weight of what Layla sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Layla feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The next thought does not contain the answer any more than the last one did. Layla does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Her hands go completely numb. Pins and needles race up both forearms. Vera is lying awake at 1 AM running tomorrow's schedule when she feels the thoughts multiply beyond counting and the chain reaction passes critical mass. The operating assumption — the answer lives somewhere in the next thought if the thinking continues — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Vera does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. The thinking is the problem wearing the mask of the solution. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Nora moves through in bed calculating how many hours of sleep remain. Her jaw locks. Teeth press together hard enough to ache. The spiral arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Nora drops the thread mid-sentence and stands in the silence that follows. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Nora acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Nora is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.
---

Lying awake at 1 am running tomorrow's schedule. Cold sweat sheets across her back. Her shirt clings to the skin. The spiral pushes with full force against her sternum. Elena drops the thread mid-sentence and stands in the silence that follows. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Elena moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/spiral/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
In bed calculating how many hours of sleep remain. Weight presses her chest flat. Breathing becomes a conscious act. The spiral operates in plain view for the first time. Camille catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The chain builds itself because each link feels like evidence, when in fact each link is only a guess wearing the costume of a fact. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Camille watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
