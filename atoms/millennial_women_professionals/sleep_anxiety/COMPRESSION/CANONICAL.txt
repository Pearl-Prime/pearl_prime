

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C3
The sleep anxiety is not about sleep. It's about control. You want to control whether you're anxious, whether you sleep, how you feel. You want to optimize everything including your nervous system. You can't. Once you accept what you can't control, the grip of the anxiety loosens, and paradoxically, your sleep often improves.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C5
Try this: before bed, write down three things you are still carrying. Not solutions. Just the carrying. The page is allowed to know what your nervous system has been protecting your conscious mind from at midnight. You are not transferring the load. You are telling the body that the load has been acknowledged. That is often what the body was waiting for.
