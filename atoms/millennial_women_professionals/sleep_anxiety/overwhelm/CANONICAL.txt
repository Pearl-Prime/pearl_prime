## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Jess stands in bed calculating how many hours of sleep remain. The muscles in her back seize from the base of the skull downward. She watches the list grow while the clock shrinks. The frame that has held everything in place — the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole — strains under the weight of what Jess sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Jess feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The pile is made of individual items that can be addressed one at a time. Jess does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
In bed calculating how many hours of sleep remain. Camille feels input exceeds all bandwidth — the queue locks and the screen goes white. Her legs buckle. Camille catches the doorframe before going down. For months the frame has held: everything is equally urgent and nothing can wait. Now Camille watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Falling behind is a position, not a verdict — movement forward begins with one step. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Camille stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.
---

Claire moves through in bed calculating how many hours of sleep remain. Pressure mounts behind her eyes like a wave building against a wall. The overwhelm arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Claire puts everything down except the next single action and begins there. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Claire acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Claire is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
In bed calculating how many hours of sleep remain. Her throat closes around the words. The vocal cords tighten. The overwhelm operates in plain view for the first time. Zara catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The system conflates volume with urgency, treating everything as equally critical when triage is the actual need. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Zara watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/sleep_anxiety/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Her legs buckle. Simone catches the doorframe before going down. Simone lies awake at 1 AM running tomorrow's schedule. The overwhelm runs its program at full speed. Simone watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the overwhelm creates the illusion that everything must be handled simultaneously, hiding the fact that sequence is always possible. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Simone sees this now. Her ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Simone stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not her name.
---
