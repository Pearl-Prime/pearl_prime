

## INTEGRATION v01
---
integration_mode: BODY-LANDED
reframe_type: BODY-FACT
---
I can feel my grief in my chest. It's a physical weight. Not metaphorical. Actual. It lives where my heart is. And I'm learning to let it stay there instead of trying to move it. The urge is always to fix the grief, to resolve it, to make it smaller. But grief isn't meant to be fixed. It's meant to be carried. I'm learning to carry it. To move through my days with this weight in my chest and know that the weight is how the person stays close.
My grief is my love continuing.
