--- variant: v01
The courage to be imperfect is the one that changes everything. Not imperfect at work -- imperfect at the whole performance. The takeaway is that the armor worked, and it also cost you. The next chapter requires putting it down, not polishing it.

--- variant: v02
You've been brave in all the visible ways. The takeaway is that the invisible bravery -- the kind aimed at your own feelings, your own needs, your own truth -- is the one that's been waiting. It doesn't come with a promotion or recognition. It comes with relief.

--- variant: v03
Courage for women in professional spaces has always been framed as leaning in. The takeaway here is that sometimes the courageous move is leaning back. Into yourself. Into the feelings you've been too busy achieving to notice.
