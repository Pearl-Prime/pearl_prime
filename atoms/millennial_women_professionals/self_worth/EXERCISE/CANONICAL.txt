

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Stand in front of your mirror. Look at yourself. Name three good things you did. Three things today. At work. At home. Anywhere. Name them. Out loud. Your deeds have value. Say it. Hear yourself say it.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Write down an accomplishment you're proud of. Something you did. Something that matters. Write it down. Now tell one person. You're making yourself visible. You're claiming your work. Do this weekly.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Look in the mirror. Say: I am worthy. Say it five times. Hear yourself. Your voice is small at first. Keep going. By five it gets louder. This is reprogramming. This is the work.
---

## EXERCISE v04
---

---
Document something you made. A project. An idea. A solution. Take a photo. Write about it. Save it. You're keeping evidence of your worth. You're proving you exist. Keep the evidence.
---

## EXERCISE v05
---

---
Stop being invisible in a meeting. Say one thing. Anything. Your opinion matters. Say it. Your voice has value. Speak. Practice speaking once a meeting. That's it. One thing. That's enough.
---

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
Ask for feedback specifically on something you're proud of. Not general feedback. Specific. Ask someone to reflect on your work. You're asking to be seen. You're asking for recognition. Ask. You deserve to be seen.

## EXERCISE v07
---

---
Make a list of your skills. Don't be modest. Really list them. Teaching. Writing. Listening. Problem solving. What can you do. Write ten. Read them out loud. You're skilled. You're capable. See that.

## EXERCISE v08

---

## EXERCISE v09

## EXERCISE v10
---

---

## EXERCISE v12

---

## EXERCISE v13

## EXERCISE v14
