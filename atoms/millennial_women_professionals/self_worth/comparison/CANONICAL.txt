## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/comparison/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Tara reads her own performance review in the conference room. Her stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. She watches someone younger receive the role. The frame that has held everything in place — standing relative to others is the only metric that counts — strains under the weight of what Tara sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Tara feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The metric measures nothing real — it is a machine for producing inadequacy. Tara does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/comparison/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
The walls close in. Her lungs refuse to expand against the compression. Priya is looking at the achievement wall in the lobby when she sees the ranking arrives and the number is undeniable. The operating assumption — standing relative to others is the only metric that counts — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Priya does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. The scoreboard tracks a game nobody consented to play. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/comparison/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Comparing her trajectory to her graduate school cohort. Pressure mounts behind her eyes like a wave building against a wall. The comparison pushes with full force against her sternum. Vera walks past the comparison without pausing to measure. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Vera moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.
---

## EMBODIMENT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/comparison/embodiment/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Priya looks at the achievement wall in the lobby. Her hands go completely numb. Pins and needles race up both forearms. The comparison arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Priya closes the ranking tab and opens the project that matters. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Priya acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Priya is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/comparison/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Looking at the achievement wall in the lobby. A tremor moves through her hands. Zara presses them flat against the surface. The comparison operates in plain view for the first time. Zara catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The measuring instrument calibrates itself to find deficiency, discarding accomplishments that do not fit the verdict. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Zara watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
