

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: automatic
---
Your sense of your own value is inseparable from productivity. You are what you do. Email sent. Project completed. Problem solved. When you're producing, you know who you are. When you're resting, you disappear. Rest feels like proof you're not essential. So you don't rest. The mechanism is efficient. Productivity equals worth equals safety. But you only feel real when working. And only if it matters enough. Ordinary work doesn't count. It has to be impressive. The kind of work that proves you're not ordinary.
