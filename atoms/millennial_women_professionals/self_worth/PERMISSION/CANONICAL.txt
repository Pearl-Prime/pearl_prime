--- variant: v01
You're allowed to matter even on days when you accomplish nothing. Your worth isn't earned. It exists. You have permission to stop treating every unproductive hour as evidence of your insufficiency.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to take up space without earning it. Not just physical space -- emotional space, conversational space, space in relationships. You're allowed to have needs, opinions, and desires that aren't filtered through what's convenient for everyone else.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to be enough exactly as you are today. Not the future version with the better job, the better body, the better habits. This version. You have permission to stop treating the present you as the rough draft of someone more deserving of love.
