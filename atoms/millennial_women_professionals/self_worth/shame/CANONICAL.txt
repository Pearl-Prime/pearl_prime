## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/shame/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Her shoulders climb toward her ears and lock there. Ren is comparing her trajectory to her graduate school cohort when she feels exposed in front of the people who matter most. The operating assumption — the exposure confirms the defect that was always there — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Ren does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. The flaw is an event, bounded and specific — not a self, permanent and total. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/shame/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Looking at the achievement wall in the lobby. Jess feels the core gets laid open in full view with no buffer and no exit. Her vision narrows to a single point. The periphery dissolves. For months the frame has held: the shame is a verdict, delivered by the body, and the body does not lie. Now Jess watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. The shame is a sensation moving through the body, not a verdict handed down by truth. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Jess stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.
---

Priya remains upright in the visibility and lets the shame be sensation without becoming story. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. ## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Her breath catches mid-inhale. The air thickens. Layla reads her own performance review in the conference room. The shame runs its program at full speed. Layla watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the shame machinery feeds on particular incidents to fuel a general indictment against the entire self. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Layla sees this now. Her ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Layla stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not her name.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/self_worth/shame/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Camille pauses while comparing her trajectory to her graduate school cohort. Her vision narrows to a single point. The periphery dissolves. The shame has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The shame converts specific, bounded events into proof of fundamental deficiency while skipping every piece of contrary evidence. Camille traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Camille watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
---
