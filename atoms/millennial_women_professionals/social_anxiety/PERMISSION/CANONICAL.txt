--- variant: v01
You're allowed to not be 'on' socially. You have permission to show up at the dinner and be quiet, to attend the event and leave early, to be present without being sparkling. The social performance is optional. Your presence is enough even when it's low-energy.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to cancel without a reason anyone would consider valid. 'I'm too tired' is valid. 'I don't want to' is valid. You're allowed to protect your social energy the same way you protect your time at work -- intentionally and without guilt.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to need different social things than you used to. Your social needs have changed because you've changed. You have permission to stop forcing yourself into social patterns that no longer fit and to seek connections that meet you where you actually are.
