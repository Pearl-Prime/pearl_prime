## TAKEAWAY v01
---
---
Presence was never a place you arrive at and stay. It's a place you keep walking back to, on purpose, meeting after meeting.
---

## TAKEAWAY v02
---
---
Your mind will always wander to the Slack thread. Your job is just to notice faster and come back sooner.
---

## TAKEAWAY v03
---
---
The meeting doesn't remember what you missed. You do — as a blank spot where a memory should be.
---

## TAKEAWAY v04
---
---
Ten short returns beat one long stretch of forced focus that snaps by minute six.
---

## TAKEAWAY v05
---
---
You're not training yourself to never leave the room. You're training how fast you come back.
---

## TAKEAWAY v06
---
---
The catch is the skill. The drift was never yours to control.
---

## TAKEAWAY v07
---
---
A meeting you can describe afterward is a meeting you were actually in. That's the whole test.
---

## TAKEAWAY v08
---
---
Half-presence doesn't feel like a mistake in the moment. It feels like a mistake an hour later, when there's nothing there to recall.
---

## TAKEAWAY v09
---
---
The rehearsed defense in your head is usually for a trial that was never scheduled.
---

## TAKEAWAY v10
---
---
Your attention isn't broken. It's just doing what unattended attention always does — drifting toward whatever feels urgent, real or not.
---

## TAKEAWAY v11
---
---
Presence isn't the absence of leaving. It's the return, done again, and again, and again.
---

## TAKEAWAY v12
---
---
One clean catch in one meeting is not a small win. It's the entire mechanism, working exactly as designed.
---
