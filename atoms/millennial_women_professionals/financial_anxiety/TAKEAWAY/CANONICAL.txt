--- variant: v01
The financial anxiety is personal and political simultaneously. You're not just anxious about your money. You're anxious about what money means for women -- independence, safety, options. The takeaway is that addressing the anxiety requires honoring both dimensions: the practical and the historical.

--- variant: v02
No amount of savings will satisfy an anxiety that's really about control in a world that hasn't always let women have it. The takeaway is that financial security is real and important, and the anxiety that outlives the security is a different problem requiring a different solution.

--- variant: v03
The takeaway is that you can be financially responsible and still need help with the financial anxiety. The two are separate. One is a spreadsheet problem. The other is a nervous system problem that inherited fears from generations of women who didn't have what you have.
