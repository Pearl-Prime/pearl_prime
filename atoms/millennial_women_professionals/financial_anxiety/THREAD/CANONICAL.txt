You named the hypervigilance around money. The thread: the financial anxiety has a generational dimension. It's not just your anxiety. It's the accumulated anxiety of women in your family who didn't have financial autonomy. The thread leads to what you inherited and what's actually yours.

--- variant: v02
The thread from financial anxiety leads to control. Money is the domain where you feel most in control. The anxiety spikes when that control feels threatened -- not because you'll literally starve, but because control is the proxy for safety. The thread: what does safety actually require? Is it the same as what the anxiety demands?

--- variant: v03
Following this thread means separating the financial planning from the financial terror. They look similar from the outside. From the inside, one feels productive and the other feels compulsive. The thread is learning to tell the difference -- and trusting that you can make good financial decisions without the terror driving them.
