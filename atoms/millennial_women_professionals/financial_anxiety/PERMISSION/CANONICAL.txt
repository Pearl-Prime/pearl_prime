--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be anxious about money even when the numbers say you're fine. The anxiety doesn't follow the spreadsheet. It follows a much older map. You have permission to feel the anxiety without it meaning you're ungrateful or irrational.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to stop performing financial ease. The brunch, the wardrobe, the 'I'm fine' when someone asks about money. You're allowed to admit that financial security, even when you have it, doesn't always feel secure. That honesty is allowed.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to spend money on yourself without justifying it as an investment. Not everything needs an ROI. You have permission to buy something, do something, or save something purely because it makes you feel better. Your financial life is allowed to include pleasure.
