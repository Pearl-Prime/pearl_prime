COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Mika scrolls past another friend's house-buying announcement. The pulse in her neck hammers against the skin. Mika feels it in her fingertips. She feels the raw self surface where others can see it. The frame that has held everything in place — the shame is a verdict, delivered by the body, and the body does not lie — strains under the weight of what Mika sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Mika feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The shame is a sensation moving through the body, not a verdict handed down by truth. Mika does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Splitting the dinner check while pretending the number is fine. The base of her skull throbs. The tension radiates forward into her forehead. The shame pushes with full force against her sternum. Layla holds ground while seen, without flinching, without performing, without defending. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Layla moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

Priya scrolls past another friend's house-buying announcement. The walls close in. Her lungs refuse to expand against the compression. The shame arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Priya remains upright in the visibility and lets the shame be sensation without becoming story. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Priya acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Priya is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.
