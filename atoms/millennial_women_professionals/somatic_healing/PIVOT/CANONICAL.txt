--- variant: v01
You carry everything in your shoulders, your jaw, your lower back. The body has been keeping score while you've been keeping everything together. Somatic healing is the process of checking that score -- and discovering that the body's accounting is more honest than the narrative you've been telling yourself about how fine you are.

--- variant: v02
You were taught to push through physical discomfort the same way you push through professional discomfort: with grace and no complaint. The somatic pain isn't weakness. It's years of unfelt feelings stored in a body that learned early that 'dealing with it' meant ignoring it. Healing starts where the ignoring stops.

--- variant: v03
Your body has been trying to get your attention. The tension headaches, the stomach issues, the fatigue that sleep doesn't fix. These aren't random. They're the physical expression of emotional experiences you've been too busy, too competent, and too committed to other people's needs to process. Your body is the backlog. Somatic healing is opening the queue.
