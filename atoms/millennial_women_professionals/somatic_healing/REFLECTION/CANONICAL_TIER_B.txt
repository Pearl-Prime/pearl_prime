

## REFLECTION v01
---
family: F1
voice_mode: teacher
mechanism_emphasis: automatic
---
I can name this.. I understand the framework.. Here's what's happening: The mechanism running is automatic protective bracing.. My body has learned to stay braced, and this learning is automatic.. I'm not consciously holding tension.. The system has learned that tension equals safety.. So it stays tense.. My nervous system processed something—an event, a pattern, a repeated message—and wrote the response into my body.. Now my shoulders stay up.. My jaw stays clenched even in sleep.. What's strange is that the body has memory.. The mind has moved on from the original threat.. I've processed it, talked through it, made sense of it cognitively.. But the body is still executing the original defensive response.. Still protecting against a danger that no longer exists in this form.. In a yoga class, and the instructor invites relaxation, and my shoulders start shaking.. Not from exertion.. From the unfamiliarity of not defending.. The body has learned to brace so thoroughly that releasing that bracing feels dangerous.. The mechanism is so automatic I can't even access it consciously.
