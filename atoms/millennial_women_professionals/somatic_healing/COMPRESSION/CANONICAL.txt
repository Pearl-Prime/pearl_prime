

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Lower your shoulders every time you notice them raised. Small practice. Nervous system relearning. The body records what the mind edits out. The record doesn't disappear just because it isn't being read. The tension that lives in the body as posture, as bracing, as held breath — it arrived for a reason. It stays for the same reason.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
Your shoulders have been doing a job for years. The job is to hold something the rest of you was not allowed to put down. Lower the shoulders for a moment now. Notice what comes up to be held instead. That something is what the shoulders have been protecting you from feeling.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
The body is not a problem to solve. It is a record. Every breath you held to seem fine, every time you sat through a meeting in pain because asking for accommodation felt unprofessional — the record kept all of it. The healing is not about deleting the record. It is about being willing to read it without flinching.
