--- variant: v01
You're allowed to prioritize your body without calling it self-care. You have permission to attend to the tension, the pain, the fatigue as if they're important. Because they are. The body's needs aren't a luxury that comes after everything else is done. They're the foundation everything else depends on.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to feel things physically without understanding them intellectually. The tightness in your chest doesn't need a narrative. The knot in your stomach doesn't need a diagnosis. You're allowed to just feel them, breathe through them, and let them move without managing the process.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to take up space in your own body. To feel your weight, your breath, your presence without evaluating any of it. You have permission to inhabit your body without grading it. The somatic healing starts when you stop treating your body as a project and start treating it as home.
