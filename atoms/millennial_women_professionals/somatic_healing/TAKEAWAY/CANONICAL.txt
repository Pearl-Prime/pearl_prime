--- variant: v01
The takeaway is that your body has been keeping the real record. Not the one in your performance review or your calendar. The one that tracks what you actually felt versus what you were allowed to show. Somatic healing is reading that record and finally believing it.

--- variant: v02
Your body isn't broken. It's honest. The takeaway is that the physical symptoms are the body's way of telling the truth the mind has been editing. Somatic healing doesn't fix you. It listens to you -- the version of you that lives below the neck and has been waiting for someone to ask.

--- variant: v03
The takeaway is that healing the body and healing the emotional backlog are the same project. The tension, the pain, the fatigue -- those are emotions that got converted to physical storage because there was nowhere else for them to go. Releasing them is emotional work that happens through the body.
