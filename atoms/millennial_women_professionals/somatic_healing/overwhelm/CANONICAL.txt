## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Vera stands on the yoga mat trying to locate her own body. Sweat breaks along her hairline. Her shirt collar dampens. She watches the list grow while the clock shrinks. The frame that has held everything in place — the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole — strains under the weight of what Vera sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Vera feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The pile is made of individual items that can be addressed one at a time. Vera does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Pressing her palms against the cold porcelain sink. Aisha feels input exceeds all bandwidth — the queue locks and the screen goes white. The air evacuates the room. Her ribs lock. The diaphragm freezes. For months the frame has held: the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole. Now Aisha watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Sequence exists even when the panicking mind denies it — one thing, then the next. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Aisha stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.
---

## EMBODIMENT v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/embodiment/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Ren presses her palms against the cold porcelain sink. The base of her skull throbs. The tension radiates forward into her forehead. The overwhelm arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Ren picks up one item from the pile and sets the rest aside with steady hands. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Ren acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Ren is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
In the bathroom between back-to-back meetings. Nausea rolls through like a rogue wave. Tara grips the counter to stay vertical. The overwhelm pushes with full force against her sternum. Tara picks up one item from the pile and sets the rest aside with steady hands. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Tara moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/somatic_healing/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Layla pauses while pressing her palms against the cold porcelain sink. Her stomach drops hard. The nausea is sudden and specific. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The pile presents itself as one massive indivisible object when it is actually made of individual, addressable items. Layla traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Layla watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.

Claire pauses while pressing her palms against the cold porcelain sink. Her legs buckle. Claire catches the doorframe before going down. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The system conflates volume with urgency, treating everything as equally critical when triage is the actual need. Claire traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Claire watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.
