## EXERCISE v01
---

---
Your body is holding tension from being overextended.
You're pushing all the time. Work. Family. Obligations.
Your body needs to release this holding.
Lie down. Feel where you're gripping.
Shoulders? Belly? Jaw?
Put your hand there. Breathe toward it.
Don't force release. Just bring awareness.
Awareness itself creates softening.
Your body can't soften what it can't feel.
Notice what shifts when you pay attention.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
Stand and do slow, intentional movement.
Shoulder rolls backward. Five times. Slow.
Then forward. Five times. Slow.
Neck rolls. Side to side. Slow.
Gentle spinal twist. Hold each side.
Feel your spine. Vertebra by vertebra.
This is your central line. Feel it.
Feel where you're aligned. Where you're twisted.
Slow movement is how you find yourself again.
Not exercise. Presence.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You're disconnected from your own body sensations.
This happens when you're overworked and overextended.
Put music on that makes you feel something.
It doesn't need to be exercise.
Move however your body wants to move.
Fast or slow. Big or tiny.
You're not exercising. You're listening to your body.
Your body wants to move. Let it.
Feel the aliveness returning.
This is how you remember you're in a body.
---
