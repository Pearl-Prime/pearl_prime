## EXERCISE v30
---

---
Get a notebook. Divide a page into four boxes. Label them: AFRAID, SAFE, REAL, MYTH. When anxiety hits, write down what you're anxious about. Put it in one of the four boxes. AFRAID is what you're afraid will happen. SAFE is what will probably happen. REAL is what's actually happening. MYTH is the anxiety's lie. Do this every time you feel anxious. You're training your brain to see reality. Anxiety loses power when you name it. Name it every time.
