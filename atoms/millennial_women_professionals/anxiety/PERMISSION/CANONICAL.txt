--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be anxious without it meaning you're not handling things well enough. The anxiety and the handling coexist. You have permission to stop using the handling as proof that the anxiety doesn't count. It counts. You're allowed to say so.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to drop a ball. Just one. On purpose. To prove to your nervous system that the catastrophe it's been preparing for every waking moment might not actually come. You're allowed to find out what happens when you're not perfect. It's probably less than the anxiety has been advertising.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to take up space with your anxiety instead of hiding it behind competence. You're allowed to say 'I'm struggling' to someone who matters. You're allowed to need support without it being a reflection on your capability. Needing help and being capable coexist.
