--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be depressed and still be the woman everyone relies on. But you're also allowed to stop being the woman everyone relies on while you're depressed. Both are allowed. You have permission to choose the version that serves your actual needs.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to not be okay in visible ways. Not just privately, where the depression has been living. Visibly. To the people who love you. You're allowed to let them see it because the secrecy is its own weight, and you're carrying enough already.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to get help that isn't a self-help book, a wellness podcast, or a curated routine. Professional help. Clinical help. The kind that treats depression as a medical reality and not a failure of optimization. You have permission to be a patient, not just a performer.
