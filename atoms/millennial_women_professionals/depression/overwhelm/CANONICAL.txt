## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/depression/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Lying on the couch at 4 pm watching light cross the wall. Priya feels another demand land on top of a pile that already wobbles. Priya feels blood pounding in her temples. The room pulses. For months the frame has held: the pile is one solid object that must be lifted whole. Now Priya watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. Sequence exists even when the panicking mind denies it — one thing, then the next. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Priya stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.
---

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/depression/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
The room tilts. Her legs lose the certainty of ground beneath them. Jess is standing in the kitchen without knowing why she walked there when she feels the pile collapses under its own mass and every task blurs into one indivisible wall. The operating assumption — falling behind means failing entirely — meets a fact it cannot absorb. Her jaw unclenches. Not relief. Disorientation. The frame that held her world in place tilts on its axis. Jess does not fall. She stands in the vertigo and lets the room reorient around her new position. Sequence exists even when the panicking mind denies it — one thing, then the next. She does not grasp for a replacement frame. She breathes into the space where the old certainty stood and finds the space survivable.

## EMBODIMENT v05
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 5
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Standing in the kitchen without knowing why she walked there. Her breath catches mid-inhale. The air thickens. The overwhelm pushes with full force against her sternum. Zara chooses one task, moves it forward, and lets the pile exist without attending to it. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Zara moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

## EMBODIMENT v06
---

BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity

Leah stands in the kitchen without knowing why she walked there. Her chest caves inward. Breathing stops. The body refuses the air. The overwhelm arrives — hot, familiar, insistent, demanding the old response. This time Leah puts everything down except the next single action and begins there. Not as strategy. Not as technique. As identity. The sensation remains in her body — chest tight, breath shallow, adrenaline still circulating. Leah acts through it. The acting is not despite the sensation. It is through the sensation and alongside it. The action carries her name. This is who Leah is now. Someone who moves with the fire still burning.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/depression/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Lying on the couch at 4 pm watching light cross the wall. The base of her skull throbs. The tension radiates forward into her forehead. The overwhelm operates in plain view for the first time. Vera catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The system conflates volume with urgency, treating everything as equally critical when triage is the actual need. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Vera watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
---

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/depression/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 2
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Her chest caves inward. Breathing stops. The body refuses the air. Claire sits in the car in the office parking garage. The overwhelm runs its program at full speed. Claire watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the overwhelm creates the illusion that everything must be handled simultaneously, hiding the fact that sequence is always possible. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Claire sees this now. Her ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Claire stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not her name.
---
