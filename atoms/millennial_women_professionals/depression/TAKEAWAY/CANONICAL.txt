--- variant: v01
The depression isn't a contradiction of your success. It's the emotional underside of it. The takeaway is that achievement without connection, recognition without rest, and competence without compassion for yourself will always leave a deficit. The depression is the deficit.

--- variant: v02
You can't think your way out of depression the way you think your way through a project plan. The takeaway is that the tool that got you here -- relentless cognitive effort -- is not the tool that gets you out. Something else is needed. Something softer and harder at the same time.

--- variant: v03
The takeaway from high-functioning depression is that functioning isn't the same as living. You've been so good at performing that the performance became the whole thing. The depression lives in the gap between what you show and what you feel. Closing that gap is the work that matters now.
