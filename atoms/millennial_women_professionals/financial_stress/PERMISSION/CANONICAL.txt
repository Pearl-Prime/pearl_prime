--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be financially stressed without it diminishing your accomplishments. Financial stress doesn't erase what you've built. You have permission to hold both realities: you've achieved extraordinary things, and the finances are hard right now. Those coexist.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to ask for financial help without shame. From a professional, a trusted person, an institution designed to help. The financial stress isolated you because you believed asking was failing. It's not. It's the most adult, responsible thing you can do.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to be angry about the financial situation. At the system, at the economy, at the gap between what you were told and what you got. The anger isn't unbecoming. It's honest. You have permission to feel it without it being unprofessional.
