--- variant: v01
You're allowed to be burned out without having a solution yet. The burnout doesn't need a plan before it's valid. You have permission to just be depleted, without immediately trying to fix the depletion. The fixing instinct is part of what depleted you.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to let things be imperfect while you recover. The house can be messy. The email can wait. The meal can be ordered. You're allowed to lower the standard temporarily -- or permanently -- and let the world adjust. It will adjust. It always does when women stop carrying things silently.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to rest in a way that looks like doing nothing. Not a spa day. Not a self-care Sunday with curated activities. Actual nothing. You have permission to stare at a wall and not feel guilty about it. The guilt is the burnout's last trick -- making even your rest feel unproductive.
