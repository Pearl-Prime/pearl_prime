

## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You need to stop giving energy away. Turn off your phone. Not silent. Off. Go sit on your couch. Close your eyes. Don't do anything. Just sit. For fifteen minutes, do nothing. No help. No advice. No energy. Just sit. Feel your body. Feel your tiredness. This is real. After fifteen minutes, open your eyes. You just reclaimed yourself. Do this every day. Same time. Same place. Non-negotiable.

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You're thinking about all the people who need you. Stop. Write them down. Write each person. Now draw a circle around your name. You're the most important person on this list. You're in the center. Everyone else is on the outside. This isn't selfish. This is survival. You can't pour from an empty cup. So make yourself the priority. Put your name in the center. Protect that circle.

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
Make a do not disturb list. Write the times when you are unavailable. Eight pm to ten am. Weekends. Your lunch hour. Whatever is true. Now enforce it. Text your people: I'm not available during these times. I'll respond after. They don't like it. That's fine. Your energy is finite. You're protecting it. Send the text. Enforce it. You'll be exhausted for another week if you don't.

## EXERCISE v04
---

---

You're protecting yourself. You can't help anyone if you're empty. Empty is where you are. So stop trying. Hang up. Go be alone. Fill yourself up. The person will survive without you for a few hours.

## EXERCISE v06
---

---
