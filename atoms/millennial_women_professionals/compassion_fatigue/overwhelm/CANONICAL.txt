## TURNING_POINT v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Absorbing someone else's crisis for the second time this week. Vera watches the list grow while the clock shrinks. Her jaw locks. Teeth press together hard enough to ache. For months the frame has held: everything is equally urgent and nothing can wait. Now Vera watches the frame buckle under evidence it cannot metabolize. Her body registers the shift before her mind can catch up. The chest opens half an inch. Something loosens at the base of her spine. The muscles that held the old posture begin to release. The pile is made of individual items that can be addressed one at a time. The old story does not end with a conclusion. It ends with a silence. Vera stands in that silence. Her hands open at her sides.

## TURNING_POINT v07
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/turning_point/micro/v07.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 3
COST_TYPE: opportunity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: experimentation
---
Priya listens to another colleague cry in the hallway. Her legs buckle. Priya catches the doorframe before going down. She feels the pile collapses under its own mass and every task blurs into one indivisible wall. The frame that has held everything in place — everything is equally urgent and nothing can wait — strains under the weight of what Priya sees. For one long second the old architecture holds. Then it cracks. Priya feels the fracture travel through her chest. The story that organized every decision, every reaction, every judgment stops organizing. The pile is made of individual items that can be addressed one at a time. Priya does not reach for a replacement. She stands in the gap where the certainty lived, breathing into the new and terrifying openness.

## EMBODIMENT v05

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 4
COST_TYPE: identity
COST_INTENSITY: 4
IDENTITY_STAGE: self_claim
---
Listening to another colleague cry in the hallway. A tremor moves through her hands. Claire presses them flat against the surface. The overwhelm pushes with full force against her sternum. Claire chooses one task, moves it forward, and lets the pile exist without attending to it. The body shakes with the effort. The choice holds anyway. This is not willpower overriding sensation. This is ownership absorbing sensation. Claire moves from the new ground she has claimed. Her hands tremble and her hands act. Both facts are true simultaneously. The identity is not a concept. It lives in her muscles, in the steadiness of her spine, in the way her feet stay planted while the old pattern screams for retreat.

Nausea rolls through like a rogue wave. Zara grips the counter to stay vertical. Zara stands after the third emotional one-on-one today. The overwhelm screams at peak volume, demanding the old compliance. Zara picks up one item from the pile and sets the rest aside with steady hands. Not despite the sensation — she feels every degree of it. Her body carries the heat. Her choices carry her name. She does not wait for the feeling to pass before acting. She acts while the feeling burns at full intensity. The acting is the embodiment. The body and the choice exist in the same moment. Zara owns this. The ownership lives in her hands, in her breath, in the ground beneath her feet.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v05.txt
BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Mika pauses while absorbing someone else's crisis for the second time this week. The pulse in her neck hammers against the skin. Mika feels it in her fingertips. The overwhelm has run for hours without interruption. Now she sees the machinery laid bare. The overwhelm creates the illusion that everything must be handled simultaneously, hiding the fact that sequence is always possible. Mika traces the loop in real time: trigger, activation, the relief that never actually arrives, then the trigger again. Her stomach clenches as she maps the circuit from inside it. Seeing the mechanism does not stop the gears. But it strips the mechanism of its authority. Mika watches the loop turn without feeding it another round.

## MECHANISM_PROOF v06
---
path: story_atoms/millennial_women_professionals/anchored/compassion_fatigue/overwhelm/mechanism_proof/micro/v06.txt
BAND: 5
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Her body shakes — not a tremor but a quake that starts in the core. Layla absorbs someone else's crisis for the second time this week. The overwhelm runs its program at full speed. Layla watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the overwhelm creates the illusion that everything must be handled simultaneously, hiding the fact that sequence is always possible. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Layla sees this now. Her ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Layla stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not her name.
