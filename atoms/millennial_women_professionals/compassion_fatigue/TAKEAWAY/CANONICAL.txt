--- variant: v01
The compassion fatigue is telling you something the culture won't: being the emotional labor department for everyone in your life is a full-time job you were never paid for and never applied for. The takeaway is that you need to resign from at least some of those positions.

--- variant: v02
You haven't lost your capacity to care. You've lost your tolerance for caring being treated as free and unlimited. The takeaway is that compassion without reciprocity isn't virtue. It's exploitation, and recognizing that isn't cynical. It's honest.

--- variant: v03
The takeaway from compassion fatigue isn't how to care more. It's learning that you're allowed to care differently. Selectively. Strategically. With the same thoughtfulness you bring to your professional work, applied finally to the question of where your emotional energy goes.
