## EXERCISE v01
---

---
You're spiraling about something said in a meeting.
You're replaying it in your head obsessively.
Your mind is trying to solve something unsolvable.
Ground yourself in the present moment.
Name three things you can see right now.
Name three things you can touch.
Name one sound you hear.
Your nervous system is in the past.
Your body is safe in the present.
Feel that difference.
---

## EXERCISE v02
---

---
You're overthinking a decision you already made.
Second-guessing. Replaying. Wondering if it was right.
You can't change it now.
Overthinking doesn't improve a past decision.
It just steals present peace.
Say out loud: "I made the best choice I could with the information I had."
That's true. Now stop replaying.
Do something that requires full attention.
Work. Exercise. Conversation. Something engaging.
Your mind will stop spiraling when it's occupied.
---

## EXERCISE v03
---

---
You're thinking about future scenarios that haven't happened.
Anxiety creates stories to prepare you for danger.
But the stories are fictional.
Notice: this is my anxiety narrating.
Feel your feet on the ground.
Feel your hands holding something real.
This moment is safe.
Future moments are for future you to handle.
Right now, you're fine.
Let the anxiety story move past like a cloud.
---
