--- variant: v01
You caught the pattern. The thread: the overthinking has two layers. One is the actual decision. The other is the perception management -- how will I be seen? The thread leads to separating those layers. When you remove the perception layer, what's the actual decision? Usually it's much simpler.

--- variant: v02
The thread from overthinking leads to the body. While your mind runs loops, your body is holding a feeling that the loops are preventing you from noticing. The thread: what do you feel in your body when you force the thinking to pause? That sensation is the signal the overthinking was drowning out.

--- variant: v03
The thread is about permission. The overthinking will ease when you give yourself permission to be wrong, to be disliked, to be misunderstood. Each loop is trying to prevent one of those outcomes. The thread: which outcome are you most afraid of? That's the specific permission you need.
