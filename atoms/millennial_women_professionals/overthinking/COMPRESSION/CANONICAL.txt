

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C3
You know, on some level, that the loop isn't helping. You do it anyway. This isn't weakness or stupidity. It's the difference between knowing something intellectually and knowing it in your bones. You're in the process of learning your way into believing that the loop isn't necessary. That's the real work, and it takes time.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
You have been called thoughtful since you were small. You learned that catching the problem before it arrived was love. So you scan. You forecast. You rehearse the difficult conversation in the shower. The cost of the scanning is not visible to anyone. It does not show up in performance reviews. It shows up in the quiet between obligations, when the scanning has nothing to scan.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C5
Try this: when the loop starts, name it out loud. "I am rehearsing." "I am forecasting." "I am running the conversation again." Naming does not stop the loop. It changes who is watching it. The watcher is the part of you that already knows the loop is not the answer. She has been waiting for you to come back.
