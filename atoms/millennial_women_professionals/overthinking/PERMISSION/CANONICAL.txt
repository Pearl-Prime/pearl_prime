--- variant: v01
You're allowed to make a decision without running it through the perception filter first. You have permission to choose based on what you think, not on how the choice will be received. The perception management is extra labor you're allowed to put down.

--- variant: v02
You have permission to be misunderstood. Not every miscommunication is your fault and not every one needs to be prevented. You're allowed to say the thing, send the email, make the call without rehearsing it twelve times. Some of the overthinking is optional. You're allowed to opt out.

--- variant: v03
You're allowed to trust yourself faster. The overthinking is a lack of self-trust wearing the costume of thoroughness. You have permission to make the call, trust your judgment, and deal with whatever follows. You've been dealing with whatever follows your entire life. You're good at it.
