## MECHANISM_PROOF v05
---

BAND: 4
MECHANISM_DEPTH: 2
COST_TYPE: internal
COST_INTENSITY: 3
IDENTITY_STAGE: destabilization
---
Her fingers dig into her own palms. Crescents form in the skin. Priya stands at dinner when the work phone buzzes again. The alarm runs its program at full speed. Priya watches with the detachment of someone reading their own diagnostic. The mechanism reveals itself in full: the threat detector runs on outdated code — it responds to the shape of the past, not the content of the present. Every previous attempt to fight the pattern used the same logic that powers it. Priya sees this now. Her ribs expand with the recognition. Not relief — clarity. The pattern does not stop. But Priya stops mistaking it for truth. It is machinery. It is not her name.

Reading the 10 pm slack notification. Her chest caves inward. Breathing stops. The body refuses the air. The alarm operates in plain view for the first time. Tara catches the pattern mid-cycle and holds still long enough to see the entire architecture. The threat detector runs on outdated code — it responds to the shape of the past, not the content of the present. The mechanism persists because it disguises itself as protection — it wears the costume of something necessary. Tara watches the loop close and reopen. Her body holds the tension of seeing without intervening. The seeing does not resolve the pattern. But it makes the pattern visible, and visibility changes everything.
