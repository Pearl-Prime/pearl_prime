

## COMPRESSION v30
compression_family: C5
Try this: Claim one accomplishment this week. Not to brag. Just to practice believing yourself. The doubt isn't evidence of incompetence. It's evidence of self-awareness that hasn't caught up to itself yet. The credential doesn't silence the doubt because the doubt isn't looking at the credential. It's looking for the exception.

## COMPRESSION v31
compression_family: C1
The first time someone called you an expert, you waited for the correction. The correction never came. So you doubled the proof. Credentials. Specialized vocabulary. Late nights. Each one quieted the doubt for an afternoon. None of them rewrote the original story: that being good was suspicious until proven otherwise.

## COMPRESSION v32
compression_family: C3
Watch what happens when someone says nice work. The instinct is not to receive. It is to deflect, qualify, or credit the team. The deflection is so practiced it feels like humility. It is not humility. It is a body refusing to let praise land because the body learned that landed praise becomes a target. Try letting it land for ten seconds. Then notice what gets loud.
