--- variant: v01
You named the feeling. The thread: the imposter syndrome has two sources. One is internal -- your own doubt. The other is external -- a system that genuinely treats you differently. The thread is learning to separate the two, because the solutions are different. One needs self-compassion. The other needs systemic awareness.

--- variant: v02
The thread from imposter syndrome leads to belonging. Not fitting in -- belonging. Fitting in requires adjustment. Belonging requires acceptance. The thread: where have you been adjusting yourself to fit, and what would it feel like to simply belong? That shift is the thread worth following.

--- variant: v03
Following this thread means examining what 'qualified' means to you. The standard you hold yourself to is different from the standard you'd apply to someone else. The thread: whose standard are you using? Usually it's an internalized version of the harshest critic you've encountered. That critic's standards aren't yours.
