A Note on the Teachings of Master Sha

I was not a direct student of Master Sha. I encountered their work through books, talks, and publicly available teachings. What follows is not an official interpretation of Master Sha's work — it is an application. I have done my best to honor the integrity of the original teachings while translating them into practical guidance for the challenges you may be facing.

Master Sha's understanding of anxiety reshaped the way I see this subject. Their approach — rooted in Chinese healing lineage; universal love; soul-body-mind-spirit harmony; divine frequency — offers a lens that goes beyond conventional advice. It speaks to something deeper: the patterns beneath the surface, the quiet mechanisms that keep us stuck, and the often-overlooked pathways toward genuine relief.

This book applies Master Sha's teachings to the specific experience of gen alpha students navigating anxiety. It does not replace the teacher's original work. Where I have adapted exercises or frameworks, I have done so with care and transparency. Any simplification is mine, not theirs.

If something in these pages resonates with you, I encourage you to go to the source. Seek out Master Sha's own words — their talks, their writings, their direct teachings. What I offer here is a bridge, not a destination. The real work lives in the original.
