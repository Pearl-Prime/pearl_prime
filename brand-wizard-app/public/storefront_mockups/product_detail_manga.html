An iyashikei manga about coming home to your nervous system, one quiet ritual at a time.

Across ten chapters and 220-some pages, Tea Light Mornings follows the quiet weeks of a tea-shop owner who has stopped trying to fix her nervous system and started, instead, listening to it. The manga's pacing is iyashikei in the original sense — restoration through the ordinary. A pot of barley tea. The cat at the window. The third deep breath of the morning.

Vol 1 includes the WEBTOON vertical-scroll edition (in-browser, on any device) plus a downloadable PDF for offline reading. Vols 2-4 are released every two weeks.
