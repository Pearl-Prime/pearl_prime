How to Calm Anxiety and Reclaim Your Nervous System — for mid-level leaders

★★★★★ 4.6 · 184 reviews

You've been the one in charge so long you don't remember being able to put it down. The team needs you. Your inbox needs you. The slack ping at 9:47 PM needs you. And by Sunday afternoon, your body has already started rehearsing for Monday — even though Monday is still 18 hours away.

Quiet Safe Enough is a nervous-system-first companion for the leader who has tried discipline, willpower, and the right calendar app and still can't make Sunday feel like Sunday. Across nine chapters, ahjansam offers practices that take 60 seconds, 90 seconds, sometimes 5 minutes — small enough to do between meetings, honest enough to actually shift something. No 30-day plans. No optimization. Just the practices a body needs to remember it is, in fact, safe enough.
