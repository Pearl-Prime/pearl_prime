2. Operational lesson — Pearl Star concurrency:
   3 parallel agents pushing prompts simultaneously saturate ComfyUI's
   single-threaded HTTP handler (cascading urlopen timeouts, VRAM exhaustion).
   ALWAYS use ONE single sequential agent for sustained batches >50 calls.
   Multiple agents may be safe for small batches with infrequent submissions.
