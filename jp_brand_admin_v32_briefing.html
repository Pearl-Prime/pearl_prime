Cultural Protocols

文化的プロトコル

Japan market requires specific adaptations that go beyond translation. These protocols are enforced automatically by the pipeline.

Honorific System

敬語システム

All teacher/reader interactions use appropriate keigo. Teacher profiles include honorific preferences. Exercise prompts never use casual forms in initial encounters.

Seasonal Resonance

季節の共鳴

Content release maps to Japanese seasonal calendar. Cherry blossom (awakening), summer festivals (connection), autumn reflection (mastery), winter solitude (transcendence).

Visual Density

ビジュアル密度

JP readers expect higher info density per page. Panel layouts use 6-8 panels vs. Western 4-6. Text-to-image prompts increase detail parameters by 30%.

Reading Direction

読み方向

Right-to-left panel flow for manga formats. Left-to-right for prose/exercise content. Layout system handles direction switching per atom type.

Group Harmony

調和 (Wa)

Social Wiring adapted: sharing framed as "introducing a helpful resource" not personal endorsement. Anonymous recommendation option reduces social friction.

Quiet Authority

静かな権威

Transformation Promise uses indirect framing. "After reading, you may notice..." vs. "You will..." Indirect language outperforms direct claims by 2.4x in JP conversion.
